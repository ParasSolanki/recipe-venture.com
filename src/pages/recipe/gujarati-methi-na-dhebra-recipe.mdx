### Ingredients

- 2 inch Ginger - ground to paste.
- 2 teaspoons Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 5 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar - (optional).
- 1 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 2 cups Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Jowar Flour (Sorghum).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour - More for dusting.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 cup Bajra Flour ( Pearl Millet).
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- 3 Green Chillies - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Gujarati Methi Na Dhebra Recipe, get all the ingredients ready.
1. In a large bowl, mix fenugreek leaves, ginger paste, green chilies, sesame seeds, chili powder, turmeric powder, asafoetida.
1. Mix it well with a teaspoon of oil.
1. Keep aside for 5 minutes to help fenugreek leaves soften a bit.
1. Add millet flour, whole wheat flour, sugar and salt.
1. Mix well using your finger so fenugreek mixture will incorporate well with flours.
1. It will look crumbly texture.
1. Add tablespoon yogurt at a time and knead to form soft dough.
1.  You will need 5 or 6 tablespoon of yogurt depends on your flour or depends on the consistency of your yogurt .
1. So add little yogurt at a time so will come to know how much you need.
1. Cover it and let it stand for 10-15 minutes.
1. After 15 minutes, knead it again to make it smooth.
1. Divide it into 10 equal portions.
1. Roll each portion between your palm and press into a circle.
1. Heat the tawa on medium heat.
1. Dip the flattened ball into dry chapati flour and coat well.
1. Roll it gently in to circle about 6 inch in diameter.
1. Once hot placed rolled dhebra on the skillet.
1. When you see bubbles on it flip it.
1. After few seconds apply some oil on it and flip it again and press gently using spatula and cook until you see some brown spot on bottom side.
1. Apply some oil again (optional) on other side and flip and cook on that side.
1. When done, take it out from skillet/tawa proceed similarly till all the dough is used up.
