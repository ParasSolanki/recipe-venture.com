### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 3 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split) - soaked for an hour.
- Salt - Salt to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 2 tablespoons Mustard oil.
- 2 Tomatoes.
- 1 Onion - roughly chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Moong Dal Dubka Recipe, wash and soak the moong dal for an hour, then grind it to a coarse paste using a mixer grinder and keep aside.
1. Make a fine paste of the ingredients mentioned in the spice paste table using a mixer and keep aside.
1. Smoke mustard oil in a wide pan on medium heat till it is seasoned well.
1. Add the ground spice paste into it and fry until the raw smell of the onion garlic goes away.
1. Add the tomato puree, turmeric powder, season with salt and saute until the tomato puree is cooked well.
1. Now, add the coarsely ground moong dal mix and enough water and let it simmer until the dal is cooked.
1. It keeps getting thicker, so adjust the amount of water to your desired consistency and season with required salt.
1. Switch off the flame and serve the Moong Dal Dubka Recipe along with Steamed Rice and Kalonji Gobi Aloo recipe for a hearty winter lunch.
