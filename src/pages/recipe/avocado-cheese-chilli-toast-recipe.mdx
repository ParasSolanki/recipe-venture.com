### Ingredients

- 2 Tomatoes - thinly sliced.
- Red Chilli flakes - as required.
- 1 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 Cup Cheese - grated.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 4 tablespoons Classic Mayonnaise (With Egg).
- 2 tablespoons Butter.
- 1/2 Avocado - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Avocado Cheese Chilli Toast, take each bread slices and apply butter evenly on either side, sufficient enough to toast the bread.
1. Heat a skillet and put the slice on it.
1. Let it cook on medium for 2 to 4 minutes on either side, or until bread is toasted.
1. Once done, spread a thin layer or mayonnaise on each slice of bread.
1. Put tomato and avocado slices and sprinkle black pepper powder + red chilli flakes on each toast.
1. Top each toast with some grated cheese.
1. Serve Avocado Cheese Chilli Toast with Espresso Coffee for your tea time break.
