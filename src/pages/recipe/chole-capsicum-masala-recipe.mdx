### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 teaspoons Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Cashew nuts.
- 1 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- Salt - to taste.
- Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - diced.
- 1 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - soaked and cooked until soft.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chole Capsicum Masala recipe, soak cashew nuts in warm water for 12 to 15 minutes.
1. Heat oil in a wok/kadai.
1. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
1. Add garlic paste and saute for 1 minute.
1. Next add the cubed capsicum pieces and saute for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Then add cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder and chilli powder.
1. Fry for a minute, taking care not to burn.
1. Now add the tomato sauce/puree and boiled chickpeas along with 2 cups of water.
1. Let this mixture come to boil and then simmer for 5 minutes until the flavours mix together.
1. Meanwhile, make a paste of soaked cashew nuts.
1. Add the cashew paste to the curry along with salt.
1. Simmer for 4 to 5 minutes.
1. Add kasoori methi, remove from flame and serve hot.
1. Serve Chole Capsicum Masala along with Kachumber Salad, Steamed Rice and Phulkas for a weekday meal.
