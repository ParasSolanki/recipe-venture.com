### Ingredients

- 1 Brinjal (Baingan / Eggplant) - cubed.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 1/2 tablespoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery - powdered (optional).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 10-15 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Kathirikai Rasavangi Recipe - Eggplant is a Tangy Coconut Gravy, pressure cook the toor dal with 1 cup of water for 4 to 5 whistles.
1. Turn off the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Once the dal is cooked, whisk the dal well and keep it aside.
1. In the meantime, heat a small pan on medium heat and dry roast chana dal, coriander seeds, red chilly and peppercorns till it gets aromatic and turns golden brown.
1. Once roasted, add the grated coconut to this and keep stirring till it turns brown.
1. Turn off the heat, cool and grind to a smooth mixture by adding a 1/4 cup of water.
1. Keep this aside.
1. Heat oil in a preheat pan; add the mustard seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Add the curry leaves and brinjal, sprinkle salt and give it a stir.
1. Cover the pan and cook the brinjal until roasted and soft.
1. To the cooked brinjal, add the tamarind water, turmeric powder, jaggery, cooked dal, coconut mixture and salt to taste.
1. Simmer the Kathirikai Rasavangi  for a good 5 minutes until the raw smell of tamarind goes away and the rasavangi has good taste and flavours.
1. Check the salt and adjust to taste accordingly.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and transfer the Kathirikai Rasavangi to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve the Kathirikai Rasavangi Recipe with Steamed Rice, Keerai Masiyal, Potato Curry to make this recipe a complete south indian meal.
