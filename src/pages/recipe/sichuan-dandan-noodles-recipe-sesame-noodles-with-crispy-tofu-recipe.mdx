### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 300 grams Egg noodles - cooked until firm.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 2 tablespoons Peanut Butter.
- 2 tablespoons Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 2 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Red Chilli sauce - (preferably chili oil).
- 2 tablespoons Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 2 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Vinegar.
- 3 Stalks Spring Onion Greens - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 200 grams Tofu - cubed salt to taste.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spicy Sesame Noodles With Crispy Tofu Recipe, first prep all the ingredients and keep them aside.
1. In a small mixing bowl, whisk together all the ingredients for the peanut chili sauce and keep it aside.
1. Cook the noodles in a saucepan with enough water till it is soft and cooked.
1. Drain the noodles and keep it under cold running water for 10 seconds and add a tablespoon of oil and toss the noodles so that it does not stick.
1. Heat a teaspoon of oil in a wok or a  sauce-pan; add ginger, garlic, red chilies, onions and bell peppers and saute for 5 minutes till it becomes soft and brown.
1. Add the tofu, sprinkle some salt and roast until lightly browned.
1. Then add the mixed peanut chili sauce mixture and toss the tofu well into the sauce.
1. Cook till it is evenly coated but make sure the mixture does not become too lumpy and thick.
1. Add the cooked noodles and toss all the ingredients together until well combined (at least for 2 minutes).
1. Toss the noodles till it is wet and not dry.
1. Once done, turn off the heat, adjust the salt and seasoning to your taste and serve by sprinkling some spring onions.
1. Serve the with Sichuan Dan Dan Noodles Recipe (Sesame Noodles With Crispy Tofu Recipe) with Dry-Frieddinner meal.
