### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 3 Onions - ground into a paste.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Ginger - finely chopped.
- 6 Cashew nuts.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Tomatoes - pureed.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 2 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 5 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Restaurant Style Green Peas Masala, we have to first cook the peas.
1. Pressure cook the green peas along with 2 tablespoons water for 1 whistle.
1. Release the pressure of the cooker immediately.
1. Keep the cooked peas aside.
1. Meanwhile, soak cashew nuts in warm water for 15 minutes.
1. Once the cashews have softened, add them to a mixer and grind them along with curd to make a smooth paste.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, heat oil on medium flame.
1. Once the oil is hot, reduce it to a low flame, add bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cardamom and cloves.
1. Once the aroma of the spices start wafting in the air, add chopped garlic & ginger and saut√© until the raw smell goes away.
1. This will take about a minute or two.
1. Next, add onion paste, tomato puree and saute until the raw smell of onions goes away.
1. This will take about 5 minutes.
1. Then add all the dry spice powders including turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, red chilli powder and salt to taste.
1. Mix them well and add 2 tablespoons of water to the mixture to avoid burning of the spices.
1. Once the spices are well cooked, add required water to get the required consistency of your choice.
1. Boil for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Next, add the cashew-curd mixture (paste) and stir continuously for the proper mixing of ingredients.
1. Now ,add the cooked peas and let the Green Peas Masala cook on a low flame for 5 minutes.
1. Finally, switch off the gas and add a dollop of butter and chopped coriander leaves on top.
1. Serve Restaurant Style Green Peas Masala with Phulkas or Tawa Paratha along with a Cauliflower and Cucumber Raita for your everyday meals.
