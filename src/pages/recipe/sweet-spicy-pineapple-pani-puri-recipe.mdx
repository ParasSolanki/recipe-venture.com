### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 1 tablespoon Sugar.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- Black Salt (Kala Namak) - to taste.
- 2 cups Pineapple - peeled.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera) - 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder.
- 2 Potato (Aloo) - boiled.
- 1/2 cup Green Moong Sprouts.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 18 Pani Puris.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sweet & Spicy Pineapple Pani Puri Recipe, we will first make the Sweet & Spicy Pineapple Pani.
1. In a mixer jar, combine the pineapple pieces along with green chilli, chaat masala, cumin powder, sugar, salt and grind it along with some water.
1. Transfer to a serving bowl, add the lemon juice and adjust the salt and spices of the pineapple pani according to your taste.
1. Place the pineapple pani in the refrigerator to chill.
1. Note, you don't have to strain the fiber from the pineapple, it tastes delicious with the fiber when had along with the crisp puri.
1. For the Pani Puri filling, in a mixing bowl, mash the potatoes, add the green moong sprouts and the dry masalas- chaat masala, cumin powder and red chilli powder.
1. Mix well.
1. Serve Sweet & Spicy Pineapple Pani Puri , by slightly crack the puris on the top to make an opening, stuff the potato-sprouts filling, fill it with the Sweet & Spicy Pineapple Pani and serve.
1. You can either use store bought puris, or make them at home by following this link Puri for Pani Puri Recipe.
1. Serve Sweet & Spicy Pineapple Pani Puri at your chaat party long with Dahi Batata Puri Chaat Recipe and Chole Aloo Tikki Chaat Recipe.
