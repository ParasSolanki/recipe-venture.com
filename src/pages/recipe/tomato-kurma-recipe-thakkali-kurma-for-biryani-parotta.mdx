### Ingredients

- 3 Green Chillies.
- 3 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 4 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- Salt - as per your taste.
- 1 tablespoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 tablespoon Water.
- 2 Star anise.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 tablespoons Sambar Powder.
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Ghee.
- 2 tablespoons Roasted Gram Dal (Pottukadalai).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Tomato Kurma Recipe, we will first grind all the ingredients needs for the Kurma paste.
1. In a mixer combine, add roasted gram dal, fresh grated coconut, green chillies, fennel seeds and a tablespoon of water.
1. Grind the ingredients until you get a smooth paste.
1. Keep aside for later.
1. Next, place a heavy bottomed pan on medium heat and add 2 teaspoons of ghee.
1. Once the ghee is warm, add cumin seeds and allow it to sizzle.
1. When the cumin seeds are sizzling, add the star anise, cinnamon and saute it for about 30 to 35 seconds.
1. Add the chopped onions and stir until they are caramelized.
1. Next, add the chopped tomatoes along with sambar powder and saute until the tomatoes are soft and mushy.
1. Once the tomatoes are mushy, add the ground kurma paste, salt to taste and simmer the flame to low.
1.  Add 1/4 cup water, stir and close the lid of the pan.
1. Let the tomato kurma simmer for 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes, open the lid of the pan, give it a good stir and turn off the flame.
1. Garnish it with coriander leaves and it is ready to be served.
1. Serve Tomato Kurma Recipe along with Vegetable Biryani and Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita.
1. You can also serve Tomato Kurma along with Kerala Style Parotta for a weeknight dinner.
