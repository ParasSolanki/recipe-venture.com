### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 tablespoon Dried Thyme Leaves.
- Tomato Salsa - to serve.
- Salt.
- 2 teaspoon Paprika powder.
- 1 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 1 cup Rajma (Large Kidney Beans) - soaked overnight.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoon Paprika powder.
- Sour cream - to serve.
- 4 Tortillas - to serve.
- 1 Onion - sliced.
- Guacamole - to serve.
- 2 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 Green bell peppers - sliced.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- Sunflower Oil.
- Cheddar cheese - to serve.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mexican Veg Fajitas Recipe,To make the Stir Fried Beans Pressure cook the rajma that is soaked overnight in a pressure cooker for 6-7 whistle.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally when done.
1. Heat a flat skillet on medium heat, add oil, saute the onions till it gives a nice golden color.
1. Add in chopped tomatoes and sprinkle some salt, cook until it turns slightly mushy.
1. Once the tomatoes are half done , add spice powders like paprika, pepper powder, cumin powder salt to taste and give it a toss.
1. Add in the cooked rajma beans along with any water left.
1. Cook in simmer for at least 10 minutes until the water evaporates and it thickens.
1. Your stir fried beans are ready.
