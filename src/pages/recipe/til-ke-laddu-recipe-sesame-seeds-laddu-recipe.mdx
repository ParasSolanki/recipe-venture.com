### Ingredients

- 250 grams Jaggery.
- 1/2 cup Dessicated Coconut.
- 2 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1/2 cup Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - crushed.
- 250 grams Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - 2 cups.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Til Ke Laddu Recipe (Sesame Seeds Laddu Recipe) by heating a flat nonstick Kadai.
1. Keep it in a medium heat, add the sesame seeds and dry roast them till it changes colour.
1. Remove and keep it aside.
1. Then add the peanuts and dry roast them till it is brown.
1. Remove from the flame and let it cool down, and ground it into a coarse mixture using mortar pestle.
1. In the same pan, dry roast the desiccated coconut till it is golden brown and keep it aside.
1. Mix all the 3 ingredients together along with cardamom powder and keep it aside.
1. Next to prepare the jaggery syrup, heat the kadai and add the powdered jaggery with Â½ cup water and keep stirring till all the lumps is mixed well.
1. Keep stirring till it boils and reaches a soft boil stage.
1. Which means when you add a drop of jaggery syrup on the table it has to stay in the shape of a drop and not spread.
1. You can also check by add the the drops of jaggery into a bowl containing water, once you remove the jaggery out it should be sticky and have the soft ball shape.
1. At this point switch of the flame, add the dry roasted mixture carefully and mix well.
1. Take the pan away from the heat and keep it on the kitchen slab.
1. You can wait for few minutes or when it is hot only, apply some ghee on to your palm and start making small balls and keep it on a plate to hold its shape.
1. Serve the Til Ke Laddu Recipe (Sesame Seeds Laddu Recipe) as a Party Dessert for any festival occasion.
