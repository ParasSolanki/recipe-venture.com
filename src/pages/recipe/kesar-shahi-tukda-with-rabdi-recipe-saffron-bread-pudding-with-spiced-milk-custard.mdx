### Ingredients

- 1/3 cup Water.
- Saffron strands - few.
- 1/3 cup Sugar.
- 6 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 1/4 cup Ghee - (for shallow frying).
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1 cup Rabri.
- Mixed nuts - Chopped (almonds.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kesar Shahi Tukda With Rabdi Recipe, we will first prepare the sugar syrup for the Shahi Tukda and Rabri.
1. Prepare the Rabri according to the Rabri recipe and keep aside.
1. Heat water and sugar in pan, add the saffron strands and allow the water to come to a brisk boil.
1. Turn the heat to low and simmer the sugar water until you get a one string consistency.
1. Turn off the heat and keep the sugar syrup aside.
1. The next step is to prepare the bread slices and roast them in ghee for the shahi tukda recipe.
1. Cut the edges of the bread and slice them into a triangle.
1. Heat ghee in a skillet and shallow try the bread pieces until they are crisp and golden brown in color on both sides.
1. Once the bread slices are fried, soak each slice in the sugar syrup for about a minute and place them on a serving platter.
1. Pour the prepared Rabri over the bread slices and garnish with the chopped nuts and serve.
1. You can serve the Kesar Shahi Tukda With Rabdi either chilled or warm based on the season and mood.
