### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4 cup Buttermilk.
- pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 cup Barnyard Millet - washed and soaked for 30 minutes.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Maheri, heat ghee or oil in a pressure cooker.
1. Once the ghee is hot add hing, cumin seeds and allow the seeds to crackle.
1. Once they starts crackling, add turmeric powder.
1. Add 2 cups of water and salt and bring it to boil.
1. Once the water starts boiling add buttermilk and bring it to boil again.
1. Now add the barnyard millet and mix well.
1. Cover the pressure cooker and turn the heat to low.
1. Pressure cook for 2 whistles.
1.  Let the pressure release naturally and then open the pressure cooker.
1. Transfer the Maheri to a serving dish and serve.
1. Serve Maheri as a healthy breakfast with pickle or Green Chutney for a comforting breakfast.
