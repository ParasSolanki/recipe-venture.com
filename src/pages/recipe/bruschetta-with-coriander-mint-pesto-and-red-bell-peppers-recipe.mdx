### Ingredients

- 8 Whole Wheat Brown Bread - sliced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - cut in slices.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 8-10 Badam (Almond).
- 2-3 cloves Garlic - peeled.
- 4 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 Onion - sliced.
- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese - grated.
- 1/2 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Bruschetta with Coriander Mint Pesto And Red Bell Peppers, toast the whole wheat bread slices in a toaster, cut into half and keep it aside.
1. In a mixer, grind coriander, mint, almonds green chilli, garlic and olive oil.
1. Make a chunky paste and keep them aside.
1. Heat oil in a small pan, add the sliced onions and sweat it for two minutes.
1. Add the sliced red bell peppers and sauté for two more minutes.
1. Add the seasoning, oregano and red chilli flakes, sauté and switch off the flame.
1. To arrange the bruschetta, take individual pieces of the toasted bread slice, apply a layer of chunky coriander mint pesto and top it with a spoon of onion red bell pepper mix.
1. Serve Bruschetta With Coriander Mint Pesto And Red Bell Peppers Recipe along with Baked Farfalle Pasta with Mushrooms,Peas and Lemon White Sauce and Vanilla Panna Cotta Recipe With Strawberry Coulis for an interesting weekend brunch.
