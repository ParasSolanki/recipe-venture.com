### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Milk.
- 1/4 Baking powder.
- Ghee - as required.
- 1 tablespoon Cocoa Powder.
- 2 tablespoons Palm sugar.
- Salt - a pinch.
- 2 tablespoons Chocolate chips.
- 3/4 teaspoon Apple cider vinegar.
- 2 tablespoons Ragi Flour (Finger Millet/ Nagli).
- 1 tablespoon Whole Wheat Flour.
- 3 tablespoons Coffee Decoction - (3rd degree).
- 1/4 teaspoon Baking soda.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Ragi Choco Coffee Mug Cake Recipe, prepare the filter coffee in the regular way by adding 5 scoops of filter coffee and less than 1/2 cup boiling water.
1. Once the decoction is brewed, use them as first degree coffee.
1. Repeat the same process to get the second & third degree decoction.
1. We will be using the third degree decoction.
1. Take 3 tablespoons of decoction and mix it with 1 tablespoon milk.
1. Keep it asideAdd ragi flour, wheat flour, palm sugar in a bowl and mix them well.
1. Add ghee to this and mix well again.
1. Add baking soda, baking powder and apple cider vinegar.
1. Just mix them for a second.
1. Add the coffee mix and mix well.
1. Add the cocoa powder.
1. This can be added along with the dry ingredients also.
1. Mix all of them without any lumps.
1. Add 1 tablespoon of choco chips and give it a mix.
1. Transfer them to a claypot mug or regular microwave mug.
1. Sprinkle the remaining choco chips.
1. Microwave high for a minute.
1. Make sure the cup is only filled with 3/4th of the mix else they would spill.
1. Enjoy it as it is or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
1. Serve Ragi Choco Coffee Mug Cake Recipe along with a hot cup of Coffee Latte during your Tea time.
1. You can also serve it as a dessert after your Continental meals.
