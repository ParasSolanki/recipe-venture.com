### Ingredients

- 3 cups Water - (chilled preferably).
- 6 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1/2 cup Jaggery - powdered.
- Tulsi (holy basil) - to garnish.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1/2 Lemon - squeezed.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds - pounded.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Traditional Panakam Recipe, take water in a bowl, add powdered jaggery to the water and mix until it dissolves.
1. Add washed ginger, pepper seeds and cardamom to a mortar.
1. Crush everything coarsely using a pestle and add it to the jaggery water.
1. Squeeze juice of half lemon to the jaggery water.
1. Stir well and filter using a strainer.
1. Add basil leaves and serve the Traditional Panakam Recipe chilled.
1. Serve Traditional Panakam Recipe during hot afternoons or during tea time.
