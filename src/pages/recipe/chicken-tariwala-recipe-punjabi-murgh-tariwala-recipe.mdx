### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Onions - roughly chopped.
- 1/2 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 1 inch Ginger - roughly chopped.
- 1/2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini) - Â½ inch cinnamon.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Green Chillies - roughly chopped.
- 1 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 2 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 5 cloves Garlic.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli Paste.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 Star anise.
- 300 grams Chicken - small curry cut pieces.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken Tariwala Recipe, thoroughly wash and clean the chicken pieces.
1. *To marinate the chickenIn a mixing bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, red chili powder, salt and turmeric powder.
1. Add the chicken pieces and mix well.
1. Set aside to marinate for 30 minutes.
1. *To make the gravy for the Chicken TariwalaIn a mixer-jar combine the onions, ginger, garlic and green chilli, grind to a smooth paste along with some water.
1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
1. Heat oil in a pressure cooker on low flame, add the whole spices - bay leaf,  clove, cinnamon, elaichi, and star anise and allow them to sizzle.
1. To this add the onion paste, and allow this to cook well stirring continuously.
1. This will take a good 10-12 minutes.
1. You will see the onion paste get thicker, due to the evaporation of moisture.
1. Next add in the tomato puree and continue to cook till the mixture comes together, making sure the tomato is well cooked.
1. At this stage, add the spice powders- garam masala, cumin powder, red chili powder, coriander powder and salt.
1. Now add in the marinated chicken along with the marinade and mix well.
1. Add 1/2 - 3.
1. 4 cup of water into the pressure cooker, mix well and close the pressure cooker.
1. Pressure cook the Chicken Tariwala for 2 whistles and turn off the flame.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Once released, check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Transfer the Chicken Tariwala to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Chicken Tariwala on a lovely summer afternoon along with Jeera Rice, Pickled Onions, a wedge of lemon, green chilli and Sweet & Spicy Coriander Tadka Raita- finger licking good meal.
