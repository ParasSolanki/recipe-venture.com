### Ingredients

- 30 Grams Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 50 Grams Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 10 Grams Cloves (Laung).
- 10 Grams Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 30 Grams Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 50 Grams Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 Nutmeg - whole nutmeg.
- 10 Grams Dry ginger powder.
- 10 Grams Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 20 Grams Fennel seeds (Saunf).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Punjabi Garam Masala Powder Recipe, we will first get all the ingredients together and ready.
1. Preheat a skillet on medium heat and roast all the ingredients together except the ginger powder, on low to medium heat until you can smell the aromas coming through.
1. Make sure you keep stirring continuously, so the spices don't get burnt.
1. Once the spices are roasted well (about 4 to 5 minutes), turn off the heat and allow it to cool.
1. Once the spices are cooled down, using a Food Grinder, make a fine powder of the spices.
1. The Punjabi Garam Masala Powder is now ready.
1. Store the Garam Masala in an airtight glass jar and use as and when required.
1. For longer shelf like, you can freeze the bottle and use it as desired.
