### Ingredients

- 1 cup chana dal.
-  1/2 cup coconut - cut into small pieces.
-  1 tsp turmeric powder.
-  1 inch ginger - peel and tighten.
-  1 tbsp ghee.
-  1 bay leaf.
-  1 tsp sugar.
-  3 dry red chillies.
-  1 inch cinnamon.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  2 long.
-  2 green chilies - cut straight and thin.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.

### Instructions

1. To make Bengali style Cholar dal recipe, first soak the dal and keep it aside for 1 hour.
1. Pour this lentil in a pressure cooker with 2-1 / 2 cups of water and cook it till 2 years come.
1. Heat ghee in a pan.
1. Add coconut to it and cook till it turns golden.
1. Remove and keep the coconut aside.
1. After the dish is cooked, put it in a saucepan and turn on the gas.
1. Add turmeric powder, salt, sugar and cook for 5 to 6 minutes.
1. Now heat ghee in a tempering pan for tempering.
1. Add cumin seeds, dry red mirch, long, bay leaves, green chillies, ginger and cook for 15 seconds.
1. Turn off the gas.
1. Add this tempering to the dal with coconut and mix well.
1. Cook for 2 more minutes and turn off the gas.
1. Serve Bengali Style Cholar Dal Recipe with Bengali Lachhi and Began Bhaja for dinner.
