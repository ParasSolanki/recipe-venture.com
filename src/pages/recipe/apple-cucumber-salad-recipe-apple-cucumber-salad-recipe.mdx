### Ingredients

- 1 apple - chopped.
-  3 sprigs mint - chopped.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  crush 1/2 tsp whole pepper - 1 tsp honey.
-  1 tsp lemon juice.
-  1 cucumber - cut round round.

### Instructions

1. To make Apple Kukumbar Salad Recipe, add apple, honey, lemon juice, black pepper, salt and mix in a mixing bowl.
1. Now add mint leaves and mix it again.
1. Serve.
1. Serve the Apple Cucumber Salad Recipe with Lemon Coriander Soup and Broccoli Cheese Mayo Sandwich.
