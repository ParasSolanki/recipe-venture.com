### Ingredients

- 600 grams Potatoes (Aloo) - partly boiled.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 5 sprig Fresh Thyme leaves.
- 2 tablespoons Butter.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Roasted Coriander Potatoes With Thyme Recipe, par boil the potatoes in a pressure cooker for one whistle and let the pressure release.
1. Peel the skin and cut them in medium slices and keep aside.
1. Heat a wide heavy bottomed pan on low heat and add butter.
1. When it melts, place the sliced potatoes and cook it until it is roasted.
1. It will take 6 to 7 minutes.
1. Likewise, flip and cook on the other side and keep aside.
1. In the same pan add the coarsely crushed whole coriander seeds, fresh thyme sprigs and saut√© till aromatic.
1. To this add the already roasted potatoes, season with salt and pepper and give it a gentle toss.
1. Serve the Roasted Coriander Potatoes with Thyme along with Burnt Garlic Pasta With Mushroom for a weeknight dinner or serve it as a salad for lunch.
