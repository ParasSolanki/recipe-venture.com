### Ingredients

- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 2 tablespoons Milk Powder - optional.
- 1/2 cup Chiroti Rava - super fine.
- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 3-4 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds - powdered.
- 1 Mango (Ripe) - big or 2 medium sized.
- Mixed nuts - chopped.
- 3/4 cup Sugar - granulated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- pinch Salt.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2-3 tablespoons Hot water.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Eggless Mango Cake Recipe, prep up with all the ingredients first.
1. Chop nuts and de seed mango and chop its pulp, discarding the peel.
1. Preheat the oven at 190 degree Celsius for 10 minutes.
1. Prepare the baking pan by greasing with a little oil or butter and dust 1-2 teaspoon of maida/all-purpose flour.
1. Mix the dry ingredients-maida, chiroti rava, milk powder (if using), baking soda, baking powder and salt well in a mixing bowl.
1. Keep aside.
1. In a Mixer, puree mango along with sugar and cardamom.
1. No extra water is required at this stage.
1. Later mix all the wet ingredients in another mixing bowl-mango and sugar puree, oil, and curd.
1. Give a nice stir to mix them evenly.
1. Slowly mix the dry and wet ingredient mixtures now by combining well to form a thick cake batter of dropping consistency.
1. Add hot water about 2 tablespoons (not any more than 1/4 cup water is required).
1. Add chopped nuts and mix once.
1. Reserve above 1 tablespoon of nuts for topping.
1. Transfer the batter to the prepared cake pan.
1. Top the cake batter with remaining chopped nuts.
1. Bake in the oven at the same temperature for 30-40 minutes.
1. Check after 30 minutes.
1. The top should have turned to a golden brown in color and if you check with a toothpick in the center of the cake it should come out clean when the cake is done.
1. My cake took 38 minutes.
1. Cool the eggless mango cake recipe for 15-20 minutes on a wire rack, unmold and slice them.
1. Serve the Eggless mango Cake is ready to be relished with a scoop of Mango ice cream or vanilla ice cream with chopped mango slices to go with it perfectly.
