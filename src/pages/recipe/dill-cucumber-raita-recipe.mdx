### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/4 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Dill leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1-1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Cucumber - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dill Cucumber Raita Recipe, whisk the yogurt until smooth in a mixing bowl.
1. To the yogurt, add water, salt, cumin powder, pepper powder, chaat masala, cucumber, mint leaves and dill leaves .
1. Mix the Dill Cucumber Raita well and serve chilled.
1.  Serve Dill Cucumber Raita Recipe along with Ragi Tawa Paratha - Healthy Vitamin Rich & Diabetic Friendly Paratha and Spinach Paneer Kofta Curry Recipe or Aromatic Vegetable Pulao Recipe for a simple weekday lunch.
