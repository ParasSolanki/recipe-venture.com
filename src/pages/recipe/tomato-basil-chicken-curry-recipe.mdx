### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 Carrot (Gajjar) - cut diagonally.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 2 tablespoon Paprika powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Dried Thyme Leaves.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 6 Basil leaves.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - for cooking.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 Chicken breasts - cut into chunks.
- 1 tablespoon Rosemary.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tomato Basil Chicken Curry Recipe, heat olive oil over medium heat in a saucepan.
1. Add chopped garlic and onions and saute them as well till the onions turn translucent.
1. Once done add the chopped carrot, sprinkle some salt and toss well.
1. This will help to cook the vegetable faster.
1. Once the carrot is half cooked, add in the chicken pieces and give it a mix.
1. Saute for about a couple of minutes just to sear the meat.
1. Do not cook for a longer time as the chicken will dry out soon.
1. Add tomato puree, dried seasonings like dried rosemary, thyme, cumin powder and paprika powder and mix well to combine.
1. Once the seasonings have mixed well, add salt to taste and a teaspoon of sugar to cut the sharp tanginess.
1. Add 1 cup of water and give it a stir and bring to a brisk boil.
1. Turn down the heat and let the Tomato Basil Chicken Curry stew for 15 minutes until the chicken and carrots are done.
1. Check for seasonings in the Tomato Basil Chicken Curry, if your require anything more, you can add them now and adjust the taste.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and transfer the Tomato Basil Chicken Curry to a serving bowl and serve hot.
