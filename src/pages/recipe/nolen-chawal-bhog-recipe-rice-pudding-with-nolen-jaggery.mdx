### Ingredients

- Glazed Cherries - a few.
- 1 cup Nolen Gur.
- 1 liter Milk - Full cream milk  - 1 litter.
- Slivered Almonds (Badam) - a few.
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 100 grams Basmati rice - or any fragrant rice.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Nolen Chawal Bhog Recipe (Rice Pudding With Nolen Jaggery), wash and drain the rice.
1. Keep all the other ingredients handy.
1. Boil milk in a heavy bottomed saucepan.
1. Once milk starts boiling, simmer it and keep stirring occasionally until it is reduced to half its original quantity.
1. Add rice into the simmering milk and allow it to cook on low flame for 10 - 15 minutes or until the rice is almost cooked.
1. Keep stirring the milk continuously otherwise rice tends to stick to the bottom.
1. When the rice is almost done, simmer the flame and add jaggery slowly.
1. Keep stirring constantly.
1. Simmer the rice pudding for another 5 minutes or until Nolen Chawal Bhog Recipe thickens a bit.
1. Then add a pinch of salt to enhance taste and has uniform colour.
1. Switch off the flame and allow it to come room temperature.
1. Transfer to serving bowl and garnish with chopped almonds/slivered almonds and glazed cherries.
1. Serve Nolen Chawal Bhog Recipe (Rice Pudding With Nolen Jaggery) as a dessert to end a festive meal with more relevant festive recipes.
