### Ingredients

- 1 Kiwi - peeled.
- Salt and Pepper - (optional).
- 1/4 cup Green seedless Grapes.
- Chaat Masala Powder - (optional).
- 1 Apple - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Red grapes.
- 2 Strawberries - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Orange - peeled.
- 1 Ripe Bananas - peeled.
- 2 tablespoon Fresh Pomegranate Fruit Kernels.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Fresh Fruit Bowl Recipe, prep with all the cut fruits first.
1. Try to use seasonal fruits so that they pack in the nutrition demanded of a particular season.
1. Once all the fruits are peeled and chopped (try to use the peel of fruits which have an edible peel, like apples or chikku), take a large mixing bowl and put all the fruits together and toss lightly, with spices if you are using any.
1. Serve Fresh Fruit Bowl Recipe in individual bowls for a mid morning snack or along with Home Made Breakfast Granola Recipe and yogurt or milk as a complete breakfast.
1. You can also accompany it with a glass of Fresh Orange Juice Recipe and a Boiled Egg.
