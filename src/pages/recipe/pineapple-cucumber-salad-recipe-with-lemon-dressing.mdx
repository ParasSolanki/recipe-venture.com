### Ingredients

- 1/2 Lemon - juiced.
- 3 Cucumbers - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder - (adjust to taste).
- 1/4 teaspoon Lemon zest.
- 2 cups Pineapple - chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Pineapple Cucumber Salad Recipe With Lemon Dressing, prep with all ingredients required, chop the veggies, greens and fruit.
1. In a large mixing bowl, mix all the chopped pineapple and cucumbers and add in the coriander leaves and lemon dressing with salt and pepper.
1. Add lemon zest for a zing of taste and mix everything well.
1. Serve immediately.
1. Serve this tantalising simple Pineapple Cucumber Salad Recipe With Lemon Dressing with Mexican Vegetarian Bean & Cheese Enchiladas Recipe or Mexican Salsa Chicken With Italian Cream Cheese Recipe.
