### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Potato (Aloo) - cut into small piece.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 3 Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish) - cut into small piece.
- 3 tablespoons Kasundi mustard sauce.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard oil - adjustable.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bengali Mooli Aloo Ki Sabzi recipe, heat mustard oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add the mustard seeds, cumin seeds, nigella seeds and saute them for about 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add green chilli and onion.
1. Cook till the onion becomes soft and translucent.
1. Once the onions become translucent, add the potatoes in the pan.
1. Stir them for a while on a medium heat.
1. Then put the radish into the pan.
1. Fry them for 4-5 minutes on a medium flame.
1. Next add in all the spice powders, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, sugar and salt to taste.
1. Mix well to combine.
1. Add little water to the vegetables and cover them with a lid.
1. Allow them cook for few minutes on a low flame and keep stirring in between.
1. When the veggies are cooked, add the the mustard paste and mix it with the vegetables properly.
1. You can make the curry semi liquid or dry.
1. Cook for 2 more minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer Bengali Mooli Aloo Ki Sabzi into a serving bowl.
1. Serve Bengali Mooli Aloo Ki Sabzi along with Bengali Style Lau Die Tetor Dal and Steamed Rice for a weekday lunch.
