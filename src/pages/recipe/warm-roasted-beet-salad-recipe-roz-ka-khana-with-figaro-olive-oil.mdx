### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Pickled Jalapenos - sliced.
- 2 tablespoon Red Wine Vinaigrette.
- 2 Beetroots - sliced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cloves Garlic - minced.
- Basil leaves - a few sprigs roughly torn.
- 1/4 cup Feta Cheese - crumbled.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 3 tablespoons Walnuts - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 1 Onion - cut into rings.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Warm Roasted Beet Salad Recipe, first peel and slice the beets.
1. Heat the Extra Virgin Olive oil in a heavy skillet, add the beets and sprinkle some salt.
1. Cover and allow it to steam cook until just about done.
1. Once the beets are cooked through, add the chopped garlic, feta cheese, red wine vinegar, honey, jalapenos and onion rings.
1. Toss for a few minutes, turn off the heat and serve warm.
