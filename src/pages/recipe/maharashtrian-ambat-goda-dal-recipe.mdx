### Ingredients

- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 3/4 tablespoon Maharashtrian Goda Masala.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 3/4 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 1/2 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1 tablespoon Jaggery.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 2 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Ambat Goda Dal we will soak the dal in water for about 30 minutes.
1. After soaking cook the dal with the water, salt and turmeric powder and pressure cook for about 4 whistle till it is well cooked.
1. Once done, keep it aside to release the pressure by itself.
1. Meanwhile you can heat a kadai with oil and keep it ready.
1. Add the mustard seeds, cumin seeds and fenugreek seeds and allow them to splutter for few seconds.
1. Once done add the curry leaves and allow it to crackle.
1. Add a pinch oh hing and chopped green chilli and saute till it softens for about 10 seconds.
1. Add the cooked dal and tamarind water together and allow it to cook in simmer.
1. At this point you can add the goda masala and salt.
1. Cook for another 10 more minutes till you get the desired consistency.
1. Garnish it with coriander leaves and serve.
1. Serve the Ambat Goda Dal with steamed rice and a dollop of ghee over it with a Kadai Vegetable Sabzi Recipe along side and a homemade pickle to go along with it for a comforting meal.
