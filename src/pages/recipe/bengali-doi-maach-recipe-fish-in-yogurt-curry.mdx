### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1/2 inch Ginger.
- 2 tablespoons Raisins.
- 1 cups Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 3 tablespoons Mustard oil.
- 5 Cloves (Laung).
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 400 grams Rohu fish - sliced.
- 1 tablespoons Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - sliced.
- 6 cloves Garlic.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bengali Doi Maach recipe, heat 1 tablespoon oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add garlic, ginger and onion and saute it for a minute.
1. Let it cool down and then make a paste of this using a mixer grinder.
1. Marinate fish with raisin, turmeric, red chilli powder, salt and 1 tablespoon oil for 15 to 30 minutes in a mixing bowl.
1. Mix everything properly.
1. Add 1 tablespoon oil to the same pan that is used for frying onion and heat it.
1. Add cumin seeds and let it sputter.
1. Add cinnamon, clove, green cardamom and saute for a few seconds.
1. Reduce the flame to very slow.
1. Add the marinated fish with curd to the pan.
1. Cook for about 10 minute at medium flame.
1. After 10 minutes, add green chillies, 1/2 teaspoon sugar and mix.
1. Cook for another 4 to 5 minutes at slow flame.
1. After 4 to 5 minutes, switch off the stove and it is ready to be served.
1. Serve Bengali Doi Maach along with Chingiri Paturi and hot steamed rice for a perfect Bengali weekday lunch or dinner.
