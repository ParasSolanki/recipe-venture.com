### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Paprika powder - (or red chilli powder).
- 2 tablespoon Fresh Herbs - chopped (or dried).
- 4 Sweet Potatoes - peeled and cut into 3 inch wedges.
- 5 cloves Garlic - grated or finely minced.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Corn flour.
- Rock Salt - to taste.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Oven baked Sweet Potato fries, first preheat the oven to 230 C.
1. Prepare 2 baking sheets greased with oil or butter.
1. In a small mixing bowl, mix together the oil, garlic, paprika, salt and herbsPlace the sweet potato sticks in a large bowl and drizzle the oil and spice mixture over it.
1. Use your fingers and mix to coat all the sweet potato sticks evenly.
1. Sprinkle the cornstarch over the sweet potatoes and mix again.
1. Place the spice coated sweet potato sticks on the baking sheet and bake for about 20-25 minutes till well browned and crisp.
1. Halfway through the baking, stir the fries to ensure even baking.
1. Once done, remove from the oven and transfer to a serving platter.
1. Serve the Oven Baked Sweet Potato Fries along with a Spicy Mexican Salsa or Cottage Cheese Dip or Peanut Chilli Dipping Sauce.
