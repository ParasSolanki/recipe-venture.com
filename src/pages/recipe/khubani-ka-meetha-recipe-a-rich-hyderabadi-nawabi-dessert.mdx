### Ingredients

- Saffron strands - a pinch.
- 250 grams Apricots.
- Mixed nuts - almonds.
- 1/4 cup Sugar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Khubani Ka Meetha Recipe, first soak the apricots and saffron overnight in water.
1. Next day, in a saucepan, add all the soaked apricots and the golden soaking water and place it on the heat.
1. Cooking the apricots on a low heat, and keep the pan covered.
1. Continue to cook them till they can be mashed with a spoon.
1. Then, add sugar, keeping in mind that the apricots are already sweet.
1. Add only as much sugar as required.
1. Cook further till the mixture comes together.
1. It should be syrupy in consistency.
1. Now, chill it in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours.
1. Garnish the Khubani Ka Meetha with almond and pistachio flakes and dried rose petals before serving along with Vanilla Custard or with whipped cream or ice cream as a delicious party dessert.
