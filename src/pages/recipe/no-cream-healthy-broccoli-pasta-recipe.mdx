### Ingredients

- 225 grams Spirali pasta.
- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- Red Chilli flakes - to taste.
- 3/4 cup Vegetable stock.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese - (optional).
- 4 cups Broccoli - cut into florets.

### Instructions

1. To Begin making this No Cream Healthy Broccoli Pasta Recipe, cut the broccoli into small florets and wash them under running water to make it dirt free.
1. Boil pasta as per the instruction on the packet.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Drain water and keep the florets aside.
1. Also finely chop some garlic or you can even mince it.
1. Heat a pan and add some olive oil.
1. Add chopped garlic and Red chilli Flakes to the oil.
1. Let the oil get infused with garlic and red chili flakes while it heats up.
1. Add broccoli florets and give it a toss in the oil.
1. Add your vegetable stock at this time.
1. Season with salt and let it cook covered on low-medium heat till broccoli is partially cooked.
1. You will be able to cut the broccoli with a spatula.
1. Add boiled pasta to the broccoli and combine.
1. Season it with Ground Black pepper.
1. Add a little butter to make it smoother.
1. Top it up with Parmesan Cheese.
1. Serve No Cream Healthy Broccoli Pasta along with Pizza Margherita for a weekend night dinner.
