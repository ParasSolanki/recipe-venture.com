### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit (adjust taste).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1/2 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1/2-1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- Salt - to taste.
- 3 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled and mashed.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing) - +a pinch.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- Sunflower Oil - as needed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Potato Sagu Recipe for Rava Idli, get prepped up with all the ingredients.
1. In a kadai, heat some oil and add mustard seeds and let the seeds splutter.
1. Once they splutter, add chana dal and urad dal and saute till they change slightly brown in colour.
1. Once done add curry leaves, a pinch of asafoetida, green chilies, and grated ginger and saut√© it for a few seconds.
1. After few seconds add onions, turmeric powder and a pinch of salt and cook until the onions are cooked but not browned.
1. Once the onions are cooked add the mashed potatoes, remaining asafoetida and turmeric powder, coriander powder, chili powder, salt and sugar and stir well to combine.
1. Add a cup of water and cook until you see a gravy consistency, continue to cook for 5-6 minutes on a low heat so that the potatoes absorb all the flavor.
1. Once the potato sagu has reached the desired consistency add chopped coriander leaves and lemon juice.
1. Stir to combine and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer the potato sagu into a serving bowl and serve.
1. Serve Potato Sagu Recipe for Rava Idli with Broccoli Rava Idli and Green Coriander Coconut Chutney for a wholesome breakfast.
