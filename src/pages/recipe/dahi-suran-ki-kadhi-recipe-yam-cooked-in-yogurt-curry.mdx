### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 300 grams Elephant yam (Suran/Senai/Ratalu) - peeled and diced.
- 1 tablespoon Gram flour (besan).
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Dahi Suran Ki Kadhi Recipe (Yam Cooked In Yogurt Curry Recipe) by cooking the diced yam in the pressure cooker with 1/2 cup water and a little salt, pressure cook it for 4 whistles till it is soft and firm.
1. Let the pressure release naturally by itself.
1. The next step is to make the Kadhi so for that add curd in a saucepan and then add 2 cups water and besan flour.
1. Keep mixing it on a low heat and bring it to a boil.
1. Add some salt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander seeds, stir well and let it cook in low heat for few more minutes.
1. Later add in the boiled yam to the kadhi.
1. Once that is done, it is time to make the temper for the kadhi.
1. Heat a small shallow pan with oil and then add cumin seeds, cinnamon, bay leaf , fenugreek seeds and fresh ground peppercorns.
1. Let the spices get roasted little and sprinkle the tempered spices on to the kadhi mixture.
1. Serve the Dahi Suran Ki Sabzi Recipe (Yam Cooked In Yogurt Curry Recipe) with Bhindi Masala, Jeera Rice and Phulka for an everyday meal.
