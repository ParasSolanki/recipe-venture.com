### Ingredients

-  1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida.
-  1 / 2 teaspoons Cumin seeds.
-  1/4 cup peanuts - soaked in hot water for 30 minutes.
-  2 Green Chili - cut.
-  2 teaspoons Jaggery - Tighten.
-  2 tablespoons chana dal - soaked in hot water for 30 minutes.
-  7 Dry Coconut - Chop finely.
- 50 gm arabic leaves - washed and chopped.
-  1/4 teaspoon Turmeric Powder.
-  1/2 teaspoon Rye.
-  1 teaspoon oil.
-  1 teaspoon Amchur.
-  salt - to taste.

### Instructions

1. To make Aluchi Bhaji, first prepare the saree material and keep it aside.
1. Put the chana dal, peanuts in 1/4 cup of water and salt in the pressure cooker and cook till 3 to 4 cities come.
1. Turn off the gas and let the pressure drain.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
1. After 10 seconds, add asafetida, turmeric powder, green chillies, Arabic leaves, mango powder, jaggery, salt, coconut and mix.
1. Cook until the arbi leaves become soft.
1. After that add chana dal and peanuts and let it boil.
1. Turn off the gas after cooking and serve hot.
1. Serve Alochi Bhaji with Khandshi dal and Bhakri for dinner.
