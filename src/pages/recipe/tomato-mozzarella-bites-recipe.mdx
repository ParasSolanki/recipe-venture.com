### Ingredients

- 20 Cherry tomatoes.
- 20 Toothpicks.
- Salt - to taste.
- Black pepper powder.
- 20 Basil leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Balsamic Vinegar.
- 20 Mozzarella cheese - cut into small pieces.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Rosemary - for garnish.

### Instructions

1. To make these easy Tomato Mozzarella Bites recipe, wash tomatoes and basil thoroughly.
1. Now place a tomato, basil leaf and mozzarella piece each on a toothpick.
1. Place on serving plate and whisk together with olive oil and vinegar.
1. Now sprinkle some salt, black pepper powder and rosemary.
1. Now this delicious Tomato Mozzarella bites is ready to serve.
1. Bite everything off the toothpick all at once for a nice flavorful starter.
1. Serve the Tomato Mozzarella Bites Recipe as a Starter along with Minestrone Soup Recipe and Roasted Eggplant & Olive Lasagna to make it a meal for your sunday lunch.
