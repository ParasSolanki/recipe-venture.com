### Ingredients

- 1/2 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 teaspoon Green Chilli Paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 tablespoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds) - soaked overnight.
- 1-1/2 cups Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - (Sour).
- 1 cup Boondi.
- 5 Cloves (Laung).
- 2 tablespoons Ghee.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 5 Whole Black Peppercorns - pounded.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 4 tablespoons Gram flour (besan).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Punjabi Style Boondi Kadhi Recipe, firstly combine yogurt, gram flour, red chilli powder, turmeric powder and salt in a bowl.
1. Add 500 ml water and whisk well.
1. Make sure that no lumps are formed.
1. Heat ghee in a deep non-stick pan, add cloves, peppercorns, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds and sauté for a minute.
1. After a minute, add asafoetida, chopped ginger and red chillies, mix and sauté for about a minute.
1. Now, add methi seeds (soaked in the night) and stir for few mintues.
1. Now, add the gram flour and yogurt mixture and stir continouly until the bubbles form in the kadai.
1. As the mixture gets thick while boiling, add 500 ml water and keep the flame low and allow to cook for 15 to 20 minutes.
1. After 15 to 20 minutes, switch off the gas, add boondi and and garnish with coriander leaves.
1. Make this easy and nutritional Punjabi Style Boondi Kadhi Recipe and pair it along with Bajri-Til Bhakri Recipe, a piece of gur and a dollop of ghee smeared on roti.
1. You can also serve Kachumber Salad on the side.
