### Ingredients

- Salt - if required.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 Tablespoon Tomato Ketchup.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried Thyme Leaves - optional.
- Parmesan cheese - for garnishing (optional).
- 30 Mussels - big ones.
- 4 cloves Garlic - minced or grated.
- 1 Tablespoon Sunflower Oil - for sauting.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Stuffed Mussels recipe, open the mussels and remove the meat out.
1. Wash and keep around 15-20 shells to stuff later.
1. In a medium wok, kept on medium heat, add oil and saute garlic and onions till onions become translucent.
1. Add green chilies, thyme, red chili powder and fry for few more seconds.
1. Add mussel meat, cover and cook on medium heat for 7-8 minutes.
1. Sprinkle turmeric powder and mix.
1. Add ketchup, mix and remove from heat.
1. Take a teaspoon of mixture and scoop into each of the mussel shells.
1. Place all the stuffed shells on a baking pan.
1. Sprinkle some Parmesan cheese on each of the stuffed shells and broil for about 4 minutes at 350 degree Fahrenheit.
1. Remove out and serve immediately.
