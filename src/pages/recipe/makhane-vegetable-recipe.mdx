### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 + 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Fresh cream.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 tablespoon Pumpkin seeds.
- Sunflower Oil - to cook.
- 6 Cashew nuts.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 2 cups Phool Makhana (Lotus Seeds).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 tablespoon Poppy seeds.

### Instructions

1. To start making Makhana ki Sabzi Recipe, get all the ingredients handy.
1. Pressure cook 2 onions, cashews, magaj and poppy seeds along with enough water.
1. Blend it into a smooth paste in a mixer grinder and set aside.
1. In a pan heat the ghee and add Makhana to it.
1. On a medium flame roast the Makhana till they crisp up completely.
1. When you eat them it should be crunchy till the centre and should not be chewy.
1. Set aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan.
1. Add bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom and sauce it till they give out a nice aroma.
1. Add green chilli and 1 chopped onion and saute till the onion turns light goldAdd fresh green peas and saute for a minute.
1. Add the ground paste, red chilli flakes and saute for a minute.
1. Add 2 cups of water, grated ginger and let it boil for a couple of minutes.
1. Add roasted Makhana to this and let it soak up the liquid and get soft.
1. You will see that the Makhana shrinks in size.
1. Turn off heat.
1. Serve Makhana ki Sabzi with hot with roti, whole wheat naan, butter naan or phulkas to make a filling lunch or dinner.
