### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Caster Sugar.
- 1 Pastry dough.
- 1 tablespoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 3 tablespoons Jam - Mixed Fruit.
- 1 tablespoon Caster Sugar.
- 2 Apples - chopped in cubes.
- 1/2 cup Badam (Almond) - roughly chopped.
- 1/2 cup Raisins.
- 1/2 cup Butter - melted.
- 2 tablespoons Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Eggless Apple Strudel Recipe With Homemade Puff Pastry Sheet, first prepare the pastry dough and refrigerate for about an hour.
1. Once chilled, roll it out very thin and cut a big square sheet from it.
1. Keep aside.
1. The next step is to prepare the stuffing for the Eggless Apple Strudel Recipe.
1. In a bowl, add dry fruits along with bread crumbs, caster sugar, cinnamon, chopped apples, raisins and mix it well.
1. Apply the mix fruit jam on center of the sheet and place the chopped apples on it.
1. Apply the melted butter on one side of sheet and fold it inside over the apple stuffing.
1. Fold other side as well and press gently.
1. It should form an envelope.
1. Press gently top and bottom part as well to seal the strudel.
1. Apply the butter on over the top and around and make the vertical cuts on it using a knife.
1. Place the Eggless Apple Strudel on a greased baking sheet.
1. Preheat the microwave at 190 C and bake this apple strudel for 40 minutes until golden brown and crisp on all sides.
1. Once baked, cut the Eggless Apple Strudel and serve warm or chilled.
1. Serve the Eggless Apple Strudel after a light salad like Sweet Potato & Green Bean Salad Recipe or Grilled Winter Vegetable Salad Recipe  as it is or along with a vanilla ice cream for dessert.
