### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 1 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 8 to 10 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 2 teaspoons Dried oregano.
- Mozzarella cheese - .
- 250 grams Chicken - minced.
- 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Onion powder.
- 3/4 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- 2 Whole Eggs - beaten.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Garlic Chicken Cheese Balls Recipe, prep all the ingredients mentioned.
1. In a wide bowl, add minced chicken, onion powder, minced garlic, finely chopped ginger pieces, pepper powder, oregano, salt to taste and mix well.
1. In a frying pan, heat oil for deep frying.
1. Make lemon sized balls of the chicken mix.
1. Place a small cube of mozzarella cheese in the center and roll well into a neat balls.
1. Coat the chicken balls with all-purpose flour first, then dip it in beaten eggs and finally coat bread crumbs.
1. Repeat this step to make all the chicken and cheese balls.
1. Once the oil is hot, reduce heat to medium.
1. Drop the rolled balls 2 to 3 at a time into the oil and deep fry until golden brown.
1. Remove from oil and drain them on an oil absorbent paper to absorb excess oil.
1. Serve Garlic Chicken Cheese Balls Recipe immediately with any dipping sauce of your choice, like Roasted Tomato Sauce or Peanut Chilli Dipping Sauce.
