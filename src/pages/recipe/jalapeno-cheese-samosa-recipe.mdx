### Ingredients

- 2 cups All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- Water - as required.
- 200 grams Britannia Cheezza - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- 2 tablespoons Ghee.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1/4 cup Pickled Jalapenos - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Britannia Cheese Spread - Four Pepper Tango - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Jalapeno Cheese Samosa Recipe, first we will make the samosa crust.
1. To make the Samosa crustInto a mixing bowl add the flour, ghee, ajwain and salt to taste.
1. Mix all the ingredients till you get a crumb like texture.
1. Add more ghee if required.
1. The texture should be such that when you holds together the dough should form into a ball and when left breaks apart.
1. Once you achieve this texture add little water at a time and knead to make a smooth dough.
1. Divide the dough into small portions.
1. Cover and keep aside.
1. For the Samosa fillingInto a mixing bowl add the grated cheese, britannia Four Pepper Cheese Spreadz, Jalapenos and coriander leaves.
1. Mixx well to combine.
1. Keep aside.
1. To make the samosa.
1. Dust a surface with flour and roll each portion of the dough into 6 inch diameter circle.
1. Cut the circle into half.
1. Take a semicircle and spread water lightly all along the edges of the semicircle.
1. Fold the semi circle into a cone shape and fill the cone with two tablespoons of the cheese filling.
1. Seal the cone to make a triangle shape samosa.
1. Slide the cheese samosa into the preheated oil and deep fry on medium heat until golden brown and crisp.
1. Once done transfer the Jalapeno Cheese Samosa Recipe into a plate with an oil absorbent paper and serve hot.
1. Serve Jalapeno Cheese Samosa Recipe as an evening snack or as a party appetizer with Green Chutney Recipe - Coriander Mint Chutney Recipe and tomato ketchup.
