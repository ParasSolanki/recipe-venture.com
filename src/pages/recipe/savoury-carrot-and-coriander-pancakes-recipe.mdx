### Ingredients

- Baking powder - A pinch.
- 1/2 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - grated.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - grated.
- Butter - to make pancakes.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Buttermilk.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1 cup Oats Flour.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Savoury Carrot and Coriander Pancakes Recipe, take a big bowl and add all the ingredients except buttermilk and mix it evenly.
1. To this add add the buttermilk slowly to make a thick batter.
1. The consistency of the batter should be a little thicker than the regular pancake batter.
1. Heat a griddle an on medium heat.
1. Pour a ladle full of the pancake batter on it and gently spread it to form a medium sized circle and apply a little butter on the sides.
1. Cook on medium heat for two minutes.
1. Flip and cook for 2 minutes till done.
1. Serve the Savoury Carrot and Coriander Pancakes with Tomato Garlic Chutney or spiced hung curd dip to make an interesting breakfast item on a lazy weekend.
