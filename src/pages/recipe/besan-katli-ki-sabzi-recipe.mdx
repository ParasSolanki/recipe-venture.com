### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- Water - as required.
- Ghee - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/2 cup Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - paste.
- 1 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 2 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped for garnishing.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Besan Katli Ki Sabzi recipe, add gram flour, ginger, green chillies, salt and turmeric powder in a bowl and mix everything till it forms a dough.
1. Cut it into cubes and then boil it in a saucepan with the required water.
1. Keep them aside.
1. Heat ghee in a wok.
1. Add cumin seeds and onions.
1. Saute till the onions turns translucent.
1. Add ginger garlic paste and let it cook for another 2 minutes.
1. Add the tomato puree and all the masalas including salt, turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chilli powder and garam masala powder.
1. Cook for another 3 to 5 minutes and then add the required water.
1. Cook for another 5 minutes.
1. Now add the steamed gram flour dumplings and cook it for 5 minutes.
1. Once done, switch off the stove and garnish with coriander leaves.
