### Ingredients

- Salt - as per your taste.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Dry Red Chilli.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 Onions - pureed.
- 2 cups Gram flour (besan).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 3 Tomatoes - pureed.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 tablespoon Khuskhus.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 3 cups Water.
- 3 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sindhi Dharan Ji Kadhi Recipe, we have to first make the dharan or the besan chickpea flour tikkis.
1. In a bowl, mix the besan, salt, finely chopped onion, red chilli powder, khus khus and 2 tablespoons oil.
1. Mix all the ingredients well and then add the curd.
1. Mix well together and make a soft dough with his mixture.
1. Make lemon sized balls with the dharan dough and press flat to make it into a cutlet shape.
1. In a deep fry pan, add cooking oil and allow to heat up.
1. Once the oil is hot, add the prepared dharan/besan tikkis in it and fry until they are golden brown.
1. Drain the dharan/ tikki in a kitchen napkin to absorb the excess oil and keep it aside.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, add 2 teaspoons oil.
1. Once the oil is hot, add cumin seeds, cloves and let the spices sputter.
1. Next add the onion puree and saute until the puree changes colour.
1. Next add the ginger garlic paste and stir until the raw smell goes away.
1. This will take about 2 minutes.
1. Next, add the tomato puree and chopped chilies.
1. and saute until, the gravy combines well.
1. Finally add in the coriander powder and mix well until the kadhi gravy comes together.
1. Simmer and add the besan tikkis into the gravy.
1. Add 2 cups of water and cook the Dharan Ji Kadhi a low flame with the lid on.
1. Let the Dharan Ji Kadhi simmer until the tikkis turn soft.
1. Turn off the flameAdd finely chopped coriander leaves and serve your Dharan Ji Kadhi.
1. Serve Dharan JI Kadhi along with Phulkas or Tawa Parathas along with a Carrot Salad for a simple dinner meal.
