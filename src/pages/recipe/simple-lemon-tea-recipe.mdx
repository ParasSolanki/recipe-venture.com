### Ingredients

- 1 Lemon - squeezed.
- 2 teaspoons Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 1/4 teaspoon Lemon zest.
- 2 Lemon wedges - or slices.
- 2 cups Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Simple Lemon Tea Recipe, keep all the ingredients handy.
1. In a carafe or saucepan, heat water on high heat with added lemon zest.
1. Once the water comes to rolling boil, squeeze in lemon juice, demerara sugar and switch off heat.
1. Keep the container covered for a minute.
1. Stir to dissolve any demerara sugar that is still left at the bottom.
1. Strain the tea, pour to tea cups and adorn it with a lemon slice/wedge to serve.
1. Serve Simple Lemon Tea Recipe with light snacks like Jwarichya Lahyancha Chivda Recipe or Crispy Oven Roasted Curried Chickpeas Recipe.
