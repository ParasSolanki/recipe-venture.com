### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 4 tablespoons Sugar - (adjust).
- Black Salt (Kala Namak) - to taste.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - a few.
- 1 pinch Black pepper powder.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1/4 cup Lemon juice.
- Ice cubes - a few per glass.
- 1 cup Aerated water (Soda Water).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Sweet Lime Soda Recipe, keep all the ingredients handy.
1. Take a mixing bowl, add water, lemon juice, cumin powder, black pepper powder, and black salt.
1. Mix.
1. Add sugar and stir till dissolved, for a quicker version, you can even add powdered sugar.
1. Once sugar is dissolved, add soda, mint leaves and ice cubes.
1. Serve immediately.
1. Serve Sweet Lime Soda Recipe with Indian Style Jeera Biscuit Recipe and North East Khapse Recipe (Deep Fried Biscuits From Arunachal) during evening.
