### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1/2 tablespoon Dried oregano.
- 1/2 cup Corn Kernels - boiled.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - boiled and mashed.
- Sunflower Oil - as required for deep frying.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 1/2 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- 3 tablespoon Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- 1/8 tablespoon Garlic.
- 1/2 cup Cheese - grated.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Corn Cheese Balls Recipe, get all the ingredients handy- grate the cheese, boil veggies and mash boiled potatoes.
1. In a mixing bowl, add the grated cheese, boiled and mashed potato, boiled yellow corn, green capsicum, coriander leaves, red chili flakes, crushed oregano, a little pepper, garlic paste, cornflour and salt and mix everything well.
1. Apply a little oil on your palm and make about 1/2 inches balls from the mixture.
1. In a bowl, add all-purpose flour and water and mix them to get pouring consistency batter.
1. In a kadai, heat enough oil to deep fry.
1. Put some plain flour on one side and bread crumbs on the other side on your work surface.
1. Roll the cheese balls in plain flour, dip them in all-purpose flour batter and then in bread crumbs.
1. Repeat the same process to coat all corn and cheese balls in a similar way.
1. Deep fry coated cheese balls on a medium flame.
1. Serve Corn Cheese Balls Recipe hot with dips like Cottage Cheese Dip, Tzatziki or Peanut Chilli Dipping Sauce.
