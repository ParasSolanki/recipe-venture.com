### Ingredients

- 3/4 cup Sugar.
- 1/4 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1/4 cup Corn flour.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini) - to garnish.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 2 1/3 cups Watermelon - chopped into chunks.
- 2 teaspoon Walnuts - chopped for garnish.

### Instructions

1. To make Sicilian Watermelon Pudding Recipe With Cinnamon Twist, first start by preparing the watermelon puree.
1. Deseed fresh watermelon and cut into large chunks.
1. Place in a Hand blender and process until smooth and well pureed.
1. Strain the mixture into a bowl and measure 2 cups and reserve 5 teaspoon puree from it.
1. Stir and mix cornflour in a small bowl with 1/4 cup watermelon puree to it without any lumps.
1. In a Saucepan, combine watermelon puree, cornstarch mixture, sugar, vanilla extract and cinnamon stick.
1. Bring the watermelon mixture to a boil on medium heat, and then let it simmer for 10-15 minutes until the mixture thickens.
1. Stir continuously to avoid any lumps.
1. Rinse Ramekins/molds with water.
1. Do not pat them dry.
1. Pour the watermelon pudding mixture into the ramekins.
1. Cover it with a plastic clear wrap and refrigerate it for 6 hours.
1. To serve, remove the plastic cover.
1. You can serve it in the ramekins itself or unmold the pudding.
1. To unmold, rub ramekin between your palms and invert it on a plate, slightly tilted, give a hard whack or two.
1. Garnish the Sicilian Watermelon Pudding Recipe With Cinnamon Twist (Gelo Di Melone) with ground cinnamon powder and chopped walnuts.
1. Serve Sicilian Watermelon Pudding Recipe during tea-party or as a dessert after a meal of Spaghetti Aglio Olio Pasta Recipe with Bacon and Garlic Bread With Herb Butter Recipe.
