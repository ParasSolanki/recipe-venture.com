### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Cucumber - finely chopped.
- 1/3 cup Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1/3 cup Tomatoes - deseeded and finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Mango (Raw) - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Raw Mango Carrot Cucumber Salad Recipe With Peanuts, prep all the ingredients and keep them ready.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the raw mango, carrots, cucumber, tomatoes, roasted peanuts, coriander leaves, mint leaves and give it a good mix.
1. Into this bowl, add the salt, pepper, chaat masala, cumin powder and toss the salad.
1. Give it a taste and adjust the seasonings accordingly.
1. Once done, transfer the Raw Mango Carrot Cucumber Salad Recipe With Peanuts to a serving bowl and serve chilled.
1. Serve Raw Mango Carrot Cucumber Salad Recipe With Peanuts along with Nawabi Paneer Curry Recipe, Cheese Garlic Naan Recipe and Papad Raita Recipe to make a delicious north Indian dinner or lunch.
