### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 250 grams Chicken - with or without bones.
- 6 Shallots.
- 2 Shallots.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 5 cloves Garlic.
- 1 Tomato - fully ripe.
- Salt - as needed.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chettinad Kozhi Rasam, clean and wash the chicken thoroughly.
1. Add chicken with the minced shallots, tomato, chilli powder, turmeric powder, and salt as needed along with 4 cups of water in a pressure cooker.
1. Pressure cook for 1 whistle and then simmer the gas.
1. Cook on low flame for another 5  minutes and switch off the gas.
1. Meanwhile, prepare rasam powder by grinding fennel seeds, peppercorns, cumin and chilies and grind using a mixer-grinder.
1. Now add some coriander powder to the spice mix.
1. Grind once again and collect in a bowl.
1. In the same grinder jar add garlic and onion and grind together without adding any water.
1. .
1. Heat 1 teaspoon of oil in a wok and season the rasam with some mustard seeds, cumin, dry red chilies, asafoetida and curry leaves.
1. Allowing it to crackle.
1. Add this to the rasam in the pressure cooker.
1. Add the rasam powder and the salt needed.
1. Bring the rasam to a nice boil and switch off the stove.
1. Garnish the Rasam with some fresh chopped coriander leaves before serving.
1. Serve Chettinad Kozhi Rasam along with Steamed Rice and Elai Vadam for a weekday meal.
