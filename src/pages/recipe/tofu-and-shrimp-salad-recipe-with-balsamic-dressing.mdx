### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1/2 cup Iceberg lettuce - shredded.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - husked and crushed.
- 250 grams Shrimps.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 100 grams Tofu.
- 1/2 cup Beetroot - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Balsamic Vinegar.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - shredded.
- 1/2 cup Red baby radish - sliced to roundels.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 6 Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 1/2 cup Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tofu & Shrimp Salad Recipe With Balsamic Dressing, clean and wash the shrimp properly.
1. Heat olive oil in a pan and saute shrimps till golden and cooked.
1. Keep aside in a bowl.
1. Cut tofu into small cubes and grill on stove top.
1. To the shrimps add balsamic vinegar, some crushed pepper, a pinch of salt and some lemon juice.
1. Mix well to combine.
1. In a large mixing bowl add all the sliced, shredded and grate vegetables like the lettuce, cabbage, carrots, beetroot, baby red radish, yellow bell peppers and green chillies.
1. Season them with salt, pepper, chili flakes and some lemon juice and mix well with a spatula.
1. Add seasoned shrimps to the veg mix and toss well to combine.
1. Now add some more lemon juice if required, check salt and then add some sugar and roasted peanuts.
1. Mix again.
1. Garnish with some fresh chopped coriander and mint and serve.
1. Serve Tofu & Shrimp Salad Recipe With Balsamic Dressing along with Mexican Salsa Chicken With Italian Cream Cheese Recipe.
