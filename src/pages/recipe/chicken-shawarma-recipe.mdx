### Ingredients

- 2 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Tomato - pulp removed and julienned.
- 1 tablespoon Active dry yeast.
- 1 tablespoon Sugar.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - cut into julienne.
- 2 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 cup Homemade Hummus.
- 2 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 cup Tzatziki.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1/4 cup Chilli vinegar.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 Cucumber - cut into julienne.
- 1 Chicken breasts - cut into thin strips.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken Shawarma Recipe we will first make the Pita bread.
1. To make the Pita BreadFor the pita bread dough, in a bowl mix together yeast & sugar.
1. Heat the milk until lukewarm and mix it with yeast and sugar.
1. Allow this to sit for a while till you notice bubbles rising.
1. This is when the yeast starts to activate.
1. Now add flour, salt and water slowly and combine to form a soft dough.
1. Keep kneading for at least 10 minutes on to a working surface and place it inside a big bowl.
1. Cover it with a damp cloth and rest the dough in a warm place, allowing it to rise and doubles its size.
1. Marinade for the chickenIn a mixing bowl, add cumin powder, coriander powder, red chilli powder, black pepper powder, turmeric powder, ginger-garlic paste and olive oil.
1. Combine everything well.
1. Add in the chicken pieces and mix them well till it is nicely coated.
1. Marinate the chicken pieces for at least 30 minutes.
1. Meanwhile pickle the cucumber, tomatoes and carrots.
1. In a separate bowl, add chilli vinegar, salt and sugar and the vegetables.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Make the Tzatziki Recipe - A Greek Yogurt Dip and Classic Homemade Hummus Recipe With Lemon And Coriander and keep aside.
1. Check on the bread dough, knock out the all the air by kneading it once again.
1. Make small sized dough balls.
1. Dust some flour on to your surface and with the help of the rolling pin start rolling one of the ball to a circle shaped flat bread.
1. The bread should not be rolled too thin and it has to big enough to stuff the chicken and roll it up to form a roll.
1. Heat a flat skillet, on medium flame and cook the rolled out bread on both sides until you see small brown spots.
1. Do the same for the rest of the dough and keep the bread aside as you move ahead to cook the chicken.
1. Heat a pan with oil, add chicken pieces and keep turning them over as they are getting cooked and charred on all the side.
1. This will take about 20 minutes to cook.
1. Assembling the Chicken ShawarmaTake one square of parchment paper and place one cooked pita bread, and spoon in 2 tablespoons of hummus evenly, and a tablespoon of the tzatziki dip as well.
1. Place one cucumber, carrot and tomatoes julienne in the middle.
1. Then place some cooked chicken pieces, add chopped onion and roll it tightly along with the parchment paper and pack both ends of the paper into the roll if you want it as a take away.
1. Serve the Chicken Shawarma Recipe along with Summer Lettuce Salad Recipe to make it a complete meal for your Sunday lunch.
