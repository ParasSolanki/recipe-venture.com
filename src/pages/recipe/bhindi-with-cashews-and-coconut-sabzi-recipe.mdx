### Ingredients

- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 4 Garlic.
- 8 Cashew nuts.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/2 kg Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra) - cut to bite size.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Curry leaves - few.

### Instructions

1. To make Bhindi with Cashews & Coconut Sabzi Recipe, cut ladies finger into small pieces.
1. Get prep with the other ingredients as well.
1. Take a pan/kadai, add oil, mustard, cumin seeds, channa dhal, dry red chili and garlic.
1. Let it get fried.
1. Add cashew nuts and cook until they are lightly browned, this would take about 1 -2 minutes only.
1. Do not make it completely brown.
1. Add bhindi, turmeric, chili powder, salt & curry leaves.
1. Keep mixing and cook until okra/bhindi are fried well.
1. At this stage, add fresh coconut, mix well and immediately switch off gas.
1. Serve Bhindi with Cashews & Coconut Sabzi Recipe with rasam.
