### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Homemade Hummus.
- 1 cup Broccoli - cut into small florets.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Cherry tomatoes - slit into half.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Jowar Flour (Sorghum).
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 1/2 cup Bajra Flour ( Pearl Millet).
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 3/4 cup Whole Wheat Flour.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Soft Millet Taco Recipe Topped With Hummus & Veggies, add all the flours – jowar, bajra and atta into a bowl.
1. Sprinkle some salt as well.
1. Add water little at a time and knead it to form a soft dough.
1. Once the dough is done, cover it with a cloth and rest until you make the topping.
1. To make the hummus spread, you can check out recipe link – Classic Homemade Hummus Recipe With Lemon And Coriander.
1. Keep the hummus ready.
1. Make the boiled egg and keep it ready you can check out our recipe link here - Boiled Eggs RecipeOnce the eggs are boiled, shell them and cut into quarters and keep it aside.
1. *To Make the Topping For the Millet TacoHeat a skillet with butter, add chopped garlic and saute until softens.
1. Add in sliced onions and saute till it turns light golden brown.
1. If you want you can also add some teaspoon sugar to caramelize.
1. Add in chopped broccoli and cherry tomatoes and give it a stir.
1. Sprinkle some salt and pepper and cumin powder .
1. Cover it with a lid and cook until the broccoli is just about cooked.
1. Once the broccoli is cooked and turned bright green, turn off the heat.
1. Keep aside.
1. *To Make the Soft TacosShape the kneaded dough into medium sized balls.
1. Dust it with wheat flour and roll it gently to make a circle.
1. Repeat the same process with the remaining dough portions and keep it ready to cook them on a skillet.
1. Preheat the skillet on medium flame.
1. Place the rolled soft taco gently on the skillet and cook on both sides, cook until you notice brown spots on either side.
1. Cook on medium heat and allow the taco to get lightly crisp.
1. Do the same for the rest of the rolled dough.
1. *To assemble the TacoTake one soft taco and spread a tablespoon of hummus evenly over it.
1. Add sauteed broccoli, cherry tomatoes and place the boiled egg and sprinkle some pepper powder and serve.
1. Serve the Soft Millet Taco Recipe Topped With Hummus & Veggies along with fresh Fruit bowl, Chikoo Banana Date Smoothie or a hot cup of hot chocolate to make your breakfast healthy and filling.
