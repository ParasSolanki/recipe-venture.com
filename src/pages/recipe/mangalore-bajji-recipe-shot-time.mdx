### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 3 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 3/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - sour.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 cups Sunflower Oil - for frying.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1.5 cups All Purpose Flour (Maida).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Mangalore Bajji (Goli Baje), take a big bowl, add the sour curd and whisk it till you attain a creamy texture.
1. To this add the chopped green chillies, coriander leaves, grated coconut and mix well.
1. Slowly add in the maida and mix well.
1. Add water little at a time till you get a thick pouring batter consistency.
1. Add the baking soda, season with salt, mix well and leave it aside for an hour.
1. To make the Mangalore Bajji (Goli Baje), heat oil in a wok.
1. In the meanwhile, whisk the Mangalore Bajji batter.
1. Once the oil is hot, drop half a tablespoon amount of batter into the hot oil.
1. Let it cook and turn golden on one side.
1. Flip over and cook the other side as well.
1. This will take 2 minutes.
1. Remove it in a kitchen towel and proceed to make the rest of the Gole Baje with the remaining batter.
1. Serve Mangalore Bajji (Goli Baje) along with Coriander Coconut Chutney and hot Filter Coffee for the evening snack or when you have guests at home.
