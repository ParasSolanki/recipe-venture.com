### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 4 Ripe Bananas.
- 100 grams Sugar.
- 1/2 cup Chocolate chips.
- 100 grams Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/2 cup Walnuts.
- 100 grams Butter - melted.
- 2 Whole Eggs.
- 1 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Banana Bread With Chocolate Chip And Walnut first, take a 9” by 5” inch loaf pan and grease it properly,dust it with flour and tap on the base to remove excess flour.
1. Take flour ,baking soda and baking powder together in a strainer and strain it to make uniform mixture.
1. In a blender add brown sugar, white sugar and grind them to a fine powder.
1. In a bowl break 2 eggs and whick them.
1. Now on the other hand grind the bananas into a nice pureeAdd the Banana's and sugar mixture into another bowl and mix them well.
1. To this mixture add some vanilla essence and butter and give it a good mix until they combine together.
1. Now Add this mixture to the flour and also add the whisked eggs, oil.
1. With a hand blender mix the mixture.
1. Now once you get a good mix add chocolate chips and give a light mix to the flour mix.
1. Transfer the mixture to a loaf pan, in a preheated oven at 180 degree celsius bake the bread for 45 - 50 minutes.
1. Once cake is done ,you can do the skewer test ,if its come out clean then your bread is done.
1. Serve the Banana bread with chocolate chips and walnut with a yummy scoop of ice cream for a tea time snack.
