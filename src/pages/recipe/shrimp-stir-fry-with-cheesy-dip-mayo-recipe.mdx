### Ingredients

- 1/2 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - julienned.
- 1/2 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - julienned.
- 2 tablespoon Del Monte Cheesy Dip.
- 1/2 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - julienned.
- 1/2 cup Broccoli - chopped florets.
- 15 Shrimps - de-veined.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 7 Garlic - minced.
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Green Chilli Sauce.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 4 Basil leaves - torn.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Shrimp Stir Fry With Cheesy Dip Mayo Recipe, de-vein the shrimps.
1. Wash and marinate them with the ingredients mentioned for marination.
1. Keep them aside for 30 minutes.
1. Heat a wok/kadai and add oil.
1. Once the oil is heated, add chopped onion and fry till onion turns translucent.
1. Add garlic and saute till the raw smell of garlic goes away.
1. Add tomato and fry till tomatoes turns mushy and cooked well.
1. Add the bell peppers and broccoli florets and stir fry until cooked.
1. Now add the marinated shrimps and cook for 12-15 minutes stirring occasionally until the shrimp gets cooked well.
1. Check the taste and adjust the flavors accordingly Once done, stir in the basil leaves and turn off the heat.
1. Serve Shrimp Stir Fry With Cheesy Dip Mayo along with steamed rice and Sangacho Ross Recipe for a lunch or dinner.
