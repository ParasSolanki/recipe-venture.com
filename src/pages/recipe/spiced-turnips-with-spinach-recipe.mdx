### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 3/4 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - paste(optional).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger - paste.
- Red Chilli powder - to taste.
- 3/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Spinach Leaves (Palak) - bunch.
- Sunflower Oil - to cook.
- 2 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 3/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 3 Turnips - peeled and diced into cubes.
- 3 Green Chillies - slit.

### Instructions

1. To start preparing Spiced Turnips with Spinach Recipe, get all the ingredients handy.
1. In a pan, heat oil.
1. Once the oil is hot, add in the cumin seeds and allow them to sizzle.
1. Add sliced onions and saute it till it changes colour on a medium flame.
1. Add ginger and garlic paste and saute it for a minute until the raw smell goes.
1. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, salt and coriander powder.
1. Cover and cook for another 2 minutes till done.
1. Add turnips, mix it well.
1. Cover it and cook on a low flame till it 3/4th done, takes about 5-10 minutes.
1. Add chopped spinach, green/red chillies and cilantro.
1. Mix well, cover it and cook on a low flame till it changes the colour or looks roasted.
1. Serve the prepared Spiced Turnips and Spinach Recipe hot with chapatis or naan.
