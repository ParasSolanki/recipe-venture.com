### Ingredients

- 2 inch Ginger - julienned (fine).
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 Red Yellow or Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 cup Black Eyed Beans (Lobia) - soaked in water overnight.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder - or red chilli flakes.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice - adjustable.
- 3 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 2 Tomatoes - blanched.
- 2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lemon and Ginger Spicy Black Eyed Beans recipe, firstly pressure cook the Black eyed beans with 2-3 cups of water in a cooker, till there is still a bite left to them.
1. Drain and reserve the water.
1. In a pan heat the oil.
1. Add the onion, garlic and sauté for a minute till the onions are soft.
1. After the onions are soft, add the pepper and sauté for another minute.
1. Add the ginger reserving a few juliennes for garnish.
1. Add the cumin powder and chili powder or flakes.
1. Add the tomatoes and a ladleful of the water leftover from cooking the beans.
1. Cook for 3-4 minutes and add the beans.
1. Sprinkle salt and mix well.
1. Stir and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Take Spicy Black Eyed Beans off the heat, and transfer into a serving dish.
1. Garnish with the coriander leaves and lime juice.
1. Serve the Lemon and Ginger Spicy Black Eyed Beans along with Spinach And Tortellini Soup Recipe for a simple meal.
