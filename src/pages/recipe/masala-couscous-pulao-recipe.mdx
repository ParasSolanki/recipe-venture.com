### Ingredients

- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few to garnish.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 400 grams Couscous.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - few.
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/2 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - chopped.
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Masala Couscous Pulao recipe, firstly heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add cinnamon stick, cloves and bay leaf and saute for atleast 30 seconds.
1. Add sliced onions and cook until they become soft and transparent.
1. Add ginger garlic paste and saute for 2 minutes.
1. Add vegetables, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, salt and saute for 5 minutes more.
1. Add 4 cups of water and allow the mixture to boil.
1. One the water starts boiling, add mint leaves and couscous.
1. Simmer the flame and cook until the water is completely absorbed If couscous is not yet cooked, add a cup of hot water.
1. Garnish with chopped coriander and it is ready to be served.
1. Serve Masala Couscous Pulao with Beetroot Raita, Lauki Raita or any other raita of your choice.
1. A roasted papad will add a crunch to this meal.
