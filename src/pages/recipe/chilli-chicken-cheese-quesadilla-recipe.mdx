### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 1/2 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1/3 cup Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - chopped.
- 6 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese - grated.
- 2 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Tomato Ketchup.
- 1 Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Sweet and Spicy Red Chilli Sauce (Tomato Chilli Sauce).
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 300 grams Chicken breasts - cut into strips.
- 4 tablespoons Butter - softened.
- 4 Tortillas.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chilli Chicken & Cheese Quesadilla Recipe, we will first make the filling for this quesadillas.
1. *For the Chilli Chicken filling To begin making the filling for the Chilli Chicken & Cheese Quesadilla , we will first make the base sauce by mixing corn flour with 1/4 cup of water.
1. in a mixing bowl.
1. To this stir in the soya sauce, ketchup, chilli sauce and salt and mix well.
1. Heat oil in a wok  on high heat; add garlic, ginger, green chillies, spring onions and stir fry for about 30 seconds.
1. Add in the chicken strips and  capsicum on medium heat.
1. Once the capsicum is tender and the chicken is cooked , stir in the corn flour mixture to the wok and cook for a few minutes until the chilli sauce thickens.
1. At this stage turn the heat to high and continue to cook for a few minutes until well combined with the sauce.
1. Garnish  with spring onion leaves, transfer to a bowl and set aside to cool.
1. *To assemble the Chilli Chicken & Cheese Quesadilla You can either use store bought tortillas or make them at home.
1. You can choose to either make Corn Tortillas by following the Homemade Corn Tortilla Recipe or make whole wheat tortillas by following Homemade Whole Wheat Flour Tortillas Recipe.
1. These tortillas can be made ahead and stored in an airtight container.
1. Heat a skillet on medium-low flame, lightly heat up the tortillas for about 10-15 seconds on each side.
1. Place the tortillas on a flat surface like that of a working counter or a chopping board, place the Chilli Chicken filling on one half side of the tortillas, place grated cheese over it and fold it to get a semi circular filled quesadilla.
1. Return these Chilli Chicken & Cheese Quesadilla to the skillet on medium-low flame, smear some softened butter on the top and grill the quesadillas on the pan over medium heat until it is crisp and golden on both sides.
1. Serve Chilli Chicken & Cheese Quesadillas as a party starter along with a refreshing Pomegranate Mint Cocktail, followed by a dessert of Mini Key Lime Pie.
