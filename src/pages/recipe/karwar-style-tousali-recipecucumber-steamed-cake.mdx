### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).
- 1 teaspoon Enos fruit salt.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut.
- 1/2 cup Rice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1 Cucumber - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Karwar style Tousali Recipe-Cucumber Steamed Cake by soaking the rice in water for 2 hours and then drain the water, spread the rice on a kitchen paper towel to let it dry completely.
1. Then using a mixer, grind the rice to a coarse mixture and not too smooth and add it to a mixing bowl.
1. Add the remaining ingredients to the batter, mix well.
1. Add the batter on a greased dhokla plate.
1. Steam the batter in the Dhokla steamer for about 10 minutes and serve.
1. Serve the Karwar style Tousali Recipe-Cucumber Steamed Cake along with Green Chutney Recipe  to make it your breakfast meal.
