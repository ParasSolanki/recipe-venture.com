### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1 cup Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Karnataka Style Menthe Kadubu Recipe (Fenugreek Leaves Wheat Dumpling Recipe) by making the dumpling first.
1. Mix all the ingredients and add little water and knead it into a firm dough.
1. Take a pinch of the dough and roll it into a ball shape and press it little so that you will get a flat disc.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat a sauce pan with enough water and when the water comes to a boil add the dumplings and boil them for about 10 minutes.
1. Skim them out and keep it aside.
1. Prep all the ingredients needed to stir fry.
1. Heat a kadai and crackle the mustard seeds, cumin seeds and then add in the onions.
1. Sauté till it is translucent.
1. Then add in the menthe leaves, along with the spice powders and salt.
1. Sauté them till the leaves are cooked.
1. Once done you can add your steamed dumpling and cook for about 2 more minutes and serve.
1. Serve the Karnataka Style Menthe Kadubu Recipe (Fenugreek Leaves Wheat Dumpling Recipe) as a side dish to steam rice , Kollu Rasam Recipe (Nourishing HorseGram Stew) and Karnataka Style Sorakkai Palya Recipe.
1. Or it can be had as a breakfast as well.
