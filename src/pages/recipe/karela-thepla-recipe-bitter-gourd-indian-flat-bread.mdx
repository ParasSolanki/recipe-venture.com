### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1/2 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1/2 cup Lukewarm Water.
- 2 tablespoons Butter - melted.
- 2 Green Chillies - ground to paste.
- 2 to 3 tablespoons Karela (Bitter Gourd/ Pavakkai) - grated.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Ghee - or oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Karela Thepla Recipe, in a large mixing bowl, add wheat flour and besan along with grated karela, ajwain, turmeric, red chili powder, cumin powder, green chili paste, ginger paste, ghee/oil and mix well with a spoon or fork.
1. Now slowly add warm water and knead the flour mix to make a soft dough.
1. Heat a flat bottomed tawa and meanwhile prepare the theplas.
1. Pinch a lemon sized ball from the dough and roll out to make a thin roti on the dusted floor.
1. Once the tawa is hot, place the thepla on the tawa and dry roast it from both sides.
1. Now brush some melted butter on both sides of the thepla one by one and roast till golden in colour.
1. Transfer the thepla on a serving plate and serve hot.
1. Serve Karela Thepla along with Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita and Aam Ka Achaar for a light lunch or breakfast.
