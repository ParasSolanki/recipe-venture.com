### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Ghee.
- 1 cup Sugar.
- 1/2 cup Khoya (Mawa).
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1 tablespoon Cashew nuts - to garnish.
- 1 tablespoon Badam (Almond).
- 1/2 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).
- 1 tablespoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Atte Ka Halwa Recipe (Whole Wheat Flour Halwa with Khoya), first get all the ingredients ready.
1. Heat a tablespoon of ghee in skillet on medium heat.
1. Add the almonds and cashew nuts and roast until browned and crisp.
1. Turn off the heat and keep aside.
1. Heat 1/2 cup of water and sugar in a saucepan and allow the sugar the melt completely and keep the hot sugar water aside.
1. Heat 1/4 cup of ghee in a heavy bottomed pan or a kadai.
1. Add the wheat flour and sooji and roast on medium heat in the ghee for a good 5 to 6 minutes until you get a roasted aroma from the flour and the flour resembles coarse crumbs and has changed to a little darker brown color.
1. Add the cardamom powder, khoya and again mix it well.
1. Cook the Atte Ka Halwa along with a khoya for another 5 minutes.
1. Keep stirring continuously and when the halwa begins to thicken add the sugar water and keep stirring continuously.
1. You will notice the halwa will splutter and start thickening.
1. Keep stirring for another 5 minutes till you notice the halwa leaves the sides of the pan.
1. Once it does turn off the heat and transfer the Atte Ka Halwa to a serving bowl.
1. Garnish with the roasted almonds and cashew nuts and serve.
1. Serve the Atte Ka Halwa along with a traditional Indian meal or simply make it as a dessert for festivals when you have friends and family over.
