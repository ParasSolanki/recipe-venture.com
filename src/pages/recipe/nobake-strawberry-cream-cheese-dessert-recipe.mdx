### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Vanilla custard.
- 1 Condensed Milk - sweetened.
- 1 Britannia Cream Cheese - (I used Philadelphia) softened.
- 14 Marie Biscuit - or 10 Graham Crackers.
- 2 cups Strawberries.
- 1 tablespoon Sugar - powdered.
- 1/4 cup Sugar.
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 2 tablespoon Butter - melted.
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 1/3 cup Lemon juice.
- 1 Lemon.

### Instructions

1. For making No-Bake Strawberry Cream Cheese Dessert, begin with preparing the crust.
1. CrustGrind the biscuits in a mixer.
1. In a small bowl, combine the crumbs, sugar and butter.
1. Divide this mixture between 2 glasses and let it chill for 15 minutes.
1. FillingIn a small bowl, beat the cream cheese until its smooth.
1. Gradually add in the condensed milk until it is blended well.
1. Beat in the lemon juice and vanilla essence.
1. Spoon approximately 1/4 cup into each glass.
1. Then let it chill for about 1 hour.
1. Strawberry CompoteRinse the strawberries under cold water and use the point of the knife to remove the hull out of the tops.
1. When you have big strawberries cut in half.
1. Zest and juice the lemon.
1. In a small Saucepan over medium heat, combine sugar, water, and cornflour and whisk well.
1. Add the lemon zest and juice.
1. Add in strawberries and mix gently.
1. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring occasionally.
1. The mixture will sizzle for a while, but then juice will begin to form.
1. Continue stirring; mash a few strawberries with a wooden spoon or heat-proof spatula to help produce the syrup.
1. Cook until sauce thickens, about 10-15 minutes.
1. Remove from heat.
1. In a Blender, puree about 1/3 of the sauce, then mix back into the remaining topping.
1. ServingTop each glass with the strawberry compote, about 1/4 cup in each and serve No-Bake Strawberry Cream Cheese Dessert Recipe instantly.
1. Note: You can also use 1 can of strawberry pie filling which is readily available in the market.
1. You can substitute the blueberry pie filling or any another fruit flavor of your choice for the strawberry filling.
