### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Carrots (Gajjar) - finely grated.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Green Moong Sprouts.
- 1/4 cup Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - soaked in water for 3-4 hours.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - freshly grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Satvik Carrot Sprouts Salad recipe, grate the carrots and keep it ready.
1. In a mixing bowl, add all the ingredients- carrot, green moong sprouts, capsicum, coconut, peanuts, lime juice and season it with salt and pepper.
1. Mix the Carrot Sprouts Salad well and serve fresh.
1. Serve Satvik Carrot Sprouts Salad as a side dish along with Panchmel Dal, Bhindi Masala and Phulkas.
