### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Sesame powder - roasted.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Curry leaves - few.
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 3 Raw Banana.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Rasam Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Andhra Style Aratikaya Vepudu Recipe, wash and peel the raw bananas and chop it into thin slices.
1. Keep the chopped slices in a bowl filled with water until its ready to use.
1. This will prevent from it turning back.
1. Drain the water.
1. To the raw banana slices add salt, turmeric powder, red chilli powder and rasam powder.
1. Toss it and mix it well such that the masala gets coated well to the slices.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan, add mustard seeds and urad dal.
1. When the mustard seeds splutter, add garlic and curry leaves.
1. Saute till the garlic is done.
1. Later, add the coated raw banana pieces to the pan and toss them gently.
1. Keep the flame on low and stir in between.
1. When the raw banana is cooked sprinkle the roasted sesame powder.
1. Toss gently such that it gets coated well.
1. Add 1-2 teaspoon of oil and let the raw banana pieces get well fried on low flame and gets crisp.
1. Once done, turn off the heat, check the taste and seasonings and adjust according.
1. Serve Andhra Style Aratikaya Vepudu along with Mixed Vegetable Sambar, Steamed Rice and Elai Vadam for a weekday meal.
