### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Tomato Ketchup.
- 1 teaspoon Paprika powder - or red chilli flakes.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - uncooked.
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- 2 Tomatoes - diced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 cups Water.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Pasta - whole wheat rotini.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 2 cup Mixed vegetables - frozen or fresh.
- 1 teaspoon Dried basil leaves.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin with Vegetable Pasta Soup, heat olive oil in a pan and add garlic & onion.
1. Cook it for about 1-2 minutes.
1. After 1-2 minutes, add all remaining ingredients except pasta.
1. Cook the mixture for 10 minutes or until vegetables are soft and tender.
1. After 10 minutes, add the rotini pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes more.
1. Season the delicious soup with more salt and pepper if required.
1. Serve Vegetable Pasta Soup along with Beetroot Salad Recipe with Mixed Greens and Garlic Bread for a weekday dinner.
