### Ingredients

-  1 green - finely chopped.
-  1 cucumber - peel and grind.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin powder.
-  1 cup gourd - peel and tighten.
-  salt - as per taste.
- 2 cups curd.
-  1 green coriander - little.

### Instructions

1. To prepare gourd raita, prepare all the ingredients first.
1. Add grated gourd, cucumber, curd, green chillies, salt, cumin powder and coriander in a large bowl.
1. Mix well and your raita is ready.
1. Serve gourd raita with Garlic Dal, gourd elder greens and phulka for dinner.
