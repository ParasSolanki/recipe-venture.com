### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil - hot.
- 3 tablespoons Corn flour.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 teaspoons Sambar Powder.
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar) - (optional).
- Cooking soda - a pinch.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- Sunflower Oil - to deep fry.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Potato Bonda Recipe Flavoured With Sambar Powder, get prep with all the ingredients needed.
1. Heat oil in a pan, once the oil is hot, add mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
1. Once it splutters add green peas and saute well.
1. Add the mashed potato to it and add little water along with required salt.
1. Cook on medium heat.
1. Add turmeric powder, chilli powder, asafoetida, sambar powder and coriander powder to it.
1. Stir well and cook.
1. Keep mashing the pieces with ladle and cook until water evaporates and combined.
1. The stuffing is ready.
1. Remove from flame and allow it to cool, make small balls from it and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a kadai/deep frying pan for frying bonda.
1. Meanwhile, for making batter, take all the ingredients mentioned under “for batter” and mix well adding little water.
1. The batter has to be thick enough to coat the potato balls.
1. Dip the prepared potato balls into the batter and drop into hot oil.
1. Fry on medium heat until it turns golden on all sides.
1. Remove from oil and drain excess oil on kitchen tissues.
1. Serve Potato Bonda Recipe Flavoured With Sambar Powder along with tomato ketchup, Dhaniya Pudina Chutney Recipe, and Masala Chai Recipe.
