### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 3 Green Chillies.
- Salt - as required.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 250 grams Chicken - boneless and minced (cleaned and washed).
- 1 Whole Eggs.
- 5 Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 6 Cashew nuts.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 5 Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped.
- 5 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped to garnish.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Onions - finely sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder - (adjust according to your choice).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Keema Balls Recipe, we will first make the Keema Balls.
1. To make the keema balls, in a mixer-jar, combine, boneless chicken pieces along with fresh coriander leaves, green chillies, ginger garlic paste, cashew nuts, egg yolk, and salt and grind it to smooth paste in a grinder.
1. Transfer the keema mixture into a bowl and shape the keema into lemon sized balls.
1. Heat oil in a kadai, on medium flame, deep fry the keema balls in the hot oil until it turns into a nice brown colour.
1.  Drain on absorbent paper and set aside.
1.  For the masalaIn a pan heat oil on medium flame, add the onions and fry until it turns golden brown.
1. This will take a good 4-5 minutes.
1. Once the onions turn brown, add ginger garlic paste and fry until the raw smell goes off.
1. Add chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, pepper powder, a pinch of salt and continue to cook.
1. Now that the onions and the masalas have come together, add chopped tomatoes, and cook till they turned mushy.
1. Add the prepared keema balls, sprinkle some water and cover and cook for 5 minutes.
1. Turn off the flame, add  lemon juice and chopped coriander leaves to garnish.
1. You will have a semi-gravy for the Keema Balls RecipeServe Keema Balls Recipe along with phulkas or whole wheat lachha paratha for a weeknight dinner.
