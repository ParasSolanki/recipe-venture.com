### Ingredients

-  1/4 tsp red chilli powder.
-  1/4 4 tsp asafoetida.
-  1/4 tsp turmeric powder.
-  1/4 tsp rye.
-  6 curry leaves - finely chopped.
-  1/4 tsp cumin seeds.
-  salt - as per taste.
- 2 cups roti - cut into small pieces.
-  1 tsp oil.

### Instructions

1. To make Vagharela Rotli recipe, firstly take a big bowl.
1. Add bread crumbs, turmeric powder, curry leaves, red chili powder, asafoetida and mix well.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add the mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and let it splutter.
1. After tempering, add the bread mixture, salt as per taste and mix well.
1. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes and turn off the gas.
1. Serve Serve the Wagharela Rotli recipe with Masala Tea for breakfast in the morning or evening.
