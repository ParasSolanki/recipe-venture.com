### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1/2 cup Masoor Dal (Whole).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 Ridge Gourd (Turai/ Peerkangai) - peeled and sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1/2 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Andhra Style Beerakai Pappu, in a pressure cooker add all the dals, ridge gourd, tomato, onion, ginger garlic paste, green chilli, turmeric, salt and 2 cups water and pressure cook it for 4 whistle.
1. Once done allow the pressure to release by itself and keep the cooker on the heat again and add tamarind water and heat the dal till it thickens little.
1. To temper, heat a small tempering pan with oil and add mustard seeds and cumin seeds and allow it splutter for 10 seconds.
1. Add curry leaves, dry red chilies, hing and leave the curry leaves to crackle for 10 seconds.
1. Add in the crushed garlic and brown them and pour it over the dal and serve.
1. Serve the Andhra Style Beerakai Pappu Recipe along with some steamed rice, Kathirikai Poondu Pirattal and Roasted Papad Chaat  by the side to enjoy your everyday meal in a healthy way.
