### Ingredients

- 2 cups Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - finely shredded.
- Salt - as required.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped finely.
- Curry leaves - Few.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 3/4 cup Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Mustard oil.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 3 tablespoons Garlic - minced.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cabbage Palya recipe, cook shredded cabbage in boiling water for 5 to 7 minutes or until it is slightly transparent and drain excess water using a colander.
1. Heat mustard oil in a wide pan, add mustard seeds and let it crackle.
1. Once it crackles, add hing, curry leaves, green chillies and garlic.
1. Saute it until the garlic changes its colour slightly.
1. Add the onions and a tiny bit of salt and let it cook until it turns slightly golden.
1. At this stage add the cabbage and add the required salt and mix well and cook until you see it drying.
1. This shouldn't take too long.
1. Now add the chopped tomato, grated coconut and coriander.
1. Mix well and cook covered until the tomato becomes soft.
1. Dish it out into bowl and serve.
1. Cabbage Palya is best served with steamed rice and mixed vegetable sambar, or as a tiffin box dish with chapattis.
