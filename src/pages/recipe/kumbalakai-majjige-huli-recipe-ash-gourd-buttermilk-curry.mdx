### Ingredients

- 3 Green Chillies.
- 1 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 teaspoons Coconut Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 250 grams Vellai Poosanikai (Ash gourd/White Pumpkin) - seeds and skin removed.
- 1 tablespoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 4-5 Curry leaves.
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - churned with 1 cup of water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kumbalakai Majjige Huli Recipe  first soak the chana dal in 1/4 cup of water for 20 minutes.
1. Steam the ash gourd either using the pressure cooker or a steamer.
1. Follow How to Cook Vegetables in a Pressure CookerAdd  the coconut, soaked chana dal, green chillies, ginger and salt into a mixer grinder and make a smooth paste.
1. Keep aside.
1. In a saucepan, stir together the cooked pumpkin and the ground coconut mixture.
1. Stir in 1 cup of water, turn on the heat and allow the mixture to come to a boil.
1. The Majjige Huli will begin to thicken.
1. At this point stir in the buttermilk and boil for another minute.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. Now we will temper the Kumbalakai Majjige Huli Heat oil in a small saucepan; add the mustard seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Once the mustard seeds have crackled add cumin seeds and saute for 5 seconds, then add the curry leaves and asafoetida and turn off the heat.
1. Add this tempering to the above Majjige Huli.
1. Serve Kumbalakai Majjige Huli Recipe with Steamed Rice, Carrot Beans Thoran, Pisarna Manga Recipe and Elai Vadam .
