### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese - coarsely grated.
- 1 tablespoon Dried oregano.
- 2 Green zucchini - sliced thin.
- 1/4 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- 1/4 cup Corn flour.
- 1 Whole Egg - whisked.
- Salt - (adjust according to your taste).
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Zucchini Parmesan Fritters Recipe, cut the Zucchini into thin 1/4 inch rounds.
1. Combine the bread crumbs and cheese in a bowl.
1. In another plate add cornflour, black pepper powder and salt, mix well and keep it aside.
1. Don’t add too much salt as the parmesan cheese has also a lot of salt already and zucchini as a vegetable can’t handle too much salt.
1. Break an egg in another bowl and whisk it well till it froths up.
1. Now keep the ingredients ready for pan frying.
1. Toss the zucchini first with cornflour and dust the excess flour.
1. Dip them in egg and finally crumb coat it with the bread crumbs and parmesan cheese mixture.
1. Shake off any excess crumbs and carefully place in the pan.
1. Drizzle oil and pan fry the zucchini fritters until browned and crisp.
1. Serve the Zucchini Parmesan Fritters Recipe along with other dips like Tzatziki Recipe - A Greek Yogurt Dip and Mexican Chilli & Cheesy Bean Dip Recipe to enjoy the crispy fritters.
