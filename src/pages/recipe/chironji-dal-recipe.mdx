### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 2 tablespoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 cup Chironji - soaked in water.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 250 grams Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra) - chopped small.
- 1 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 Green Chilli - slit.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chironji Ki Dal, soak the chironji seeds in water for at least 1 hour and drain the water.
1. Rub the seeds against your palm to remove the outer layer and discard them.
1. Grind the cleaned seeds in mixer to a coarse paste and keep it aside.
1. Heat a flat skillet with oil, add cumin seeds, cardamom, pepper corns and allow it to splutter for few seconds.
1. Add ginger and cook till it softens.
1. Add chopped ladies finger, green chilli, sprinkle little salt and turmeric and cook till the ladies finger is done.
1. This will take about easily 10 minutes.
1. Add spice powders and curd and keep saut√©ing until the water from the curd is all drained out.
1. At the end add the ground chironji seed and mix well.
1. Check for salt and add little water to adjust the consistency.
1. Serve the Chironji Ki Dal Recipe along with Bhatura Recipe (Fried Puffed Yogurt Bread) and some Pickled Onions Recipe to make you breakfast complete.
