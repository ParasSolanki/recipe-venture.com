### Ingredients

- 1 Raw Banana - skin peeled and chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 Green peas (Matar).
- 1 cup Tamarind Water.
- 3 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 cup Kaddu (Parangikai/ Pumpkin) - skin peeled and chopped.
- 1 cup Fresh coconut.
- 1/4 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Goan Style Khatkhatem Recipe pressure cook the dal along with all the vegetables (except green peas) with water in a pressure cooker for 1 whistle.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally by itself.
1. Meanwhile, grind the coconut along with turmeric powder dry red chilli into a smooth paste and keep it aside.
1. Keep the pressure cooker back on the heat and add the ground coconut paste with 1/2 cup water.
1. Check for salt, add the green peas and tamarind water.
1. Leave it on the simmer for 5 minutes until the gravy thickens.
1. Check for salt again and serve.
1. Serve the Goan Style Khatkhatem Recipe along with Pav or tawa parathas to enjoy your everyday lunch in a healthy way.
