### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Heavy whipping cream.
- 200 grams Dark chocolate.
- Cocoa Powder - for dusting.
- 250 grams Bitter sweet chocolate - chopped or grated.
- 1 tablespoon Butter - at room temperature.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin with Decadent Chocolate Truffles Recipe, add chopped chocolates in a large bowl.
1. Add cream and cinnamon powder in a pan and bring it to a boil.
1. Once the cream has come to boil, pour it over the chocolate.
1. Mix and whisk the mixture until it is smooth and shiny.
1. Line up a plate with butter paper and pour the melted chocolate mixture and refrigerate it for about 2 hours or until it is set.
1. After 2 hours, take a spoonful of truffle mixture and roll it into a small ball.
1. Do this as quickly as possible as heat generated in your palm while rolling will start melting the mixture.
1. Place the rolled truffle balls onto a plate and refrigerate for another couple of hours or put it in a freezer for an hour to chill it completely before coating it with chocolate.
1. The next step is coating.
1. Melt the chocolate either in a double-boiler or in a microwave.
1. Add butter into the chocolate mixture and whisk it well until it is melted.
1. When mixture comes to a warm temperature, dip the truffle balls and place it immediately on the tray lined with butter paper.
1. Repeat the same process for the remains chocolate balls.
1. Chill it for further an hour or you can just roll the truffles in a cocoa powder and chill itServe Decadent Chocolate Truffles To your friends and family during some special occasion at your home.
