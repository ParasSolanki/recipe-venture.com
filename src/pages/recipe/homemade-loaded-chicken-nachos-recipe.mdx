### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Mozzarella cheese.
- 150 grams Nachos - 1 standard size pack.
- 1/4 cup Pickled Jalapenos - sliced.
- 1/4 cup Guacamole.
- 1 Chicken breasts - cut into cubes.
- 2 tablespoons Black olives - sliced.
- 1/2 cup Tomato Salsa.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt and Pepper - to sprinkle.
- 1/4 cup Sour cream.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Homemade Loaded Chicken Nachos Recipe , we need to make the Toppings for the Homemade Loaded Chicken Nachos.
1. Below are the recipes for the various toppings.
1. You can click on them and get the recipes and keep them ready.
1. Spicy Mexican Salsa RecipeGuacamole Dip RecipeSour Cream RecipeHeat a skillet with olive oil, add the chicken and stir fry the chicken until well cooked this will take about 5 minutes.
1. Sprinkle some salt and pepper and turn off the flame.
1. Once you have all the dips and other elements ready, it's time to assemble the Nachos.
1. Arrange the Nachos on a large serving plate (ensure it's an microwave/oven proof plate).
1. You can either use one large plate or use 4 quarter plates to make nachos from a 150 gram packet.
1. Sprinkle the chicken cubes, the guacamole, salsa, sour cream, slices of jalapenos, olives and finally the mozzarella cheese.
1. Place this Loaded Chicken Nachos plate into the microwave and turn on the high power mode and wait till the cheese melts.
1. This will take about a minute.
1. Once the cheese melts, remove the Loaded Chicken Nachos from the oven and serve immediately.
