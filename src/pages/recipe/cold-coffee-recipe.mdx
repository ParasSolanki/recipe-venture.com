### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Sugar - powdered.
- Ice cubes - as required.
- 2 cups Milk.
- 2 tablespoon Instant Coffee Powder.
- 1/2 cup Ice cream - vanilla or chocolate (optional).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cold Coffee recipe, firstly keep all the mentioned ingredients ready.
1. Next take a blender and add milk, required sugar and coffee powder.
1. Blend it for about 30 seconds.
1. Open the jar and add ice cubes.
1. If you want a thick consistency, add less ice cubes.
1. Close the jar and blend it again for about 30 to 40 seconds till everything is mixed properly.
1. You can also add 1 tablespoon ice cream in the coffee to add an extra flavour to the coffee.
1. Once it is done, pour it in the glass and sprinkle some coffee powder on the top.
1. Serve Cold Coffee along with Scrambled Egg Sandwich With Cucumber or with any other Sandwich of your choice for a weekday breakfast or enjoy it with your family after a tiring day at work.
