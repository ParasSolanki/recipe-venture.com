### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/4 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - shredded.
- 2 Corn flour tortilla.
- 1/2 Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Chicken breasts - cubed.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/4 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/4 cup Sour cream.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Mexican Chicken Tostadas Recipe, heat oil in a small Kadai.
1. Once the oil is hot, add chicken pieces.
1. Add red chilli powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder and salt to the chicken pieces.
1. Cook chicken over medium heat until the pieces are fried well.
1. Set aside.
1. Alternatively, you can grill chicken in a preheated Oven at 180 degrees Celsius for 15-20 minutes if you are diet conscious.
1. Heat a Tawa over medium flame.
1. Cook corn tortillas until both the sides turn lightly brown.

1. In a corn tortilla, spread sour cream evenly.
1. Place fried chicken pieces above the sour cream.
1. Sprinkle the shredded cabbage on top of the chicken.
1. Season with lime juice.
1. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve chicken tostadas with spicy Mexican salsa recipe on the sides, as an appetizer long with the Cheesy Corn On The Cob Recipe.
