### Ingredients

- Water - to knead the dough.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - boiled & mashed.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - optional.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder - with black pepper powder (adjust according to your taste).
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese).
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 100 grams Buckwheat Flour (Kuttu Ka Atta).
- Rock Salt - to taste.
- Sunflower Oil - to fry.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vrat Ki Kachori Recipe (Savory Buckwheat-Paneer Pastry), start with making the kachori/pastry mix.
1. Mix flour, salt, green chilies, mashed potatoes in a mixing bowl.
1. Add red chilli powder or pepper powder as per preference and mix everything well.
1. Add a little water to knead the dough and knead a soft dough.
1. Once it is done, keep the dough covered for 5 minutes.
1. Meanwhile, we will prepare the paneer filling for Vrat Ki Kachori.
1. For the filling, mash the paneer, along with the rest of ingredients for filling.
1. You can optionally add raisins to oomph up the flavour.
1. Keep it aside.
1. To prepare the Vrat Ki Kachori, heat oil in a Shallow fry pan.
1. Take a small ball of the dough between your palms.
1. The dough will be soft.
1. Press in the center and make an indent.
1. Put the paneer filling in the indent and softly bring the edges together till the center and close into a ball again.
1. Now, press between your palms and flatten the kachori lightly.
1. Take some dry buckwheat flour and lightly cover the kachori on all sides.
1. Make all the kachoris like this.
1. Next, heat some oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Put these kachoris in the wok and shallow fry them in the pan on medium heat, till they are crisp on both sides.
1. Take them out on a kitchen towl to absorb the excess oil.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve this crispy Vrat Ki Kachori Recipe with Paneer filling for evening snacks with No Onion No Garlic Dhaniya Pudina Chutney.
