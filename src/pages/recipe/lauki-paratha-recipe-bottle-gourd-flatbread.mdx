### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1 Bottle gourd (lauki) - peeled and grated.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee - for smearing on parathas.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 2 cups Whole Wheat Flour.
- 2 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lauki Paratha Recipe, peel the outer layer of the lauki, grate and squeeze the excess water and keep aside.
1. Heat a heavy bottomed pan, add the grated lauki and saute until the water completely evaporates from the lauki.
1. This will take around 10 to 15 minutes.
1. Take a big bowl, add the whole wheat flour, evaporated grated lauki, green chilli, grated ginger, all the spice powders, season with salt and make a soft dough using the required amount of water.
1. Divide the dough in equal portions and roll out each ball into a thick medium size paratha.
1. Heat a skillet on medium heat, and place the rolled paratha on top and allow it to cook on one side.
1. After about 10 to 15 seconds, flip the paratha.
1. Smear some ghee to cook the paratha on both the sides Cook until you see golden brown spots on both sides.
1. Prepare the rest of the Parathas in a similar way and serve hot.
1. Serve Lauki Paratha along with Boondi Raita and Aam Ka Achaar for breakfast or for light dinner.
