### Ingredients

- 1 cup Heavy whipping cream.
- 1/2 Apple - peeled and cubed.
- 1/2 cup Condensed Milk - sweetened.
- 1/2 cup Fresh orange juice.
- 4 tablespoons Caster Sugar.
- 1 Canned Cherries - for garnish (optional).
- 1/2 teaspoon Orange Zest (Rind).
- 1 tablespoon Orange Zest (Rind).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Eggless Orange and Apple Ice Cream Recipe, prep and bring all the ingredients together and keep them ready to make the ice cream.
1. Blend orange juice, apple cubes, and condensed milk together in a blender.
1. Pour the mixture into a bowl and fold in the orange zest.
1. Keep aside.
1. In a big bowl, beat heavy whipping cream using an electric beater or wire whisk until stiff peaks.
1. Pour a half of the whipped cream into the blended mixture.
1. Blend it by cutting and folding in further slowly using a spatula.
1. Once this is thoroughly mixed, add the remaining whipped cream into the mixture and repeat cutting and folding.
1. There is no cause to worry even if the zest doesnâ€™t blend in completely since the mixture blends very well upon freezing.
1. Pour this ice cream mixture in a pan or a container.
1. Cover the container (preferably with a silver foil) and freeze in the refrigerator for 5 to 6 hours.
1. Take the Orange and Apple Ice Cream out before 15 minutes of serving.
1. Scoop it out into a bowl or on a cone.
1. Sprinkle some orange zest over it and press a Cherry atop the scoop (Optional step).
1. And here you present the refreshing homemade Eggless Orange and Apple Ice Cream with lots of goodness of fruits for your sweet tooth.
