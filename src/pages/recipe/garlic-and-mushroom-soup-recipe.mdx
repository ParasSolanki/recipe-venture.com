### Ingredients

- 2 Lemon wedges.
- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 7 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 3 sprig Fresh Thyme leaves.
- 1 liter Vegetable stock.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - as per taste.
- Parsley leaves - A fistful.
- 1 Leek - cleaned and finely chopped.
- 200 grams Button mushrooms.
- 2 Whole Wheat Brown Bread - (slices).
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- 3 Button mushrooms - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Garlic and Mushroom Soup Recipe, we will first clean, wash and chop the button mushrooms.
1. Finely chop the garlic and fresh thyme leaves.
1. Set aside.
1. In a saucepan heat 2 tablespoon olive oil; add in chopped garlic and thyme, saute for about a minute until you get the flavors of the garlic.
1. Dont over brown the garlic.
1. Add chopped leek and saute for another 2 - 3 minutes until leeks turn tender and translucent.
1. Finally add in the chopped mushroom and start sauteeing over medium high heat until the mushrooms become soft and cooked.
1. This process takes about 5 to 10 minutes.
1. Saute the mushrooms until most of the moisture is evaporated.
1. Add in the salt and pepper to taste and add hot vegetable stock to the above mushrooms garlic, leek mixture.
1. Once the mixture cools a bit, blend using a blender or a hand mixer until you get a smooth soup like consistency.
1. Give the Garlic and Mushroom Soup a quick boil just before you are ready to serve.
1. Check the salt and pepper levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Method to make the Grilled Toast Topped with MushroomsMeanwhile grill toast in the oven or toaster.
1. Once grilled cut slices into triangles.
1. For the topping, heat olive oil in a frying pan.
1. Saute the chopped onion until tender and lightly browned.
1. Add in the chopped tomato, mushroom, salt and pepper.
1. Mix well and stir fry for a couple of minutes on high heat just so all of them get cooked just a bit.
1. Dont over cook them, as the tomatoes and the mushrooms will release a lot of water and you will have to cook a lot more to evaporate its liquids if that happens.
1. To serve the Garlic and Mushroom Soup, pour a ladle of soup in serving bowl and place the grilled toast topped with mushroom topping by the side.
1. Sprinkle chopped parsley over soup and toast, squeeze lemon juice on topping.
1. Serve hot.
1.
