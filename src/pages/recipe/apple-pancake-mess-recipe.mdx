### Ingredients

- 2 Tablespoon Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 2 teaspoons Baking powder.
- 3 tablespoons Butter - melted.
- 2 Tablespoon Butter (unsalted).
- Salt - a pinch.
- 1 Cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 Whole Egg.
- 1 Cup Milk.
- 2 tablespoons Sugar.
- 2 Tablespoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Fresh Pomegranate Fruit Kernels.
- 2 Apple.
- 1 Tablespoon Chia Seeds.
- 1/2 Tablespoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Apple Pancake Mess Recipe, firstly prepare the pancakes.
1. In order to make the pancakes, take a mixing bowl, crack open eggs and sugar.
1. Beat till it turns light fluffy.
1. Add in milk and beat well.
1. Add in melted butter and mix well.
1. Add in flour, salt, baking powder and mix gently.
1. Once it is combined.
1. Set aside for a minute.
1. Heat a nonstick pan.
1. Once it is hot, pour batter in the middle and let it spread and make sure it covers the whole pan.
1. Cook this on medium high heat till small bubbles form on top.
1. Now flip over and cook for few more seconds till it becomes golden.
1. These pancakes should be thin as keep them warm in a kitchen towel until your start plating.
1. Slice Apples into thin pieces.
1. In a large skillet, heat the butter until bubbling.
1. Add the apple wedges to the skillet and cook over medium high heat for approximately 4-5 minutes or until tender and beginning to brown.
1. Turn several times to brown evenly.
1. Add powdered cinnamon and brown sugar and continue to cook until the syrup starts to caramelize.
1. Add sea salt and turn off the heat.
1. Now tear the pancakes into thin strips using your hands and start plating.
1. You have a make a layer a layer of pancake strips followed by caramalized apples.
1. Repeat the process until you consume all the strips and apples.
1. In the end, sprinkle chia seeds, pomegranate, coconut flakes and then add dollops of yogurt.
1. Enjoy !Serve Apple Pancake Mess Recipe along with a healthy smoothie like Peanut Butter Oats & Banana Smoothie Recipe for breakfast.
