### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 cloves Garlic - finely minced or crushed.
- Baby spinach - Handful.
- 2 tablespoon Red Wine Vinaigrette.
- 1/2 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 5 Basil leaves - roughly torn.
- Salt - to taste.
- 7 Cherry tomatoes - halved.
- 2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- 4 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 8 Black olives - halved (pitted).
- 3 tablespoon Feta Cheese.
- 3/4 cup Orzo pasta - cooked.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Mediterranean Orzo Pasta Salad with Feta Vinaigrette, first, place the Feta cheese in a small mixing bowl.
1. Whisk it using a fork or a small whisk till it becomes creamy.
1. Add the other ingredients under Vinaigrette and whisk till everything blends together.
1. Adjust the quantity of vegetables according to your taste.
1. In a salad bowl, place the pasta, tomatoes, pepper, olives, spinach and Basil.
1. Toss together till everything mixes properly.
1. Adjust quantity of salt and pepper if required.
1. Serve Mediterranean Orzo Pasta Salad with Feta Vinaigrette with Cheesy Spaghetti Verdi Pasta Recipe With Roasted Garlic for a perfect meal.
