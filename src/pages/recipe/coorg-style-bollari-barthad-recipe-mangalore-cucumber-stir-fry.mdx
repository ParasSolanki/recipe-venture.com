### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 3 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Mangalorean cucumber - chopped to chunks.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 10 Curry leaves.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - broken.
- 2 cloves Garlic - crushed.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 3/4 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Coorg Style Bollari Barthad Recipe (Mangalore Cucumber Stir Fry), halve the cucumbers and scoop out the seeds and rind.
1. Cut it into small pieces without peeling.
1. Heat oil in a kadai and add mustard seeds, allow them to splutter.
1. Once they do splutter, on a medium heat, add urad dal to the pan immediately.
1. Once the urad dal is crisp and changes colour, add the cumin seeds, curry leaves and broken red chilli and saute a bit till the curry leaves sizzle.
1. Immediately add crushed garlic cloves and onions.
1. Saute them till the onions are translucent.
1. Add the diced Mangalorean cucumber, turmeric, salt and mix.
1. Cook with the lid closed.
1. Sprinkle a little water if you feel the barthad needs more water to cook.
1. While the cucumber is cooking, in a mixer, grind coriander seeds, cumin seeds and red chillies with a bit water.
1. Finally add fresh coconut and pulse once or twice.
1. When cucumber is about 3/4th done, add the ground masala paste and jaggery.
1. Mix and cook till all the water has been absorbed .
1. Serve Coorg Style Bollari Barthad Recipe (Mangalore Cucumber Stir Fry) along with Steamed Rice, Nuggekai Kharbyaali Recipe (North Karnataka Spiced Drumstick Sambar), Pineapple Gojju Recipe, and Phulka for a complete meal.
