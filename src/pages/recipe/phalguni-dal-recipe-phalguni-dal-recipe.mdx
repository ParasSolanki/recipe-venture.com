### Ingredients

-  1 tsp rye.
-  1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds.
-  2 tsp mustard oil.
-  2 cloves garlic - grated.
-  1 onion - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup lentils - wash.
-  asafoetida - a pinch.
-  1 tsp garam masala powder.
-  1/4 tsp Kalonji seeds.
-  1/2 inch ginger - grated.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  2 green chillies - Cut.
-  1/2 tsp fennel.
-  1/4 cup green peas.
-  1/2 tsp cumin seeds.
-  1 tomato - finely chopped.
-  1/2 tsp fennel seeds.

### Instructions

1. To make Falguni Dal recipe, first wash the lentil lentils thoroughly.
1. Now put lentils, tomatoes, onions, ginger, garlic, 1-1 / 2 cups of water in a pressure cooker and turn off the cooker.
1. Turn on the gas and cook till 3 cities come.
1. Turn off the gas and let the pressure drain automatically.
1. Open the cooker and mash the lentils.
1. Keep it separately.
1. Now heat 2 tsp mustard oil in a pan for tempering.
1. Add rai, fenugreek seeds, fennel seeds, cumin, kalonji and cook till the cumin seeds crackle.
1. Now add green chilies and cook for 1 minute.
1. Add asafetida, turmeric powder and green peas, cover the pan and cook on medium heat for 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes add masoor dal, garam masala, salt and mix well.
1. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Garnish with green coriander and serve.
1. Serve Phalguni Dal with Bhindi Masala, Bundi Raita and Phulka for a day's dinner.
