### Ingredients

-  oil - as per use.
-  2 inch ginger.
-  1 tsp turmeric powder.
-  1 tsp red chilli powder.
-  1 cup potatoes - boiled.
-  6 cloves garlic.
-  2 bay leaves.
-  1 inch cinnamon.
-  cut 3 sprigs of mint - 1/2 cup onion - finely chopped and cook till it turns brown..
-  make 1 cup tomato-puree.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 onion - chopped Take 2 green chilies.
-  1/2 cup carrots - chopped steam.
-  1 cup cabbage - steamed chopped.
-  1 Small spoon coriander powder.
-  1 cup curd.
-  4 cardamom.
-  2 cups rice - wash.
- 1/2 cup green beans - chopped steam.

### Instructions

1. To make Hyderabadi Vegetable Biryani Recipe, first put the rice in a saucepan with salt and oil in 2 cups of water and cook till the rice is half cooked.
1. Now add carrots, green beans, potatoes, cauliflower in a steamer and cook till it becomes soft.
1. Keep this aside.
1. Now add cardamom, cinnamon, long and grind it in a Hamdasta.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now heat 2 tablespoons oil in a large saucepan.
1. Add garlic paste onion ginger and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Now add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, ground cinnamon powder, bay leaves and cook for 1 minute.
1. Now add tomato puree and cook it for 5 minutes.
1. Now add curd and let it cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Now add mint leaves, potatoes, cooked vegetables, half cooked rice, mint leaves, salt to taste and mix it and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Reduce the flame, cover the saucepan and cook until it is well cooked.
1. Turn off the gas and leave it covered for 5 minutes.
1. Garnish with fried onions and serve.
1. Serve the Hyderabadi Vegetable Biryani Recipe with Burani Raita and Mirchi Salon for dinner.
