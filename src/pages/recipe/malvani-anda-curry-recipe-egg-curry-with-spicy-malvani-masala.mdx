### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Kokum (Malabar Tamarind).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 teaspoons Malwani masala.
- 1/4 cup Cashew nuts - soaked in warm water.
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/2 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 2 teaspoons Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 4 Whole Eggs - hard boiled.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Malvani Anda Curry Recipe, boil the eggs in a saucepan for 10-12 minutes, drain water, peel the eggs, halve them and keep the eggs aside.
1. Heat a little oil in a kadai, add the sliced onions.
1. On a medium heat, saute till the onions are caramelized.
1. Once the onions are caramelized, grind it to a smooth paste along with soaked cashew nuts and fresh grated coconut using a mixer grinder and keep aside.
1. Heat a tablespoon of oil in the same pan on a medium heat, add the cumin seeds and let it sizzle.
1. Add the curry leaves and slit green chilli and let it splutter.
1. Add the ginger garlic paste and cook until the raw smell goes away.
1. This will take 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Add the ground onion, cashew and coconut mix and cook for a minute.
1. Now add the red chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, malvani masala and cook until the curry gravy leaves the sides of the pan and deep in color.
1. Add the tomato puree and cook until the gravy is cooked well.
1. Add a cup of water, kokum, season with salt and let the gravy simmer for about 10 minutes on low heat.
1. Now, add the slit boiled eggs into the gravy, check for salt and spices, adjust if required.
1. Simmer for another 5 minutes and switch off the heat.
1. Serve Malvani Anda Curry Recipe (Egg Curry With Spicy Malvani Masala) along with Cauliflower & Cucumber Raita Recipe and Phulkas or Sweet Potato Thepla for a hearty weeknight dinner.
