### Ingredients

- 1 Raw Banana.
- 1 Tomato.
- 2 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 teaspoon Panch Phoran Masala.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Bengali Style Kanch kolar Kofta Curry Recipe (Green Plantain Kofta Curry Recipe) by boiling the peeled raw banana and potatoes in a pressure cooker for 4 whistles and release the pressure naturally.
1. In the mean while heat the pan with oil and saut√© the tomatoes till it is soft and mushy.
1.  Add salt and sprinkle little sugar to reduce the tanginess.
1. Then let it cool down and grind the tomato into a puree consistency.
1. Once the potatoes and raw bananas are cooked, smash it along with the all the masala powders and make it into a ball.
1. Pan Fry them on the Kuzi Paniyaram Pan till it is brown evenly.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat a kadai with oil, add bay leaf and crackle cumin seeds, then add the tomato puree, add in all the spice powders mentioned in the gravy list.
1. Add 1 cup of water and let it boil for about 15 minutes.
1. Once it thickens slightly add in the koftas and sprinkle with chopped coriander seeds.
1. Serve the Bengali Style Kanch kolar Kofta Curry Recipe (Green Plantain Kofta Curry Recipe) with hot Phulkas and steam rice.
