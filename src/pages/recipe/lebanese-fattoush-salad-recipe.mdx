### Ingredients

- 1 Iceberg lettuce - small bunch.
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 2 Tomato - cut into chunks.
- 1/4 cup Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish) - sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Fresh Thyme leaves - finely chopped.
- 100 gram Feta Cheese - crumbled (optional).
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/4 cup Parsley leaves - finely chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 2 Whole Wheat Pita Bread.
- Few Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - roughly chopped.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - diced.
- 1 clove Garlic - grated.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Red Wine Vinaigrette.
- 1 Cucumber - peeled.
- 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.

### Instructions

1. To start making Lebanese Fattoush Salad, first cut the pita bread into small wedges.
1. In a medium bowl, toss the pita wedges with a tablespoon of olive oil.
1. Season with salt and pepper as per taste.
1. Heat the grill pan over medium heat.
1. Spread out the pita wedges on the grill pan and grill pita wedges till crisp on both sides.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. Remove from the pan and keep aside until required.
1. In a salad bowl add the chopped green onions, lettuce, parsley, tomatoes, cucumber, radish, bell pepper.
1. Toss to combine.
1. Set aside while you prepare the dressing.
1. To make the dressing, combine the remaining olive oil, grated garlic, lemon juice, red wine vinegar, thyme, chili powder in a bowl or jar with a tight fitting lid.
1. Shake the jar to combine the dressing ingredients or whisk until combined well.
1. Season with salt and pepper.
1. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and gently toss the vegetables in the dressing.
1. Add the toasted pita wedges to the salad.
1. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese over the salad.
1. Serve Lebanese Fattoush Salad along with Lebanese Style Mujaddara and Creme Caramel Flan for a complete weeknight dinner.
