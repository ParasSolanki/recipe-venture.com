### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 tablespoon Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - crushed to garnish.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Peanut oil - to garnish.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1-1/2 cups Water.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 3-4 tablespoon Rice flour.
- 2-3 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1/4 cup Buttermilk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Ukadpendi, first whisk the buttermilk with the rice flour till there are no lumps.
1. Heat oil in a wok/pan and add the mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
1. and let them splutter.
1. Once they splutter add the curry leaves, green chillies and hing and carefully add the water.
1. Add salt and sugar, and let the water boil.
1. Now add the rice flour slurry, stirring constantly so that lumps are not formed.
1. Cook on low flame, stirring continuously, till the mixture thickens and the rice flour is cooked.
1. Once done turn off the heat and transfer Ukadpendi into serving bowl.
1. You can top it with some peanut oil if you like before serving it.
