### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 Tomato.
- 2 cups Basmati rice.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - for garnish.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 4 Fresh Red Chilli.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Dates - pitted.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Pakistani Chickpeas Pulao With Sweet Hot Date Onion Chutney, firstly soak chick peas for an hour.
1. Cook chickpeas in a pressure cooker with water for 3 whistles.
1. Drain the water from the cooked chickpeas, mix salt and keep it aside.
1. On the other hand, soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
1. In a medium pan/pressure cooker, heat oil over medium heat.
1. Add onions, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, bay leaf and onion.
1. Sauté onion until they become soft and transparent.
1. Once the onions are cooked, add ginger garlic paste and mix well.
1. Add cooked chickpeas, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder and mix everything properly.
1. Cook for about a minute.
1. After a minute, add soaked basmati rice and required amount of water.
1. Cover and cook until the water is drained and rice is cooked well.
1. In case of using pressure cooker, cook over medium heat for 2 whistles.
1. Garnish it with coriander.
1. The next step is to make the Sweet Hot Date Onion Chutney.
1. Firstly, soak the red chilies in a cup of hot water for 10 minutes and keep it aside.
1. Meanwhile, heat oil in a frying pan and add onions.
1. Add required amount of salt.
1. Sauté until the onions become slightly brown.
1. Once browned, remove from the stovetop and keep them aside.
1. Chop dates finely.
1. It’s good to soak dates in water for 10 minutes so that it can be chopped easily.
1. Drain water from chilies.
1. Grind chopped dates and chilies in a mixer for about 5 minutes.
1. Add the onion mixture and grind to a fine smooth paste in a mixer grinder and the chutney is ready to be served.
1. Serve the Pakistani Chickpeas Pulao With Sweet Hot Date Onion Chutney, Burani Raita and a roasted Papad for a delicious meal.
