### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Beetroot - peeled.
- 1 clove Garlic.
- 1/2 cup Walnuts - chopped.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 2 Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Roasted Beet and Walnut Dip or Spread Recipe, steam the peeled beets.
1. I like steaming the beets before roasting them, as it cuts down the time taken to roast the beets.
1. Roasting the beetroots can be done on the stovetop in a wok or in the oven.
1. The process of roasting brings out the rustic flavors of the vegetable.
1. Heat a tablespoon of olive oil in a wok; add in the garlic cloves, the spring onions and the steamed beet roots.
1. Stir fry on medium high heat until you get a nice roasted flavors from the beet.
1. The process will not take more than 4 to 5 minutes.
1. Once the beetroots are well roasted, we will blend the roasted beets along with the remaining ingredients, including the juice from one lemon and pulse to get a slightly coarse, almost smooth dip (spread).
1. You can drizzle in some olive oil while pulsing to give it a smoother texture.
1. Transfer the dip to a serving dish and stir in the chopped coriander leaves.
1. Check the salt and spice levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. You can use the Roasted Beet & Walnut Dip as a spread for kids sandwiches or even wraps.
1. It tastes delicious and is wholesome too.
1. Serve the Roasted Beet & Walnut Dip Recipe along with Homemade pita bread to enjoy your Snack.
