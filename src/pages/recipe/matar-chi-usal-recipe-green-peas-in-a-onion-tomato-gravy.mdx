### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 1 Tomato.
- 2 Onions.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoons Garam masala powder.
- 1/2 inch Ginger.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder - preferably byadgi chilli powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 2 cups Green peas (Matar) - fresh preferably.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Matar Chi Usal Recipe Recipe, keep all the ingredients ready.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, heat oil on medium flame.
1. Once its hot, add mustard seeds.
1. As soon as they splutter, add the asafoetida.
1. Next, add the onions and saute.
1. When the onions turn translucent, add the ginger garlic paste and mix well.
1. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
1. When the onions are well cooked, add the turmeric powder, red chilli powder and saute.
1. Cookf or about a minute or two.
1. Now, add the drained cubed potatoes and mix.
1. Add 1 cup of water to cover the potatoes and bring to a boil.
1. Cover and cook until the potatoes are half cooked.
1. This will take about 10-12 minutes.
1. Once the potatoes are half cooked, add green peas, chopped tomatoes and mix well.
1. Adjust the consistency of the gravy and cover and let the Matar Chi Usal cook.
1. Finally.
1. add the garam masala and salt to taste.
1. Cover and cook until you get the desired thickness of the Matar Chi Usal gravy.
1. Turn off the heat and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Matar Chi Usal is ready to be served.
1. Serve Matar Chi Usal Recipe, along with Rotis and Carrot Cucumber Tomato Salad with Lemon and Coriander for a delectable weeknight dinner.
