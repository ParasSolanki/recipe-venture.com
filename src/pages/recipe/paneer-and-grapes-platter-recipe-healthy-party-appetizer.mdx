### Ingredients

- 5 teaspoons Dill leaves - chopped.
- 20 Black grapes - washed and halved.
- 2 teaspoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 20 Toothpicks.
- 2 teaspoons Canned Cherries - for garnish.
- 200 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cut into dices.
- 2 teaspoons Parsley leaves - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make the Paneer And Grapes Platter, cut the paneer into 1 inch cubes.
1. You can either cut it into small cubes or use a shaped cutter.
1. In a bowl, mix in the olive oil, chopped dill and parsley leaves.
1. Use a brush to oil the paneer cubes/pieces.
1. Place a piece of halved grape and a piece of chopped cherry on top of the paneer cube.
1. Pass a toothpick through the centre of the cherry, grape and paneer stack.
1. You can even finely chop cherries and add as a garnish.
1. It is your choice.
