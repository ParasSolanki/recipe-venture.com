### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - diced small.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - diced small.
- 4 Whole Eggs - boiled with salt.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Awadhi Style Nargisi Kofta Recipe (Boiled Egg Wrapped With Spiced Potato Recipe) by heating a saucepan with oil and sauté the vegetables except the potatoes, mentioned in the list for the outer layer.
1. Once the vegetables are cooked, add all the spice powders and give it a toss.
1. Season it with salt and pepper.
1. Cover and cook it for 2 more minutes.
1. Add these sautéed vegetables to the boiled potatoes and give it a nice mash using a potato masher and bind all the ingredients into a paste.
1. Take the boiled egg and start wrapping the egg with this potato paste as a coating.
1. Tightly seal the potato mixture.
1. Coat it with bread crumbs evenly and heat a flat bottomed pan with oil and carefully place these koftas and evenly fry them till it is nice and brown all over.
1. Serve the Awadhi Style Nargisi Kofta (Boiled Egg Wrapped With Spiced Potato) as a party appetizer.
