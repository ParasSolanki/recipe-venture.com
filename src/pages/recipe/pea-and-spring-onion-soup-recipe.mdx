### Ingredients

- 1 cup Spring Onion Greens - roughly chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 500 ml Vegetable stock.
- 2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 4 cloves Garlic - smashed.
- 2 tablespoon Fresh cream.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 4 - 5 Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - chopped.
- 2 cup Green peas (Matar) - steamed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Pea And Spring Onion Soup, separate greens from spring onion.
1. Chop the spring onion and set aside.
1. Similarly chop the spring onion greens and keep in a separate bowl.
1. In a large pot simmer vegetable stock over low flame so that it remain hot while adding to the soup.
1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat; add chopped spring onion and garlic.
1. Sauté until onion turn translucent but not brown for about 1 to 2 minutes.
1. Once cooked the spring onions are through, add peas, spring greens and sauté for 2 minutes more.
1. Season with salt and pepper.
1. Puree the green peas mixture in a mixer along with the vegetable stock into a smooth puree.
1. Check the salt and pepper and adjust to suit your taste.
1. When you are ready to serve the Pea and Spring Onion Soup , bring the soup to a brisk boil over Saucepan.
1. Serve Pea And Spring Onion Soup along with Whole Wheat Rosemary Focaccia Bread as a weeknight meal appetizer.
