### Ingredients

- 500 grams Tinda - Cut into round and straight.
-  1-1 / 2 teaspoon Amchoor powder - add as per your requirement.
-  1 teaspoon turmeric powder .
-  1 tablespoon jaggery.
-  2 tablespoons oil.
-  1 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  peanuts - take it out.
-  4 sprigs of green coriander - chop.
-  1 teaspoon cumin powder.
-  1/2 teaspoon mustard.
-  5 curry leaves - break it.
-  salt to taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  Asafoetida - a pinch.
-  1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To make sour sweet tinda vegetable, first cut the tinde.
1. Now add tinda, salt, 2 tablespoons water in a pressure cooker.
1. Turn off the cooker and cook till 2 cities come.
1. Place the cooker under water and take out its pressure.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. After the oil is hot, add mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
1. Allow to cook for 10 seconds.
1. Now add curry leaves, cooked vegetables, salt and other ingredients.
1. Do not add baked peanuts and coriander.
1. Mix well.
1. Cook until the tinda is cooked.
1. After cooking add peanuts and coriander.
1. Mix and serve.
1. Serve the sweet and sweet tinda curry with Gujarati lentils and gourd theplas for dinner.
