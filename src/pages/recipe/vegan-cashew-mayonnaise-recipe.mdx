### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard sauce.
- 2 tablespoons Apple cider vinegar.
- 2 teaspoons Date syrup.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 2 teaspoons Dill leaves - finely chopped (optional).
- 1 cup Cashew nuts - soaked for at least 4 hours.
- 1 clove Garlic - minced.
- 1/2 Salt.
- 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Vegan Cashew Mayonnaise Recipe, soak the cashews with enough water overnight.
1. Drain excess water.
1. Get prep with the other ingredients as well.
1. Add Cashews to a food processor/blender along with all the other ingredients except dill leaves and blend it smooth with 1/4 cup of water.
1. Top the creamy Vegan Cashew Mayonnaise with dill leaves and serve with your sandwich.
