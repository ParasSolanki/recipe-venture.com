### Ingredients

- Ghee - or oil for cooking.
- 1/4 cup Jaggery.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut.
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1 cup Rice - soaked overnight and ground.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Andhra Style Paakundalu by soaking the rice in water for overnight.
1. Add rice and little bit of water and grind it into a smooth batter.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat a sauce pan with jaggery, allow the jaggery to melt and add the cardamom powder and freshly grated coconut and mix well.
1. Add this into the rice batter and mix.
1. You can add little water to create thick batter to pour it into a kuzhi paniyaram pan.
1. Heat a paniyaram pan, add ghee or oil in each depression and pour some batter into it.
1. Cook the Sweet Paniyarams till they turn light brown from one side, then flip each paniyaram using a skewer and cook them the other side.
1. Add ghee or oil as needed.
1. Once cooked and they light brown from both sides, transfer them to a plate and serve hot.
1. Serve the Andhra Style Paakundalu along with kesari bath or Carrot Badam Kheer Recipe to enjoy your festive mood in a joyous way with your friends and family.
