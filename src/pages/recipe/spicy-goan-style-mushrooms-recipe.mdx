### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 250 grams Button mushrooms.
- 3 cloves Garlic - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/4 cup Rice flour.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spicy Goan Style Mushrooms Recipe, first wash the mushrooms and pat them dry.
1. Cut them into halves lengthwise along with the stem.
1. If the mushroom is big, cut them into quarters.
1. In another bowl, combine the ingredients for coating the mushrooms.
1. Add the Mushrooms into this coating mixture and toss them well.
1. Heat a couple of tablespoons of oil in a shallow frying pan over medium heat.
1. Add the sooji coated mushrooms into the pan and fry it on low flame, flipping over occasionally until crisp and browned on all sides.
1. This would take about 15 minutes.
1. Once browned and crispy, remove from the pan and serve.
1. Serve the Stir Fried Spicy Goan Style Mushrooms Recipe as an appetizer or side dish for dinner.
1. You can also optionally top these mushrooms with sliced onions, green chilies and lemon to add to the tang.
1. Serve Spicy Goan Style Mushrooms along with Whole Wheat Lachha Paratha and Palak Raita for a weekday meal.
