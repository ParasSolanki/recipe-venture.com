### Ingredients

- 15 Amla (Nellikai/ Gooseberry) - medium size.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- 4 Green Chillies.
- Asafoetida (hing) - A pinch.
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - as per taste.
- Sunflower Oil - for tempering.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Andhra Style Usirikaya Pachadi recipe, wash the amla in running water and place them in a pressure cooker.
1. Add water and boil the amlas till soft or till the cooker release 2 whistles.
1. Once cooled, remove the seeds from them.
1. Add the boiled amla, salt, green chillies & a pinch of asafoetida in the blender and blend until smooth.
1. Take some oil for tempering in a pan, add mustard seeds to till.
1. Once they start to crackle, add the dry red chilies and fry for a minute.
1. Switch off the flame.
1. Add the tempering to the chutney.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve Andhra Style Usirikaya Pachadi with Mixed Vegetable Sambar and Steamed Rice for a weekday meal.
