### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 4 tablespoon Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - crushed.
- 1 cup Raw papaya - grated.
- 1 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cup Cooked rice.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 4 tablespoon Thai Yellow Curry Paste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thai Style Vegetables & Raw Papaya Rice Recipe, heat oil in a kadai on medium flame, add ginger and garlic, saute for 30 seconds until it has slightly changed the color.
1. Once that is done, you can add chopped bell peppers and saute for a couple of  minutes.
1. Add chopped cabbage and sprinkle some salt and saute till the cabbage becomes slightly soft.
1. After the cabbage is cooked you can now add grated papaya and saute till they turn soft.
1. Add thai yellow curry paste and mix it well into the vegetables.
1. At this stage you can add the cooked rice and toss well into the vegetables and check for salt.
1. If the seasoning is perfect, switch off the heat and add chopped coriander leaves and peanuts and serve hot.
1. Serve the Thai Style Vegetables & Raw Papaya Rice Recipe along with salad or Spicy Stir Fried Broccoli to make it a delicious meal for your lunch box.
