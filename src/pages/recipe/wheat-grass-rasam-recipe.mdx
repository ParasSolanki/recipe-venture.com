### Ingredients

- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Water - as required.
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- Salt - to taste.
- Curry leaves - Few.
- 20 grams Tamarind.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Rasam Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 8 Cherry tomatoes.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - Few.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 100 grams Wheat Grass.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Wheat Grass Rasam recipe, wash the wheat grass thoroughly.
1. Soak Tamarind in water and take the juice out of it.
1. Blend wheat grass and tamarind water.
1. Add additional water if needed but do not go beyond 500 ml.
1. Strain the wheat grass juice and throw the extra fibre which comes out after straining.
1. Take a heavy bottomed pan and add hing, tomatoes, wheat grass & tamarind juice, turmeric, salt, curry leaves and allow it to boil.
1. Once it is boiling, add rasam powder and switch off the gas once it becomes frothy.
1. Add ghee in a tadka pan and add, mustard seeds, red chilli, garlic and cumin seeds and add them to the rasam.
1. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
1. Serve Wheat Grass Rasam with hot steamed rice and Cauliflower Thoran for a comfort meal.
