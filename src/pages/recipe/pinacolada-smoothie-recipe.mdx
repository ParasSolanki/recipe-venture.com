### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Ripe Bananas - heaped.
- 4 tablespoons Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- Pineapple - chunks as required.
- 1/2 cup Pineapple - heaped.
- Dessicated Coconut - as required.
- Fresh Cherries - as required.
- 5 Dried Figs.
- 1/8 teaspoon Pineapple Essence.
- 1 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 2 tablespoons Dessicated Coconut - or fresh coconut.
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 1 cup Coconut milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pinacolada Smoothie recipe, soak the figs in warm water for 15 to 20 minutes.
1. Add in all the ingredients to a blender & grind them into smooth paste.
1. Super delicious and healthy Pinacolada Smoothie is ready to serve.
1. Serve Pinacolada Smoothie along with some tea time snacks such as Mumbai Style Spicy Murmura Recipe or Ragi Masala Twisters.
1. You can also serve this to your sudden guests at home.
