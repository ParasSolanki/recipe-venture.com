### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - roughly pounded using pestle and mortar.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Green Chilli - roughly chopped.
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - for garnish.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli flakes.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 500 grams Tinda (Apple Gourd) - sliced.
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pakistani Style Tinde Ki Tarkari Recipe (Apple Gourds Cooked With Tomatoes), wash, dry and cut the apple gourd into thin slices discarding the seeds portion in the centre.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan/kadhai on medium heat, add the mustard seeds and let it crackle.
1. Add the dry red chillies, pounded garlic cloves and saute until the oil becomes fragrant.
1. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they turns soft and a little mushy.
1. Once the tomatoes are soft and mushy, add the sliced apple gourd/tinda, season with salt and cook until the gourds are half cooked.
1. Now, add in the red chilli flakes, mix well and let the apple gourd cook until done with the lid closed.
1. Once done, switch off heat and garnish the Tinde Ki Tarkari with coriander leaves and green chilli slices.
1. Serve Pakistani Style Tinde Ki Tarkari Recipe (Apple Gourds Cooked With Tomatoes) along with Methi Ragi Soy Wheat Thepla Recipe and Kale Chane Ki Kadhi Recipe for a weeknight dinner.
