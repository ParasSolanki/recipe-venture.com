### Ingredients

- 1/2 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - julienned.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 tablespoon Dried oregano.
- 1 Corn flour tortilla - wheat tortilla.
- 1 Tomato - sliced.
- 2 tablespoon Homemade Pizza And Pasta Sauce.
- 1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese - shredded.
- 1/2 Onion - julienned.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tortilla Pizza With Tomato, Onion And Capsicum Recipe, prepare all the ingredients and place the tortilla on a baking tray.
1. Pre-heat the oven at 180 degrees celsius for 5 minutes.
1. Meanwhile, spread the pizza sauce all over tortilla.
1. Once you spread the sauce, now sprinkle shredded cheese all over the tortilla.
1. Next, plate the sliced tomatoes 2 inches apart from each other.
1. Now add onion and capsicum on the top of tomatoes.
1. Again sprinkle chesse all over the vegetables.
1. Once the oven is preheated, place the baking tray with tortilla pizza inside the oven and bake for 8-10 minutes or until the cheese is completely melted and the edges appear crisp.
1. Once the cheese has melted completely, take the tortilla pizza out and garnish with oregano and red chili flakes.
1. Serve the Tortilla Pizza With Tomato, Onion And Capsicum along with a Tomato Noodle Soup and a Rick Dark Chocolate Mousse to end the delicious meal.
