### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- Ghee - as required + for roasting kokhi.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Anardana Powder (Pomegranate Seed Powder) - dry and slightly crushed.
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped (adjustable).
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Masalewari Koki recipe, take wheat flour in a big bowl.
1. Add onion, ginger, green chilli, herbs, carom seeds, cumin seeds, dry pomegranate seeds, asafoetida, salt, red chilli powder and turmeric powder.
1. Mix everything properly.
1. Next, add water little by little and make a soft dough.
1. Add 2 tablespoons ghee, knead again, cover and keep the dough aside for 10 to 15 minutes to set.
1. After 10 to 15 minutes, knead the dough again, until smooth.
1. Preheat tawa over medium flame.
1. Meanwhile, make orange sized balls from the dough.
1. Dust dry flour and make 3-4 inch thick roti using rolling pin.
1. Place the roti on the hot tawa.
1. Cook both sides on medium flame till its crisp.
1. Brush ghee generously on both sides.
1. Once done, take off the flame and serve it hot.
1. Serve Masalewari Koki along with Boondi Raita and Dhaniya Pudina Chutney for breakfast.
