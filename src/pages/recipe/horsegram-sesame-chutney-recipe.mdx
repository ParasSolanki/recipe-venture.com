### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Horse Gram Dal (Kollu/ Kulith).
- 1/2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 6 Pearl onions (Sambar Onions).
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 tablespoons Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1/2 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 4 Fresh Red Chilli.
- 20 grams Tamarind - lemon sized.
- 1/2 cup Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 2 tablespoons Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 6 cloves Garlic.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Horsegram Sesame Chutney Recipe, dry roast Horsegram and sesame seeds separately in medium flame until it turns a dull brown hue.
1. Turn off the flame and keep aside.
1. On a tawa, warm a tablespoon of cooking oil and add Tur dal, Channa dal, Fenugreek seeds and Coriander seeds.
1. Roast until golden and add red chilli, pearl onions, garlic and Tamarind to it.
1. Roast everything for a minute and allow it to cool.
1. Once it cools, add it to blender along with roasted horse gram and sesame.
1. Next add grated coconut, required salt and required water and grind everything into smooth paste.
1. Transfer into serving bowl.
1. Heat a tadka/tempering pan with oil, add mustard seeds, chana dal and Urad dal.
1. Once it sputters add curry leaves.
1. Stir well and add this tempering to grounded chutney.
1. Serve Horsegram Sesame Chutney along with Oats And Coconut Dosa Recipe and Vengaya Sambar for a healthy and sumptuous Sunday morning breakfast.
