### Ingredients

- 2 sprig Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens).
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1/4 cup Rice - boiled.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1/2 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- Black pepper powder - for seasoning.
- 2 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Anhui Style Egg Dumpling by half cooking the rice in a pressure cooker.
1. Then release the pressure naturally.
1. Heat a small skillet, add oil and then add ginger, garlic, onions fry for few minutes.
1. Then add the remaining sauce.
1. That is mentioned along with sugar.
1. Transfer the cooked rice on to the pan, season it with salt and pepper, and toss it well.
1. Then at the end toss it along with some spring onions.
1. Keep it aside.
1. In a bowl, crack 2 eggs, season it well and add chopped ginger.
1. Beat till it is mixed well.
1. Heat another small skillet with little oil.
1. Add two tablespoon of egg mixture in the middle of the pan, till it becomes a small circle.
1. Then place a tablespoon of the rice mixture on one end and fold over the egg.
1. Leave it on the pan for 10 seconds and then serve.
1. Serve the Anhui Style Egg Dumpling as a Part appetizer or can be eaten along with Sichuan Style fried rice.
