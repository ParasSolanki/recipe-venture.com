### Ingredients

- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 cup Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Sweet corn - coarsely ground.
- Salt - to taste.
- 3 tablespoons Sriracha sauce.
- Water - to knead.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Corn Spring Onion Momo Recipe, first we will make the momo dough.
1. To make the momo doughIn a mixing bowl, take maida add water slowly and knead to form a medium stiff dough.
1. Ensure the dough is smooth and not sticky.
1. Keep the momo dough covered for an hour before making the corn stuffing.
1. For the Corn Spring Onion Momo Stuffing Heat a sauce pan with oil, add coarsely ground sweet corn and stir fry until the corn is cooked.
1. Add in spring onions, sriracha sauce, salt, pepper powder and saute well.
1. Cook for a few minutes until the ingredients are well combined and turn off the heat.
1. Keep the filling aside to cool down.
1. To make the Corn Spring Onion MomoTake a bamboo dimsum maker, or the regular idli steamer and fill it with water and place it over a medium heat.
1. Later divide the momo dough into equal lemon size portions.
1. Take each ball and start rolling it out on a surface.
1. Roll out the dough as thin as you can so that the outer layer of the momo is thin and delicate.
1. In every flattened dough leaf, add 1 tablespoon of the Corn Spring Onion Momo filling and fold the edges to semi circles and start pleating to give a nice characteristic pleats.
1. Do the same with all the rest of the dough and filling.
1. Keep the completed Corn Spring Onion Momo under a moist cloth to prevent drying.
1. Grease the steamer with oil or place butter paper before placing Corn Spring Onion Momo.
1. This will  prevent it from sticking.
1. If using traditional steamer it has own boiler or if using bamboo steamer then boil water in a shallow pan and when water starts to boil, place the bamboo steamer on it and steam the Corn Spring Onion Momo for 10 - 15 minutes until the skin will begin to shine.
1. As soon as you notice this, turn off the heat.
1. Transfer the Corn Spring Onion Momo from the steamer into a serving platter and serve hot.
1. Serve the Corn Spring Onion Momo Recipe along with Peanut Chilli Dipping Sauce Recipe and Vegetarian Thukpa Recipe  for a delicious weekend dinner.
