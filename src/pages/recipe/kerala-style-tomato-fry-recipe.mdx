### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 4 Tomatoes - chopped (big pieces).
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/2 tablespoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Onions - thinly sliced.
- 3 Dry Red Chillies - torn into pieces.
- 5 Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kerala Tomato Fry Recipe, heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add mustard seeds, dry red chillies and let the mustard seeds crackle.
1. Once mustard seeds start to crackle, add curry leaves, green chillies and onions.
1. Saute it for 1 minute and add ginger garlic paste.
1. Saute it again till onion turn into golden brown in colour.
1. Next add kashmiri red chilli and turmeric powder.
1. Mix well and add the chopped tomatoes.
1. Let it cook 5 for 7 minutes till the tomatoes turn soft.
1. Add the required salt and cook for another few seconds.
1. Turn off the flame, transfer it in a bowl and garnish it with fresh chopped coriander leaves.
1. Our Kerala Style Tomato Fry is ready to serve.
1. Serve Kerala Tomato Fry Recipe along with Tomato Onion Sambar and Homemade Soft Idlis for breakfast or for a light dinner.
