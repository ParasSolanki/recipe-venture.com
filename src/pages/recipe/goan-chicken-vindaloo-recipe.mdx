### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Salt - or to taste.
- 1/2 tablespoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Onions - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 tablespoon Tamarind - washed and soaked in hot water..
- 1/2 inch Ginger.
- 1/2 tablespoon Black pepper corns.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 5 Kashmiri dry red chilli.
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 8 cloves Garlic.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - as required.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4 tablespoon Vinegar.
- 500 grams Chicken - cut into curry cut pieces.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 cup Homemade tomato puree.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Goan Chicken Vindaloo recipe, heat 1 teaspoon oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add the ginger, garlic and saute till they start softening and browning just a little.
1. Next, add the black pepper corns, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, fenugreek seeds, cloves, cinnamon stick and Kashmiri red chillies and roast on low flame.
1. In about 4 minutes, the coriander seeds will start to brown and the masalas will start to roast and smell.
1. Turn off the heat and let the masalas cool down.
1. Once cool, transfer to a blender along with vinegar, salt, turmeric and jaggery.
1. Add about 3-4 tablespoons water and blend to a smooth paste.
1. Marinate the chicken with this paste for at least 2 hours or upto 24 hoursThe next step is to make the curry for the chicken vindaloo.
1. Heat the 1 tablespoon oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Once the oil is hot, add the green chillies and onions.
1. Let it cook till the onions become golden brown.
1. Once the onions are cooked, add the tomatoes and cook till they becomes soft and mushy.
1. Once it is done, add the tomato puree and let it cook for about 2 to 3 minutes.
1. After 2 to 3 minutes, add the marinated chicken and the remaining marinate mixture.
1. Mix everything well and let it cook for about 10 minutes.
1. If the Chicken Vindaloo becomes too dry, add some water and mix again.
1. Add 1 cup of water and cook the Chicken Vindaloo on medium flame, covered for 25-30 minutes till the chicken is falling off the bone.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and check the seasonings and adjust to taste.
1. Transfer the Chicken Vindaloo to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Goan Chicken Vindaloo along with Whole Wheat Lachha Paratha or Steamed Rice and a salad of your choice for your weekend meal.
