### Ingredients

- 3/4 cup Jamun (black plum) - deseeded.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- Crushed ice - as required.
- 2 cups Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - (low fat).
- Honey - to taste.

### Instructions

1. To begin making this Healthy & Delicious Jamun Fruit Smoothie - Black Plum Smoothie Recipe, into a mixer or Blender, blend deseeded jamun fruit and honey till smooth.
1. Once done add milk, curd, crushed ice and blend for a few seconds.
1. Pour Healthy & Delicious Jamun Fruit Smoothie into tall glasses and serve immediately.
1. Serve Healthy & Delicious Jamun Fruit Smoothie with Grilled Spinach And Cottage Cheese Sandwich for a wholesome breakfast.
