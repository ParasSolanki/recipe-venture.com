### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - mixed with 1 tablespoon sugar.
- 1/3 cup Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled and chopped.
- 1/3 cup Sweet Chutney (Date & Tamarind).
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Onions - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Sweet corn.
- 1 tablespoon Anardana Powder (Pomegranate Seed Powder).
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped (adjustable).
- 3 tablespoons Lahsun Ki Chutney (Red Garlic Chutney).
- 1 tablespoon Green seedless Grapes - chopped.
- Red Chilli powder - to taste.
- Chaat Masala Powder - to taste.
- 1/3 cup Tomatoes - chopped.
- 1/3 cup Boondi - spicy.
- 1/2 cup Sev.
- 8 Hard taco shells.
- 1/2 cup Cheetos - crushed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Taco Chaat recipe, in a large mixing bowl take the chopped potatoes, onion, tomatoes, pomegranate seeds, chopped green grapes, chopped cilantro, green chilies and sweetcorn.
1. Toss them and dress with some chaat masala and red chilli powder.
1. Add all three chutneys according to your taste in the mixture and mix them well.
1. Add sev, spicy boondi and crushed Cheetos in mixture and toss them again.
1. Adjust spices and chutneys according to your taste.
1. To assemble, scoop the mixture filling into each taco shell, then add about 1 tablespoon curd on top.
1. Follow up with little drops of green chutney, garlic chutney and tamarind chutney according to your taste.
1. Sprinkle some chopped cilantro and crushed Cheetos, sev and boondi.
1. Sprinkle some chaat masala and red chilli powder.
1. Serve them immediately.
1. Serve Taco Chaat along with Sev Puri and Paneer Stuffed Millet Patties for your house parties or serve it to your sudden guests.
