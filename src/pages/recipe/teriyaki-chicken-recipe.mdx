### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 2 cups Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 3 Chicken breasts - boneless and skinless.
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour - dissolved in 1 tablespoon cold water.
- Rice - cooked as required (white or brown).
- 1/4 cup Soy sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil - + little extra for greasing the pan.
- 1/2 cup Honey - (I used raw honey but normal honey can be used).
- 3 cloves Garlic - minced or grated.
- Broccoli - cut into small florets.
- 1/2 cup Chilled water.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Teriyaki Chicken recipe, clean the chicken well and pat them dry.
1. Brush a baking pan with some oil and arrange these chicken breasts on top.
1. In a saucepan combine, brown sugar,  honey, soy sauce, hot sauce, garlic, ginger, black pepper powder and water on medium heat.
1. Mix well and let it boil on medium high heat.
1. Once it boils,switch the heat to low and add corn starch paste.
1. Mix well.
1. You will see the sauce getting thickened instantly.
1. Turn off the heat and keep the pan aside.
1. Pour half of the sauce over chicken breasts.
1. Cover the pan with aluminium foil and bake at 400 degree Fahrenheit preheated oven for 25 minutes.
1. In the meantime, fry the veggies - bell peppers and broccoli with a tablespoon of oil in a pan.
1. Sprinkle some pepper powder while frying.
1. Cover and let them fry on medium heat for 7 to 8 minutes.
1. Keep them aside.
1. Once the chicken is baked, remove the baking pan out.
1. You will see some juices released from chicken after they are baked.
1. You can either use it in your one pot meal for extra taste, or refrigerate it and can use it for making soup.
1. Let it stand for a few minutes and then cut the breasts into thick strips either vertically or horizontally.
1. Now take a ladle full of rice and place it in the bowl.
1. Add some cooked veggies on top and finally, place baked teriyaki chicken pieces on top.
1. Drizzle some of the remaining sauce on top.
1. Serve Teriyaki Chicken with Chicken Yakisoba Recipe - Japanese Noodles for a weeknight dinner with a glass of Red Wine Sangria Cocktail .
