### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - ground.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garlic - ground.
- Salt - to taste.
- 400 grams Salmon fillet - (indian Salmon works fine).
- 1/4 teaspoon Lemon zest.
- 1 cup Egg noodles - boiled.
- 1/4 teaspoon Lemongrass.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1/2 teaspoon Fish sauce.
- 1 teaspoon Rice Wine Vinegar.
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 1/2 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 1 cup Broccoli - chopped into small florets.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - ground.
- 1/4 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - freshly ground.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - freshly ground.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger - ground.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Broccoli Chilli Noodles With Grilled Salmon recipe, mix together all the ingredients for broccoli and grill in a preheated oven at 180 C for 15 minutes.
1. You can also stir fry the broccoli in a wok/kadhai at high heat till it is nicely browned.
1. Mix all the salmon ingredients i.
1. e sesame oil, fish sauce, rice wine vinegar and all the spices in a small bowl.
1. Whisk well and keep aside.
1. Keep the salmon fillets in a foil and pour the spice mixture over salmon.
1. Close the foils from all four sides sealing the salmon fully.
1. Grill in a preheated oven at 180 C for 10 to 12 minutes.
1. Open and broil for 2-3 minutes till slightly caramelized.
