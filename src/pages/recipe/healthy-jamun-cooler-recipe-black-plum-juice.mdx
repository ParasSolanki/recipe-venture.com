### Ingredients

- 1 Lemon - juice squeezed out.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - for garnish.
- 2 cups Chilled water.
- 1 pinch Black pepper powder.
- 1 cup Jamun (black plum) - washed and deseeded.
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 1 tablespoon Honey - (add or reduce as per taste).

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Healthy Jamun Cooler Recipe (Black Plum Juice) first, into a blender, add the deseeded jamun, honey, and cold water, pepper, and salt.
1. Blend till a smooth texture is formedAdjust lemon juice, pepper, and salt at this stage.
1. Add more if necessary.
1. Pour into serving glasses; garnish with mint leaves and serve immediately.
1. Serve the Healthy Jamun Cooler with some Phool Makhana and Palak Pakora during your tea time snack.
