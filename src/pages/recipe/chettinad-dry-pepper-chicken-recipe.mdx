### Ingredients

-  2-1 / 2 tbsp. Black pepper powder.
-  1 onion.
-  1 tomato.
-  oil - as per use.
-  1 tbsp coriander powder.
-  2 sprig curry leaves.
-  1 tbsp ginger garlic paste.
-  1 tsp cumin powder - compress.
-  1/2 tsp turmeric powder.
-  coriander - for garnish.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 tsp fennel Powder - roast.
- 500 grams of chicken.
-  water - as per use.

### Instructions

1. To make Chettinad Dry Pepper Chicken Recipe, first wash and clean the chicken thoroughly.
1. Now add turmeric powder, water and chicken in a bowl and keep it aside for 10 minutes.
1. Remove water after 10 minutes and marinate with salt.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add onions and cook till they become soft.
1. After the onion becomes soft, add ginger garlic paste to it and cook it for 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes add curry leaves and mix it well.
1. Now add tomatoes and cook till the tomatoes become soft.
1. It will take 3 to 4 minutes.
1. After 3 to 4 minutes, add marinated chicken and cook for 3 to 5 minutes.
1. Now add cumin powder, fennel powder, coriander powder, black pepper powder, salt and mix.
1. Cook for 5 minutes.
1. Now add 1/4 cup of water and cook it for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Serve.
1. Serve the Chettinad Dry Pepper Chicken Recipe with tomato rasam and rice for dinner.
