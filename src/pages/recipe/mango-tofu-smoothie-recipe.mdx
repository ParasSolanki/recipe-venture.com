### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Black sesame seeds.
- 100 grams Tofu - soft.
- 1/2 cup Fresh orange juice.
- Kiwi - Few pieces or any other fruit.
- 1 Ripe Bananas - spotty and frozen.
- Basil leaves - for garnish.
- 1-1/2 cups Mango (Ripe) - frozen.
- 1/2 cup Coconut milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mango Tofu smoothie, place all the ingredients in a blender and blend the ingredients until smooth.
1. Serve in a tall glass or a jar and top it with black sesame, basil and kiwi.
1. Try this Mango Tofu smoothie for breakfast or a post-workout protein rich meal, whichever way its a winner.
