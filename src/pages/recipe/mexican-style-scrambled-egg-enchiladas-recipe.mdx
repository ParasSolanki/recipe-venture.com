### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 4 Tomatoes - blanched.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Green bell peppers - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1/4 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1 cup Makki Ka Atta (Yellow Corn Meal Flour).
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- Black pepper powder - for seasoning.
- 4 Whole Eggs.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Scrambled Egg Enchiladas Recipe, we will start making the tortilla dough.
1. Add all the ingredients into a bowl and knead slowly by adding Lukewarm water.
1. Knead it into a firm dough and rest it covered for some time.
1. To make the sauce, blanch the tomatoes by slitting them crosswise at the bottom of the tomatoes.
1. Heat  enough water over a sauce pan and once it boils add the tomatoes and blanch them for 30 seconds.
1. Remove the tomatoes, once it cools down, peel off the skin and blitz the tomatoes in a mixer till it becomes a puree.
1. Heat a sauce pan with oil, add the chopped garlic, onions and sauté till the onions soften.
1. Add the pureed tomatoes and cook the tomatoes for 2 more minutes.
1. Add the sugar, red chilli flaxes, oregano and salt and adjust the consistency by adding little water if required.
1. Close it with the lid and cook the sauce for about 5 more minutes and switch it off.
1. To make the bell peppers fajitha, heat oil over a sauce pan, add garlic, onion, bell peppers and saute until they soften.
1. Add red chilli powder, salt, oregano and cumin powder and saute till the spice powder is coated well.
1. Switch off the heat.
1. To make the sour cream, whisk the curd and lemon juice together and place it in the fridge until you start to assemble.
1. To make the tortillas, divide the dough into small equal balls and roll it on the board to a 6 inch diameter.
1. Do the same with the rest of the doughHeat a flat Skillet, add a teaspoon of oil and just toast the rolled dough lightly on both the sides.
1. Do the same for the rest and keep it aside.
1. To make the egg scramble, heat a small pan with oil, break the eggs separately into a bowl add salt, pepper and whisk well till it fluffs up.
1. Once the pan is heated, add the chopped garlic and onions and saute till the onions softens.
1. Slowly add the whisked egg and leave it for 10 seconds now gradually fold in to make the scramble.
1. To assemble, take a toasted tortilla, spoon out some scrambled egg and roll it tightly, place it on a serving plate, pour the tomato sauce over it, add the sautéed bell peppers fajita, add a dollop of the sour cream and serve.
1. Serve the Scrambled Egg Enchiladas Recipe along with tomato salsa, Chunky Cucumber Salsa and a glass of Mulled Apple Juice.
