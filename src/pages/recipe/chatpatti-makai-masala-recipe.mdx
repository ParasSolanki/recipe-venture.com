### Ingredients

-  1 inch ginger - peel and tighten.
-  1/4 tsp asafoetida.
-  make 3 tomatoes - puree.
- 200 grams sweet corn.
-  1/2 tsp turmeric powder.
-  1 tsp cumin seeds.
-  3-4 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  1/2 tsp garam masala powder.
-  4 tbsp oil.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  4 sprig coriander - chopped.
-  1 cup peanuts - grind to a paste.
-  1 Tsp red chili powder.

### Instructions

1. To make the spicy maize masala recipe, first put sweet corn in a pressure cooker with water and salt.
1. Cook till 2 cities come and turn off the gas.
1. Let the pressure drain automatically.
1. Open the cooker and keep the corn aside.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add cumin, ginger, asafoetida, groundnut powder and cook for 1 minute.
1. Now add tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, garam masala, green chilli and cook till it boils.
1. After the boiling comes add corn.
1. Add salt as per taste and let it cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Cover the pan and cook for another 5 minutes.
1. Turn off the gas and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve the spicy Corn Masala Recipe with Phulka and Tomato Onion Tadka Raita for dinner.
