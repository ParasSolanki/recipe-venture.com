### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 250 grams Spinach Leaves (Palak).
- 4 Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 Fresh Red chillies - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dry Palak Dal Sabzi, wash and finely chop the spinach and keep it aside.
1. Cook toor dal with half cup of water and make sure water should be in 1:1 ratio and make sure that it is half cooked and not mushy.
1. Heat a wok/kadhai and add oil, cumin seeds, red chillies and garlic cloves.
1. Let the cumin seeds splutter and then add chopped onion and stir for a while.
1. Add spinach, turmeric powder and stir well.
1. Add salt and stir again for 5 to 6 minutes.
1. When spinach gets cooked, add toor dal and stir well for 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes, add red chilli powder and stir well.
1. When dal gets cooked, add garam masala and stir for few minutes and switch off the flame.
1. Serve Dry Palak Dal Sabzi with Steamed Rice, Phulka or Whole Wheat Lachha Paratha.
