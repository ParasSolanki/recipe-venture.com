### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1-2 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger - grated.
- Green Chillies.
- 1 cup Rice.
- Lime Wedges - Lime wedges.
- 1/2 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- Salt - as per taste.
- 3 cups Water.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1/2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Khichdi with Fresh Spices Recipe, first rinse and wash rice and the lentils together.
1. Soak them for about 30 minutes in water.
1. Heat 1 tablespoon ghee in a pressure cooker and add cumin seeds.
1. When it starts to sputter add bay leaf, dry red chillies, green cardamom, cinnamon, ginger and asafoetida.
1. Saute for 10 to 15 seconds and add the drained  rice and moong dal to the pressure cooker and saute for a minute.
1. Pour 3 cups of water into the pressure cooker, turmeric powder and salt to taste.
1. Close the lid tightly and pressure cook the khichdi on a medium heat till the cooker releases a whistle.
1. Once the whistle comes, turn the flame to low and cook for 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes turn off the heat and wait till the pressure is released naturally and open the lid.
1. Transfer the Khichdi with Fresh Spices into a serving bowl.
1. Add ghee on top if you like and garnish the Khichdi with Fresh Spices with coriander leaves, green chillies and a wedge of lemon and  serve hot.
1. Serve Khichdi With Fresh Spices Recipe with Pudina & Dhaniya Raita Recipe and Aam Ka Achaar Recipe for a simple comforting weeknight dinner.
