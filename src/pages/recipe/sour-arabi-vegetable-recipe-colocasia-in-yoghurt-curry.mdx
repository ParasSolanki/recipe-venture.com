### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 4 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.
- 500 grams Colocasia root (Arbi) - skin peeled and boiled.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Khatti Arbi Ki Sabzi Recipe, we will first pressure cook the arbi in a cooker with water and little salt for at least 4 whistles.
1. Once it is done, allow the pressure to release naturally and drain all the water and set aside to cool.
1. It will be now easy to peel off the skin, cut the arbi into half.
1. In a mixer, add all the ingredients like black pepper powder, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds and fresh coconut, add little water and grind into a coarse paste.
1. Heat oil in a kadai, add mustard seeds and allow it to splutter for few seconds.
1. Add in curry leaves and leave it to crackle.
1. Drop in some dry red chillies and saute for few seconds.
1. Add in cooked arbi, salt, turmeric powder and saute well till the arbi is roasted well.
1. Once the arbi is saut√©ed, you can now add the ground coconut paste and mix well.
1. Also add the curd, toss around so that the masala is coated well.
1. Check for salt, and cook Khatti Arbi Ki Sabzi until the moisture from the curry is evaporated and the dish becomes little dry.
1. Once the Khatti Arbi Ki Sabzi is done you can turn the heat off and serve it in a bowl.
1. Serve the Khatti Arbi Ki Sabzi Recipe along with Gujarati Methi Thepla and Matar Paneer to make it a complete meal.
