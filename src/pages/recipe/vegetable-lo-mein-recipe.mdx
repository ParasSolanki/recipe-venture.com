### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Broccoli - cut into small florets.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 1/4 teaspoon Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil - + 1 teaspoon.
- 2 cups Wheat Noodles.
- 2 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - julienned.
- 1/2 cup White onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sriracha sauce.
- 1/4 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - julienned.
- 1/4 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - julienned.
- 1/4 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - julienned.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 1/2 cup Spring Onion Greens - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Vegetable Lo Mein, in a saucepan of boiling water, cook noodles as per packet instructions, drain, rinse twice, rub 1 teaspoon of oil all over the noodles.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Whisk together soy sauce, red chilli sauce, brown sugar, siracha and keep it aside.
1. Heat oil in a wok.
1. Add minced garlic and white onions, saut√© for about a minute.
1. Add all the vegetables, cook, stir frequently, until tender or for about 3-4 minutes.
1. Stir in cooked noodles and soy sauce mixture, and gently toss to combine.
1. Serve immediately.
1. Serve Vegetable Lo Mein with Vegetarian Wonton Soup for a weekend dinner.
