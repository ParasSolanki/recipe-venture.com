### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns - coarsely pounded.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1/4 cup Walnuts - toasted.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese - grated.
- 250 grams Basil leaves - with stems removed.
- 1 Chicken breasts.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 tablespoons Garlic Butter.
- 1 tablespoon Fresh cream.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Basil Pesto Grilled Chicken Recipe, make the fresh basil pesto and keep it ready.
1. For the Basil PestoPlace the walnuts,garlic, basil leaves, salt, and pepper in the food processor and churn until coarse.
1. With the processor running, slowly pour the olive oil into the bowl through the feed tube and process until the pesto is thoroughly pureed.
1. Add the Parmesan and puree for a minute.
1. In a mixing bowl, marinade the chicken breast along with the basil pesto, cream, and lemon juice.
1. Let it sit for about an hour.
1. Heat a grill pan/skillet on medium flame, put some garlic butter and place the chicken breast on it.
1. Cook till the chicken is completely cooked through.
1. IT will take about 8 to ten minutes on either sides to cook the chicken.
1. Once done transfer the Basil Pesto Grilled Chicken onto a serving plate and serve hot.
1. Serve Basil Pesto Grilled Chicken Recipe with Summer Lettuce Salad Recipe and French Onion Soup Recipe for lunch or dinner.
