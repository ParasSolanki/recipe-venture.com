### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 250 grams Macaroni pasta.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - cubed.
- Salt - to taste.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 1 tablespoon Vinegar.
- 3 tablespoon Tahini.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Macaroni with Tahini sauce recipe, add olive oil in a pan and roast the garlic in the same.
1. Combine tahini, vinegar, salt and pepper in a small bowl and mix everything well.
1. Smash the garlic cloves and add to the sauce.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat some olive oil in a skillet.
1. Cut the capsicum into small cubes and add it into the pan.
1. Season with salt and pepper and cook them till they become soft.
1. While the veggies are roasting, bring a medium-sized pot of water to the boil and cook the pasta al dente.
1. When done, transfer a portion of macaroni to a plate or bowl, add the roasted veggies, and top off with the roasted garlic tahini sauce.
1. Serve Macaroni with Tahini sauce along with Pizza Margherita and a Cucumbertini Recipe for a weekend night dinner with friends.
