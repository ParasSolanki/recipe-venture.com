### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 Red onion - thinly sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns - coarsely pounded.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Raisins.
- 1 tablespoon Dijon Mustard.
- 2 sprigs Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - roughly chopped.
- 1/4 cup Fresh cream.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - diced and steamed.
- 3 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled and diced.
- 1/4 cup Green beans (French Beans) - cut into inch pieces and steamed.

### Instructions

1. To Creamy Potato and Vegetable Salad Recipe, boil the potatoes and steam the vegetables.
1. Cool and keep them aside.
1. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, whisk together the ingredients for the dressing.
1. Check the salt and seasoning and adjust accordingly.
1. Keep aside.
1. Add the cooked vegetables to a mixing bowl, toss it along with the prepared dressing and serve.
1. Serve the Creamy Potato and Vegetable Salad as a one dish meal, along with a bowl of Vegetable Clear Soup with Lemongrass  and Fennel Pesto Pull-Apart Bread for dinner.
