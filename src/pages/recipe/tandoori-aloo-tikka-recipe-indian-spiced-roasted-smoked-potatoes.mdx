### Ingredients

- 300 grams Baby Potatoes - boiled & peeled.
- 1/4 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Coal - for giving a smoked flavour.
- 1 tablespoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Gram flour (besan).
- 1 tablespoon Ghee - for cooking.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tandoori Aloo Tikka Recipe, get all the ingredients prepped and ready.
1. Into a large mixing bowl add all the ingredients for Tandoori Aloo in a bowl except the boiled potatoes and ghee and stir them well until combined.
1. Add the boiled baby potatoes into it and allow it to marinate for 30 minutes.
1. Heat a tablespoon of oil or ghee in a wok or a skillet, add the tandoori aloo mixture.
1. Saut√© over high heat for four to five minutes, stirring occasionally and roast the potatoes until all the marination thickens and forms a coat over the aloo.
1. The potatoes/ aloo should be well crusted and golden brown.
1. Once done, check the salt and spice and adjust to suit your taste of the Tandoori Aloo.
1. Keep this aside in the same pan.
1. Next step is the give the Tandoori Aloo Tikka a smoked flavour.
1. Place a large piece of coal on the gas flame and allow it to turn red hot.
1. Place a katori in the center of the tandoori aloo tikka pan, place the red hot coal in the katori/ cup and spoon a little bit of ghee over the coal.
1. It will begin to emit smoke.
1. Cover the pan and allow the tandoori aloo tikka to get the smoky flavours.
1. After about 30 seconds, remove the coal cup and the smoked Tandoori Aloo tikka is ready.
