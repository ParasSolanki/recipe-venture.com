### Ingredients

- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Achari Masala Recipe, we will dry roast all the ingredients over a flat skillet.
1. After dry roasting the spices, grind the entire mixture in a grinder to a smooth powder.
1. Transfer Achari Masala Recipe in an airtight container for a later use.
1. This can stay good for at least a month.
1. You can use the Achari Masala powder for Achari Paneer Tikka Recipe or Achari Paratha Roll Recipe to make a delicious meal.
