### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Pistachios - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Sugar - powdered.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 1/4 cup Dates - pitted and cut lengthwise.
- 100 grams Milk chocolate - chopped.
- 2 cups Chenna (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - (2 recipe portions).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bengali Special Chocolate Sandesh Recipe,click on the recipe link Chenna Recipe (Homemade Cottage Cheese | A High Protein Snack) to keep the paneer ready.
1. In a sauce pan add the butter and chocolate.
1. With heat on medium melt the chocolate stirring continuously.
1. Once the chocolate has melted turn off the heat and allow it to cool completely.
1. Keep aside.
1. Once the chocolate has cooled cooled, in a stand mixer beat together the chocolate and cottage cheese until well combined.
1. Heat a heavy bottomed pan on medium heat; add the above mixture and the chopped pistachios and stir continuously until it comes together like a ball of dough.
1. The dough will leave the sides of the pan; at this stage turn off the heat and allow the mixture to cool completely.
1. Keep aside.
1. The chocolate sandesh is ready to be shaped.
1. Take a rolling pin, grease the surface with butter and place the sandesh mixture on the greased surface.
1.  Roll to make the sandesh flat evenly and shape them into a square.
1.  Cut the Chocolate Sandesh into equal size square pieces.
1. Press in the sliced dates on top of the Chocolate Sandesh and refrigerate in an airtight box to set for a couple of hours.
