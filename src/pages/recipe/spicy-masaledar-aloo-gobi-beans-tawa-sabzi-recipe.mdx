### Ingredients

- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - peeled and diced.
- 1 Cauliflower (gobi) - cut into florets.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 3 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 cup Green beans (French Beans) - cut into 1 inch pieces.
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 2 teaspoons Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spicy & Masaledar Aloo Gobi & Beans Tawa Sabzi Recipe, heat a kadai with oil over medium heat.
1. Add ginger and garlic, allow it to soften.
1. Once done add chopped onions and cook till they turn translucent.
1. Add chopped tomatoes and sprinkle with little salt and cook until the tomatoes become mushy and soft.
1. Once the tomatoes soften, add all the spice powders and saute well.
1. Stir in the potatoes, cauliflower and green beans along with salt to taste.
1. Sprinkle 1/4 cup of water and cook until the vegetables are cooked through.
1. Check for salt and add if required.
1. Cook until the Aloo Gobi & Beans Tawa Sabzi  becomes semi dry.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and stir in the chopped coriander leaves and mix well.
1. Serve the Masaledar Aloo Gobi & Beans Tawa Sabzi along with Tawa paratha, Punjabi Kadhi Pakora and Curd rice to make it a complete meal.
