### Ingredients

- Salt - salt to taste.
- 1/2 Banana leaf - to line the steamer.
- 10 Toothpicks.
- 3/4 teaspoon Poppy seeds - powder using mortar and pestle.
- 1 cup Cauliflower (gobi) - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard oil.
- 1/2 Banana leaf - lightly toss in heat and cut them into squares and remove the stem.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 4 Garlic - cloves.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- Sunflower Oil - few drops for greasing.
- 1/4 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds - powder using mortar and pestle.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Healthy Fulkopir Paturi Recipe, wash and grate cauliflower and soak in hot water for 2-3 minutes.
1. Drain the water, squeeze the grated cauliflower, and set them aside in a wide bowl.
1. Grind together all the ingredients given under 'To Be Ground For Marination' using very little water to make a thick paste.
1. Add the ground paste, salt, sugar, turmeric powder, chilli powder, mustard oil, and cumin seeds to the cauliflower.
1. Mix them well using your hands, cover and set it aside for 45 minutes.
1. Line a Steamer with banana leaf.
1. Take small portions of the marinated vegetables and shape them into dumplings using your hand.
1. Place the shaped portion in the center of the cut banana leaf squares, close with banana leaf and seal them using a toothpick.
1. Repeat this method for the remaining marinated cauliflower dumplings.
1. Steam for 12- 15 minutes.
1. Serve Healthy Fulkopir Paturi Recipe along with Phulka, Chirer Pulao, and Chaler Payesh.
1. It can also be served with plain cooked rice.
