### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 2 Green Chillies.
- Salt - to taste.
- 8 Curry leaves.
- 1/2 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 inch Ginger - coarsely chopped.
- 1/2 Onion - finely chopped.
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 1 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - soaked in water for 3-4 hours.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 tablespoon Baking soda.
- 1/2 tablespoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Baked Chana Dal Vada Recipe, wash and soak dal for 3-4 hours.
1. Rinse, drain and set aside.
1. Preheat oven at 200 degree celsius.
1. Take a blender or food processor, add chana dal, fennel seeds, cumin seeds, green chillies, curry leaves, fresh coriander, ginger and garlic cloves ,salt to taste; grind it to coarse mixture.
1. Add some water only if required.
1. Now transfer mixture in a mmixing bowl, add chopped onion and baking powder.
1. Apply some oil on palms and start shaping the mixture into 2-3 inch size flat discs and place it on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
1. Bake for 18-20 mins or till the vadas turn to golden brown in colour.
1. Take them out and let them cool down a bit.
1. Serve Baked Chana Dal Vada Recipe warm with Mint chutney and Khajur Imli Chutney.
