### Ingredients

- 300 grams Boneless chicken.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Gram flour (besan).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 Whole Egg.
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour.
- Sunflower Oil - to deep fry.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Crispy Chicken Pakoda Recipe, clean and wash chicken pieces well.
1. Add chicken pieces, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, pepper powder, salt to taste into a large bowl and mix well to combine.
1. Next add gram flour, corn flour, and mix again to coat all chicken pieces well.
1. finally add the egg and mix again.
1. Cover and let it sit in the fridge to marinate for about 15 minutes.
1. Heat oil in a Pan for deep frying, on a medium flame.
1. Add a handful of chicken pieces into the oil separately.
1. Do not over crowd the kadai.
1. Remove from oil when cooked well and crisp and drain excess oil on kitchen towel.
1. Repeat deep frying all the chicken pieces in batches.
1. At last slit green chilies and curry leaves into the oil and mix with the deep fried Chicken Pakoda and serve hot.
1. Serve Crispy Chicken Pakoda immediately with tomato sauce or any delicious dip like Mirchicha Chatka or Peanut Chilli Dipping Sauce during tea time.
