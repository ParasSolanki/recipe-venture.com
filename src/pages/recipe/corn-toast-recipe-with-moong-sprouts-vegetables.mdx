### Ingredients

- Red Chilli flakes - for garnish.
- 1 teaspoon Corn flour.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Butter - softened.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1/2 cup Green Moong Sprouts.
- 1/2 cup Sweet corn - steamed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Corn Toast Recipe With Moong Sprouts & Vegetables, mix the milk and flour together in a small mixing bowl.
1. Prepare all the remaining ingredients and keep them handy.
1. In a small skillet, add the butter, corn, carrots,tomatoes, sprouts and the corn flour milk mixture.
1. Add the salt and pepper to taste as well.
1. Keep stirring the mixture until it is thickened and the corn flour is cooked through.
1. In the meanwhile, toast the bread slices in the bread toaster or in the oven.
1. Apply some butter over it and cut into triangles.
1. Spoon the Corn and Moong Sprouts & Vegetables topping mixture over the bread slices and serve immediately.
1. Serve The Corn Toast Recipe With Moong Sprouts & Vegetables along with Muskmelon (Kharbuj) Juice Recipe for breakfast or an after school snack.
