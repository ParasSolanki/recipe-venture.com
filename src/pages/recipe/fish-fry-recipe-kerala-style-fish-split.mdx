### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 Lemon - juiced.
- Curry leaves - a few sprigs.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 5 Fish fillet.
- Black pepper powder - freshly ground to taste.
- Coconut Oil - for shallow frying.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder - (adjust).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Meen Varuthathu Recipe - Kerala Style Fish Fry, wash and clean the fish well.
1. Drain off the excess water.
1. Set aside.
1. In a mixing bowl, make a thick paste of all the ingredients under “for the Marinade” including ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, black pepper powder, lemon, salt adding a little water.
1. Coat the spice mixture over the fish steaks well.
1. Set aside for 30-40 minutes.
1. Heat a frying pan with the oil, add the curry leaves and allow to splutter for a few seconds.
1. Once the curry leaves are spluttered, immediately add the fish fillets, and shallow fry the fish till done and crisp on the outside.
1. Flip the fillet to even brown on the other side also.
1. Make sure to fry the fish over medium low heat so that they crisp up.
1. Serve Meen Varuthathu Recipe as an appetiser with Steamed Rice, Kerala Chicken Curry and Snake Gourd Thoran and Paruppu Podi.
