### Ingredients

- Water - as required.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 cup Fresh green chickpeas - or frozen.
- 2 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 3 Green Chillies - adjustable.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 6 cloves Garlic.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Green Chana Chutney recipe, take a grinder jar and add green chana, green chillies, garlic, water and salt.
1. Grind the mixture in a paste.
1. Take out the paste in a bowl.
1. Next, heat a pan and add the required oil.
1. Once oil is hot, add cumin seeds.
1. Let it crackle.
1. Next, add curd and keep stirring.
1. After a minute, add the green chana paste and let it cook for another 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes, switch off the stove and the chutney is ready to serve.
1. Serve Green Chana Chutney as a side dish along with your meal of Panchmel Dal, Boondi Raita and Phulkas.
