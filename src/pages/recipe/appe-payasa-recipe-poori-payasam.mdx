### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- pinch Saffron strands.
- 1 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava) - fine rava or maida.
- 4 cups Milk - full fat milk.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon Nutmeg powder.
- 1/2 cup Water - for the dough.
- 1/2 cup Sugar.
- 2 tablespoons Mixed nuts.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Appe Payasa recipe, we will first make the appe for the payasa.
1. To make the appe In a mixing bowl, combine the rava, ghee and mix them together.
1. Add water little by little to knead into a dough.
1. Leave the dough.
1. Let it rest for an hour.
1. After the resting time, take a handful of the dough and roll into a large flat circle of 1 mm thickness.
1. Cut dough into small circle similar to pooris, using a cookie cutter or the lid of a small dabba.
1. In a small kadai, add oil and heat it over medium flame.
1. Once the oil is hot, add bite sized flattened dough and fry till they become golden and crispy.
1. Place the fried appe in a paper towel and drain the excess oil.
1. Repeat the same for all the rolled dough.
1. To make the payasa In a saucepan, add milk and boil over medium flame.
1. Cook until the milk reduces to its half it's quantity.
1. Now, once the milk becomes thick, add fried appe to the milk and cook until appe soaks the milk in.
1. The cooked appe will float on the top and becomes soft.
1. Add sugar to the payasam and cook until the sugar dissolves completely into it.
1. Add cardamom powder, nutmeg powder to the payasa.
1. In a small bowl, add saffron to a tablespoon of warm water.
1. Add saffron water to the payasa.
1. Once the payasa becomes thick, switch off the flame.
1. One can adjust the consistency of the payasa according to their wish.
1. If the payasa looks thick, add warm milk to it.
1. .
1. Add the chopped almonds, fried cashews and pistachios to the kheer.
1. Serve Appe Payasam warm.
1. Serve Appe Payasa during festivals or as a dessert after your meal of Mixed Vegetable Sambar, Beetroot Poriyaland Steamed Rice.
