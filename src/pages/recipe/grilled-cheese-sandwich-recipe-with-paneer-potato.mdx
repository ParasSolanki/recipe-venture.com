### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - crumbled.
- 1 tablespoon Butter - per sandwich.
- 3 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled and peeled.
- 8 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe Made With Paneer & Potato, first prep all the ingredients - like boiling the potato and making the homemade paneer.
1. In a large mixing bowl, add the boiled potatoes and mash them well.
1. Add the paneer, green chilies, salt, onion and salt to taste.
1. Mix all the ingredients well and check the salt and adjust as required.
1. Place a large tablespoon of the mixture over a slice of bread and spread it.
1. You can add more if you like a filled sandwich.
1. Cover with the other slice.
1. Melt butter in a skillet pan over medium heat and place the Cheese Sandwich in the pan and grill on both sides until crisp till it is done.
1. Serve the Grilled Cheese Sandwich With Paneer & Potato along with a hot cup of Adrak Chai or Banana Almond & Prunes Smoothie for your breakfast or tea time snacks.
