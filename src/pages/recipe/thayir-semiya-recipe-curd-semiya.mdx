### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 5 to 6 Cashew nuts - for garnishing.
- 1/2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 cup Semiya (Vermicelli) - roasted.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thayir Semiya recipe, firstly boil the vermicelli in 2 cups water.
1. Drain it and spread on a colander.
1. Pass cold water through it.
1. This ensures that the vermicelli does not become sticky.
1. Keep it aside and let it cool down.
1. Heat a kadai/wok and add oil.
1. Add the peanuts and roast till it turns brownish.
1. Remove and keep it aside.
1. In the same kadai, add more oil if needed and add the mustard seeds and after it splutters add the other ingredients meant for tempering.
1. Add the boiled vermicelli and mix everything.
1. Add salt to taste and mix.
1. Finally just before serving add curd and mix well.
