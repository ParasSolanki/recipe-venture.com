### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Jaggery - can be adjusted.
- 1 cup Instant dosa Mix Powder - or you can skip this and add 2 cups broken rice.
- 4 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 1 cup Jackfruit Raw (Kathal) - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Udupi Style Pelakai Teepe Dose Recipe (Halasina Hannina Dosa Recipe) by making the  instant dosa mix powder.
1. Click on the recipe link and follow the steps.
1. Mix about 1/2 cup water and curd to the instant dosa mix powder to form a thick dosa batter consistency.
1. Boil the chopped jackfruit with 1/2 cup water on a kadai over a medium heat.
1. Leave it on the heat, for about 20 minutes till the jackfruit become soft.
1. Then switch off the heat.
1. Grind it into a smooth paste.
1. Dissolve the jaggery with 1/4 cup water and keep it on a low heat for few minutes till all the jaggery melts.
1. Once the batter has rested you can add the jackfruit puree, dissolved jaggery water, coconut, salt and mix well.
1. Make sure the batter does not become runny.
1. Preheat a griddle or nonstick tawa on a medium heat.
1. Take 1 ladle of batter and pour it on the griddle.
1. Slightly spread it around in concentric circles like you would make a dosa, but ensure that youâ€™re making a thick pancake.
1. Next, drizzle a little oil or ghee to allow it to cook well.
1. Cover the dosa with a lid and cook on medium heat for 2 to 3 minutes or until the base is cooked.
1. Flip the dosa when the base is cooked to cook the reverse side as well.
1. Then remove from griddle and serve immediatelySoak the rice if you are using the broken rice instead of the dosa mix powder 2 hours in advanceIf you are using the broken rice, after it has soaked, you can grind all the ingredients together at one go and make a batter.
1. Serve the Udupi Style Pelakai Teepe Dose Recipe  topped with some jackfruit and along with a refreshing drink like Nongu Paal - Palm Fruit Drink or Masala Chai Recipe - Indian Spiced Tea for you morning Breakfast.
