### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 6 tablespoon Herb Butter.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 2 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chilli Garlic Toast Recipe, melt the butter in the microwave for about 30 seconds.
1. Once it is melted, add the garlic and green chilli to the melted butter.
1. Now apply the butter on one side of the bread slice and grill it on a pan for 5-6 minutes.
1. Cook it until it gets crispy.
1. Serve Chilli Garlic Toast Recipe along with Masala Chai for your tea time snack or you can serve it along with Baked Pasta Recipe With Paneer And Spinach for your weeknight dinner.
