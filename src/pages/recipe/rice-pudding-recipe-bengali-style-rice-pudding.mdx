### Ingredients

- 3/4 cup Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Cashew nuts.
- 3 to 4 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1-1/2 liter Milk.
- 100 grams Govind Bhog Rice - or any rice.
- 5 teaspoon Condensed Milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chaler Payesh recipe, boil the milk in a sauce pan over low flame till it reduces to 3/4th volume.
1. Add ghee to the rice and mix well.
1. Once the milk is ready add the rice and mix well.
1. Add sugar, green cardamon and milkmaid to the milk and rice mixture.
1. Stir well till the sugar melts.
1. Add the cashew nuts to it.
1. Garnish with few more cashew nuts and serve.
1. Serve Chaler Payesh as a sweet dish after your delicious Bengali meals.
