### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 2 Green Chillies.
- 1/2 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cut into small cubes.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 5 cloves Garlic.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 2 cups Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - tighly packed.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 cups Green peas (Matar) - steamed.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Hariyali Matar Recipe, peal the green peas, wash and steam it using a steamer and keep aside.
1. Make a fine paste of the ingredients listed in the green masala, coriander leaves, green chillies, garlic, ginger and lemon juice, using a mixer grinder adding very little water and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a wide pan, add the asafoetida, cumin seeds and let the cumin seeds splutter.
1. Add the ground green masala and saute for 2 minutes until the masala is well cooked and comes together.
1. Add the steamed green peas, season with salt, add 1 cup water, give it a stir and let it simmer for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Once the gravy has thickened, add the paneer cubes and mix well.
1. Let the mixture simmer for another 2 minutes and switch off the flame.
1. Remove into a serving bowl.
1. Serve the Hariyali Matar Recipe along with Tawa Parathas and Gajar Shimla Mirch Sambharo for the weeknight dinner or pack away for your lunch box.
