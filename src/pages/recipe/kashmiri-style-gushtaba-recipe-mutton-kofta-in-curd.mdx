### Ingredients

- Ghee - for cooking.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Dry ginger powder.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 300 grams Mutton Kheema.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 cup Milk.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 3 Cloves (Laung).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kashmiri Style Gushtaba Recipe, Add keema, cardamom powder and ginger into a mixer and give it a blitz.
1. Once it is done, you can take a little of the kheema mix and shape them into a cylindrical shape.
1. Do the same for the rest of the mixture.
1. Heat a kadai with ghee, add all the whole spices (Bay leaf, cumin seeds, Cinnamon Sticks, cloves and fennel seeds) and allow it to sizzle for few seconds.
1. Whisk in curd with 1 cup of water and add the curd into the kadai.
1. Keep the flame low.
1. Add turmeric powder, dry ginger powder and salt, mix well.
1. Add milk as well and give it a stir.
1. Slowly drop in the kheema balls and cook for at least 20 minutes.
1. Let the meatballs soak in all the flavors really well.
1. Serve the Kashmiri Style Gushtaba Recipe along with Butter Garlic Naan Recipe or Phulka to make it a complete meal.
