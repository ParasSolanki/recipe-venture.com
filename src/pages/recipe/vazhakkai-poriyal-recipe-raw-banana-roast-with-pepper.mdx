### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 tablespoons Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 2 Raw Banana.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves - roughly chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Vazhakkai Poriyal Recipe, cut the raw banana into half along with the skin and 1/2 cup of water and pressure cook for 4 to 5 whistles.
1. Turn off the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Once the pressure releases, peel the skin off the slice/ dice the cooked raw banana into small pieces.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan over medium heat; add the mustard seeds, urad dal, and curry leaves and allow them to crackle and the dal to turn golden and crisp.
1. Add the asafoetida, cooked raw banana, turmeric powder, black pepper powder and salt to taste.
1. Roast the Vazhakkai Poriyal until it gets well combined and has a light crispy texture.
1. Once done, turn off the heat, check the salt and spices and adjust accordingly.
1. Transfer the Vazhakkai Poriyal / Raw Banana Roast into a serving bowl.
1. Serve the Vazhakkai Poriyal Recipe - Raw banana Roast as side dish along with Steamed Rice and Sambar for a delicious weekend lunch.
