### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/4 cup Del Monte Tandoori Mayo.
- 3 cloves Garlic - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 Banana leaf.
- 8 Chicken Wings.
- 1 tablespoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 tablespoon Fresh cream.
- 1/4 tablespoon Cheese - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Creamy Kulfi Kebab Recipe With Tandoori Mayo recipe, wash and clean chicken wings and keep aside.
1. In a mixing bowl add del monte tandoori mayo, ginger garlic paste, lemon juice, sour curd, cream, mustard oil and salt.
1. Mix them to make a fine paste.
1. Add chicken wings to the mayo mix and coat them well.
1. Cling wrap the bowl and refrigerate the marinate for 30 minutes approximately.
1. Take a large banana leaf and cut into 4 square measuring 10”x10” inch.
1. Remove the centre rib/stem.
1. Switch on the stove and hold a banana leaf square with a tong and place over the flames.
1. Keep flipping for few seconds.
1. This will make the leaf soft and won’t tear while folding.
1. Follow the same step for the other leaf pieces too and keep aside.
1. Take the marinated chicken wings and some grated cheese.
1. Place two wings at a time in one banana leaf.
1. Fold the sides inside to make an envelope, tie the envelope with a string from both sides so that it doesn’t opens.
1. Similarly prepare more packets and keep aside.
1. Heat a non stick pan and grease it well with some oil.
1. Once the pan is hot, place the banana packets prepared (two at a time), cover the lid and cook in low flames till the colour of the leaf changes to pale brown.
1. Flip the banana leaf packet and similarly cook from the other side as well.
1. This might take 15 to 20 minutes till the chicken is nicely cooked.
1. Do not open the wrap before serving, open the wrap just before eating.
1. Serve Creamy Kulfi Kebab Recipe With Tandoori Mayo on its own or along with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney as a starter for your house parties.
