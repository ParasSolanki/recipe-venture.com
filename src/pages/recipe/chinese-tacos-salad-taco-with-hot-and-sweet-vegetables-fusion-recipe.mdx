### Ingredients

- 1/2 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced lengthwise.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli sauce - (adjust according to taste).
- Salt - salt to taste.
- 2 teaspoons Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 3 Tomatoes.
- 100 grams Tofu - cubed.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced lengthwise.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 1/2 inch Ginger - grated.
- 10 Lettuce leaves.
- 1/2 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - peeled and diced.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - cut into smaller pieces (optional).
- 10 Button mushrooms - sliced into halves.
- 2 teaspoons Honey.
- 1 Cauliflower (gobi) - cut into florets.
- 4 cloves Garlic - grated.
- 3 cloves Garlic - grated.
- 2 teaspoons Spring Onion Greens - finely chopped.
- 10 Baby corn - sliced into halves.

### Instructions

1. To start with Chinese Tacos (Salad Taco with Hot and Sweet Vegetables) -Fusion Recipe, get all the ingredients ready and handy.
1. Preparing the cabbage or lettucePeel the leaves carefully so that they do not split or tear.
1. Keep a wide bowl with cold water and place these leaves in the bowl till assembling the tacos.
1. Pat dry the leaves before you start assembling the Chinese Tacos (Salad Taco with Hot and Sweet Vegetables) -Fusion Recipe.
1. Instruction for the Spicy Tomato SaucePuree the tomatoes with green chilli in a mixer without adding any water.
1. Heat a wok/karahi with oil over low flame.
1. Add grated garlic.
1. When the garlic slightly changes colour, add the pureed tomatoes.
1. Let the tomatoes cook over low flame till the excess water evaporates and the oil separates.
1. This step takes up to 10 minutes.
1. Keep stirring occasionally.
1. Your spicy tomato sauce is ready!Instruction for the Hot and Sweet VegetablesHeat a vessel with 6 cups of water.
1. Add the mushrooms, baby corn, carrots, cauliflower to the boiling water.
1. Let the vegetables boil for 2-3 minutes, then switch off.
1. Transfer the vegetables to a colander to drain the water.
1. Refresh the vegetables by pouring cold/normal water on them.
1. Heat a wok/karahi with oil over a low flame and add the grated ginger-garlic, chilli flakes/chopped red chilli and mix well to incorporate.
1. Add onions, tofu and salt to fry till the onions turn soft.
1. Now add the boiled vegetables and stir-fry over low heat for 1-2 minutes.
1. Reduce the flame, add the soya sauce, chilli sauce and honey.
1. Keep stirring the vegetables and cook in medium to high flame for 3-4 minutes.
1. Switch off and garnish with spring onion greens to get the Hot and Sweet Vegetables ready.
1. Assembling the TacoTake a single pat dried cabbage leaf and add the onions and bell peppers.
1. Spoon the hot and sweet vegetable.
1. Garnish with the spicy tomato sauce and spring onion greens.
1. Your Chinese Taco is ready!To prepare the cabbage or lettuce:Peel the leaves carefully so that they do not split or tear.
1. Keep a wide bowl with cold water and place these leaves in the bowl till assembling the tacos.
1. Pat dry the leaves before you start assembling the Chinese Tacos (Salad Taco with Hot and Sweet Vegetables) -Fusion Recipe.
1. Serve Chinese Tacos along with Pasta In Tomato Onion Chutney for your fusion dinner.
