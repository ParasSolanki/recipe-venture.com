### Ingredients

-  1 tbsp parcel.
-  2 cloves garlic - chopped.
-  capsicum (pili) - chopped Take 1 tsp Apple cider vinegar.
-  1 onion - chopped.
-  100 gm button mushrooms.
-  1 tsp butter.
-  2 tsp cream.
-  capsicum (red) - chopped.
-  2 cups vegetable stock - or chicken stock.
-  black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1 cup kale - chopped.
-  salt - to taste.

### Instructions

1. To make kale and mushroom French soup recipe, first wash and cut the mushrooms thoroughly.
1. Cut them all and keep them separately.
1. Now heat the butter in a saucepan.
1. Add finely chopped garlic and cook for 1 minute.
1. Now add onion and cook till it becomes soft.
1. After the onion becomes soft, add mushrooms and cook for 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes, add vegetable or chicken stock to it and let it boil.
1. After boiling, add 1 teaspoon apple cider vinegar and mix it.
1. Now add black and capsicum to it and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Add parcel, salt, pepper and mix after 2 minutes.
1. Now add cream and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Turn off the flame and serve.
1. Serve kale and mushroom French soup with garlic bread and fetchini pasta with tomato sauce for dinner.
