### Ingredients

- 1 liter Milk - full fat.
- 2 tablespoons Poppy seeds.
- 1/4 cup Badam (Almond) - blanched and skin removed.
- 3/4 cup Sugar.
- 3 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds - powdered.
- 1 Saffron strands - few.
- 1 tablespoon Bhang paste.
- 1 tablespoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bhang Thandai Recipe, first get all the ingredients ready.
1. Blanch the almonds, peel the skin and keep aside.
1. Use a mixer grinder and powder all the ingredients listed under bhang thandai masala - poppy seeds, fennel seeds, blanched almonds, Whole black peppercorns, cardamom seeds and bhang paste in a mixie into a smooth powder or paste.
1. In a saucepan, bring the milk and saffron to a brisk boil.
1. Once the milk comes to a boil, stir in the bhang thandai masala, sugar and stir.
1. Stir well or use a electric hand blender to whisk the thandai well.
1. Turn off the heat and allow the thandai to cool.
1. Once cooled refrigerate the Bhang Thandai for at least 5 to 6 hours.
1. You can also add ice to cool it faster.
1. Serve Bhang Thandai during Holi along with some snacks like Baked Maddur Vada and Makki Paneer Pakora.
