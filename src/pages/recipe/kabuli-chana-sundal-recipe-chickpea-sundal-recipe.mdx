### Ingredients

-  salt - to taste.
-  1/4 tsp asafoetida.
-  2 tbsp Spoon coconut - grated.
-  1/2 tsp rye.
-  1 inch ginger - tighten.
- 1 cup black gram - soak overnight and cook till it becomes soft.
-  1 tsp green chilli.
-  coriander - little.
-  1 tsp oil.

### Instructions

1. Heat oil in a pan to make Kabuli Chana Sundal recipe.
1. Add mustard seeds and cook till it sizzle.
1. Now add ginger, green chillies, hing, salt, and kabuli gram.
1. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
1. Now add coconut and cook for 1 minute more.
1. Turn off the gas, garnish with green coriander and serve.
1. Serve Kabuli Chana Sundal Recipe along with mixed vegetable sambar, rice and papad for dinner.
