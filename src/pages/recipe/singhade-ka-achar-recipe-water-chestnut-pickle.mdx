### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard powder.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 tablespoons Salt.
- 2 tablespoons Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoons Fennel seeds (Saunf) - powdered.
- 1 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 kg Singhade (water chestnut) - boiled.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Singhade Ka Achaar recipe, peel the pointed sides, wash and cut into two.
1. Boil it with a glass of water for 10 to 12 minutes in a saucepan, cool and keep in strainer.
1. Lay it in an open space for 1 hour now.
1. Take a large bowl and mix all the ingredients including the boiled singhade properly.
1. Keep the jar in sun light for 2 days and it's ready to serve.
1. Serve Singhade Ka Achaar along with Pudina Tawa Paratha or Nutty Aloo Paratha for breakfast on a weekend morning.
