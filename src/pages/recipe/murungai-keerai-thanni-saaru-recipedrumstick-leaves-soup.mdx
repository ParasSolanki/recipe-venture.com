### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1 1/2 cups Drumstick Leaves (Moringa/Murungai Keerai).
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- 6 Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera) - crushed.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 2 cups Rice Water.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- 1 cup Coconut milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Murungai Keerai Thanni Saaru Recipe-Drumstick Leaves Soup, in a pan add the rice water and bring it to a boil.
1. Once it is boiled, add the cleaned drumstick leaves, slit green chillies, chopped onions and tomato.
1. Mix well and boil in medium flame until leaves are cooked.
1. Meanwhile extract coconut milk and keep aside.
1. Check our recipe on how to Homemade Coconut Milk Recipe.
1. With a mortar and pestle, crush cumin seeds and peppercorns and keep aside.
1. Once the drumstick leaves are cooked soft, add coconut milk, crushed Cumin Pepper mixture and required salt.
1. Simmer for 2 minutes and turn off the flame.
1. In a tadka pan, heat a tablespoon of oil.
1. Add Cumin seeds and once it sputters, add curry leaves and red chilli.
1. Switch off and add it to the soup.
1. Mix well and remove from flame.
1. Check for salt and serve hot.
