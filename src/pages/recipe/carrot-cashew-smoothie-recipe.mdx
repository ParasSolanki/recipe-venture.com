### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoon Honey.
- 1/2 cup Cashew nuts.
- Cardamom Powder (Elaichi) - a pinch.
- 2 cups Carrots (Gajjar) - cut into chunks.

### Instructions

1. To start making Carrot Cashew Smoothie Recipe, Pressure-cook the carrots and cashew nuts in 2 cups of water for a whistle, or microwave them on high for 5 minutes.
1. Let them cool.
1. Place the cooled carrots, cashew, the cooked carrot broth, and honey in a high-powered blender and blend until smooth.
1. Add cardamom and stir.
1. You can also make this ahead of time and chill in the fridge.
1. Carrot Cashew Smoothie Recipe is a great welcome drink for parties too!Serve Carrot Cashew Smoothie along with Akuri and Toast for breakfast.
