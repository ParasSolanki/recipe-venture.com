### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Walnuts.
- 1 cup Lukewarm Water.
- 2 Ripe Bananas - sliced.
- Salt - a pinch.
- 1/4 cup Coconut milk.
- 3 Dates - sliced.
- 2 tablespoon Anardana Powder (Pomegranate Seed Powder).
- 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 1/8 teaspoon Dry ginger powder.
- 2 teaspoons Honey.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Spiced Coconut Oatmeal with Assorted Fruits Recipe by boiling the oats with water, pinch of salt and spice powders in a saucepan till it thickens and gets cooked.
1. This will take about 10- 12 minutes.
1. Take it out from heat and transfer it into a serving bowl.
1. Add coconut milk , top it up with fruits, and drizzle some honey and give it a stir.
1. Serve Spiced Coconut Oatmeal with Assorted Fruits topped with some more fruits during a quick morning breakfast along with Cantaloupe (Melon) Juice.
