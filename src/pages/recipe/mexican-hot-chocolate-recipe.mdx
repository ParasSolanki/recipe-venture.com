### Ingredients

- 500 ml Milk.
- 20 grams Palm sugar - jaggery also will do.
- 2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 50 grams Dark chocolate.
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mexican Hot Chocolate Recipe, boil the milk along with cinnamon stick, chocolate and palm sugar over a medium heat.
1. Boil until the chocolate and palm sugar has dissolved well.
1. In a separate bowl add the corn flour and mix it with 1/4 cup water till it combines well.
1. Pour the corn flour mixture and keep stirring the milk till it thickens.
1. Serve the Mexican Hot Chocolate Recipe along with a cookie or a banana bread or Lemon Pound Cake that will satisfying your evening craving.
