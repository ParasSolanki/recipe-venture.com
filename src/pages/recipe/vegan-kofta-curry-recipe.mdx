### Ingredients

- 4 Tomatoes - blanched.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - for garnish (as required).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 8 Cashew nuts - soaked in warm water for 30 minutes.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 teaspoon Green Chilli Paste.
- 2 teaspoon Rice flour.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 Onions - roughly chopped.
- 1/4 cup Bottle gourd (lauki) - grated.
- 1/4 cup Beetroot - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder) - optional.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 tablespoon Multigrain flour - or besan.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- Sunflower Oil - for frying (as required).
- 1/4 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1/4 cup Green zucchini - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 cup Tofu - grated.
- 1/4 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - grated.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vegan Kofta curry recipe, first add all the ingredients under ‘For the koftas’ except oil in a mixing bowl.
1. Mix and make lime sized balls of the mixture (adjust the amount of besan if required, so that the mixture is easy to handle)Heat oil in a wok and deep fry the koftas till golden brown.
1. Take out with a slotted spoon and drain on absorbent paper.
1. Alternatively, shallow fry in appe patra/paniyaram pan with a drizzle of oil till it is evenly browned, flipping once in between.
1. To make the gravy- Blend the onion, tomato, cashew nuts separately in a Blender to a fine pasteHeat oil in a Kadai.
1. Add the cumin seeds.
1. Once they sizzle add the onion and ginger garlic paste.
1. Fry, stirring continuously till the paste turns brown.
1. Add the tomato paste and cook till the water evaporates and you can see the oil oozing out.
1. Add garam masala, red chilli powder and mix.
1. Saute for a few seconds.
1. Add the cashew paste, sugar and salt.
1. Mix and add enough water to get the desired consistencyBoil the gravy for a couple of minutes.
1. To serve, pour the gravy in a serving bowl.
1. Place the koftas in it and garnish Vegan Kofta curry recipe with fresh coriander leaves.
1. Serve Vegan Kofta curry recipe with Tandoori Roti or Naan for dinner.
