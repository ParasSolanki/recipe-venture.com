### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Dry ginger powder.
- Salt - a pinch.
- 3 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds - pounded.
- 1 cup White Urad Dal (Split).
- 4 tablespoons Gingelly oil.
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 2-1/2 cups Water.
- 1 cup Palm jaggery.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Ulundhu Kali Recipe (Urad Dal Sweet), add urad dal, fenugreek seeds and Cardamom to a mixer and grind into smooth powder.
1. Keep aside.
1. Now for preparing jaggery syrup, add jaggery and water to a pan and boil.
1. Stir until jaggery dissolves and strain to remove impurities.
1. Pour the filtered jaggery syrup back to the pan.
1. Add salt and dry ginger powder to it.
1. Stir well and bring it to boil.
1. Add the Urad dal flour little by little and keep stirring without forming lumps.
1. Once the flour and jaggery is combined well, add 2 tsp of gingelly oil at regular intervals and keep mixing with ladle.
1. The mixture has to become thick combining together without sticking to sides of the pan.
1. Once done, switch off the flame and allow it to cool.
1. Grease the hands with oil and take small portion of dough and roll into medium sized balls and wait till they cool down to serve.
1. Serve Ulundhu Kali Recipe (Urad Dal Sweet) during tea time with Irani Chai Recipe and Namak Para Recipe (Deep Fried Biscuits).
