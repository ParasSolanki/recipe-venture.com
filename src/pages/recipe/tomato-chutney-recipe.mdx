### Ingredients

- Salt.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 to 3 Dry Red Chillies.
- 200 grams Onion - roughly chopped.
- 400 grams Tomato - roughly chopped.
- 3 Kashmiri dry red chillies.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 Asafoetida (hing) - big pinch.
- 1 tablespoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- Sunflower Oil - as needed.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped finely include more stem.
- Curry leaves - few.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tomato Chutney recipe, add oil in a wide pan and roast the channa dal and chillies and add it into the mixer along with the coconut and set aside.
1. In the same pan, add a little oil and add the chopped onions and cook until the onions turn soft and translucent.
1. Once they turn translucent, add the tomatoes and cook until they are soft and lose some of their juices.
1. Set aside and let it cool completely.
1. Blend the ingredients in the mixer jar until they are coarsely pounded and add the onion-tomato mixture into the jar along with some salt and blend till its smooth.
1. Transfer into a bowl and add the coriander and mix gently.
1. The next step is to temper the chutney.
1. In a tadka pan, heat a teaspoon of oil, add mustard seeds and let them crackle.
1. Once they crackle, add the asafoetida, curry leaves and chillies and pour the tempering on the tomato chutney.
