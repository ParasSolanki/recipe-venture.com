### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil - more to smear.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish) - peeled and grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To Muli Paratha, We have to be ready with all the ingredients necessary for the paratha.
1. In a deep bowl, add  grated radish, chilli powder,  turmeric powder, cumin seeds, a pinch of asafoetida, oil and salt to taste.
1. Kneed the dough and make it soft by adding little water.
1. Divide the dough in equal portions and roll out each ball into a thick medium size roti.
1. Cook the paratha on a hot tawa and wait until you find brown spots on both sides.
1. This Muli Paratha should be served hot for breakfast with lemon pickle and a bowl of masala curd.
