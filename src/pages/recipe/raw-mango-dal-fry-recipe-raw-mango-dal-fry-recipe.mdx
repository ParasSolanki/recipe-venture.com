### Ingredients

- 1 cup tur dal.
-  1 tomato - chopped.
-  1 tsp ghee - for tempering.
-  1 onion - chopped.
-  1/2 tsp turmeric powder.
-  4 cloves garlic - grated.
-  1 sprig curry Leaf.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 tsp red chilli powder.
-  2 dried red chillies.
-  2 green chillies - thin and straight cut.
-  2 tbsp raw mango - tighten it.

### Instructions

1. To make raw mango dal fry recipe, prepare all the ingredients first.
1. In a pressure cooker, add dal, salt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, green chillies, tomatoes, raw mangoes and 2 cups of water.
1. Mix and close the cooker.
1. Cook till 3 to 4 cities come.
1. Turn off the gas and let the pressure drain automatically.
1. Open the cooker, mix and keep it aside.
1. Now heat ghee in a pan.
1. Add dry red chillies, onions and cook till the onion becomes soft.
1. Now add garlic, curry leaves and cook for 1 minute.
1. Now add dal mixture and cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
1. Reduce the gas and serve.
1. Serve raw mango dal fry recipe with bhindi masala, phulka and gourd raita for dinner.
