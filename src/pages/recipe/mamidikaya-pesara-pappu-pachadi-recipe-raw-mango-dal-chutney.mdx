### Ingredients

- 1 Mango (Raw) - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mamidikaya Pesara Pappu Pachadi Recipe, roast the moong dal and dry red chillies in a griddle pan till it is light brown.
1. Add the roasted dal, green chillies, jaggery cumin seeds into a mixer-jar and grind it into a coarse powder.
1. Add the cut raw mango and little water, salt and grind them into a coarse mixture.
1. To temper, heat a kadai with oil, add mustard seeds allow it to splutter then and urad dal and let it turn into anice golden brown colour.
1. Add curry leaves, dry red chilies, hing and leave the curry leaves to crackle for 10 seconds.
1. Pour this tempered oil over the Mamidikaya Pesara Pappu Pachadi and serve.
1. Serve the Mamidikaya Pesara Pappu Pachadi along with dosa or Mini Idli Upma Recipe to enjoy your morning breakfast.
