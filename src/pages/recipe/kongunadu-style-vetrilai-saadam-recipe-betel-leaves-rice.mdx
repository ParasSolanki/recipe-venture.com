### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali).
- Salt - as required.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 cups Cooked rice.
- 5 Betel leaves (paan).
- 10 cloves Garlic - crushed.
- 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 tablespoons Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 10 Cashew nuts.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 4 Fresh Red chillies - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kongunadu Style Vetrilai Sadam Recipe, wash and cook rice adding just sufficient water so that the rice is not sticky and the grains are cooked yet separate.
1. You can also use leftover rice for preparing this.
1. Next, we will prepare the masala for the rice.
1. Dry roast Cumin seeds, Fennel seeds, Pepper seeds and Sesame seeds until golden.
1. Add the curry leaves and roast for another minute.
1. Allow it to cool completely and grind into a coarse powder.
1. Heat a Kadai with oil, add mustard seeds, chana dal and Urad dal.
1. Once the dals turn golden brown add curry leaves, chopped red chillies, peanuts and cashews.
1. Saute until the cashews and peanuts turn golden color.
1. Add crushed garlic and saute until raw smell goes away.
1. Tear the betel leaves roughly and add it to the pan and saute until the leaves shrink.
1. Now add the ground masala powder and stir all ingredients well for a minute.
1. Turn off the heat and add cooked rice along with required salt.
1. Toss and mix well until everything is combined.
1. Check for salt an serve Kongunadu Style Vetrilai Sadam hot.
1. Serve Vetrilai Sadam or Betel Nut Leaves Sadam along with Tomato Cucumber Raita and Masala Vadai for a wonderful Sunday lunch.
