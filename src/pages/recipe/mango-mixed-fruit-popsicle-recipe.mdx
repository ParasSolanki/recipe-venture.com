### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Strawberries - sliced.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - a few.
- 2 Kiwi - peeled and sliced.
- 2 cups Mango juice.
- 1/4 cup Green Grapes - sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Mango & Mixed Fruit Popsicle Recipe, chop your favorite fruits and keep them aside.
1. Drop the fruit pieces into the Popsicle molds, dividing them equally.
1. Top with one or two mint leaves in each mold.
1. Gently pour mango drink or fruit juice till almost to the top.
1. If using plastic sticks that come with the mold, place it in the juice to close the moulds.
1. If using store bought wooden Popsicle sticks, stick the moulds in the freezer for about 30 to 40 minutes.
1. After 30 minutes, remove the moulds and gently insert the Popsicle sticks.
1. Stick the Mango & Mixed Fruit Popsicle  back in the freezer to completely set (8 hours to overnight).
1. After the Mango & Mixed Fruit Popsicle have completely set, remove from the freezer.
1. Place the moulds under running water for a few seconds and gently pull out the Mango & Mixed Fruit Popsicle.
