### Ingredients

- Salt - .
- 5 Stalks Lemongrass.
- 1 cup Red matta rice.
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 1 Onion - roughly chopped or 1/4 cup freshly chopped spring onions.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - thinly sliced.
- 1 sprig Basil leaves - or kaffir lime leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera) - roasted.
- 3 sprig Basil leaves.
- 3 tablespoons Thai Green Curry paste.
- 2 inch Ginger - sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds - whole roasted.
- 8 Button mushrooms - quartered.
- 5 Green Chillies - fresh green thai bird chilies.
- 1 cup Lukewarm Water - or vegetable stock.
- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 300 grams Chicken breasts - cut into bite sized pieces.
- 1 tablespoon Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 400 ml Coconut milk - unsweetened.
- 5 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thai Green Curry with Chicken & Red Rice RecipeMethod for Thai Green Curry PasteTo prepare the lemongrass, we will first have to trim away and discard any root section below the bulb base.
1. We will also now remove the dried part of the root until we reach a stage we know that the root cuts easily and is tender.
1. We don't use the leaf part of the lemon grass for the curry paste, you can use it for flavoring a tea or a soup.
1. Continue the same procedure for the remaining lemon grass stalks.
1. Add all the ingredients for the paste into a blender and a little bit of water.
1. Cover the blender and grind the mixture.
1. After a few seconds of grinding, open the blender and stir to combine the ingredients and if required add a little more water to grind into a smooth paste.
1. Transfer the Thai curry paste and store it in a glass jar.
1. You only need to use 3 tablespoons of the paste for a single recipe of Thai curry.
1. So with this paste you will be able to make Thai curry at least 3 times over.
1. Method to make the Thai Green CurryNext we will proceed to make the Thai Curry with this paste.
1. Heat a teaspoon of oil in a heavy wok.
1. Add in chopped ginger and vegetables.
1. Add in some salt and stir fry the vegetables on high heat until lightly tender.
1. Once tender turn off the heat and keep aside.
1. Add a teaspoon of oil into the hot wok, next add in 2 to 3 tablespoons of the thai curry paste and saute for a few seconds in the oil.
1. Next add in a cup of water and stir to combine into the curry.
1. Next we will add in a tablespoon of brown sugar and finally 400 ml of the coconut milk.
1. Stir to combine all the ingredients.
1. Once combined, add in the salt, stir and allow the mixture to come to a thicken a little and come to a boilOnce the mixture comes to a boil, add in some torn basil leaves and the stir fried vegetable, Drop in the chicken pieces.
1. Stir the mixture, give it a light boil for about 20 minutes until the chicken is tenderly cooked and the thai curry is ready to be served.
1. To cook the red rice, wash the red rice and place it in a cooker with 2 1/2 cups of water and cook for about 4 whistle.
1. Allow the pressure to release by itself.
1. Place a cup of Steamed Red Rice in a serving bowl.
1. Pour the freshly cooked hot thai chicken curry on top of the rice until the rice soaks well into the curry and there is enough gravy to soup out and serve immediately to get the best flavors.
1. Serve the Thai Green Curry with Chicken & Red Rice Recipe as a one dish meal for your Sunday lunch along with a glass of Mango Iced Tea Recipe or Chamomile Tea Recipe.
