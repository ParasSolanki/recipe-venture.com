### Ingredients

- 6 Tindora (Dondakaya/ Kovakkai) - cut into roundeles.
- 2 Brinjal (Baingan / Eggplant) - chopped into medium chunks.
- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 2 tablespoons Udupi Sambar Powder.
- 1/2 cup Tamarind Water.
- 2 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - chopped lengthwise.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Udupi Style Thondekayi And Badnekayi Sambar Recipe, keep all the ingredients ready.
1. Add toor dal to boil in a pressure cooker with 2 cups water so that dal is fully immersed, add salt and turmeric powder.
1. Close the cooker with lid and let it cook for 4 whistles.
1. Let the pressure release naturally.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Meanwhile, heat saucepan with 2 cups water, boil the vegetables with salt and turmeric powder for 25 minutes.
1. Once that is boiled, add the cooked dal along with udupi sambar powder, tamarind water and jaggery.
1. Give it a stir.
1. Check for salt and adjust according to your taste.
1. Leave the sambar to boil over a medium heat for 5 more minutes and switch it off.
1. The next step is to temper the Sambar.
1. Heat a tadka pan with oil, add mustard seeds, urad dal and leave it to splutter.
1. Add asafoetida, dry red chilli, curry leaves and keep it for about 10 seconds and pour it over the sambar.
1. Give it a mix and it is ready to be served.
1. Serve the Udupi Style Thondekayi And Badnekayi Sambar with Steamed Rice, Cabbage Palya and a dollop of ghee for your vegetarian weekday meal.
