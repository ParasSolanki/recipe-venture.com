### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 4 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 3 cups Red Amaranth Leaves - tightly packed.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tambdi Bhaji Recipe (Goan Style Red Amaranth Stir Fry), clean wash and chop the red amaranth leaves roughly and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan, add the cumin seeds, curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. Add the garlic cloves and saute for a minute.
1. Add the chopped onion, green chillies and saute till onions turn translucent.
1. Add the chopped amaranth, season with salt and let it cook.
1. This will take around 5 minutes.
1. Once the water is all dried out, add the grated coconut, mix well and switch off the heat.
1. Serve Tambdi Bhaji along with Goan Style Mushroom Vindaloo and Steamed Rice for a weekday meal.
