### Ingredients

-  1 capsicum (green) - finely chopped.
-  as per taste.
-  1 onion - finely chopped.
-  2 tablespoons lemon juice.
- 1 rice.
-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  1/2 cup broccoli - finely chopped.
-  2 teaspoons black pepper powder.
-  coriander - finely chopped (little).
-  salt - 1 tablespoon oil.
-  3 cloves garlic - finely chopped.
-  1 capsicum (red) - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make Broccoli and Pepper Rice, firstly cook the rice.
1. In a saucepan, add 2 cups of water, salt, oil, lemon juice along with rice.
1. Cook until the rice is cooked.
1. After cooking, cover and keep it aside to cool.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add onion, green chilli, capsicum and cook until the capsicum becomes soft.
1. After the capsicum is cooked add broccoli and mix it well.
1. Cook until the broccoli becomes soft.
1. Now add cooked rice, black pepper powder, salt and coriander leaves.
1. Mix, cook for 2 more minutes and serve hot.
1. Serve Broccoli and Pepper Rice with cucumber and chilli raita for dinner.
