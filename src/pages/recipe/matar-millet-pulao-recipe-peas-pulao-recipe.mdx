### Ingredients

- Ghee - for cooking.
- 2 teaspoons Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 cup Bajra Flour ( Pearl Millet) - soaked in water for 30 minutes.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Matar Millet Pulao Recipe (Peas Pulao Recipe), pressure cook the millet with 1-1/2 cups of water in a pressure cooker for about 2 whistles till they are just cooked.
1. Heat a heavy bottomed pan with ghee.
1. Once the ghee is hot, add the whole spice like cardamom pods, cinnamon sticks, bay leaf, cumin seeds and allow them to splutter.
1. Once done, add the cooked millets and mix everything properly.
1. After a minute, add salt, mix it again and switch off the heat.
1. Next, heat a saucepan with enough water for the peas to blanch.
1. Once the water starts to boil, add the peas and cook for 30 seconds and drain the water.
1. Sprinkle peas over the tossed millet, give it a stir and serve hot.
1. Serve the Matar Millet Pulao Recipe (Peas Pulao Recipe) along with Boondi Raita and a roasted papad for a weekday dinner and you are sorted.
