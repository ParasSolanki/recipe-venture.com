### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 Bottle gourd (lauki) - cut into small pieces.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Coconut milk.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli -.
- 2 teaspoons Gram flour (besan).
- 2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dudhi Kajuchi Sonari Amti Recipe, heat a small pan with little oil, we will first roast the spices for the sonari masala including cinnamon stick, cloves, coriander seeds, mustard seeds, whole black peppercorns and dry red chilli.
1. Lightly roast them until you can smell the aroma of the spices, this will take about 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Cool it down and grind it into a smooth paste by adding little water.
1. Heat another big pan with oil, add cinnamon stick and pinch of hing, leave it sizzle for 10 seconds.
1. Add onions and cashew nuts and saute them until it softens.
1. Add the bottle gourd with a pinch of turmeric powder and salt.
1. Saute them for 5 minutes.
1. Add the ground masala paste and coat the bottle gourd well and cover the pan cook for another 10 minutes.
1. Cook until the raw smell of the masala goes away.
1. In a separate bowl add gram flour and 4 tablespoon water and mix to form a fine paste.
1. Slowly add this to the pan and mix until the paste thickens.
1. Add coconut milk immediately and switch off the flame and mix.
1. Check for salt and adjust to your palate.
1. Garnish it with chopped coriander leaves and serve.
1. Serve the Dudhi Kajuchi Sonari Amti Recipe along with Bhakri Recipe (A Multigrain Gujarati Flat Bread) and Khandeshi Dal Recipe for a complete lunch.
