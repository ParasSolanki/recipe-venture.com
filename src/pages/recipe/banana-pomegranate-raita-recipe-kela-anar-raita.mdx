### Ingredients

- 1 cup yogurt.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin powder - take it out.
-  1 teaspoon Dijon mustard.
-  1/4 teaspoon red chili powder.
-  1 cup banana - fried.
-  1 teaspoon sugar.
-  1/4 cup pomegranate.
-  salt - According to taste.

### Instructions

1. To make Banana Pomegranate Raita, firstly add curd, salt, red chili powder, sugar, Dejon mustard and cumin powder in a bowl.
1. Mix well.
1. After mixing, add pomegranate and banana.
1. Put the bowl in the fridge for 2 hours and mix.
1. Serve.
1. Serve banana pomegranate raita with gourd elder greens and phulka for dinner.
