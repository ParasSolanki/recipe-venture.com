### Ingredients

- 250 grams Kaddu (Parangikai/ Pumpkin) - peeled and chopped.
- 1/4 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1/4 cup Masoor Dal (Whole).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/4 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pumpkin Dal with Garlic Tadka Recipe, we will be cooking all the ingredients for the dal in a pressure cooker.
1. First, get all the ingredients ready.
1. Wash the dal, cut the pumpkin and the remaining ingredients ready.
1. Add all the ingredients into the pressure cooker (except the ingredients for the tadka), add 2-1/2 cups of water, salt and cover the pressure cooker.
1. Cook until you hear two whistles.
1. After a couple of whistles, turn the heat to low and simmer for a couple of minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Once the pressure has released, open the cooker and give the dal a stir and transfer to a serving bowl.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. For the garlic tadka, heat ghee in a small tadka pan on medium heat.
1. Add the cumin seeds, garlic and red chilies.
1. Allow them to roast for a few seconds and pour the tadka over the dal.
1. Serve the Pumpkin Dal with Garlic Tadka Recipe along with steamed rice, papad and achaar for a quick weeknight dinner.
