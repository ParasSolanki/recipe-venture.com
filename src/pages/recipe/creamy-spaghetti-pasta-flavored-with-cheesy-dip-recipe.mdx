### Ingredients

- Red Chilli flakes - to garnish.
- Fresh Herbs - to garnish.
- 1 Red Yellow or Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 250 grams Del Monte Spaghetti Pasta.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar) - steamed.
- 1/4 cup Del Monte Cheesy Dip.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Creamy Spaghetti Pasta Recipe with Del Monte Cheesy Dip, first cook the pasta as per packet instructions.
1. Drain and keep aside.
1. Reserve a small ladleful of the pasta water to add if required.
1. In a pan heat the oil.
1. Add the onion and sauté till translucent.
1. Add the capsicum and peas and sauté for 1-2 minutes until softened.
1. Sprinkle little salt and stir.
1. Add the Del Monte Cheesy dip.
1. Mix and add some of the reserved pasta water to make a thick sauce.
1. Toss in the cooked spaghetti pasta.
1. Stir for a couple of minutes so that the creamy Mayo sauce coats the pasta.
1. Once done, take it off the heat, garnish with red chilli flakes, fresh herbs (if using).
1. Serve Creamy Spaghetti Pasta Recipe with Del Monte Cheesy Dip hot with Whole Wheat Oats And Pesto Bread Recipe by the side.
