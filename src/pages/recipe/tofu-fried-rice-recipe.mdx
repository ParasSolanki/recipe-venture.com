### Ingredients

- 1 cup Basmati rice.
- 8 Button mushrooms - chopped.
- 5 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 Red Yellow or Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - julienned.
- 1 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 4 Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - finely chopped.
- 4 tablespoon Spring Onion Greens - finely chopped.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - peeled and julienned.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 200 grams Tofu - cubed.
- 1 tablespoon Green Chilli Sauce.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tofu Fried Rice, wash the rice and keep aside for 15 minutes.
1. Par boil the rice by adding the bay leaf and 1/4 teaspoon salt to it.
1. Drain and spread it on a colander.
1. Sprinkle cinnamon powder on it and let it dry.
1. Meanwhile in a kadai/wok, add the required oil and shallow fry the tofu cubes till they turn brownish from both the sides.
1. Remove and keep it aside.
1. In the same kadai, add oil if needed.
1. When oil gets heated add chopped garlic and saute for a few seconds.
1. Then add chopped spring onion and onion and saute till onion become soft.
1. Once done add ginger garlic paste and mix well.
1. Next add capsicum, carrot and mushroom.
1. Saute till the vegetables and mushrooms get cooked.
1. Once the vegetables are cooked add the green chilli sauce and soy sauce and mix well to combine.
1. Add salt to taste, remember that salt had already been added to rice too while cooking.
1. Add the fried tofu cubes and gently mix everything well.
1. Finally add the rice, toss and stir it for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. After 2 to 3 minutes, garnish with chopped spring onion greens and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer the Tofu Fried Rice into a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Tofu Fried Rice for a weekend dinner along with Indo Chinese Mixed Vegetable Recipe In Szechuan sauce and Chinese Darsaan Recipe With Ice Cream.
