### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Honey.
- Black pepper powder - optional.
- Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Nutmeg powder.
- 1 Star anise.
- 2 Cup Milk - or almond milk.
- 1/2 teaspoon Maple syrup.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Turmeric Latte Recipe, take a saucepan, lightly warm the milk and add fresh turmeric powder.
1. Give it a good stir and taste if you want to increase the amount of turmeric.
1. Now add cinnamon & nutmeg powder into the milk and stir it again.
1. Turn off the heat and add honey + Maple Syrup/ Vanilla Extract into the milk.
1. Mix everything well and let it sit for a couple of seconds.
1. Now pour the warm milk into a mug and top it with grated coconut, a pinch of black pepper powder and star anise.
1. Cuddle up with your favorite book and enjoy this heartwarming drink.
1. Serve Turmeric Latte along with Savoury Baked Arbi Chips for your tea time break.
