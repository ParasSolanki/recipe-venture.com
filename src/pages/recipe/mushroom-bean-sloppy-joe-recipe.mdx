### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 200 grams Button mushrooms - sliced.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 1 cup Rajma (Large Kidney Beans) - soaked and cooked.
- 4 tablespoons Eggless Mayonnaise.
- 4 Burger buns.
- 1 Cucumber - sliced.
- 6 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Sriracha sauce.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/4 cup Homemade tomato puree.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mushroom & Bean Sloppy Joe Recipe, soak the rajma beans for at least 8 hours and keep it ready.
1. Pressure cook the rajma for about 30 minutes until the rajma is soft and completely cooked through.
1. Once done, allow the pressure to release completely.
1. After the pressure is released open the pressure cooker & drain out the excess water from the rajma.
1. You can use this as a stock or when cooking rice.
1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
1. Add garlic, mushrooms, onion and capsicum.
1. Saute until the onions and capsicum have become tender.
1. Once done add in the cooked rajma beans, tomato puree, brown sugar, sriracha sauce,and salt to taste.
1. Stir fry on high heat for about a minute and then add 2 tablespoon of mayonnaise.
1. Stir fry for 2 to 3 minutes till the mixture becomes thick.
1. Stir continuously to ensure that the rajma mixture does not stick to the bottom of the pan.
1. Once the mixture has come together turn off the heat and keep it aside.
1. Slice and toast the buns with olive oil in a skillet until it has become browned and crisp.
1. The final step is to assemble the Mushroom & Bean Sloppy Joe.
1. Take a slice of burger bun and spread mayonnaise on the bun.
1. Add sliced cucumber and then add a tablespoon of the rajma filling.
1. Next add some sliced onions on top of the rajma filling.
1. Finally place the other half of the burger bun and serve.
1. Serve the Mushroom & Bean Sloppy Joe Recipe along with delicious Rich Chocolate Almond Brownie along with vanilla ice cream for a delicious weekend dinner with your family.
