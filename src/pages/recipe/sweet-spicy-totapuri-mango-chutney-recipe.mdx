### Ingredients

- 250 grams Mango (Raw) - totapuri.
- Salt - to taste.
- 400 grams Jaggery - grated.
- 2 tablespoons Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 tablespoons Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.

### Instructions

1. To  begin making the Sweet & Spicy Totapuri Mango Chutney Recipe, wash and wipe Totapuri Mango.
1. Peel the mango and roughly chop into small pieces.
1. In a mixer-jar combine the mango, jeera, salt, Kashmiri red chilli powder and half the amount of jaggery and grind to a fine paste Add remaining amount of Jaggery and blend until a consistency of Chutney is attained.
1. Store the Sweet & Spicy Totapuri Mango Chutney Recipe in a sterilized bottle, in a dry place.
1. Serve this Sweet & Spicy Totapuri Mango Chutney Recipe along with Puri Recipe and Aamras Recipe for a light summer meal.
