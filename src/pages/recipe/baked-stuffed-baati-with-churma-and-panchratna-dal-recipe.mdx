### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 cup Green peas (Matar) - frozen.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1/2 cup Caster Sugar.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 3 tablespoon Ghee - melted.
- Green Chilli - sliced.
- Ghee - melted (as required).
- 1/4 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 1 Kashmiri dry red chilli.
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- Curry leaves - as required.
- 1/4 cup Green Moong Dal (Split).
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Badam (Almond) - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Gram flour (besan).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 tablespoon Pistachios - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- Lemon - sliced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Tomato - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Papad.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 4 tablespoons Ghee - Melted.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Baked Stuffed Baati With Churma And Panchratna Dal recipe , firstly we will make the churma.
1. For Churma, in a mixing bowl take wheat flour, semolina and ghee, add water to form a stiff dough.
1. Make patties with your palms with a indentation at the center.
1. Prepare all and keep them aside.
1. The next step is to make the dal.
1. Rinse the daals well and pressure cook for 5 whistles along with a pinch of turmeric powder, salt and 3 cups of water.
1. Once pressure releases, mash it well with a laddle and set aside.
1. In a pan heat ghee, add the items listed under ‘To temper‘ and let it splutter.
1. Then add tomatoes along with red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder and garam masala along with salt and saute for few minutes until the tomatoes becomes soft.
1. Now add mashed dal and mix well.
1. Let the dal boil for 5 minutes, add little water if its too thick.
1. Finally garnish with coriander leaves.
1. Next, we will make the Baati.
1. In a mixing bowl, take all the ingredients listed under ‘BATI‘, mix well and knead it together to form a stiff dough.
1. Divide the dough into small balls and keep them aside.
1. Now for stuffing, into medium sized pan, heat oil, add fennel seeds and coriander seeds.
1. Add green chillies, and green peas.
1. Add salt, remaining dry ingredients and mix it well.
1. Cook for 5-7 minutes and mash the peas using spatula and mix it well.
1. Cook for another 5 minutes and switch off the flame, allow them to cool down.
1. Once it's completely cool, divide them into equal portions.
1. Now flatten the baati and stuff one portion of the stuffing we made, and again flatten them using your palms.
1. Prepare all the baatis likewise.
1. Make a small indentation in the centre of the baatis using your thumb.
1. Preheat the microwave on 200 degrees.
1. Place the Churma and Baatis into it and bake them first for 15 minutes.
1. When beep sounds, turn over them and bake again for 15 more minutes.
1. Let them cool down at room temperature.
1. So your baatis are ready!For Churma, once it is completely cool, break churma into small pieces and grind it using a mixer.
1. Grind it a little coarse.
1. Collect the mixture in a bowl, add castor sugar and mix well.
1. Add almonds, pistachios along with ghee and give a quick mix.
1. Churma is ready!So your delicious treat is ready! Serve it with papad, green chilli, lemon slice and sudh deshi ghee!Serve Baked Stuffed Baati With Churma And Panchratna Dal with Masala Chaas for a weekend lunch.
