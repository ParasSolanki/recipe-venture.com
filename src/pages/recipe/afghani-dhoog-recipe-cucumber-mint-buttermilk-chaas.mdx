### Ingredients

- 2 cups Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Ice cubes - a few.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 1 cup Cucumber - peeled and chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Afghani Dhoog Recipe - Cucumber Mint Buttermilk/ Chaas into the preethi zodiac blender tall jar add yogurt, mint leaves, cucumber, cumin, ice cubes and salt to taste.
1. Turn on the puree function and blent to make a smooth drink.
1. Pour the Afghani Dhoog into serving glass and serve chilled.
1. Serve this Afghani Dhoog Recipe - Cucumber Mint Buttermilk/ Chaas with Lehsuni Methi Paneer and Tawa Paratha Recipe - Plain Paratha for lunch.
