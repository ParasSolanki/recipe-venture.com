### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 cups Broccoli - cut into florets.
- 4 Chicken sausage.
- 1 teaspoon Green Chilli Sauce.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 tablespoon Vinegar.
- 1/2 teaspoon Lemon juice.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chinese Style Sweet And Spicy Broccoli Stir Fry With Sausage recipe, wash and cut broccoli in small florets and keep aside.
1. Keep the chicken sausages in a microwave and keep aside to cool.
1. Heat oil in a non stick pan/wok and add broccoli florets and saute for a minute or two.
1. Now add salt, pepper, vinegar, sauces and saute for another couple of minutes.
1. Cut the sausages in round slices and add in the pan.
1. Mix well with broccoli and cook till the florets are tender.
1. Sprinkle sugar to give mild sweetness to the vegetable and turn off the gas.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve Chinese Style Sweet And Spicy Broccoli Stir Fry With Sausage Recipe on its own or along with a Tomato Noodle Soup for weeknight dinner.
