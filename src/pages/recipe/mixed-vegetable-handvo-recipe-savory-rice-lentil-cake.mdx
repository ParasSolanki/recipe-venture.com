### Ingredients

- 3 Dry Red Chillies - tore them and take out the seeds.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/4 cup White Urad Dal (Whole) - coarsely ground.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - extra for sprinkling.
- 1/4 cup Green Moong Dal (Split) - coarsely ground.
- 2 teaspoons Ginger Garlic Paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black sesame seeds - extra for sprinkling.
- 1 cup Bottle gourd (lauki) - shredded.
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/4 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - coarsely ground.
- 1/2 cup Basmati rice - coarsely ground (similar to coarse semolina).
- 1/2 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - shredded.
- 10 Curry leaves - broken.
- 2 Green Chillies - ground to paste (I used 2 large ones).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Enos fruit salt.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Caster Sugar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vegetable Handvo recipe, soak the coarsely ground rice and lentils overnight using yogurt (curd/ dahi) in a bowl.
1. Add some warm water (1 tablespoon at a time) to bring the batter to a very thick consistency.
1. Ferment the batter overnight or for 10-12 hours.
1. Once it is fermented, add shredded veggies in the morning along with chopped onion, lemon juice, sugar, red chili powder, turmeric powder, salt and ginger garlic green chili paste.
1. Mix well.
1. Mix Enos fruit salt with 1 tablespoon of water and add to the batter.
1. Bring the batter to a thick consistency using a little water.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan on low heat and add mustard seeds and red chilies.
1. Fry them till they attain a deep color and a smoky flavor.
1. Next, add in the sesame seeds.
1. Add curry leaves and once everything starts crackling, remove from the heat and add it to the batter.
1. To make handvo, grease a medium sized non stick pan with a little oil.
1. Heat it and pour in the batter.
1. Sprinkle the black and white sesame seeds, cover the pan and reduce the heat.
1. Cook for approximately 10 minutes.
1. When cooked, it will leave sides.
1. Turn it over and cook for another 5 minutes.
1. Remove from the pan and slightly cool the handvo.
1. It is ready to be served.
1. Vegetable Handvo Recipe is easy to make and you can also pack it in your and your kid's lunch box with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney or Lahsuni Pudina Chutney.
