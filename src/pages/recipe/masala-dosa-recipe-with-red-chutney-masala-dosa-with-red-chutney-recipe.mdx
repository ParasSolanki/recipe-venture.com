### Ingredients

-  oil - as per use.
-  1-1 / 2 cup potato masala.
-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  1 cup Onion - cut straight and thin.
-  1/4 tsp turmeric powder.
- 3-1 / 2 cup idli dosa mixture.
-  6 curry leaves - finely chopped.
-  1/2 tsp chaat masala powder.
-  coriander - little.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  2 cups potato - boiled and mashed.
-  1 tsp ginger - tighten.
-  garlic chutney - as per use.

### Instructions

1. To make Masala Dosa recipe with red chutney, we will first make potato masala for dosa.
1. Heat the oil in a pan.
1. Add the mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal and cook till it becomes light brown.
1. After getting brown, add ginger, onion, curry leaves, green chillies, asafoetida and cook till the onion becomes soft.
1. After the onion becomes soft, add turmeric powder, salt, potatoes and mix it well.
1. Cover the pan, reduce the flame and let it cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Turn off the gas and keep it aside.
1. Now to make the dosa, heat a tawa.
1. Pour the mixture of dosa on it and make a round dosa from the back of the spoon.
1. Pour oil around the dosa and let it cook for a few seconds.
1. Put some sauce on it and put potato masala over it.
1. After cooking, fold the dosa and serve.
1. Masala Dosa recipe with red chutney served with coconut chutney and filter coffee for breakfast.
