### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi) - 1/2 tsp Turmeric.
- 1/2 teaspoon Panch Phoran Masala.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 500 grams Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra) - cut to 1-inch (remove head and tail).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 tablespoons Mustard seeds - (black or yellow.
- 4 tablespoons Water - (adjust).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Assamese Bhendir Sorsori recipe (Okra In Mustard Sauce), soak mustard in water for 15-20 mins before cooking.
1. In a mixer grinder, add ginger paste and turmeric powder with mustard and grind to make a fine paste.
1. Collect the paste and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a kadai and add bay leaf and panch phoron and wait till they crackle.
1. Now add okra or bhindi and fry.
1. Add slit green chilies along with turmeric powder, red chili powder and salt to taste.
1. Cook till the okra is semi soft and then add the mustard paste and mix well.
1. Cover the lid and cook for another 5-6 mins in low flame such that the flavors incorporate well.
