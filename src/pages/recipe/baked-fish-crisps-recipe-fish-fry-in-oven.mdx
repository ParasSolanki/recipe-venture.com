### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Water.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil - optional.
- 2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 10 Fish - pieces.
- 5 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Baked Fish Crisps Recipe, clean the fish and in a large vessel add in the ingredients mentioned in the “To Marinate” table and make a fine paste and add the fish to it and mix well.
1. Allow it to marinate for half an hour.
1. Meanwhile line your baking tray with Parchment paper.
1. Preheat the oven at 180 Deg c for about 10 mins.
1. Arranged the marinated fish pieces in the lined baking tray and using grill mode grill it for 7 mins.
1. After 7 mins flip the fish and then grill for another 7 mins.
1. Now that the colour of the fish would have slightly turned dark.
1. Flip the side of the fish once again and brush it with oil if required.
1. And grill for another 5 mins.
1. Once done flip the fish once again and brush with little oil or sprinkle little oil and grill for another 5 mins.
1. Fish would have cooked by now and it is ready to be served.
1. If you feel the fish is not yet cooked grill for another 4 mins by flipping the fish sides after 2 mins.
1. Serve hot as an appetizer/starter.
1. Serve Baked Fish Crisps Recipe along with Dhaniya Pudina chutney or any other chutney of your choice.
