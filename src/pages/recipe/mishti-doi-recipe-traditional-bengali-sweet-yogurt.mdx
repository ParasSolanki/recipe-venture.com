### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - whisked.
- 1 tablespoon Water.
- 3 cups Milk.
- 3/4 cup Sugar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mishti Doi Recipe, place a heavy bottom pan on the heat.
1. Add the milk and warm it on a medium heat.
1. Reserve 1/3 of the sugar and add the remaining sugar to the milk.
1. Allow it to dissolve while the milk comes to a boil.
1. Turn the heat down and allow the milk to continue boiling until it reduces by half.
1. Then, take it off the heat and set aside.
1. In another pan,add the reserved sugar and place the pan on the heat.
1. Keeping a low-medium heat allow the sugar to warm and melt, and you will notice it gradually changes colour to brown and starts caramelizing.
1. When the sugar is a light golden bubbly mixture, remove from heat and add a tablespoon of water in the caramelized sugar and pour it in to the reduced milk.
1. Stir well until combined.
1. Let the milk cool slightly.
1. Then add the whisked yogurt, stir well and pour the mixture in earthen pots or glass bowls.
1. Let the doi set in a warm place for few hours.
1. When set, store the Mishti Doi in refrigerator and serve chilled.
1. Serve Mishti Doi after a delicious meal of Aloo Dum, Bengali Lucchi and Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita.
