### Ingredients

- 250 gram Bok Choy.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 2 tablespoon White vinegar.
- 2 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Paprika powder.
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 250 gram Button mushrooms - quartered.
- 2 Fresh Red chillies - cut into half.
- Salt - as per taste.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. To prepare the Mushroom And Bok Choy Stir Fry Recipe, we will first prepare the vegetables.
1. Clean, wash and quarter the mushrooms into even size pieces.
1. Then wash and pat dry bok choy with a kitchen towel.
1. Once the bok choy is dry, slice the stem and leaves into broad pieces of about 2 inches long.
1. Heat a teaspoon of oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
1. Once the oil is hot enough add the ginger, garlic and sliced red chilies.
1. Stir fry, stirring continuously until the aroma comes through, for about 2 minutes.
1. Take care not to burn the garlic.
1. Add the mushrooms, bok choy, soy sauce, vinegar, paprika and stir fry until the mushroom is cooked al dente.
1. Once mushrooms are cooked through, turn off the heat.
1. Drizzle some honey over the stir fry and mix to combine evenly.
1. Turn off the heatTransfer the Mushroom And Bok Choy Stir Fry to a serving plate and garnish with sesame seeds.
1. Serve it as a side dish along with Broccoli & Pepper Rice for a wholesome meal.
