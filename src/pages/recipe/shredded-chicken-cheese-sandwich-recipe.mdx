### Ingredients

- 4 Basil leaves.
- 1/2 Cup Mozzarella cheese - grated.
- 2 Tablespoon Homemade Pizza And Pasta Sauce.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 Cup Cheese - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 Chicken breasts.
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning.
- 2 tablespoon Butter - melted.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Shredded Chicken Cheese Sandwich recipe, we will start by boiling the boiling chicken breastIn a saucepan, boil water on medium flame, add the chicken pieces until it becomes tender.
1. Once it is ready & cooked, drain out the water and let it cool down.
1. Then shred the chicken using a knife.
1. Set aside.
1. Next heat up some olive oil in a heavy bottom pan on medium flame and add chopped garlic into it.
1. Saute it for a couple of minutes then add tomato pasta sauce into the pan.
1. Now add chopped basil leaves, Italian seasoning & black pepper into the pan and give it a good stir.
1. At this stage add the shredded chicken pieces into the pan and let it cook for a couple of minutes.
1. Next, add 1 tablespoon of both the cheese and cook for another 2 minutes.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. Now apply melted butter on the outer sides of sandwich bread and then stuff it with chicken.
1. Add both of the cheeses on the top.
1. Put another bread on top to finish the sandwich.
1. Pop the sandwich into the preheated oven at 150 C for 8-10 minutes.
1. Once done, slice it into two halves & enjoy the cheesy goodness.
1. Serve Shredded Chicken Cheese Sandwich along with Espresso Coffee for your evening snacks.
1. You can also make this Sandwich for your tea parties along with other snacks.
