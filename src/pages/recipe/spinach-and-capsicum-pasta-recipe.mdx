### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 2 cups Spirali pasta.
- 1/2 cup Spinach - chopped.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil - + 1 tablespoon.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spinach And Capsicum Pasta recipe, Heat 1 teaspoon oil in a wide pan and add spinach.
1. Saute the spinach till it is cooked.
1. Blend it into smooth mixture in a mixer grinder and add lemon/lime juice into it.
1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a wide pan and add chopped onions and sauté it for 1 to 2 minutes.
1. Add sliced capsicum and again sauté till both are done.
1. Now add spinach mixture, oregano, black pepper powder, green chillies and salt.
1. Mix well and cook it for about 1 to 2 minutes.
1. Add boiled pasta, mix well and sauté it for more 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Serve Spinach And Capsicum Pasta with Garlic Bread for a quick and lip smacking Italian dinner.
