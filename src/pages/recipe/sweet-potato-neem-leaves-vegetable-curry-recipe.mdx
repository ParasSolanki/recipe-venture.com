### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Neem leaves.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Sweet Potatoes.
- 1 tablespoon Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - powdered.
- 1/3 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/3 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1.5 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Sweet Potato & Neem Leaves Vegetable Curry Recipe, clean the neem leaves and tear the stem out of it.
1. Pressure cook the sweet potato for just 1 whistle or 5 minutes whichever is earlier, on full flame.
1. The pressure will take longer time if you put a lot of water.
1. Take out, peel & chop them into cubes.
1. Take a non-stick pan, add oil, hing, mustard, urad dhal, red chilli & bengal gram dal.
1. Fry them well.
1. Add the neem leaves and let it fry for 3-4 minutes until they turn slightly crispy.
1. Add the chopped sweet potato and salt.
1. Cook well until the sweet potato is fried well.
1. Garnish with roasted peanut powder.
1. Serve Sweet Potato & Neem Leaves Vegetable Curry Recipe with hot steaming rasam rice.
