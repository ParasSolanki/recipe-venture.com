### Ingredients

- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds - crushed.
- 1 cup Jaggery.
- 900 ml Coconut milk.
- 2 tablespoons Ghee - or coconut oil.
- 100 grams Rice flour.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Karnataka Style Haalbai Recipe, get pep with all the ingredients first.
1. Keep a plate or moulds well greased to pour the haalbai mixture when done.
1. Mix all the ingredients (except pounded cardamom and ghee) together such that the rice flour is well dissolved in coconut milk.
1. In a saucepan or a heavy bottomed kadai, bring the mixture to boil, on a medium low flame and add cardamom powder and ghee.
1. Keep stirring continually since the rice flour might tend to stick to the bottom.
1. Keep stirring till the mixture comes together as a lump.
1. Pour the haalbai mixture on the greased plate/mould and cut to diamond shapes when the haalbai cools a bit and firms up.
1. Serve Karnataka Style Haalbai Recipe warm or chilled, as a dessert after festive lunch or during evenings with tea.
1. Optionally, you can garnish with poppy seeds when the sweet is poured onto the plate.
