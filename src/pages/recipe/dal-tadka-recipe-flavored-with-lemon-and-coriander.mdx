### Ingredients

- Lemon juice - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta) - torn into half.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Dal Tadka Recipe, cook the lentils (split moong dal) with 2 and half cups of water, turmeric powder and salt until soft.
1. Whisk the dal if you like a finer texture, else let the dal have grainy texture.
1. Allow it to rest in the pan/pressure cooker it was cooked.
1. Heat a teaspoon of ghee in a small pan; add in the cumin seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Next add in the ginger, green chillies and bay leaf and stir fry for a few seconds.
1. Add the seasoning to the cooked lentils.
1. Give the lentils along with the seasoning a brisk boil , add in the butter and adjust the salt levels to suite your taste.
1. After a couple of minutes of boiling the dal, turn off the the heat, squeeze in the lemon juice and stir in the chopped coriander leaves.
1. Transfer the Dal Tadka to a serving bowl and Serve Dal Tadka along with Phulkas and Kachumber Salad for a weekday meal.
