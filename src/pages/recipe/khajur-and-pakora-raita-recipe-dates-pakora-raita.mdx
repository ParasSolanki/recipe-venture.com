### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 tablespoons All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 6 Dates - deseeded and cut length ways.
- 1 pinch Cooking soda.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- Red Chilli powder - to sprinkle.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Khajur And Pakora Raita Recipe, let’s make the pakoris first for the raita.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine besan, maida, salt, using water make a thick batter which is of dropping consistency.
1. Heat a paniyaram pan on medium heat, add a little oil in each one of the cavities, once the pan is steaming hot, drop a tablespoon of pakora batter in the cavities.
1. Cook for 3-4 minutes on one side, flip over and cook on the other side.
1. Once cooked, remove them from the pan and drop them directly in a bowl of hot water.
1. Alternatively, you can also fry the pakoras in oil in a kadai.
1. Drain the pakoras and set aside.
1. The next step is to make the raita.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the yogurt, cumin powder, pepper powder, chaat masala and salt.
1. Whisk the mixture with 1/4 cup of water.
1. Finally, add in the dates and the drained pakoras.
1. Garnish with a sprinkle of red chili powder.
1. Serve this Khajur And Pakora Raita Recipe with Mande Roti Recipe and Aloo Beetroot and Baby Corn Mixed Sabzi Recipe for a weekday meal with your family.
