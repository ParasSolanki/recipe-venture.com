### Ingredients

- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Honey - or jaggery (optional).
- Salt - or kala namak to taste.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - small bunch.
- 1 Bottle gourd (lauki) - peeled and diced.
- Lemon juice - from 2 lemons.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bottle Gourd & Mint Vegetable Juice Recipe (Lauki Juice), place the bottle gourd, ginger and mint leaves into a Juicer.
1. Run the juicer until all the juice comes out.
1. You can add a little more water to the juicer and get more of the nutrition from the existing pulp.
1. Pour the Bottle Gourd Juice (Lauki Juice) into a Jug, add the salt to taste, juice from the lemon and serve it for breakfast or the first thing in the morning before breakfast.
