### Ingredients

- 2 cups All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Dried oregano.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 1 cup Lukewarm Water - (adjust).
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli flakes - (adjust).
- 2 tablespoons Black sesame seeds.

### Instructions

1. To make Crispy Lavash Recipe | Lebanese Bread (Eggless), Take a bowl or stand mixer, add salt, oregano, chilli flakes and olive oil, and mix everything well.
1. Now add warm water little by little, and start kneading the dough till crumbly.
1. Knead further.
1. Once the dough comes together and soft enough, then cover it with a damp cloth and let it rest for 25 minutes.
1. Preheat oven at 180 degree C.
1. Divide the dough into 7 to 8 equal size balls.
1. Dust one ball with flour and roll it into a circle as thin as possible.
1. cut each circle into 5-6 triangles and sprinkle black and white sesame seeds on top.
1. Arrange them on a greased baking tray and bake it for 10-12mins or until light brown and crisp.
1. Take it out and let them cool.
1. Serve Crispy Lavash Recipe | Lebanese Bread (Eggless) with Roasted Broccoli And Potato Dip Recipe or your favourite dip.
