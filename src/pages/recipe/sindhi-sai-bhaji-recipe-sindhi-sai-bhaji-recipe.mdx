### Ingredients

-  1/2 cup gourd - finely chopped.
-  1 tsp turmeric powder.
-  1 1/2 cup carrots - finely chopped.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 cup green beans - finely chopped.
-  1 tomato - Finely chop.
-  1 tablespoon Ghee.
-  1 tablespoon ghee.
-  1/4 cup green moong dal - split.
-  1 raw mango - finely chopped.
-  1/4 cup chana dal - washed and soaked.
-  1 cup fenugreek - finely chopped.
-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 cup spinach - finely chopped.
-  1/2 cup eggplant - finely chopped.
-  1 inch ginger - finely chopped.
-  1 tablespoon Red chilli powder.
-  1 tablespoon coriander powder.
-  1/2 cup heart leaves - finely chopped.
-  4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
-  2 onions - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make Sindhi Sai Bhaji recipe, first wash and soak chana dal and green moong dal in water for 30 minutes.
1. Wash and cut the sari vegetable thoroughly and keep it separately.
1. Heat the oil in a pressure cooker.
1. Add onion and cook till it becomes soft.
1. Now add ginger, green chilies and cook for 30 seconds.
1. After 30 seconds, add carrots, beans, brinjals, gourd, tomatoes and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Add chana dal, green moong dal, coriander powder, turmeric powder, salt and cook for 1 minute.
1. Now add spinach, fenugreek and mix.
1. Add 2 cups of water, close the cooker and cook until 4 to 5 cities come.
1. Turn off the gas and let the pressure drain automatically.
1. With the help of masher, mash it and keep it aside.
1. For tempering, heat ghee in a tempering pan.
1. Add the garlic and cook till it turns light brown.
1. Now add red chillies to it and turn off the gas after 10 seconds.
1. Delve, mix and serve in the bhaji.
1. Serve Sindhi Sai Bhaji recipe with Bhuga rice, Sidhi Koki and gourd raita for dinner.
