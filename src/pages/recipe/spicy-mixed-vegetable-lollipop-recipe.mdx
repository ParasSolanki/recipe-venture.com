### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spicy Mixed Vegetable Lollipop Recipe add oil into a pre heated pan and add onions.
1. Saute the onions till they turn translucent and once done add the carrot and cabbage.
1. Saute the vegetables for 2 to three minutes till they get cooked.
1. Once the vegetables are cooked, add all the spice powders like the red chili powder, turmeric powder,coriander powder and the garam masala and mix well to combine.
1. Season it with salt and pepper.
1. Cover and cook it for 2 more minutes.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. Add these saut√©ed vegetables to the boiled potatoes and give it a nice mash using a potato masher and combine all the ingredients well.
1. Divide the mixture into lemon sized balls.
1. Heat a paniyaram pan, add oil into each cavity and place these small balls in each cavity and cook the potato lollipops till it is golden brown on one side.
1. Turn the Spicy Mixed Vegetable balls and cook on the other side as well.
1. Once the Spicy Mixed Vegetable balls are evenly browned and cooked through remove them onto an oil absorbent paper.
1. Insert a tooth pick into each ball and place on a serving platter and serve.
1. Serve the  Spicy Mixed Vegetable Lollipop Recipe as a party appetizers along with Green Chutney and a meal of Spicy Paneer Pulao With Vegetables, Navratan Korma and Burani Raita.
