### Ingredients

-  1 asafoetida - a pinch.
-  2 tbsp mustard oil - or any other oil.
-  1 tsp coriander powder.
-  2 tsp tamarind paste.
-  1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds.
-  1 tsp cumin powder.
-  1 green chili - chopped.
-  1 bay leaf.
-  1 tsp cumin seeds.
-  1/2 tsp amchur powder.
-  1/2 tsp garam masala powder.
-  1 tsp kasoori methi.
-  1/2 tsp chaat masala powder.
-  1/3 cup tomato puree.
-  1/3 cup green coriander - finely chopped.
-  1 tsp red Chilli powder.
-  1/2 tsp Turmeric powder.
- 5 Potatoes - peeled and chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make Patiala Potato Recipe, first peel and chop the potatoes.
1. Soak them in water and keep them separately.
1. Now heat mustard oil in a pan.
1. After heating, add cumin seeds, bay leaves, fenugreek seeds, asafoetida and cook until the cumin seeds crackle.
1. Now take out the potatoes from the water and put them in the pan.
1. After 1 minute, add green chili, salt, turmeric powder and mix it well.
1. Cover the pan and cook until the potatoes are cooked well.
1. It will take 12 to 15 minutes.
1. After this add red chili powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, mango powder, chaat masala powder and garam masala powder.
1. Add some water and mix well.
1. After 1 minute add tomato puree, tamarind paste and mix well.
1. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes, garnish with green coriander and serve.
1. Serve the Patiala Aloo Recipe with dal tempering and phulka for dinner.
