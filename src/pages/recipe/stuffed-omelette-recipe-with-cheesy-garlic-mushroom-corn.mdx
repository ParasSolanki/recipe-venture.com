### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Nutralite Garlic & Oregano Spread.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Nutralite Garlic & Oregano Spread.
- 1 sprig Basil leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Nutralite Cheesy Garlic Mayo.
- 1/4 cup Sweet corn.
- 100 grams Button mushrooms - sliced.
- 4 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Stuffed Omelette Recipe With Cheesy Garlic Mushroom & Corn, break the eggs into a bowl sprinkle some salt, pepper and whisk till it combines well.
1. Heat a flat skillet with oil, add mushroom and saute until all the water has been evaporated.
1. Add sweet corn along with Nutralite Cheesy Garlic Mayo.
1. Toss well to combine.
1. Keep the stuffing aside.
1. Heat a another flat nonstick skillet to make the omelette, Add a tablespoon of Nutralite Garlic & Oregano Spread.
1. Keep the heat to medium and slowly add the whisked eggs.
1. Allow the egg to cook for 5 minutes until both sides are cooked.
1. Once done,  add saut√©ed mushroom and sweet corn to one side and fold.
1. Turn off the heat and transfer the Stuffed Omelette Recipe With Cheesy Garlic Mushroom & Corn to a serving platter and serve hot.
1. Serve the Stuffed Omelette Recipe With Cheesy Garlic Mushroom & Corn along with some baked beans, Semolina French Toast Recipe and warm chai to make your breakfast complete.
