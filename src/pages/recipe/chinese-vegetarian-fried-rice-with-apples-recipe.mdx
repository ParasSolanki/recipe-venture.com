### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 Apples - diced.
- 1 Red Yellow or Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Cashew nuts.
- 2 cups Basmati rice.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - as needed.
- Water - as needed.
- 1 tablespoon Raisins.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Chinese Vegetarian Fried Rice With Apples Recipe, soak basmati rice for 25-30 minutes.
1. Then cook rice with enough water.
1. Once the rice is cooked, spread it on a plate and allow to cool down completely.
1. Heat oil in a pan.
1. Add the cashews, raisins and fry them till golden.
1. Take out from oil and set aside.
1. To the same pan, add shredded ginger.
1. Saute till aromatic and raw aroma disappears.
1. Add capsicum, sprinkle little salt and fry till they are half cooked on a high flame.
1. Then add apple, soya sauce and sugar.
1. Within a few minutes, the apples are partially cooked and release a sweetish aroma.
1. Do not over saute since the apples are best when still crunchy.
1. Add the cooked long grain basmati rice, fried cashews and raisins and finally, pepper powder.
1. Mix gently without breaking rice grains on high flame for a minute.
1. Reduce the flame and fry for a couple of minutes more until everything is combined well.
1. Finally switch off the flame.
1. Serve Chinese Vegetarian Fried Rice With Apples with Vegetarian Wonton Soup Recipe and Paneer Satay Recipe on sides.
