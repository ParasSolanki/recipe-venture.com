### Ingredients

-  1 capsicum (green) - finely chopped.
-  4 sprig green onions - chopped.
-  1 tsp red chilli sauce.
-  as per taste.
- 1 Hakka Noodles - whole packet.
-  1/2 cup green beans - small spoon.
-  1/2 tsp soy sauce.
-  1/2 tsp chilli vinegar.
-  1 cup cabbage - small spoon.
-  1/2 tsp pepper powder.
-  1 inch ginger - finely chopped.
-  1 carrot - thin and straight cut.
-  Salt - 1 tbsp oil.
-  2 onions - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make Vegetarian Hakka Noodles recipe, first boil the noodles according to the method given on the packet.
1. After boiling keep the bottom of the sieve and cool it well.
1. After the water runs out, add some oil and mix, so that the noodles do not stick to each other.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add onion, ginger and cook till the onion becomes soft.
1. After the onion is thin, add capsicum, carrot, beans, cabbage, green onion, salt, pepper and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Now add soy sauce, chilli sauce, chilli vinegar, mix and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Now add cooked noodles and mix it well.
1. Turn off the flame and serve in a bowl.
1. Serve the Vegetarian Hakka Noodles recipe for dinner with Vegetarian Manchurian.
