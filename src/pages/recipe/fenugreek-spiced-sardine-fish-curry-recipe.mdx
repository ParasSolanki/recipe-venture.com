### Ingredients

- Curry leaves - a few sprigs.
- 1-1/2 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 4 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoons Tamarind Paste.
- 3/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 6 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 tablespoon Red Chilli powder - (adjust to taste).
- 1-1/2 Onion - coarsely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- Gingelly oil - or sunflower oil to cook.
- 500 grams Sardine fish - cleaned and chopped.
- 10 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Fenugreek Spiced Sardine Fish Curry Recipe, prepare all the ingredients first, clean the fish and chop it to desired size.
1. Now heat a tawa with some gingelly oil.
1. Once it is heated, add the jeera, whole black peppercorns, dry red chillies, coriander seeds on medium heat and roast them till a nice aroma emerges.
1. Add the coarsely chopped onions, and garlic and saute a bit till the onions are translucent.
1. Once done, add the fresh grated coconut and stir for a minute or so, till toasted.
1. Switch off heat once done, and allow to cool on a plate, pot which we will need this to grind.
1. Meanwhile in the same kadai/pot, heat a bit of gingelly oil, add cumin seeds and allow to sizzle.
1. After a few seconds of adding cumin seeds, add the curry leaves, stir a bit so that the curry leaves splutter.
1. Immediately add the finely chopped onions and fenugreek seeds, saute and cook till the onions are translucent.
1. To the kadai, add chopped tomatoes, and continue cooking on medium heat till the tomatoes turn mushy.
1. Turn off till the ground paste is ready.
1. By this time, the oil roasted ingredients would be cooled.
1. Take those in a mixer grinder and grind them into a smooth paste by adding enough water.
1. Add this paste to the mushy tomatoes in the kadai on medium heat and cook further for about 5 minutes or the gravy kind of comes together from sides.
1. Add the tamarind paste and required water for the gravy.
1. After a minutes, add in the sardine fish pieces, required salt and cook covered for about 8-10 minutes on medium heat or till the fish is cooked well.
1. Serve Fenugreek Spiced Sardine Fish Curry Recipe with Chicken Cutlet Recipe and Steamed Rice Recipe  or Ghee Rice Recipe | Neychoru for a complete meal.
