### Ingredients

-  6 cloves garlic - finely chopped.
-  1/2 tsp turmeric powder.
-  1 cup coconut milk.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 tsp cumin.
-  1/2 tsp cumin powder.
-  1/4 cup water.
-  1 tsp ghee.
-  1/2 tsp Oil.
-  1/2 cup green peas - boil.
-  2 green chillies.
-  1 tsp pepper powder.
-  1/2 tsp black pepper powder.
- 300 grams Paneer - cut.
-  1 capsicum (red) - cut.
-  1 inch ginger - finely chopped.
-  1/2 cup coriander - Finely chop.
-  1/2 cup coconut - tighten.
-  1 tsp lemon juice.
-  1/3 cup water.
-  6 cashews - soak in hot water.
-  1 inch cinnamon.
-  2 cardamom.
-  1/2 tsp chaat masala powder.
-  2 long.
-  2 onions - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Nawabi Paneer Curry Recipe, first of all, add cheese, lemon juice, salt, cumin powder, chaat masala powder, black pepper powder and oil to a mixing bowl.
1. Mix and keep aside for 20 minutes.
1. Heat oil in a skillet.
1. Add paneer to it and cook till it turns golden brown from both the sides.
1. Once cooked, take it out separately.
1. Now to make curry, firstly put coconut, cashew, green chilli, cinnamon, long, cardamom, cumin, water in a mixer jar and grind it well.
1. Keep it separately.
1. Heat ghee in a pan.
1. Add ginger, garlic and cook for 10 seconds.
1. Add onions and cook till the onion becomes soft.
1. It will take 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Now add ground spices, salt, turmeric powder, black pepper powder and cook it for 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes add coconut milk to it and mix well.
1. After 1 minute, add peas, capsicum, water, coriander and let it boil.
1. Turn off the gas after boiling and add cottage cheese and serve.
1. Serve the Nawabi Paneer Curry Recipe with Tawa Paratha and Bundi Raita for dinner.
