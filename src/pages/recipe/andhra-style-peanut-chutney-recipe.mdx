### Ingredients

-  1 tsp rye.
-  3 cloves garlic.
-  5 dry red chillies.
-  1 sprig curry leaves.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 tsp white urad dal.
-  2 tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1 cup peanuts - peel.
-  1 tsp oil.

### Instructions

1. To make Andhra Style Peanut Chutney Recipe, first put peanuts, red chillies in a pan and cook them until the peanuts turn light brown.
1. After cooking, turn off the gas and keep it aside.
1. Now heat the water in a saucepan.
1. Add tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
1. Remove the skin from the tomato and keep it aside.
1. Now in a mixer grinder add tomato, peanut mixture, garlic, salt, some water and grind it well to make a paste.
1. Now heat the oil in a tempering pan for tempering.
1. Add urad dal, curry leaves and cook for 1 minute.
1. Add this tempering to the chutney, mix and serve.
1. Serve Andhra Style Peanut Chutney Recipe with Ghee Roast Dosa and Filter Coffee for breakfast.
