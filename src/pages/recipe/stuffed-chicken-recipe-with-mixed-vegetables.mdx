### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Tomato Ketchup.
- 1 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Chicken - (whole).
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - boiled and mashed.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - grated and blanched.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder - freshly ground.
- 3 tablespoon Tandoori masala.
- 1/4 cup Fresh cream.
- 2 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped and more to garnish.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Lemon - juiced.
- 4 tablespoon Cashew nuts - ground to paste.
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 2 tablespoon Green peas (Matar) - boiled.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Stuffed Chicken Recipe With Mixed Vegetables, wash and clean the chicken thoroughly well.
1. Now marinate the whole chicken with tandoori masala, curd, salt and lemon juice.
1. Let the chicken marinate for about 2-3 hours.
1. After 2-3 hours, bake the marinated chicken at 425 F for 45 minutes.
1. Make stuffing in a large mixing bowl by mixing boiled potatoes, Carrots ,peas, coriander leaves,chaat masala , salt and black pepper.
1. Set it aside.
1. Preheat oven to 350 degree F.
1. Heat oil in a pan on medium flame, to this add tomato puree, tomato ketchup, salt, red chilli powder and the cashew paste.
1. Once the mixture begins to bubble up, turn down the heat, and add in the cream.
1. Simmer for a couple of minutes more until the gravy is nice thick and creamy.
1. Now cut open the chicken to slice in middle and stuff with vegetable stuffing and top it with tomato gravy then bake it on 350 degrees F for 15 minutes.
1. Serve Stuffed Chicken Recipe With Mixed Vegetables along with Butter Garlic Naan Recipe (Garlic Flavoured Flat Bread) and Carrot Cucumber Tomato Salad with Lemon and Coriander Recipe.
