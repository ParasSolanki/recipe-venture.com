### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 2 Potato (Aloo) - cooked and smashed.
- 1/2 teaspoon Fennel Powder.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- 1 cup Rajma (Large Kidney Beans) - soaked and pressure cooked.
- 3 tablespoons All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Rajma Cutlet, firstly take the potatoes and kidney beans in a mixing bowl.
1. Add red chilli powder, fennel seed powder, garam masala powder and salt in the mixture and mix everything well.
1. Once it is done, make medium sized patties out of this mixture.
1. In a different bowl, mix all-purpose flour with enough water to make a paste.
1. Dip the patties in the flour paste and roll them gently in the bread crumbs.
1. Once finished, arrange them in the fridge for an hour.
1. After an hour, heat oil in a deep fry pan to fry the cutlets.
1. Drop the cutlets and cook on both sides until they turn golden brown.
1. Serve Rajma Cutlets with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney and Masala Chai during your tea time break.
