### Ingredients

- pinch Black Salt (Kala Namak) - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 Stalk Celery.
- 2 Beetroot - diced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Beetroot And Celery Juice Recipe, get all the ingredients ready and all you need to do is add the raw cut vegetables into the Juicer.
1. Extract the first juice and add more water to get the 2nd extract out from the pulp.
1. Pour the juice into a jar, add the salt and lemon if required.
1. Check the taste and adjust the consistency of the juice by adding more water if required.
1. Serve the Beetroot And Celery Juice immediately along with Whole Wheat Pancake Recipe With Banana, Honey And Nuts.
1. to get the maximum benefits from the raw vegetables.
