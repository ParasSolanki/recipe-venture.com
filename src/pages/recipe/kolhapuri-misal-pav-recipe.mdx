### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Tamarind Paste.
- 1 cup Bean Sprouts.
- 3 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1/2 cup Sev.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 5 Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 3 teaspoon Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - fried.
- 1/2 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 tablespoon Fresh coconut - shredded.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Salt - as required.
- 2 teaspoon Kolhapuri Masala.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - boiled.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 tablespoon Dry coconut (kopra) - shredded.
- 2 tablespoon Kolhapuri Masala.
- 1/2 teaspoon Tamarind Paste.
- 2 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 tablespoon Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - roasted.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Kolhapuri Misal Pav, we have to first make the Kolhapuri masala, dry roast coriander seeds, cumin seeds, dry shredded coconut, black peppercorn, cloves , fennel seeds, sesame seeds.
1. Cool them for a while and grind them to fine powder.
1. Now for the Usal, in a pan add oil and once it gets heated add mustard seeds, cumin seeds and when they splutter add asafoetida and curry leaves.
1. Now add onions and once onions turn golden add peanuts, sprouted matki, potato, green chillies, turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt and kolhapuri masala.
1. Mix well and add 5-6 cups of water and cook on medium heat.
1. See to that the sprouts are cooked and are tender, once everything is cooked add tamarind pulp and keep it in low flame for 2 minutes.
1. For Rassa or Kat (Spicy Gravy), heat oil in a pan and add cumin seeds and once they splutter add cinnamon stick, asafoetida, garlic cloves, ginger, chopped onions and shredded coconut.
1. Saute it till onions gets translucent.
1. Add tomatoes, red chilli powder, kolhapuri masala and cook it for another 2 minutes.
1. Cool the mixture completely and grind to a smooth paste.
1. If needed add some water.
1. Heat oil in another pan; add turmeric powder, kat paste, tamarind pulp, salt and 1 to 1 1/2 cups of water.
1. Mix well and bring it to boil and simmer for 5 minutes.
1. Now to serve the Kolhapuri Usal Pav, in a serving bowl transfer the gravy and add some Farsan and sprouts, now pour the gravy and top it with chopped onion, tomato, cilantro and save.
1. Serve the Kolhapuri Usal with some hot pav toasted in butter as a Delicious Tea Time Snack for Kids after School.
