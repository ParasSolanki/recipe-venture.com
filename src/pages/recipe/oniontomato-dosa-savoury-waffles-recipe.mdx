### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Green Chilli Paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Idli Dosa Batter.
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 1 Tomato - finely diced.
- 6 Curry leaves - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Onion-Tomato Dosa Savoury Waffles Recipe, in a bowl add the dosa/Idli batter, add the remaining ingredients and mix well.
1. Preheat the waffle maker, grease the waffle maker lightly using a paper towel or grease it with cooking spray.
1. Ladle out 1/4 cup batter into each cavity and cook the waffles according to the instructions of your waffle maker.
1. It should take between 1-1/2 to 2 minutes depending on your waffle maker.
1. Serve Onion-Tomato Dosa Savoury Waffles Recipe with Red Chilli Coconut Chutney Recipe as a dips and side dishes for fusion breakfast.
1. This also makes quick appetizers for chaat party as well.
