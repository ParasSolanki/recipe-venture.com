### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 3 to 4 Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 2 Mango (Raw).
- Saffron strands - few for garnishing.
- 1-1/2 cups Sugar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Khatta-Meetha Aam Ka Murabba, keep all the ingredients ready, including the grated raw totapuri mangoes.
1. In a saucepan, add water and sugar on medium heat and bring it to a boil.
1. Once you see it come to a rolling boil.
1. Turn off the flame.
1.  Add the grated mango into the syrup.
1. Bring back to flame and cook it for 5 to 6 minutes and then add red chilli powder, cardamom powder and a pinch of salt.
1. Cook further for 2 more minutes and then switch off the gas.
1. Garnish Khatta-Meetha Aam Ka Murabba with saffron stands and serve it as a side dish with a hearty Rajasthani Thali with rice or Roti and Rajasthani gatte ki sabzi and Dal fry.
