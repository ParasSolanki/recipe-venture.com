### Ingredients

- 4 Carrots (Gajjar) - roughly diced.
- Lemon juice - from one lemon (optional).
- Honey - (optional).
- 1 inch Ginger.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Carrot Ginger Juice Recipe first add the carrots and ginger to the Juicer and extract the juice out.
1. Add a little more water, so we can extract more from the pulp of the carrot juice that remains in the juicer.
1. Pour the juice into a jar and squeeze the juice from one lemon.
1. Add just the taste by adding salt or honey of required.
1. You can also adjust the concentration of the juice by adding water or even squeezing some orange juice.
1. Serve the Carrot Ginger Juice as soon as it is made to get maximum benefits.
1. Serve it with Grilled Sub Sandwich Recipes With Paneer & Roasted Vegetables for a healthy breakfast.
