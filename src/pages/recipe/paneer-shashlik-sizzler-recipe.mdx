### Ingredients

- 1 Tomato - diced.
- 100 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese).
- 1 tablespoon Tomato Ketchup.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 2 Onions - diced.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds - crushed.
- 1 tablespoon Sugar.
- Iceberg lettuce - Lettuce.
- Onion - sliced to circles and grilled.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 1 cup Cooked rice.
- 1/2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Green Chillies - slit.
- 1/2 Bay leaf (tej patta) - torn.
- 1 cup Green peas (Matar) - steamed.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 3 Red Yellow and Green Bell Peppers (Capsicum) - (one each).
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Tomatoes - sliced and grilled.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Paneer Shashlik Sizzler Recipe,we will prepare the shashlik first.
1. Take a bowl and add sugar, pepper powder, vinegar, soy sauce, garam masala powder, tomato ketchup and salt.
1. Take a whisk and mix all ingredients well.
1. Add diced onions, bell peppers, paneer, tomatoes, mix and rest for an hour.
1. Once they have marinated for an hour, take a skewer and skew all vegetables one after one, alternating with your pattern.
1. Put in on hot grill pan, drizzle a little oil and cook on low medium flame till all sides are done.
1. Take care not to burn veggies or paneer.
1. Peas PulaoTo prepare peas pulao, in a kadai, heat a little ghee and sizzle cumin seeds.
1. Once the cumin seeds have sizzled add bay leaf, cardamom, clove, cinnamon and saute till aromatic.
1. Next add the green chillies peas and salt to taste .
1. Cook covered for a minute.
1. Add cooked rice and stir carefully until the flavours blend into rice.
1. Check for salt and adjust.
1. Switch off flame.
1. Arranging the Paneer Shashlik Sizzler sizzler Take a sizzler plate (cast iron) and add few drops of oil all over.
1. Heat the sizzler plate for 15 minutes in an oven or on the flame.
1. Line it with cabbage or lettuce leaves.
1. Place flavored peas pulao on it.
1. On the sides place the grilled onions and tomatoes.
1. Serve Paneer Shashlik sizzler with Masala Soda Shikanji  and Rajma Cutlet for your next party.
