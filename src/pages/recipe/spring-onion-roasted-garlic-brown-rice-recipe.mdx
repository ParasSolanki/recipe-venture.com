### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1-1/2 cup Brown Rice - washed and soaked.
- 1/4 cup Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - bulbs.
- 6 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Spring Onion Greens.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Spring Onion & Roasted Garlic Brown Rice Recipe, keep the rice soaked for a minimum of 30 minutes.
1. Heat a pressure cooker with olive oil on medium flame, to this add the garlic cloves and roast until it turns into a light brown colour.
1. Next add in the spring onions bulbs and cook until it turns transparent.
1. This will take only about a minute or two.
1. Next add in the salt, drained brown rice and 2-1/2 cups of water and close the pressure cooker.
1. Pressure cook for about 4-5 whistles and turn off the flame.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally before opening the pressure cooker.
1. Once the pressure is released, open  the pressure cooker and fluff the Spring Onion & Roasted Garlic Brown Rice  with a fork, add in the finely chopped spring onions greens and give it a gentle mix, without breaking the rice grains.
1. Serve Spring Onion & Roasted Garlic Brown Rice along with Chicken with Creamy Mushroom Sauce Recipe or Spinach Ricotta Balls in Garlic Tomato Basil Sauce Recipe, and a side of Pan Roasted Zucchini & Mushroom Recipe.
1. Serve Greek Yogurt Chocolate Mousse Recipe as dessert.
