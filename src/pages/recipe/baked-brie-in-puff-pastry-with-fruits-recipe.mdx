### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Honey.
- Fresh fruits - for serving.
- 1 Puff Pastry Sheet - (about 6*6 inches).
- 125 grams Brie cheese.
- 1 Apple - peeled and chopped.
- 1 pinch Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- Crackers - for serving.
- 1/2 Whole Egg - whisked.
- 6 Strawberries - hulled and chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Baked Brie in puff pastry with fruits first pre-heat the oven to 190 deg C.
1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
1. Place the rolled pastry on the baking sheet.
1. Place the cheese wheel at the centre of the pastry.
1. Mix the apple, strawberries, honey and cinnamon.
1. Top the cheese with the fruits.
1. Gather up the pastry from the edges and enclose the cheese and fruits completely.
1. Press around so that it is neatly packed.
1. Brush the egg over the top and sides of the pastry.
1. Bake for about 20-25 minutes or till the pastry is golden.
1. Serve Baked Brie In Puff Pastry With Fruits Recipe with more fruits and crackers.
