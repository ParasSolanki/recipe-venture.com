### Ingredients

- 5 Star anise.
- 3 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 3 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 3 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 6 Cloves (Laung).

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Chinese 5 spice powder by grinding all the ingredients in a hand blender to a coarse powderOnce done store it in an airtight container for further use.
1. Serve the Chinese 5 spice powder in a Zhejiang Style Crispy Five Spice Powder Spring Roll Recipe.
