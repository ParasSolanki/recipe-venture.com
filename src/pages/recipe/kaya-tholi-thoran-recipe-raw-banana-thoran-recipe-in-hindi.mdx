### Ingredients

-  3 cloves garlic.
-  1 tablespoon oil.
-  2 cups water.
-  8 curry leaves.
-  1/2 cup coconut - grated.
-  4 green chillies.
-  2 dry red chillies.
-  salt.
-  4 small onions.
-  1/4 teaspoon mustard.
-  1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder.
- 2 raw banana peel.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin.

### Instructions

1. To make Kaya Tholi Thoran, first take out the peel of raw banana and cut it into small pieces.
1. Pour it in a saucepan with turmeric powder and 2 cups of water and cook till it becomes soft.
1. Now put the grinding ingredients in a mixer grinder and grind it.
1. Heat oil in a small pan.
1. Add mustard seeds and cook for 10 seconds.
1. Add curry leaves, dry red chillies and cook for 10 seconds.
1. Now add ground spices and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. After 3 minutes add raw banana and mix it.
1. Cook and serve for 1 to 2 minutes.
1. Serve Kaya Tholi Thoran with kirai sambar and rice for dinner.
