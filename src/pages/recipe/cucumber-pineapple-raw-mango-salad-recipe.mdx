### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Mango (Raw) - diced.
- 1/4 cup Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- 1/2 cup Pineapple - diced.
- 1 cup Cucumber - diced.
- 2 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina) - roughly torn.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cucumber Pineapple Raw mango salad Recipe, wash and cut the fruits and vegetables.
1. In a mixing bowl, add the diced cucumber, pineapple and raw mango and mix in the Dhaniya Pudina Chutney and mint leaves.
1. Mix Cucumber Pineapple Raw mango salad until well combined.
1. Keep it aside for some time so that the fruits and vegetables absorbs the flavour.
1. Serve the salad chilled.
1. Serve the Cucumber Pineapple Raw Mango Salad Recipe along with Thakkali Sadam Recipe (Spicy Tomato Rice) and Papad Raita Recipe for a complete meal.
