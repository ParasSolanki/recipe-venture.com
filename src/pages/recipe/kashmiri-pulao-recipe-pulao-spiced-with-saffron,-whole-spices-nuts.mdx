### Ingredients

- 1-2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 3 tablespoon Ghee.
- 3 cups Basmati rice - soaked for 45 minutes.
- 2 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 4-5 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 3 cups Water.
- 1 tablespoon Cashew nuts - roughly broken.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Walnuts - roughly chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger - thinly sliced.
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 1/4 cup Apples - diced small.
- Saffron strands - few strands soaked in warm milk.
- 1 tablespoon Badam (Almond) - roughly chopped.
- 1/2 tablespoon Raisins.
- 1/4 cup Pineapple - diced small.
- 2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 2-1/2 cups Milk.
- 1 tablespoon Pistachios - roughly chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 3/4 teaspoon Dry ginger powder.
- 1/4 cup Caramelized onions.
- 6 Cloves (Laung).
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kashmiri Pulao Recipe, first wash and soak the rice for about 45 minutes.
1. After 45 minutes, drain the water and keep aside.
1. In a heavy bottom sauce pan, heat 2 tablespoons of ghee.
1. Add bay leaf, shahjeera, black cardamom, cloves, green cardamom, cinnamon sticks and fry them for few seconds until they release a roasted aroma.
1. Add a tablespoon of broken cashews and fry for another minute until the cashews are roasted golden.
1. Add ginger juliennes, ginger powder, fennel powder, Kashmiri chili powder, salt and the soaked rice and fry for few seconds.
1. Add milk, saffron milk, water and bring the pulao to a brisk boil.
1. Once it comes to a boil, turn the heat to low, cover the pan and allow the rice to cook, until all the liquid has be absorbed.
1. Meanwhile, heat another tablespoon of oil in the pan and roast the dry fruits until light golden brown.
1. Take it off the heat and keep aside.
1. Once the rice is done, add roasted nuts to the rice and gently fluff up the rice with the fork.
1. Transfer the rice to a serving platter, garnish it with fried onions and diced fresh fruits.
1. Serve the Kashmiri Pulao Recipe along with Paneer Tikka Masala, Butter Naan and Mint Raita to make a complete dinner.
