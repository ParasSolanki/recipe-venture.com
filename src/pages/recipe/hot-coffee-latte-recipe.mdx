### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cups Milk.
- 2 teaspoons Sugar.
- 1/2 cup Coffee Decoction.
- 2 pinch Cocoa Powder - (optional).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Hot Coffee Latte Recipe, in a large jar with leak-proof lid, add milk and shake vigorously for about 1 minute or two.
1. Open the lid and microwave the jar on a high power for 2 minutes.
1. Make sure the container has space for the milk to foam up.
1. In a big mug, add the espresso in equal quantity and hold the mug in a tilted position.
1. Keep adding the frothed milk into one cup from the mug's edge in a steady pattern till the cup is full such that it forms a coffee art on top.
1. You can optionally sprinkle with a dash of coa powder on top.
1. Serve Hot Coffee Latte Recipe with Whole Wheat Apple Muffins Recipe or a Spiced Saffron & Coconut Pumpkin Cake Recipe for a rainy evening.
