### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Rice - soaked in warm water for 5 minutes.
- 2 sprigs Curry leaves.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Coconut Oil.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 200 grams Raw papaya - peeled and diced.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Raw Papaya Mor Kooru recipe,  first prep all the ingredients and keep them handy.
1. Add the raw papaya into the pressure cooker, along with 1/4 cup of water and salt.
1. Pressure cook for two whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Release the pressure immediately, by placing the cooker under cold water so the papaya does not get over cooked.
1. Blend the coconut, cumin seeds, peppercorns, green chillies, rice and curry leaves using a little bit of warm water to make a smooth paste.
1. Add the cooked raw papaya into a sauce pan, the coconut paste and yogurt.
1. Stir in the salt to taste.
1. Add a little water if you want to make the consistency little thinner.
1. Place the saucepan on medium and simmer the raw papaya mor kootu for about 5 minutes until it comes together and turn off the heat.
1. Heat a tadka pan with oil; add the mustard seeds and curry leaves.
1. Allow it to crackle and pour it over the raw papaya mor kuzhambu.
1. Once done, serve warm.
1. Serve the Raw Papaya Mor Kootu along with Phulkas, Beetroot Poriyal and Pineapple Rasam Recipe for a wholesome lunch.
