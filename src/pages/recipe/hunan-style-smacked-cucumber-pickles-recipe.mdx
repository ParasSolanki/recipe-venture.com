### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Cucumber - sliced or diced.
- 4 cloves Garlic - sliced.
- 2 teaspoon Light soy sauce.
- 4 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 4 teaspoon Caster Sugar.
- 2 tablespoon Chilli oil.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli sauce.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Hunan Style Smacked Cucumber Pickles Recipe by slicing the cucumber very thinly and keeping it aside.
1. Heat a wok with oil, add chili flaxes, ginger and garlic, saut√© them for few seconds.
1. Then add in all the sauces and give it a stir.
1. Finally, switch off the heat, add the sliced cucumber and give it a toss.
1. Garnish it with chopped spring onions.
1. Serve the Hunan Style Smacked Cucumber Pickles Recipe along with Chinese Vegetarian Fried Rice With Apples Recipe to enjoy your meal.
