### Ingredients

- 10 Walnuts.
- 8 Dried Figs - cut into quarters.
- 2 tablespoons Apple cider vinegar.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 tablespoon Red Wine Vinaigrette.
- 2 Whole Eggs.
- 2 cups Arugula leaves.
- 1 teaspoons Soy sauce.
- 1 Onions - thinly sliced.
- Salt and Pepper - to sprinkle.
- 1/4 cup Black olives.
- 2 teaspoons Honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Poached Eggs Recipe With Arugula Fig Walnut Salad, we will first make the dressing for the salad.
1. To make the dressing for the Arugula Fig Walnut SaladIn a Mixing bowl, combine the honey, soy sauce, red chilli flakes and red wine vinaigrette.
1. Mix well and allow it to chill.
1. To make the Arugula Fig Walnut SaladIn a mixing bowl, combine the arugula leaves along with onions, olives, walnuts and figs, toss in the already prepared dressing and mix well.
1. Arrange the Arugula Fig Walnut Salad in a serving platter and set aside.
1. To poach the eggs Bring water to boil in a saucepan oh high heat.
1. When you see the water come to a rolling boil, add in the apple cider vinegar and reduce the heat to medium-low.
1. Break the egg in a bowl and gently slip the whole egg into the bubbling water, let the egg coagulate.
1. This will take about 6-8 minutes.
1. When you feel the egg white is firm enough to hold the runny yolk, gradually dip in a slotted spoon and remove the egg from the water.
1. Repeat the same for the other egg as well.
1. Carefully place the poached eggs over the Arugula Fig Walnut Salad, sprinkle some salt and pepper and serve.
1. Serve Poached Eggs Recipe With Arugula Fig Walnut Salad along with Maple Roasted Cinnamon Spiced Latte Recipe, some fresh fruit and nuts.
