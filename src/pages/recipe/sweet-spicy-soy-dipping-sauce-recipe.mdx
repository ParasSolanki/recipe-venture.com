### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 1 teaspoon Sriracha sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 1/8 teaspoon Paprika powder - 1/8 teaspoon paprika powder.
- 1 tablespoon Spring Onion Greens -  chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Chilli vinegar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sweet & Spicy Soy Dipping Sauce Recipe, in a mixing bowl combine the brown sugar along with water,  soy sauce, chilli vinegar, paprika powder,  spring onion greens, Sriracha sauce and salt.
1. Whisk the Soy Dipping Sauce well until well combined.
1. Rest the Soy Dipping Sauce for 30 minutes and store in a bottle or serve.
