### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- All Purpose Flour (Maida) - All-purpose flour (for dusting).
- 1 cup Black Eyed Beans (Lobia) - soaked overnight and cooked.
- 3 cups Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/4 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vegan Black Beans Stuffed Paratha, take the wheat flour and salt in a large mixing bowl add gradually the water and knead everything as a soft and smooth dough.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Meanwhile, grind the cooked black bean as bit coarse paste.
1. Heat enough oil and add the cumin seeds.
1. Once it splutters, add the grounded black-bean paste, give a stir and add immediately the red chilly powder, garam masala powder and salt.
1. Cook everything on low flame for few minutes and then switch off the heat.
1. Once the stuffing is ready, make 5-6 balls from the paratha dough.
1. Dust the balls with all purpose flour and flatten them with rolling pins.
1. Place 1-2 tablespoons of black bean masala on the flattened dough and cover the masala with the dough and roll them in ur palms again as a ball.
1. Roll them as parathas, cook these parathas on a hot Tawa drizzled with enough oil until they get well cooked.
1. Serve Vegan Black Beans Stuffed Paratha with Burani Raita or any raita of your choice and pickle.
