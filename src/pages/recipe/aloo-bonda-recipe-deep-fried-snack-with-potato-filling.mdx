### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Water - as required.
- 2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 5 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil - hot.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 2 tablespoons Rice flour.
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 10 Curry leaves - chopped.
- 1 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 2 to 3 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 3 Green Chillies - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Aloo Bonda recipe, firstly we will make the potato filling.
1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add the mustard seeds and once they splutter add the cumin seeds.
1. Add the green chillies, ginger, hing, curry leaves, turmeric powder and sauté for a few seconds.
1. Add the potato mash, salt and mix.
1. Take it off the heat.
1. Add the lime juice and coriander leaves and mix.
1. Keep aside to cool.
1. Heat a deep fry pan for deep frying the bondas.
1. In a mixing bowl add the ingredients under ‘for the batter’ except water.
1. Gradually, add water and make a thick batter.
1. Make small balls out of the potato mixture.
1. Dip each ball in the batter and deep fry on low to medium heat till golden brown.
1. Take out with a slotted spoon and keep on absorbent paper.
1. Serve Aloo Bonda with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney, Date Tamarind Chutney or any other chutney of your choice.
