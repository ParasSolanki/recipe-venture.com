### Ingredients

- 1 cup Spinach Leaves (Palak) - fresh and washed.
- 1 tablespoon Tabasco Original - Hot Sauce.
- Salt and Pepper - for seasoning.
- 6 Orange - cut into segments.
- 1 Apple - cut into diagonals.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower seeds.
- 1 Orange - squeezed into juice.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spinach & Apple Salad Recipe With Orange Dressing prep all the ingredients and keep it ready.
1. Add the spinach, orange, apple and sunflower seeds into a mixing bowl.
1. In another small bowl whisk in the orange juice, lemon juice, tabasco sauce, salt and pepper and mix well to combine.
1. Check the seasoning and adjust according to taste and then drizzle the dressing over the fruits and vegetables.
1. Toss the salad gently till it all combines and then sprinkle few sunflower seeds over Spinach & Apple Salad and serve.
1. Serve the Spinach & Apple Salad Recipe With Orange Dressing with Mexican Vegetarian Bean & Cheese Enchiladas Recipe for lunch or dinner.
