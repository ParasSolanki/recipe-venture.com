### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Fresh Pomegranate Fruit Kernels.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh coconut - dessicated.
- 4 Multigrain Digestive Biscuits.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1 Orange - peeled and diced.
- 4 tablespoon Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 1 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 2 tablespoons Honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making High Fiber Oat And Orange Coconut Parfait first cook oats in one cup water until done and cool the oats completely.
1. Next in a Large mixing bowl add the Greek yogurt, cooked oats,vanilla extract, desiccated coconut and honey and mix everything together till it is well combined.
1. Break some Nutri Choice Oats & Orange Biscuits into smaller pieces and crumble a few biscuits.
1. Slice the oranges and keep aside.
1. To assemble the High Fiber Oat And Orange Coconut Parfait take a jar of your choice and into the jar add the crumbled Nutri Choice Oats & Orange Biscuits.
1. Add 3 tablespoons Greek yogurt oats and coconut mix , layer with orange segments and a few broken Nutri Choice Oats & Orange Biscuits.
1. Add some more yogurt, oats and coconut mix and top with pomegranate Kernels.
1. Refrigerate Nutri Choice Oats & Orange Biscuits for two hours before serving.
1. Serve High Fiber Oat And Orange Coconut Parfait along with Savory French Toast With Cheesy Garlic Scrambled Eggs or Spinach Omelette for a fulfilling breakfast.
