### Ingredients

- Ripe Bananas - slices.
- 2 tablespoons Pistachios.
- Pistachios - for garnishing.
- 2 cups Musk Melon - pulp.
- 3 tablespoons Chia Seeds.
- 1/2 cup Milk - or coconut milk.
- 2 tablespoons Honey - or agave nectar.
- 2 Mango (Ripe) - alphanso.
- Mango (Ripe) - cubes.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Mango Musk Melon Chia Seeds Pudding, take mango and muskmelon pulp into a large bowl and add the milk.
1. Blend the mixture using a hand blender till everything is mixed properly.
1. Take the smoothie in a large bowl, add chia seeds and pistachios, whisk well to combine.
1. Cling wrap and refrigerate for 4 hours minimum.
1. To serve, spoon the pudding into the bowls, top it with banana and mango slices, sprinkle some pistachios, drizzle a little honey and serve.
1. Serve Mango Musk Melon Chia Seeds Pudding with some fresh fruits and Coffee for your breakfast.
