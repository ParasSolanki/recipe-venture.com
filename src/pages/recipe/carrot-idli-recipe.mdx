### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/4 cup White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 cup Idli Dosa Batter.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar).
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger - minced.
- Salt - as needed.
- Curry leaves - a spring.
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 cup Idli Rice.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Carrot Idli recipe, wash the carrot really well and chop into small pieces.
1. Add the chopped carrot pieces in a blender and grind it to form course pieces.
1. Alternatively you can also grate the carrot.
1. Add the Carrot into the prepared Idli batter (Check notes for Idli dosa batter)Meanwhile in a pan add in oil once it becomes hot add in mustard seeds, minced ginger and curry leaves add in the prepared tadka to the idli dosa batter.
1. Mix well Add in the Required Salt to the dosa batter and mix well.
1. Prepare the Idli pan and pour in the prepared batter steam cook in a medium flame for about 12-15 mins till the idli is fully cooked.
1. Once it is done.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve Carrot Idli with South Indian Coconut Chutney, Mysore Chutney or any other chutney of your choice.
