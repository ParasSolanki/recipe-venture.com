### Ingredients

- 2 Homemade Whole Wheat Khakras.
- 1 sprig Basil leaves - roughly chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 teaspoon Pizza seasoning.
- 2 tablespoons Britannia Cheese Spread - Peppy Pizza.
- 1/4 cup Sweet corn - steamed.
- 2 Tomatoes - blanched & chopped.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Khakhra Pizza Recipe (Whole Wheat Crisp Flatbread Pizza), you can either make your khakra at home or buy the store bought ones.
1. Here is the recipe to make the homemade khakhra recipeHeat the butter in a broad skillet pan, add capsicum and saute for a few minutes until softened.
1. Add the blanched and chopped tomatoes, corn and italian seasoning and cook until the corn gets well coated with the capsicum and seasoning.
1. Stir in the basil leaves and turn off the heat.
1. The final step is to make the Khakhra pizza.
1. Place a khakhra on a serving plate, spread a large tablespoon of Britannias Peppy Pizza Cheese Spread which adds a great taste and flavor to the pizza,Once spread, sprinkle the corn topping over the khakhra and voila the pizza is ready to be served.
1. Serve the Khakhra Pizza as a tea time snack or as a quick after school snack for kids or serve it as a snack along with Masala Chai/Espresso Coffee.
