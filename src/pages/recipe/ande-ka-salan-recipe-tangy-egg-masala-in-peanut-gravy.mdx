### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 6 Whole Eggs - hardboiled.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 cup Tamarind Water.
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 6 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/4 Cup Fresh coconut - freshly grated.
- 2 tablespoons Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Peanuts - roasted.
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 1/2 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Powder.
- 1/2 cup Water - lukewarm.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Ande Ka Salan Recipe, into a preheated pan, add the roasted peanuts, sesame seeds and roast for a minute till the sesame seeds start to pop.
1. Once done turn off the heat and let it cool.
1. Add the roasted peanuts and the sesame seeds into a mixer grinder along with coconut, warm water and grind into a smooth paste.
1. Keep aside.
1. Next we will proceed to make the gravy.
1. Into a preheated pan, add oil, once the oil is hot, add the mustard seeds, cumin seeds, methi seeds and allow the seeds to crackle.
1. Once the seeds crackle, add the curry leaves, onions, garlic, ginger, green chillies and saute until the onions turn golden brown in color.
1. Once done, add in the tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, coconut peanut sesame mixture, tamarind water and salt to taste.
1. Stir the Ande ka Salan gravy well to combine.
1. Simmer the gravy for Ande ka Salan on medium heat for about 5 to 7 minutes.
1. After 5 to 7 minutes, stir in chopped coriander leaves, add slit hard boiled eggs to the gravy, give a brisk boil and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer Ande Ka salan to a serving dish and serve hot.
1. Serve Ande Ka Salan Recipe along with Chicken Biryani or Tawa Paratha and Burani Raita for your weekend dinner.
