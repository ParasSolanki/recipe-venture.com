### Ingredients

- 250 grams Spinach Leaves (Palak) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 2 tablespoon Whole Wheat Bread crumbs - toasted.
- 1/4 cup Almond Meal (Badam Powder).
- 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese - grated.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/4 cup Soy Flakes.
- 100 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making High Protein Spinach & Soy Bites first dry roast the soya flakes in a skillet over medium heat for three minutes.
1. Once roasted cool and  grind to a coarse powder in mixer.
1. Transfer to a bowl.
1. In a large mixing bowl combine all the ingredients, ground soya flakes, almond meal, bread crumbs, paneer, red chilli flakes, salt and pepper, together and bind together to form a dough like mixture.
1. Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape each portion into small size balls.
1. Check the salt and adjust to suit your taste.
1. While you are shaping the spinach balls preheat the oven at 200'C.
1. Grease a baking sheet with little oil.
1. Bake Spinach Bites in a preheated oven for about 20 minutes or till outer layer turns crisp and brown.
1. Alternatively Spinach Bites can be cooked in the Paniyaram Pan by pan frying it with a little oil.

1. Once cooked transfer Spinach & Soy bites to a serving platter.
