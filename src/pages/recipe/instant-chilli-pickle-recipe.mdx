### Ingredients

- 250 grams Green Chillies.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Pickle masala.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To make instant chilli pickle, wash and chop the green chillies.
1. Heat oil in a thick bottomed Pan.
1. Now add chillies, turmeric powder, and salt.
1. Mix well.
1. Cover and cook for approximate 2 to 3 minutes on low heat or till tender.
1. Now open the lid and add pickle masala.
1. Stir fry for a minute on low heat.
1. Switch off.
1. Instant chilli pickle recipe is ready to be served as an accompaniment with cooked rice, Roti, Poori or Aloo Paratha.
