### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 2 tablespoons Tandoori masala.
- 2 teaspoons Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Pickled onions.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Whole Eggs - beaten.
- Chaat Masala Powder - to sprinkle.
- Sunflower Oil - for kneading.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.
- 200 grams Chicken breasts - cut into thin strips.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- Sunflower Oil - to roast the parathas.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken Tikka Kathi Roll Recipe we will first make the chicken tikka filling.
1. To make the Chicken Tikka Filling In a mixing bowl, combine the washed and cleaned chicken strips along with the yogurt, salt, turmeric powder, tandoori masala, and lemon juice.
1. Allow it to sit for at least 30 minutes.
1. Heat a pan with oil on medium flame, to this add the ginger, garlic and saute for 30 seconds, now add in the sliced onions and capsicum.
1. Continue to cook for another 3-4 minutes.
1. Now add the marinated chicken along with the marinade.
1. Cover and cook for 5-7  minutes until the chicken tikka masala has cooked through.
1.  We need our chicken tikka to be just about coated with the masala.
1. Turn off the flame, and transfer to a bowl and set aside.
1.  To make the tawa paratha To begin making the Tawa Paratha, in a large bowl combine the flour, salt, a tablespoon of oil and knead adding little water at a time to make a firm and smooth dough.
1. Add one tablespoon of oil to coat the dough and knead again.
1. Cover and set aside for 15 minutes.
1. Knead once again and divide the dough into 4 portions.
1. Preheat the iron skillet on medium heat.
1. Roll the dough portions into balls and flatten them with the palm of your hand.
1. Toss them on flour and roll them out into circles of approximately 8 inches in diameter.
1. Continue the same process of rolling with the remaining dough portions.
1. With skillet on medium high heat, place a rolled out paratha on the skillet.
1. After a few seconds flip on the other side At this point flip the plain paratha and add some oil.
1. Cook until almost done.
1. Now spread two tablespoons of the beaten egg on one side of the paratha and flip.
1. Cook for about a minute till the egg is cooked.
1. Remove from the tawa and place it on a plate.
1. To assemble the Chicken Tikka Kathi RollPlace the prepared chicken tikka masala filling on one side of the paratha, place pickled onions next to it and sprinkle some chaat masala.
1. Fold like that of a roll and serve.
1. Serve Chicken Tikka Kathi Roll along with Spicy Chilli Mango Virgin Mojito Recipe and finish it off with a dessert of Apple Pie Ice Cream Recipe.
