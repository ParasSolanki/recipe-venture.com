### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 250 grams Elephant yam (Suran/Senai/Ratalu) - cut into small florets.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Andhra Style Kandagadda Vepudu Recipe, firstly we will pressure cook the chopped yam.
1. Add the Yam in a pressure cooker with 1 cup water and salt.
1. Close the lid and cook till the cooker releases 2 whistles.
1. Switch off the heat and allow it to rest so that it releases the pressure by itself.
1. Heat a kadai with oil.
1. Once the oil is hot, add mustard seeds and allow it to splutter for few seconds.
1. Add the curry leaves and crackle them for 10 more seconds.
1. Add the ginger and garlic, chopped green chilli and cooked yam.
1. Add the spice powders also (red chilli powder, turmeric powder, pepper powder and salt) and mix everything well.
1. Mix until the masalas are evenly coated and saute until the dish becomes dry (This will take about 2 to 3 minutes).
1. Check for salt and serve.
1. Serve the Andhra Style Kandagadda Vepudu along with Steamed Rice, Palak Tovve or Palak Dal Recipe and Palya for an everyday Lunch or Dinner.
