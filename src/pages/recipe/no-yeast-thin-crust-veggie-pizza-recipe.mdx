### Ingredients

- 12 Black olives - sliced.
- 2 tablespoon Homemade Pizza And Pasta Sauce.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 3/4 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 3 Tomato - sliced.
- 1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese.
- 8 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 1/4 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/4 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 8 Onion - sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the No Yeast Thin Crust Veggie Pizza recipe, firstly get all the ingredients handy.
1. Take a large bowl, add all purpose flour, salt, yogurt, baking powder, oil and mix well with your hands for 2 minutes.
1. Now with the lukewarm water make a soft dough.
1. Cover and keep it aside for 30 minutes.
1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees Celcius (400 degrees Fahrenheit) for about 10 minutes.
1. Meanwhile, place the dough ball on the greased baking tray.
1. Flatten it to circular shape 1/3 inch thick.
1. Spread a teaspoon or two of olive oil on the pizza base.
1. Now spread Del Monte pizza sauce evenly on the base.
1. Place onion, tomato and black olives.
1. Now sprinkle mozzarella cheese over it.
1. Bake the pizza for 12-15 mins or until done.
1. Take out the pizza from the oven and serve hot.
1. Treat this No Yeast Thin Crust Veggie Pizza recipe with Aglio Olio Pasta and Homemade Apple Pie for your weeknight dinner.
