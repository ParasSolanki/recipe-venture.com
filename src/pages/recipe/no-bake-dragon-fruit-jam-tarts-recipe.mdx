### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Butter - (melted).
- Strawberries - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 2 teaspoons Sugar.
- 7 Digestive biscuit.
- 1/2 Dragon fruit (Pitaya) - scooped.
- 10 Dates - deseeded.

### Instructions

1. For the Tart Crust:To begin making No Bake Dragon Fruit Jam Tarts Recipe, prepare tart crust first.
1. In a mixer, take dates and crackers and grind till you get a smooth mixture.
1. Now take the mixture in a bowl and gradually add about 2 tablespoons melted butter and mix well until the mixture resembles a dough.
1. Line tart moulds with parchment paper.
1. Divide crust between the moulds and press firmly using your hands to form a uniform crust.
1. Press it more in the centre with your thumb to make space for our filling.
1. Put in the freezer section of the refrigerator for about 10-15 minutes.
1. For The Filling:Cut the dragon fruit into two halves and scoop out the flesh using a spoon.
1. Take scooped flesh in a mixer/grinder and blend to a pulp.
1. In a thick bottomed non-stick pan, combine dragon fruit pulp and sugar.
1. Cook on a medium flame and simmer until it has a thick jam like consistency.
1. Keep on stirring occasionally.
1. This will take about 20-25 minutes.
1. Once you notice that it has begun to thicken, add lemon juice to it.
1. Stir well and cook for about a minute.
1. Remove from flame and allow it to cool.
1. Assembling:Take out the prepared tarts from the refrigerator.
1. Put about a spoonful of dragon fruit jam on it.
1. Garnish with sliced strawberry.
1. Serve No Bake Dragon Fruit Jam Tarts Recipe chilled as a party dessert, along with appetizers like Vegetarian Chinese Samosa Recipe.
