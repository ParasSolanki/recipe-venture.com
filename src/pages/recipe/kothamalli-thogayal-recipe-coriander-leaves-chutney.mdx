### Ingredients

- 1 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Tamarind.
- 6 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery - (adjust).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh coconut.
- 1 Salt - as needed.
- 1 tablespoon White Urad Dal (Split).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Kothamalli Thogayal Recipe | Coriander Leaves Chutney, in a Kadai add in the oil, once the oil heats up add in dry red chilli and Urad dal and fry till they become golden brown.
1. Then switch off the flame and allow it to cool down.
1. In a blender add in coconut and the roasted urad dal and red chilli, jaggery and tamarind.
1. Blend everything to a coarse paste and then add in the salt and Coriander leaves and make it into a smooth paste by adding a very little water.
