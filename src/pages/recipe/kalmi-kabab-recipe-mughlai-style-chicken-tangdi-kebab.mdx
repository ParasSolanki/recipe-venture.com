### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 3 Chicken legs.
- 1/4 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 tablespoon Cashew nuts.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 tablespoon Fresh cream.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kalmi Kebab Recipe, thoroughly wash and clean the chicken leg pieces.
1. In a mixer jar, grind the cashew nuts to a fine thick paste using little water.
1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine yogurt, cream, ginger garlic paste, lemon juice and mix well.
1. Add turmeric powder, cashew nut paste, cumin powder, garam masala, red chilli powder, black pepper powder and salt.
1. Mix well to combine.
1. Add the chicken pieces, coat well with the marinade and refrigerate to marinate for a minimum of 30 minutes or overnight.
1. Heat some oil in a non-stick pan.
1. Place the marinated chicken on it and shallow-fry on high heat till the underside turns deep golden in colour.
1. Flip and cook the other side on high heat till golden.
1. Cover and cook on medium heat for 10-15 minutes.
1. Alternatively, you can also cook/roast the chicken in an oven/tandoor.
1. Serve Kalmi Kebab as a party starter along with Pickled Onions and Dahi Pudina Chutney.
1. You can also make it as a side dish with your simple meal of Lahsuni Dal and Steamed Rice.
