### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Badam (Almond) - blanched and chopped.
- 1-1/2 cups All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1/2 cup Dark chocolate - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Raisins.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/2 cup Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 1/2 cup Butter - softened.
- 2 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chocolate Almond Raisin Cookie Recipe.
1. first preheat the oven to 180 C and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
1. In a bowl sift the flour, baking powder and salt.
1. Keep aside.
1. In another mixing bowl, beat the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
1. Add the egg one at a time and beat well into the butter mixture.
1. Gradually beat in the flour mixture, raisins and almonds until just combined.
1. Take care not to over beat the cookie dough.
1. Shape the Chocolate Almond Raisin Cookie dough into round lemon size balls and slightly flatten them.
1. Place the cookies on the baking tray and into the preheated oven.
1. Bake Chocolate Almond Raisin Cookie for about 12 minutes until golden.
1. Once golden, remove from the oven and allow it to be on the cookie sheet for a few more minutes and then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
1. The cookies will get crisp in the cooling process.
1. Store the Chocolate Almond Raisin Cookie in airtight containers and serve as a snack with tea or coffee.
1. Serve Chocolate Almond Raisin Cookie Recipe as an after school snack to your kids along with Banana Orange & Spinach Smoothie Recipe.
