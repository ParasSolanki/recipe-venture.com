### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 1 cup Green zucchini - diced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Badam (Almond) - blanched.
- 1 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - diced.
- 1 cup Basil leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Spirulina.
- 1 Tomato - diced.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 cup Pasta.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Tofu.
- 2 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/2 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Spirulina and Mint Basil Pesto Pasta Recipe by first making the pesto.
1. For that wash the herbs and put all the ingredients in a blender and blend it into a coarse mixture.
1. Once done keep it aside.
1. Next is to boil the pasta, for that heat a sauce pan with enough water and add a little salt and let it boil, once it starts boiling add in the pasta and then boil till it is al dante stage (Just done).
1. Strain the water and run the pasta under cold water and drizzle with olive oil and coat it well.
1. Heat a sauce pan with oil, add chopped garlic and the rest of the vegetables except tomato and saute them well till it is cooked but still firm.
1. Once done switch off the flame add the seasoning and the tomato.
1. Also add in the cooked pasta and the pesto.
1. Mix well till it combines.
1. Serve the Spirulina and Mint Basil Pesto Pasta Recipe along with Fresh Vegetable Salad Bowl to enjoy your Lunch meal.
