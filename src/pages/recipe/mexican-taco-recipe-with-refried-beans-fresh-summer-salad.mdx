### Ingredients

- 4 Hard taco shells.
- 1/4 cup Guacamole - homemade.
- 1/4 cup Tomato Salsa - homemade.
- 1 cup Summer Salad.
- 1/2 cup Sour cream - homemade.
- 1/2 cup Refried beans - homemade.
