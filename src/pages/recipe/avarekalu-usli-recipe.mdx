### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 4 tablespoons Fresh coconut.
- 2 cups Avarekai (Broad Beans).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Avarekalu Usli Recipe, wash and pressure cook the avarekalu by adding 1 cup of  water into a pressure cooker and cook it for 2 whistles and keep aside for the pressure to release naturally.
1. Make a coarse mix of grated coconut, green chilli, cumin seeds using a mixer grinder and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan, add the mustard seeds, urad dal, red chillies and let it crackle and the dal to turn golden brown and crisp.
1. Add the curry leaves and saute for a few seconds.
1.  Add the chopped onion, saute until the onions soften and turn golden in colour.
1. Once the onions have softened, add the cooked avarekalu, season with salt and saute until it all comes together.
1. Add the prepared coconut mix, coriander leaves, mix well and switch off the heat.
1. Serve the Avarekalu Usli Recipe along with Beetroot Poriyal, Thakkali Vengaya Sambar, Rice, Papad for a wholesome sunday lunch.
