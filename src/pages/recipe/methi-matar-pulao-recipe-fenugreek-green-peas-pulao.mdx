### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta) - torn.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 cup Rice.
- 200 grams Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves) - rinsed and chopped.
- 1 cup Green peas (Matar) - steamed.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 2 tablespoons Ghee - or Sesame oil.
- 1 cup Coconut milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Methi Matar Pulao Recipe, we will be cooking the pulao in a single pot.
1. Keep the steamed green peas aside, we will add it towards the end when the entire pulao is cooked.
1. Begin by heating oil in a heavy bottomed pan over medium heat.
1. Add cumin seeds, grated ginger and saut√© for a few seconds.
1. Stir in the fenugreek leaves, turmeric powder and saute until the leaves have softened.
1. Add in the whole spices, salt, green chilies and the rice,Add in 1 cup of coconut milk and 1 cup of water.
1. Check the salt and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Bring the methi rice to a quick boil.
1. Once the rice comes to a boil, turn the heat to low, cover the pan and allow the rice to cook along with the spices and methi leaves until all the water is completely absorbed.
1. Once you notice all the water is completely absorbed, turn off the heat and allow the Methi Matar Pulao to rest for about 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes open the pan, add the steamed peas and a tablespoon of ghee.
1. Stir in to combine well into the rice and the Methi Matar Pulao is now ready to be served warm.
1. The Methi Matar Pulao makes a great lunch box meal filled with nutrition of methi and rice, when served along with Tomato Onion Raita and a roasted Papad.
