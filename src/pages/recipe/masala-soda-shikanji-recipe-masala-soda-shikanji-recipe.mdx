### Ingredients

-  2 Lemon - extract the juice.
- 3 cups Sprite - or any Lemon Soda.
-  Black Salt or Rock Salt - as per taste.
-  peppermint - little.
-  2 Green Chillies - cut.
-  10 ice.
-  1 tsp Chaat Masala Powder.
-  3 Lemon - wedges.
-  1/2 tsp Cumin Powder.

### Instructions

1. To make the Masala Soda Shikanji recipe, first of all add ice to a pitcher.
1. Add lemon juice, sprite and mix it.
1. Now add green chillies and chaat masala in a hamadasta and tighten the pumpkin.
1. Add some water to it and then mix this mixture in the sprite.
1. Add lemon wedges and mint and mix well.
1. Serve.
1. Serve Masala Soda Shikanji recipe with Pav Bhaji or Aloo Tikki Chaat for a chaat party.
