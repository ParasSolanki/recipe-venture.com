### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 2 Whole Eggs - well beaten or Flax meal Egg Replacer.
- Fresh fruits - or apples for serving.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- Maple syrup - for serving.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Whole Wheat Buttermilk Vanilla Waffles Recipe, stir together the wheat flour, baking powder and salt.
1. Add eggs (or the flax eggs), yogurt, vanilla extract, and oil and whisk until well blended.
1. You should have a thick batter that coats well on the back of a spoon.
1. Pour a ladleful of batter into the waffle maker and cook until the waffles are browned and crisp.
1. Serve these Whole Wheat Buttermilk Vanilla Waffles for breakfast topped with butter, maple syrup and some fruits along with Banana Almond & Prunes Smoothie, or in the kids' snack box.
