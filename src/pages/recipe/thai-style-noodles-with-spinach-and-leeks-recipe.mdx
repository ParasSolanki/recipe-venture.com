### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 200 grams Spaghetti Pasta - or pad thai noodles (whole wheat).
- 1 Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - roughly chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- Salt and Pepper - as needed.
- 1 Leek - ith white and green parts.
- 1/4 kg Baby spinach - roughly chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 1 teaspoon Peanut Butter.
- 1 clove Garlic - minced.
- 1/3 cup Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - pounded.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 1/3 cup Coconut milk.

### Instructions

1. To start making Thai noodles With Spinach And Leeks, in a small bowl mix together red pepper flakes, coconut milk, peanut butter, soy sauce, brown sugar, chili garlic sauce and keep aside.
1. Bring a quart of water to boil with salt in a large pot.
1. Add the noodles and cook according to the package instructions.
1. Drain the noodles and keep aside.
1. Drizzle a teaspoon of oil over the noodles, so it does not get sticky.
1. Heat a tablespoon of oil in the same skillet.
1. Add the leeks along with ginger and garlic.
1. Saute until translucent and you can smell the aromas coming through.
1. Add the spinach and saute for a minute.
1. Add the prepared coconut milk sauce and cook until it thickens.
1. Stir in the cooked noodles, the  fresh coriander leaves and peanut.
1. Check the salt and pepper and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and serve warm.
1. Serve the Thai Style Noodles With Spinach And Leeks as a quick and easy weeknight dinner along with a Vegetarian Thai Massaman Curry.
