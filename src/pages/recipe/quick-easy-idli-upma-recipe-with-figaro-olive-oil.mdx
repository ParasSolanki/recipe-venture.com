### Ingredients

- 6 Curry leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Figaro Pure Olive Oil.
- 10 Idli - crumbled.
- 10 Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - finely chopped (or 1 large onion).
- 3 Green Chillies - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Idli Upma Recipe, heat a teaspoon of Figaro Pure Olive Oil in a heavy bottomed pan on medium-low heat, add in the mustard seeds and allow it to crackle for a few seconds.
1. Then add in the half urad dal and allow it to roast and turn a light brown in color.
1. Once the dal is roasted we will add in the chopped onions, then the grated ginger, green chillies and curry leaves.
1. Saute on medium heat until the onions are soft.
1. Once soft we will add in the turmeric powder and the crumbled idlis and some salt to taste.
1. Stir it until well combined.
1.  Finally stir in the coriander leaves, check the salt and spices and adjust to taste accordingly.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and serve.
1. Serve the delicious Idli Upma along with Tomato Onion Chutney Recipe - Thakkali Vengayam Chutney and a hot cup of coffee makes the breakfast complete.
