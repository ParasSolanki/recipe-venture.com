### Ingredients

- 20 Green Chillies.
- 2 tablespoons Poppy seeds.
- 3 Mango (Raw) - diced into 1/2 centimetre cubes.
- 3 Lemons - juiced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 3 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 20 cloves Garlic.
- 100 grams Carrot (Gajjar) - diced into 1/2 centimetre cubes.
- 3 inch Ginger.
- 1 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - small bunch.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 50 grams Cashew nuts.
- 100 grams Green beans (French Beans) - chopped similarly.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Instant Mixed Vegetable Pickle recipe, clean and dry all the vegetables on a kitchen towel.
1. Chop them finely and tip them into a mixing bowl and add the juice of one lemon and a teaspoon of salt and mixed through the vegetables.
1. In a small mixing jar, add the ingredients for the marinating paste and grind to a smooth paste in a grinder without adding any water.
1. Mix this paste through the vegetables and let it sit for at least 2 hours before serving.
1. It stays fresh for a week to a fortnight and needs to be kept in the fridge at all times.
1. Serve Instant Mixed Vegetable Pickle with curd rice for a comforting week day meal.
