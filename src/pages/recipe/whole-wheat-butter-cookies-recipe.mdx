### Ingredients

- 100 grams Butter - (adjust).
- 50 gram Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/3 cup Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 1 pinch Baking powder.
- 1/4 cup Decorative sugar (Cake/ Candy Sprinkles).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Whole Wheat Butter Cookies Recipe, powder Sugar and cardamom in a Mixer into a fine mix.
1. Mix flour, sugar, cardamom, sprinkles, baking powder in a mixing bowl and mix them well.
1. Add the olive oil and mix well.
1. Melt the butter (optional) and add into the mix little by little.
1. You may choose not to melt the butter as the hand heat itself is enough while mixing.
1. Make the whole grain butter cookie dough.
1. Grease your hands with butter and make small balls and flatten them into round shaped cookies.
1. Or you can shape them the way you like, with a cookie cutter.
1. Take a Baking tray and place baking sheet/parchment paper.
1. Place the cookies one by one giving a good gap of at least an inch between each one of them.
1. If required use a fork and just put a simple design.
1. Preheat Oven for 5 minutes at 180 degrees Celsius.
1. Place the cookies and let it bake.
1. It will take around 12 â€“ 15 minutes.
1. Keep watching after 10 minutes as the baking varies with ovens.
1. Once baked, place them on a wire rack and let Whole Wheat Butter Cookies cool completely.
1. Store Whole Wheat Butter Cookies Recipe in an air-tight container and served with Milkshake to the kids or with Masala Chai or Qahwa to adults.
