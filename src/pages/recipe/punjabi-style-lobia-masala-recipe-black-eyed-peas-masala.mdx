### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit in between.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1/2 tablespoon Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 cups Black Eyed Beans (Lobia) - boiled.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 tablespoon Tamarind Paste.
- 1 tablespoon Salt.
- 1/2 tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Punjabi Style Lobia Masala recipe, heat oil in a wok/kadai.
1. Add cumin seeds, fennel seeds, cinnamon and saute for a minute.
1. Add onion and green chillies.
1. Add ginger garlic paste and saute them till onion turn golden in colour.
1. Add all dry spices including turmeric powder, red chilli powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, sugar, salt and tamarind paste.
1. Mix it well and add tomato puree.
1. Cook mixture till oil separates from it.
1. Add cooked black eyed peas(lobia) and mix well.
1. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
1. Add milk and mix well.
1. Cook on slow heat for 5 minutes.
1. Lobiya is ready to serve.
1. Serve Punjabi Style Lobia Masala along with Phulka and Kachumber Salad for a weekday lunch or dinner.
