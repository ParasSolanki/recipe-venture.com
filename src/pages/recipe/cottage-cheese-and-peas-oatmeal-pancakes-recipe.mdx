### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - or butter for making pancakes.
- 1/2 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1-1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal) - rolled.
- 1-1/2 cup Green peas (Matar) - blanched.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cottage Cheese and Peas Oatmeal Pancakes, blanch the green peas in hot water and keep aside.
1. Save a few teaspoons to be included later.
1. Now, add the oatmeal flour, blanched green peas and grated paneer in a blender and blend it to a coarse mix.
1. Take the ground pancake mix in a bowl, add the saved few teaspoons of green peas, season with salt and mix well.
1. The mixture should be little thicker than the usual pancake batter.
1. Heat a skillet on medium flame, grease it with little oil or butter, pour a ladle full of the pancake mix and gently spread it.
1. Let it cook for 3 minutes till it gets cooked at the bottom.
1. Now gently flip over and cook the other side for another 2 minutes and remove it in a plate.
1. Likewise, prepare rest of the pancakes with the remaining batter.
1. Serve Cottage Cheese And Peas Oatmeal Pancakes with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney and Masala Chai as a tea time snack or for breakfast.
