### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 tablespoon Red Wine Vinaigrette.
- 2 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 1/4 cup Feta Cheese - crumbled.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 2 sprig Basil leaves - roughly torn.
- 2 Beetroot - peeled and cut into wedges.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 1 Onion - cut into rings.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Roasted Beet Feta Salad Recipe roast the beetroot on a pan with olive oil till it is cooked and has a charred colour on them.
1. Reduce the heat and add the vinaigrette,honey, salt and pepper.
1. Toss all of it with the beet till it well coated.
1. Then finally turn off the heat and add the onion rings , feta cheese and walnuts give it a final toss and serve the salad warm.
1. Serve the Roasted Beet Feta Salad Recipe along with Spinach And Feta Cheese Rotolo Recipe as a side dish to enjoy your warm sunday meal.
