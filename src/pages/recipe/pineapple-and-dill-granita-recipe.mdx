### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Water.
- 750 grams Pineapple - peeled and cubed.
- 1/3 cups Sugar.
- 2 sprig Dill leaves.
- 1 cup Ice cubes.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Pineapple and Dill granita, firstly add sugar, dill, water in a pan and heat it until the sugar has completely dissolved.
1. Set it aside.
1. Discard the dill springs.
1. Place the pineapple, sugar syrup, ice cubes in a blender and blend until smooth.
1. Pour the mixture into a 9 inch freeze safe/baking pan and freeze for an hour.
1. Using a fork scrape the granita.
1. Freeze for about 2 hours or until set.
1. Scrape into serving bowls and enjoy!Serve Pineapple and Dill Granita as a sweet dish with your weeknight dinners.
1. Pair this sweet dish with Mushroom Pulav and Lauki raita for a light dinner.
