### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese - grated.
- 1 cup Macaroni pasta - boiled.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Chilli Garlic Sauce.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - or butter.
- 1 Baguette.

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Mac And Cheese On Crostini first, In a mixing  bowl add boiled pasta, salt and chilli garlic sauce and give it a mix and keep it aside.
1. Cut the baguette 1/3 inch slices and spread olive oil or butter on each side of the bread.
1. Preheat the oven to 400 F and place the baguette slice on the baking tray and bake it for 5 to 7 minutes.
1. Cut baguette into 1/3 inch slices.
1. Now place some pasta mixture on each bread slice.
1. Add some grated mozzarella cheese over it.
1. Put bread again in the oven until the cheese has melted.
1. Garnish with the chopped basil and serve immediately.
1. Serve the Mac And Cheese On Crostini with Cumin Spiced Potato And Leek Soup.
