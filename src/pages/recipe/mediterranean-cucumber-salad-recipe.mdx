### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 Cucumber - chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/4 cup Lemon juice.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- 3 tablespoons Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mediterranean Cucumber Salad, firstly slit the cucumber and take out the seeds from it.
1. Donâ€™t peel the skin of the cucumber.
1. Once you take out the seeds, chopped it into small pieces.
1. Next, cut the tomatoes and take out the seeds from it.
1. Then, finely chopped the tomatoes.
1. Also chop the onions finely.
1. Mix all these three in a big mixing bowl.
1. Add mint leaves, coriander leaves, olive oil, salt.
1. lemon juice and black pepper powder.
1. Mix everything well.
1. Cover the bowl with a plastic wrap and freeze it for about 1 to 2 hours.
1. Take it out and serve chilled.
1. Serve Mediterranean Cucumber Salad along with Mujaddara Recipe (Traditional Middle Eastern Rice) and end it with a Mascarpone Fruit Dessert Recipe  for a light and delicious weekend dinner.
