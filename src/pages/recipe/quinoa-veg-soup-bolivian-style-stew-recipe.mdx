### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - beans and peas.
- 1 cup Kala Chana (Brown Chickpeas) - cooked.
- 2 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/4 cup Quinoa - soaked in warm water for 10 mins.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 cup Vegetable stock.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1 Onions - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 cup Homemade tomato puree - or pasta sauce.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1/2 tablespoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Quinoa Veg Soup, firstly add oil in a pressure cooker.
1. Add onions, grated ginger, garlic and bay leaf and sauté till the onions turn golden brown.
1. Once the onions turn golden brown, add tomato puree, bell pepper, veggies and sauté with salt.
1. Add the cooked garbanzo, red chilli powder, jeera, dhania powder, quinoa, vegetable stock, water and pressure cook it for 2 whistles.
1. Let the pressure die down on its own.
1. Simmer for 3-4 mins till you get desired consistency and lastly add cilantro.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste and serve.
1. Serve Quinoa Veg Soup with Chilli Cheese Toast or any other toast of your choice.
