### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 3 cups Puffed rice.
- Salt - to taste.
- 3 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 pinch Sugar.
- 2 tablespoons Kolhapuri Khanda Lasun Masala.
- 4 cloves Garlic - ground into paste.
- 3 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3 tablespoons Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Maharastrian Bhadang Recipe, first make the Kolhapuri Khanda Lasun Masala by following the recipe link.
1. Heat a kadai with oil add peanuts and roast them till they are light brown.
1. Add curry leaves, garlic, hing and leave it to crackle for few seconds.
1. Add the spice mix, sugar, and turmeric powder, salt and allow the oil to observe the masala and create a paste.
1. At this point add the puffed rice and allow the masala to coat well.
1. Once the masala is coated well you can turn the heat off.
1. Serve the Bhadang Recipe (Puffed Rice Snack Mix) for evening time snack along with hot cup of chai and some pakodas by the side.
