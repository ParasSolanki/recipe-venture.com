### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Garlic - ground to paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 2 tablespoons Butter - melted.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 4 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida) - (more for dusting).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Garlic Naan Without Yeast recipe, mix all the ingredients except 1/2 teaspoon garlic and butter in a mixing bowl.
1. Make a soft dough out of it and keep it aside to rest for 1/2 an hour.
1. Mix the butter and the remaining garlic in an another bowl.
1. Now take a small portion of dough, dust it with all purpose flour and roll it in the shape of naan with the help of rolling pin.
1. With the help of water wet one side of the naan.
1. Take a normal tava and put the wet side on it.
1. When little bubbles appear on it.
1. Turn the pan upside down and let it cook on simmer gas.
1. About after 1 minute its fully cooked.
1. Take it on a plate, apply butter and garlic combination on it and serve hot.
1. Serve Garlic Naan Without Yeast along with Dal Makhani and Vegetable Malai Kofta for a delicious weekend dinner.
