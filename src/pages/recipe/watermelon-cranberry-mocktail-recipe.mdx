### Ingredients

- Ice cubes - to serve.
- 1 cup Cranberry juice.
- 1 teaspoon Rose water - or a drop of rose essence (optional).
- 250 kg Watermelon - diced and deseeded.
- Palm sugar - (if required).

### Instructions

1. To begin with Watermelon-Cranberry Mocktail, blend watermelon, cranberry juice, rose syrup/essence and palm sugar in a juicer.
1. Add water and make sure everything is mixed well.
1. Serve chilled with ice cubes.
1. Serve Watermelon-Cranberry Mocktail to your guests with some homemade snacks like Mozzarella Stuffed Potato Balls and Mushrooms On Toast.
