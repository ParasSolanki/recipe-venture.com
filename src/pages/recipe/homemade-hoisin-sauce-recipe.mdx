### Ingredients

- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 3 tablespoons Peanut Butter.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chinese 5 Spice Powder.
- 1 teaspoon Red Wine Vinaigrette.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 1 tablespoons Soy sauce.
- 1 clove Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Homemade Hoisin Sauce Recipe by heating all the ingredients in a sauce pan.
1. Bring it to a boil till it becomes a thick paste.
1. Allow it to cool down and pour it into a container and store it in the refrigerator for later use.
1. Serve the Homemade Hoisin Sauce Recipe in Hoisin Tofu & Mushroom Stir Fry with homemade Hoisin Sauce Recipe.
