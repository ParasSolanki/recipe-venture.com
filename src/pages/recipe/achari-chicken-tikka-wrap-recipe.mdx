### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 300 grams Boneless chicken.
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Tomato - pulp removed and chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 tablespoon Gram flour (besan).
- 1 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- 4 Lettuce leaves - or spinach leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 4 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 4 tablespoons Achari Mayo.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 Whole Wheat Tawa Paratha.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken Tikka Wrap Recipe marinate the chicken with lemon juice, ginger garlic paste, turmeric powder and salt in a mixing bowl.
1. Marinate this for about an hour.
1. Heat a sauce pan with oil on medium flame, add ajwain seeds and allow them to sizzle for few seconds.
1. Reduce the flame and add the gram flour and mix well switch off the heat.
1. Into the marinated chicken, add curd and the spice powders, add the heated gram flour mixture and mix well.
1. Rest this marinated chicken mixture in the fridge for one more hour.
1. Heat a grill pan, grease it with oil, once it heated, take the chicken pieces out and skewer them into a wooden skewers.
1. Place the skewers gently and cook on all the sides until the chicken pieces are charred and cooked well.
1. Keep them aside.
1. Make the Tawa paratha using the link in the recipe or you can also use tortillas.
1. Place the tawa paratha on a flat surface, spread lettuce leaves, sliced onions, tomatoes and the chicken pieces to one corner.
1. Drizzle the Nutralite Achari Mayo and start rolling them tightly and wrap it using a foil and serve.
1. Serve the Chicken Tikka Wrap Recipe as a snack along with glass of Mulled Apple Juice Recipe or a Chickoo Banana Date Smoothie Recipe by the side.
