### Ingredients

- 3/4 cup Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Red Chilli powder.
- 4 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil - for the stuffing.
- 10 cloves Garlic.
- 300 grams Pearl onions (Sambar Onions).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kathiyawadi Stuffed Onion Recipe, peel the baby onions, keeping the base intact make 4 slits in the shape of a cross to help us fill the stuffing.
1. Keep aside.
1. For the stuffing, make a coarse dry mix of the roasted peanuts, garlic cloves, coriander powder, red chilli powder, cumin, turmeric and salt using a mixer grinder and transfer into a bowl.
1. Now add the chopped coriander, about 2 teaspoons oil to the filling mixture and mix well till the mixture turns crumbly.
1. Take the baby onions and carefully fill in the ground mixture into the slits of each one of them.
1. Heat oil in a non stick pan on medium flame, place the stuffed onions on the surface of the pan and let it cook on low heat for 5 minutes.
1. Add about 1/4 cup of water, add the remaining stuffing mix, close it with a lid and let the onions cook in the steam.
1. This will take about 10 minutes.
1. Saute sparingly if required in between.
1. Make sure the onions are cooked well while still retaining its shape.
1. Once the onions are cooked, switch off the heat and garnish Kathiyawadi Stuffed Onion with coriander leaves.
1. Serve the Kathiyawadi Stuffed Onion Recipe along with Methi Jowar Roti Recipe and Gujarati Dal Recipe for a delightful weeknight dinner.
