### Ingredients

- Sweet and Spicy Red Chilli Sauce (Tomato Chilli Sauce) - for serving.
- 1 cup Mixed vegetables - (carrot.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 100 gram Tofu - masala.
- 2 cup Hakka Noodles.
- 1/4 teaspoon Chilli vinegar.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 3 teaspoons Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Ginger.
- 1 Onion.
- 2 teaspoon Dijon Mustard.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Scrambled Tofu Noodles recipe, first chop ginger, green chilli, onion and vegetables including carrot, green beans and scallions.
1. Take a bowl and scramble the tofu.
1. Add the yellow mustard and mix it properly.
1. Heat some oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add onion, ginger, green chilli, salt and vegetable and cook for about for 3 minutes.
1. After 3 minutes, add the tofu and vinegar, fry for few minutes.
1. Toss the noodles with little salt and pepper.
1. Turn off the flame after 2 minutes.
1. Check Seasoning and serve hot with sauce and few cubes of tofu garnish.
1. Serve Scrambled Tofu Noodles along with Asian Style Stuffed Omelette for a weekend brunch with your family.
