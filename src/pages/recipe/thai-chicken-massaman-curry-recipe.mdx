### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - cut into 1/2 inch pieces.
- 1/2 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Tamarind Paste.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 Lemon Grass - bottom half chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Fish sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 tablespoon Black pepper corns.
- 6 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - cubed.
- 6 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 250 grams Chicken breasts - cut into 1/2 inch pieces.
- 1 Onion.
- 1 teaspoon Palm sugar.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 tablespoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 cup Coconut milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Thai Chicken Massaman Curry Recipe first heat a pan on medium heat and add all the dry spices, cardamom seeds, cinnamon, peppercorn, fennel seeds, coriander seeds,dry red chilies, listed under massaman curry paste and roast for a minute and keep aside in a bowl.
1. Then add all the wet ingredients listed under massaman curry paste like onions, lemongrass, ginger and garlic to the pan and roast till all the ingredients turn light brown in colour and keep aside in a bowl.
1. Once all the ingredients are cooled grind into a smooth paste in a mixer grinder.
1. Add little water if required.
1. Now heat a wok and add oil to it then add the massaman curry paste and stir for a minute.
1. Add the cubed chicken breast and diced vegetables and stir.
1. Add in the roasted peanuts and thick coconut milk.
1. Once the Thai chicken curry comes to a boil reduce flame and simmer.
1. Add the fish sauce, tamarind paste, palm sugar, salt and bay leaves.
1. Simmer and cook the Thai Chicken Massaman Curry for half an hour till the chicken and vegetables are cooked.
1. Serve Thai Chicken Massaman Curry with Thai Stir Fried Green Beans and Thai Jasmine Sticky Rice for a nice weekend Thai lunch.
