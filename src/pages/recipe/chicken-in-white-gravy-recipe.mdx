### Ingredients

- 300 grams Boneless chicken.
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Salt.
- 3 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - for garnish.
- 2 tablespoon Butter.
- 1 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- Tomato - for garnish.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon White pepper powder.
- 1 Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken In White Gravy recipe, melt butter in a pressure cooker, add onions, cardamom and cinnamon and sauté till the onions turn golden.
1. Next add ginger garlic paste and sauté for a couple of minutes till the raw smell goes away.
1. At this stage add curd and mix well.
1. Next add in the chicken pieces, salt and white pepper powder and mix well.
1. Add 1/3 cup water and close the lid of the pressure cooker.
1. Pressure cook for 2 whistles and switch off the gas.
1. Once the pressure has released transfer to a serving bowl.
1. Serve Chicken In White Gravy along with Garlic Naan and some Pickled Onions for a complete meal.
