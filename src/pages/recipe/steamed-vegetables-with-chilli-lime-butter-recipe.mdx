### Ingredients

- 1 cup Baby corn - cut into half.
- 1 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - cut into diagonals.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Button mushrooms - quartered.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 cup Green beans (French Beans) - cut into 2 inch.
- 2 tablespoons Butter.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Steamed Vegetables With Chilli Lime Butter Recipe, chop all the vegetables and keep it ready.
1. Add carrot, green beans and baby corn into a pressure cooker, add in salt & pepper with 2 tablespoons water and pressure cook for just one whistle.
1. Turn off the heat and release the pressure immediately, but placing the pressure cooker under running water.
1. Open the cooker and keep the steamed vegetables aside.
1. Heat a sauce pan with butter, once the butter starts to melt add mushroom saut√© well.
1. Once the mushroom is cooked, add in steamed vegetables, salt, pepper, red chilli flakes, oregano and lemon juice.
1. Stir and check the taste and adjust the condiments accordingly.
1. Stir fry for a about a minute on high heat to combine all the ingredients to make a delicious tasting Steamed Vegetables With Chilli Lime Butter.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and transfer the Steamed Vegetables With Chilli Lime Butter to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve the Steamed Vegetables With Chilli Lime Butter Recipe along with Lemon butter chicken, Creamy Garlic Mashed Potatoes and Smoked Vegetarian Spanish Rice to make it a delicious continental dinner for your family.
