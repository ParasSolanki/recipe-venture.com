### Ingredients

- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds - crushed.
- Jaggery - heated till molten or condensed milk.
- Rice flour - as required.
- 1 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split) - boiled.
- 2 Sweet Potatoes - boiled.
- 1-1/2 cups Nolen Gur.
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 1 cup Fresh coconut.
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera) - roast and ground.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Wheat Flour.
- Sunflower Oil - to deep fry.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Bhaja Pithe Recipe, get ready with all the ingredients.
1. Take a kadai and dry roast coconut and jaggery, stir constantly till fragrant and done, to avoid burning.
1. Keep aside.
1. To make the dough, in a bowl, mash the boiled moong dal and boiled sweet potatoes.
1. Add jaggery, salt, roasted cumin powder and sesame seeds and mix well.
1. Add wheat flour and rice flour and make the dough soft.
1. To make Bhaja PitheMake small balls from the prepared dough.
1. Take one by one and Flatten using your palm then with thumb and finger make shapes as of a bowl.
1. Stuff the coconut filling in it and close its edges and make a shape like the puli pithe.
1. Deep fry in hot oil on medium flame in a deep frying pan, till they are golden brown.
1. Deep fry in batches.
1. Allow it to cool and drizzle condensed milk or liquid jaggery to serve Bhaja Pithe Recipe.
