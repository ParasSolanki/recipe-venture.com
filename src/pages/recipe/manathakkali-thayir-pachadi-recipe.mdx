### Ingredients

- 1 Mor Milagai (Dried Green Chillies).
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Manathakkali Kai Vathal.
- Water - as needed.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 1/3 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Manathakkali Thayir Pachadi Recipe, heat a pan and add oil.
1. Once the oil is hot, add mustard and hing.
1. Tear the mor milagai into smaller pieces and add them to the oil.
1. Add the manathakkali kai vathal and fry them.
1. Add salt and mix well.
1. The manathakkali berry would start to burst out after few minutes.
1. Hence close with a lid and keep stirring at regular intervals.
1. Once cooked, add coriander leaves and switch off and allow it to cool.
1. Add Yogurt and dilute with water to make it a raita consistency.
1. Mix well.
1. Let this marinate for 1 hour (Minimum) in fridge or at room temperature.
1. Serve Manathakkali Thayir Pachadi Recipe chilled, or at room temperature, along with Maharashtrian Multigrain Thalipeeth or Chettinad Vegetable Pulao Recipe.
