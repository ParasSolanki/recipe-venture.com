### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Tamarind Paste.
- Curry leaves - a few.
- 1 tablespoon Gram flour (besan).
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 3 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a few.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - powdered.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 pinch Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Dry coconut (kopra) - grated/powdered.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 teaspoons Jaggery - (amla size).

### Instructions

1. To Make Balls/Golyachi (kofta)To begin Maharashtrian Golyachi Amti Recipe (Besan Balls In Tamarind Based Spicy Gravy), heat a tablespoon of oil in a tadka pan.
1. Take besan in a mixing bowl and break lumps if any, using your hand.
1. Add hot oil to it.
1. Mix as it bubbles up.
1. Later add carom seeds, ginger garlic paste, chilli, salt, turmeric and water.
1. Make a dough such that it is nor too stiff or loose.
1. It should be stiffer than the pakoda dough.
1. Make small balls from the dough and keep aside.
1. To Make AmtiTake a kadai and heat oil.
1. Temper it with mustard, wait till it splutters and add cumin.
1. Once it sizzles, add hing and curry leaves.
1. When they splutter, add onion and ginger garlic paste.
1. Saute till the raw smell disappears.
1. Add coriander powder, haldi, salt and chilli powder.
1. Saute 1-2 mins.
1. Meanwhile, make a slurry by mixing besan with water (about 4 cups)such that it is lump-free.
1. Add it to the kadai.
1. When water begins to boil, add the besan balls .
1. Simmer and allow to cook for 8-10 mins .
1. Add tamarind water, jaggery, sesame powder, dry coconut powder and bring to boil.
1. Do not stir too much since the golyachi might break.
1. Finally garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve Maharashtrian Golyachi Amti Recipe (Besan Balls In Tamarind Based Spicy Gravy) hot with Tawa paratha or steamed rice.
