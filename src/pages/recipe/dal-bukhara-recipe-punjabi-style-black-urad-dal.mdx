### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 tablespoon Butter.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 cup Black Urad Dal (Whole) - soaked overnight.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- 1/4 cup Fresh cream.
- 3 Tomatoes - pureed.
- 1 Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dal Bukhara recipe, pressure cook the black grams with required cups water till done.
1. Remove and mash the dal mixture.
1. Heat butter in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Splutter cumin and bay leaf.
1. Add onions, green chilli, ginger garlic paste and saute till light brown.
1. Add tomato puree and salt.
1. Mix everything well and cook for about 3 to 5 minutes.
1. After 3 to 5 minutes, add all the spice powder including Kasuri methi, red chilli powder, chaat masala, cumin powder, turmeric powder and cook for two minutes.
1. After 2 minutes, add the dal mixture, required the amount of water and let it simmer for few minutes.
1. Finally, add garam masala powder and cream and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve Dal Bukhara along with Bharma Baingan and Phulka for a weekday meal.
