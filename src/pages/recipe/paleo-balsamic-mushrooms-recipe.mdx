### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 tablespoons Balsamic Vinegar - (Organic).
- Spring Onion Greens - for garnish.
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - freshly ground.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Button mushrooms.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 3 tablespoon Butter.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the  Paleo Balsamic Mushrooms Recipe, clean mushrooms with a damp cloth to remove any dirt.
1. Slice them into halves and keep aside.
1. I removed only the stalks and used mushrooms, which are smaller ,as a whole.
1. In a skillet, add butter and allow it to melt on medium flame.
1. Once the butter is melted, add chopped garlic and chopped onions.
1. Saute until the onions become translucent.
1. Once done add mushrooms and cook until they become tender, for about 10 minutes.
1. Don’t overcook as the mushrooms become rubbery.
1. Add salt, balsamic vinegar and pepper to the mushrooms; Saute for about 2 – 3 minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Garnish with  spring onions and serve Paleo Balsamic Mushrooms Recipe warm.
1. Serve this Balsamic mushrooms as sides for your Mexican Lime Chicken or Grilled Chicken With Vegetables.
