### Ingredients

- 2 Pineapple - sliced thin.
- 2 Ripe Bananas - peeled and sliced.
- 1 Apple - or chickoo.
- 3 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 2 tablespoon Jam - Mixed fruit.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Fruit Sandwich Recipe, get all the ingredients handy.
1. Apply jam to one side of 2 bread slices.
1. To assemble the sandwich, take one bread with jam applied and over it place the fruit slices of pineapple, bananas, chickoo or apple in any order.
1. Arrange the fruits slices completely on the bread.
1. Over this, place another bread slice.
1. The jam side should be facing on the top.
1. Over this arrange the sliced fruits again and close the sandwich with a slice to which no jam has been applied.
1. Grill this fruit sandwich on a Grill sandwich toaster till the topmost slice of bread turns brownish.
1. If you donâ€™t like to grill, simply have the sandwich as it is.
1. To serve, cut the sandwich into 2 triangles and serve Fruit Sandwich with a cup of Espresso Coffee for your weekday breakfast.
