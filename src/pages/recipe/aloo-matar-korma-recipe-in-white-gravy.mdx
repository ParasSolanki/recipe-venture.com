### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- 1 cup Green peas (Matar) - boiled.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 tablespoons Cashew nuts.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 500 grams Baby Potatoes - boiled.
- 1/4 cup Fresh cream.
- 1 Onion - roughly chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 2 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 4 Cloves (Laung).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Aloo Matar Korma Recipe In White Gravy, wash and pressure cook the baby potatoes to 2 whistles.
1. Peel and keep aside.
1. Similarly, steam or boil the green peas and keep them aside.
1. Make a smooth paste of the ingredients mentioned under 'grind into a smooth paste' which includes onion, cashew nuts, green chillies and ginger using a mixer grinder.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now, heat ghee in a heavy bottomed pan, add the bayleaf, cardamoms, cloves and let it release its aroma into the ghee.
1. This will take about 10 to 15 seconds.
1. Add the ground onion ginger cashew mixture into the pan and saute for 3 minutes.
1. Gently whisk the curd and garam masala into the cream to a smooth texture and add it to the pan.
1. Add a cup of water, season with salt and let it simmer for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Add the boiled baby potatoes and green peas, simmer for another 2 minutes and switch off the flame.
1. Serve this delicious Aloo Matar Korma Recipe In White Gravy along with Tawa Paratha and Palak Raita for a weekday meal.
