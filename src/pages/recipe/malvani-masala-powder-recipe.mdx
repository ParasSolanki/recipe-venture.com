### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 2 Star anise.
- 2 tablespoon Poppy seeds.
- 3 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 tablespoon Nutmeg powder.
- 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 5 Mace (Javitri).
- 2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 6 Dry Red Chillies.
- 5 Cloves (Laung).
- 2 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 tablespoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 3 Stone flower - (Optional).

### Instructions

1. To begin preparing the Malvani Masala Recipe, heat a sauce pan, roast the coriander seeds, cumin seeds, fennel seeds, peppercorns, poppy seeds first for about 2 minutes.
1. Add the dry red chillies, star anise, mace, cloves, stone flower, nutmeg powder, turmeric, and hing and roast them for about 3 more minutes.
1. Cool them down and grind it into a coarse powder and store it in a mason jar for later use.
1. Serve the Malvani Masala Recipe in a Maharashtrian Style Kelfulachi Bhaji Recipe (Banana Floweret Stir Fry Recipe) and Malvani Kurkuri Bhindi Recipe - Moongphali Bhindi Sabzi.
