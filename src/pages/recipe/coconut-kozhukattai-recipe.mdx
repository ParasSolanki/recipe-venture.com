### Ingredients

- Salt - a bit.
- 150 grams Rice flour.
- Ghee - as required to bind.
- 50 grams Fresh coconut.
- 80 grams Jaggery.
- Water - as needed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Coconut Kozhukattai Recipe, powder the jaggery in a ammikallu or by pounding or in a mixer grinder, sieve the flour and add them in a large bowl.
1. Add the grated coconut, little ghee and salt to it.
1. Boil the water in a saucepan, and add to the mixture in the mixing bowl, little by little, while kneading the mixture into a soft dough.
1. Shape the dough into small balls.
1. Prepare a steamer and preheat the vessel till the water beneath the steamer plate is boiling and steaming.
1. Arrange the kozhukattai on the steamer plate.
1. Steam cook the balls in a preheated steamer for approximately ten minutes or till steamed and cooked well.
1. Serve Coconut Kozhukattai Recipe as a dessert or as a snack with Turmeric Milk Recipe (Haldi Doodh).
