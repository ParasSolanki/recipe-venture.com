### Ingredients

- 250 gram Boneless chicken.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil - or butter.
- 1/4 cup Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens).
- 7 tablespoon Del Monte Cheesy Garlic Mayo.
- 4 Slider bun.
- 3 Whole Eggs - hard boiled.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken Boiled Egg Sliders With Cheesy Garlic Mayo recipe, clean and wash chicken well.
1. Cut into small cubes.
1. Sprinkle salt and pepper powder over the chicken.
1. Add 3 tablespoons DelMonte Cheesy Garlic Mayonnaise over it, mix well to coat evenly all over the chicken.
1. Cover and keep it refrigerated for about 30 minutes.
1. Heat olive oil in a pan.
1. Thrown in the marinated chicken pieces and cook for about 7 to 10 minutes until chicken gets cooked through.
1. Remove the cooked chicken from the pan and transfer into a bowl, let it cool down a bit.
1. Shred the chicken.
1. Break down boiled eggs into smaller pieces and put them into the same pan.
1. Add 4 to 5 tablespoons of DelMonte Cheesy Garlic Mayonnaise over the chicken and egg, mix with a spoon to coat evenly.
1. Sprinkle chives over it and mix gently.
1. Cut the bun in the middle half way through making sure not to cut them into 2 separate pieces.
1. Scoop a big spoon of this mixture and place it between the 2 slider buns and press the top bun.
1. Serve immediately.
1. Serve Chicken Boiled Egg Sliders With Cheesy Garlic Mayo along with Potato Wedges and a glass of your favourite beverage for a movie night or for a get-together.
1. Serve Chicken Boiled Egg Sliders With Cheesy Garlic Mayo for breakfast with a glass of Nutty Delight Smoothie Recipe.
