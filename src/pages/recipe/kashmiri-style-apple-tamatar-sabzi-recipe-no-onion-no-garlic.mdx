### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Tomatoes - pureed or grated.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta) - torn.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Apples - peeled and cubed.
- 1 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - or black salt to taste.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Apple Tamatar Ki Sabzi  heating a kadai with a little bit of oil and add bay leaf and cinnamon.
1. Saute till the aroma is released.
1. Next, add the tomato puree and the other spice powders.
1. Saute for about 10 minutes till the raw smell of the tomato goes away.
1. Add the cubed apple and give it a stir.
1. Cook for 10 more minutes till the apple becomes mush and imbibes a little bit of the gravy.
1. Serve your sweet and tangy Apple Tamatar ki Sabzi with Singhare ka Cheela or Ajwain Puri to make it a complete meal.
