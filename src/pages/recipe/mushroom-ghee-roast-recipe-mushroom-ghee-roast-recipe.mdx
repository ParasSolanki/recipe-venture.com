### Ingredients

-  2 tbsp ghee.
-  1 tsp whole black pepper.
-  1 tbsp tamarind paste.
-  1 tsp fenugreek seeds .
-  1 tbsp lemon juice.
-  2 sprig curry leaves.
-  1/2 tsp turmeric powder.
-  6 cloves garlic.
-  2 bud long.
-  3 tbsp curd.
-  6 dry red chillies.
-  1 tsp cumin.
-  salt - as per taste.
- 400 grams Button Mushroom - chopped.
-  2 tsp coriander seeds.
-  2 tsp jaggery.

### Instructions

1. To make Mushroom Ghee Roast recipe, first marinate the mushroom.
1. In a bowl, add mushrooms, curd, turmeric powder, lemon juice and mix well.
1. Keep it aside for 30 minutes.
1. Now to make Ghee Roast Masala, add dry red chilli, whole black pepper, long, fenugreek seeds, coriander seeds, cumin seeds and roast for 4 to 5 minutes in a pan.
1. Turn off the gas and cool the spices Let it happen After cooling, add garlic, tamarind paste, a little water and grind it to a paste in a mixer grinder.
1. Keep it aside.
1. To make Mushroom Ghee Roast: Heat the ghee in a pan.
1. Add curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. After the curry leaves crackle, add mushrooms and marinated spices.
1. Cook on medium flame till it becomes soft.
1. After it becomes soft, add salt, ghee roast masala, jaggery and mix well.
1. Let it cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Turn off the gas and serve.
1. Serve Mashrum Ghee Roast Recipe with Kerala and served with beetroot raita for dinner.
