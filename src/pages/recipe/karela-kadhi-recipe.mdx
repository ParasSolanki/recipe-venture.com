### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 3 Green Chillies - crushed to paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1-1/2 cups Water.
- 2 Karela (Bitter Gourd/ Pavakkai) - de skinned.
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 inch Ginger - crushed to paste.
- 1 teaspoon Gram flour (besan).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1/2 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Karela Kadhi Recipe, whisk together curd, water and besan well making sure no lumps are formed.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Slice bitter gourd, soak them in salted water until use, this will remove some of the bitterness from the gourd.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds and let it splutter.
1. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
1. Once the onions are soft and translucent, add curry leaves, green chilli, garlic paste and sauté until there is no raw smell of ginger left.
1. Add sliced bitter gourd and sauté for about 5 to 7 minutes.
1. Add salt to taste.
1. Finally, add besan yogurt mix and stir.
1. Simmer and let it cook until you it begins to boil.
1. Once done, switch off the heat and serve.
1. Serve Karela Kadhi Recipe along with Jeera Rice, Phulka, Bharwa Besan Mirch and Kachumber Salad for a weekday meal.
