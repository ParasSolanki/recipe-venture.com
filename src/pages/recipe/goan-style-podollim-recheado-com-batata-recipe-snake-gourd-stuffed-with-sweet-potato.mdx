### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Sweet Potato - skin peeled and boiled.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Snake gourd (pudulangai) - peeled and cut into roundels.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Goan Style Podollim Recheado Com Batata Recipe, we will first pressure cook the sweet potato in a pressure cooker with water for about 5 whistle.
1. Meanwhile, soak the cut snake gourd in water mixed with salt and leave it rest for atleast 15 minutes.
1. Once the pressure is released naturally from the cooker, remove and drain any excess water that is present.
1. Heat a flat skillet with oil, add mustard seeds and allow it splutter for few seconds.
1. Add curry leaves and leave it to crackle.
1. Add chopped ginger, garlic and soften for about 3 minutes.
1. Add chopped onions and saute them until they turn translucent.
1. Add cooked sweet potato along with the spice powders and salt and give it a toss.
1. Mash the sweet potato well using a masher and combine them together.
1. Switch off the heat and allow it to rest.
1. Take the snake gourd from the water and pat dry them.
1. Once the sweet potato is dried.
1. Take some of it and carefully stuff it in between the snake gourd to form discs or cutlets.
1. Coat them with bread crumbs on either side or heat a flat skillet with oil to shallow fry them.
1. Cook them on either side for at least 10 minutes till it becomes light brown in color.
1. Serve the Goan Style Podollim Recheado Com Batata as a kids snack after they come back from school along with a glass of smoothies or freshly squeezed Mulled Apple Juice Recipe.
