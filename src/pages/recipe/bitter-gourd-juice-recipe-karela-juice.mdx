### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 pinch Salt.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - few.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black Salt (Kala Namak).
- 1 Ginger.
- 2 teaspoons Lemon juice.
- 2 Karela (Bitter Gourd/ Pavakkai) - deseeded.
- Honey - to taste (optional).
- Black pepper powder - a pinch.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bitter Gourd Juice Recipe - Karela Juice, get all the ingredients ready.
1. When you cut the bitter gourd to make the juice, do not remove the skin, as the skin has the maximum benefits and is full of nutrients.
1. If the seeds are tender enough then you don't have to remove the seeds as well.
1. Place the bitter gourd and ginger into the Juicer and extract the juice out.
1. The first juice that comes out is very concentrated, so add some water into the juicer and squeeze out the juice from the remaining pulp.
1. Pour the juice into a jar, add all the remaining ingredients including lemon juice, turmeric powder, black salt, honey, salt, black pepper powder, mint leaves and give it a good stir.
1. Check the concentration and add water to adjust.
1. Check the salt and flavor levels and adjust as required.
1. Serve the Bitter Gourd Juice Recipe - Karela Juice immediately for maximum benefits to health and build immunity.
