### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 500 ml Bisleri Fonzo - The Bubbly Mango Drink.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black Salt (Kala Namak).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mango Shikanji Recipe, get all the ingredients handy first.
1. In a large jug combine, the add the Bisleri Fonzo, black salt, cumin powder, chaat masala powder, black pepper powder lemon juice and some ice cubes.
1. Mix the Shikanji well and serve chilled immediately.
1. Serve Mango Shikanji Recipe along with Cheesy Pav Bhaji Recipe and Chole Aloo Tikki Chaat Recipe for a weekend chaat party.
