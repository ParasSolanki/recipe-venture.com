### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Dried oregano.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Basil leaves - chopped.
- Black pepper powder - as required.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1-1/2 cup Homemade Pizza And Pasta Sauce.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garlic - minced.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 Green zucchini - cut lengthwise into very thin slices.
- 1 cup Mozzarella cheese - shredded.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - crumbled.

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Zucchini Lasagna Recipe first, arrange zucchini slices on a colander and sprinkle some salt and leave it free for 30 minutes.
1. Meanwhile heat oil in a pan Add chopped onion and minced garlic and saut√© it till onion turn translucent.
1. Now add pasta sauce and stir the mixture for about 3 minutes.
1. Add the cottage cheese, salt, black pepper, oregano, basil and cook for another 2 minutes.
1. Rinse the salt off from the zucchini and squeeze the water out.
1. Now in another skillet heat some oil and add the zucchinis to it.
1. Let them cook till they turn brown on both the sides.
1. Now we are ready to assemble the lasagna, take an oven saved bowl and spread cooked pasta in the bottom and start to layer the zucchini slices.
1. Spread some more pasta sauce over the zucchini and now sprinkle the mozzarella cheese.
1.  Repeat this step for another 3 times.
1. Preheat oven to 425 F and bake the lasagna for 10-15 mins and plate them on a serving plate.
1. Garnish it with the chopped basil and serve them hot.
1. Serve the Zucchini Lasagna with some Mocha Pana Cotta Recipe for a complete meal.
