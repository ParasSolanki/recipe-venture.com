### Ingredients

- 2 cloves Garlic.
- Salt - to taste.
- 10 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 4 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 300 grams Prawns - shelled and deveined.
- 5 Curry leaves.
- 1-2 teaspoons Malt vinegar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Jeera Miri Kolambi Recipe (Cumin & Black Pepper Prawns), grind all the ingredients for the spice paste including ginger, garlic, turmeric powder, dry red chillies, whole black peppercorns, salt, malt vinegar with about 2 tablespoons water.
1. Make a smooth spice paste.
1. Marinade the prawns with the paste and add some salt.
1. Keep aside.
1. Heat the oil in a kadai.
1. On a medium high heat, add the curry leaves and the onions.
1. Stir occasionally.
1. When the onions are caramelised, add the tomato paste or chopped tomato.
1. Cook on medium heat till the tomatoes are mushy and the onions and tomatoes have come together.
1. Add the prawns along with all the spice paste they were marinaded in.
1. Add about 1/4 cup of water and cook on high heat till the prawns are done and all the water has evaporated.
1. (If you want, you can add more water and you can make a gravy dish from the same prawns as well.
1. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
1. Serve Jeera Miri Kolambi Recipe (Cumin & Black Pepper Prawns) with some Coconut Rice and Raw Mango Raita Recipe or along with Green Chutney as a party appetizer served with Cucumbertini Recipe for cocktail party.
