### Ingredients

- 250 grams Banana Stem - skin peeled and cut into small pieces.
- 1 cup Kala Chana (Brown Chickpeas) - soaked in water overnight.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 3 Dry Red Chilli.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves - roughly torn.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Kondakadalai Vazhathandu Puli Thengai Kuzhambu Recipe (Banana Stem Curry with Black Chickpeas Recipe) by pressure cooking the Kala channa with some water first in the pressure cooker for 4 whistle.
1. Release the pressure naturally.
1. Then empty the black chickpea into a bowl, then in the same pressure cooker, add the banana stem with little water, salt and cook it for 1 whistle and rest.
1. Heat a heavy bottomed pan, add oil and crackle mustard seeds and curry leaves.
1. Then add the ground paste and saut√© for few minutes, you can add little water.
1. Finally add the cooked banana stem and chickpeas, season it well and add some water to make it little gravish.
1. Serve the Kondakadalai Vazhathandu Puli Thengai Kuzhambu Recipe (Banana Stem Curry with Black Chickpeas Recipe) along with steam rice or Hot Phulka to enjoy your dinner meal.
