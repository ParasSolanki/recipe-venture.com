### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - (black and white).
- 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 1 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - grated.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1 tablespoons Basil leaves - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Rice Vinegar.
- Red Chilli flakes - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 3 tablespoons Peanut Butter - unsweetened.
- 150 grams Soba noodles.
- 2 cups Red Yellow or Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - julienned.
- 2 tablespoons Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - chopped.
- 2 cups Red cabbage - shredded.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Soba Noodle Salad recipe, in a pot boil the soba noodles as per package instructions.
1. Strain under cold water and keep aside.
1. In a salad bowl, add the red cabbage, bell peppers, green onions, carrots, coriander leaves, mint leaves and basil leaves.
1. Mix all the vegetables.
1. Then take another bowl and add the peanut butter, honey, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, sriracha, ginger, salt, pepper, chilli flakes and lime juice.
1. Whisk well to combine all the ingredients to form a dressing.
1. Add the noodles to the veggies in the salad bowl followed by the dressing and sesame seeds.
1. Mix well and serve cold.
1. The salad tastes better if you let it sit in the refrigerator for some time.
1. Enjoy!Serve Soba Noodle Salad along with Chinese Vegetarian Fried Rice With Apples for a weekend night dinner.
