### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3 Dry Red Chillies.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cups Mixed vegetables - I have used radish turnips.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 cup Water.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split) - can use toor dal as well.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Mulakootal Recipe first, in a blender grind fresh coconut and dried red chilies together to a coarse paste.
1. Now in a pressure cooker wash the lentils and cook them completely.
1. Take a Kadai add the vegetables, cook the vegetables completely.
1. After the vegetables are cooked add the dal to it and mix them well.
1. Combine and cook the vegetables and dal for 5 minutes and then add coconut paste to the mixture.
1. On low flame cook for another 5 minutes.
1. You can add some water if you feel the consistency is thickNow in a small pan heat some oil and add mustard seeds, wait till they splutter and then add urad dal, curry leaves.
1. Add this tempering to the mulakootal and serve it hot.
1. Serve your Mulakootal with hot rice or roti with some curd.
