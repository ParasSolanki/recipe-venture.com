### Ingredients

- 2 Green zucchini - sliced.
- 1 Onion - diced.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 cups Water.
- 1 cup Sweet corn.
- Salt - to taste.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 4 cups Vegetable stock.
- 2 Tomatoes - diced.
- 1/2 cup Red Yellow and Green Bell Peppers (Capsicum) - sliced to thin long strips.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli sauce - (adjust).
- 2 teaspoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 4 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 250 grams Canned black beans - drained and rinsed.
- 2 Avocado - diced.
- 1/2 Pickled Jalapenos - finely chopped.
- 1-1/2 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 50 grams Tortillas.
- 6 cloves Garlic - minced.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Hearty Vegetable Tortilla Soup Recipe, heat some olive oil on a medium flame until hot in a large saucepan.
1. Add the onion, garlic, and jalape√±o.
1. Saute for about 3 minutes or until softened, stirring occasionally.
1. Stir in the cumin, then the chopped tomatoes, zucchini, bell peppers, sweet corn, water and vegetable stock.
1. Simmer for about 3 minutes, then add black beans and the red chilli pepper flakes.
1. Cook for 13 minutes, or until slightly thickened, stirring occasionally.
1. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
1. Mix all the ingredients mentioned to prepare pico de gallo in a mixing bowl.
1. In a soup bowl, crumble few pieces of tortilla chips roughly, add few pieces of avocado, pour 2 ladles of soup with veggies.
1. Top it with some pico de gallo, few pieces of avocado, cilantro.
1. Enjoy Hearty Vegetable Tortilla Soup Recipe with a crusty bread like focaccia.
