### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 125 Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- 1 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 250 gram Sugar - (you can substitute with palm granulated sugar).
- 3 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 375 gram All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 2 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 25 gram Milk Powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 2 Egg yolks.
- 200 grams Butter.
- 175 ml Lukewarm Water.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Roti Gambang Recipe, Boil the Java red sugar with the water until completely dissolved and becomes a syrup consistency.
1. Switch off flame and let it cool.
1. In a bowl, mix together flour, bread crumbs, milk powder, salt, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, vanilla.
1. Add in egg yolk, if using.
1. Pour the sugar syrup and knead well for ten minutes.
1. Add in the butter and knead again.
1. Cover the bowl with cling wrap and let it rest for 30 minutes.
1. Divide the dough into equal parts (about 12).
1. Shape the dough balls into ovals and arrange on Baking tray.
1. Brush with water on top of the bread dough and sprinkle sesame seeds on top.

1. Serve Roti Gambang Recipe with coffee/tea for breakfast, or a hearty soup like Israeli Couscous Minestrone Soup Recipe for dinner.
