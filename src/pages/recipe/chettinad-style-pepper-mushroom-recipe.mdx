### Ingredients

- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1/4 teaspoon Nutmeg powder.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 2 inch Ginger.
- 1/2 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Star anise.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 cup Button mushrooms - cleaned and sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Poppy seeds.
- 10 Shallots.
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- 2 teaspoons White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 5 cloves Garlic - peeled.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 3 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 4 teaspoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 Mace (Javitri) - small flower.
- 4 Cloves (Laung).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chettinad Style Pepper Mushroom Recipe, start with making the aromatic spice mix.
1. Take a pan and dry roast the ingredients listed in the spice mix till aromatic, grind it to a fine paste using a mixer and keep it aside.
1. Now, make a paste of shallots, ginger, garlic, curry leaves and turmeric powder to fine paste and keep it aside.
1. Heat a wide pan in low flame, pour oil, put the bay leaf, fennel seeds, and curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. Now add the chopped tomato and the slit green chili and saut√© for five minutes.
1. Add the onion, ginger garlic paste that was made earlier and cook till the raw smell of the ginger garlic goes away.
1. Add the aromatic spice mixture, season with salt and add a little water and let it cook for ten minutes, keep stirring the gravy in between.
1. Now add the cleaned, sliced mushrooms and cook it with the lid on for few more minutes.
1. Once the mushrooms are cooked, switch off the flame and garnish with the finely chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve Chettinad Style Pepper Mushroom Recipe with rotis or steamed rice for lunch or dinner.
