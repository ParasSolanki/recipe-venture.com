### Ingredients

- Butter - as required.
- 1 Baguette - cut into slices.
- Red Chilli flakes - as needed.
- 1/4 cup Green olives - chopped.
- 250 grams Shrimps - cleaned and deveined.
- 4 tablespoons Del Monte Cheesy Dip.
- Salt - as needed.
- 3 sprig Dill leaves - chopped.
- Salad leaves - (rocket leaves or any other salad leaf).
- Black pepper powder - as needed.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - as required.
- 3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Walnuts - roughly broken.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Shrimp Walnut Bruschetta Recipe Flavored With Cheesy Dip Mayo, get all the ingredients prepped and ready.
1. Apply little oil on each side of the baguette slices, season with salt and pepper.
1. Heat the pan and toast the slices until brown and crisp.
1. You can also toast it in preheated oven at 180 C for 10 minutes until crisp and roasted.
1. Take the baguette slices out of the pan and set aside.
1. In the same pan, melt 1 tablespoon of butter.
1. Add garlic and saute until golden and aromaticAdd shrimps and toss on high for a minute.
1. Cook for 5 to 6 minutes until the shrimp is nicely seared and done.
1. Season with salt pepper and chili flakes.
1. Take it off the heat and keep the shrimp aside.
1. In a bowl, add butter tossed prawns, walnuts, dill leaves, lemon zest, olives and mix.
1. Stir in the Del Monte Cheesy Dip and mix well until combined.
1. Check for salt and season if required and adjust to taste.
1. Now, take the baguette slices and smear one side of the slices with Del Monte Cheesy Dip and top it with prepared Shrimp Walnut Mayo Mixture.
1. Place the Shrimp Walnut Mayo Bruschetta on serving platter and serve with rocket leaves as an appetizer for parties.
