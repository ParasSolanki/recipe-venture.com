### Ingredients

- 6 Curry leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 2 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated (omit ginger if jain).
- 1 cup Mango (Ripe) - pureed.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 teaspoons Jaggery.
- 3 tablespoons Gram flour (besan).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Gujarati Fajeto (Mango Kadhi) recipe, into a saucepan combine all the main ingredients along with 2 cups of water and whisk them together until well combined.
1. Bring the mixture to come to a boil whisking occasionally.
1. Once the mixture begins to boil, turn the heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes.
1. To make the seasoning for the Fajeto Mango Kadhi; Heat ghee in a large saucepan on medium heat; add mustard seeds and cumin seeds allow them to crackle.
1. Add the cinnamon, dry red chilli, curry leaves, ginger and green chillies and stir for a few seconds.
1. Check the salt and spice levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Pour the seasoning over the simmering Fajeto Kadhi.
1. Turn off the heat and garnish the Kadhi with chopped coriander leaves.
