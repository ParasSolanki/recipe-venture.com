### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 cup Pearl onions (Sambar Onions).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Fennel seeds (Saunf) - pound.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Coconut Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 cups Elephant yam (Suran/Senai/Ratalu) - chopped into small cubes.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 6 cloves Garlic.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kerala Style Chena Mezhukkupuratty Recipe, firstly boil the yam in a pressure cooker with water, salt and turmeric for about 3 whistle.
1. Do not boil it till it becomes mushy.
1. Strain the water and keep it aside.
1. In a mixer, add in the onions and garlic and grind it into a coarse paste.
1. Heat a kadai with oil, splutter the mustard seeds, curry leaves and then add in the ground onions and garlic.
1. Add in the spice powders including red chilli powder, turmeric powder, fennel seeds, black pepper powder and fry till the raw smell goes away.
1. Once done add in the boiled yam and coat the mixture well into the yam.
1. Stir fry till it crisps up and check for seasoning by adding salt.
1. Serve the Kerala Style Chena Mezhukkupuratty Recipe along with Thandu Keerai Sambar, Steamed Rice and Elai Vadam for your everyday meals.
