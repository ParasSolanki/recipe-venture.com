### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 cup Broccoli - cut into small florets.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1 cup Spinach Leaves (Palak).
- 4 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Whole Eggs.
- 1 cup Avarekai (Broad Beans).
- 1 teaspoon Dried Thyme Leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spinach & Broad Bean Shakshuka Recipe, heat a sauce pan with oil.
1. Add onions and garlic and saute until they turn translucent.
1. Add chopped tomatoes and sprinkle with little salt and stir fry until the tomatoes become soft and the raw smell goes away.
1. Add spinach leaves and saute until they wilt, this will take about 10 minutes.
1. Add all the spice powders and give it a toss.
1. Add broccoli, broad beans and about 1/2 cup of water and cover it with a lid and cook for about 10 minutes till the broccoli and green beans become tender.
1. Once done open the lid and mix all the ingredients together.
1. Finally, crack the eggs into the tomato mixture and space them out evenly.
1. Sprinkle with little salt and pepper and Cover it with lid and cook for about 5 minutes.
1. Serve the Spinach & Broad Bean Shakshuka Recipe along with garlic bread or focaccia bread that would go really well along with curry.
