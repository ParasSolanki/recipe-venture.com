### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 inch Ginger - grated.
- 3 Green Chillies - chopped (adjustable).
- 1 cup Ragi Rava.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 tablespoon Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - crushed.
- 1/2 cup Cauliflower (gobi) - florets.
- 1/4 cup Green beans (French Beans) - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves - torn with hands.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Nagli (Ragi) Rava Upma recipe, first heat ghee in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add the rava and roast on low flame for 2 to 3 minutes till you get a nice aroma.
1. Stir constantly so that it does not burn.
1. If you do not want to use ghee dry roast the rava.
1. Take it out onto a plate.
1. Heat oil in the same pan, add the mustards seeds and once they crackle, add cumin seeds.
1. Add the garlic, green chillies and the curry leaves.
1. Fry for a few seconds and then add the onions.
1. Saute for a minute and then add asafoetida and ginger.
1. Add the vegetables and mix everything well.
1. Add salt, mix and cook covered for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Add about 2-1/2 to 3 cups water and mix well.
1. Adjust the amount of salt if required, add sugar, groundnuts and amsul.
1. Once the water boils add the roasted rava, stirring constantly.
1. Cook covered on low heat till it becomes soft and the water has evaporated.
1. Garnish with chopped mint or coriander leaves, lime juice and serve immediately.
1. Serve Nagli (Ragi) Rava Upma along with South Indian Coconut Chutney and Masala Chai for a healthy and delicious breakfast on weekdays.
