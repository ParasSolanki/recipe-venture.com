### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Curry powder.
- 4 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 7 Whole Eggs.
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - juliennes for garnish.
- 3 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Akoori with Green Peas Recipe, heat oil in a pan add the chopped onions, ginger garlic paste and saute till onions turn translucent.
1. Add the green chillies, tomatoes and saute for 2 minutes.
1. Add the green peas, turmeric, red chilli, curry powder, and saute until the peas are cooked well.
1. Now, whisk eggs in a big bowl till it becomes fluffy.
1. Add this to the pan, season with salt and saute to make runny scrambled eggs.
1. Switch off the flame, add the coriander leaves, lemon juice, and mix well.
1. Garnish with ginger juliennes and serve hot.
1. Serve the Akoori with Green Peas Recipe (Parsi Style Scrambled Eggs with Green Peas) along with toasted bread or pav along and Masala chai for breakfast.
