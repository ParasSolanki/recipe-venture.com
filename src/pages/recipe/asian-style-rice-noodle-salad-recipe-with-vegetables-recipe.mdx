### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 200 grams Rice Noodles (Flat) - or thin.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 4 sprig Thai basil leaves - roughly torn.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 2 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 12 Green beans (French Beans) - cut into 1 inch pieces.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 1 Broccoli - cut into florets.
- 4 Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - cut into 1 inch pieces.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Asian Style Rice Noodle Salad Recipe With Vegetables, cut the vegetables according to instructions and bring all the ingredients together.
1. Cook the rice noodles in boiling water until al dente (firm and not too soft) in a saucepan.
1. Once done, turn off the heat, drain the water and wash the noodles in water to prevent further cooking.
1. Steam the broccoli, beans and peas using a steamer on high heat for about 4 to 5 minutes.
1. Keep the vegetables firm as it is a salad, you would like it to have a crunch.
1. The next step is to make the dressing.
1. Toss all the ingredients for the dressing in a mixing bowl and whisk it with a fork.
1. In a large mixing bowl, add the cooked noodles, steamed vegetables, the spring onions, basil leaves and the dressing.
1. Toss all the ingredients well until well combined.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Serve the Asian Style Rice Noodle Salad along with Cream Of Celery Soup for a perfect summer dinner along with a glass of fresh juice or beer.
