### Ingredients

- Salt.
- 1/4 cup Black olives - halved.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano - crushed (can chop if using fresh oregano).
- 2 Avocado - mashed.
- 1/4 cup Cherry tomatoes - halved.
- 1/4 cup Sweet corn - boiled.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - a few sprigs finely chopped.
- Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 Carrots (Gajjar) - sliced into small pieces.
- 1 Cucumber - cut into small pieces.
- 1 cup Iceberg lettuce - roughly chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin the preparation of Delicious Avocado Salad Recipe with Vegetables chop all the vegetables (carrot, avocado, cherry tomatoes, iceberg, cucumber and sweet corn) mentioned and mix in a salad bowl.
1. In another bowl, add all the ingredients (olive oil, honey, salt, pepper, mint leaves and dried oregano) for the dressing.
1. Mix it well.
1. Refrigerate both separately and mix them only while serving.
1. This will make sure the vegetables remain crunchy and do not leave water which is naturally present in them.
1. Add sesame seeds and mix the salad well.
1. Serve the Delicious Avocado Salad Recipe with Vegetables as a healthy evening snack, or as a dinner appetizers before Pan Grilled Balsamic Chicken Breast And Mushrooms.
