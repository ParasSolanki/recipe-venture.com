### Ingredients

- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4 Cloves (Laung) - garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - sliced diagonally.
- 50 grams Green beans (French Beans) - sliced diagonally.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Stir Fried Carrot and Green Beans Recipe, firstly heat a flat skillet with some oil, crackle the cumin seeds.
1. Add in the chopped vegetables and saute.
1. You can add some salt and saute as it will fasten the process.
1. Next add in all the spice powders including red chilli powder, black pepper powder, coriander powder, salt and saute for another 5 minutes till the raw smell goes away.
1. You can serve it on a plate.
1. Serve the Stir Fried Carrot and Green Beans Recipe along with Keerai Sambar Recipe and Steamed Rice or with a Mattar Paneer Recipe and Makki Ki Roti Recipe.
