### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli.
- 2 tablespoon Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 4 tablespoon Fresh coconut.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 tablespoon Maharashtrian Goda Masala - or garam masala.
- 500 grams Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra).
- 1/2 Lemon juice.
- 8 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Maharastrian Style Bharli Bhindi Recipe, wash the ladies finger and slit them in the middle, cut off the top head of the ladies finger and keep it aside.
1. For the stuffing, grind the ingredients for the masala which includes roasted peanuts, green chili, cloves, coriander leaves in a mixer to a coarse paste.
1. Combine this paste with the rest of the other ingredients like the coconut, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, goda masala (Garam masala), lemon juice and sugar.
1. Check for salt and mix well.
1. Start stuffing the ladies finger with this filling and keep aside the left over stuffing for later use.
1. Heat a sauce pan, add oil, add mustard seeds and allow it to splutter for 2 seconds.
1. Once it splutters, add the turmeric powder and stuffed bhindis.
1. Saute well, and cover them for 2 minutes.
1. Open the lid and sprinkle some salt and water and add the remaining stuffing and saute them for 2 more minutes.
1. Cover and cook at low heat for 2 more minutes till the Bhindis are soft and properly cooked.
1. Serve the Maharashtrian Style Bharli Bhindi Recipe along with Tawa paratha, Smoked Dal Makhani Dhaba Style Recipe and Boondi Raita to enjoy your everyday dinner.
