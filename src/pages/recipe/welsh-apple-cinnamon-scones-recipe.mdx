### Ingredients

- Honey - to glaze.
- 1/8 teaspoon Salt.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 4 tablespoon Milk.
- Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 3 tablespoon Chilled butter - grated.
- 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 8 Pecans - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 4 tablespoon Sugar.
- 1-1/4 cups Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/2 Apple - peeled and diced.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Welsh Apple Cinnamon Scones, firstly sift the flours, cinnamon powder, salt, baking powder and sugar powder.
1. Add the cold butter and rub the butter in till it resembles bread crumbs.
1. Mix in the chopped apple and chopped pecans.
1. Whisk milk and yoghurt and leave it for about 2-3 mins.
1. When the apples start leaving a little water, add the milk and yoghurt mixture into it to make a firm dough.
1. Oil your fingers a bit and pat this dough directly onto a baking sheet.
1. Cut into wedges (adjust size according to your preferences).
1. Brush a little milk and some cinnamon powder on it and bake them in a pre-heat oven for 18-20 minutes at 180 Deg C.
1. After 18 to 20 minutes, take it out and let it cool for 4-5 mins.
1. Serve Welsh Apple Cinnamon Scones topped with honey as a sweet dish during your Weekend dinners with your friends and family.
