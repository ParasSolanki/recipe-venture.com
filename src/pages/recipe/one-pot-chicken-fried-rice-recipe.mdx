### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1 Fresh Red Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - peeled and minced.
- 10 Button mushrooms - quartered.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 4 sprig Spring Onion Greens - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - grated or minced.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1/2 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - minced.
- 1/2 cup Bean Sprouts.
- 2 tablespoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 1/2 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Cooked rice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - freshly ground.
- 100 grams Chicken breasts - cut into small pieces.
- 4 Shallots - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the One Pot Chicken Nasi Goreng Recipe, we will keep cooked rice ready.
1. Either use the leftover rice or make fresh rice and cool it down completely.
1. Heat a kadai with oil, add ginger and garlic and sauté till they soften.
1. Add in onions and sauté till they turn translucent.
1. Once the onions are soft and translucent, add carrots, bell peppers and cook until the carrots become soft.
1. You can sprinkle some salt and water, cover it with the lid and cook for atleast 10 minutes.
1. After 10 minutes, add cabbage and saute till they are cooked well.
1. This will take about 10 more minutes.
1. After the cabbage is done, add some mushroom and cook for another 10 minutes till it leaves out all the moisture.
1. At this point, add both the sauces along with salt and pepper powder.
1. Add in bean sprouts and cook the mixture.
1. Cook for about 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes, add cooked rice and stir fry them until it is evenly coated.
1. At the end sprinkle some spring onions and set aside.
1. Into a separate pan, heat some oil, add chicken pieces.
1. Sprinkle some salt, soy sauce and stir fry until the chicken is done.
1. This will take 5 to 6 minutes.
1. Add the chicken pieces into fried rice, toss it over and serve hot.
1. Serve the One Pot Chicken Nasi Goreng Recipe along with Quick Vegetable Noodles or Gobi Manchurian to make it a complete meal.
