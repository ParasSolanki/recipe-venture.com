### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1 tablespoon Melon seeds.
- 1/4 cup Cashew nuts.
- 1/2 tablespoon Feta Cheese.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese.
- 20 ml Lemon juice.
- 1/3 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 cup Dill leaves.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Dill Leaves & Parmesan Dip Recipe, wash & chop the dill leaves.
1. Take a Blender add dill leaves, 1/2 of the cashew nuts, melon seeds, yogurt, salt and feta cheese.
1. Blend well, if needed you may add water but the dip should be thick enough.
1. So add only little if needed.
1. Add the remaining cashews, parmesan cheese, pepper, and blend again.
1. This is done the second time so that the sauce would have some crunchiness of cashews and we can feel the taste of parmesan.
1. Pepper can be added in first or second batch.
1. Squeeze lemon juice and blend once so that it gets mixed well.
1. Serve Dill Leaves & Parmesan Dip Recipe with some healthy pita chips, crackers, healthy vegetable fries or nachos.
1. Dill Leaves & Parmesan Dip Recipe is an apt dip during evenings.
