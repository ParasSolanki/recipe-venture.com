### Ingredients

- 1 sprig Curry leaves - roughly torn.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 3 tablespoons Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 2 teaspoons Sugar.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 1/3 cup Green peas (Matar).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a small bunch finely chopped.
- 2 cups Poha (Flattened rice).
- Figaro Pure Olive Oil - for cooking.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mixed Vegetable Poha Recipe, heat Figaro Pure Olive Oil in a pan, add the raw peanuts and roast  until crisp.
1.  Keep the peanuts aside.
1. Heat oil in a kadai, add the mustard and cumin seeds; allow it to crackle.
1. Add the curry leaves, onions and saute until softened.
1. Once the onions have softened, add the remaining vegetables, sprinkle some salt and water and allow it to steam cook.
1. Place the poha in a colander and rinse it well under running water.
1. Stir the poha gently by using your fingers while rinsing, otherwise they will become mushy at the time of cooking.
1. Once washed set aside the poha.
1. Stir in the turmeric powder, peanuts, salt and sugar.
1. Stir to combine and finally add rinsed poha.
1. Give the poha a gentle stir until all the ingredients come together.
1. Turn the heat to low and cover the pan.
1. Allow the mixed vegetable poha to cook in the steam along with the vegetables.
1. This will take about 3 to 4 minutes.
1. After 3 to 4 minutes, you will notice the poha has puffed up slightly.
1. At this stage turn off the heat.
1. Stir in the juice from one lemon and the coriander leaves.
1. Check the salt and seasonings and adjust to suit your taste and serve.
1. Serve the Mixed Vegetable Poha along with Bhujia, Fruits and a hot cup of Adrak Chai for a wholesome breakfast.
