### Ingredients

- 2 Spring Onion Greens - finely chopped.
- 12 Green beans (French Beans) - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - finely chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 3 Whole Eggs.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 cups Brown Rice - cooked.
- 80 grams Rolled Oats Or Instant Oats.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chinese Egg Fried Rice Recipe With Oats, in a wok, add a teaspoon of oil, spring onions, carrots, beans, oats and cabbage.
1. Sprinkle some salt and stir-fry on medium heat until the vegetables are softened and cooked.
1. You can optionally cover the pan and cook the vegetables faster.
1. Covering the pan and lowering the heat, also helps the vegetables to sweat a little and the oats will get cooked along with it.
1. Once the vegetables are cooked, add the 3 eggs along with the yolks and stir fry to make it a scramble.
1. Once the eggs are scrambled, add the cooked rice and give it a stir.
1. Add the salt and black pepper to taste.
1. Stir well to combine and turn off the heat.
1. Cover the pan and allow the flavors to get into the cooked rice,once done, serve.
1. Serve the Chinese Egg Fried Rice Recipe with Oats along with your favorite Sweet and Sour Asian Paneer Curry.
