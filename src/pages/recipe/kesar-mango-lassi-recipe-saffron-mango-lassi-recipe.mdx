### Ingredients

- Ice cubes - a few.
- 3 tablespoons Milk - luke warm+more cold milk as needed.
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - (low fat).
- 3/4 cup Mango Pulp (Puree).
- 2 pinch Saffron strands.
- 1 tablespoon Sugar - or honey.
- 3 tablespoons Badam (Almond) - roughly chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kesar Mango Lassi Recipe, soak one pinch kesar in 2 tablespoons warm milk and keep it aside for few minutes.
1. Get prep with other ingredients as well.
1. Take out the pulp from the mangoes and keep aside.
1. In a blender, add mango pulp, curd/yogurt, cold milk and blend till combined.
1. Once it is done, add chopped almonds (2 tablespoons), honey or sugar, some ice cubes and blend to make the Kesar Mango Lassi Recipe.
1. Kesar Mango Lassi Recipe is ready to serve, garnish it with the rest of chopped almonds and saffron and serve with Bhel Puri Recipe or Asian Watermelon Salad Recipe during evening.
