### Ingredients

- 3 Dates - deseeded and cubed for garnish.
- 1/4 cup Jaggery - grated.
- 2 cups Milk - or coconut milk for vegan option.
- 1 tablespoon Cashew nuts.
- 1 cup Dates - deseeded and finely chopped.
- 1/2 tablespoon Pistachios - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1/2 tablespoon Badam (Almond) - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Karnataka Style Utthatthi Payasa Recipe, deseed the dates and chop them into small pieces.
1. In a mixer-jar, add dates, a cup of milk and grind them into a fine paste.
1. Mix the ground date paste with the remaining cup of milk.
1. Add cardamom powder and combine them well.
1. Heat this payasa on medium flame and bring it to a brisk boil.
1. Once it starts boiling, switch off the flame.
1. In a small pan, add ghee and heat it over a medium flame.
1. Add cashews and fry until they turn golden.
1. Season uttatthi payasa recipe with fried cashews and chopped pistachios and almonds.
1. Serve Karnataka Style Utthatthi Payasa Recipe for dessert after meal of Keerai Sambar, Steamed Rice and Chow Chow Thoran or serve it as part of a festive meal.
