### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 250 grams Green beans (French Beans).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Green Beans Fry Recipe, clean the beans and chop them finely.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan and add hing and cumin seeds.
1. Saut√© it for 15 to 20 seconds.
1. After 15 to 20 seconds, add green chillies and beans.
1. Add salt and turmeric powder and let it cook for 3 to 5 minutes.
1. After 3 to 5 minutes, add in the red chilli powder and coriander powder.
1. Cover the lid and cook it till the beans becomes soft and cooked.
1. Keep stirring in between so that the beans don't get burned.
1. Once done, switch off the stove and the Green Beans Fry Recipe is ready to serve with boiled/steamed rice and Mixed Vegetable Sambar to make a wholesome meal.
