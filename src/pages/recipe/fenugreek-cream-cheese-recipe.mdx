### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- 1 tablespoon Butter - or ghee/oil.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- Salt - to taste.
- 100 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - diced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 cup Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves) - chopped.
- 10 Cashew nuts - soaked in water.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 Onions - sliced.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoon Fresh cream.
- 2 Tomatoes.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Methi Malai Paneer Recipe, soak cashews in warm water for 15 to 20 minutes.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add fenugreek leaves with little salt and cook till the raw smell evaporates.
1. To the same heavy bottomed pan, add 1 teaspoon of oil and add sliced onion, ginger, garlic and green chillies.
1. Saute till raw smell goes.
1. Add diced tomatoes and salt.
1. Saute till tomatoes are mushy.
1. Once onion tomato mixture has cooled, grind it very smooth along with soaked cashews.
1. Our masala for methi malai paneer is ready.
1. Add remaining oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add bay leaf, cumin seeds and let it cook for about 15 seconds.
1. After 15 seconds, add ground onion tomato paste and cook for about 2 to 4 minutes.
1. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, kasuri methi and salt.
1. Saute well.
1. Add sauteed methi leaves, diced paneer, milk and required water to get the desired consistency.
1. Mix everything well and keep it covered.
1. Stir in between.
1. When the curry is boiling well, add malai and garam masala powder and let it cook for another 1 minute.
1. Mix Methi Malai Paneer and switch off.
1. Serve Methi Malai Paneer Recipe along with Whole Wheat Lachha Paratha, Burani Raita and Kachumber Salad for a weekday meal.
