### Ingredients

- 2 cloves Garlic.
- 200 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cut into cubes.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 2 to 3 teaspoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil - to grill.
- 7 to 8 Walnuts.
- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese - grated.
- 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 cup Basil leaves - tightly packed.
- 2 tablespoons Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Grilled Pesto Paneer Recipe, make a fine paste of all the ingredients listed in the pesto sauce table using a mixer grinder and keep aside.
1. In a big bowl, add the paneer cubes, curd and 3 to 4 table spoon on the pesto sauce mix and massage it nicely with hand.
1. Let the paneer cubes marinate in this mix for an hour.
1. Now heat a grill pan on medium heat, brush it with olive oil and place the paneer cubes gently onto the grill pan.
1. Alternatively, you can also add the paneer cubes to a skewer and grill it.
1. Cook until the paneer well done on one side.
1. This will take 2-3 minutes.
1. Brush the paneer cubes, flip it over and cook on the other side for another 2 to 3 more minutes.
1. Serve this flavourful Grilled Pesto Paneer as a starter for your next party you throw at home along with Sichuan Style Chilli Potato Recipe and Paprika Chicken Skewers Recipe.
