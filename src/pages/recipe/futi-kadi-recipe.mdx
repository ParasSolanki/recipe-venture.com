### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 10 Kokum (Malabar Tamarind).
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 2 cups Lukewarm Water.
- 1/4 inch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 Green Chilli - slit and chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Futi Kadi recipe, take the kokum petals in a plate and sprinkle 1/4 teaspoon salt on it.
1. Now soak these kokum petals in warm water for 30 minutes in a mixing bowl.
1. After 30 minutes, add the other ingredients and stir well.
1. Serve the Futi Kadi with Steamed Rice and Kachumber Salad or simply drink it at the end of a hearty Goan meal.
