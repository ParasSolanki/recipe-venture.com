### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Ginger Juice.
- 30 ml Apple cider vinegar.
- 2 teaspoons Sugar.
- 60 ml Apple Vodka.
- 30 ml Orange liquor.
- 1 Apple - sliced.
- 6 Mint Leaves (Pudina) - plus more for garnish.
- Ice cubes - as needed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Apple And Ginger Cocktail, place the ice cubes into the cocktail shaker.
1. Pour the vodka, orange liqueur, lemon juice and apple cider over it.
1. Add the sugar, ginger juice and crushed mint leaves to the cocktail shaker.
1. Cover the cocktail shaker and shake vigorously until mixed well.
1. Strain the Apple And Ginger Cocktail into a martini glass, garnish with apple slices.
1. Serve Apple And Ginger Cocktail with an Indo-Chinese or Asian meal.
