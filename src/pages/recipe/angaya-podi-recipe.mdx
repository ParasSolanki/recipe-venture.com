### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 inch Dry ginger powder.
- 1/2 Dry Red Chilli.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 tablespoon Turkey Berries.
- 1 tablespoon Manathakkali Kai Vathal.
- 1 tablespoon Dried Neem Flowers.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 Arisithipli - (optional).
- 1 Kandathipli.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Angaya Podi Recipe, in a heavy bottomed kadai dry roast the ingredients above individually (except salt and hing powder).
1. Take them out on a large plate as the ingredients are roasted one after the other.
1. Once the ingredients are cooled to room temperature, powder all the ingredients in a mortar pestle or mixer along with hing powder and salt to taste.
1. Serve Angaya Podi Recipe alongside Thalicha Thayir Sadam Recipe (Spicy Seasoned Curd Rice) for a simple lunch or with Carrot Onion Uttapam Recipe for breakfast.
