### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Sweet corn - boiled.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 4 Tomatoes - ripe.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Corn Tamatar Bharta Recipe, get prepped with all the ingredients.
1. Heat a Kadai/Wok in medium flame, add butter and oil, once butter is melted add cumin seeds and let it splutter.
1. Add the finely chopped onion and cook till it turns translucent.
1. Add the finely chopped tomatoes and green chilli and cook until the tomatoes are well cooked and squishy.
1. Next, add the dry spice powders - turmeric, coriander, and garam masala powder, and saut√© until the butter leaves the sides.
1. Now, add the boiled sweet corn and season with salt.
1. At this stage, you can add a little water to the pan depending on the consistency you like for the bharta.
1. Add the finely chopped coriander leaves and simmer for 2 minutes.
1. Switch off the flame, add the lemon juice and mix well.
1. Serve the Corn Tamatar Bharta Recipewith light Phulkas for dinner along with Chanar Dalna Recipe (Bengali Style Paneer & Potato Gravy) and steamed rice to make it a complete meal.
