### Ingredients

- 1 Mango (Raw) - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 2 teaspoons Sambar Powder.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Raw Mango Sambar Recipe, we will firstly pressure cook the dal.
1. Add the dal with required water and turmeric powder in the pressure cooker.
1. Close the cooker with the lid and let it cook till it releases 5 whistles or till it is completely soft and cooked.
1. Heat a saucepan with oil, add dry red chillies, mustard seeds, urad dal, cumin seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Add ginger, garlic and let it cook for about a minute.
1. Once that is done, add onions and saute till they turn soft and translucent.
1. This will take around 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Add turmeric powder, raw mango, 1 cup of water and bring it to a boil.
1. Boil until the mangoes become soft.
1. Once that is done, add sambar powder, jaggery and cooked dal and give it a stir.
1. Check for salt and keep stirring the raw mango sambar until it comes to a brisk boil.
1. The next step is to prepare the tempering for raw mango sambar.
1. Heat a small tadka pan, add dry red chillies, asafoetida, mustard seeds, curry leaves, urad dal, cumin seeds and let it cook for about 30 seconds.
1. Once it is done, pour it over the Raw Mango Sambar, give it a mix and serve hot.
1. Serve the Raw Mango Sambar Recipe along with Steamed Rice, Cabbage Poriyal and Elai Vadam for lunch or you can simple serve it with Homemade Soft Idlis and Coconut Chutney for your morning breakfast.
