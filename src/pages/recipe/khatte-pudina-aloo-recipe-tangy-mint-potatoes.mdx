### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste or sendha namak.
- 2 Green Chillies.
- 5 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled.
- Ghee - or cooking oil as required.
- 1/3 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/4 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Khatte Pudina Aloo Recipe, wash and boil some potatoes in water until al dente.
1. Peel off their skin and cut them into cubes, set aside.
1. You can also use baby potatoes for this.
1. In a blender, add some washed coriander and mint leaves separated from their stems and also green chilies.
1. Blitz them and make a smooth puree.
1. Set aside.
1. In a pan or wok add some ghee or clarified butter.
1. The vegans can substitute it with cooking oil.
1. Add cumin seeds and wait for them to sputter.
1. Add the mint coriander puree and cook for a minute until the rawness fades away.
1. This will take about 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Once done, add in the red chilli powder, turmeric powder, amchur powder and salt to taste.
1. Mix well and let it cook for about a minute.
1. After a minute, add the min puree, mix well and cook for about 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes, add in the cubed boiled potatoes and toss them in the masala.
1. Add a little water and keep it on simmer till the potatoes absorb all the spices and the liquid evaporate.
1. Serve hot and enjoy the Khatte Pudina Aloo.
1. Serve Khatte Pudina Aloo Recipe along with Panchmel Dal and Phulkas for a weekday meal.
