### Ingredients

- 1 cup Corn meal - coarse.
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoon Butter.
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil - or butter.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 teaspoon All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/2 cup Vegetable stock.
- 3 cups Water - (or 4 if you want it thinner).
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely minced.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- Parmesan cheese - grated or shaved.
- Parsley leaves - Handful.
- 16 Button mushrooms - sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Creamy Polenta with Mushrooms, first bring water and milk to a boil in a large saucepan.
1. Once it is boiled, add bay leaf, salt and pepper.
1. Once the mixture starts boiling, gradually add the corn meal (in a stream) stirring constantly.
1. Keep stirring/whisking till the mixture begins to thicken.
1. Reduce the heat and cook till it is thick, stirring frequently for about 35-40 minutes.
1. Add a little water if required to make it thin and whisk.
1. Stir in the butter and whisk again.
1. The next step is to make the mushrooms.
1. Heat oil in a pan and add onion and garlic.
1. Saute it for about 1-2 minutes.
1. After 1-2 minutes, add the flour and cook for a few seconds.
1. Later, add the mushrooms and stir.
1. Add salt, pepper and saute for 2-3 minutes.
1. Add the stock and cook till the sauce thickens a bit.
1. Take off the heat, add parsley and mix and your dish is ready to be served.
1. To serve, take a serving dish.
1. Ladle the hot creamy Polenta and serve the mushrooms over the Polenta, garnished with Parmesan cheese.
1. Serve Creamy Polenta With Mushrooms with Fresh Tomato Basil Pasta with Spinach for a perfect weekend dinner.
