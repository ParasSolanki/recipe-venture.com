### Ingredients

- 4 Mini Pita Breads - sliced into half circles from the center.
- 1 tablespoon Tahini.
- 11/2 cup Cheddar cheese - grated.
- 1 Green zucchini - sliced into rounds.
- 2 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.
- 1 cup Tomato Basil Pasta Sauce.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mini Pita Pizza With Tahini Roasted Vegetables, heat 1 teaspoon of oil.
1. Add onion, green zucchini, red bell pepper, sprinkle some salt and roast until the vegetables are soft.
1. Once done, toss in the tahini sauce, cook for another 1 minute and keep aside.
1. Preheat the oven to 200 C.
1. Place the sliced pita bread on a baking sheet, spoon the tomato basil sauce on each pita slice, spoon the roasted vegetables over the pita and sprinkle the cheddar cheese.
1. Bake in the oven until the cheese melts and the pita bread is lightly crisp and golden.
1. Take it out of the oven and serve hot.
1. Serve the Mini Pita Pizza With Tahini Roasted Vegetables Recipe as a party starter along with other starters like Mustard Mayo Baby Potatoes and Peri Peri Fish Fingers.
