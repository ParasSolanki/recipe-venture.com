### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - soaked in hot water for 30 minutes.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Andhra Style Patoli Recipe grind the soaked chana dal with green chilli in a mixer, add little water and grind it to a thick paste.
1. Heat a kadai with oil, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, urad dal and splutter for few seconds.
1. Add curry leaves and leave it to crackle for 2 seconds.
1. Add the chopped onions, ginger garlic paste and saute till the onions turn translucent.
1. Add the ground chana paste and keep mixing it.
1. Add turmeric powder and red chilli powder and by using a wooden spatula try to separate the dal and create a crumbly texture.
1. Add salt and check for seasoning.
1. Serve the Andhra Style Patoli Recipe with Mamsaam Chops Recipe, phulka and Steamed rice by the side.
