### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- 1/4 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1 Green Chilli.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 3/4 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- Chaat Masala Powder - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1 cup Garlic - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish) - grated.
- 1/2 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Ram Ladoo Recipe, soak the moong dal and chana dal separately in enough water for 2-3 hours.
1. Grind chana dal, green chilli and ginger in a mixer-grinder to smooth paste without adding water.
1. Similarly, grind split moong dal in a mixer-grinder to smooth paste.
1. Combine both the paste along with salt and chopped coriander leaves.
1. Keep it aside.
1. The next step is to prepare the green chutney.
1. Grind all the ingredients mentioned under ‘For chutney’ to a smooth paste adding a little water.
1. Transfer it to a bowl and keep aside.
1. To prepare Ram Ladoo recipe, heat Paniyaram pan on low-medium flame.
1. Pour 1 teaspoon oil into each cavity of the pan.
1. After this put 1 tablespoon of batter into each cavity till it is half filled.
1. Close the pan with a lid and cook on low-medium flame.
1. After 2 or 3 minutes, open the lid and turn over each ladoo to allow it to cook.
1. Once each ladoo is cooked, remove from the pan and pour another batch of batter.
1. At the time of serving, assemble ram ladoo in a plate or bowl.
1. Pour green chutney and garnish with grated radish and sprinkle some chat masala or black salt.
1. Serve Ram Ladoo with hot Masala Chai as a tea time snack with family and friends.
1. Serve ram laddos as an appetizer to your party meal made up of  Whole Wheat Palak Naan, Creamy Dal Makhani and Boondi Raita.
