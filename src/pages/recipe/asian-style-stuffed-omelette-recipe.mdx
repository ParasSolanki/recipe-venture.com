### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 1/2 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 2 sprig Spring Onion Greens - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Chilli vinegar.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 3 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1/2 Green bell peppers - sliced.
- 1/2 cup Baby corn - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Green Chilli Sauce.
- 2 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Asian Style Stuffed Omelet Recipe, add eggs into a bowl and mix with with salt, pepper and spring onions.
1. Give a good whisk till it fluffs up.
1. Heat a flat skillet with oil, add ginger and garlic and saute till it softens.
1. Add baby corn and cook till it is done, which will take about 20 minutes.
1. Add bell peppers and saute for 10 more minutes.
1. Add all the sauces along chilli vinegar and stir fry.
1. Check for Salt and add if needed.
1. Take the stir fried vegetables out of the pan and keep it aside.
1. Heat oil over a medium heat and coat the surface of the pan with a few drops of oil (not required when using a good non stick pan).
1. Pour the egg mixture and spread it well with ladle.
1. Once it starts getting cooked, after about 30 seconds on medium flame, sprinkle the Vegetable stuffing over the egg omletteFold the egg with spatula from the sides and transfer it to a plate by bringing it to the edge of the pan.
1. You can also cook the omelette on both sides and them go with putting cheese and rice, if you prefer.
1. Serve the Asian Style Stuffed Omelette Recipe along with a smoothie or a glass of juice to kick start your day with good meal.
