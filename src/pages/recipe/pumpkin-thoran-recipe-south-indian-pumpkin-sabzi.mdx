### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 500 grams Kaddu (Parangikai/ Pumpkin) - diced.
- 15 Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - finely chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pumpkin Thoran Recipe, prepare all the ingredients and keep them ready.
1. Into a mixer grinder, make a paste of the coconut, curry leaves, cumin seeds and green chillies.
1. You don't need to add any water to grind to a paste.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Once the oil is hot, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
1. Immediately, add curry leaves and stir for a couple of seconds.
1. Add finely chopped onions and saute the onions until they soften.
1. Once the onions soften, add the slit green chillies.
1. Once the onions turn pink, add the cut pumpkin, turmeric powder, salt and stir.
1.  Sprinkle some water and cover the pan.
1. Cook the pumpkin until it is soft and tender.
1. Stir in between, as required.
1. Once the pumpkin is cooked, add ground coconut mixture and toss for a few seconds on high heat and turn off the heat.
1. Check the taste of the Pumpkin Thoran and adjust the seasonings accordingly.
1. Once done, transfer the Pumpkin Thoran to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Pumpkin Thoran with Cherupayar Curry, Steamed Rice and Moru Kachiyathu for a weekend lunch or dinner.
