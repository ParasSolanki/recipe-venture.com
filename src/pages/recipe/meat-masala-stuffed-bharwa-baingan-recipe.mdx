### Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons Meat masala.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 3 tablespoons Mustard oil.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste - (optional).
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 250 grams Brinjal (Baingan / Eggplant).
- 1 Tomato - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Meat Masala Stuffed Bharwa Baingan recipe, hold the tip and make two opposite slits.
1. Add salt to the meat masala and mix well.
1. With a teaspoon stuff baingan with meat masala.
1. Heat mustard oil till smoking.
1. Now add hing and jeera.
1. Also add the onion.
1. Cook till the onions are soft.
1. Add prepared baingan and roast for one minutes.
1. Add tomatoes and any remaining meat masala stuffing.
1. Add 1/2 cup water and cook till baingan is done.
1. Serve Meat Masala Stuffed Bharwa Baingan with Dal Tadka and Tawa Parathas for a weekday meal.
