### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon Ginger - paste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 teaspoon Chana Masala Powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - soaked overnight and boiled.
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 2 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sukhe Chole Recipe, cook the Chole Chana in a pressure cooker with salt and water for 3 whistles until they are soft.
1. Drain and keep aside.
1. Heat the oil in a wok/kadai, add the cumin seeds.
1. When the seeds crackle, add ginger paste and cook for seconds.
1. Add the chole masala, red chilli powder, amchur powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin seed powder and salt and saute for about a minute.
1. After a minute, add the chana and mix well.
1. Cook Sukhe Chole for another 2 minutes.
1. Once done, switch off the gas and add chopped coriander leaves.
1. Garnish Sukhe Chole with some ginger julienne.
1. Serve Sukhe Chole Recipe during your fasting day along with Rajgira Puri.
1. You can also have it as a snack with your cup of Masala Tea.
