### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 tablespoon Fresh coconut.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1/2 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 tablespoon Mustard seeds.
- 5 Dry Red Chillies - (adjust to taste based on pungency).
- 5 Curry leaves.
- 2 tablespoon Salt.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 10 cloves Garlic.
- 2 cups Buttermilk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Karnataka Style Majjige Huli Recipe, keep all the ingredients ready.
1. Whisk buttermilk in a mixing bowl for a few seconds until smoothIn a mixer, grind together red chillies, ginger, half of the garlic, coriander powder, pepper powder, cumin seeds, and grated coconut with a little water.
1. Grind into a fine paste.
1. Add this paste into the whisked buttermilk and keep aside.
1. Now take a tadka pan.
1. Heat a bit of cooking oil for tadka.
1. Once the oil is hot, add mustard seeds and wait for a few seconds to splutter.
1. Immediately add two red chillies, remaining garlic cloves, and saute a bit till garlic cloves are fried.
1. Add curry leaves, and let them splutter.
1. Add in the sliced onions and stir well till the onions are golden brown and caramelized.
1. Once onions are caramelized, switch off heat and add the tadka mixture to buttermilk in the mixing bowl and stir well.
1. Add salt to taste and stir in well till dissolved.
1. Do a taste check and adjust.
1. Now the yummy Karnataka Style Majjige Huli Recipe (Tasty Buttermilk Rasam) is ready to be served with Harive Soppina Saaru Recipe, Instant Carrot Pickle Recipe, steamed rice and Akki Peni Sandige (Rice idiyappam Fryums) for a hearty lunch.
