### Ingredients

- 1 Red Radish - diced.
- 2 Tomatoes - diced.
- Lemon juice - from one lemon.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 Cucumber - peeled and diced.
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cucumber, Red Radish, Tomatoes & Onion Salad Recipe, first get all the ingredients chopped.
1. In a large mixing bowl, add all the vegetables- cucumber, radish, tomatoes, onions, and add salt, pepper, green chilies, lemon juice and coriander.
1. Toss the salad well and serve it fresh.
1. Serve the Cucumber, Red Radish, Tomatoes & Onion Salad along with Golden Glow Smoothie Recipe for a simple evening snack during the summer season.
