### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Tamarind - Marble size ball.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Coconut Oil.
- 15 Ash Gourd Chips.
- 3 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1/4 Cup Fresh coconut - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vadyancho Ross recipe, On a medium high heat, fry the ash gourd chips on a frying pan using a teaspoon of oil.
1. Once they are nice and crisp on both the sides, remove them out on a plate and keep aside.
1. Grind together grated coconut, peppercorns, coriander seeds, turmeric powder, dry red chilies and tamarind using very little water.
1. Take the ground paste in a saucepan kept on medium heat and allow to boil.
1. Once it boils, add the fried ash gourd chips and mix.
1. Allow the mixture to boil for 2-3 more minutes.
1. Finally add salt, mix and remove from heat.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve Vadyancho Ross along with Steamed Rice and Suran Fodi for a weekday meal.
