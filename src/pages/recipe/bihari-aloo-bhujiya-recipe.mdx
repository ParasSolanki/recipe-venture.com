### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Green Chillies - slit into halves.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - peeled and cut lengthwise.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Mustard oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bihari Aloo Bhujiya Recipe, wash potatoes with water.
1. Cut them into lengthy pieces similar to the one for finger chips.
1. If you donâ€™t prefer to use the skin, peel it and then chop them into piecesIn a non-stick wok, add oil.
1. Once the oil is hot, add cumin seeds and allow it to splutter.
1. Now, add green chilies and saute it.
1. Stir in the chopped potatoes and turmeric powder.
1. Season it with salt.
1. Reduce the flame.
1. Cook until the potatoes are cooked completely.
1. Towards the end, when the potatoes have almost cooked, open the lid and allow it to become crispy.
1. At this stage you can optionally add more mustard oil and then sprinkle some more salt.
1. Ensure you keep stirring occasionally so it gets cooked from all the sides.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and transfer to as serving bowl.
1. Serve the Bihari Aloo Bhujiya along with Pudina Paratha, Punjabi Methi Kadhi Recipe for a wholesome weeknight dinner.
