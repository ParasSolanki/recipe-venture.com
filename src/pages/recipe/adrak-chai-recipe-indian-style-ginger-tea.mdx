### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoons Loose leaf tea - Red Label or Lipton or Assam teas.
- Sugar - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Milk - low fat.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 2 cups Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Adrak Chai Recipe - Indian Style Ginger Tea, warm milk and keep aside.
1. In a saucepan bring two cups of water and the grated ginger to a boil.
1. Allow it to boil for a few minutes so the water gets flavored with flavours of ginger.
1. Add in the tea leaves and allow the tea mixture to come to a boil and let it simmer for about a minute.
1. Turn off the heat, cover the pan and allow the tea to brew for a few minutes.
1. Pour in the warm milk and give the tea a stir.
1. Using a small strainer filter the adrak chai into individual cups or a teapot.
1. Stir in the sugar to taste.
1. Serve the Adrak Chai along with a Iyengar Bakery Khara Biscuit for a tea time snack.
