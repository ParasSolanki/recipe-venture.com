### Ingredients

-  1 onion - chopped.
-  1/4 tsp asafoetida.
-  1 lemon - take out the juice.
-  1/2 tsp Spoon turmeric powder.
-  1 green chili - finely chopped.
-  coriander - little.
-  1/2 tsp garam masala powder.
- 1 cup white urad dal.
-  1 tsp cumin.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 inch ginger - finely chopped.
-  1/4 cup water.
-  1 tsp ghee.
-  1/2 tsp coriander powder.

### Instructions

1. To make dry urad dal recipe, first wash the urad dal thoroughly with water.
1. Soak the lentils in water for 4 to 5 hours.
1. After 4 to 5 hours, drain the water and keep the lentils separately.
1. Now pour some water in a pressure cooker.
1. Add steamer pan and add dal to it.
1. Turn off the cooker and cook till 3 cities come.
1. Turn off the gas and let the pressure drain.
1. Open the cooker and keep the lentils separately.
1. Now heat ghee in a pan.
1. Add cumin seeds, asafoetida and cook until the cumin seeds crackle.
1. After the cumin seeds crackle, add onion, ginger, green chili and cook till the onion becomes soft.
1. Now add cooked urad dal, turmeric powder, salt, garam masala powder, coriander powder and mix well.
1. Cover the pan and let it cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Now add lemon juice to it and mix.
1. Garnish with green coriander and serve.
1. Serve the dry urad dal recipe with Gujarati kadhi, phulka and amaras for dinner.
