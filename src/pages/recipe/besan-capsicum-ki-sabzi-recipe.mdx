### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Water - as required.
- 1/2 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1/2 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1-1/2 cups Gram flour (besan).
- 2 Onion - sliced.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - as required to garnish.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Besan Capsicum Ki Sabzi recipe, in a deep pan, boil the chopped peppers in 2 cups of water and once tender drain and keep aside.
1. Sieve besan twice so that there are no lumps and keep aside.
1. In a heavy bottom pan, heat oil and add sliced onions and fry till slightly browned.
1. Add dry masalas and capsicum to the same and saute for about 2 minutes.
1. Add besan to this with hand slowly and stir using the other, taking care there are no lumps.
1. Sprinkle some water, cover and cook for about 5 to 6 minutes till the sabji is cooked and darker in colour.
1. Garnish with chopped coriander and it is ready to be served.
1. Serve Besan Capsicum Ki Sabzi with Phulka, Pitor Ki Sabzi and rice for a perfect meal.
