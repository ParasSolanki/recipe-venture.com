### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - diced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 Green peas (Matar).
- 5 cloves Garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 5 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 2 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - diced.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Maharashtrian Style Sheng Sola Recipe (Vegetable Stew), we will toast the sesame seeds on a pan over a medium heat.
1. Allow it cool down and grind it along with other ingredients by adding little water to a smooth paste.
1. Heat a pressure cooker with oil, add mustard seeds and cumin and allow it crackle for 10 seconds.
1. Add the vegetables and turmeric powder saute for about 5 minutes.
1. Add the ground paste along salt and jaggery give it a toss.
1. Add about 1 cup water, check for salt and cover it with the lid.
1. Pressure cook for 2 whistle until the vegetables are cooked.
1. Serve the Maharashtrian Style Sheng Sola Recipe (Vegetable Stew) along with steamed rice or phulka to enjoy your everyday lunch.
