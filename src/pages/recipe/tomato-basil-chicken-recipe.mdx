### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Basil leaves - fresh leaves.
- 2 teaspoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 teaspoons Mixed Herbs (Dried).
- 1 tablespoon Mixed Herbs (Dried).
- 2 Chicken breasts.
- 10 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- Sugar - to taste.
- 1 sprig Basil leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 3 cups Homemade tomato puree.
- 3 tablespoons Lemon juice.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tomato Basil Chicken Recipe, wash and thoroughly clean the chicken breasts.
1. Into the bowl add the chicken, add mixed herbs, lemon juice, olive oil and salt.
1. Mix well.
1. Heat a skillet on medium-high heat to pan sear the chicken pieces.
1. Place the chicken pieces and cook only for a minute on both sides till it gets a light brown colour and is just about cooked.
1. To make the tomato basil sauceHeat a saucepan with olive oil, add the garlic and onions and saute till until the onions soften and till the aroma wafts in the air.
1. Next add the torn basil leaves, tomato puree, herbs, red chilli powder, salt and sugar .
1. Bring the tomato basil sauce to a brisk boil.
1. After a few minutes, add the grilled chicken breasts into the tomato basil sauce.
1. Turn the heat to low, cover the pan and cook the Tomato Basil Chicken for about 10 minutes, until the chicken has absorbed all the flavours and is cooked through as well.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and transfer the Tomato Basil Chicken Recipe to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1.
