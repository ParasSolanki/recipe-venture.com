### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Sugar - powdered.
- 1/4 cup Corn flour.
- 1/3 cup Cocoa Powder - unsweetened.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 2 cup Almond Milk (Badam Milk) - unsweetened.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dairy Free Chocolate Pudding sift cocoa powder.
1. In a bowl combine cocoa powder, powdered sugar, cornstarch and salt together.
1. Now, add the almond milk to the dry powder and stir them well to ensure there are no lumps.
1. Over medium heat, bring the mixture to boil.
1. Whisk them occasionally to avoid sticking to the bottom of the pan and avoid lumps.
1. Once it starts boiling, reduce the flame.
1. Add vanilla extract and stir them well.
1. Let it boil for 3-4 minutes until the mixture thickens.
1. Whisk continuously to prevent lumps.
1. Pour the mixture into bowls.
1. Allow the mixture to cool down at least for about two hours.
1. Cover the pudding with foil to prevent the formation of the layer on the top.
1. Ensure there are no air bubbles.
1. Once the mixture cools down, enjoy the Dairy Free Chocolate Pudding with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
1. Serve Dairy Free Chocolate Pudding as dessert after a meal of Moroccan Chicken Burgers With Feta And Carrot Recipe and Zucchini Parmesan Fritters Recipe.
