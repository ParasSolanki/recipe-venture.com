### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Curry leaves - as required.
- 1 tablespoon Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- 1 cup Dried Yellow/White Peas (Vatana) - soaked for 4 hours.
- Black Salt (Kala Namak) - as required.
- 1 Onion - chopped for serving.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera) - roasted.
- 2 tablespoons Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - roasted or fried.
- Salt - per taste.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1/2 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar - powdered.
- 1/8 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1 teaspoon Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1 tablespoon Sweet Chutney (Date & Tamarind).
- 2 teaspoons Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Sunflower Oil - for frying.
- 3 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).

### Instructions

1. To begin with Pakodi Chaat recipe, firstly soak white peas for about 4 hours.
1. Add turmeric and salt and pressure cook up to 3 whistles until those are too soft.
1. Mash peas with back side of a spatula and add black pepper powder, coriander powder, cumin powder and cook with 1/4 cup of water for 2 minutes.
1. In the meantime fry pakodi.
1. Make a thick batter of gram-flour, salt, curry leaves, chopped onion, baking soda with 4 to 5 tablespoons of water.
1. Heat oil, drop the batter in the oil to form small fritters.
1. Reduce heat to medium and deep fry till they turn golden brown in a deep fry pan.
1. Whisk the curd and add sugar powder in it.
1. Mix it properly.
1. Take two plates.
1. Spread spiced mashed peas on it.
1. Add pakodis to it.
1. Top it with curd, dhaniya pudina chutney and date tamarind chutney as per your taste.
1. Serve  the Pakodi Chaat on its own with a hot cup of Masala Chai for a evening snack or serve along with Homemade Canape Papdi Chaat  and Moong Sprouts Usal  for your next chaat party.
