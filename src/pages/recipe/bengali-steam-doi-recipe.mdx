### Ingredients

- 1 cup Milk.
- 400 grams Condensed Milk.
- Strawberries - few.
- 1-1/2 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- Raisins - few.
- 4 tablespoons Pistachios - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Bhapa Doi, firstly in a large pan whisk hung curd and condensed milk together with an electric hand blender till it is smooth and creamy.
1. Add milk slowly and whisk again till you see the froth appearing.
1. Add cardamom powder and handful of raisins and give it a quick stir again.
1. Preheat the oven at 180 degree C and meanwhile, take 6 ramekins or earthen ware or any oven safe bowl in which you want to bake the curd.
1. You can also bake it in a large baking tray and later cut into pieces.
1. Pour in the curd mix into the ramekins till the rim.
1. In a baking tray or a drip tray of the oven, place the filled ramekins and pour water in the tray till the ramekins are half immersed.
1. Place the tray in the oven and bake for 40-45 mins or till the toothpick inserted comes out clean.
1. The baking tie may vary from oven to oven.
1. Take out the tray and let it cool.
1. Once the ramekins are cool completely, garnish with chopped pistachios, strawberries or any fruit or nuts.
1. Refrigerate the Bhapa Doi for minimum 2 hours before use and serve it chilled.
1. Serve Bhapa Doi to your friends and family during Holi with some snacks like Baked Paneer Bread Pakora and Jhure-Jhure Aloo Bhaja.
