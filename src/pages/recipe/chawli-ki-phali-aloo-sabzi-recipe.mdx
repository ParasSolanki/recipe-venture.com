### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Sunflower Oil.
- 3 teaspoons Gram flour (besan).
- 21/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Salt - to taste.
- 300 grams Green Chawli Beans (Yard long beans/Karamani/Barbati) - cut into 2 inch long.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - peeled cut into 2 inch long.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chawli Ki Phali & Aloo Sabzi Recipe, pressure cook the yard beans with little bit of water and salt for about 1 whistle and keep it aside.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally and strain the water.
1. Heat a skillet with oil, add fennel seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Add potatoes and saute them till they crisp up and is cooked.
1. You can cover it with the lid and cook for about 5 minutes.
1. Add cooked yard beans along with all the spice powders and give it a mix keep cooking till the masala loses the raw smell.
1. Add besan and salt and mix Chawli Ki Phali & Aloo Sabzi well, saute for another 5 minutes and switch off the heat.
1. Serve the Chawli Ki Phali & Aloo Sabzi Recipe along with Phulka or tawa paratha, kadhi to make a complete meal.
