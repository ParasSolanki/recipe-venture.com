### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1/4 cup Green beans (French Beans) - cut into one-inch pieces.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - cut into cubes.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - cut into inch long pieces.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 cup Cauliflower (gobi) - broken into small florets.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kaigari Pirattal (Chettinad Style Mixed Vegetables) Recipe, heat a wok in medium flame and pour oil in it.
1. Now, put the mustard seeds, curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. Now, add all the cut vegetables into the tempering and give it a stir.
1. Sprinkle some water over it and cook with the lid on for five more minutes.
1. Keep stirring in between till the vegetables are fully cooked.
1. Now make a coarse mixture of grated coconut, red chilies, cumin and fennel seeds using a mixer grinder.
1. Add this spice mix to the vegetables, season with salt, and give it a good stir and switch off the flame.
1. Serve this Kaigari Pirattal (Chettinad Style Mixed Vegetables) with steamed rice and spicy Manathakkali Kai Vathal Kuzhambu.
