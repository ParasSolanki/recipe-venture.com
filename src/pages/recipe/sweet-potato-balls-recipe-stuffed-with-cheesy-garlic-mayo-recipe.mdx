### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 4 sprigs Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 3 tablespoon Corn flour.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - coarsely pounded.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- 1/4 cup Del Monte Cheesy Garlic Mayo.
- 3 Sweet Potatoes - large one.
- 1/4 cup Rice flour.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Sweet Potato Balls Recipe Stuffed With Cheesy Garlic Mayo, prep all the ingredients according to the ingredients.
1. In a large mixing bowl add the grated sweet potatoes, coriander leaves, salt, chilli powder, cumin powder, amchur powder and rice flour.
1. Divide the Sweet Potato mixture into 8 to 10 portions and keep aside.
1. In another bowl mix the rest of the corn flour, salt and black pepper.
1. Add some water to make a slurry (neither too thick nor too thin) to dip and coat the balls.
1. Place the breadcrumbs on a flat plate and the Del Monte Cheesy garlic Mayo in a piping bag or a plastic bag.
1. This makes it easy to fill the center of the balls.
1. Make a hole in the balls with your finger.
1. Fill it with the Del Monte Cheesy garlic Mayo (about 1/2 teaspoon), leaving some space at the top.
1. Carefully cover and seal the top.
1. Dip each ball in the corn flour-water mixture.
1. Shake off excess mixture.
1. Roll each sweet potato balls in the breadcrumbs.
1. Again shake off excess breadcrumbs.
1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat and pan fry the sweet potato balls in batches till they turn golden brown.
1. Take the sweet potato balls out of the pan with a slotted spoon.
1. Drain on absorbent paper.
1. Serve the Sweet Potato Balls Recipe Stuffed With Cheesy Garlic Mayo hot with roasted tomato sauce.
1. or chutney of choice.
