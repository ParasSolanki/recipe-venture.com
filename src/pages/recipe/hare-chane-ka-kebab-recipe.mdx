### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3 tablespoons Spinach - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 tablespoon Mango (Raw) - grated.
- 1/4 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 2 tablespoons Gram flour (besan).
- 1 cup Hara Chana - soaked overnight in water.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Hare Chane Ka Kebab, pressure cook green channa with enough water till the cooker releases 4-5 whistles.
1. Remove from the heat and drain the water completely.
1. Reserve the water for later use.
1. You can use the water to prepare any curry.
1. Combine all the ingredients except besan and blend together with cooked channa to coarse paste.
1. Add a little water if needed.
1. Transfer the paste to a bowl.
1. Add 1 or 2 tablespoons besan and knead to smooth dough.
1. Close it and keep aside for an hour.
1. You can either deep fry, shallow fry or bake these kebab.
1. Make small patties of the dough and shallow fry them in a wide pan with little oil.
1. Cook till it turns golden brown on both the sides.
1. Serve Hare Chane Ka Kebab with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney or Lahsun Ki Chutney for your tea time break.
