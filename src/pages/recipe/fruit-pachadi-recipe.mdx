### Ingredients

- Water - as required.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 cups Mixed fruits - cubed to bite size (I used pineapple.
- 1 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - whisked.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 2 Green Chillies - roughly chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Dry Red Chilli - broken.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Fruit Pachadi recipe, take a paste of coconut, green chillies and mustard seeds in a blender using a little water.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat a pan and add chopped fruits and two tablespoons of water.
1. Cook on simmer for about 5 minutes or until fruits are softened.
1. Add turmeric powder, salt and sugar or jaggery.
1. Stir and add the coconut paste.
1. Cook for a few minutes more until the water is absorbed.
1. Remove fruit pachadi from heat and add whisked yogurt.
1. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds, dry red chillies and curry leaves.
1. Saute for a minute.
1. Pour the tempering over the fruit pachadi.
1. Mix to combine.
1. Refrigerate until served.
1. Serve Fruit Pachadi Recipe with Fried Samvat Rice recipe or with Amritsari Ajwaini Paneer Recipe with hot Phulkas.
