### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 cups Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 1/2 teaspoon Black Salt (Kala Namak).
- 3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Carrot Tadka Raita Recipe, in a mixing bowl, combine yogurt, black salt, cumin powder, salt to taste and whisk well until smooth.
1. Once done add coriander leaves, mint leaves, green chillies and grated carrots to the whisked curd.
1. Mix well to combine and keep it aside.
1. To make the tadka, heat oil in a pan on medium heat, add the cumin seeds, asafoetida and chopped garlic, and saute until the garlic is golden brown in colour.
1. Turn off the heat once done.
1. Pour the tadka over the carrot raita and serve chilled.
1. Serve Carrot Tadka Raita along with Stuffed Mooli Paratha Recipe or Baby Corn and Tomato Mixed Rice.
