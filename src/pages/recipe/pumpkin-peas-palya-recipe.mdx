### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar) - cooked.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1-1/2 cup Kaddu (Parangikai/ Pumpkin) - cubed.
- 1 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- Salt - as per taste.
- 1 tablespoon Coconut Oil.
- 2 cups Water.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- 5 Curry leaves.
- 2 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Pumpkin Peas Palya Recipe, take a vessel and boil water.
1. Now add pumpkin cubes and cook for 5 to 10 minutes or until they are half cooked.
1. Strain water and keep this aside.
1. Heat a heavy wok, add oil.
1. Now add mustard seeds and saute well until it splutters.
1. Now add chana dal, fry until they turn golden.
1. Add curry leaves, fry for few seconds.
1. Now add the cooked pumpkin cubes and cooked peas.
1. Add amchur powder, garam masala and red chili powder, mix well.
1. Let it cook for about a minute.
1. Now add salt, grated coconut, and mix.
1. Close and cook for 3-5 minutes.
1. Serve Pumpkin Peas Palya along with Tomato Onion Sambar and Steamed Rice for a weekday meal.
1. You can also serve Curry Leaves Buttermilk with this meal.
