### Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons Classic Mayonnaise (With Egg) - (preferably tandoori mayonnaise.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - fine chopped.
- 4 tablespoons Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- 500 grams Chicken - boneless.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Butter - to baste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 2 tablespoons Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 2 teaspoons Lemon juice.
- 2 teaspoons Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 4 tablespoons Mixed vegetables - (I used Cabbage & Onion) thin sliced and drenched in vinegar.
- 2 Ciabatta bread - cut into 1/2 inch thick ovals.

### Instructions

1. For Marinade: To prepare Tandoori Chicken Crostini Recipe, first prepare the marinade.
1. In a large mixing bowl mix Kashmiri red chili powder, lemon juice, yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, salt and mustard oil to make a paste.
1. Add chicken pieces and mix well with the marinade such that everything incorporates well.
1. Cover and refrigerate for an hour.
1. Thread the chicken onto a skewer and cook in a moderately hot tandoor or in a preheated oven at 200Â°C for 12-15 minutes, or until almost done.
1. Baste chicken with a little butter and cook for another 4-5 minutes.
1. Remove, sprinkle some chaat masala.
1. Fine chop tandoori chicken pieces with a sharp knife and collect in a bowl.
1. For Crostini: Toast or Grill Ciabatta Bread slices, until crisp and keep aside.
1. Spread tandoori mayo over a bread, top with some vinegar soaked cabbage and onion slices, chopped tandoori chicken, drizzle mint and coriander dip generously, sprinkle some chopped green chilies, few drops of lemon juice and garnish with chopped coriander.
1. Similarly make many more Tandoori Chicken Crostini Recipe and serve hot with Spinach Corn Pasta in Whole Wheat White Sauce and a salad.
