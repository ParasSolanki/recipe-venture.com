### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - boiled.
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped for garnish.
- Salt - for taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 3 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - cut in juliennes (long thin strips) for garnish.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 2 teaspoons Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chana Aur Aloo Ki Sookhi Sabzi, soak the chickpea or chana over night and pressure cook the soaked chana in enough water for 3 whistles and keep aside.
1. Similarly, wash and boil the potatoes with 1 cup water in a pressure cooker for 2 whistles; peal the skin, cut into cubes and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan on medium heat, add the cumin seeds and let it splutter.
1. Add the finely chopped onions and saute until it turns transparent.
1. Add the ginger and garlic paste and cook until the raw smell goes away.
1. Add the chopped tomatoes and green chilli and saute until the tomatoes are soft and well cooked.
1. Now add the red chilli, turmeric, coriander and garam masala powder and sauce until the spices are cooked.
1. Add the cooked chickpeas and the boiled potato cubes, season with salt and mix well.
1. Let this mix cook for another 10 minutes.
1. Add the amchoor powder, give it a good mix and switch off the flame.
1. Garnish with coriander leaves and ginger juliennes and serve the Chana Aur Aloo Ki Sookhi Subzi Recipe along with phulkas or parathas for your kids lunch box or for the weeknight dinner.
