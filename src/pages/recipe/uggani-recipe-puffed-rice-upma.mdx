### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 3 cups Puffed rice.
- 1/2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split) - 1/2 tsp urad dal.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Green Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- Curry leaves - few.
- 4 tablespoon Roasted Gram Dal (Pottukadalai).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - few.
- 1 Lemon.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Uggani recipe, powder roasted chana dal and keep aside .
1. Fill water into a large bowl and pour puffed rice into it.
1. Wash it well and immediately drain it out of water.
1. Use both your palms to squeeze out excess water from puffed rice.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. When it is hot, add mustard seeds, urad dal, chana dal and peanuts.
1. When they turn brown add curry leaves and green chillies.
1. Cook for about 40 seconds.
1. Next, add onion and turmeric and saute till the onions turn translucent.
1.  Once they turn translucent, add chopped tomatoes and salt and saute till they are mushy.
1. Add puffed rice and mix well.
1. Add powdered roasted chana dal, chopped coriander leaves and lemon juice.
1. Mix everything well and saute for 2-3 minutes so that they take in flavors.
1. Serve Uggani along with Fresh Fruit Bowl And Filter Coffee for your everyday breakfast.
