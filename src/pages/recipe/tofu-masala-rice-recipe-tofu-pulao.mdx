### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 Red Yellow or Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 cup Tofu - cut into cubed.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Rice.
- 2 Onions - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Ghee.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 2 Tomatoes - chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - to garnish.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Tofu masala rice, wash and soak basmati rice in water for 1/2 an hour.
1. Drain and keep aside.
1. Heat ghee in a sauce pan on medium heat.
1. Once hot, add the cumin seeds and let it splutter.
1. Add chopped onions, green chilli and ginger garlic paste and saute till onion turns golden brown.
1. Now add the tomatoes and capsicum in the pan along with chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder and garam masala.
1. and let it cook for 5-7 minutes.
1. Now add the diced tofu along with salt and saute till the tofu turns little brown in colour.
1. Add 2 cups of water and check for salt.
1. (add more salt if necessary).
1. Cover the pan and once the mixture comes to a boil, turn the heat to low and simmer until all the water is absorbed.
1. Turn off the heat and allow the rice to rest for about 5 minutes before opening.
1. Garnish with chopped coriander and serve the Tofu Masala Rice hot with Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita Recipe or any other vegetable raita of your choice.
