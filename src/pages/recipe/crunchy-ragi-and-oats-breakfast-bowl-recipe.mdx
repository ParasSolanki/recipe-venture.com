### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 4 Multigrain Digestive Ragi Biscuits - broken.
- 1 tablespoon Pistachios - sliced.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 1 tablespoon Badam (Almond) - sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Ragi Flour (Finger Millet/ Nagli).
- Honey - to taste.
- 1/8 cup Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Crunchy Ragi & Oats Breakfast Bowl Recipe, firstly take a saucepan and switch on the heat.
1. Into the pan, add 1 tablespoon Ragi Flour, 2 tablespoons Instant Oats, 1/8 cup Water, 1/2 cup Milk, Honey to taste and mix it well.
1. Cook the mixture till it becomes thick - this will take about a couple of minutes.
1. Once done, switch off the heat and allow the Ragi oats porridge to cool.
1. Once the Ragi & Oats Porridge cool down, we will now assemble the porridge to make a breakfast jar.
1. Into a small jar, add the broken NutriChoice Diabetic Friendly Essentials Ragi Cookies to the bottom of the har.
1. Spoon the Ragi & Oats Porridge over the cookies.
1. Add sliced bananas and sprinkle some chopped almonds and pistachios on top.
1. It is ready to be served.
1. Serve Crunchy Ragi & Oats Breakfast Bowl for your breakfast on its own or along with a Fried Eggs and Toast for a wholesome breakfast.
