### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- 1 teaspoon Enos fruit salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - to garnish.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Green Chutney Rava Dhokla Recipe keep the green chutney ready.
1. To make the green chutney, follow this recipe - Green Chutney Recipe - Coriander Mint Chutney Recipe.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the sooji, green chutney, curd, cooking oil and salt to taste.
1. Mix vigorously and allow it to sit for a good 30 minutes.
1. After the 30 minutes you will see the sooji has fluffed up well.
1. Add a little water if required to make a thick dhokla batter.
1. Preheat the steamer with water and keep it ready.
1. Grease a dhokla plate generously with oil.
1. Add eno's fruit salt and give it a good mixPour the Green Chutney Rava Dhokla batter into the grease dhokla plate.
1. Now place the plate in the steamer, cover and steam the Green Chutney Rava Dhokla for 10-12 minutes until firm.
1. Open the lid, insert a greased knife to check if its done.
1. If the greased knife comes out clean the dhokla is steamed.
1. De-mould and transfer to a serving plate.
1. To make the tadka for the Green Chutney Rava Dhokla RecipeIn a tadka pan, heat oil on medium high flame, add the mustard seeds and allow them to crackle, next add the asafoetida, cumin seeds, sesame seeds, and curry leaves.
1. Once the curry leaves crackle, turn off the flame.
1. Pour this tadka over the Green Chutney Rava Dhokla along with coriander leaves and serve.
1. Serve this Green Chutney Rava Dhokla Recipe as is with a cup of Adrak Chai Recipe - Indian Style Ginger Tea.
1. You could also serve Green Chutney Rava Dhokla Recipe as breakfast along with Tomato Garlic Chutney Recipe, a side of fresh cut fruits and Cocoa Banana Almond Date Smoothie Recipe.
