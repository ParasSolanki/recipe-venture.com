### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 Broccoli - chopped into small florets.
- 1/4 teaspoon Paprika powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 cup Mozzarella cheese - grated.
- 1 Pickled Jalapenos - deseeded.
- 4 Tortillas - or corn tortillas.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder - freshly grounded.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Broccoli Bell Pepper Cheese Quesadilla Recipe, get prep with all the ingredients first.
1. In a Skillet, add oil and heat the oil over medium heat.
1. Once the oil is hot, add chopped broccoli and bell pepper.
1. Now, add the spice powders and salt to the skillet.
1. Cook them until the vegetables become tender and blended with spices.
1. Heat a Shallow frying pan over a medium heat.
1. Place a wheat tortilla/corn tortilla on the pan.
1. Arrange the grated mozzarella cheese evenly on one side of the tortilla.
1. Top the cheese with broccoli mixture.
1. Sprinkle cheese on top of the vegetable blend.
1. Fold the tortillas and cook them on the shallow frying pan until they become brownish.
1. Flip to the other side and cook until it turns slight brown.
1. Serve Quesadilla with Homemade Guacamole Recipe on the sides or along with Spinach Enchilada Recipe (A Classic Mexican Dish) to enjoy your mexican starter for your sunday brunch.
