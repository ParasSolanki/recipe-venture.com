### Ingredients

-  oil - as per use.
-  1 cup wheat flour.
- 1 cup maize flour.
-  1/2 tsp celery.
-  salt - as per use.

### Instructions

1. To make the entire recipe of maize, first of all add maize flour, wheat flour, salt, celery, oil and mix well.
1. Add a little water and knead well.
1. Cover and keep aside for 15 to 20 minutes.
1. Now take small portions and make a round ball.
1. Now roll these balls one by one and make them poori.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan.
1. Add the poori one by one and cook till it becomes golden.
1. Serve.
1. Serve the entire Makki Recipe with Aloo Dum and Bundi Raita for dinner.
