### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a small bunch finely chopped.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Green Moong Sprouts.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Green Moong Sprout Salad Recipe With Vegetables, chop and keep the vegetables ready.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine all the ingredients- green moong sprouts, carrots, capsicum, tomato, lemon juice, salt and coriander leaves.
1. Toss and serve.
1. Serve this  Green Moong Sprout Salad Recipe With Vegetables  along with Watermelon And Cucumber Gazpacho Recipe and Boiled Egg Mayo Sandwich Recipe for a light summer meal.
