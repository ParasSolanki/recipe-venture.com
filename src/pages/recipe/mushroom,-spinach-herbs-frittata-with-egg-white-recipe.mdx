### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 4 Egg white.
- 1 cup Button mushrooms - roughly shopped.
- 1 cup Spinach - roughly chopped and sweated.
- 1 Onion - caramelized.
- 1 sprig Basil leaves - for garnish.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Mushroom, Spinach & Herbs Frittata with Egg White Recipe by separating the egg whites from egg yolk and keep it in a bowl.
1. Whisk well.
1. Chop the mushroom and saut√© them on a small skillet with some salt and pepper.
1. Next wash the spinach leaves and saute it on the pan for few minute till it releases water.
1. Sprinkle some salt and mix well.
1. Once done keep it aside.
1. Next to make the caramelized onions, you can watch our video or see the recipe on how to make the caramelized onion.
1. Keep it ready in a bowl.
1. Heat a flat skillet, drizzle some oil, let the pan heat up and whisk in the egg white.
1. Spread it evenly on the pan.
1. Sprinkle the mushroom, spinach and onions top.
1. Add the seasoning.
1. Close it with a lid and keep it on low heat.
1. Once done carefully slide it on a plate and serve.
1. Serve the Mushroom, Spinach & Herbs Frittata with Egg White Recipe can be served along with a smoothie to enrich your Breakfast.
