### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Avocado - sliced as needed.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera) - roasted.
- 2 cups Black beans - cooked/canned (reserve the liquid).
- 2 cups Vegetable stock - or water.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 Carrot (Gajjar) - halved and sliced.
- 1/2 Green zucchini - diced.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - as needed.
- 1/3 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 1-2 tablespoons Pickled Jalapenos - sliced.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 cup Sour cream - as needed.
- 4 Tortillas - as needed.
- 1/2 cup Sweet corn.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Mexican Black Bean Corn Soup Recipe first, Blend 1 cup of beans in a mixer jar to a paste.
1. and prepare all the ingredients required.
1. Heat oil in a large saucepan, add garlic and sweat the onions with a pinch of salt to this add the carrots and 1 cup of vegetable stock and cook for 3-4 minutes.
1. Now add the beans, sweet corn and zucchini and bring it to a boil.
1. Add the pureed beans, tomato puree, cumin powder, chilli powder, jalapenos,remaining vegetable stock, reserved liquid from the can, lime juice and cook for 2-3 minutes until it returns to a boil.
1. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with coriander leaves over the top.
