### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Onion Raita Recipe, slice the onions and chop the green chilli, mint and coriander leaves.
1. In a mixing bowl, add yogurt, salt, and jeera powder and whisk well until smooth.
1. Next add water to adjust the consistency and add the onions, green chilies, mint and coriander leaves.
1. Mix well to combine.
1. Transfer into a serving bowl and serve.
1. Serve this Onion Raita Recipe along with Ambur Star Chicken Biryani Recipe or Chettinad Mint Potato Biryani Recipe and Mirchi Ka Salan Recipe for a simple Sunday lunch.
