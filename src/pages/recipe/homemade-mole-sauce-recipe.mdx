### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 2 inch Dark chocolate.
- 1 cup Chicken stock.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 1 tablespoon Makki Ka Atta (Yellow Corn Meal Flour).
- 1 tablespoon Raisins.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 2 Tomatoes.
- 2 Chipotle peppers.
- 1 Star anise.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Homemade Mole Sauce Recipe we will first, roast the tomatoes by heating a sauce pan with oil, add the whole tomatoes.
1. In the meanwhile preheat the oven to 180 degree Celsius for 10 minutes.
1. Once the tomatoes roasted slightly and got light brown on skin, switch off the heat.
1. Take a foil add the tomatoes and drizzle some olive oil and cover the foil and place it in the oven and roast them for about 15 minutes.
1. In the same pan add some more oil, sliced onions and cloves of garlic and saute until it softens.
1. Once the onions are sauted switch off the flame, allow it to cool down.
1. Add them into a mixer along with star anise, cloves, cinnamon and raisins.
1. Add little water to blitz it into a smooth paste.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Take the tomatoes out and chop them up and blitz them also along with toasted peanuts and chipotle chilies to a smooth paste.
1. Letâ€™s start to make the sauce.
1. Heat the pan with butter once the butter starts to melt, add maize flour and keep whisking it to make a roux.
1. Add chicken broth and mix well so that there are no lumps formed.
1. Keep whisking till the sauce thickens.
1. Add in the onion paste and tomato paste and bring it to a boil.
1. Add in your seasonings and broken chocolate now and whisk well.
1. Adjust the salt level and boil for 10 more minutes switch off the flame.
1. Use the Homemade Mole Sauce to make Chicken mole sauce with brown sweet corn rice recipe to make a nice lunch meal for yourself.
