### Ingredients

- 1 cup Basmati rice.
- 2 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder - (or pulao masala).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 3 Onions - thinly sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chana Dal Pulao, first wash and soak rice for 30 minutes or so before cooking.
1. Similarly clean, wash and soak bengal gram in water for at least 1 hour.
1. Next heat oil in pressure cooker over medium heat.
1. Add cumin seeds and saute for few seconds till they turn brown in color.
1. Add the dry red chilies, green chilies and saute for a minute or so.
1. Add the sliced onions and saute till they turn golden brown in color.
1. Once the onion is sauteed, add the chopped tomatoes, saute till tomato turn soft and mushy.

1. Stir to combine.
1. Close the lid of the pressure cooker, put the weight on and let pulao cook over medium heat for one or two whistle.

1. Open the lid of the pressure cooker, add the lemon juice and combine with a spoon.
