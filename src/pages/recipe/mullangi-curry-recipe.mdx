### Ingredients

-  1/8 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  1 tablespoon sesame oil.
-  1 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  1 teaspoon Cumin seeds.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 teaspoon chana dal.
-  1 teaspoon black urad dal (Split).
- 2 radishes - washed.
-  1 sprig Curry leaves.
-  2 teaspoons red chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To make Mulangi curry, first heat oil in a pan.
1. Add the given ingredients one by one for tempering.
1. After 15 seconds, add carrots to it and mix.
1. After adding the carrots, add turmeric powder, red chili powder, coriander powder and salt.
1. Cook out for 1 minute then add 1/2 cup of water.
1. Cover the pan and let the radish cook till it becomes soft.
1. Stir in the bitch.
1. After cooking, open the pan and cook on high heat for 20 seconds.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve mulangi curry with mixed vegetable sambar and rice for the day's mine.
