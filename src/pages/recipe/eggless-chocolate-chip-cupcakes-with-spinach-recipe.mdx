### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 teaspoons Baking powder.
- 1/2 cup Chocolate chips.
- 6 tablespoons Coconut milk - or milk.
- 1/2 cup Cocoa Powder.
- 1-1/4 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal) - powdered.
- 2 Ripe Bananas - (spotted bananas).
- 200 grams Coconut Yogurt - or hung yogurt (greek yogurt).
- 2 tablespoons Chia Seeds.
- Spinach Leaves (Palak) - a small bunch.
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1/4 cup Badam (Almond) - powdered.
- 1/2 cup Coconut Sugar.

### Instructions

1. To prepare this yummy Eggless Chocolate Chip Cupcakes With Spinach Recipe, preheat the Oven at 200 degrees Centigrade and line a mini Muffin tray with 24 muffin cups.
1. In a small bowl, add chia seeds and coconut water and mix well and let it sit for 10 minutes to form a chia egg.
1. In a large Mixing bowl, add all the dry ingredients and 1/4 cup chocolate chips.
1. In a Blender/mixer add bananas, yogurt, spinach and blend until smooth.
1. Add the chia eggs and turn on the blender for a few seconds until combined.
1. Pour this mixture onto the dry ingredients and gently mix everything using a spatula until everything is combined.
1. Fill the cavity of the muffin cups until its 3/4th full, sprinkle the remaining chocolate chips on top and bake for 14-16 minutes or until done.
1. Remove it on to a cooling rack to cool and store it in an airtight container.
1. Eggless Chocolate Chip Cupcakes With Spinach Recipe Keeps well in the fridge for a week.
1. Serve them during tea time or as a dessert after lunch/dinner.
