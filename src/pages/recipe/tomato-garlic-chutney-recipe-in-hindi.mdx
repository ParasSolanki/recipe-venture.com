### Ingredients

-  2 green chilies.
-  oil.
-  6 cloves garlic.
-  1 teaspoon mustard.
- 300 grams Tomatoes - finely chopped.
-  1 teaspoon red chili powder - according to taste.
-  salt - according to taste.

### Instructions

1. To make tomato garlic chutney, firstly cut the tomatoes and peel the garlic.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add mustard seeds and let it cook for 10 seconds.
1. Now add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
1. After 1 minute add tomatoes and cook till they become soft.
1. After the tomatoes are soft, add red chili powder and salt.
1. Mix and let it cook for 1 minute.
1. Turn off the gas, let it cool down a bit and then put it in the mixer grinder.
1. Grind it well and take out in a bowl.
1. Serve tomato garlic chutney with tawa paratha, besan paratha or gram flour chile.
