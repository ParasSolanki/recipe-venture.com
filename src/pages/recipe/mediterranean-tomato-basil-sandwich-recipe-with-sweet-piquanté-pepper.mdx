### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons Feta Cheese.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - ground to paste.
- 1 Tomato - sliced.
- 3 tablespoon Homemade Hummus.
- Lettuce leaves - as needed.
- 1 Cucumber - sliced.
- 1 Red onion - sliced.
- 1/4 tablespoon Green Chilli Paste.
- Lemon juice - to taste.
- 4 Sweet piquanté pepper - sliced finely.
- Salt and Pepper - for seasoning.
- Summac - (or lemon zest & salt.
- 8 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mediterranean Tomato Basil Sandwich Recipe With Sweet Piquanté Pepper, get all the ingredients prepped for the sandwich.
1. Chop all veggies and keep chilli, coriander pastes ready.
1. In a mixing bowl, mix green chilli paste and coriander paste with homemade hummus, and drizzle some lemon juice on it and combine well till the paste is even.
1. Spread this hummus mixture on one side of the bread slice.
1. Place the lettuce leaves on it.
1. Now place sliced onions (2-3 per slice of each veggie), cucumber, tomato slices, sweet piquant pepper slices and finally season with salt and pepper and dash of sumac (or alternatively lemon zest) on top.
1. Add crumbled feta on top of each prepared bread slice and place another slice of bread on top and prick a toothpick to hold the sandwich in place.
1. You can cut the sandwich to half and serve.
1. Serve Mediterranean Tomato Basil Sandwich Recipe With Sweet Piquanté Pepper along with Roasted Tomato Sauce and a hearty Vegetarian Pumpkin And Chickpea Soup Recipe or a light Light And Healthy Spinach Soup Recipe for a complete meal.
