### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 200 gram Carp fish.
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - paste.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- Garam masala powder - a pinch.
- 2 cups Spinach - chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Mustard oil.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Hariyali Machli recipe, firstly clean the fish and dry it.
1. Season with salt and pepper for about 5 minutes.
1. Grind together spinach, coriander, green chillies, lemon juice and a little water.
1. Heat a frying pan with oil and fry the fish for 5 another five minutes till cooked.
1. Remove the fish.
1. Once you remove the fish, add cumin seeds and red chillies.
1. After 30 seconds add in garlic, ginger, onion and cook for about 5 minutes.
1. Add kashmiri red chilli powder and garam masala powder.
1. Pour the spinach puree and fry for few minutes.
1. Check the seasoning of salt.
1. Add the fried fish and coat it for 2 minutes.
1. Serve immediately.
1. Serve Hariyali Machali along with Whole Wheat Lachha Paratha or Steamed Rice for a weekday meal.
1. You can also serve Kachumber Salad along with the meal.
