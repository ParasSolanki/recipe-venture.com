### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 100 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese).
- 10 Green beans (French Beans) - finely chopped.
- 4 Pearl onions (Sambar Onions).
- 1 cup Fried Bread Cubes (Croutons).
- 3 Green Chillies.
- 2 cups Basmati rice.
- 1 cup Cauliflower (gobi) - florets.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled and diced.
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - bunch.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 3 teaspoons Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Karnataka Style Mixed Vegetable Pulao Recipe, first get all the ingredients ready.
1. Grind the ingredients mentioned in "For pulao masala" including fresh coconut, pearl onions, garlic, ginger, coriander seeds, cumin seeds, fennel seeds, coriander leaves, tomato, cinnamon stick, cloves, cardamom and green chillies to a fine paste using a mixer grinder.
1. You can a little warm water to make it into a smooth paste.
1. Steam the vegetables using a steamer and keep aside.
1. Cook Basmati rice in 3-1-/2 cups of water in a sauce pan, along with salt and bring it to a brisk boil.
1. Once it comes to a boil, turn the heat to low, cover the pan and cook the rice until all the water is absorbed.
1. Turn off the heat and allow the rice to cool and then fluff the rice up with a fork.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan, add the bay leaf and the ground paste.
1. Saute the pulao masala till it turns brown and fragrant.
1. Add the cooked rice, paneer, bread croutons and vegetables to this masala and mix well.
1. Check the salt and adjust to suit your taste and serve.
1. Serve the Karnataka Style Mixed Vegetable Pulao Recipe along with a bowl of Tomato Onion Raita for lunch or dinner or even pack it into your lunch box.
