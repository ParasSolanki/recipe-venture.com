### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- 100 grams Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - ground.
- 2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Black Salt (Kala Namak) - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar - optional.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 250 grams Elephant yam (Suran/Senai/Ratalu) - chopped and boiled.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Suran Moongphali Curry Recipe, take a heavy bottomed pan and heat the oil.
1. Add cumin seeds once the oil is hot enough, and finely chopped green chillies.
1. Add the finely chopped boiled pieces of suran and stir well.
1. Add ground peanuts, black salt, sugar and turmeric powder to the suran and mix well.
1. Add 1 cup of water to the suran curry, cover it and let it cook for 5 to 10 mins.
1. Once the suran is cooked through, turn off the heat.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Serve the Suran Moongphali Curry Recipe hot with Sabudana Khichdi during a fast or along with steamed rice for a weeknight dinner.
