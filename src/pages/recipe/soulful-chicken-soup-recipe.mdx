### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Chicken drumstick - cut into small pieces.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - cut into medium sized.
- 1/4 cup Sweet corn.
- 1 teaspoon Fresh cream.
- Black pepper powder - for seasoning.
- 2 sprig Spring Onion Greens - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Soulful Chicken Soup Recipe, thoroughly wash and clean the chicken.
1. In a pressure cooker add chicken pieces, onions, carrot, peppercorns, garlic and ginger, 2 cups of water and pressure cook for 3 whistle.
1. Turn off the flameAllow the pressure to release naturally.
1. , drain the stock through a muslin cloth and transfer the stock into a saucepan.
1. Place the saucepan with the stock on medium flame, add sweet corn, spring onions and cream and bring it to a boil.
1. Add salt and crushed peppercorns,adjust the seasoning and serve hot.
1. Serve the Chicken soup with garlic bread or toast to enjoy it as your evening snack or as your Morning Breakfast.
