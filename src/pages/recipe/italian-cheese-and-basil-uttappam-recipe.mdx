### Ingredients

- 3 cups Rice.
- 1 cup White Urad Dal (Whole).
- 4-5 teaspoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil - or any other cooking oil.
- 1 tablespoon Salt.
- 1 cup Parmesan cheese - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 4-5 cups Idli Dosa Batter.
- 1-2 teaspoons Dried basil leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Italian Cheese And Basil Uttapam Recipe , wash and soak rice, dal and methi dana overnight.
1. Grind to make a thick batter and let it rest for other 4-5 hours to ferment.
1. Mix well with hand or a ladle and add salt before use.
1. Heat a nonstick flat bottomed pan/tawa and add few drops of oil and spread all over.
1. Wipe off excess oil with a tissue paper and let the pan heat till smoking hot.
1. Simmer the flames and take the ladle full of batter and place it in the center of the pan slowly spread it outwards in the circular motion to make a thick pancake.
1. Add a drop of oil from sides and cover the lid to get it cooked properly from both sides.
1. Now add grated cheese all over and sprinkle some dry basil over it.
1. Cover the lid again and cook till cheese melts down completely.
1. Do not flip the Uthappam, carefully lift and transfer it on a serving plate.
1. Serve Italian Cheese And Basil Uttapam Recipe hot with coconut chutney and hot Thakkali Vengayam Sambar and enjoy your fusion breakfast.
