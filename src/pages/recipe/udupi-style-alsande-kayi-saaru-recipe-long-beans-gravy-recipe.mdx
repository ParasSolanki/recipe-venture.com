### Ingredients

- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 4 Dry Red Chilli.
- 4 cloves Garlic - crushed.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 teaspoon Rice.
- 1 cup Long String Bean (chavli) - chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1/2 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Udupi Style Alsande Kayi Saaru Recipe (Long Beans Gravy Recipe) by pressure cooking the long beans in a pressure cooker with 1/2 cup water for about 1 whistle.
1. In the meantime, dry roast the ingredients to be ground.
1. Then Powder it finely using a mixer.
1. Now open the cooker once all the pressure is released.
1. Add the powdered masala, tamarind and salt to taste.
1. Keep string till it thickens little.
1. Then to make the tempering heat a kadai with oil, crackle the mustard seeds, garlic cloves, cumin seeds and curry leaves.
1. Then add the dry red chilli also.
1. Once aroma is released pour it over the curry and serve.
1. Serve the Udupi Style Alsande Kayi Saaru Recipe (Long Beans Gravy Recipe) can be had along with steamed rice and Yellow Pumpkin Dal Recipe for a comforting Sunday lunch.
