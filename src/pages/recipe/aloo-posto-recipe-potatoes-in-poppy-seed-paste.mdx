### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds).
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 cup Poppy seeds.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Mustard oil.
- 1 Onion - sliced (optional).
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - diced.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Aloo Posto (Potatoes in Poppy Seed Paste), get prep with all the ingredients and start by soaking khus khus in enough water for 15-20 minutes.
1. Grind soaked khus khus to make a fine paste and keep aside.
1. Heat vegetable oil and mustard oil together in a Wok/Kadai (You can use any of them either).
1. Add kalonji and wait till it crackles.
1. Now add sliced onions if using, and then the green chilies.
1. Saut√© for a while and add potatoes and fry.
1. After a while, add turmeric powder and mix well.
1. Cook till potatoes start to soften.
1. Add the khus khus paste and a little water, stir, and season with salt.
1. Mix well and close the lid.
1. Cook in low flame till khus khus thickens.
1. Add finely chopped coriander and turn off the gas.
1. Serve Aloo Posto hot with Rice, Bengali Luchi and Phalguni dal with a dash of lemon juice.
