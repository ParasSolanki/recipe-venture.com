### Ingredients

- 3 sprig Spring Onion Greens - white and pale-green parts only.
- 1 cup Udon noodles - cooked.
- 4 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 1 cup Kimchi - finely chopped  plus ⅓ cup kimchi juice.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - toasted.
- 2 tablespoons Gochujang (Korean hot pepper paste).
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Korean Style Kim chi Udon Stir Fry with Scallions Recipe by boiling the noodles in hot boiling water, heated in a sauce pan.
1. Once the noodles is just cooked, strain the water and wash it under cold tap running water and drizzle some oil and keep it aside.
1. This will stop any further cooking.
1. Heat a Kadai with oil, add onion and saute for few minutes.
1. Add in the kimchi and sauté for some more time.
1. In a separate bowl mix all the ingredients mentioned in the sauce list.
1. Combine it together and keep it ready.
1. Then add this sauce mixture slowly into the kadai give it a stir.
1. Add in the cooked noodles and toss it over.
1. Check for the seasoning if required.
1. Serve the Korean Style Kim chi Udon Stir Fry with Scallions Recipe along with Healthy General Tso's Chicken Recipe to enjoy your Sunday Meal.
