### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard oil.
- 4 Tomatoes - quartered.
- 1 inch Cinnamon stick.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Bay leaves (tej patta) - torn into half.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tamatar Pyaz Ki Sabzi, first prep all the ingredients and keep them ready.
1. Heat oil in a pressure cooker over medium heat; add the mustard seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Once it crackles, add the curry leaves, bay leaves, cloves, cinnamon stick and saute for a few seconds.
1. Add the onion, green chillies and garlic.
1. Saute the onions until they become slightly soft.
1. Once soft, add the tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chilli powder and salt to taste.
1. Add just a little water - about 2 to 3 tablespoons and cover the pressure cooker.
1. Cook for 2 whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Once the pressure released, check the salt and spices in the Tamatar Pyaz Ki Sabzi and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Serve the Tamatar Pyaz Ki Sabzi along with Aloo Bhindi Sabzi, Phulka, Gujarati Kadhi for a delicious Sunday lunch.
