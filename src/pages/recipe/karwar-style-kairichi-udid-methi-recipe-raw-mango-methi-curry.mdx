### Ingredients

- 1 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- Coconut Oil - as required.
- 2 Mango (Raw) - peeled and chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Jaggery - adjustable.
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 tablespoon Basmati rice.
- 5 Dry Red Chillies.
- Curry leaves - few.
- 1 tablespoon White Urad Dal (Split).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Karwar Style Kairichi Udid Methi Recipe (Raw Mango Methi Curry), chop raw mango into bite sized pieces.
1. Into the pressure cooker, add  turmeric powder, salt and 1/4 cup of water and pressure cook for 3 whistles.
1. Meanwhile, in a small pan, roast the urad dal, fenugreek seeds, rice and red chillies till crisp.
1. Grind this along with coconut adding little water and make a smooth paste.
1. When the raw mangoes are cooked add the ground masala to it.
1. Add salt, jaggery and few curry leaves.
1. Mix everything well and bring the Kairichi Udid Methi Curry to boil.
1. Add some water to get the desired consistencyThe next step is to prepare the tempering.
1. Add oil in a small tadka pan and when its hot add mustard seeds, hing and few curry leaves.
1. Pour into Kairichi Udid Methi and it is ready to be served.
1. Serve Karwar Style Kairichi Udid Methi Recipe (Raw Mango Methi Curry) along with Punjabi Style Tinda Sabji, Palak Raita and Phulka for your everyday meal.
