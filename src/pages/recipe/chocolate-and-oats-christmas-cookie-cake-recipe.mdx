### Ingredients

- 5 grams Pitted Cherries - chopped into small pieces.
- 5 gram Kiwi.
- 250 gram Chocolate chips.
- 2 cups Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 2 tablespoons Chocolate chips.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 3 tablespoons Butter.
- 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/2 cup Condensed Milk.
- 50 gram Pitted Cherries.
- 1/2 cup Raisins.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chocolate And Oats Christmas Cookie Cake Recipe we will make the cookie cake first.
1. Measure all the quantities and store in small bowls.
1. In a pot add water and bring it to a boil on low heat.
1. Once the water is boiling, keep a wide bowl on top of the boiling pot of water.
1. This is called the double boiler method.
1. Add the chocolate chips and butter.
1. Once the butter and chocolate has melted, turn off the boiling water and add in the vanilla extract into the liquid chocolate Place a saucepan on medium heat and add milk into it.
1. When the milk gets warm add the oats.
1. Stir for few minutes until the milk is totally absorbed into the oats.
1. Pour in the chocolate mixture along with the chopped cherries and raisins for about 10 minutes.
1. Once all the ingredients are well combined, turn off the flame.
1. Take a round baking pan (24 inch), grease all sides of the pan with butter.
1. Pour in the chocolate oats mixture and press it so that it forms a smooth outer layer.
1. Refrigerate for 20 minutes so that it sets.
1. After 20 minutes, take it out of the fridge and invert the Chocolate Cookie Cake onto a cake plate.
1. Pour some condensed milk on top and decorate the cake with cherries, kiwis and chocolate chips as you like it.
