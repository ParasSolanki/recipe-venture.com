### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoons Garam masala powder.
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- Coconut Oil - for cooking.
- 300 grams Chicken - minced.
- 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kerala Style Chicken Chatti Pathiri Recipe, we will first heat a saucepan over  medium heat.
1. Add coconut oil, add curry leaves and allow it splutter.
1. Add chopped ginger and garlic and saute for few seconds.
1. Add sliced onions and fry them till they turn translucent.
1. At this point you can add minced chicken along with all the spice powders - turmeric powder, red chilli powder, garam masala powder, pepper powder, salt and give it a toss.
1. Adjust the seasoning according to your taste and saute well till the chicken is cooked.
1. Keep it aside.
1. To make the pathiri batter, in a mixing bowl, combine wheat flour, all purpose flour along with Â½ cup of water and mix to form batter that is similar to that of a pancake batter.
1. Now heat up a small flat skillet on medium heat, add a ladle full of the pathiri batter and swirl it around, Shape it like crepes and keep it as thin as possible.
1. Flip and cook, ensuring it is juts about cooked and no brown spots appear.
1. Repeat the same process till you finish the batter, to make enough crepes for layering.
1. Now layer each crepe with the chicken mixture and build it up.
1. Crepe-chicken-crepe, repeat the layers.
1.  Heat a skillet with oil, carefully place the layered Chicken Pathiri and roast till it is light brown.
1. Carefully flip it over and roast it on the other side.
1. Turn off the flame.
1. Serve the Kerala Style Chicken Chatti Pathiri Recipe along with Masala Chai Recipe - Indian Spiced Tea or Ginger Cardamom Chai  to enjoy your evening snack.
