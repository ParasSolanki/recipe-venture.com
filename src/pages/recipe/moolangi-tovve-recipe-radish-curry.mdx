### Ingredients

- 3 Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish) - peeled and sliced (5mm thick).
- 2 tablespoon Fresh coconut - freshly grated.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - Few sprigs.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 2 Lemon wedges.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making  Moolangi Tovve Recipe - Radish Curry, wash the moong dal couple of times with water and add it to the pressure cooker along with radish, turmeric powder, green chillies and 1.
1. 5 cups of water.
1. Pressure cook for 3 whistles.
1. After three whistles turn off the heat and let the pressure release naturally.
1. Once done, open the lid and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan, add asafoetida, mustard and wait for the mustard to pop and then add red chillies and ginger and ler the red chillies crisp up.
1. Now add the cooked dal mixture in the pan with salt to taste and 1.
1. 5 cups of water.
1. Brisk boil till the dal gets slightly thick.
1. Once it is done, add the chopped coriander and grated coconut in the Moolangi Tovve and give it a stir.
1. Tun off the heat and transfer the Moolangi Tovve to a serving bowl and serve.
1. Serve Moolangi Tovve Recipe - Radish Curry with Steamed Rice Recipe and Elai Vadam for a comforting lunch.
