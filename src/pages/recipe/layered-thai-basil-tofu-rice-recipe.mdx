### Ingredients

- 8 cloves Garlic.
- 6 Thai Red chilli (Birds Eye Chilli).
- 1/2 teaspoon Dark soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 3/4 cup Jasmine Rice.
- 1-1/2 cups Water.
- Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - few to garnish (optional).
- 1/2 teaspoon Light soy sauce.
- Thai basil leaves - A small bunch.
- 1 Onion - chopped to chunks.
- 150 grams Tofu - cubed.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Shiitake mushrooms - fresh.
- 2-1/2 tablespoons Sesame (Gingelly) Oil - or groundnut oil.

### Instructions

1. To start cooking Layered Thai Basil & Tofu Rice Recipe, get prep with all the ingredients and then pound red chilli and garlic in a pestle & mortar.
1. Cook rice in an electric rice cooker.
1. Electric cooker is the best for the measurements given above.
1. While the rice is getting done, heat oil in a wok.
1. Add the ground red chilli and garlic paste and fry them well.
1. Add Onion and fry for a minute.
1. Add tofu and continue to fry until they turn golden brown.
1. Add, light & dark soya sauce, shitake mushroom to this mix.
1. Add very little salt as soya sauce has already salt in it.
1. Mix them all well and fry for 2 minutes.
1. Keep stirring continuously.
1. Add basil leaves and mix them well.
1. Cook for a minute.
1. For layering the rice, take a serving bowl and layer the bottom portion with rice followed by the basil tofu curry.
1. Repeat layering for 2 or 3 times and end with tofu basil curry on top.
1. Garnish with roasted peanuts (optionally).
1. Serve Layered Thai Basil & Tofu Rice Recipe along with Thai Yellow Curry Recipe.
