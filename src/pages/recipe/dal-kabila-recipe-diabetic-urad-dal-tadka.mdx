### Ingredients

- 3 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 3 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/4 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 inch Cinnamon stick.
- 1 cup White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1/2 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper corns - lightly crushed.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 3 cups Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Dal Kabila Recipe first wash urad dal and soak it for 2 hours.
1. Prep all the ingredients.
1. Add ghee into the pressure cooker and place over medium heat.
1. Add in the dry red chillies, cinnamon stick, cloves and saute until you can smell the aromas of the spices coming through.
1. Add Onions and slit green chillies and saute until the onions soften and change colour.
1. Stir in the tomatoes, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, and saute.
1. Finally add the split urad dal, salt along with 3 cups of water and cook for 5 to 6 whistles (about 15 minutes).
1. Once done, turn off the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Once the pressure has released, check the salt and adjust according to taste.
1. Transfer the Dal Kabila/ Urad Dal Tadka to a serving bowl.
1. The final step is the tadka.
1. Heat the ghee in a pan over medium heat; add the cumin seeds, crushed black pepper and sauce for a few seconds until the cumin seeds crackle.
1. Spoon the takda over the Dal Kabila and serve hot.
1. Serve the Dal Kabila/ Urad Dal Tadka with Palak Ragi & Oats Wheat Thepla and Kachumber Salad Recipe With Cucumber, Onion & Tomatoes for a wholesome weeknight diabetic indian dinner or lunch.
