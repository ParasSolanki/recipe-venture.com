### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 3 Chayote.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chow Chow Thoran Recipe, peel the chow chow and cut them into medium size dices.
1. Steam the vegetable with salt in the pressure cooker adding just about 2 tablespoons of water or a steamer until it is soft and cooked.
1. In a blender, make a coarse mixture of the coconut, one sprig of curry leaves, green chillies and cumin seeds.
1. Combine the coconut mixture into the steamed chow chow and keep aside.
1. The final step is to make the seasoning.
1. Heat a small pan with a teaspoon of oil; add in the mustard seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Once it crackles, stir in the other sprig of curry leaves and the turmeric powder.
1. Turn off the heat and pour this seasoning into the Chow Chow Thoran mixture.
1. Stir to come well and check the salt levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. You can serve Chow Chow Thoran along with Steamed Rice, Tomato Rasam, Mixed Vegetable Sambar and Elai Vadam for an everyday meal.
