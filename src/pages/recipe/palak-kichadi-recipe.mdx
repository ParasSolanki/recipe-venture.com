### Ingredients

- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1/2 cup Pink Masoor Dal (Split).
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder - (optional).
- 1/2 Tomato - sliced.
- 50 grams Tofu - diced.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/3 cup Broccoli - florets cleaned.
- Ghee - or oil as required.
- 3 cloves Garlic - cut into strips.
- 1 cup Spinach Leaves (Palak) - tightly packed and cleaned.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Rice.
- 2 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Garlic - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Palak Kichdi recipe, In a pressure cooker add rice, dal, broccoli, peas, turmeric, one pinch of hing, little oil and 2 -1/2 cups of water and pressure cook.
1. In a saucepan, bring water to a boil and add the cleaned spinach leaves and let it sit for a minute in the pot while the flame is still on.
1. Now drain it in a colander and transfer it into a bowl of cold water.
1. Once its cool grind it to a paste in a mixer grinder and set aside.
1. In a wide pan, heat some oil and add bay leaf, jeera, chopped garlic, pinch of hing, green chillies, chopped onions and saute it until the onions have started to change colour to this mixture.
1. Once it is done, add the pureed spinach and mix everything properly.
1. Lower the flame, add the cool dal and rice mixture into the pot and mix until combined with the palak mixture, add the chopped tofu, some water if necessary and season with salt.
1. Also add the garam masala if using at this stage.
1. Cook for 2-3 minutes and turn of the flame.
1. In a tadka pan, add some ghee/oil add sliced garlic and cook till its starts to brown, pour this onto the khichdi.
1. Garnish with sliced tomatoes and it is ready to be served.
1. Serve Palak Kichdi with Lauki Raita, Avocado Raita or any other raita of your choice for a comforting weekday meal.
