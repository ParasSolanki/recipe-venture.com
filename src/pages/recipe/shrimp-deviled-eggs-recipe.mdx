### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Mustard sauce.
- 4 Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - finely chopped.
- 10 Shrimps - peeled and deveined.
- Spring Onion Greens - Few for garnish.
- 2 tablespoons Classic Mayonnaise (With Egg).
- 4 Whole Eggs - hard boiled.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Shrimp Deviled Eggs recipe, heat a skillet and add butter.
1. After the butter melts, add chopped spring onion whites.
1. Fry for 2 minutes till onion turns pinkish.
1. Add shrimps and toss till the shrimps get cooked.
1. Add garlic powder and toss.
1. Let it cook for 10 minutes.
1. After cooling, mince the shrimps or chop into very small pieces.
1. Take the hard boiled eggs and scoop out the egg yolks with a spoon and add the egg yolks to a mixing bowl.
1. Mash it using a fork.
1. Add mayonnaise, mustard sauce, garlic powder and black pepper powder ansd mix well.
1. Add a pinch of salt or as per taste.
1. Add the onion and shrimp mixture and mix well.
1. Keep a few shrimp pieces aside for garnishing.
1. Take a piping bag or a plastic bag of which a corner is cut.
1. Add the egg yolk and mayo mixture to the piping bag.
1. Gently fill the egg white cups with 1 tablespoon of the filling.
1. Garnish the deviled eggs with shrimp pieces and spring onion greens.
1. These shrimp deviled eggs can be served cold as appetizers or as starters for the parties or along with Pizza Margherita for a card party.
