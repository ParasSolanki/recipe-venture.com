### Ingredients

-  oil - as per use.
-  3 cloves garlic.
-  1 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  12 small onions - peel and cut in half.
-  1 onion - chopped.
-  1 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  1 bay leaf - break.
-  1 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  3 sprig coriander - finely chopped.
-  2 green chillies.
- 4 Potatoes - boil and chop.
-  1 small Spoon garam masala powder.
-  3/4 cup tomato puree.
-  1 inch ginger.
-  salt - according to taste.
-  1 teaspoon Kashmiri red chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To make Jaipuri Potato Onion Vegetable, first boil the potatoes and chop the onions.
1. Make tomato puree and keep it aside.
1. Put the paste making ingredients in the mixer grinder and grind them.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add cumin seeds and let it cook for 10 seconds.
1. Now add small onions and cook till it becomes soft.
1. After softening, add potatoes and salt and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. After that remove it separately.
1. Add some more oil to the pan.
1. Add grated garlic paste to it.
1. Cook for 3 minutes and then add all the spices and bay leaves.
1. Cook it for 1 more minute.
1. After 1 minute add tomato puree, cooked small onions, potatoes and salt.
1. Cover the pan and cook for 10 minutes on low heat.
1. Turn off the gas after 10 minutes and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve Jaipuri Aloo Onion vegetable along with Tadka Raita and Phulka for dinner.
