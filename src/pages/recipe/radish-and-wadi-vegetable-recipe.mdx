### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish) - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- Red Chilli powder - to taste.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped to garnish.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard oil.
- 10 Badi (wadi) - punjabi masala one.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mooli And Wadi Ki Sabzi, firstly finely chop or grate the radish.
1. Crush the wadis and roast them till they are crisp and keep them aside.
1. To the same pan add mustard and cumin seeds.
1. Let them splutter.
1. Next, add radish, salt and saute for about 3-0 seconds.
1. Cover the lid and keep stirring in between.
1. When almost done, add fried crushed wadi, turmeric and red chilli powder.
1. Saute for 3 to 4 minutes so that radish takes in all the spices.
1. Switch off, garnish Mooli And Wadi Ki Sabz with coriander leaves and serve hot.
1. Serve Mooli And Wadi Ki Sabzi along with Panchmel dal, Phulka and Boondi Raita Recipe Spiced With Black Salt for weekday lunch or dinner.
