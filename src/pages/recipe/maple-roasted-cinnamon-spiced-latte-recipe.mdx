### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 1 tablespoon Maple syrup.
- 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 2 Dates - pitted and coarsely chopped.
- 3/4 cup Milk.
- 1/2 tablespoon Instant Coffee Powder - instant Bru Gold.
- 6-8 Ice cubes.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Maple Roasted Cinnamon Spiced Latte Recipe first get all the ingredients handy and ready.
1. Add all the ingredients into the blender (mixer grinder) jar and blend to make a smooth latte.
1. Allow the latte to froth in the blender jar.
1. Pour the latte into tall mason jars or a tall glass and serve.
1. Serve the Maple Roasted Cinnamon Spiced Latte as a delicious drink along with your favorite coffee cake as your evening tea time dessert.
