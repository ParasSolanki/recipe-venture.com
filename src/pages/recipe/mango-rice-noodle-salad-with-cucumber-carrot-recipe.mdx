### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - sliced into roundels.
- 1/2 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely diced.
- 1 Mango (Ripe) - cut into cubes.
- 1/2 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - finely diced.
- 3 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina) - for garnish (optional).
- 1 inch Ginger - finely grated.
- 4 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 1/4 cup Lemon juice.
- 2 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 200 grams Rice Vermicelli Noodles (Thin) - blanched.
- 8 sprig Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - finely chopped.
- 10 Cashew nuts - toasted.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 1 Cucumber - sliced into roundels.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mango Rice Noodle Salad With Cucumber & Carrot Recipe, we will boil the rice noodles over a sauce pan with enough water.
1. This will take about 10 minutes to boil.
1. Once done strain the water and wash the rice noodles under running water to stop cooking.
1. Chop all the vegetables and fruits and keep it ready.
1. To make the dressing, in a bowl, grate the ginger, add soy, honey, sesame oil, lemon juice, pinch of salt and keep mixing till it combines well.
1. Drizzle some dressing onto the boiled rice noodles and toss it well.
1. Place the noodles over a serving plate.
1. Add the remaining dressing into the vegetables and mango and toss well.
1. Place them over the bed of rice noodles, sprinkle spring onions and mint leaves.
1. Add in toasted nuts and serve the Mango Rice Noodle Salad immediately as a refreshing side dish for dinner.
1. Serve the Mango Rice Noodle Salad With Cucumber & Carrot Recipe along with a Lettuce Wrap Recipe with Asian Style Roasted Vegetables and drink to make special weekend meal for your loved ones.
