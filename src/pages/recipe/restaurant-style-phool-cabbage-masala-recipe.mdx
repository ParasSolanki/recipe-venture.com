### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 2 teaspoons Water.
- 1/4 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 2 teaspoons Cashew nuts - broken.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1/4 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 tablespoons Fresh cream.
- 1/4 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 12 Cauliflower (gobi) - cut to clorets.
- 1 cup Onion - finely chopped.
- 2-1/2 tablespoons Mustard oil - (adjust as per need).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Restaurant Style Phool Gobhi Masala Recipe, first clean the florets and toss it along with salt and turmeric powder.
1. Heat a kadai with oil on a medium heat and fry the florets for about 5 minutes or till the raw smell vanishes.
1. Take them out on to a plate.
1. In the same kadai, add the onions, fry till translucent.
1. Switch off the stove and let the onions cool down.
1. Once it cools down, add the onion mixture in a mixer grinder and make it into a fine paste.
1. Add bay leaf and cumin seeds in the leftover hot oil.
1. Once the cumin seeds crackle, add ginger and garlic paste with 2 teaspoons water.
1. Let it cook for about a minute or two.
1. After a minute, add the fried florets, tomato puree and mix everything well.
1. Immediately add the fried onion paste, kashmiri red chilli powder and 1 cup water, cook for about 5 minutes.
1. Turn to low flame.
1. After 5 minutes, add the cream, garam masala powder and ghee.
1. Check for salt seasoning.
1. Garnish with cashews and serve.
1. Serve Restaurant Style Phool Gobhi Masala Recipe along with Tawa Tandoori Roti Recipe, Mushroom Do Pyaza Recipe and Boondi Raita for a dinner meal.
