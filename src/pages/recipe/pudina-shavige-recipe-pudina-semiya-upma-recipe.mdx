### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 tablespoon Ghee - for flavour.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- 2 cups Semiya (Vermicelli) - rice vermicelli or foxtail millet vermicelli will also do.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 2 tablespoons Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 1/2 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Karnataka Style Pudina Shavige Recipe prep all the ingredients and keep it aside.
1. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat, add cumin seeds and allow it to crackle for few seconds.
1. Add ginger garlic and saute till it softens.
1. Add onions and saute till the onions turn translucent.
1. Finally add mint leaves, coriander leaves and green chilli and saute till the leaves wilt and turn off the heat.
1. Grind the pudina mixture into a coarse paste by adding very little water.
1. Finally to make the pudina Pudina Shavige, heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
1. Add the mustard seeds and urad dal and allow it to crackle and the dal to turn golden and crisp.
1. Add the peanuts and roast on low heat until crisp and golden.
1. Stir in the curry leaves, the semiya (vermicelli), salt to taste, pudina paste and 1-1/2 cups of water.
1. Cover the pan and cook the Pudina Shavige until all the water is absorbed and the semiya is cooked completely.
1. Ensure you give the Pudina Shavige a stir half way through.
1. Once the Pudina Shavige is cooked completely, add the ghee and give it a stir.
1. Transfer the Pudina Shavige to a serving bowl and serve hot for breakfast.
1. Serve the Pudina Shavige Recipe along with coconut chutney, raita and fruit bowl to relish your morning breakfast.
