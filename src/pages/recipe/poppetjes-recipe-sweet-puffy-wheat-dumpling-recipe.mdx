### Ingredients

- 4-5 Badam (Almond) - powdered.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 1 cup Coconut milk - or toddy.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 teaspoon Nutmeg powder.
- pinch Salt.
- 4-5 Raisins.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds - skin removed and pound.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Sugar.
- 1 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Poppetjes Recipe -  Sweet Puffy Wheat Dumpling Recipe mix the dry ingredients such as the wheat flour and the rava with toddy.
1. Mix the ingredients to a smooth, medium thickness, porridge consistency.
1. Set it aside to ferment for about 1 hour.
1. Once it is fermented mix the raisins, powdered almond, nutmeg and cinnamon and a pinch of salt.
1. Mix thoroughly.
1. You can add a little water if the batter has become thick.
1. Heat the paniyaram pan with little oil in each hollow holes.
1. Once it is heated, add a tablespoon of the batter in each cavity.
1. Cook for about 3 minutes on each side.
1. Meanwhile, heat a saucepan with sugar and water, add a cardamom for extra flavor in the syrup, let it thicken.
1. Once the poppetjes are cooked dip them in the hot sugar syrup for 10 seconds and take them out.
