### Ingredients

- 1 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped or sliced.
- 1 French Loaf.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Garlic - paste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoon Butter.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1 cup Mozzarella cheese.
- 1 teaspoon Pizza seasoning.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Tomato Ketchup.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Parsley leaves - chopped.
- 8 Cherry tomatoes - halved.

### Instructions

1. Preparing Crostini With Peppers & Cheese Recipe includes these easy steps.
1. Firstly, prep up with all the ingredients mentioned & cut the French loaf into thin Slices.
1. Take a bowl and add butter, oregano, garlic paste, fresh parsley and mix well.
1. Apply this Butter mixture evenly on the Loaf slice on both the sides.
1. Take a baking tray and apply a little amount of butter on it and Put these slices and put it in the Oven and bake it for 5 minutes at 180 degree Celsius.
1. In a frying pan put some Olive Oil and add just 1/4 teaspoon of garlic paste, bell peppers, fresh parsley, pizza seasoning, dry oregano, Del Monte Tomato Ketchup, coarsely ground black pepper and fry them for a few minutes.
1. Turn off the flame, let the topping cool a little and salt to taste.
1. Take out the bread slices from the oven and top the bread slices with the bell peppers topping.
1. Top it up with mozzarella cheese and sprinkle pizza seasoning on the cheese.
1. Again, put the bread slices back in the oven for 5 - 6 minutes and bake it till the cheese melts.
1. Then top Crostini With Peppers & Cheese Recipe with a few halved fresh cherry tomatoes on each and serve as the appetizer or for an evening snack.
1. Serve Crostini With Peppers & Cheese along with Penne Arrabiata for a weeknight dinner.
