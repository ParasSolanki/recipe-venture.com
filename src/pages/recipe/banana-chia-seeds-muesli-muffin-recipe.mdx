### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Chia Seeds.
- 2 teaspoons Baking powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 cup Muesli.
- 1/2 cup Ripe Bananas - mashed.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/4 cup Cocoa Powder.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 2 tablespoons Honey.
- 8 Dates - pitted and chopped.
- 2 Egg whites - beaten.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Banana Chia Seeds Muesli Muffin Recipe by pre heating the oven at 180 degree Celsius for about 10 minutes.
1. Using electric stand mixer, you can also use a hand mixer as well.
1.  Add the egg, vanilla essence, honey and olive oil.
1. Whip it till it becomes fluffy.
1. Then slowly sift the wheat flour, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt.
1. Give it a whip for few more minutes.
1. Now add in the mashed ripe bananas and milk, mix slowly using a pastry palate knife or just with a spoon.
1.  Finally add in the muesli and chia seeds.
1. Give it one last mix till it all combines.
1. Grease a cup cake tray, drop in a dollop of this batter into the tray till it reaches the brim and bake well.
