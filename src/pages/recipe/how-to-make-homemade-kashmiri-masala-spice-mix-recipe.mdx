### Ingredients

- 3 Whole turmeric.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cloves (Laung).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 Nutmeg - broken.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2-1/2 teaspoon Garlic powder.
- 2 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2-1/2 teaspoon Dry ginger powder.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To make Homemade Kashmiri Masala/ Spice Mix Recipe, get prep with all the ingredients first.
1. Preheat a skillet on medium heat and roast all the ingredients together except the spice powders (garlic-ginger-chilli), on low to medium heat until you can smell the aromas coming through.
1. Make sure you keep stirring continuously with preferably a wooden spoon while dry roasting.
1. Once the spices have cooled down, dry grind them along with garlic powder, ginger powder and chilli powder till finely done.
1. Store Homemade Kashmiri Masala/ Spice Mix Recipe in an airtight container and use as required.
1. You can also freeze the masala for longer shelf life.
