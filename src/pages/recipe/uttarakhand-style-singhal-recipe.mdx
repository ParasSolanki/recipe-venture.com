### Ingredients

- Milk - as needed.
- 1/2 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- Sunflower Oil - to deep fry.
- 1/2 cup Sugar.
- 1 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).
- 1/2 Ripe Bananas - mashed.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Uttarakhand Style Singhal Recipe Take a large mouth bowl.
1. Add semolina, thick yogurt, mashed banana, sugar and green cardamom powder.
1. Mix well.
1. Add enough milk and make a smooth batter.
1. Cover and keep it aside for 3-4 hours.
1. Heat oil in a kadai/deep frying pan.
1. Take a clean plastic bag and make a cut in the end.
1. Fill the bag with semolina batter and press the bag so that batter will reach at the point, where bag has a cut.
1. Drop the batter in the oil in a spiral shape like jalebi.
1. Fry it until golden brown.
1. Drain on an absorbent paper and repeat the process.
1. Serve Uttarakhand Style Singhal Recipe with rabdi as a dessert or with tea as a snack.
