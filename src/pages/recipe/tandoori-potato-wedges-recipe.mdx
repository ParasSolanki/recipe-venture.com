### Ingredients

- 4 teaspoon Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - one sprig.
- 400 grams Potato (Aloo).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).

### Instructions

1. To begin with Tandoori Potato Wedges, wash, peel and chop the potatoes into wedges.
1. Mix all the spices mentioned in the ingredients into yogurt and whisk manually into a smooth paste.
1. Marinate the potatoes in the mixture for about 30 minutes.
1. Spread it evenly on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
1. Bake it at 200 C for 45-50 minutes or until done in a preheated oven.
1. Garnish with chopped coriander (optional)Serve Tandoori Potato Wedges with Tzatziki or ketchup.
