### Ingredients

-  8 cashews.
-  oil - as per use.
-  1/2 tablespoon kasoori methi.
-  1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  2 green chillies.
-  asafoetida.
-  2 tomatoes.
- 1 cup spinach.
-  1 tablespoon lemon juice.
-  1 tablespoon garam masala powder.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  1/2 small Tablespoon red chili powder.
-  1/2 cup sweet corn - simmer.
-  salt - to taste.
-  1 tablespoon sugar.

### Instructions

1. To make Jain Style Corn Spinach, first blanch the spinach and then put it in cold water.
1. Take out the water and puree it in a mixer grinder.
1. Cook the given ingredients for grinding in 1 teaspoon of oil.
1. Cook until tomatoes are soft.
1. After cooling, add some water and grind it.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add cumin seeds and after 15 seconds add spinach puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder and salt.
1. Let it cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. After 4 minutes, add tomato puree, dried spices and corn.
1. Mix well and let it cook for 8 to 10 minutes.
1. Add a little water, kasuri methi, salt and lemon juice.
1. Mix well and serve after 2 minutes.
1. Serve Jain Style Corn Spinach along with Boondi Raita and Phulka for dinner.
