### Ingredients

- Salt - a pinch.
- 340 grams Tofu - drained (Firm Silken).
- 1/4 cup Coconut Oil - melted.
- 1/2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract.
- 1/2 cup Almond Milk (Badam Milk).
- 1/3 cup Maple syrup - or honey.
- 3/4 cup Cocoa Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tofu Chocolate Mousse recipe, firstly add silken tofu, cocoa powder, maple syrup, salt, Vanilla extract, coconut oil and almond milk into the blender jar.
1. Blend the mixture until smooth.
1. Once it runs smooth, spoon them into serving cups and refrigerate at least for 1-2 hours before serving.
1. Adding extra liquid depends on the brand and the consistency of the tofu, add 2 tablespoons at a time if needed until it reaches desired consistency.
1. You will have smooth, soft, only a just about set tofu chocolate mousse.
1. Serve Tofu Chocolate Mousse as a dessert after your simple meal of Cheese Garlic Bread Recipe, and Cheesy Baked Spaghetti Pasta Recipe In Tomato Basil Sauce.
