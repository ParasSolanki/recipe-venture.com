### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina) - roughly torn.
- 1 cup Mango (Ripe) - puree.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 2 inch Ginger - grated.
- Salt - a pinch.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped thin diagonally.
- 2 Lemon - cut in four pieces each.
- 250 ml Aerated water (Soda Water).
- Ice cubes - as required (crushed).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spicy Chilli Mango Virgin Mojito Recipe, sieve mango puree well and keep it aside.
1. In a bowl, muddle the mint leaves and ginger together but don't crush them too much.
1. Mix this mint lemon ginger mixture in mango puree.
1. Add the green chillies, salt and the soda and give it brisk stir,Pour the Spicy Chilli Mango Virgin Mojito into glasses.
1. Add ice cube and serve immediately.
1. You can place the lemon wedges on the glass and serve.
1. Serve the Spicy Chilli Mango Virgin Mojito Recipe to your guests along with starters like  Oats and Vegetable Kebab and a Achari Paneer Tikka.
