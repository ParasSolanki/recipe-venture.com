### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Split Mustard Seeds (Rai Kuria).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 cup Split Methi Seeds (Methi Kuria).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Achari Masala Recipe - combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl.
1. Stir well until all the ingredients are well combined.
1. Store the Achari Masala Recipe/ Sambharo Masala in an air tight bottle and into the freezer.
1. Use the Achari Masala Recipe/ Sambharo Masala to make instant pickles as and when required.
