### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- 1/4 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- Salt - to sprinkle.
- 1/4 cup Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- Sev - to garnish.
- Puffed rice - to garnish.
- 4 Botis - Golden finger fryums.
- 1/4 cup Sweet Chutney (Date & Tamarind).
- Red Chilli powder - to sprinkle.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Boti Masala Chaat Recipe, cut  the botis/ fryums into half horizontally, making sure you get boat shaped like botis.
1. In a mixing bowl combine, chopped onions and grated carrots, lemon juice and salt and mix well.
1. Set aside.
1. In a serving platter arrange the botis, put a teaspoon of each dhaniya pudina chutney and date tamarind chutney in each boti.
1. Now fill each of those botis with the onion- carrot filling, sprinkle some red chilli powder and garnish the Boti Masala Chaat with puffed rice and sev.
1. Serve this Boti Masala Chaat Recipe - Fryums Chaat as an evening snack with Cinnamon Spiced Black Lemon Tea Recipe or Masala Chai Recipe - Indian Spiced Tea.
