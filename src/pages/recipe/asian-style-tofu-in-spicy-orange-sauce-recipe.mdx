### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - toasted.
- 2 tablespoon Sriracha sauce.
- 2 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon All spice powder.
- 400 grams Tofu - firm.
- 2 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - freshly ground.
- 1/2 Fresh orange juice.
- 2 teaspoon Orange Zest (Rind).
- 3 tablespoon Rice Vinegar.
- 4 tablespoon Ginger - minced.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Asian Style Tofu in Spicy Orange Sauce Recipe, toss tofu, cornstarch, orange zest, salt and ground pepper and all spice powder together in a mixing bowl and set aside.
1. Whisk together vinegar, brown sugar, orange juice and sriracha sauce in another bowl and keep aside.
1. In a pan over medium heat, cook tofu in sesame oil until the tofu begins to turn golden.
1. Add ginger, garlic and continue to sauté for 2-3 minutes.
1. Pour in the vinegar, orange juice mixture and cook until the liquid has reduced and starts to look a bit sticky.
1. Adjust spices if requiredAdd sesame seeds and sauté further for a minute.
1. Turn off heat and serve Asian Style Tofu in Spicy Orange Sauce Recipe warm as a starter or as a side dish during a weekday meal.
1. Serve Asian Style Tofu In Spicy Orange Sauce along with Hakka Noodles for your weeknight dinner.
