### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Fresh coconut.
- 10 Raisins.
- 4 tablespoon Jaggery.
- 2 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 cup Milk.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 teaspoon Rice.
- 3 tablespoon Poppy seeds.
- 10 Cashew nuts.
- 1 pinch Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Karnataka Style Gasagase Payasa, roast the poppy seeds and rice  in a skillet on low-medium heat  until it turns into a nice golden brown.
1. This will take about 4-6 minutes.
1. Turn off the flame and allow it to cool.
1. Transfer into a mixer jar.
1. and grind it into a coarse mixture, add grated coconut and water and grind again to a smooth mixture.
1. Heat a sauce pan with milk on medium flame, add the ground poppy seeds and coconut mixture, cardamom pods, jaggery and bring it to a boil.
1. Boil the payasam till it thickens.
1. Heat ghee in a tadka pan, on low flame and fry the cashew nuts and raisins for 2 minutes.
1. Turn off the flame and pour it over the payasam and serve.
1. Serve the Gasagase Payasa after having a Spicy Mixed Vegetable Pulao Recipe with Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita to cool down your palate with a sweet note.
