### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Mustard oil - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 inch Ratan jot (alkanet) - (infuse it in heated oil).
- 1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 500 grams Mutton - with bones.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 2 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 2 teaspoons Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 4 Cloves (Laung).
- 4 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kashmiri Rogan Josh recipe, wash mutton nicely and drain water completely.
1. Marinade the mutton with mentioned ingredients under Marination for approximately for 1 and 1-1/2 hours.
1. Cling wrap the bowl and refrigerate it for better results.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed wok and add bay leaf and other whole spices mentioned.
1. Immediately, add marinaded mutton and mix well.
1. Fry on medium to high flames stirring occasionally.
1. Now add asafoetida and stir well.
1. Add almost 1 cup of water in the mutton mix and allow it to cook with lid closed.
1. In a separate bowl, add yogurt and red chilli powder & fennel powder and whisk nicely.
1. Take off the lid and add whisked yogurt and mix well such that everything incorporates nicely.
1. Adjust salt as needed.
1. Now add Ratan Jot / root infused in oil in the mutton and cook till mutton is soft and tender.
1. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.
1. Serve Kashmiri Rogan Josh with hot steamed rice or rumali rotis for a delicious weekend meal.
