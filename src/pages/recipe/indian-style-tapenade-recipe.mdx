### Ingredients

- 3-4 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - as required for garnishing.
- 1/2 Dry Red Chilli - crushed for garnishing.
- 2 tablespoon Parsley leaves - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Green olives - chopped.
- 1 loaf French bread.
- Salt - Salt to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Dry Red Chillies - crushed.
- 1/2 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Onions - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Lemon zest.
- 1/4 cup Black olives - chopped.
- 1 cups Green Moong Dal (Whole) - boiled.
- 1 tablespoon Garlic - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Indian Style Tapenade, heat the wide pan with 1-1/2 tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil.
1. Add chopped onions, chopped garlic, chopped black & green olives.
1. Mix everything well and saute for a minute.
1. Add boiled moong along with salt and mash them lightly.
1. Add lemon zest and mix.
1. Turn off the stove and transfer the mixture into a bowl.
1. Add chopped cilantro or chopped parsley into the mixture.
1. Add crushed dry red chilies, lemon juice and remaining 1-1/2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil.
1. Mix everything well.
1. Cut diagonal slices of French bread.
1. Toast them very lightly and arrange bread slices on a serving platter.
1. Spread the moong tapenade mixture over each slice.
1. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil and sprinkle some crushed red chilies over them.
1. Serve Indian Style Tapenade with hot Masala Chai for your tea time break or you can also serve it for breakfast.
