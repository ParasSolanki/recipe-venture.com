### Ingredients

- Water - as required.
- 4 teaspoon Anardana Powder (Pomegranate Seed Powder).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 pinch Sugar.
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 2-3 Green Chillies.
- 1/2 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).

### Instructions

1. To prepare the Kashmiri Anardan Chetin Recipe (Pomegranate Seeds Chutney), chop onion, swash coriander and mint leaves thoroughly with water.
1. Chop them finely.
1. In a Mixer jar, add pomegranate seeds, chopped onions, mint leaves, coriander leaves, cumin seeds, green chilies, chat masala, salt, and sugar.
1. Add water and grind them into a fine paste.
1. Add lemon juice and combine them well.
1. Check for salt and adjust accordingly.
1. Serve Anardan Chetin Recipe (Pomegranate Seeds Chutney) as a dip for homemade idlis.
