### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Chow chow (chayote squash) - cut into cubes.
- 1 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 2 teaspoons White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chow Chow Milagu Kootu Recipe, wash and peel the outer skin of the chayote quash with a peeler.
1. Cut it into cubes and keep aside.
1. Wash and soak the moong dal in water for 10 minutes.
1. In a small pan, dry roast the peppercorns, cumin seeds, urad dal and grated coconut till they turn golden and fragrant in medium heat.
1. Grind it to a coarse mix using a mixer grinder and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a pressure cooker, add the mustard seeds, let it crackle.
1. Once the mustard seeds have crackled add the asafoetida, curry leaves, red chillies and let it splutter.
1. Add the chayote squash and saute for a minute.
1. Drain and add the moong dal, and saute for a minute.
1. Add the ground pepper mix, turmeric powder, salt to taste, 1.
1. 5 cup of water and mix well.
1. Put the lid on and pressure cook for 2 whistles on medium heat.
1. Once the pressure is completely released, remove the lid, mix well and its ready to serve.
1. Serve the Chow Chow Milagu Kootu Recipe along with Phulkas or with Jeera Rasam, Steamed Rice and Elai Vadam for the weeknight dinner.
