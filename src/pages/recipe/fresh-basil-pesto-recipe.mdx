### Ingredients

-  1 / 2 cup parmesan cheese - grated.
-  6 cloves garlic - finely chopped.
-  1/4 cup walnuts - bake.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  2 tbsp olive oil.
- 250 grams Basil leaves - take out the stem.
-  grind 1 tbsp whole black pepper - grind.

### Instructions

1. To make the Basil Pesto Recipe, first prepare all the ingredients.
1. Now add walnuts, garlic, basil, salt, pepper to a blender and grind them.
1. When the blender is running, add olive oil to the bitch and grind again to make a paste.
1. Now add parmesan to it and make a paste again for 1 minute.
1. Your pesto is ready.
