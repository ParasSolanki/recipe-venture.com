### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 2 tablespoons Parsley leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Lemon zest.
- 3 Ripe Bananas - sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut.
- 1/2 cup Ricotta Cheese.
- 1 clove Garlic - peeled and crushed.
- 1 Lemon juice.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Coconut & Ricotta Dip with Banana Chips recipe, preheat oven to 250 degree Fahrenheit.
1. Slice bananas thinly and arrange the slices in the baking pan lined with parchment paper.
1. Combine juice of one lemon with little amount of water.
1. Coat each slice with lemon water.
1. Bake bananas until the moisture is absorbed completely for about 1 hour.
1. Check and continue baking for about 10 minutes if not done.
1. Check the bananas during half way and turn them for even baking.
1. The chips are done once they are crisp and moisture is absorbed.
1. The chips will become crisp once they cool down.
1. Allow the chips to cool down.
1. Meanwhile, prepare coconut ricotta dip.
1. Combine all the ingredients given for the dip and grind them into a fine paste using a mixer/mortar.
1. Serve baked crispy banana chips with coconut & ricotta dip.
