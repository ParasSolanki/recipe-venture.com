### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 2 tablespoons Muesli - Saffola Nutty Crunch.
- Water - for cooking.
- 1 tablespoon Chia Seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Badam (Almond) - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 2 Prunes - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Strawberries - chopped.
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - low fat.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Strawberry Smoothie Bowl with chia seeds and Muesli Recipe by combining oats, honey, yogurt and water in a saucepan.
1. Cook them for about 5 minutes till the oats get cooked.
1. To this stir in the prunes and almonds and mix well.
1.  Transfer the smoothie to a serving bowl.
1. Top it up with some chopped strawberries, Muesli and crunchy chia seeds.
1. Serve the Strawberry Smoothie Bowl with chia seeds and Muesli Recipe as your favorite Breakfast in the morning.
