### Ingredients

-  1 green chili.
-  1 onion - finely chopped.
-  1/2 teaspoon sugar.
-  2 cloves garlic.
-  1 teaspoon turmeric Powder.
-  1 tablespoon oil.
-  coriander - chopped.
-  1 cup tomato puree.
- 1 cup cup cheese.
-  1-1 / 2 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  1 teaspoon celery.
-  2 long.
-  salt.
-  1 inch ginger.
-  5 cashews.
-  1 teaspoon garam masala powder.
-  1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To make Amritsari Ajwaini Paneer, first of all, add onion, garlic, ginger and green chilli in a mixer grinder and make a paste.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now in a mixer grinder add some water with cashew nuts and make a paste.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now add celery and long pieces in a hamadasta and grind it.
1. Heat oil in a pan.
1. Add onion paste to it and cook for 3 minutes.
1. Now add turmeric powder, ground spice and garam masala and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Now add tomato puree, coriander powder, red chili powder and salt.
1. Cover the pan and cook it for 4 to 5 minutes.
1. After this add cashew paste, sugar and cook for 1 minute.
1. Now add cottage cheese and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve Amritsari Ajwaini Paneer with Onion Thepla and Coriander Mint Chutney.
