### Ingredients

- 4 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 2 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.
- 1 cup Chicken breasts - cut into strips.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Rice - washed.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped for garnish.
- 2 cloves Cloves (Laung).
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- ¼ cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 2 teaspoons Garam masala powder.
- 6 cloves Garlic - crushed.
- 3 1/2 cups Water.
- 2 teaspoons Tandoori chicken masala - Everest brand.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped for garnish.
- 2 teaspoons Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- 2 Green Chilli - slit length ways.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 tablespoons Butter.
- ¼ cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 Star anise.
- 2 teaspoons Cumin powder (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the One Pot Butter Chicken Khichdi, wash the chicken well, drain and cut into long strips.
1. In a pressure cooker, heat butter on low flame, add in the whole spices including bay leaf, cumin seeds, cardamom, star anise, cloves and cinnamon.
1.  Add in the ginger, garlic and green chilies and saute for a few seconds, now add in the sliced onions.
1. Continue to cook the sliced onions until they turn translucent, then add in the chopped tomatoes and tomato puree, cover and cook for a couple of minutes.
1. After a couple of minutes mix in the spices- red chilli powder, tandoori chicken masala, cumin powder, garam masala, and coriander powder.
1. Cover and let it cook for about 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Open the lid,turn the flame to low, add in the yogurt and chicken pieces, and saute this butter chicken mixture.
1. Let it cook for about 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Finally, add the washed and drained rice, water and chopped coriander, mint leaves and close the pressure cooker.
1. Pressure cook the One Pot Butter Chicken Khichdi for 3 whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Serve the Butter Chicken Khichdi hot along with a dollop of butter or gheeServe this One Pot Butter Chicken Khichdi Recipe with Tomato Onion Tadka Raita and Papad for a special dinner.
