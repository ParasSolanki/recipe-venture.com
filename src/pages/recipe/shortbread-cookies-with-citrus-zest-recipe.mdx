### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Butter.
- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 pinch Kosher salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1/4 cup Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon zest.
- 1 teaspoon Orange Zest (Rind).

### Instructions

1. To start preparing Shortbread Cookies With Citrus Zest Recipe, cream together the butter and sugar, saving 2 teaspoons for later use, and form a fluffy mix.
1. Mix the dry ingredients, flour, baking powder and kosher salt, add in the creamed sugar and butter to it.
1. Knead to form a crumbly dough and let it refrigerate for at least 20 minutes.
1. After 20 minutes, knead the dough along with orange zest, lemon zest and sugar, roll out the dough and using a cookie cutter, cut out shapes of your choice.
1. Bake in an Oven at 375 degrees Fahrenheit on a greased baking sheet for 8-10 minutes rotating the baking tray halfway in between if needed, for even cooking.
1. Serve Shortbread Cookies With Citrus Zest Recipe with tea or milk during teatime, pair it up with Iced Tea Recipe and Corn and Paneer Baked Samosa Recipe or just binge whenever you are hungry.
