### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Mixed Herbs (Dried).
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 cups Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- 1-1/2 cups Classic Mayonnaise (With Egg).
- 2 kg Potatoes (Aloo) - peeled and cut to bite size.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Vinegar.
- 2 cups Vegetable stock.
- 2 Stalks Spring Onion Greens - or Roasted Peanuts for garnish (optional).
- 1/2 cup Fresh cream.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Herbed Potato Salad Recipe,eat a heavy bottomed pan on medium heat; pour in the vegetable broth, add in the garlic,salt and potatoes, and cook them until the potatoes become soft.
1. Once the potatoes soften, drain them and keep aside.
1. Spread them out on a paper lined tray, so that the excess water dries up and they don`t get sticky and mashed.
1. In another bowl, mix in all the remianing ingredients, (except the peanuts and a few coriander leaves ) to make a the salad dressing.
1. Once the potatoes are cooled down, stir in the salad dressing and garnish with coriander and spring onion greens or peanuts.
1. You can also add in some cayenne pepper to make it more spicy.
1. Finally, refrigerate the herbed potato salad until chilled and serve.
1. Serve Herbed Potato Salad Recipe with Roasted Tomato and Pumpkin Soup or Beetroot Gazpacho Soup Recipe for a light dinner.
