### Ingredients

- 1 pinch Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Gram flour (besan).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 150 grams Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - chopped.
- 3/4 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to atste.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 2 cups Water.
- 2 teaspoons Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Ullikadala Pulusu Recipe | Spring Onion Curry, in a pan, dry roast coriander seeds, mustard seeds, cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds separately and grind into a fine powder.
1. Keep aside once they are roasted till aromatic.
1. In the same pan, add oil, once it is hot on medium low heat, temper with mustard seeds.
1. Once they splutter, add cumin seeds, red chillies, hing and add spring onions (white part+green part) cut into small pieces.
1. Fry until they turn soft and reduce in size on a low heat.
1. Add about 2 cups of water and bring to boil.
1. Add salt, turmeric, tamarind puree and let it boil for about 2 minutes.
1. In a bowl, mix gramflour and a tablespoon of prepared powder with little water without lumps and add to the boiling mixture.
1. Let it boil for another 2 to 3 minutes and serve.
1. Serve Ullikadala Pulusu Recipe | Spring Onion Curry on a winter day and serve with hot steamed rice and ghee, along with Andhra Gongura Pachadi Recipe, and Andhra Style Kakarakaya Vepudu Recipe (Bitter Gourd Stir Fry).
