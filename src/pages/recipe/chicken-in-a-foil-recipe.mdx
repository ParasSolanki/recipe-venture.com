### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Dessicated Coconut.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 3 tablespoons Red Chilli sauce.
- 2 tablespoons Cashew nuts - ground to paste.
- 6 Chicken legs - washed and cleaned.
- 1-1/2 tablespoons Poppy seeds - ground to paste.
- 3/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 3 tablespoons Tandoori masala.
- 1 cup Tomato Ketchup.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 Lemon - juiced.
- 1/2 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chicken In A Foil Recipe, marinate chicken legs in a mixing bowl by adding in the ginger garlic paste , tandoori masala, curd, red chilli sauce, salt and lemon juice.
1. Mix well to coat the chicken and let the chicken legs rest for about 30 minutes.
1. Preheat oven at 400 Deg F for about 10 minutes.
1. Arrange the chicken pieces on a sheet of foil, close the foil around the chicken to make a parcel and bake for 25 minutes.
1. In a pan add oil, tomato puree, chilli powder, poppy seed paste, dry coconut powder, cashew paste, cumin powder and cook till the mixture is cooked and comes together.
1. When the chicken is done in oven, mix it with the prepared sauce.
1. Serve Chicken In A Foil Recipe along with Cheese Garlic Naan Recipe, Pickled Onions, Pakki Mutton Biryani Recipe and Burani Raita for a complete Sunday lunch.
