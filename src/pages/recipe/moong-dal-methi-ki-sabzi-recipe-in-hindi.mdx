### Ingredients

-  4 green chillies - chopped.
-  3/4 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  1 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  2 tablespoons oil.
-  1 Teaspoon ginger - grated.
-  1 onion - thinly sliced.
-  1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  asafoetida - a pinch.
- 1/2 cup green moong dal - soaked for 3 hours.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin powder.
-  2 tomatoes - finely chopped.
-  1/2 cup Water.
-  2 cups fenugreek - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make Moong Dal Methi vegetable, first heat oil in a pan.
1. Add cumin seeds and let it cook for 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add green chillies, asafoetida and let it cook for a few seconds, now add onions and cook till the onions become soft.
1. After the onion becomes soft, add ginger, tomatoes and let it cook for 2 minutes.
1. Now add moong dal, turmeric powder, coriander powder and cook for 1 minute.
1. Now add 1/2 cup of water, salt and mix it all.
1. Cover the pan and let it cook for 7 to 8 minutes.
1. Add the fenugreek and cook for another 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve fenugreek moong dal with Rajasthani kadhi and phulka for dinner.
