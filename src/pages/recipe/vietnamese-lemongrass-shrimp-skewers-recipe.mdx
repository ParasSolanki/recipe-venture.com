### Ingredients

- 500 grams Shrimps.
- 6 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum).
- Nutralite Garlic & Oregano Spread.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 Fresh Red chillies - slit.
- 1/2 cup Rice flour.
- 6 Lemongrass - stalks.
- 10 Shallots - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 2 teaspoon Fish sauce.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vietnamese Lemongrass Shrimp Skewers Recipe, you will have to clean the prawns and de-vien them.
1. Wash it thoroughly again.
1. Add them into a mixer and give it a blitz to make it a paste but it still has to remain quite chunky.
1. Heat a skillet with Nutralite Garlic & Oregano Spread, add chopped garlic and saute till softens, add in chopped onions and sprinkle salt and saute till they turn golden brown and is caramelised.
1. Add chopped red capsicum, and saute till they turn soft.
1. Once done add the sautéed vegetables into the ground shrimp mixture.
1. Add chopped coriander leaves, fish sauce, soy sauce, salt to taste, rice flour and give it a mix.
1. Divide the mixture into 6 portions.
1. In order to skewer them on the lemon grass.
1. Take a lemongrass stick and take one portion of the shrimp mixture and shape it as a lollipop at one end.
1. Do the same for the rest.
1. Heat skillet with on medium heat, drizzle oil and place the skewers and roast the Lemongrass Shrimp Skewers evenly till it is golden brown.
1. The shrimp will take about 10 minutes to cook well.
1. Once done, serve hot.
1. Serve the Vietnamese Lemongrass Shrimp Skewers Recipe along with Vietnamese Rice Noodle Salad and Chicken Bánh Mì Recipe for your weekend party.
