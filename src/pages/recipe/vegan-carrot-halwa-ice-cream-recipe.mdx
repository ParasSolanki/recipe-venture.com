### Ingredients

- 2-1/2 cup Coconut milk.
- 2-1/2 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - finely grated.
- 3 teaspoons Vanilla Extract.
- 10 Cashew nuts - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 7 Pistachios - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Sugar.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Vegan Carrot Halwa Ice Cream Recipe, prep up with chopping and grating the ingredients and extracting the coconut milk required.
1. Mix shredded carrot, coconut milk, water in a thick bottom pan.
1. On a low to medium flame, bring the whole mixture to a boil and then simmer.
1. The mixture will start to reduce a bit.
1. Then add sugar, cardamom powder and chopped pistachios, cashews.
1. Stir well and continue to simmer.
1. Stir in a couple of times to ensure even cooking.
1. The liquid will reduce and thicken.
1. Switch off the flame when completely cooked and keep the vegan carrot halwa aside to cool.
1. Transfer mixture into a freezer-safe container, layer the surface of the mixture with parchment paper.
1. Press gently and seal the container with lid.
1. Freeze for at least 4 hours or overnight until it is firm.
