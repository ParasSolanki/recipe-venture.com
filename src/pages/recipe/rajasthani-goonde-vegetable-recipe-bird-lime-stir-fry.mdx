### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- Water - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 500 grams Gunde (bird lime).
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Rajasthani Gunde Ki Sabzi, firstly boil the gunde in a saucepan with water, drain the water, remove the seeds and keep it aside to cool down for about an hour.
1. Once it is done, heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, fennel seeds, fenugreek seeds and hing.
1. Let it cook for about a minute and then add the boiled gundas and mix it properly.
1. Next, add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, amchur powder and the required salt.
1. Give it a stir and let it cook for about 3 to 4 minutes.
1. After 3 to 4 minutes, switch off the stove and garnish it with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve Rajasthani Gunde Ki Sabzi with Panchmel Dal and hot Phulkas for a perfect meal.
