### Ingredients

- 1 cup White Chocolate.
- 1/3 cup Condensed Milk.
- 1 tablespoon Butter (unsalted).
- 1/2 cup Pistachios - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making White Chocolate Pistachio Bars Recipe | White Chocolate Pistachio Fudge, scrape in the chocolate in a microwave safe bowl.
1. To this add in condensed milk and butter.
1. Microwave it on medium for 1 minute, the chocolate will now start to melt.
1. With the help of a spatula, mix everything well.
1. If the chocolate is still chunky, melt in microwave for another 30 seconds.
1. To the melted chocolate, now add in the chopped pistachios and mix well.
1. Prepare a tin lined with parchment paper.
1. Now pour the melted chocolate and refrigerate for an hour.
1. After refrigeration the chocolate would have set .
1. With a serrated knife chop into any desired shapes or break into bars and serve White Chocolate Pistachio Bars Recipe | White Chocolate Pistachio Fudge as a dessert with hot coffee.
