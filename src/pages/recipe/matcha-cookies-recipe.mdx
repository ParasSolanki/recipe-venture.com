### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 tablespoons Matcha powder - (japanese Green tea).
- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/2 cup Butter - I have used salted butter.
- 1/2 cup Caster Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Butter - for greasing.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Matcha Cookies Recipe, cream the butter and powdered sugar in a steel mixing bowl with the help of a hand mixer.
1. Once that is done, add in the vanilla essence and mix well till combined.
1. Sift in the all purpose flour and Matcha powder in a bowl and add it to the wet ingredients.
1. Mix well to combine.
1. Once all the ingredients are incorporated, take the mixture out of the bowl and put it in a cling film to refrigerate for around 1 hour.
1. After and hour, take it out of the refrigerator, make even sized balls (this recipe made 14) out of the mixture and place them on a baking tray greased with butter.
1. Preheat the oven to 160 degree Celsius.
1. Place the Matcha Cookies in the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes or until the cookies start turning slightly brown from the edges.
1. Once done remove from the oven and allow them to cool.
1. Serve Matcha Cookies Recipe along with  Irani Chai Recipe for evening.
