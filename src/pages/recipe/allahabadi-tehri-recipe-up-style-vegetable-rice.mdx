### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 4 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 2 teaspoons Lemon juice.
- 2 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 3 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 Black cardamom.
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 4 cloves Garlic - roughly crushed.
- 2 tablespoons Mustard oil.
- 1 cup Mixed vegetables - mix of potato.
- 1 inch Ginger - roughy crushed.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1-1/2 cup Basmati rice.
- 4 Cloves (Laung).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Allahabadi Tehri Recipe (UP Style Vegetable Rice), wash and soak the basmati rice for 10 minutes in water.
1. Cut all the vegetables, namely potatoes, beans, cauliflower and carrots to similar size and keep aside.
1. Heat mustard oil in a wide pan, and let it smoke.
1. Add the asafoetida, whole garam masalas - cinnamon, bay leaf, green and black cardamoms, pepper corns and cloves and let the oil become fragrant.
1. Now, add the sliced onions and saute until translucent.
1. Now, roughly pound the ginger garlic using a mortar and pestle and add it to the pan, and saute until the raw smell goes away.
1. Add the green chillies, turmeric, red chilli, cumin and coriander powder and saute for a minute.
1. Add the cut vegetables and mix well till they turn a bit golden.
1. Now, add the whisked yoghurt and saute until the mixute comes together.
1. Add in the drained rice, 4 cups of water, season with salt and cover it with a lid to cook for 20 minutes untouched.
1. Once its cooked, add in the ghee, lemon juice, gently mix everything together and switch off the flame.
1. Serve the Allahabadi Tehri Recipe (UP Style Vegetable Rice) along with Mirchi Ka Salan and Burani Raita for the weeknight dinner.
