### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 1 cup Tofu.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 2 tablespoon Green Chilli Sauce.
- 2 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 1 cup Celery - chopped.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Stir Fried Tofu and Celery Recipe by mixing the cornstarch, soy, pepper powder, salt in a bowl with 1/4 cup water.
1. Mix well.
1. Heat a flat skillet with oil, once it is hot, dip the cubed tofu into the cornstarch batter and carefully fry them on both the sides till it is golden brown.
1. Once done, keep them aside.
1. In the same skillet add the onion and ginger garlic paste and saute till it is translucent.
1. Then add in the celery and the fried tofu along with the sauces and the seasonings mentioned in the list.
1. Check for seasoning.
1. Keep stir frying till the celery is cooked well and not too soft.
1. Serve the Stir Fried Tofu and Celery Recipe along with Quick Cauliflower Fried Rice Recipe to have a good meal for your Sunday lunch.
