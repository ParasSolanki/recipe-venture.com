### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 2 Kashmiri dry red chillies - broken.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 3 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 8 Curry leaves.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 250 grams Green beans (French Beans) - cut into 1/2 inch pieces.
- 3 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.
- Sugar - a pinch.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Goan Style French Beans Foogath Recipe, wash the french beans, remove the string and chop them into 1/2 inch piece.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, dry red chillies and green chillies.
1. Saute for about 1 to 2 minutes.
1. After 1 to 2 minutes, add onions.
1. Saute for 3 to 4 minutes till onion turns translucent.
1. Then add beans, salt and a pinch of sugar.
1. Saute.
1. Add around 1/4 cup of water, cover and cook for 8 to 10 minutes.
1. Don't over cook to maintain the crunchiness.
1. When beans is cooked, finally add grated coconut, toss and switch off.
1. Goan Style French beans foogath is ready to relish.
1. Serve Goan Style French Beans Foogath Recipe along with Tomato Onion Sambar and Steamed Rice with a dollop of ghee.
