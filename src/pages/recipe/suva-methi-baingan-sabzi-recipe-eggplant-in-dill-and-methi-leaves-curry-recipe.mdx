### Ingredients

- 2 Tomatoes - chopped roughly.
- 1/4 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 5 Small Brinjal (Baingan / Eggplant).
- 1 cup Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 3 Green Chillies - chopped small or minced.
- 3 cloves Garlic - minced or crushed.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 cup Dill leaves.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Suva Methi Baingan Sabzi Recipe ( Eggplant in Dill and Methi leaves Curry Recipe) by heating pressure with oil.
1. Crackle the mustard seeds, cumin seeds, fennel seeds and hing.
1. Add in the tomatoes, ginger and green chillies.
1. Sauté for few seconds.
1. Then add in the aubergine, dill leaves and dry fenugreek leaves.
1. Sprinkle salt and check for season.
1. Add ½ cup water and pressure cook for 2 whistle.
1. Release the pressure naturally.
1. Serve the Suva Methi Baingan Sabzi Recipe ( Eggplant in Dill and Methi leaves Curry Recipe) along with Tawa Paratha and Hot Steam Rice.
