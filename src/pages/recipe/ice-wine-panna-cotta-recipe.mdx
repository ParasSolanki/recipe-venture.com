### Ingredients

- 3/4 cup Sugar.
- 1/2 cup Ice wine - (or any other sweet dessert wine).
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- Blackberries - or any berries for garnish.
- 2-1/2 cups Heavy whipping cream.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Ice Wine Panna Cotta Recipe, pour cream and sugar into a saucepan and heat on low heat just until the sugar dissolves; stir occasionally, not to let it char.
1. Once the sugar has dissolved, take the cream off the heat and stir in the lemon juice and wine.
1. Wait for about 10 minutes, pour the dessert mixture into serving glasses and chill overnight.
1. Once the dessert is chilled, top each glass with fresh fruit if you like,  just before serving.
1. Serve Ice Wine Panna Cotta Recipe along with Cucumbertini Recipe (A Refreshing Cucumber Cocktail) or Watermelon-Cranberry Mocktail Recipe during cocktail parties.
