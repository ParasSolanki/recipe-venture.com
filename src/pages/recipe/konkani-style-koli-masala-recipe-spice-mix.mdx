### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 2 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 2 Star anise.
- 1 tablespoon Nutmeg powder.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 Kalpasi flower (Stone flower).
- 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 tablespoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 5 Byadagi Dried Chillies.
- 5 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 tablespoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 3 Mace (Javitri).
- 10 Dry Red Chilli - (sankeshwari dry red chilli).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 tablespoon Poppy seeds.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Konkani Style Koli Masala Recipe by dry roasting all the spices on a flat skillet.
1. Cool it down and then grind them into a smooth mixture and use it for various recipes.
1. You can double the quantity and store it in the jar for future use.
1. Use the Konkani Style Koli Masala Recipe in Kala Chana Cooked In Koli Masala Recipe (Maharashtrian Koli Style Kala Chana Recipe).
