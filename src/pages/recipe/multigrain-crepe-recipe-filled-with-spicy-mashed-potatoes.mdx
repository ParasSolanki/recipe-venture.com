### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- 1 cup Bajra Flour ( Pearl Millet).
- 3 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- Salt - as per taste.
- 2 cups Whole Wheat Flour.
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Ragi Flour (Finger Millet/ Nagli).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 4 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled and mashed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Multigrain Crepe Recipe Filled With Spicy Mashed Potatoes, first get all the ingredients ready, including boiling and mashing the potatoes.
1. In a large mixing bowl, add the flour and a pinch of salt, black pepper powder and water and make smooth batter.
1. The batter should be thick and yet pouring consistency, similar to a dosa batter.
1. Keep the crepe batter aside.
1. Next in heavy bottomed pan, heat a teaspoon of oil over medium heat.
1. Add the cumin seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Add the green chilies and chopped onion and saute until the onion gets slightly caramelized.
1. Once the onion gets brown, add the mashed potatoes, red chili powder and salt.
1. Stir to combine all the ingredients well.
1. Check the salt and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Finally stir in the chopped coriander leaves and turn off the heat.
1. The next step is the make the crepe.
1. Heat a nonstick crepe skillet over medium heat.
1. Pour a ladleful of the crepe batter on the pan and swirl the pan to spread the batter around.
1. Crepes cook very quickly, so once you notice the top is getting cooked, flip over to cook on the other side for a few seconds.
1. Remove from the skillet and place it on the platter.
1. Proceed the same way to make the remaining crepes.
1. Once done, place a spoonful of the potato filling in the center and roll the crepe and serve.
1. Serve the Multigrain Crepe Filled With Spicy Mashed Potatoes along with a Yogurt Dip or Mexican Salsa for breakfast, tea party snack, appetizer or even a quick weeknight dinner.
