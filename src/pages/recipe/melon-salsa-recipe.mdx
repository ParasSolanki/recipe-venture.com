### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- Salt - as required.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1 English Cucumber - cut into cubes.
- 1 cup Honey dew melon - cut into cubes.
- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Melon Salsa recipe, add all the ingredients together in a large bowl.
1. Adjust the seasoning and give it a gentle mix.
1. Once mixed properly, it is ready to be served.
1. Serve Melon Salsa with chips, nachos, whole-wheat lavash or throw it into your wraps, burgers, tacos or burritos to make it a summer meal.
