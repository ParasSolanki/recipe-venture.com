### Ingredients

-  1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds.
-  1 capsicum (green) - chopped.
-  1 teaspoon turmeric powder .
-  1 tablespoon jaggery.
-  1 teaspoon cumin.
-  2 tomatoes - chopped.
-  1 green chili - finely chopped.
-  1/2 teaspoon mustard.
-  salt - as per taste.
- 1 kg pumpkin.
-  1 teaspoon oil.
-  1 teaspoon sambar powder.

### Instructions

1. To make pumpkin capsicum vegetable, first heat oil in a pan.
1. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds and fenugreek seeds.
1. Allow to cook for 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add green chili, capsicum and let it cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Now add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, tomato, pumpkin, sambar powder, salt and jaggery.
1. Mix and let it cook for 2 minutes.
1. Allow the pumpkin to cook.
1. After cooking, turn off the gas and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve the Pumpkin Capsicum vegetable with Phalguni Dal and Tawa Paratha.
