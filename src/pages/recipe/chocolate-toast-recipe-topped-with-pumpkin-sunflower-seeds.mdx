### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Butter - softened to apply on bread.
- 1/2 cup Dark chocolate - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower seeds.
- 5 teaspoon Pumpkin seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Flax seeds.
- 5 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh cream.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chocolate Toast With Toasted Seeds Recipe, firstly trim off the edges of bread and then cut the slices diagonally into triangles and keep it aside.
1. Place a saucepan on low heat and add in the fresh cream, dark chocolate chunks.
1. Keep stirring until the dark chocolate chunks melt and you get a creamy consistency.
1. Once all the dark chocolate melts, stir in the vanilla extract and turn off the heat.
1. Toast the bread slices using a bread toaster until it turns crisp and golden.
1. Apply some butter over these toasts and keep aside.
1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and roast the Pumpkin Seeds, Sunflower Seeds, Sesame Seeds and Flax Seeds for about two minutes and keep aside.
1. Place the toasted bread slices over a platter; spread the melted dark chocolate on to the toasted bread slices and sprinkle the toasted nuts on top.
1. The Chocolate Toast With Toasted Seeds are now ready to be served! Serve Chocolate Toast With Toasted Seeds along with a hot cup of Coffee for breakfast or an evening snack.
1. You can also give this to your kids for their evening snacks.
