### Ingredients

- 1 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - soaked for 8 hours.
- 1/4 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Garlic - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Healthy Kabuli Chana Pakora Recipe first prep all the ingredients and keep aside.
1. Take the soaked kabuli chana, drain all the water and pulse it in the mixer grinder until you get a coarse mixture.
1. Make sure that you do not grind it into a smooth paste.
1. Remove the pulsed kabuli chana in a bowl and add in all the other ingredients like chopped onions, green chillies, coriander and mint leaves, fennel seeds, chilli powder and salt.
1.  Heat a Kuzhi paniyaram pan over medium heat, add 1/4 teaspoon of oil in each of the cavities of the pan.
1. Spoon a tablespoon of Kabuli Chana Pakora mixture into each of the cavities of the pan.
1. Cook Kabuli Chana Pakora on medium high heat and turn them once they turn golden brown on one side.
1. This will take about three minutes.
1. Cook the other side of the pakoras till they turn golden brown as well.
1. Make sure the inside of the Kabuli Chana Pakora is well cooked and the outer covering is nice and crisp.
1. Drain the well fried Kabuli Chana Pakoras on an absorbent paper and serve hot.
1. Serve the Kabuli Chana Pakora as high tea snacks, or after school snacks to your kids along with Green Chutney Recipe - Coriander Mint Chutney Recipe or Date and Tamarind Chutney and Ginger Cardamom Chai.
