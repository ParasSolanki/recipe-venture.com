### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1/2 Watermelon - cut into cubes.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - a small bunch.
- 2 Cucumbers - peeled and diced.
- 1 Tabasco Original - Hot Sauce - drizzle over as per preference.
- Salt - as per taste.
- 1/2 Musk Melon - cut into cubes.
- Lemon juice - from 2 lemons.
- 2 teaspoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Savory Melon Salad Recipe, we will first cube or ball the melons to bite size pieces.
1. Add the cut melons into a large salad mixing bowl.
1. Our next step is to combine all the ingredients for the dressing.
1. Add the dressing ingredients into a small bowl and using a fork whisk all the ingredients together until well combined.
1. You can prepare the dressing ahead of time and store it in a small jar in the refrigerator and use as desired.
1. Once the salad dressing is ready, pour it over the cut melons and give the salad a good stir.
1. Stir in the chopped mint leaves and feta cheese.
1. Serve Savory Melon Salad along with Grilled Sub Sandwich With Paneer & Roasted Vegetables for a light summer lunch.
