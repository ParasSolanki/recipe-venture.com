### Ingredients

- 1 recipe All Purpose Pie Crust.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 2 Tomato - cut into round slices.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Gouda cheese - grated (or sharp cheddar).
- 1 cup Mozzarella cheese - grated.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Tomato Basil Pasta Sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Mixed Herbs (Dried) - italian.
- 1 cup Green seedless Grapes - halved..
- 1/4 cup Black olives - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To prepare the pie crust, click here to see the recipe of easy all-purpose pie crust.
1. Keep in the fridge wrapped in the cling foil while you prepare the filling.
1. Next preheat the Oven to 200 C.
1. Grease a 10 inch pie tin or baking sheet with little oil or line with parchment paper.
1. Roll out the pie dough on a lightly dusted surface to about 10 by 7 inch long rectangle.
1. Place the rolled out pastry in the pie tin and remove the excess pastry from the edges.
1. Now with the spoon spread the tomato-basil sauce over the pie leaving an inch margin around the edges of the tart.
1. Arrange the tomato slices neatly over the tomato sauce.
1. Sprinkle grated gouda, mozzarella, grapes and olives over the tomato slices.
1. Next fold the edges of the tart so that the tart gets sealed around the edges.
1. Drizzle some extra virgin olive oil, salt, herbs, chili flakes over the filling and bake for about 30-35 minutes in the preheated oven until the pastry is crisp and golden from outside and the cheese is melted.
1. Transfer the Savory Tart Recipe with Grape and Gouda Cheese on a cooling rack and let it rest for 10 minutes or so before serving.
1. Serve Savory Tart with Grape and Gouda Cheese for a special weekend dinner with friends and family.
