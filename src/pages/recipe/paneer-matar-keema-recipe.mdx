### Ingredients

- 1 cup Green peas (Matar) - boiled and slightly mashed.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few sprigs.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- Salt - to taste.
- Water - as required.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - boiled and mashed.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - crumbled.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 cup Homemade tomato puree.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Paneer Matar Keema Recipe, heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, asafoetida and let it splutter.
1. Once the mustard seeds splutter, add the finely chopped onions.
1. Cook them till they turn soft and translucent.
1. Once the onions are soft, add the ginger garlic paste and let it cook for about 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes add the finely chopped tomatoes and tomato puree.
1. Cover with lid and let it cook it for about 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Next, add the spices including red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder and salt.
1. Let it cook for 5 minutes.
1. Now add the mashed potato, crumbled paneer and 1/4 cup of water.
1. Mix everything properly and let it cook for 6 to 8 minutes.
1. After 6 to 8 minutes, add the boiled green peas, mix well, cover the pan and let the Green Peas Gravy With Vegetables cook for another 3 to 5 minutes.
1. Once it is done, garnish Matar Keema recipe with coriander leaves and serve hot.
1. Serve Paneer Matar Keema Recipe along with Dal Palak, Lauki Raita and Phulka for an everyday meal with your family.
