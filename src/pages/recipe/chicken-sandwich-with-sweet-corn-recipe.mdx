### Ingredients

- 2 Iceberg lettuce - as needed.
- Whole Black Peppercorns - freshly ground to taste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 3 tablespoon Sweet corn - boiled till tender.
- 1 Onion - sliced to rings (about 5 slices).
- 1 Tomato - sliced (4-5 slices).
- 4 tablespoon Classic Mayonnaise (With Egg) - (garlic flavour tastes best).
- 2 Hot Dog Bread.
- 3 tablespoon Tomato Ketchup.
- 1 Chicken breasts - pressure cooked till tender (3 whistles).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chicken Sandwich With Sweet Corn Recipe, thaw the sweet corn if using frozen corn, or boil them till soft.
1. Also pressure cook chicken for about 3 whistles or till tender and get prep with other ingredients as well.
1. Shred cooked chicken breast into small pieces.
1. In a large mixing bowl, smear garlic mayonnaise, ketchup, black pepper and salt as per taste in the shredded chicken & sweet corn and mix till well combined.
1. Halve the hot dog bread roll.
1. Arrange the lettuce leaves, onion rings and tomatoes inside the roll add this mixture over the lettuce, onions & tomato and close the sandwich.
1. Enjoy Chicken Sandwich With Sweet Corn Recipe as it is, or grilled further to taste.
1. Serve with Oven Baked Sweet Potato Fries for lunch.
