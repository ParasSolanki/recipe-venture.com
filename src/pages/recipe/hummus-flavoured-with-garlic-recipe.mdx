### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Dark soy sauce.
- 2 tablespoons Parsley leaves - fresh.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 1 Garlic - head.
- 2 cups Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - boiled.
- 1 tablespoon Tahini - (sesame paste).
- 2 tablespoons Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Hummus Flavoured With Garlic recipe, preheat oven to 215 C.
1. Remove the outside skin from the garlic head without separating the cloves.
1. Slice off the top 1/2 inch.
1. Wrap in a small square of foil and roast until the garlic is very soft, about 40 minutes.
1. Let the garlic cool down slightly.
1. Separate the cloves and peel.
1. Add the garlic, chickpeas, lemon juice, soy sauce, tahini and water in a food processor.
1. Add water as necessary to make a fairly firm dip.
1. Grind it to a smooth paste, transfer to a small serving bowl, stir in parsley and season with salt.
1. It is now ready to be served.
1. Serve Hummus Flavoured With Garlic along with Pita Bread and Baba Ganoush for your tea time break.
