### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 500 grams Brinjal (Baingan / Eggplant) - small green seedless variety cut in long slices.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Galangal - or Ginger.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - julienne for garnish.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1/4 cup Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - dry roasted.
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Aubergine in Spicy Peanut Sauce, cut the aubergines in long strips and soak it in water in order to avoid oxidizing further.
1. Heat a pan/Kadai in low flame, add oil, the sliced aubergine and saut√© for a minute.
1. Keep stirring it in little intervals until done.
1. In the meanwhile, make a fine paste of roasted peanuts, grated coconut, coriander leaves, green chillies, and ginger/galangal with a little water using a mixer grinder.
1. Add this paste to the cooked aubergines and add a cup of water, let it cook and thicken a bit.
1. Adjust the water as per your required consistency.
1. Season with salt, bring it to a boil and switch off the flame.
