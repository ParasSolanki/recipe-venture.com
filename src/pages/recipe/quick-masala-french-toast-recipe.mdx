### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 3 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- Salt - as per your taste.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 3 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 2 to 3 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Milk.
- Sunflower Oil - or butter as required.
- 1/4 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- 4 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Quick Masala French Toast recipe, beat the eggs in a mixing bowl.
1. Add milk, beat it again and make sure that it is mixed properly.
1. Add chilli flakes, salt and black pepper.
1. Once it is done, heat oil in a wide pan.
1. Take a bread slice, dip it in the egg mixture and put it on the pan.
1. Spread some onion, green chillies and coriander on the top of bread and pour some egg mixture on it again.
1. Turn the bread and let the other side also cooked properly.
1. Once it turns light brown in colour, take it out of the stove and it is ready to be served.
1. Serve Quick Masala French Toast on its own with a cup of hot Espresso Coffee or Masala Tea for a filling breakfast.
