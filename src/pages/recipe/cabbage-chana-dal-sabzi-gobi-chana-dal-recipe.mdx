### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1/2 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - washed and soaked.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 3 cups Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - shredded.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cabbage Chana Dal Sabzi, we need to wash and soak the chana dal in hot water for about an hour.
1. Heat a skillet with oil on medium flame, to this hot oil, add cumin seeds and mustard seeds and allow them to sizzle and splutter.
1. Next add in the curry leaves, asafoetida and the soaked and drained chana dal.
1. Add some water over the chana dal, cover and cook until the chana dal is well cooked.
1. This will take about 15 minutes.
1. Once the chana dal is cooked, open the lid, add the turmeric powder, kashmiri red chilli powder, cumin powder and garam masala powder.
1. Give it a good mix, add the slit green chillies and the shredded cabbage, cover and cook.
1. After about 5 minutes, check if the cabbage is cooked and has a slight crunch.
1. If you wish, the cabbage to be softer, cook for a few more minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Check the salt and other seasonings and adjust according to taste.
1. Once done, transfer the Cabbage Chana Dal Sabzi to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Cabbage Chana Dal Sabzi along with Bajre Ki Roti Recipe, Yellow Pumpkin Dal Recipe, Steamed Brown Rice for a wholesome Indian diabetic lunch or dinner.
