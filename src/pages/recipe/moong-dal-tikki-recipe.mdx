### Ingredients

- 1/4 Cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - grated.
- 1/2 tablespoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 6 Green Chillies.
- 1 Cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 Tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 4 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 3 tablespoons Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 tablespoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1 Inch Ginger.
- 1-1/2 tablespoons Corn flour.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 3 tablespoons Green peas (Matar) - boiled and mashed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Moong Dal Tikki Recipe, soak moong dal for a couple of hours.
1. Boil some water in a saucepan and add moong dal.
1. Boil moong dal for about five minutes on medium heat.
1. Drain water and wash dal with cold water.
1. This avoids the moong dal getting cooked any further.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Take a grinder and add dal, green chillies, coriander and ginger.
1. Grind together to a coarse paste.
1. Take a bowl, add grinded dal mixture, chopped onion, grated paneer, mashed peas, and mix everything well.
1. Add corn flour, cumin powder, chaat masala, raw mango powder and salt to taste, mix well.
1. Make flat round tikkies.
1. Heat oil in a non stick shallow fry pan, shallow fry them till golden on one side.
1. Flip and shallow fry on the otherServe Moong Dal Tikki Recipe along with Green Chutney and Khajur Imli Chutney and a cup of Masala Chai for your Monsoon evenings.
