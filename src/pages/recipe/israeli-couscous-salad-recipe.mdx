### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger - minced.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Dried Figs - sliced.
- 1/4 cup Lemon juice - freshly squeezed.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Apricots - drieds.
- 3 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - diced.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - diced.
- 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 1 teaspoon Orange Zest (Rind).
- 1/2 cup Cranberries.
- 1-1/2 cups Couscous - Israeli.
- 1 cup Pistachios - roasted.
- 2 cup Water.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1 teaspoon Lemon zest.
- 1/4 cup Fresh orange juice - freshly squeezed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Israeli Couscous Salad Recipe, place a saucepan on medium heat.
1. Add 2 cup water to it and bring it to boil.
1. Add turmeric powder to it and keep it boiling.
1. Meanwhile place a pan on medium heat.
1. Add couscous and dry roast the couscous until golden brown.
1. Once done add the couscous to the boiling water pan and mix well.
1. Let the couscous simmer on low heat for 10-15 minutes until all the water has been absorbed and the couscous is well cooked.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. Cover the pan and keep it aside for 10 minutes.
1. After 10 minutes, using a fork, fluff the couscous evenly to ensure there are no lumps.
1. To prepare the dressing for the salad, In a small mixing bowl, combine all the ingredients under 'for dressing' like cumin powder, coriander powder, lemon juice fresh orange juice, ginger, honey, olive oil and lemon zest .
1. Mix well to combine.
1. Transfer the cooked couscous to a salad mixing bowl.
1. Add all the ingredients under'for salad' to the couscous.
1. Toss all the ingredients and give the salad a good mix.
1. Drizzle the salad dressing over it and mix well.
1. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve.
1. Serve Israeli Couscous Salad Recipe with some warm soup like Roasted Cherry Tomato Soup Recipe, Lemongrass Tomato Corn Soup Recipe for a healthy weeknight dinner or lunch.
1. Israeli Couscous Salad Recipe is a great party food recipe as well.
