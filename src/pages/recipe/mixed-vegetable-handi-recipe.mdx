### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Button mushrooms - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Green beans (French Beans) - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 7 Cashew nuts.
- 1/4 cup Sweet corn.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 1/2 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - diced.
- 4 Tomatoes.
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1/2 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Cauliflower (gobi) - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 1 teaspoon Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cubed.
- 1/2 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 Lemon - juice.
- 1 Onion - 1/2 diced and 1/2 thinly sliced.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1 tablespoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 tablespoon Fresh cream.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mixed Vegetable Handi recipe, add tomatoes and cashews in a mixer grinder, puree them and keep it aside.
1. Next, heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add bay leaves, cumin seeds and cook for 15 seconds.
1. After 15 seconds, add sliced onions, green chilli, ginger, garlic and saute for about a minute.
1. After a minute, add finely sliced onions and cook till the onion turns golden brown in colour.
1. Next, add tomato puree and cook for about 3 to 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes, add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder and garam masala powder.
1. Add butter and mix everything well, let it cook for about a minute.
1. After a minute, add the diced onion, diced carrot, corn, peas, french beans, capsicum, cauliflower, button mushrooms and paneer.
1. Give it a toss and add water.
1. Add salt and cover the lid.
1. Let it cook for 10 to 12 minutes on medium flame, keep checking and stirring in between if the vegetables are cooked properly or not.
1. Meanwhile, take a small bowl, add cream and curd together.
1. Mix it well and see that no lumps are formed.
1. Once the vegetables are soft, add the cream curd mixture and mix it with the vegetables.
1. Cook Mixed Vegetable Handi on low flame for 1-2 minutes, sprinkle kauri methi, add lemon juice and mix everything nicely.
1. Once it is done, garnish Mixed Vegetable Handi with chopped coriander and mint leaves.
1. Mixed Vegetable Handi is ready to serve.
1. Serve Mixed Vegetable Handi along with Tawa Parathas and Boondi Raita for a weekday meal.
