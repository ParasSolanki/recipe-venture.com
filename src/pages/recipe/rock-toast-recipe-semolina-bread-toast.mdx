### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking the toast.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Onion powder - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Rock Toast, in a mixing bowl, combine semolina, yogurt, onion, capsicum, coriander leaves, green chilli, salt and pepper powder.
1. Adjust the topping mixture with a little water, making sure you get a spreadable consistency.
1. Let it sit aside for about 5-7 minutes.
1.  In the meanwhile, heat a griddle on medium flame, spread some oil.
1. Take a slice of bread, spread the semolina topping on it with a spoon and place the bread in a way that the topping is  facing  down on the griddle.
1. Cook this on medium-high heat for about three minutes and flip.
1. Remove from the tawa and serve hot.
