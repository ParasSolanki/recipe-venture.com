### Ingredients

- 2-3 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - cut into cubes.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- pinch Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Rice - raw.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - soaked.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Chayote - cut into cubes.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chow Chow And Carrot Kootu Recipe, grind the ingredients mentioned in the to grind table to a smooth paste using a mixer or hand blender and keep aside.
1. Heat a wide , add the bite sized cut cubes of chowchow, carrot, a pinch of turmeric powder, salt and 2 cups of water and cook until the vegetables are firmly cooked.
1. Once the vegetables are cooked, add the ground spice paste to the pan and simmer for 2-3 minutes till the kootu thickens.
1. Add water at this stage, if the kootu is too thick.
1. bring it to a boil and switch off the flame.
1. In another tadka pan, heat oil, add the mustard seeds and urad dal and let it splutter.
1. Add the curry leaves and dry red chilli saute for a minute and add this tempering to the kootu prepared.
1. Serve the Chow Chow And Carrot Kootu  Recipe with rotis or with Steamed Rice, Vengayam Thakali Kuzhambu, Beans Poriyal and Rice crackers to make an elaborate Tamil Saapad lunch menu on a weekend.
