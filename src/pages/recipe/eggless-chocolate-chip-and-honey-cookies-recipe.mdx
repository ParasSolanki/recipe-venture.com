### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Butter - at room temperature.
- 3/4 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/4 cup Caster Sugar - powdered.
- 2 tablespoons Chocolate chips - chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Eggless Chocolate Chip And Honey Cookies, add maida, powdered sugar, honey and vanilla essence in a mixing bowl.
1. Add butter little by little and knead to form a dough.
1. Do not add water.
1. Place the bowl inside the refrigerator for about 20 minutes.
1. Take it out, form 8 small balls and flatten them.
1. Add chocolate chips on top and gently press.
1. Preheat the oven at 180 degree Celsius for 10 minutes.
1. Meanwhile grease an aluminium tray with butter and place the cookies on it at equal intervals.
1. Place the tray in the central rack of the oven and bake with upper and lower rods on for 10 to 12 minutes or till the cookies are golden brown in colour.
1. Allow the cookies to cool down completely and transfer to airtight containers.
1. Serve Eggless Chocolate Chip And Honey Cookies along with Masala Tea and Grilled Rajma Masala Sandwich for a perfect tea party.
