### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon coconut oil.
-  1 teaspoon coconut oil.
-  2 tablespoons dry coconut - cut straight and thin.
-  1 sprig of green coriander - finely chopped.
-  4 cloves garlic - cut straight.
-  1 cup water.
-  1 cup coconut milk.
-  salt - flavored According.
-  1 cup rice - washed and soaked for 20 minutes.
-  4 long garlic - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make Thai Coconut Garlic Rice Recipe, first heat coconut oil in a pressure cooker.
1. Add garlic and cook for 40 seconds till it becomes light brown.
1. After the garlic turns light brown, add rice, 1 cup of water, 1 cup of coconut milk, salt, mix and close the cooker.
1. Cook till 2 cities come and turn off the gas.
1. Let the pressure drain automatically.
1. Open the cooker and drain the rice in a serving plate.
1. To make the garnish, heat coconut oil in a tempering pan.
1. Add garlic and coconut and cook till they become crisp and brown.
1. Turn off the gas Pour this mixture on the rice with green coriander and serve.
1. Serve Thai Coconut Garlic Rice Recipe with Yellow Thai Chicken Curry for dinner.
