### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Ripe Bananas - thinly sliced.
- 2 Whole Wheat Flour Tortilla.
- 1/4 cup Peaches - thinly sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 1/4 cup Fresh raspberries - roughly chopped.
- 4 tablespoon Britannia Cream Cheese - softened.
- 1/4 cup Strawberries - thinly sliced.
- 1/3 cup Granola - or mixed nuts.

### Instructions

1. To start making Sweet Mixed Fruits And Nut Breakfast Wraps, in a small bowl add all the fruits and stir in the honey, make sure fruits are coated well.
1. Warm up the Tortillas on a skillet and place it on a serving platter.
1. Spread the cream cheese on the tortillas leaving an inch from the border.
1. Arrange the prepared fruit mix one half down the center of each tortilla, sprinkle the granola.
1. Fold the sides of each tortilla and roll to make a wrap (should look like burrito).
1. Serve Sweet Mixed Fruits And Granola Breakfast Wraps for breakfast along with Spiced Pumpkin Smoothie.
