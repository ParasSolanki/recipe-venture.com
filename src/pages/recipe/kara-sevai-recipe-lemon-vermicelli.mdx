### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 2 teaspoons Coconut Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 3 Green Chillies.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 cups Semiya (Vermicelli) - cooked.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 teaspoons Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- sprig Curry leaves - A sprig of curry leaves.
- 2 tablespoons Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 1 tablespoon White Urad Dal (Split).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kara Sevai, firstly make a coarse paste of coconut, green chillies & mustard in a grinder.
1. Do not add water while grinding.
1. Heat oil in a wok/ kadai & temper it with the ingredients that are listed under ‘to temper’ table.
1. When the dal & peanuts turns golden brown, add in the turmeric powder & asafoetida.
1. Saute this for a second & add in the ground paste.
1. Roughly saute them & add in the sevai, salt & sugar.
1. Mix them well.
1. Let them cook them in low flame for 2 mins.
1. When done, add in coconut oil, coconut, lemon juice & coriander leaves.
1. Mix everything properly and serve hot.
1. Pack Kara Sevai along with Burani Raita or Tomato Onion Cucumber raita for your Lunch box.
