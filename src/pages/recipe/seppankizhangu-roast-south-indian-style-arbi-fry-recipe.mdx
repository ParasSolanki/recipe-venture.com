### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoon Rice flour.
- Salt - to taste.
- 500 grams Colocasia root (Arbi).
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- Sunflower Oil - to cook.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 teaspoon Gram flour (besan).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Seppakizhangu Roast (South Indian Style Arbi Fry) Recipe, take the arbi and pressure cook them with enough water for 1 whistle.
1. Switch off the heat and release the pressure, this will avoid the overcooking of arbi.
1. Now peel the skin of the arbi, cut each one of them into 2-3 pieces and keep it aside.
1. Take a big bowl, add all the dry ingredients except oil and mix well.
1. Now add the arbi mix it lightly to coat all the vegetable with spices.
1. Marinate the arbi/ colocasia in this mix for 20 minutes.
1. Heat oil in a pan on medium heat, add the marinated arbi and give it a gentle stir.
1. Let the arbi cook and get roasted in medium heat, occasionally give it a gentle stir.
1. Serve the Seppakizhangu Roast (South Indian Style Arbi Fry) Recipe with Steamed Rice and Rasam.
1. This also makes a great combination with hot Phulkas or Chapatis.
