### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Broccoli - cut into small heads.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 4 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Broccoli Egg Scramble Recipe, cut the broccoli by the florets and keep it aside.
1. Heat a skillet with oil, add in the broccoli and sprinkle some salt and stir fry till they turn bright green and are soft.
1. The broccoli will take about 10 minutes to cook.
1. Take the broccoli out and keep the skillet on with a tablespoon of oil.
1. Break the eggs into a bowl and whisk well.
1. Add the eggs into the pan, and keep stirring until the eggs get cooked.
1. Add enough salt and pepper powder and stir until the scramble is cooked.
1. You can prepare the scrambled eggs according to the consistency that you like.
1. Finally add stir fried broccoli along with the egg scramble and combine together.
1. Switch off the heat and serve warm.
1. Serve the Broccoli Egg Scramble Recipe along with fresh fruits and a Chickoo Banana Date Smoothie to make your breakfast healthy.
