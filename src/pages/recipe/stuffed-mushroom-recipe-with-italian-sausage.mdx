### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Paprika powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Britannia Cream Cheese.
- 1 tablespoon Cheese - grated (cheddar and parmesan).
- 1 tablespoon Parsley leaves - finely chopped.
- 4 Pork sausage - (Italian sausage used here).
- 20 Button mushrooms - (or portobello mushrooms).
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil - (extra virgin).
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Stuffed Mushroom Recipe With Italian Sausage, clean the mushrooms well.
1. Wipe off any dirt on the mushrooms.
1. Remove the stem and carefully scoop out the inside part.
1. Keep aside.
1. Mince the Italian sausages.
1. In a bowl mix cream cheese, oregano, paprika powder, salt, pepper powder and mix well.
1. Add the minced sausages and chopped parsley.
1. Mix.
1. Rub little oil all over the mushrooms.
1. Sprinkle some salt and pepper all over the mushrooms.
1. Place a teaspoon full the sausage cream cheese mix into the mushroom cavity.
1. Fill as much as possible into the mushrooms.
1. Now add a little-grated cheese over the mushrooms.
1. Pre heat oven to 150-degree celsius.
1. Arrange the stuffed mushrooms over a baking tray.
1. Bake it for 12 - 18 minutes or until the chicken in cooked and starts to brown and cheese is melted fully.
1. Once cooked remove from oven and serve Stuffed Mushroom Recipe With Italian Sausage immediately as a starter/entree or evening snack.
