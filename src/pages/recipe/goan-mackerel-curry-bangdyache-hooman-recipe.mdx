### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 2 Green Chillies.
- 6 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 20 grams Tamarind.
- 1/2 inch Ginger.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 10 Sichuan peppercorns.
- 2 Kokum (Malabar Tamarind).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 4 Kashmiri dry red chillies.
- 2 Mackerel fish - cut into 3 to 4 pieces.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Goan Mackerel Curry recipe, wash and cut the heads of the mackerel's.
1. Then cut them into 2 or 3 pieces, discard the head.
1. Wash again after 10 minutes.
1. Marinate the Mackerel's with the masala mentioned above for marinating and keep it aside for atleast 30 minutes in the fridge.
1. Grind the grated coconut, red chilies and tamarind together by adding 1/2 cup water.
1. Grind to make a thin gravy.
1. Next, in a wok/kadai, add 2 tablespoons oil.
1. When oil heats, chopped ginger and slit green chilies and fry them.
1. After the ginger and green chilies get fried in the oil, add the ground gravy and marinated mackerels together in a kadai.
1. Slightly crush the peppercorns and add them to the gravy.
1. Add the kokum petals and cook this till mackerels get cooked.
1. When the gravy starts bubbling, switch off the gas.
1. Check the salt and adjust accordingly.
1. You can make Goan Mackerel Curry and serve it with steamed rice and fried fish along with kokum kadi to enjoy a delicious Goan fish thali.
