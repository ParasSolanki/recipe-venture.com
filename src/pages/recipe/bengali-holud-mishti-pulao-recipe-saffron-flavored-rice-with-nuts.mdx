### Ingredients

- 14 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Saffron strands - few.
- 2 tablespoons Raisins.
- 11 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 cup Basmati rice - (gobindo bhog).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mace powder.
- 3 tablespoon Ghee.
- 2 tablespoons Sugar.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 3 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar) - steamed (optional).
- 2 tablespoons Cashew nuts - halved.
- 2 tablespoon Pistachios - roughly chopped.
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 1/4 teaspoon Nutmeg powder.
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta) - roughly torn.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Bengali Holud Mishti Pulao Recipe (Saffron Flavored Rice With Nuts), firstly we will cook the rice in a pressure cooker along with 1 tablespoon ghee, ginger and 2 cups of water until done.
1. Once done, allow it to rest.
1. In a small pan, heat a little ghee.
1. Add bay leaves, cinnamom stick, cardamom pods, elaichi and cloves.
1. Cook it for 10 to 15 seconds.
1. After 10 to 15 seconds, add cashews, raisins, pistachios, cumin powder, turmeric powder, nutmeg powder, mace powder, saffron strands.
1. After 1 minute, add in the cooked rice, green peas and sugar and give it a mix.
1. Let it cook for 2 more minutes and serve hot.
1. Serve your flavored Bengali Holud Mishti Pulao (Saffron Flavored Rice With Nuts) with Shorshe Ilish (Hilsa in Mustard Sauce) or Bengali Doi Maach recipe for a weekday meal.
