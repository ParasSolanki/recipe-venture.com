### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 5 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 inch Ginger - thin slices.
- 10 Curry leaves.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Coconut Oil.
- 4 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- 400 grams Mutton.
- 2 teaspoon Fresh coconut - (1/2 inch size).
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 3/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kerala Style Mutton Pepper Fry recipe, wash mutton pieces well and marinate it with all the spices mentioned in the list.
1. Heat coconut oil in a pan.
1. Add garlic and ginger pieces.
1. Fry for 1 minute.
1. Add finely chopped onions and curry leaves.
1. Cook till onions turn soft.
1. Add marinated mutton and 3/4 teaspoon salt.
1. Add pepper powder, coconut pieces, mix well.
1. Continue to cook for 4 minutes.
1. Add 2 cups water and mix well.
1. Cover and let it cook until mutton gets cooked well and water is fully evaporated.
1. Kerala style mutton sukka/fry is ready.
1. Kerala Style Mutton Pepper Fry as a side dish along with Kerala Varutharacha Mutton Curry and Steamed Rice for a weekday dinner with family.
