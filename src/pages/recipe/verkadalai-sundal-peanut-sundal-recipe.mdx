### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few sprigs.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon SSP Asafoetida (Hing).
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 2 cups Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 teaspoons White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Verkadalai Sundal - Peanut Sundal Recipe, into the pressure cooker add peanuts with 1 cup of water.
1. Pressure cook for 5 to 6 whistles and then turn the heat to low.
1. Simmer for 3 minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally and keep aside.
1. Into a preheated pan add, sesame oil, mustard seeds,urad dal and allow the seeds to crackle and the dal to turn golden brown in color.
1. Once the dal has turned golden brown in colour add hing,ginger, green chillies, curry leaves and saute for a few seconds.
1. After few seconds add the cooked peanuts, salt to taste and grated coconut.
1. Stir fry for 3 to 4 minutes and stir in the coriander leaves.
1. Turn off the heat and transfer Verkadalai Sundal into serving bowls and serve.
1. Serve the Verkadalai Sundal - Peanut Sundal Recipe during the Navratri or as a healthy tea time snack along with South Indian Filter Coffee Recipe With Filter Coffee Powder.
