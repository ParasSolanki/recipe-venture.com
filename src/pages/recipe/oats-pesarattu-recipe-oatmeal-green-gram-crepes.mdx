### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- Salt - to taste.
- Sunflower Oil - to make pessaratu.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 cup Green Moong Dal (Whole) - soaked for 3 hours.
- 1 cup Oats Flour.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Oats Pesarattu Recipe - Oatmeal & Green Gram Crepes, soak the green gram dal for 3 hours.
1. Grind green gram dal along with ginger and green chillies into a smooth paste in mixer grinder.
1. The consistency of the batter should be a little thicker than the normal dosa batter.
1. Transfer the green gram batter to a big bowl and add the oatmeal flour to the batter, season with salt and stir well to combine.
1. Heat the skillet or dosa pan and grease the surface with a bit of oil.
1. Pour a ladle full of the Oats Pesarattu batter and spread it in swift circular motions to make a thin crepe.
1. Once the bottom of the pesarattu is cooked, flip it over to cook the other side for a few more seconds until  golden  brown in color.
1. Serve Oats Pesarattu recipe for breakfast with spicy Red Chilli Coconut Chutney Recipe - South Indian Chutney and  South Indian Filter Coffee Recipe With Filter Coffee Powder.
