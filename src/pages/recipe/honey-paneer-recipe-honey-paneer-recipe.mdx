### Ingredients

-  2 tablespoons cream - or milk.
-  1 tablespoon honey.
-  1 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  2 teaspoons oil.
-  1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder.
- 1 cup Paneer - chopped.
-  1/2 teaspoon coriander Powder.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 tablespoon Kasuri methi.
-  1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  1-1 / 2 cup tomato puree.
-  2 inches ginger - grated.

### Instructions

1. To make honey paneer, first heat oil in a pan.
1. In this, add ginger, honey and cook till the honey is boiling.
1. Now add turmeric powder, coriander powder and cook it for 15-20 seconds.
1. After 15 to 20 seconds, add tomato puree, salt, red chili powder, cardamom powder and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Add milk or cream to it after 2 to 3 minutes.
1. After 1 minute add paneer, kasoori methi and cook it for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Garnish with green coriander and serve.
1. Serve the paneer with honey along with spinach raita and phulka for dinner.
