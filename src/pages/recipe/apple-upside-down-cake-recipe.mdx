### Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Baking powder.
- 1 tablespoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 10 grams Vanilla Extract.
- 1/3 cup Butter (unsalted).
- Slivered Almonds (Badam) - a handful.
- 3/4 cup Vivatta Maida.
- 1/2 cup Britannia Cream Cheese.
- Raisins - a handful.
- 2 Apples - sliced.
- 1 pinch Lemon juice.
- 1/4 cup Butter (unsalted) - (more to brush and prep).
- 2 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Apple Upside Down Cake Recipe, prep by peeling the skin of apples.
1. Remove the core of the apples and cut the apples to long thin slices.
1. Squeeze pinch of lime juice on sliced apples and keep aside till called for.
1. Grease the cake pan with butter and flour.
1. Also preheat the oven to 180 degree celsius.
1. To prepare the sauce, in a saucepan, add butter on low heat.
1. When the butter melts, add sugar.
1. The sugar melts gradually and the colour changes to brownish.
1. Thats when you take out the caramel sauce and pour it in the prepared cake pan.
1. Arranges sliced apples on it.
1. You can use more apple slices if you prefer.
1. Sprinkle a little sugar, about 1-2 tablespoons on the apples.
1. Brush generously with melted butter.
1. Now place the pan in oven at 180 degrees for about 7 minutes until we prepare cake batter.
1. Cake BatterSieve All purpose flour and baking powder into a mixing bowl and keep aside.
1. Break two eggs carefully and separate egg white and yolk in different bowls.
1. In another bowl whisk together sugar and butter until they are mixed properly.
1. Now beat by adding egg yolk, vanilla extract, and cream cheese.
1. Now add flour mixture to it and mix well.
1. Whip egg whites separately till fluffy and stiff peaks appear and add to this mixture and fold in till combined.
1. Finally add almonds, raisins and mix well.
1. Baking the upside down cakeTake out the Pan from oven that has caramelised apples and sprinkle ground cinnamon on it.
1. Pour the batter on apples and bake for about 25-30 minutes or until cake turns light brown in color.
1. When you insert a toothpick it should come out clear when done.
1. Now remove the baked cake from oven and set aside for 5 minutes.
1. and then invert the pan and tap to unmold the cake.
1. Serve Apple Upside Down Cake Recipe along with Banana Coconut Milk Ice Cream or vanilla icecream.
