### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Chia Seeds.
- 1 Kiwi - chopped.
- 1 Cucumber - chopped.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - a few.
- 1 Green apple - chopped.
- 1 cup Tender coconut water.
- 1/2 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Ice cubes.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Refreshing Smoothie Recipe With Cucumber & Kiwi, get all the ingredients ready.
1. Chop the cucumber and roughly chop kiwi.
1. In a blender, add in all the ingredients except chia seeds and blend till smooth.
1. Pour the smoothie to the serving glass.
1. Garnish with a dash of crunchy chia seeds and serve Refreshing Smoothie Recipe With Cucumber & Kiwi in the evening.
