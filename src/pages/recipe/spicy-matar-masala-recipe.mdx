### Ingredients

- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta) - broken into two piece.
- 3/4 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 3 to 4 Curry leaves.
- Salt - as required.
- 1 cup Green peas (Matar) - boiled.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 to 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Onion - paste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Spicy Matar Masala,Firstly heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan and add cumin seeds and mustard seeds.
1. Once it splutters, add curry leaves, bay leaf, cloves and badi elaichi.
1. After a minute, add green chillies and garlic ginger paste.
1. Cook it for about a minute.
1. Next add the chopped onions and cook them till they become soft and translucent.
1. Next, add the onion paste and cook for about 3 to 5 minutes.
1. Then add the tomato puree and add all the masalas and salt.
1. Cook for about 5 to 8 minutes or till the oil starts separating.
1. Now add the green peas and allow it to cook for another 2 minutes.
1. Check for salt and spice, adjust according to your taste and switch off the flame'Serve your Spicy Matar masala with roti and dal.
