### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing) - 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Casicum Bajji Recipe, remove the top stem carefully and cut the capsicum into two equal halves vertically, and deseed it.
1. Now cut long, thick strips of capsicum for the Casicum Bajji RecipeFor the Bajji  BatterIn a mixing bowl combine all the ingredients of bajji batter which includes gram flour, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, asafoetida, and salt.
1. Mix in just enough water to form a thick bajji batter to thickly coat the back of a spoon.
1. Keep the batter aside.
1. Heat oil in a kadai for deep frying the Casicum Bajji on medium heat.
1. Coat each of the sliced Casicum in the gram flour bajji batter and gradually let it into the oil and deep fry the Casicum Bajji until golden brown in color.
1. Drain the Casicum Bajji on an absorbent paper placed in a plate to absorb the excess oil and serve hot.
1. Serve the Capsicum Bajji Recipe along Coconut Chutney Recipe- For Idli And Dosa and a cup of South Indian Filter Coffee for a perfect Rainy day snack recipe.
1. It makes a great appetizer when you have sudden guests at home too.
