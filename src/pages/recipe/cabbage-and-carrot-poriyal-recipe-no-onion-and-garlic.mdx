### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 8 Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Coconut Oil - as required.
- 2 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 2 cups Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - shredded.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cabbage and Carrot Poriyal Recipe, heat a heavy bottomed pan with oil, add mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
1. Once it splutters, add urad dal and chana dal.
1. Saute until it turns golden.
1. Add curry leaves and hing saute for a minute.
1. Now add shredded cabbage, sprinkle salt and saute for 10 minutes.
1. Add little water, turmeric powder.
1. Mix well and cook covered for 5-10 minutes.
1. Once the cabbage is cooked, add carrot and mix well.
1. Sprinkle pepper, required salt and cook until the carrot is done.
1. Once done, switch off the stove and serve the Poriyal hot.
1. Serve the Cabbage and Carrot Poriyal Recipe along with Pineapple Rasam, Steamed Rice and Sambar to make it a complete South Indian meal.
