### Ingredients

- 1 Mango (Raw) - cut into 1 inch pieces.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - thinly sliced.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon SSP Asafoetida (Hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 2 1/2 cups Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kacche Aam Aur Lehsun Ki Dal Recipe, first clean and wash the toor dal.
1. Add the washed toor dal into pressure cooker along with raw mangoes, tomatoes, green chillies.
1. turmeric powder, salt to taste and 2 1/2 cups of water.
1. Cover the pressure cooker and pressure cook for 4 whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally and open the pressure cooker.
1. Now stir in chopped coriander leaves to the Kacche Aam Ki Dal, check the salt and spice levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. You can adjust the consistency of the dal by adding more water if required.
1. Transfer the dal to a serving bowl.
1. The next step is to make the tadka for the dal.
1. Heat the ghee in a small tadka pan on medium heat.
1. Once the ghee is hot add cumin seeds, dry red chilies and garlic and saute for few seconds till the garlic starts to brown.
1. Once done add asafoetida and red chili powder into the hot ghee mixture and stir and immediately turn off the heat.
1. Spoon this tadka over the Kacche Aam Ki Dal and serve.
1. Serve the Kacche Aam Aur Lehsun Ki Dal with Phulkas or Steamed Rice along with papad and Beetroot Raita for wholesome weekday lunch or dinner.
