### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Jaggery - or coconut sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Apple cider vinegar - or any vinegar.
- 1 tablespoon Tomato Ketchup.
- 5-6 Pickled Jalapenos - pickled and chopped.
- 1 cup Walnuts - roasted.
- 1 cup Brown Rice - cooked.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli sauce - or sriracha.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - as needed.
- Black olives - as needed.
- 1 Onion - cut into rings.
- 1 Avocado - ripened and pitted.
- 8 Beetroot - sliced and cooked.
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 Onion - finely diced.
- 8 Pineapple - slices.
- 8 Tomato - sliced.
- 1-1/2 cups Black beans - cooked/canned (drained).
- 1/2 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- 1/2 tablespoon Maple syrup - or honey.
- 1 cup Iceberg lettuce - as needed.
- 2 teaspoons Cumin powder (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To prepare this Healthy Black Bean Burger Recipe Without Bun, get prep with all the ingredients mentioned.
1. In a mixing bowl combine avocado, lemon juice and a pinch of salt and mash with a fork.
1. Cover with cling wrap and refrigerate until needed.
1. In a bowl combine apple cider Vinegar, honey, tomato sauce, hot sauce and pepper and whisk them together with a fork and set aside this sauce mixture when combined.
1. In a pan, sauté diced onion using little oil on medium-high flame until brown and set aside.
1. In a blender, add roasted walnuts, cumin powder, chilli powder, raw sugar and a pinch of salt and blend to a coarse powder.
1. In a large mixing bowl add black beans and mash it well using a masher.
1. To this, add cooked brown rice, walnut powder, chopped coriander, sautéed onion, chopped jalapenos, bread crumbs, sauce mixture and salt to taste.
1. Mix all the ingredients until they are well combined, cover and set aside for at least 1/2 an hour.
1. This helps the flavor soak into the burgers.
1. Divide this mixture into 8 equal portions and shape them into burger patties.
1. Grill them on a pan using a little olive oil, flipping both sides.
1. On a platter, lay the salad leaves and place pineapple, beetroot, tomato, mashed Avocado, the grilled patty on top of the other and secure these, using a skewer or a toothpick.
1. Place onion rings on top and insert olives from above.
1. Enjoy this Healthy Black Bean Burger Recipe Without Bun with sauce or dip of your choice.
1. Serve the Healthy Black Bean Burger Recipe Without Bun along with Oven Baked Sweet Potato Fries Recipe and a glass of  Mango Iced Tea Recipe to complete your meal.
