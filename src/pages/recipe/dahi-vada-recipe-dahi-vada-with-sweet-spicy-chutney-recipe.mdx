### Ingredients

-  1/4 teaspoon black salt or rock salt.
- 1/2 cup white urad dal - soaked for 4 hours.
-  tamarind sauce - red chili powder - 1/4 tsp cumin powder.
-  as per use.
-  chaat masala powder - a pinch.
-  1/4 teaspoon cooking soda.
-  salt - as per taste.

### Instructions

1. To make dahi vada recipe, first wash and clean the urad dal.
1. Now pour the urad dal with salt in a mixer grinder and make a paste.
1. Add soda dale and mix well.
1. You can also add some water if you want.
1. Make your hands wet.
1. Take 2 tablespoons of Urad Dal mixture in your hand and make it like a round ball.
1. Make other vadas as well.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Pour these vadas in hot oil and fry them till they turn golden brown.
1. After frying, remove it on an absorbent paper so that it absorbs more oil.
1. Now pour them in water and keep them aside for 30 to 45 minutes.
1. At the time of survey, squeeze the water thoroughly from the vada and pour it into a serving bowl.
1. Pour the curd over it.
1. Now add red chili powder, cumin powder, black salt, chaat masala powder and milli chutney.
1. Serve.
1. Serve dahi vada recipe with your meal.
1. Serve jaipuri potato onion vegetable, celery poori and raita for dinner.
