### Ingredients

-  1 capsicum (green) - finely chopped.
-  1 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  1 onion - finely chopped.
-  3 to 4 curry leaves.
-  1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder.
- 2 cups rice - cook.
-  1 green chili - finely chopped.
-  ghee - coriander - little (for garnish).
-  1 carrot - tightly.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To make carrot and capsicum casserole, first heat ghee in a pan.
1. Add cumin seeds, green chillies and onions.
1. Cook onion till it turns golden.
1. After curry is cooked add curry leaves and capsicum.
1. Cook until the capsicum becomes soft.
1. After it becomes soft, add grated carrots, cooked rice and mix.
1. After mixing, add salt, red chili powder and black pepper powder.
1. Mix and cover the pan.
1. Allow to cook for 2 minutes, open the pan and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve carrot and capsicum pulao along with boondi raita and papad for dinner.
