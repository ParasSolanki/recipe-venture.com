### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini) - (optional).
- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 2 Ripe Bananas - mashed.
- 1/4 cup Cranberries - (dried) chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/3 cup Milk.
- 1 tablespoon Maple syrup.
- 3/4 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal) - or oats bran.
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour - or all purpose flour.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 1 tablespoon Butter (unsalted) - melted.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Banana and Cranberry Bran Muffins Recipe, preheat oven to 175 degree Celsius.
1. In a bowl, mix together oat bran, milk, bananas and vanilla extract and let the mixture sit for 10-15 minutes.
1. This will help soak the oat bran so that the muffins are not dry.
1. In another large mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon powder (if using), cranberries and salt.
1. Set aside.
1. In a different mixing bowl, whisk together butter, honey and maple syrup.
1. Once the bran mixture has soaked for 10 minutes, add it to the butter mixture.
1. Stir to combine well.
1. Finally add the flour mixture and mix just until combined.
1. Spoon the muffin mixture into lined muffin tins.
1. These muffins don't rise too much so you can fill till the top.
1. If using mini tins, bake for 12-15 minutes.
1. If using regular sized muffin tins, bake for 18-20 minutes.
1. Once baked, let the muffins cool for a few minutes before turning them out on a wire mesh.
1. Serve these Banana and Cranberry Bran Muffins as a tea time snack or Breakfast along with a cup of Ginger Cardamom Chai  or coffee or even pack them into the school kids lunch boxes.
