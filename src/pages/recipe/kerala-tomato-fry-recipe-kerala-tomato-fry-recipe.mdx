### Ingredients

-  1 tablespoon rye.
-  oil - as per use.
-  1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste.
-  1/2 tablespoon Kashmiri red chilli powder.
-  5 curry leaves.
-  2 tablespoons green coriander - finely chopped.
-  3 dry red chillies - broken.
-  salt - as per taste.
- 4 tomatoes - chopped.
-  1/2 tablespoon turmeric powder.
-  2 green chillies - chopped.
-  2 onions - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make Kerala Tomato Fry, first heat the oil in a pan.
1. Add mustard seeds and dry red chilies.
1. When the mustard starts to crackle, add curry leaves, green chilies and onions.
1. After 1 minute add ginger garlic paste.
1. Cook onion until it becomes soft and brown.
1. After that add Kashmiri red chilli powder and turmeric powder.
1. Mix well and add chopped tomatoes.
1. Let it cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
1. After the tomatoes are soft, add salt and mix.
1. Cook for 1 minute and then garnish with green coriander.
1. For dinner, serve Kerala tomato fry with beetroot sambar and rice.
