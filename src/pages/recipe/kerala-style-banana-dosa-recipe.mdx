### Ingredients

- Ghee - for cooking.
- 1 teaspoon Dry ginger powder.
- 4 tablespoon Jaggery.
- 2 Ripe Bananas - mashed.
- 1 cup Rice flour.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 2 tablespoon Cashew nuts - ground.
- 1 pinch Salt.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Kerala Style Banana Dosa Recipe by heating jaggery with ¼ cup water into a sauce pan till it dissolves.
1. Mash the bananas into a bowl, add all the other ingredients including the jaggery syrup and add 1 cup water and mix well to form a thick pancake batter.
1. Heat a nonstick pan with ghee and pour a ladle on the hot pan and cook for about 2 minutes on either side and flip it over.
1. Do the rest to remaining batter.
1. Serve the Kerala Style Banana Dosa Recipe as a snack after school with glass of buttermilk or Mango Lassi Recipe (A Refreshing Mango Drink with Yogurt).
