### Ingredients

-  1 teaspoon black pepper powder.
-  oil - as per use.
-  2 cloves garlic - finely chopped.
-  1 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  1 teaspoon ginger - grated.
-  1 onion - chopped.
-  2 tomatoes - chopped.
-  1 teaspoon cumin powder.
-  1 teaspoon cardamom powder.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 tablespoon Kasuri methi.
-  1 small Tablespoon turmeric powder.
- 250 grams cheese.
-  1 capsicum (green) - cut.
-  1 tablespoon butter.

### Instructions

1. To make kadhai paneer, first heat oil in a pan.
1. Add onion, capsicum, ginger, garlic to it.
1. Cook onion and capsicum until soft.
1. Now add tomato, turmeric powder, cumin powder, black pepper powder, red chili powder and let it cook for 3 minutes.
1. After 3 minutes, add butter, salt, cardamom powder and big cardamom and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Add paneer and fenugreek after 2 minutes.
1. Cover the pan and let it cook for 3 minutes.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve the kadhai paneer with Amritsari dal, boondi raita and phulka for dinner.
