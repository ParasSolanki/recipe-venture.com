### Ingredients

- 4 tablespoon Mustard seeds - (or kuriya-split).
- Salt - to taste.
- 5 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/4 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 250 gram Green Chillies - washed & pat dried.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mustard Green Chilli Pickle, firstly cut the green chillies into half or more pieces.
1. Take the green chillies in a mixing bowl, add a pinch of salt and mix well.
1. Keep those chillies in a bowl for at least half an hour.
1. Drain the chilli-salt water and keep green chillies in a bowl.
1. (This way green chillies will reduce their spicyness)Take another mixing bowl.
1. Combine mustard splits (kuriya), asafoetida, oil, salt, jaggery, lemon juice and turmeric powder in a bowl.
1. Toss all the ingredients and beat them in round motion with hand for about 4 to 5 minutes.
1. After 4 to 5 minutes, add the green chilies and toss them.
1. Mix everything well.
1. Adjust salt and lemon juice according to your taste.
1. Transfer them in to a sterilised bottle or in a glass jar and store in a fridge for long.
1. Serve Mustard Green Chilli Pickle with your everyday meal for that extra hint of tanginess and spiciness.
