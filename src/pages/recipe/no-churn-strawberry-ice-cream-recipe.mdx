### Ingredients

- 2 cups Heavy whipping cream.
- 2 cups Strawberry Compote.
- 1 teaspoons Lemon juice.
- 1 Condensed Milk - can.

### Instructions

1. To begin with No Churn Strawberry Ice cream first, cut the strawberries and blend them into a nice smooth pure.
1. For the next step in a bowl, add the sweetened condensed milk and lemon juice.
1. Now take the chilled bowl and pour the heavy cream and whip them thoroughly for about 7 - 10 minutes on high speed.
1. Make sure the mixture turns out to be very thick.
1. Take a spatula and gently blend condensed milk with whipped cream and mix them well.
1. It's time to add the pureed strawberries with the whipped cream and once again give it a good mix.
1. Pour the ice cream into a metal container (I used my banana bread loaf pan)Cover tightly (or wrap with aluminium foil) and freeze for at least 6 hours.
1. Your No Churn Strawberry Ice Cream is ready to be served with whole wheat waffles and some strawberry puree can be served with the ice cream.
