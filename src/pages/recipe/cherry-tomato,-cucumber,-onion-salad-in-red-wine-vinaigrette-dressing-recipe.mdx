### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - diced.
- 3 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 Cucumber - chopped.
- 1 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 cup Cherry tomatoes - sliced.
- 1/4 cup Red Wine Vinaigrette.

### Instructions

1. To begin preparing the Cherry Tomato, Cucumber, Onion Salad in Red Wine Vinaigrette, chop all the ingredients and keep it ready.
1. Add all the dressing ingredients into a bowl and whisk till it combines.
1. Add your chopped vegetables and toss well.
1. Check for salt and season it well.
1. Serve the Cherry Tomato, Cucumber, Onion Salad along with Baked Frittata or an omelette to enjoy your morning breakfast.
