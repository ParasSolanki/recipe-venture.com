### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 Shallots.
- 2 Green Chillies.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 4 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.
- 5 Curry leaves.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 20 Button mushrooms - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To prepare this easy Mushroom thoran Recipe-Kerala Style Mushroom Sabzi, prep up with all the ingredients first.
1. Grind all the ingredients meant for spice paste into a thick coarse paste by adding 1/3 cup water.
1. Heat a kadai and add oil.
1. Add mustard seeds and after they splutter, one by one add the other ingredients meant for tempering and mix well.
1. Add the finely chopped mushrooms and turmeric powder and mix well.
1. After some time water will be released from the mushrooms.
1. Cook it for 10 to 15 minutes till the mushrooms appear tender.
1. Now add the ground coconut paste to the mushrooms and cook uncovered for 10 minutes.
1. Add salt to taste and give it a stir.
1. Serve Kerala Style Mushroom thoran Recipe-Kerala Style Mushroom Sabzi with Steamed rice and Cheriya Ulli Sambar.
