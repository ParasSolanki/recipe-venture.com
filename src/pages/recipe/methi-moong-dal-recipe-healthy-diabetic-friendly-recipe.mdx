### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves) - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar - optional.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Methi Moong Dal Recipe, Pressure cook the moong dal along with salt, turmeric powder, 1 cup of water for 3 to 4 whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Wash chopped fenugreek leaves, drain and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan over medium flame.
1. Add minced garlic and let them turn into golden brown.
1. After that add cumin seed and saut√© them pop.
1. Now add methi leaves and saute for about a minute and the leaves will wilt down.
1. Stir in the cooked moong dal, salt and sugar and add a little water to adjust the consistency of the Methi Moong dal.
1. Check salt and spices and give it a brisk boil.
1. Heat ghee in a pan; add the cumin seeds and red chilli powder.
1. Pour this tadka over the Methi Moong Dal Recipe and stir.
1. Transfer the Methi Moong Dal Recipe to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Methi Moong Dal Recipe along with Khooba Roti and Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita for a weekday meal.
