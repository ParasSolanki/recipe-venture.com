### Ingredients

- Salt - salt to taste.
- 1/2 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Nutmeg powder.
- 300 grams Chicken breasts - cut into cubes.
- 2 tablespoons All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 Star anise.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 Onions - sliced.
- 1/2 cup Apple - cut into thin strips.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 2 tablespoon Tomato Ketchup.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 3 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - cut into cubes.
- 2 teaspoons Garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 cup Water.
- 6 Black pepper corns.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 tablespoons Butter.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/8 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Japanese Style Chicken Katsu Curry Recipe, in a mixing bowl combine the washed and cleaned chicken pieces, along with salt and pepper and let it sit.
1. To make the Indo-Japanese Spice Blend Heat a small skillet on medium flame, add the coriander seeds, cumin seeds, dry red chillies, fennel seeds, fenugreek seeds, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom pods, black pepper corns and star anise.
1. dry roast this on medium-low flame for about 4-6 minutes until nice and fragrant.
1. Turn off the flame and allow it to cool.
1. Transfer to a mixer-jar and grind to a fine powder, and set aside.
1. In a small skillet, heat butter on medium flame, add the all purpose flour and cook until the butter and flour are well combined and the flour has cooked through.
1. Now add the Indo-Japanese Spice Blend to the butter and flour mixture and cook for about two minutes.
1. Turn off the flame and set aside.
1. Heat a kadai on medium flame, add oil and then the ginger and garlic, saute for 30 seconds, then add in the sliced onions and cook until it turns into a light brown colour.
1. This will take about 5 minutes.
1. To the cooked onions, add the water and the spiced flour butter mixture, salt, turmeric powder, and mix well, bring to a single boil, reduce the flame add the onions and carrots and cover and cook until the chicken is cooked through completely.
1. Open the lid, add nutmeg powder, tomato ketchup, soy sauce, honey, apple strips and mix well.
1. Turn off the flame, transfer to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Japanese Style Chicken Katsu Curry Recipe along with Thai Jasmine Sticky Rice Recipe followed by a dessert of Japanese Cotton Cheesecake.
