### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Baby corn - cut lengthwise.
- 2 Stalks Spring Onion Greens - chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 Onion - thinly sliced.
- 2 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- 1/4 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Veeba Sweet Chilli Sauce.
- 2 Whole Eggs.
- 2 tablespoons Britannia Cheezza - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Cheesy Masala Omelette With Roasted Vegetables Recipe first grate the Britannia cheezza and keep it aside.
1. Into a bowl break the eggs and add the coriander leaves, green chillies and salt to taste.
1. Whisk the egg mixture well and keep aside.
1. Add oil into a preheated pan add the garlic and onions and saute till the onions are slightly caramelized.
1. Once done stir in the baby corn and red bell peppers, sprinkle a little salt and saute on high heat until they are roasted and become soft.
1. Once done, stir in the sweet chilli sauce and the spring onion greens.
1. Saute for a few seconds and turn off the heat.
1. The final step is to make the Cheesy Masala Omelette.
1. Add oil into a preheated pan; pour the egg mixture and allow it to cook well on both sides.
1. Flip, when you notice the top is almost cooked.
1. Once both sides of the omelette is done, add a few tablespoons of the roasted veggies on the omelette, sprinkle the Britannia Cheezza and fold in half.
1. Cook for another minute till the cheese melts.
1. Once the cheese melts transfer the Cheesy Masala Omelette With Roasted Vegetables into a serving plate and serve hot.
1. Serve Cheesy Masala Omelette With Roasted Vegetables along with a toast with some Britannia Mexican Chilli Cheese Spreadz and Homemade Fresh Orange Juice.
