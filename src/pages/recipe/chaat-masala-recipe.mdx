### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 tablespoons Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 3 tablespoons Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 2 teaspoons Black Salt (Kala Namak).
- 1 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chaat Masala Recipe, heat a pan on low flame and dry roast the cumin seeds, coriander seeds and black peppercorns.
1. Roast for on medium-low heat until fragrant.
1. This will take about 8-10 minutes.
1. Turn off the flame and allow it to come to room temperature.
1. Transfer to a mixer jar, add the black salt, dry mango powder, asafoetida and salt to taste.
1. Grind to a fine powder.
1. Use Chaat Masala is various chaat and buttermilk recipes.
