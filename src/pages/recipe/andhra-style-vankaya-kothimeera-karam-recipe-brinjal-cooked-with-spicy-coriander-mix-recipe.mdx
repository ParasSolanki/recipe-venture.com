### Ingredients

- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - as required and roughly chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 400 grams Brinjal (Baingan / Eggplant) - cut into long strips.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 inch Ginger - roughly chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vankaya Kothimeera Karam recipe, wash and cut the brinjals in long strips and keep it immersed in salt water.
1. This helps in avoiding the brinjals to turn black.
1. Now, add all the ingredients mentioned in the coriander masala table into a blender and make a smooth paste and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan on medium flame, add the mustard seeds, urad dal and let it splutter.
1. Add the dried red chillies and curry leaves and let it crackle.
1. Now, add the slit brinjals and saut√© until it wilts a little.
1. Cook the brinjal until it is half done.
1. This will take 10 minutes.
1. Now, add the spicy coriander masala mix to the brinjals, season with salt, toss well, and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
1. Once the brinjals are well cooked, turn off the heat.
1. Transfer Vankaya Kothimeera Karam into a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Vankaya Kothimeera Karam along with Tomato Onion Sambar and steamed rice with a dollop of ghee doe a weeknight dinner.
