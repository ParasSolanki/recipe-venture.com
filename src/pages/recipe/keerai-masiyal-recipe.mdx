### Ingredients

-  Amaranth leaves - chopped.
-  asafetida - a pinch.
- 500 grams Spinach - chopped.
-  1 teaspoon white urad dal.
-  1/2 teaspoon mustard.
-  1 sprig curry leaves.
-  salt - as per taste 1 teaspoon oil.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.

### Instructions

1. To make Keerai Masli, firstly put Amaranth and Spinach in a pressure cooker with 1 tablespoon of water and salt.
1. Cook till 1 city comes and keep the cooker under cold water to remove the pressure.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add the mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves, asafoetida and let it cook for 30 seconds.
1. Now add spinach and amaranth and let it cook for 1 minute.
1. After 1 minute, turn off the gas and serve hot.
1. Serve the Keerai Masli with tomato onion sambar and rice for dinner.
