### Ingredients

- 2 Tomatoes - pureed.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - peeled and cubed.
- 1 Cauliflower (gobi) - washed and cut into florets.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the One Pot Aloo Gobi Ki Sabzi Recipe in the Preethi Electric Pressure Cooker, heat oil in pan and make the setting to the gravy mode; add cumin seeds, ginger, cardamom pods.
1. Allow the cumin seeds to crackle.
1. Once the cumin seeds crackle for a few seconds, add the tomatoes, aloo, gobi, salt, chilli powder, turmeric powder, garam masala and 1/4 cup of water.
1. Stir to combine all the aloo gobi ingredients.
1. Lock the lid in place.
1. Set the timer for 20 minutes and allow it to cook until you hear the beep sound.
1. Once the aloo gobi sabzi is done, allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Check the salt and pepper to taste and transfer Aloo Gobi sabzi to serving dish and serve warm.
1. Serve the One Pot Aloo Gobi Sabzi along with Traditional Punjabi Kadhi with Methi  and Chawal for a quick weeknight dinner.
