### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- Salt - as required.
- 6 Tortillas.
- 1 tablespoon Chipotle sauce.
- 1 teaspoon Butter.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 2 teaspoons Roasted tomato pasta sauce.
- 1 cup Cheddar cheese - shredded.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder - crushed.
- 250 grams Boneless chicken.
- 1/2 cup Sweet corn.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken Chilli Cheese Quesadillas recipe, firstly marinate chicken with chipotle paste, salt, crushed pepper and lime juice for minimum 40 minutes.
1. Heat butter in a non stick pan, add onion and sliced green pepper and saute for a while.
1. Now add the marinated chicken and saute for some more time.
1. Add tomato sauce and mix well.
1. Cook till chicken softens.
1. Transfer the chicken mix in a microwave bowl and add in the cheese and corn.
1. Mix well.
1. Microwave on high for 2 minutes in oven and allow it to rest for a while.
1. Take one tortilla sheet and add spoonful of chicken chilli cheese mix and spread evenly.
1. Place another tortilla over it and grill on stove top grill pan.
1. Carefully flip the other side and grill again by brushing a little butter on top.
1. Transfer in a plate and carefully cut the quesadillas into four quadrants using a pizza cutter.
1. Similarly make more quesadillas and serve hot with your favourite dip.
1. Serve Chicken Chilli Cheese Quesadillas along with Baked Mexican Rice and Corn Pineapple Salsa Salad for a perfect Mexican dinner on a weekend night.
