### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 2 Green Chillies.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 3/4 cup Lukewarm Water.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil - for seasoning.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1-1/2 cups Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - broken.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tamil Nadu Coconut Chutney Recipe, into a mixer grinder; add the fresh coconut, green chillies, and ginger along with warm water and blend to make a smooth coconut chutney.
1. The addition of warm/ hot water prevents the coconut chutney from getting curdled and sticky.
1. Transfer the Tamil Nadu coconut chutney into a bowl and stir in the lemon juice and salt to taste.
1. The next step is to make the tadka.
1. Heat oil in a small pan on medium heat; add mustard seeds, urad dal and allow it to crackle and the dal to turn golden brown.
1. Once the dal turns brown, add the curry leaves and red chillies and saute for a few seconds.
1. Once done turn off the heat.
1. Spoon the tadka over the Tamil Nadu Coconut Chutney & serve.
1. Serve the delicious Tamil Nadu Coconut Chutney Recipe along with Dosa's, Idli's and Vegetable Rava Upma's or any other dish you want to savor it with.
