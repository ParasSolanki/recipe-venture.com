### Ingredients

- 1/3 cup Water.
- 250 grams Marie Biscuit - (or digestive marie).
- 1/2 cup Raspberry Compote - or puree.
- 3/4 cup Sugar - (can reduce to Â½ cup if your mangoes are very sweet).
- 2 cups Mango Pulp (Puree).
- 500 grams Britannia Cream Cheese.
- 1 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1/2 cup Butter - melted.
- 2 tablespoons Gelatin - or Agar Agar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making No Bake Mango And Yogurt Cheesecake Recipe With Raspberries, make the crust first.
1. Crush the marie biscuits in a mixer jar, until fine crumbs.
1. You can also put the biscuits in a ziplock bag and using a rolling pin or a heavy pan, crush the biscuits.
1. Line the bottom and sides of an 8 or 9 inch springform pan with parchment paper/baking paper.
1. Melt the butter; mix together the melted butter and biscuit crumbs in a mixing bowl and press evenly onto the base of the prepared springform pan.
1. Chill this prepared crust in the freezer for at least 10-20 minutes or till the crust firms up.
1. Pour water in a microwaveable/heat proof bowl; add the gelatin powder/agar agar to the water.
1. Heat the gelatin in a microwave or over a double boiler till it has dissolved completely.
1. Keep aside.
1. Add the cream cheese to the stand mixer jar and beat for a minute.
1. Add sugar and beat till fluffy, about 2 minutes;Now add the yogurt, beat to combine.
1. Later add the mango pulp, mix well.
1. Add the gelatin and mix everything together.
1. Pour this cream cheese -yogurt-mango mixture over the biscuit base in the prepared pan.
1. Add small spoonfuls of raspberry puree on the surface of the cheesecake and create swirls using a skewer or toothpick, if you like.
1. You can leave the bits of raspberry puree as it is, also.
1. Refrigerate the mango raspberry cheesecake and chill for at least 4-5 hours or overnight.
1. Serve this colourful No Bake Mango Yogurt Cheesecake Recipe With Raspberries as a summer dinner party dessert after a meal of Spicy Vegetarian Thai Noodle Bowl Recipe, Lavender Lemonade Cooler Recipe.
