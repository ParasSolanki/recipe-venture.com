### Ingredients

- 150 grams Salted butter - softened.
- 1 tablespoon Chives - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Lemon zest.
- 1 tablespoon Parsley leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Basil leaves - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Homemade Herb Butter Recipe, combine all the ingredient in a mini food processor.
1. Once combined, transfer the herbed butter into a container where you can cover and store in the refrigerator.
1. Refrigerate the Homemade Herb Butter for at least 4 hours and or for about a month and use it as desired on toasted bread, garlic bread, over pasta or even steamed vegetables.
