### Ingredients

- 2 cups Dark chocolate - unsweetened dark chocolate (I used Valrhona Couverture 70%).
- 1/4 cup Dark Chocolate Chips - (semisweet).
- 1 cup Heavy whipping cream.
- 1 tablespoon Icing Sugar - for dusting.
- 4-5 tablespoon Butter - melted.
- 1 tablespoon Cocoa Powder - for dusting.
- 1 tablespoon Caster Sugar.
- 7-8 Bourbon Biscuits - (or hide and seek biscuits).
- 7-8 Chocolate wafers - or Bournvita biscuits.
- 1 cup Fresh Cherries - washed & dried.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Chocolate Lover's Cheesecake Recipe, get prep with all the ingredients.
1. Start by making the crust.
1. CrustPulse the cookies in a Grinder to make fine crumbs.
1. In a medium bowl, combine the cookie crumbs, sugar, and melted butter.
1. Stir with a fork until all the crumbs are moistened.
1. Add the crumb mixture to a 6-inch Spring form pan and gently press about 1-2 inches up the sides of the pan.
1. Press the rest of the crumbs into an even layer over the bottom of the pan.
1. Refrigerate for 20 minutes.
1. FillingPlace dark chocolate chunks in a bowl and set aside.
1. Heat the cream in a microwave safe bowl for 30 seconds or until you see bubbles.
1. Remove carefully and pour evenly on chopped chocolate.
1. Let it sit for 4-5 minutes undisturbed.
1. Whisk the ganache and make sure there are no lumps.
1. You'll get smooth and silky ganache.
1. AssemblingPour half of the filling into the cooled crust.
1. Add chocolate chips and pour the remaining filling on top.
1. Refrigerate until thoroughly chilled, at least 6 hours or overnight.
1. To serve, carefully un-mold the sides of the spring form pan.
1. Let it stand at room temperature for 20 minutes.
1. Top with fresh cherries and dust cocoa powder and powdered sugar on top.
1. Slice Chocolate Lover's Cheesecake Recipe and serve as a dinner party dessert.
