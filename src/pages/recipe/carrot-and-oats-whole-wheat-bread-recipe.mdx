### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Baking powder.
- 2 Whole Eggs - slightly beaten.
- 1 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - grated.
- 3/4 cup Milk.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 2 tablespoons Honey.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Carrot and Oats Bread Recipe by heating the oven at 180 degree Celsius to 20 minutes.
1. Grease a rectangular loaf tin with parchment paper and keep it ready.
1. Mix all the dry ingredients in a small bowl first and keep it ready.
1. In another bowl, whisk egg, oil and honey using a blender till it is fluffy.
1. Then add the grated carrot and whist once more.
1. Add the dry ingredients slowly and using a palate knife fold in the ingredients.
1. Finally add the milk and mix well.
1. Serve the Carrot and Oats Bread Whole wheat Recipe along with a smoothie or just drizzle some honey and can be relished for your morning breakfast.
