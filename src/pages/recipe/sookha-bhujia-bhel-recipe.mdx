### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Green Channa Sprouts - optional of your choice.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- 1/4 cup Cucumber - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 cup Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 2 cups Sev - Bhujia  (you can use any sev).
- 2 tablespoons Sweet Chutney (Date & Tamarind).
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Onions - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sookha Bhujia Bhel Recipe, take a big bowl and add all the ingredients except bhujia sev and mix well.
1. Just before serving add the crunchy bhujia sev, and give it a good stir.
1. It is ready to be served.
1. Serve the Sookha Bhujia Bhel as an after school snack for kids with Papaya Banana Smoothie or Red Wine Sangria Cocktail Recipe as a party appetizer.
