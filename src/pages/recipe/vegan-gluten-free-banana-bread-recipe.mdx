### Ingredients

- 3 teaspoons Baking powder.
- 1 cup Buckwheat Flour (Kuttu Ka Atta) - or kuttu ka atta.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/2 cup Rolled Oats.
- 1 cup Badam (Almond) - ground to a powder.
- 4 Ripe Bananas - mashed.
- 1/3 cup Date syrup - or honey.
- 1/4 cup Tender coconut water.
- 1 Ripe Bananas - slice to top (optional).
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon Apple cider vinegar - or white vinegar.
- 1 Flax seed powder (flax meal).
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1/2 cup Almond Milk (Badam Milk).
- 1/4 cup Coconut Oil - or refined oil.
- 80 grams Dark chocolate - (Dairy free) or regular chocolate.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vegan Gluten Free Banana Bread recipe, line a loaf tin with the baking paper.
1. Pre-heat the oven at 180 degrees Celsius.
1. In a large mixing bowl add all the dry ingredients except chocolate and mix with a whisk.
1. In another mixing bowl add mashed bananas, almond milk, apple cider vinegar/ melted coconut oil, vanilla, coconut water, Flax egg replacer, date syrup and whisk it all until combined.
1. Slowly incorporate the dry ingredients into wet ingredients.
1. Add the chopped chocolate and mix.
1. Pour the mixture into a lined loaf tin and place the sliced banana on top and bake for 75 minutes at 180 degree celsius.
1. If you are gluten intolerant, make sure to use certified gluten-free ingredients like oats and baking powder.
1. Serve Vegan Gluten free Banana Bread with evening tea or as a healthy snack in kids snack boxes along with some Atta Biscuit Recipe (Whole Wheat Flour Cookies Spiced with Cardamom) or Chocolate Crinkle Cookies Recipe.
