### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 to 3 Papad - masala preferably.
- Rock Salt - to taste.
- 500 ml Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - well beaten.
- 1/2 teaspoon Paprika powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Papad Raita, roast the masala papad using a stove top or microwave and keep aside.
1. Now, take a big bowl, pour the curd and whisk it to a smooth and creamy consistency.
1. Add the roasted cumin, paprika powder and rock salt to taste, mix it even and refrigerate for 2 hours.
1. Just before serving add the roughly crushed roasted papads and finely chopped coriander leaves and mix well.
1. Serve the Papad Raita Recipe along with Chana Pulao or any other Pulao for a simple weeknight dinner.
