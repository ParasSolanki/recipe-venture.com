### Ingredients

- 1 Bottle gourd (lauki) - peeled and diced into chunks.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves - roughly torn.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 2 cups Tamarind Water.
- Salt - to taste.
- 12 Shallots - quartered.
- 1 Drumstick - cut into 1 inch size pieces.
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 2 teaspoons Sambar Powder.
- 3 cloves Garlic - whole.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 Tomato - roughly chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sorakkai Puli Kuzhambu Recipe, we will make this recipe in a Pressure Cooker.
1. Add all the main ingredient including bottle gourd, shallots, drumstick, tomato, garlic, ginger, tamarind water, ginger, sambar powder and salt into the pressure cooker, add the salt and about 1 cup of water.
1. Cover the pressure cooker, place the weight on and pressure cook until you hear about 3 whistles.
1. After about 3 whistles, turn off the heat.
1. Allow the cooker to rest, until the pressure releases completely.
1. Once the pressure is released, open the cooker and give the ingredients a stir.
1. Check the salt and spice levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Transfer the Sorakkai Puli Kuzhambu into a serving bowlOur next step is to make the seasoning for the Kuzhambu.
1. Heat a teaspoon of oil in a small pan; once the oil if heated, add the mustard seeds and fenugreek leaves.
1. Allow them to crackle and roast for a few seconds.
1. Once roasted stir in the curry leaves and turn off the heat.
1. Pour the seasoning over the Sorakkai Puli Kuzhambu  and give it a stir.
1. Serve Sorakkai Puli Kuzhambu Recipe along with Steamed Rice, Elai Vadam and Masala Chaas for an everyday meal.
