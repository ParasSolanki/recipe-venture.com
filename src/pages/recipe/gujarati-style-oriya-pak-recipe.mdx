### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Ghee.
- 3 tablespoons Dry ginger powder.
- 1 cup Mixed nuts - (I used almonds.
- 1 cup Ghee.
- 1/4 cup Milk.
- Cashew nuts - a few.
- 2 cups Black Urad Dal (Whole) - ground to make atta.
- 1-1/2 tablespoons Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 250 grams Khoya (Mawa).
- 1 cup Water.
- 1 tablespoon Gond (natural gum) - fried in ghee till crisp.
- 1 tablespoon Peepramul powder (ganthoda).
- 1-1/2 tablespoons Cloves (Laung) - powdered.
- 1-1/2 cups Sugar.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Gujarati Style Adadiya Pak Recipe, in a mixing bowl combine, urad dal atta, milk, ghee.
1. Mix well.
1. Rub flour with palm for 3-4 minutes.
1. Heat the ghee in kadai or heavy bottom kadai.
1. Add flour mixture.
1. Continuously stir it on medium flame till the mixture is brown in color.
1. Meanwhile, combine sugar and water in a saucepan.
1. Prepare a sugar syrup of 1 string consistency and keep ready.
1. When flour starts to get brown color, add fried edible gum(gond) and grated khoya.
1. Saute for a couple of minutes.
1. Turn off the flame and add chopped nuts.
1. Mix well.
1. Allow the mixture to cool a bit.
1. When mixture is lukewarm, combine it with sugar syrup.
1. Mix well.
1. Allow it to set for a while.
1. Give a shape of your choice.
1. Garnish Gujarati Style Adadiya Pak Recipe with cashew nuts and serve with coffee or masala chai.
