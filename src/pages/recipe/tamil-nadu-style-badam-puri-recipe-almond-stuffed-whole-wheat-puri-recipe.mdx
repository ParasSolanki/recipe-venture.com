### Ingredients

- 20 Badam (Almond) - soaked and skin removed.
- Water - to knead.
- 4 tablespoon Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 2 tablespoons Palm jaggery.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Tamil Nadu Style Badam Puri Recipe (Almond Stuffed Whole Wheat Puri Recipe) by kneading the dough into a soft consistency.
1. Cover and rest it for few minutes.
1. Then grind the soaked and peeled almond along with jaggery to a coarse mixture.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat a kadai with oil to fry the puris.
1. Take out a small ball of dough and roll it flat not too thin.
1. Take a teaspoon of the mixture and place it in the center.
1. Bring in the sides and cover it .
1. Make sure there are no holes.
1. Flatten it and roll it again to about 1 centimeter thickness.
1. Fry the puri when the oil becomes hot.
1. Keep flipping it over and fry it so that it evenly get browned on both the side.
1. Take the puri out and strain it over a napkin.
1.  Do the rest for the remaining ingredients.
1. Serve the Tamil Nadu Style Badam Puri Recipe(Almond Stuffed Whole Wheat Puri Recipe) along with Basundi (Rabri) Recipe (A Nutty Condensed Milk Pudding) or gulab jamun to enjoy desserts after your Good sunday meal.
