### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 5 Curry leaves - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- Sunflower Oil - for making dosa.
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - coarsely pounded.
- 1/2 cup Rice flour.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 cup Oats Flour.
- Jaggery - jaggery to taste (optional).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Oats and Coconut Dosa recipe, combine all the ingredients except the oil into a large bowl.
1. Add water a little at a time (about 2 cups) to make a lump free batter.
1. The batter should be of pouring consistency and not very thick.
1. It should be similar to the regular dosa batter or a pancake batter.
1. Heat a skillet on high heat; Pour a ladleful of batter and spread the batter inside out to form a think dosa.
1. Drizzle some oil in and around the sides and cook the oat and coconut dosa until it is steamed from all sides.
1. Cook the dosa on both sides, until you noticed it has browned a little and has lightly crispy edges.
1. To make it extra crispy, cook the dosa on a medium heat and preferably on an iron skillet.
1. Iron skillets maintain heat and brings out a perfect texture to the dosa.
