### Ingredients

-  3 onions - peel and chop.
-  2 teaspoons ginger - tighten.
-  5 tablespoons jaggery - tighten.
-  thyme leaves - a little bit as per use.
-  salt - 2 tablespoons oil - or butter.
-  1 teaspoon all spice powder.
-  5 dates - cut out the seeds.
-  1/3 Cup red wine vinaigrette.
- 2 apples - peel and cut into small pieces.

### Instructions

1. To make apple and onion chutney, first heat oil or butter in a pan.
1. Now add onions and cook till the onion becomes soft and brown.
1. After browning, add ginger, apple, salt, jaggery, red wine vinaigre, all spice powder and thyme leaves.
1. Cook until the apples are soft.
1. Serve.
1. Serve apple and onion chutney with crackers or cheese.
