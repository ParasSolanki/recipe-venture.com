### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Milk.
- Ghee - for cooking.
- 1/4 cup Jaggery - powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Maharashtrian Style Dashmi Roti (Jaggery Flat Bread Recipe) add all the ingredients into a bowl.
1. Add little water and knead it slowly to form a soft and non-sticky dough.
1. Pinch out a small ball of the dough.
1. Heat a Flat bottomed pan.
1. Roll the ball of dough into a circular shape and add the rolled paratha with some ghee and cook it on both the sides for about 2 minutes.
1. Serve the Dashmi Roti Recipe along with spicy matar curry or Srilankan Chicken Curry to enjoy your hot sweetened rotis for your lunch.
