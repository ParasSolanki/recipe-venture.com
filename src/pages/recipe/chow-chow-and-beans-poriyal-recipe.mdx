### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 2 Green Chillies.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 2 Chayote - skin peeled and chopped small.
- 100 grams Green beans (French Beans) - chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chow Chow and Beans Poriyal Recipe, pressure cook the chow chow and green beans with water and salt for about 2 whistle.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally, drain all the water aside and keep it.
1. Heat a saucepan with oil, add in the mustard seeds, cumin seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Once it crackles, add both the dals and also a sprig of curry leaves.
1. Leave it for few seconds.
1. Add onions and saute until it softens.
1. Add your cooked vegetables and sprinkle little salt, turmeric powder and give it a toss.
1. At the end, add grated coconut, switch off the heat and serve it hot.
1. Serve the Chow Chow and Beans Poriyal Recipe along with Pineapple Rasam Recipe, Steamed Rice and Papad to enjoy your everyday meal.
