### Ingredients

- 2-3 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - beaten.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Celery - chopped.
- 1 cup Couscous.
- 1/4 cup Green beans (French Beans) - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Curry powder.
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1/2 cup Spinach - chopped.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Paprika powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Curried Couscous, heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a pan and add garlic and onions.
1. Saute for a minute or until the onions are light brown in colour.
1. Now add green beans, peas, celery, spinach and tomato.
1. Cover it with a lid and cook it for 2-3 minutes just to make the vegetables a little tender.
1. Meanwhile, boil 2 cups of water with salt and one tablespoon of olive oil.
1. In a mixing bowl add couscous and the boiled water.
1. Let it sit for 7-8 minutes.
1. Stir with fork to separate the grains.
1. Add yogurt, turmeric powder, curry powder and paprika powder to the vegetables.
1. Remove from heat and add the veggies to the cooked couscous.
1. Mix it properly and your dish is ready to be served.
1. Serve Curried Couscous with Watermelon Carrot Radish juice for a healthy breakfast.
