### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- 300 grams Tofu - crumbled.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tofu Bhurji Recipe, firstly crumble the tofu and keep it aside.
1. Heat a tablespoon of butter in a heavy bottomed pan; add the cumin seeds, onion, capsicum, green chillies and ginger.
1. Saute the onions and capsicum on low to medium heat until they are soft and tender.
1. Once the onions and capsicum are soft, add the tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chilli powder and give it a good stir.
1. Finally, stir in the crumbled tofu and salt to taste.
1. Stir well, check the salt and spice levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Serve the Tofu Bhurji Recipe along with a Toasted Bread or just plain Indian bread like Phulka and a Dal of your choice like Panchmel Dal, Palak Dal etc to enjoy your dinner.
