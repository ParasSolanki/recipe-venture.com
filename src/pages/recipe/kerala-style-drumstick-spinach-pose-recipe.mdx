### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Coconut Oil - for cooking.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut.
- 2 cups Drumstick Leaves (Moringa/Murungai Keerai) - or Murrungai Keerai.
- 1 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 cup Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kerala Style Murungai Keerai Thoran Recipe, firstly clean the leaves first, try removing twigs and use only the leaves and wash it thoroughly.
1. Then in a mixer grinder, add all the ingredients that are mentioned in the list (fresh coconut, cumin, turmeric powder and chilli powder).
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat a kadai, add oil, splutter mustard seeds and curry leaves.
1. Add in ginger and onions and fry till it becomes translucent.
1. Then add in the ground masala and fry them for 2 minutes and then add in the washed drumstick leaves.
1. Saut√© and combine the masala with the leaves.
1. Sprinkle some water, cover it and cook for about 10 minutes.
1. Then check for seasonings by adding some salt, give it one last stir and serve.
1. Serve the Kerala Style Murungai Keerai Thoran Recipe along with some Steamed Rice and Kerala Style Ulli Theeyal Recipe (Small Onions Roasted in Spicy Tamarind Recipe) for your lunch.
