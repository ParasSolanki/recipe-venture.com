### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread - slices.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 Lemon juice.
- Butter (unsalted) - as required.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spicy Onion Tomato Masala Sandwich Recipe, heat olive oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Once it is hot, add the onions and cook for about 1 to 2 minutes, till the onion becomes soft.
1. Once it is done, add the chopped tomatoes and green chillies.
1. Cook for 2 more minutes until the tomatoes become mushy.
1. Add the spices including salt, chaat masala powder, red chilli powder and lemon juice.
1. Mix everything properly and cook for 3 to 4 more minutes.
1. Switch off the flame and let the masala cook down for about 15 minutes.
1. After 15 minutes, heat a sandwich maker.
1. Take two slice and stuff the masala in between the two.
1. Add butter on the both up and down of the sandwich and roast it till it becomes crispy.
1. Follow the same process with the remaining brands.
1. It is ready to be served.
1. Serve Spicy Onion Tomato Masala Sandwich along with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney for your breakfast along with Plum Ginger Juice Recipe or as a tea time snack.
