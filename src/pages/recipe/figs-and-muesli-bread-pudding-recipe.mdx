### Ingredients

- 4 Fresh Figs - quartered.
- 2 tablespoon Honey.
- 1 cup Milk.
- 4 tablespoon Muesli.
- 2 teaspoon Nutmeg powder.
- 2 Whole Wheat Brown Bread - cut into small triangle.
- 2 Whole Eggs.
- 2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Figs and Muesli Bread Pudding Recipe by pre heating the oven to 180 degree Celsius for 10 minutes.
1. Meanwhile, toast the bread with a teaspoon of oil till it is golden brown and cut them into small triangles and keep it aside.
1. In a mixing bowl crack the eggs and mix all the ingredients and whisk well.
1. This recipe will serve you in two ceramic bowls.
1. Take two ceramic bowls, place the toasted bread, few figs in each of the bowls, sprinkle few muesli and top it up with little egg mixture into each bowl, make sure you pour about a tablespoon in each.
1. Then place the remaining toasted bread in each of the bowl and drop in the remaining figs.
1. Pour in the left over egg mixture equally in both the bowls.
1. Sprinkle it with muesli on top.
1. Bake the Figs and Muesli Bread Pudding for about 25 minutes till it rises up and has a golden crust on top.
1. Serve the Figs and Muesli Bread Pudding Recipe as a dessert after Spinach Pizza with Sun-dried Tomatoes & Feta Cheese to enjoy your meal.
