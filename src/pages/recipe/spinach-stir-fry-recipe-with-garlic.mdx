### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 6 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 4 cups Spinach Leaves (Palak) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mixed Herbs (Dried).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spinach Stir Fry Recipe With Garlic, heat a pan with butter on medium heat, to this add garlic and saute until for a few seconds, until you can get the aromas coming through.
1. To this add the spinach leaves and continue to stir fry until the spinach completely wilts down.
1. This will take about 3-5 minutes on medium-high flame.
1. Once the spinach is done, add the salt, pper and herbs.
1. Mix well and serve.
1.  Serve Spinach Stir Fry Recipe With Garlic along with a meal of Herbed Butter Rice Recipe With Rosemary & Thyme,  Chicken In Lemon Butter Sauce Recipe or Walnut Mushroom Veg Bolognese Recipe With A Twist.
