### Ingredients

- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 2 Sweet Potatoes - peeled and chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish) - cut into strips for garnish.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 2 cups Vegetable stock.
- 6 Red baby radish - chopped (or 2 white radishes).
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta) - or tejpatta or allspice leaves.
- 2 tablespoon Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Creamy Radish soup recipe with Sweet Potato, first, heat oil in a saucepan.
1. Add the onion and saut√© till it becomes soft.
1. Add the carom seeds, fennel seeds and bay leaf.
1. Stir and add the sweet potato and radish.
1. Mix.
1. Add 1/2 cup stock, salt and cook covered till the root vegetables soften.
1. Cool slightly, take out the bay leaf and puree in a blender.
1. Pour it back into the saucepan.
1. Add bay leaf.
1. Adjust the consistency with the stock (this is usually a thick soup).
1. Add pepper and salt (if required).
1. Bring to a boil.
1. Ladle into serving bowls, stir a tablespoon of curd/yogurt and garnish with coriander leaves and strips of radish.
1. Serve Creamy Radish soup recipe with Sweet Potato immediately with Smoked Tofu and Grilled Vegetable Salad for a light meal.
