### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Milk.
- 1/2 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cups Fresh orange juice.
- 1 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- Saffron strands - a few.
- 2 Cloves (Laung) - 2 cloves.
- 1 cup Rice.
- 2 tablespoons Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 Star anise.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Narangi Pulao Recipe wash the rice, and soak it in water.
1. In a bowl, combine the saffron strands along with the milk and microwave for 1 minute.
1. Remove and set aside.
1. Heat a pressure cooker, with ghee on medium flame, add the whole spices - star anise, cinnamon stick, cloves, cardamom, fennel seeds.
1. Next add the washed and drained rice, freshly squeezed orange juice, saffron along with the milk, and salt.
1. Close the pressure cooker and pressure cook for 2 whistles.
1. Turn off the flame, and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Open the cooker once the pressure has released, fluff the Narangi Pulao with a fork and mix in the sugar whilst its hot.
1. Once done, transfer the Narangi Pulao to a serving bowl and serve hot.
