### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1/4 teaspoon Split Mustard Seeds (Rai Kuria).
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 4 Amla (Nellikai/ Gooseberry) - cut and deseeded.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Carrot Amla Raita Recipe With Coconut, in a bowl, add yogurt, salt and beat it with a whisk for a few minutes until it is smooth without lumps.
1. Keep aside.
1. Cut the amla/gooseberries and discard the seeds.
1. Toss the amla in the rai kuria, red chilli powder and salt.
1. Allow it ti rest for 15 minutes.
1. Stir in the grated carrot, green chilli, cut amla and grated coconut into the dahi.
1. Stir well to combine the Carrot Amla Raita Recipe.
1. Check salt and adjust according to taste.
1. To temper the Carrot Amla Raita Recipe With Coconut, place a tadka pan ober medium heat with one 1/4 teaspoon oil.
1. Once the oil is hot, add the mustard seeds and jeera seeds dal.
1. Let the mustard seeds crackle.
1. Stir in the curry leaves and turn off the heat.
1. Pour this tempering over the Gajar Amla Raita and mix well.
1. Serve Gajar Nariyal Amla Raita Recipe along with Hyderabadi Vegetable Biryani Recipe and Mirchi Ka Salan Recipe for a wonderful weeknight dinner.
