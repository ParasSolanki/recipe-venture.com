### Ingredients

- Water - as required.
- 2 cups Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- Few Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 2 tablespoon Ghee - or oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- Phool Makhana (Lotus Seeds) - roasted.
- 8-10 Cashew nuts - optional.
- 1 tablespoon Phool Makhana (Lotus Seeds) - powdered.
- 3-4 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - powdered.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- Few Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 4 teaspoons Singoda Flour (Water Chestnut).
- Rock Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 1/2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Green Chilli - crushed.
- 1/2 inch Ginger.
- 2-3 Cloves (Laung).
- 8-10 Curry leaves.
- Rock Salt - as required (used during fasting).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 5-6 Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - peeled and cubed.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Makhana Moongphali Kadhi with Samvat Rice, firstly we will begin with the kadhi.
1. Add water chestnut flour, makhana powder, peanut powder, rock salt, red chilli powder and cinnamon powder to yogurt in a mixing bowl and whisk everything well.
1. Add water as required and mix well.
1. Next, heat ghee in a kadhai and add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, dry red chillies and curry leaves.
1. Saut√© them till the cumin seeds splutter.
1. Once it splutters, add the yogurt mixture and cook till it thickens slightly.
1. Add sugar, salt and simmer it for another five minutes.
1. Add peanuts and lotus seeds and cook for 2 minutes more.
1. After 2 minutes, garnish it with coriander leaves.
1. The next step is to make the rice.
1. Rinse the sama rice a couple of times in water and soak it in enough water for 20-30 minutes.
1. Dry roast the peanuts on a pan till they become crunchy.
1. Coarsely powder them in a mortar-pestle or in a dry grinder.
1. Heat oil or ghee in a wide pan and add cumin seeds, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom and black pepper.
1. Saute till the oil become fragrant and the cumin crackles.
1. Add the crushed ginger-green chili and saute for half a minute on low flame.
1. Add the cubed potatoes and curry leaves, chopped coriander leaves and saute for 3-4 minutes on a low flame.
1. Next, add the peanuts powder and stir.
1. Drain the sama rice and add to the sauted mixture.
1. Add water & salt and pressure cook till the cooker releases 4-5 whistles or till the water is absorbed and the rice is cooked.
1. While the pulao is cooking, you can dry roast the cashews, peanuts and whole chillies for garnishing.
1. Serve Makhana Moonphali Kadhi with Samvat Rice on its own during your fasting days.
1. Serve it along with Vrat Wale Aloo for a filling and tasty meal.
