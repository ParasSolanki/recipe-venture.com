### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Milk.
- 1/4 cup Walnuts - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Dates - seedless.
- 1/4 cup Pistachios - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Cashew nuts - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Dried Figs - dried.
- 2 tablespoons Ghee.
- 1/4 cup Badam (Almond) - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dry Fruit Halwa Recipe,pulse the nuts in the chopper to get roughly chopped mixture.
1. Keep the nuts aside.
1. Blend the finely chopped dates and figs with 1 tablespoon of milk and ghee until smooth.
1. Add ghee to a preheated pan and add the nuts and the dates and figs blend to the pan.
1. Saute the dry fruit halwa until it gets well combined for 4 to 5 minutes.
1. Keep stirring the halwa every couple of minutes until the mixture comes away from the sides of the pan.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. You can serve the dry fruit halwa as it is or along with vanilla ice cream, or even set into a pan and cut into slices once chilled.
1. To set into a pan, pour and layer this dry fruit halwa mixture into the pan and smoothen it well.
1. Allow it to rest until the mixture is cooled completely and set.
1. Refrigeration will help it set faster.
1. Cut the Dry Fruit halwa into equal squares and store in an airtight container.
1. Serve the Dry Fruit Barfi Recipe for any festivals along with other festival recipes like Dahi Vada, Sukha Kala Chana Masala  and Puri  or as a  Tea Time snack for children.
