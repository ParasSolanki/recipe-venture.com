### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Jaggery.
- 12 Cashew nuts.
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 cup Red matta rice.
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 3 1/2 cups Water.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 2 tablespoons Dry coconut (kopra) - cut into small pieces.
- 1 cup Coconut milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chemba Arisi Thengai Paal Payasam first wash Chemba rice well in water and soak for half an hour.
1. Add soaked rice in a pressure cooker and add three and a half cups of water.
1. Pressure cook rice for five whistles to get soft well cooked rice.
1. While the rice is getting cooked keep a saucepan on medium flame add one fourth cup water and half a cup of jaggery and let the jaggery melt.
1. This will take about five minutes on medium flame.
1. If you like your payasam to be more sweet then u can add more jaggery.
1. Open the pressure cooker and mash the rice lightly using a potato masher or the back of a spoon.
1. Strain the jaggery water and mix it in with the rice.
1. Add the cardamom powder to rice and jaggery mix.
1. Simmer the rice and jaggery mixture for about 5 mins and add coconut milk.
1. Simmer till the Chemba Arisi Thengai Paal Payasam becomes thick and creamy in consistency - about 5 minutes.
1. In a tadka pan add ghee and once the ghee is hot first fry cut coconut pieces till it turns light brown in colour and then add cashew nuts in the  pan and fry till they turn golden brown.
1. Add the fried cashew nut and coconut pieces to the Chemba Arisi Thengai Paal and serve hot.
1. Serve Chemba Arisi Thengai Paal as a dessert with a meal of White Pumpkin Mor Kuzhambu , Steamed Rice and Kerala Style Karamani Thoran for a delicious weekend lunch.
