### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 8 Chicken Wings.
- 3 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 Tomato - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken Wings With Bhuna Masala Recipe, we will first boil the chicken wings in a sauce pot with some water.
1. Boil the wings for about 20 minutes till it's half done.
1. This parboiling method will help the wings to absorb less oil once you fry them.
1. Drain the water and keep the wings in a bowl.
1. Pre heat the oven to 180 degree Celsius and keep it ready until you move on to make the Bhuna masala.
1. Heat a saucepan with oil, add onions, garlic and ginger and saute until for 3-5 minutes.
1. Add tomatoes and sprinkle some salt and cook until the tomatoes are mashed well.
1. Now add in the dry masala powders-  red chilli powder, turmeric powder, garam masala powder, cumin powder and salt.
1. Mix well and turn off the flame.
1. Allow it to cool.
1. Transfer into a mixer-jar and blitz it into a smooth paste.
1. Place the bhuna masala in a mixing bowl add the chicken wings to this and coat it well.
1. Place the wings on a baking tray.
1. Drizzle some oil and bake it in the grill mode for 15 minutes and then flip it over and bake it again for another 15 minutes.
1. Serve the Chicken Wings With Bhuna Masala Recipe along with some Greek Yogurt Dip or Pudina Dahi Chutney as a delicious appetizer.
