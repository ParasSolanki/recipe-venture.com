### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - sour or buttermilk.
- 2 Green Chilli.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 15 Spinach Leaves (Palak).
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - broken.
- 3 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Curry leaves - few.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Palak Tambuli / Udupi Style Spinach Tambli Recipe, clean, wash and roughly chop palak leaves (you can retain tender stems too).
1. In a pan add few drops of ghee and roast jeera, pepper and green chilli.
1. Fry for a minute.
1. Remove from the flame and keep aside to cool.
1. Grind leaves -spice mix, coconut and salt to a very smooth paste in a mixer by adding little water.
1. Pour the mixture to a bowl and add buttermilk / curd .
1. Mix well.
1. Adjust the consistency as required.
1. The consistency should be thin.
1. Heat the remaining ghee in a small pan.
1. Add mustard seeds and jeera, when it splutters add curry leaves .
1. Pour the tempering on the tambli.
1. Serve Palak Tambuli / Udupi Style Spinach Tambli Recipe in room temperature with hot steaming rice and pickle for dinner or relish as such as a drink during evening.
