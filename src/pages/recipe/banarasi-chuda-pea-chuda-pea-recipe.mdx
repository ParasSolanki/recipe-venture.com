### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 3/4 cup Green peas (Matar) - shelled.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Ghee - or clarified butter.
- 1/2 cup Fresh cream.
- 1 cup Poha (Flattened rice).

### Instructions

1. To begin with Banarasi Chooda Matar, wash the poha in the running tap water and soak it in the mix of cream and milk for 10 minutes and keep it aside.
1. Take a wok, pour the ghee into it and let it heat.
1. Now add the cumin seeds and let it crackle.
1. Add finely chopped green chilli & ginger and saute for a while, add the washed shelled green peas.
1. Toss the peas and now add the garam masala and black pepper powder.
1. Cover and let the peas cook.
1. Once the peas is cooked, season with salt, add the finely chopped coriander leaves and give it a good stir.
1. Add the soaked poha to this mix and give it a gentle stir and cover with a lid to cook for a few minutes.
1. Finish off with the lemon juice, give one last stir and serve hot with a cup of ginger tea.
1. Serve Banarasi Chooda Matar/Chura Matar with hot masala chai for a royal breakfast.
