### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 teaspoons Black pepper powder.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 cup Jowar Seeds.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- 2 cloves Garlic - crushed.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit or chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 2 teaspoons Lemon juice.
- Salt - as per taste.
- 1/4 cup Sweet corn - (optional).
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 4 cups Vegetable stock - or water.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Vegetable Jowar Upma Recipe, wash and soak Jowar pearls overnight or for at least 6 - 7 hours in water.
1. Jowar is a millet that takes a long time to cook, so it is important to soak it.
1. Once the Jowar is soaked, drain the excess water.
1. Place the Jowar into the pressure cooker along with 4 cups of vegetable stock or water and salt.
1. Pressure cook for one whistle, turn the heat to low and simmer for about 10 minutes and turn off the heat.
1. After 10 minutes turn off the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. The Jowar will continue to cook in the pressure that exists in the cooker.
1. Open the lid of the pressure cooker, you will see the jowar pearls have chewy texture in comparison to other grains.
1. So you will notice them begin hard.
1. If you like it to be softer, you will need to add a little more water and allow it to cook through for some more time.
1. In the next step, steam the vegetables and keep them ready.
1. See the video of how to steam vegetables in a pressure cooker.
1. Our next step is the make the upma.
1. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat and saute the chopped onion, garlic and ginger for a couple of minutes until it is soft.
1. Once the onion turns translucent, add the peanuts and saute for another couple of minutes.
1. Next add the steamed vegetables, turmeric powder, jowar, salt and black pepper.
1. Adjust the seasonings to suit your taste.
1. Cover the pan with a tight fitting lid, reduce the heat to low and let Jowar Upma cook for about 5 minutes.
1. Turn off the heat and drizzle lemon juice over upma.
1. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
1. Serve Vegetable Jowar Upma Recipe along with Andhra Style Peanut Chutney Recipe With Tomatoes and a glass of freshly squeezed fruit juice.
