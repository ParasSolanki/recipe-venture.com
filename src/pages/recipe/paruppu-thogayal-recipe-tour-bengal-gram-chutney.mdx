### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Tamarind Paste.
- 1/4 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 2 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- Salt - as needed.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 pinch Jaggery.
- 1/2 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1/8 cup Roasted Gram Dal (Pottukadalai).
- 5 Dry Red Chillies.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Paruppu Thuvaiyal recipe, dry roast toor dal, bengal gram, peppercorns, red chillies till the lentils turn golden colour.
1. Set it aside.
1. In the same pan, while it is still hot add the coconut and saute till it dries out.
1. Add just enough water, salt, sugar, coconut, tamarind paste and the dal into the blender jar and blend it into a coarse chutney.
1. Thuvaiyal is usually thick and not runny like chutney.
1. Heat oil in a small tadka pan.
1. Add hing and mustard seeds.
1. Let it crackle.
1. Add the tadka to the thuvaiyal and serve hot.
1. Serve Paruppu Thuvaiyal with hot Steamed Rice  and ghee for lunch or with Ragi and Oats Dosa for breakfast.
