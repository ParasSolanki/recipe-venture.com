### Ingredients

- 1 Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- 1 cup Phool Makhana (Lotus Seeds) - roasted.
- Black Salt (Kala Namak) - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 tablespoons Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - crushed.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf) - crushed.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 4 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Makhana Aloo Tikki Recipe (Spiced Lotus Seed Patties Recipe) by roasting the makhana in a pan with little bit of ghee until crunchy and coarsely powder it.
1. Mash the potatoes and add all the remaining ingredients in a bowl.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Shape the aloo makhane mixture into small cutlets and pan fry them in very little oil until golden brown on both sides.
1. Serve your Makhana Aloo Tikki (Spiced Lotus Seed Patties ) with Dhaniya Pudina No Onion No Garlic Chutney to relish your tea time snack.
