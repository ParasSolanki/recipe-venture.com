### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - thinly sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Cashew nuts.
- 1/2 cup Green beans (French Beans) - thinly sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 cups Basmati rice.
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - whisked.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1/2 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- 4 cloves Garlic - grated.
- 4 tablespoon Ghee.
- 2 cups Vegetable stock - or water.
- 2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 4 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 cup Cauliflower (gobi) - cut into florets.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - for garnish.
- 2 to 3 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1/4 cup Caramelized onions.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 tablespoon Raisins.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vegetable Yakhni Pulao Recipe, first roast all the spices including shahi jeera, coriander seeds, fennel seeds, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon stick and whole black peppercorns in a small pan over medium heat.
1. This will take about 4 to 5 minutes.
1. Once roasted, make a coarse powder and keep aside.
1. Prepare the caramelized onions for garnish.
1. Heat a pan with some oil.
1. Add the thinly sliced onions and cook till it turns brown in colour.
1. Keep it aside.
1. In another small pan, add a tablespoon of ghee and roast the cashewnuts over medium heat until golden brown and crisp.
1. Once golden brown, add the raisins and saute for a few minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Keep this aside for garnish.
1. The final step is to cook the rice with the stock and vegetables.
1. In a large sauce pan over medium heat, add 3 tablespoons of ghee.
1. Add the onions, ginger, garlic, green chilies, bay leaf and saute until the onions soften and you get good aromas.
1. Add the spice powder mix, the chopped vegetables and the rice.
1. Stir for a few minutes until you can see the rice is well coated with the spices.
1. After a few minutes, add the vegetable stock/water and the yogurt.
1. Add the salt and the chopped mint leaves.
1. Stir well to combine.
1. Bring the Yakhni Pulao to a brisk boil.
1. Once it comes to a brisk boil, turn the heat to low, cover the pan and cook the pulao until all the rice is absorbed.
1. The rice should be of grainy texture.
1. Once all the water is absorbed, turn off the heat.
1. Allow the Yakhni Pulao to rest for 10 minutes before you can stir.
1. After 10 minutes, open, add the chopped coriander leaves and give it a stir with a large fork and fluff up the rice.
1. Transfer to a serving dish, garnish with the caramelised onions, cashew nuts and mint leaves.
1. Serve the Yakhni Pulao Recipe along with Paneer Tikka Masala, Punjabi Dal Tadka and Phulka for a special weekend dinner.
