### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Rajma (Large Kidney Beans).
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1 cup Black Urad Dal (Whole).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1-1/2 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh cream.
- 2 tablespoons Butter.
- 1 teaspoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dal Makhani Recipe without Onion and garlic, soak black lentil and rajma overnight in water or at least 8 hours.
1. Take a pressure cooker, put in it the soaked dal then add 1 cup milk, 2 cups water, and garam masala, cardamom powder, salt, chili powder.
1. Allow it to cook for 5 to 6 whistles.
1. After 5 to 6 whistles, turn the heat to low and allow it to cook on low heat for another 20 to 25 minutes.
1. After about 25 minutes, turn off the heat.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, heat 2 tablespoons butter over medium heat, add the cumin seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Pour the tomato puree in the next step, when tomato is well cooked, add the cooked dal makhani into this mixture.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Stir the No onion no garlic dal makhani well, until it forms a creamy mixture.
1. You can also lightly mash it.
1. Finally stir in the cream and simmer on low heat for another 5 minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Serve the Delicious Dal Makhani Recipe without Onion and Garlic along with Naan, Tandoori Paneer Tikka Masala Recipe and a Raita for a delectable weekend lunch or dinner.
