### Ingredients

- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - peeled and diced.
- 2 cups Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves) - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut.
- 3 tablespoons Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 cups Avarekai (Broad Beans) - chopped.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - peeled and diced.
- 1 Sweet Potato - peeled and diced.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 8 Green Chillies.
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 1 tablespoon Jaggery.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kalle Koora/Mixed Vegetable Koora Recipe, roast the sesame seeds on low heat until they are golden.
1. Cool them to room temperature and grind into a coarse powder along with grated coconut and Jaggery.
1. Set aside until needed.
1. In a mixer, add the green chillies along with turmeric powder.
1. Keep aside.
1. Pressure cook the vegetables in 1/4 cup of water for 3 to 4 whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Release the pressure immediately.
1. Place a heavy bottomed pan on medium flame and add a 1 tablespoon of oil.
1. Once the oil is hot add the mustard seeds, cumin seeds and curry leaves.
1. Allow then to crackle.
1. Add in the coconut jaggery sesame mixture and the ground green chilli paste and saute for a minute or two until raw flavour of the green chillies disappears.
1. Next add in the chopped vegetables, stir the Kalle Koora/Mixed Vegetable Koora well and saute for couple of minutes until moisture released from the jaggery evaporates.
1. Turn off the heat, check the salt and spices and adjust accordingly.
1. Transfter the Kalle Koora/Mixed Vegetable Koora to a serving bowl and serve hot.
