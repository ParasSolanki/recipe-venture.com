### Ingredients

- 300 grams Tofu - cut into squares.
- 2 tablespoon Light soy sauce.
- 1 inch Ginger - julienned.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - cut into one inch squares.
- 1/2 cup Mixed vegetables - like broccoli.
- 1 tablespoon Rice Wine Vinegar.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 1 Onion - cut into one inch squares.
- 6 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Tomato Ketchup.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli Paste.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit lengthwise.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tofu Stir Fry Curry, first make the red chilli paste, for this soak 3 dried red chilies for 30 mins and grind to a fine paste.
1. Keep the red chilli paste aside.
1. Heat oil in a non-stick pan and add ginger, garlic and green chilies.
1. Saute on high heat for 30 seconds and add the onions.
1. Cook the onions for 2-3 minutes.
1. The onions should start to become translucent but still be crunchy.
1. Add the tofu, bell peppers and other vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
1. While the vegetables are getting cooked, combine all the ingredients for the curry sauce.
1. Add the curry sauce to the vegetables and stir to combine, until it starts to become thick.
1. Keep stirring as you cook on medium heat for a few minutes till you are happy with the consistency of the sauce and turn off the heat.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. The Tofu Curry is now ready to be served.
1. Serve the Tofu Stir Fry Curry along with a bowl of brown rice and a Beet Carrot and Apple Salad for a quick weeknight dinner.
