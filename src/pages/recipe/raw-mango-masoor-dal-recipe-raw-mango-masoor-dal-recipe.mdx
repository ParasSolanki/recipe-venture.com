### Ingredients

-  2 tablespoons mustard oil.
-  1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds.
-  3 dry red chillies.
-  1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds.
-  1/4 teaspoon Kalonji seeds.
-  1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds.
-  1/2 cup raw mango - cut.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds.
-  1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder.
- 1 cup masoor dal.
-  2 green chillies - thin and straight cut.

### Instructions

1. To make Mango masoor dal, first wash the lentil masala thoroughly.
1. Now put dal, 2 cups water, salt to taste, turmeric powder and green chillies in a pressure cooker.
1. Cook for 2 minutes and let it boil.
1. Add raw mangoes after boiling.
1. Cover the cooker and cook it until you get 2 cities.
1. Allow the pressure to drain automatically.
1. Open the cooker and if you find the lentils thick, then add some more water and mix.
1. Turn on the gas and let it cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Now we will make Tadka for Dal.
1. Heat mustard oil in a tempering pan.
1. Add cumin, rye, kalonji, fenugreek and mix.
1. After 10 seconds, add dry red chilli and cook it for next 15 seconds.
1. Add this tempering to the dal and mix it.
1. Turn off the gas and serve hot.
1. Serve Mango Masoor Dal with Sew Tomato Vegetable, Spinach Raita and Phulka for a day's dinner.
