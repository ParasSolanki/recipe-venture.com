### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Tamarind Paste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 5 cloves Garlic.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 400 grams Flat Green Beans - cut into 1/2 inch pieces.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 3 tablespoons Fresh coconut.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Val Papdi Bhaji Recipe (Goan Style Flat Beans Dry Sabzi), make a coarse mix of the grated coconut, garlic and tamarind extract using a mixer grinder and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a kadai, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, dry red chillies and let it crackle on medium low heat.
1. Add the curry leaves, once mustard crackles and let the curry leaves splutter.
1. Add in the flat beans, turmeric powder, season with salt and mix well.
1. Close it with a lid and let the beans cook in its own steam for 10 minutes.
1. Once the beans are cooked to 3/4th, add in the course ground coconut mixture and mix well.
1. Cook the beans and coconut mix till the raw garlic smell goes away and the sabzi becomes dry.
1. Occasionally keep stirring.
1. Once done, switch off the heat and serve.
1. Serve the Val Papdi Bhaji Recipe (Goan Style Flat Beans Dry Sabzi) along with Phulkas, Smoked Dal Makhani Dhaba Style Recipe and Chickpea And Peanut Salad Recipe for a weeknight dinner.
