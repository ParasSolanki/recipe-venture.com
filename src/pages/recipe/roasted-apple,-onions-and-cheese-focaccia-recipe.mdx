### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 Apples - thinly sliced.
- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Feta Cheese - crumbled.
- 1 teaspoon Nutmeg powder.
- 5 sprig Fresh Thyme leaves.
- 2 teaspoons Active dry yeast.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 2 Onions - thinly sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Roasted Apple, Onions and Cheese Focaccia Recipe pre-heat the oven to 180 degree Celsius for 10 minutes.
1. Make the dough first, so mix all the ingredients in a mixing bowl, add Luke warm water to knead the dough to a soft pizza dough consistency.
1. Keep the dough covered in a warm place to rise.
1. Next is to make the topping, so heat a heavy bottomed pan with oil and add the apple first and sauté till it is caramelized.
1. Later add the sliced onions along with brown sugar, nutmeg powder,and salt, finally, give it a toss.
1. Sauté for 5 more minutes.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Punch the resting dough and knead for 10 seconds, Grease the pan with some oil and dust it with flour, then spread the dough on the pan and press it into a flat shape using your hand.
1. Top the sautéed apple and onions on the bread, brush some honey on the sides of the bread to give it a brown crust.
1. Bake the oven for 30 minutes at 180 degree Celsius.
1. Once done add in some crumbled feta cheese.
1. Serve the Roasted Apple, Onions, and Cheese Focaccia Recipe as a Party Appetizer or as a Tea Time Snack.
