### Ingredients

- Water - as required.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 2 tablespoons Ghee.
- 1 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Nagori Puri Recipe, firstly sift wheat flour to a mixing bowl.
1. Add rava, salt, cumin seeds and melted ghee to it.
1. Mix and rub everything together to make it crumbly.
1. Add water little by little and start kneading to make stiff dough.
1. Divide the dough into small sized balls.
1. Take the balls and roll it flat into thin circles using a rolling pin.
1. Heat oil for deep frying, once hot drop the puri and fry in medium flame until puffed up and slightly changes brown on both the sides.
1. Remove from oil and drain excess oil in kitchen tissues.
1. Fry the puris one by one and serve hot.
1. Serve Nagori Puri along with Gujarati Lasaniya Batata and Palak Raita for a weekend lunch with your family.
