### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 tablespoon Tamarind Paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 tablespoon Jaggery.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 cups Kaddu (Parangikai/ Pumpkin) - cut into cubed.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 tablespoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Tangy Tamarind Pumpkin Curry Recipe, heat oil in a deep pan.
1. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds and let it splutter.
1. Add chopped onion, ginger garlic paste, green chillies, curry leaves and saute it till onion turns golden in colour.
1. Add red chilli powder, coriander powder and turmeric powder.
1. Mix everything well for a minute and add pumpkin.
1. Sprinkle salt over pumpkin and add water.
1. Cover the lid and cook it 3-4 minutes.
1. After 3-4 minutes, check if pumpkin is properly cooked or not if it is not, then let it cook for another minute.
1. If it is cooked then add tamarind paste, you can mix tamarind paste in a bowl with little water and add it to the pumpkin curry.
1. Add jaggery, cook uncovered for 1-2 minutes more and make sure that all the water is absorbed.
1. Serve Tangy Tamarind Pumpkin Curry Recipe with chapati, Spicy yogurt chapatior paratha.
