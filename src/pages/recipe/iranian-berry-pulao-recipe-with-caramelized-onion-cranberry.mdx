### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 tablespoons Cranberries - soaked in hot water.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Cranberries.
- 1 cup Rice - washed and soaked.
- 1 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 tablespoons Milk - hot.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1/3 cup Caramelized onions - to garnish.
- 1 tablespoon Cashew nuts - roasted.
- 1 pinch Saffron strands.
- 1 Star anise.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Iranian Berry Pulao Recipe, wash and soak the rice in water for 30 minutes.
1. In a bowl, combine the hot milk along  with saffron strands, and allow it to release its flavour.
1. In a pressure cooker, heat ghee on medium-low heat, add the bay leaf, cardamom, cinnamon stick, cloves, star anise and let it sizzle.
1. Next add the onions, saute until it turns into a light brown colour.
1.  At this stage add the drained and wash rice, drained cranberries, salt and sugar.
1.  Close the pressure cooker and pressure cook the Iranian Berry Pulao  for 2 whistles.
1. Turn off the flame.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Transfer the Iranian Berry Pulao into a serving bowl, stir in the caramelized onions, drizzle the saffron milk, top with cranberries and roasted cashew nuts.
1. Serve Iranian Berry Pulao Recipe along with Parsi Salli Murgh  for a simple Parsi meal and finish of with a dessert of Parsi Lagan Nu Custard Recipe.
