### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - or greek yogurt (hung yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 2 cups Button mushrooms - cut into 2.
- 1/8 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- 1/2 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - cubed.
- 1/2 cup Onion - chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Gram flour (besan).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mushroom Tikka Masala Dry Recipe, we will first mix yogurt, besan, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, chaat masala powder to form a mixture.
1. Heat a kadai with oil, add ginger and garlic and saute till they soften.
1. Add in onions and bell peppers and saute till the onions turn translucent.
1. Once the onions are softened add mushrooms and cook until all the water is evaporated well.
1. Finally, add in the yogurt besan mixture that was mixed earlier.
1. Mix well and cook until the raw smell goes away and the gravy slightly thickens.
1. Check for salt and sprinkle some kasuri methi and lemon juice and mix well.
1. Finally when the mushroom is cooked well into the gravy, you can switch off the heat and serve hot.
1. Serve the Mushroom Tikka Masala Dry Recipe along with with Pudina Tawa Paratha or Butter Garlic Naan Recipe and Bharma Baingan Aur Pyaz Ki Sabzi Recipe (Spicy Stuffed Brinjals With Onions).
