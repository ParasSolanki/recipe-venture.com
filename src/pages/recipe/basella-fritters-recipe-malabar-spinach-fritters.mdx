### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Enos fruit salt - or baking powder.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1/2 cup Corn flour.
- 200 grams Basella (Malabar Spinach).
- 1/4 cup Water.
- Tomato Ketchup.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder - for the batter.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Sunflower Oil - for frying.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Basella Fritters Recipe (Malabar Spinach Fritters), firstly pick only thin steam spinach leaves.
1. Wash the leaves thoroughly and pat dry with a kitchen towel.
1. Place the leaves in a bowl and sprinkle some red chilli powder, turmeric powder and salt.
1. Take a mixing glass and add all the ingredients needed to make the fritters - Gram flour, Corn flour, Cumin seeds, Enos Salt or Baking Powder, Red Chilli powder and then add a little water to make semi thick consistency with no lumps in the batter.
1. In a frying pan, add some oil and place the heat on medium to low flame.
1. Dip each leaf in batter and drop it in the oil and fry until golden brown.
1. Drain the oil from the fritters and place it on a kitchen paper towel.
1. Serve Basella Fritters Recipe (Malabar Spinach Fritters) with Peanut Chilli Dipping Sauce and a hot cup of Adrak Chai on a cold winter evening.
