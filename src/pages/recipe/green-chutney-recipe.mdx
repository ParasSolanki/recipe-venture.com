### Ingredients

-  1 cup mint - cut.
-  2 green chilies.
-  1 tablespoon ginger - chopped.
-  1 teaspoon sugar.
- 1 cup green coriander - chopped.
-  2 large Spoon lemon juice.
-  1/2 cup curry leaves.
-  salt - to taste.
-  1/4 cup peanuts - or almonds.

### Instructions

1. To make coriander mint chutney, first prepare sari ingredients.
1. Put the ingredients in a mixer grinder.
1. Add a little water and grind it.
1. Grind it well and put it in an air tight container.
1. Serve coriander mint chutney with onion pakoras or chilli pakoras.
