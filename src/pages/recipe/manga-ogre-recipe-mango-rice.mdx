### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/4 teaspoon Jaggery - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2-3 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1-1/2 cups Cooked rice.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a small bunch finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 3-4 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1 Mango (Raw) - peeled and grated.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Manga Ogre first,Grind together in a blender- mango, coconut, mustard seeds, green chillies, turmeric powder, jaggery (if using), little salt and coriander leaves to a fine paste.
1. (Don’t add water to the paste.
1. The rice is already cooked in salted water so add salt just enough for this chutney)Add this chutney to the cooled rice and mix.
1. In a small Kadai/wok heat oil.
1. Add the mustard seeds, once they splutter, add the cumin seeds, the chana dal and sauté for a few seconds.
1. Add the hing and curry leaves and take it off the heat.
1. Pour this tempering over the rice.
1. Mix well.
1. Your Manga Ogre Recipe is ready to be served, serve with some mint and pomegranate raita for a flavourful meal.
