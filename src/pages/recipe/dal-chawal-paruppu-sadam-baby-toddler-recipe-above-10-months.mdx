### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Rice.
- 1 teaspoon Butter.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- Salt - a pinch.
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 1/4 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dal Chawal/ Paruppu Sadam, wash and pressure cook moong dal in quarter cup of water along with a pinch of turmeric.
1. Pressure cook for 3 whistles and release the pressure naturally.
1. Remove the dal from the pressure cooker and keep aside.
1. In the same pressure cooker, add 1/4 cup of rice with 1/2 cup water and pressure cook for 3 whistles.
1. Once the 3 whistles are over, turn off the flame and release the pressure naturally.
1. Place a pan on low heat, add a teaspoon of butter.
1. Once the butter has melted, add the cooked moong dal and a pinch of salt.
1. Mix the dal/ paruppu well and simmer it for 2 minutes and turn off the flame.
1. In a bowl add the cooked rice and cooked dal and mix thoroughly.
1. If you baby prefers a more mashed consistency, use a potato masher and mash the rice before adding the dal.
1. You can also add a little salt to Dal Chawal/ Paruppu Sadam and a dollop of ghee and serve warm to your baby/ toddler.
1. Serve Dal Chawal/ Paruppu Sadam to your babies/toddlers who are above 10 months along with a vegetable like Carrot Poriyal or Buttered Mashed Potatoes to make it a complete meal.
