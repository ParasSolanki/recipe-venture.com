### Ingredients

-  250 grams cabbage - tightly.
-  oil - as per use.
-  1/2 cup curd.
-  1 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  1 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder .
-  2 onions - make paste.
-  2 bay leaves.
-  1 teaspoon red chilli powder .
- 1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste.
-  1/2 inch lentil sugar.
-  1/2 cup gram flour.
-  2 big cardamom.
-  salt - as per use.
-  2 sprig of green coriander - finely chopped.
-  water - as per use.

### Instructions

1. To make Gobi koftas, we will first make Koftas.
1. Put the ingredients for the koftas in a bowl.
1. Add some water and mix well.
1. After mixing, make pakoras and keep them aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan.
1. Add pakoras to it and cook till it turns brown.
1. Take out the kitchen towel so that more oil is released.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add long, bay leaves, big cardamom and cinnamon.
1. After 1 minute, add ginger garlic paste and cook for 5 to 8 minutes.
1. After 5 to 8 minutes, add red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, salt and cook it for 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes, reduce the flame and add yogurt.
1. Mix and add water as per use.
1. Let it cook for 4 to 5 minutes.
1. Now add the koftas and cook for 2 more minutes.
1. Turn off the gas after 2 minutes and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve gobi kofta with boondi raita and tawa paratha for dinner.
