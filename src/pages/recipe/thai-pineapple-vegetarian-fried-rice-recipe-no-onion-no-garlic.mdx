### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 cup Tomatoes - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Dry Fruits - I used black grapes and almonds.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoon Butter.
- 2 cups Cooked rice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Soya sauce.
- 1 cup Pineapple - chopped into cubes.
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - chopped.
- 1 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - add red and yellow as well.
- 1/2 cup Green beans (French Beans) - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Thai Pineapple Vegetarian Fried Rice Recipe (No Onion No Garlic), get ready with all the ingredients first.
1. In a large Wok, add butter and heat.
1. Add green chillies, French beans, green peas and carrots and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Now, add mixed chopped bell peppers and saute for a few minutes.
1. Add soya sauce, salt, sugar, pepper and garam masala and mix it well.
1. Then add cooked rice, pineapple cubes, tomatoes, coriander leaves and dry fruits.
1. Mix well with forks.
1. Switch off flame after a couple of minutes.
1. Garnish the Thai Pineapple Vegetarian Fried Rice with coriander leaves and more dry fruits.
