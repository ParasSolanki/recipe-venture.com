### Ingredients

-  take out 1 teaspoon cumin seeds - 1 tbsp jaggery.
-  ice - as per use.
-  2 lemon-juice.
-  black salt or rock salt - as per taste.
-  3 cups water.
- 1/3 cup curry leaves.
-  1/3 cup peppermint.

### Instructions

1. To make the Lemon Mint Mint Curry Curry Recipe, first of all mix the curry leaves, mint, lemon juice, cumin, jaggery, black salt in a mixer jar and grind it well.
1. Now add water and grind it again as per use.
1. Serve further to cool in the fridge.
