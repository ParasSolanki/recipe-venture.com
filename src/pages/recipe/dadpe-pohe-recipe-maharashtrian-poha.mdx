### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 tablespoons Sev - thin variety.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 2 cups Poha (Flattened rice) - thin variety.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dadpe Pohe Recipe, dry roast the thin variety of poha or flattened rice and keep aside.
1. Save a teaspoon of chopped onions, tomato, grated carrot and grated coconut for the garnish later.
1. Now take a bowl, add the roasted poha, chopped onion, tomato, grated carrot, grated coconut, lemon juice, sugar and salt and mix well with the hands.
1. Leave it to soak in the juices for 10 minutes.
1. Heat oil in a small tadka pan, add the mustard seeds, asafoetida and let it crackle.
1. Add the curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. Add the green chili, peanuts and saute until the peanuts are roasted well.
1. Add this tadka over the softened poha and mix well.
1. To serve, take a portion of the prepared Dadpe Pohe Recipe in a bowl or plate, garnish with the remaining chopped onion, tomato, grated carrot, grated coconut, coriander leaves, nylon sev.
1. Serve Dadpe Pohe along with hot Masala Chai and Linzer Cookies for your everyday breakfast.
