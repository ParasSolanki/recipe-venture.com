### Ingredients

- 1 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 1/2 tablespoon Ginger - paste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 cups Rice - soaked for 10 to 15 minutes.
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 3 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 teaspoon Dessicated Coconut.
- Salt - as required.
- 1 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 teaspoon Poppy seeds.
- 2 to 3 tablespoon Saoji masala.
- 1 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1 cup Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves) - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4 cups Water.
- 1/2 tablespoon Garlic - paste.
- 1 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 3 to 4 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Star anise.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Saoji Methi Pulao Recipe, start by preparing saoji masala first.
1. Dry roast all the spices mentioned for saoji masala like whole black peppercorns, bay leaves, cinnamom stick, black cardamom, cardamom, star anise, cumin seeds, fennel seeds, poppy seeds, dessicated coconut powder except chilli powder.
1. Grind it in Mixer grinder along with chilli powder into a fine powder.
1. (If using dry red chilli, dry roast it along with other spices.
1. )Take oil in Cast Iron Handi, add cumin seeds and then add onion into it.
1. When onion is translucent and looks cooked, add ginger garlic paste.
1. Cook it for 2 to 3 minutes, add tomatoes to it.
1. When the tomatoes become tender, add red chilli powder, and saoji masala with salt.
1. Now wait for the masala to cook well in the mixture, when it is blended well, add green peas and fenugreek leaves into it.
1. Cook for 5 minutes and then add rice with 4 cups of water.
1. Let the rice cook on a high flame.
1. When there is a little water left, lower the flame, cover pan with lid and let the rice fully cook.
1. Serve Saoji Methi Pulao Recipe along with Ajwaini Tamatar Bhindi and Phulka to make it a delicious everyday meal.
