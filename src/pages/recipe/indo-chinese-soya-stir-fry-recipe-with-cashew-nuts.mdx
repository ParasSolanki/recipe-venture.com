### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - for garnish.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 1/2 cup Green beans (French Beans) - chopped into 2 inch pieces.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - cut in larger pieces.
- 1/4 cup Cashew nuts.
- 500 grams Soy Chunks (Nuggets) - or substitute with Chicken chunks.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - cut into thin slices.
- 1/2 tablespoon Rice Vinegar - or regular vinegar.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 1/4 cup Spring Onion Greens - chopped finely.
- 2 tablespoons Sweet and Spicy Red Chilli Sauce (Tomato Chilli Sauce).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Indo Chinese Soya Stir Fry Recipe, we need to first soak the Soya nuggets in hot water.
1. Let the Soya nuggets soak for 10 minutes.
1. Take out the nuggets and squeeze out the water.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, heat one tablespoon sesame oil on medium flame, add the chopped garlic, saute for a minute until the raw smell goes away.
1. Add in the carrots, beans, red bell peppers, sprinkle salt and stir fry on medium heat high until the carrots and beans are cooked and yet have a light crunch to them.
1. You can also optionally cover the pan, so the steam will allow the carrots and the beans to get cooked through.
1. Once the vegetables are cooked, add soaked soy nuggets, soy sauce, vinegar, sweet and spicy red chilli sauce.
1. Give the Indo Chinese Soya Stir Fry Recipe a good stir on high heat for about a minute.
1. Finally, stir in the cashew nuts and the sesame seeds.
1. Give it a toss.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to taste accordingly.
1. Serve Indo Chinese Style Soya Stir Fry, as an appetizer for Indo Chinese dinner of Hakka Noodles and Asian Style Tofu with Roasted Eggplants.
