### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cups Rice - washed.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 cloves Cloves (Laung).
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 2 Onion - thinly sliced.
- inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vagharela Chawal Recipe -  Parsi Style Brown Rice, in a pressure cooker, heat ghee on medium flame, to this add the whole spices - cinnamon, cloves, cardamom.
1. Once the spices begin to sizzle, add the sliced onions and saute till it turns into a deep brown caramelised colour.
1. This will take about 10 minutes on medium heat.
1. Once the onions have caramelised, add the washed and drained rice along with 3 cups of water, salt and close the pressure cooker.
1. Pressure cook the Vagharela Chawal for 3 whistles and turn off the heat.
1.  Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Once the pressure has released, open and fluff the Vagharela Chawal using a fork and transfer to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve the Vagharela Chawal along with Dhansak or Parsi Salli Murgh Recipe - Salli Marghi and salad for a simple weekday lunch or weeknight dinner.
