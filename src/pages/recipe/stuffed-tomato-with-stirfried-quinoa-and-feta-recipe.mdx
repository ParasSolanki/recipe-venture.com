### Ingredients

- 100 grams Quinoa - soaked in water for 2 hours.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Tomatoes - pulp scooped out.
- 50 grams Feta Cheese - cubed.
- 2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - chopped finely.
- 1 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped finely.
- 100 grams Cheddar cheese - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Stuffed Tomato With Stir Fried Quinoa and feta Recipe, heat a pan with oil , add chopped garlic, saute the vegetables and add the feta cheese.
1. Once done add the quinoa and saute for 2 minutes then add 1 and 1/2 cups of water.
1. Cover and let the quinoa boil and get cooked.
1. Let the water evaporate, check for seasoning.
1. Let it cool down and stuff it inside the tomato that has been scooped out and season with salt.
1. Top it up with cheddar cheese and some chopped bell peppers and bake it in the oven for about 10 minutes at 150 degree Celsius.
1. Serve your juicy Stuffed Tomato With Stir Fried Quinoa and feta with  breakfast.
