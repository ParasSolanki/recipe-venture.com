### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 tablespoon Shallots - minced.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 1/4 cup Walnuts - toasted.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dijon Mustard.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/8 cup Lemon juice.
- 1 Grapefruit - cut into segments.
- 1 Avocado - ripe.
- 1 tablespoon White vinegar.
- 1/2 cup Cherry tomatoes - or grapes.
- 1/2 cup Mixed Greens - like spinach.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Avocado, Grapefruit and Walnut salad, slice the avocado, grapefruit and tomatoes and set them aside.
1. In a small frying pan, over a low heat, gently toss the walnuts till they are fragrant and toasted.
1. Once well toasted, set the walnuts aside to cool.
1. In a medium bowl whisk together the ingredients for the dressing: lemon juice, olive oil, dijon mustard, vinegar, soy sauce and honey till it is creamy.
1. Add in the minced shallots, salt and pepper and whisk once again.
1. Set the dressing aside until you are ready to mix and serve the salad.
1. In a large salad serving plate, layer the mixed greens and arrange the grapefruit and avocado slices over it.
1. Scatter the halved tomatoes and toasted walnuts all over.
1. Drizzle the prepared dressing generously over the salad and gently toss before you serve.
1. Serve Avocado, Grapefruit And Walnut Salad along with Roasted Vegetable Pizza With Oat Flour Crust for a weeknight dinner.
