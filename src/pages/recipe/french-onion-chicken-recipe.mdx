### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Dijon Mustard.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1-1/2 cups Chicken stock.
- 1/4 cup Gruyere Cheese.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 1 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 5 Onions - big french onions.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 250 grams Chicken thighs - cut into 2.
- 1 teaspoon Dried Thyme Leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the French Onion Chicken recipe, clean and wash the chicken thighs well.
1. Cut them into 2 pieces.
1. I used chicken without skin.
1. Add salt, pepper powder to the chicken pieces and mix well.
1. Marinate it for about 30 minutes inside fridge.
1. In a cast iron pan add olive.
1. Add crushed garlic and sauté until it turns brown.
1. Now add the marinated chicken pieces.
1. Roast it until it turns brown on both sides.
1. Remove from the pan and set it aside.
1. Now add butter and let it melt.
1. Add thin sliced onions to the pan and cook on low flame until the onions caramelize.
1. It would take some time to caramelize the onions.
1. Be patient.
1. Once the onions are caramelized, add thyme and sauté for a minute.
1. Add flour, whisk well.
1. Make sure no lumps are formed.
1. Add chicken stock, continue to whisk and cook until thickened.
1. Add the roasted chicken into the pan, cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
1. Finally add vinegar and Dijon mustard mix well and cook for 3 minutes.
1. Now add grated Gruyere cheese on top of the chicken, broil it for 2 to 3 minutes until cheese melts and bubbles up.
1. Serve French Onion Chicken along with Roasted Garlic No Knead Skillet Bread Recipe With Olives and Summer Salad Recipe-Red Cabbage, Lettuce Onions & Tomatoes for a  lip smacking Sunday Brunch.
