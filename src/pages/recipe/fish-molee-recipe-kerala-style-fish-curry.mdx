### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Ginger - paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 50 ml Coconut milk.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1/4 Lemon juice.
- 300 gram Fish.
- 1/4 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Fish Molee Recipe, firstly wash the fish cutlet pieces and season with salt.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, heat some oil on medium flame.
1. Add cumin seeds and let it crackle.
1. Add chopped onion and saute until the onions are translucent.
1. Add garlic, ginger paste and saute until the raw smell of garlic and ginger goes away.
1. Add in the chopped tomatoes and saute until the tomatoes are mushy and oil starts to leave from the sides.
1. This will take 3 to 4 minutes.
1. After 3 to4 minutes add in turmeric powder and red chili powder, mix well and pour a cup of water into the pan.
1. Once the water reaches the boiling point, lower the flame, add coconut milk and the fish pieces.
1. When the fish is fully cooked, add lime juice and vinegar.
1. Add appropriate amount of salt and give the curry a good stir.
1. Turn off the flame.
1. Serve Fish Molee along with Appams or Steamed Rice for a simple weekday Lunch.
1. You can also make it for your weekend Lunch with other Kerala recipes.
