### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/4 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 3 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 1/2 teaspoon Paprika powder.
- 100 grams Feta Cheese - crumbled.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 250 grams Chicken - minced.
- 1 cup Spinach Leaves (Palak) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 10 sprig Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Turkish Chicken Gozleme Recipe, we will first knead the dough for the bread and keep it ready.
1. In a mixing bowl add all the flour, curd, salt to taste and knead well with little water to form a firm dough.
1. Once done cover with a damp cloth and rest the dough.
1. For the Gozleme StuffingHeat a skillet on medium heat with oil, add chopped garlic and saute till it softens.
1. Add chopped onions and saute till they turn golden brown.
1. Add in tomatoes and sprinkle little salt and cook until the tomatoes turn mushy.
1. At this stage add the minced chicken and add all the spice powders, paprika, cumin, coriander and pepper.
1. Saute till the chicken is cooked.
1. This will take about 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Once done add chopped spinach leaves and mint leaves to the chicken Gozleme mixture and saute till the leaves wilt and all the moisture is evaporated.
1. Check for seasonings and adjust if required.
1. Once done turn off the heat and allow it to cool down.
1. To make the Chicken GozlemeDivide the dough the into 3 equal portions.
1. Take one portion and flatten it on the rolling surface, dust it with some flour and roll it flat to thin  circle.
1. Add the chicken stuffing in the centre, sprinkle the chopped spring onions and crumbled feta cheese on top.
1. Fold the dough from either side and seal it tightly so that it doesnâ€™t open up.
1. You will get a rectangular shaped flat stuffed bread - Chicken Gozleme.
1. Heat a flat skillet, place the Chicken Gozleme and toast it on either side for couple of minutes until you see the dough is getting partially cooked.
1. Drizzle oil on the Chicken Gozleme and cook till your notice brown spots on both sides.
1. Once done, remove the Chicken Gozleme from the pan and serve hot.
1. Serve the Turkish Chicken Gozleme Recipe along with Pickled onions, lemon wedges and Couscous Raw Mango Salad by the side to make weekend meals special.
