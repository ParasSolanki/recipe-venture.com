### Ingredients

- 1 sprig Curry leaves - roughly torn.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Drumstick - cut into 1 inch.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Rice - soaked in warm water.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Coconut Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds - roasted.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Murungakkai Mor Kuzhambu Recipe, first steam the drumstick in the pressure cooker, adding 2 tablespoons water.
1. Cook until you hear two whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Release the pressure immediately so the green color remains and the drumsticks do not over cook, keep aside.
1. Grind the coconut, raw rice, cumin seeds, coriander seeds and red chillies into a smooth paste.
1. Add a little warm water to help it grind well.
1. Add the yogurt, the coconut mixture and the cooked drumstick into a sauce pan and bring it to a brisk boil and turn off the heat.
1. For the seasoning, heat oil in a tadka pan over medium heat.
1. Add the mustard seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Stir in the curry leaves and turn off the heat.
1. Pour the seasoning over the Murungakkai Mor Kuzhambu and serve.
1. Serve the Murungakkai Mor Kuzhambu Recipe along with Chepakizhangu Roast and steamed rice to enjoy you afternoon Lunch.
