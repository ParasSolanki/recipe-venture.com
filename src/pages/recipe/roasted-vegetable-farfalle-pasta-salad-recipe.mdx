### Ingredients

- 3 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 4 Asparagus - Stem cleaned and chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Paprika powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Green beans (French Beans) - sliced diagonally.
- 1 Onion - chopped coarsely.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - sliced diagonally.
- 150 grams Del Monte Farfalle Pasta.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 2 sprig Basil leaves.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Roasted Farfalle Pasta Salad Recipe start by keeping the ingredients ready to make the salad.
1. *To cook the pastaIn a large pot, add water and bring it to a boil.
1. Once the water has boiled, add a teaspoon of salt and a teaspoon of oil.
1. Add the pasta to the boiling water and let it sit in the boiling water until it is cooked.
1. This process should take a good 12-15 minutes.
1. Once the pasta has cooked, drain the water and run the cooked pasta under running water to stop the cooking process.
1. Drizzle some oil on top of pasta and make sure to coat all of the pasta with the oil*To make the Roasted Tomato Sauce Heat a skillet with oil, add chopped garlic and saute till it softens.
1. Add in chopped onions and saute till the onions turn out light brown.
1. This will take about 5 minutes.
1. Add chopped tomatoes, sprinkle salt, paprika powder, pepper powder, sugar and roast the tomatoes well.
1. You can saute for about 15 minutes on low flame.
1. Once done cool it down and and blend it in a mixer to a smooth paste.
1. Check for salt if you require any you can adjust it now.
1. *To make the Farfalle Pasta Salad RecipeHeat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat; add carrots and green beans, sprinkle salt and roast them until it becomes soft and tender.
1. Add in red bell peppers and asparagus and roast them also well till it is soft and crunchy.
1. Finally add in green peas and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Ensure the color of the vegetables don't get pale - they should retain their fresh colors.
1. Once the vegetables are well roasted, transfer it to a large mixing bowl.
1. Toss in the cooked Farfalle pasta, the roasted tomato sauce, seasonings (salt, pepper and oregano) and torn basil leaves.
1. Toss the Roasted Vegetable Farfalle Pasta Salad well, check the taste and adjust the seasonings accordingly.
1. Serve the Roasted Vegetable Farfalle Pasta Salad Recipe along with glass of Plum Ginger Juice Recipe or a French Onion Soup Recipe for dinner or lunch.
