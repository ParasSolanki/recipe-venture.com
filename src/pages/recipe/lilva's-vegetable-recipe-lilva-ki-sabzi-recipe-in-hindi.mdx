### Ingredients

-  1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste.
-  2 tablespoons curd.
-  1 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  1 potato - cut.
-  3 sprig green coriander - finely chopped.
-  3/4 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  1 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  1 bay leaf.
-  1 onion - paste 1 onion - finely chopped.
-  2 long.
-  1 big cardamom.
- Oil - As per use.
-  make 1 cup green gram - or lilwe.
-  salt - as per use.
-  water - as per use.

### Instructions

1. To make Lilve vegetable, first heat oil in a pan.
1. After the oil is hot, add bay leaves, long and cardamom.
1. After 15 seconds, add onion and cook till it becomes soft.
1. Now add potatoes and cook for another 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes add ginger garlic and onion paste.
1. Let it cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. After 3 minutes add red chili powder, turmeric powder and coriander powder to it.
1. After cooking for 2 minutes, add curd, salt and mix it.
1. Add green gram after 3 minutes, mix it, cover the pan and let it cook for 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes, add water as per use and let it cook.
1. Turn off the gas after 4 to 5 minutes and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve the lilve vegetable along with punchy lentils, boondi raita and phulka for dinner.
