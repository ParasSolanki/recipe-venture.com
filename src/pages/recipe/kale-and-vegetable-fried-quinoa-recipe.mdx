### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 cup Green peas (Matar).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - diced.
- 2 cups Kale - stemmed and chopped.
- 1 cup Onion - chopped.
- 2 cups Quinoa.
- 1 cup Celery - chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Garlic - minced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kale And Vegetable Fried Quinoa  recipe, cook 2 cups of quinoa with 4 cups water, a spritz of olive oil and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
1. Bring the quinoa mixture to a rolling boil on high heat, about 5 minutes.
1. Lower the heat, cover and let it simmer until they are cooked completely, about 10 minutes.
1. Fluff the quinoa with a fork and set aside to cool.
1. Add a tablespoon of olive oil and minced garlic into a pan on medium heat.
1. Add in onions, peas and carrots.
1. Turn the heat to medium high and saute for a few minutes.
1. Add in the chopped kale and saute for 2 minutes.
1. Toss in the quinoa and combine the mixture.
1. Add salt and pepper; toss them all together gently.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. Devour!Serve Kale And Vegetable Fried Quinoa Recipe on its own along with a cup of hot Masala Chai for breakfast.
