### Ingredients

- 1 pinch Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1/2 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- Sunflower Oil - as needed to shallow fry.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/4 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- Water - as needed.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1/4 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 cup Cooked rice.
- 1/2 cup Potato (Aloo) - cooked & mashed.
- 1/2 tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Spicy Mixed Vegetables Rice Cutlet Recipe, take a mixing bowl and add cooked rice,mashed boiled potato,chopped onion,chopped green chilli,grated carrot,chopped coriander leaves, mix everything well.
1. Now add all the spices,start with turmeric powder,red chilli powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala and salt.
1. Mix everything well and make sure that cooked rice also get mashed properly.
1. Shape the mixture in medium size patties.
1. Take another bowl and add all purpose flour and water, mix them to get pouring consistency batter.
1. Put some plain flour on one side and bread crumbs on other side on your work surface.
1. Roll Patties in plain flour first, then dip them in all purpose flour batter and then in bread crumbs.
1. Repeat the same process, first plain flour, then dip in all purpose flour and then in bread crumbs.
1. Now heat 3-4 tablespoon oil in a frying pan or skillet, place the cutlets on pan or skillet .
1. Let it cook until the base is lightly browned and crispy, flip them and fry the other side .
1. Flip again and fry them till they are crisp and golden.
1. Once its nicely crisp and golden, remove the cutlets from pan and place it on paper towel.
1. Spicy Mixed Vegetables Rice Cutlet Recipe are ready to be served with Green chutney and date-tamarind chutney.
