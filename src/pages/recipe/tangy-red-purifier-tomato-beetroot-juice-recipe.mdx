### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/4 teaspoon Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- Black Salt (Kala Namak) - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/2 Cucumber - peeled and cut into cubes.
- 1 Beetroot - peeled and cut into cubes.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Tangy Red Purifier- Tomato Beetroot Juice Recipe, prepare all the vegetables as mentioned, after cleaning them thoroughly.
1. Take a juicer and add all the ingredients.
1. Blend for a few seconds till coarsely done and then add a little water.
1. Grind till you get a smooth consistency.
1. At this stage, check for salt and add more water to get the desired juice consistency.
1. Give it a quick pulse once again till everything is well combined.
1. Chill Tangy Red Purifier - Healthy Fresh Juice Recipe in the refrigerator for some time (if needed) or serve immediately.
1. Serve Tangy Red Purifier- Tomato Beetroot Juice Recipe with Multigrain Crepe Recipe Filled With Spicy Mashed Potatoes for a wholesome breakfast.
