### Ingredients

- 30 ml Coconut milk.
- 1 teaspoon Chia Seeds.
- 5 Badam (Almond).
- 1 cup Spinach - chopped.
- 2 Ripe Bananas - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spinach and Banana Smoothie, add all the ingredients into the mixer except the chia seeds and add little water and grind it into a smooth paste.
1. You can add little more water and adjust according to the consistency that you need.
1. Pour it in the glass and sprinkle with chia seeds and serve.
1. Serve the Spinach and Banana Smoothie Recipe as an post workout drink, giving you that immense energy and nutrition.
