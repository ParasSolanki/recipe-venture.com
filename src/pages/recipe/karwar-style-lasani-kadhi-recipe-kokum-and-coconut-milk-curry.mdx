### Ingredients

- 6 Kokum (Malabar Tamarind).
- 3 Green Chillies.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Jaggery - to taste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - as required to garnish (chopped).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1-1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated or 2 cups coconut milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Karwar Style Lasani Kadhi Recipe (Kokum & Coconut Milk Curry), soak kokum peels in 1 cup hot water for 30 to 45 minutes.
1. Squeeze well and discard the left over.
1. In a mixer take coconut, cumin seeds, garlic and green chillies.
1. Add 1 cup warm water and grind to a smooth paste.
1. Sieve to extract coconut milk.
1. For the left over coconut solids, add some water and regrind.
1. Sieve to get thin coconut milk.
1. To the coconut milk, add kokum juice.
1. Add salt and jaggery.
1. Mix and add water if required to get desired consistency.
1. Next, prepare the tempering.
1. Heat oil in a tadka pan.
1. Ass Mustard seeds, cumin seeds and curry leaves.
1. Saute for 10 seconds and pour it on the kadhi.
1. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serve hot.
1. Serve Karwar Style Lasani Kadhi (Kokum & Coconut Milk Curry) with Karwar Style Muga Ambat, Phulka and Steamed Rice for a weekday meal.
