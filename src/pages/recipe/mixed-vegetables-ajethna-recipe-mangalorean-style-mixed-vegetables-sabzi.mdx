### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - cut into small cubes.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - broken.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 cup Green beans (French Beans) - finely chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - cut into small cubes.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Mixed Vegetables Ajethna Recipe (Mangalorean Style Mixed Vegetables Sabzi), wash and cut all the vegetables and keep aside.
1. Make a dry course mix of the grated coconut, dried red chillies and mustard seeds using a mixer grinder and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan, add the mustard seeds, urad dal and let it crackle.
1. Add the asafoetida, red chilli, curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. Add the vegetables, season with salt and mix well.
1. Add the turmeric powder, mix well and let the vegetables cook in its own steam with a lid closed.
1. This will take around 10 minutes.
1. Once the vegetables are well cooked, add the coconut masala and mix well.
1. Saute for 5 minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Serve the Mixed Vegetables Ajethna Recipe (Mangalorean Style Mixed Vegetables Sabzi) along with Steamed Rice, Bendekayi Koddel/Bolu Huli Recipe and Akki Peni Sandige (Rice idiyappam Fryums) for a Mangalorean inspired weekend lunch.
