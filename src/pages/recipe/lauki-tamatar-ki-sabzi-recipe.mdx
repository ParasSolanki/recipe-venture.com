### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 3 Garlic - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Bottle gourd (lauki) - (about 4 cups of diced bottle gourd).
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - to cook.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lauki Tamatar Ki Sabzi Recipe, firstly prepare and get all the ingredients ready.
1. We will be using the pressure cooker method to cook lauki tamatar ki sabzi recipe.
1. Start with heating oil in cooker.
1. To the hot oil, add cumin seeds, onions, garlic and tomatoes; sauté for a few minutes.
1. Add turmeric powder, chilli powder, curry leaves, salt and lauki.
1. Add 1/4 cup of water and pressure cook for about 3 whistles.
1. Turn off heat.
1. Once the pressure is released, open the cooker.
1. You will notice that the lauki has given out a lot more water.
1. Turn heat on, stir the lauki in the cooker.
1. Some of the water would have evaporated.
1. You can boil it till you get the consistency that you desire and the ingredients would have all bound together.
1. At this point, turn off heat.
1. Garnish the Lauki Tamatar ki sabzi with coriander leaves and serve hot.
1. Serve the Lauki Tamatar Ki Sabzi along with Phulkas and Mint Raita for a wholesome dinner or a lunch.
