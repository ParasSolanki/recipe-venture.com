### Ingredients

- 1 cup Boneless chicken.
- 2 teaspoons Soy sauce.
- 3-4 Iceberg lettuce - shredded.
- 2 cups Cooked rice.
- Salt - as needed.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder - freshly crushed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chinese Fried Rice Recipe With Chicken And Shredded Lettuce, heat oil in a chinese wok and add chicken pieces to it.
1. Saute till chicken is soft and cooked.
1. Now add soy sauce, salt, black pepper powder and saute for another few seconds.
1. Add shredded lettuce and mix well and fry for 4-5 mins till lettuce is partially tender.
1. Take 2 cups of cooked rice and add in the chicken mix.
1. Toss for couple of minutes till everything incorporates well.
1. Turn off the gas and transfer to a platter and serve.
1. Serve Chinese Fried Rice Recipe With Chicken And Shredded Lettuce with Soy Chunks Manchurian Recipe and Chinese Darsaan Recipe With Ice Cream for dessert.
