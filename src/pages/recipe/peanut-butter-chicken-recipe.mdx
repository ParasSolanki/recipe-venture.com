### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Tomato.
- 300 grams Chicken breasts - boneless.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 3/4 cup Chicken stock.
- 1 tablespoon Chilli oil - (can be substituted with 1/8 teaspoon smoked paprika).
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 Onion.
- 3/4 cup Peanut Butter - smooth.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Peanut Butter Chicken recipe, heat oil in a large skillet, add onion and stir until translucent.
1. Once the onions are soft and translucent, add the chicken pieces; cook until chicken starts to turn white.
1. Add chilli oil, followed by salt and pepper.
1. Keep the heat on medium through the whole process and continue stirring.
1. The entire process should take 5-7 minutes.
1. Add tomatoes, chicken stock and let it simmer on low heat until the chicken is tender.
1. When the chicken is tender, stir in peanut butter, stirring continuously until mixed thoroughly and the sauce starts to thicken.
1. If the sauce is too thick, you could add some more chicken broth or hot water or if its not thickening, add some more peanut butter.
1. Give it a stir and when the chicken is done, serve hot.
1. Serve Peanut Butter Chicken with Lemon Rice on its own or as a side dish along with Srilankan Chicken Curry and Tawa Paratha for a weekend meal.
