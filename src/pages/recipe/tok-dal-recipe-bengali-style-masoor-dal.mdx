### Ingredients

- 2 to 4 Green Chillies.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Panch Phoran Masala.
- 1 cup Masoor Dal (Whole).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Mustard oil.
- 1 Mango (Raw) - tender and medium sized.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tok Dal recipe, peel and slice the mangoes, along with the seed if possible.
1. Wash and soak masoor dal for half an hour.
1. Pressure cook mango, dal, turmeric and slit green chillies together till the dal is cooked.
1. Mangoes should still hold the shape and not be overcooked.
1. Once the cooker is cooled, open the lid and mash just the dal a little.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, heat the mustard oil.
1. Add bay leaf, paanch phoran and red chilly to it.
1. Let it crackle.
1. Add the cooked dal and 1/2 cup water to the tempering.
1. Add salt to taste and boil till the dal thickens.
1. Once it is done, switch off the stove an it is ready to be served.
1. Tangy, creamy and light, Tok dal goes best with steamed rice and a dollop of ghee.
