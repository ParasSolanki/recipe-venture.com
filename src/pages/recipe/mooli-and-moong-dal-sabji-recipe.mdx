### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 2 Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish) - peeled and grated coarsely.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Mooli Ke Patte (Radish Greens) - chopped (handful).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar - (optional).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 4 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split) - soaked in warm water for 30 minutes.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4 cloves Garlic - sliced (optional).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mooli and Moong Dal Sabji Recipe, firstly heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add the mustard seeds and once they splutter, add the cumin seeds.
1. Let it cook for 30 seconds.
1. After 30 seconds, add garlic, asafoetida, turmeric powder, green chillies and saute for a few seconds.
1. Drain, add the moong dal and stir.
1. Cook covered for 2-3 minutes till the dal is almost cooked.
1. Add a tablespoon of water if required.
1. Add the radish and greens.
1. Add salt, sugar, stir and cook for 1-2 minutes till the radish softens a bit.
1. Do not overcook.
1. It is ready to be served.
1. Serve Mooli And Moong Dal Sabji along with Gujarati Kadhi, Kachumber Salad and Phulka for a weekday lunch.
