### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Mustard seeds - + 1 teaspoon extra.
- 4 Green Chillies.
- 1 Onion.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 4 Whole Eggs - boiled.
- 200 grams Coconut milk.
- Sugar - a pinch.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Panch Phoran Masala.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mustard Egg Curry recipe, grind together 1-1/2 tablespoon mustard seeds, green chillies, grated coconut, turmeric together using little coconut milk just to help in grinding.
1. Once done, keep it aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan.
1. Add 1 teaspoon mustard seeds.
1. As it crackles, add curry leaves and onions to it.
1. Saute it till the onions soften.
1. Once the onions are soft and translucent, add the ground masala to this and add 1/4 cup water and let it come to a boil.
1. Add the remaining coconut milk, salt and a sugar and bring to a boil.
1. To this add cut pieces of egg and boil it till it reaches a creamy gravy consistency.
1. Serve Mustard Egg Curry along with Tawa Paratha or Steamed Rice and Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita for a weekday meal.
