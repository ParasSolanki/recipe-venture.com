### Ingredients

- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 3 teaspoons Caster Sugar.
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 2 tablespoons Butter - melted.
- Chocolate sauce - as required.
- 1 Ripe Bananas - sliced.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- Butter (unsalted) - as required.
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 2 tablespoons Cocoa Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cooking soda.
- 3 to 5 tablespoons Buttermilk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chocolate Pancakes With Banana Recipe, firstly mix together all the dry ingredients including plain flour, cocoa powder, caster sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt in a mixing bowl.
1. Mix everything properly and add vanilla essence, melted butter and buttermilk.
1. Make sure everything is mixed and no lumps are formed.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat some butter on a cast iron skillet and spread some pancake batter on it.
1. Let it cook from both the sides.
1. Once it is done, take it on a plate.
1. Drizzle some chocolate sauce and spread some banana slices on the top.
1. Serve Chocolate Pancakes With Banana Recipe along with Papaya Banana Smoothie or Carrot Cashew Smoothie for a filling and scrumptious breakfast.
1. You can also serve Espresso Coffee along with these Pancakes.
