### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1 kg Mutton.
- 1/2 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 cup Tamarind Paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Onion - minced.
- 10 Curry leaves.
- 500 grams Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra).
- 3/4 tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Coconut milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bhindi Gosht recipe, heat half oil in pressure cooker, add onion to it and saute it till the onion turns translucent.
1. Add ginger garlic paste, add all the spices to it and cook for few minutes.
1. Add the meat to the above mixture along with coconut milk and curry leaves cook till the meat is tender.
1. Pressure cook till 15-20 minutes.
1. In another pan, add oil and saut√© big chunks of okra till 3/4 is cooked.
1. Drizzle some lemon juice on it and mix properly.
1. When done keep it aside.
1. Open the pressure cooker and add tamarind pulp and okra.
1. Mix everything well and let it cook for another 5-10 minutes till okra is completely cooked.
1. Serve Bhindi Gosht along with Tawa Paratha and Jeera Rice for your weekday meal.
