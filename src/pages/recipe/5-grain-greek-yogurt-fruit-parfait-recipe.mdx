### Ingredients

- Fresh fruits - sliced (Seasonal).
- 4 tablespoons Chia Seeds.
- 8 Multigrain Digestive Biscuits - broken.
- 2 tablespoons Honey.
- 1 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the 5 Grain Greek Yogurt Fruit Parfait, first prep all the ingredients and keep it ready.
1. Pulse the NutriChoice 5 Grain Digestive Biscuits in a mixer to make coarse crumbs and keep aside.
1. Whisk the greek yogurt with honey and keep aside.
1. Slice the seasoning fruits and keep aside.
1. To assemble the 5 Grain Greek Yogurt Fruit Parfait, into a jar, spoon the crumbled NutriChoice 5 grain digestive biscuits.
1. Add 3 tablespoons greek yogurt,  layer with seasonal fruits, top it with a tablespoons of chia seeds, some more greek yogurt and finally sprinkle biscuit crumble on top.
1. The 5 Grain Greek Yogurt Fruit Parfait is now ready.
1. Refrigerate it for at least 2 hours before serving.
1. Serve the 5 Grain Greek Yogurt Fruit Parfait as a snack for your office or an evening snack at home.
1. You can also serve the 5 Grain Greek Yogurt Fruit Parfait for breakfast along with Indian Style Masala Omelette Grilled Sandwich for a wholesome breakfast.
