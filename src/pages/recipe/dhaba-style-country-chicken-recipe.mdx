### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Ginger - paste.
- Salt - as required.
- Water - as required.
- 750 grams Chicken - curry cut.
- Mustard oil - as required.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Onion - paste.
- 4 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 2 tablespoons Garlic - paste.
- 2 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 tablespoon Fresh Red Chilli - paste.
- 1 inch Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dhaba Style Country Chicken recipe, firstly marinade the chicken with turmeric, salt, ginger paste, garlic paste, red chilli paste, 1 tablespoon onion paste and 1 tablespoon mustard oil for about 30 minutes.
1. Rub the spices well using your fingers.
1. Heat 3 tablespoons oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Once oil reaches it's smoking point cool down a bit.
1. Then heat it again, add bay leaf, cinnamon and cardamom.
1. After a minute, add chopped onion and fry till they are golden brown.
1. Add onion paste and fry for 2 minutes.
1. Add Chicken to it and saute at high flame for least 5 minutes.
1. Make a paste of all masala powder adding 2 tablespoons of water.
1. Add this paste to chicken and mix well.
1. Adjust salt according to your taste.
1. Add 2 cups of warm water to it & cook it covered until they are done.
1. You can add more water if you want more gravy.
1. Serve Dhaba Style Country Chicken with hot phulkas and jeera rice for a delicious meal.
