### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1 Cucumber - finely diced.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera) - ground.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cucumber Green Chilli Raita prep all the vegetables and keep them aside.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the cucumber, tomato, carrot, green chili along with the plain yogurt.
1. Whisk well and add the black salt, salt and pepper and give it a good mixAt the end sprinkle some cumin powder and red chilli powder as a garnish and serve.
1. Serve the Cucumber Green Chilli Raita as a side dish along with Chana Dal Pulao Recipe or Mumbai Style Tawa Pulao Recipe With Moong Sprouts.
