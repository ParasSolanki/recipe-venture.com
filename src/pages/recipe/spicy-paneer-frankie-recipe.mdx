### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1-2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 200 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - (cut into small cubes).
- 2 tablespoons Roasted tomato pasta sauce.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 Corn flour tortilla - or whole wheat flour tortilla.
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 1/2 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - shredded.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder - crushed.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 4 tablespoons Cheese spread.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 2 tablespoons Butter.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Spicy Paneer Frankie Recipe, heat oil in a wok and add chopped garlic and green chilies, saute for a while.
1. Now add chopped onions and capsicum along and saute till raw smell goes off.
1. Add all the three sauces with salt and freshly crushed pepper, stir and let it cook for half a minute.
1. Once the sauce starts to thicken, add in the paneer pieces into it and mix well.
1. Cook for another minute and turn off the heat.
1. Keep aside to cool.
1. Heat a nonstick pan/tawa and add some butter and grease it well.
1. Once the pan is hot, place one flour tortilla and roast it till golden from both sides.
1. Adjust butter if needed.
1. Transfer the butter roasted tortilla on a plate and take a spoonful of cheese spread and place it on the tortilla.
1. Spread it evenly with a butter knife or spoon and add shredded cabbage on it.
1. Now take around 2-3 tablespoons of paneer mix and place it horizontal in the center.
1. Fold in one corner and start rolling the tortilla tight.
1. Fix it with a toothpick or wrap it with a tissue paper or foil.
1. Serve Spicy Paneer Frankie Recipe hot as an evening snack over a cup of hot green tea.
