### Ingredients

-  2 tablespoons white urad dal (split).
-  3 Green Chillies - Salted.
-  1 teaspoon Cumin seeds.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 tomato - finely chopped.
-  water - as per use.
-  1 teaspoon Rye.
-  1 Large Cardamom.
-  4 Long.
-  2 tablespoons yellow moong dal.
-  1 -1/2 turmeric powder.
-  2 Onions - Thin and straight chopped.
-  2 tablespoons arhar dal.
-  1 Cassia - Broken.
-  1-1 / 2 tablespoon ginger garlic paste.
-  1 teaspoon Asafoetida.
-  1 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  2 tablespoons chana dal.
-  1 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  1 Star Anees.
-  2 tablespoons masoor dal.
-  2 Elaichi.
-  3 teaspoons green chillies.
- Ghee - As per use.
-  1 sprig of green coriander - Finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make dhaba style dal fry, first boil all the lentils.
1. Pour the lentils into the pressure cooker and add 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder and salt to it.
1. Cook the pulses till 3 o'clock.
1. Once the pressure comes out, open the cooker and mix the lentils.
1. Keep it separately.
1. Heat oil in a pan.
1. Add asafetida, cumin seeds and mustard and let it cook for 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add curry leaves, big cardamom, cardamom, star anise, long and salted green chillies.
1. Allow to cook for 30 seconds and then add green chili and ginger garlic paste.
1. Let it cook for 1 minute.
1. After 1 minute, add onions and cook until brown.
1. Once brown, add tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder and salt.
1. Add some water and cook for 5 to 8 minutes.
1. After the masala cooks well, add the cooked lentils to it.
1. Allow to cook for 8 to 10 minutes, turn off the gas and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve Dhaba Style Dal Fry with Sew Tomato Vegetable and Phulka for dinner.
