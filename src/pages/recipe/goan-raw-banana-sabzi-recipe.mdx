### Ingredients

- 3 Raw Banana - chopped to cubes.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 4 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Goan Raw Banana Sabzi Recipe, peel the skin of the plantains, chop them into cubes and keep it immersed in cold water in a bowl until further cooking.
1. Heat oil in a pan, once the oil is hot, add the mustard seeds and let it splutter.
1. Add the red chilli, curry leaves and let it crackle.
1. Add the onions and saute until it turns translucent.
1. Drain the plantains and add it to the pan.
1. Saute.
1. Add turmeric powder, salt, mix well and let the plantains cook with the lid closed for 5 minutes.
1. The plantains cook really fast.
1. Now, pound the cumin and grated coconut using a pestle and mortar to a course mix and add it to the cooked plantains.
1. Mix well and saute for another 5 minutes and switch off the heat.
1. Serve the Goan Raw Banana Sabzi Recipe along with Phulka, Steamed Rice and Feijoada Recipe (Spicy Goan Black Eyed Peas Stew) for a rainy weeknight dinner.
