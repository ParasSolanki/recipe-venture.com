### Ingredients

- 1/3 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- 1/4 cup Sun Dried Tomatoes - chopped.
- Mozzarella cheese - grated.
- 1 cup Basil leaves.
- 1-1/4 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- Cheddar cheese - grated.
- Salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon Onion powder.
- 1/4 cup Pineapple - diced.
- 3 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/4 cup Walnuts.
- 2 teaspoon Chia Seeds.
- 1/4 cup Button mushrooms - sliced.
- Salt and Pepper - as needed.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garlic powder.
- Water - as needed to knead the dough.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 3/4 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 3/4 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1/3 cup Kalamata Olives.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pesto Pizza Recipe With Sun-Dried Tomatoes, Mushrooms, Pineapples And Olives first prepare the Pesto.
1. To do this, add all the ingredients listed under "For Basil Walnut Pesto" into a food processor which includes basil leaves, olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic, walnuts and chia seeds.
1. Grind down to a smooth paste.
1. Keep this aside till later use.
1. To prepare the dough for the pizza base, add the all purpose flour, baking powder, baking soda, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, olive oil, sugar into a medium bowl and stir well.
1. Add curd and knead with water if required.
1. Work the dough into a smooth ball that is pliable.
1. Grease the bowl and the ball of dough and cover it.
1. Set aside for 2 to 3 hours.
1. When you are ready to prepare the pizzas, pre heat the oven to 200 degrees C.
1. Dust some flour on your counter top, upturn the dough onto it and knead it for about 3-4 minutes.
1. Then, roll it out into a disc about 8 inches in diameter, using a rolling pinUsing a fork, poke some holes all over the base.
1. Place the dough on the pizza stone or a flat pan and place it in the oven to bake until slightly golden.
1. Remove the pizza base from the oven and spread prepared basil walnut pesto.
1. Sprinkle the cheese generously.
1. Assemble the rest of the toppings like pineapple, mushroom, kalamata olives, sun dried tomatoes and return it to the oven.
1. Bake until the cheese melts, take it out of the oven, slice and serve hot.
1. Serve Pesto Pizza Recipe With Sun-Dried Tomatoes, Mushrooms, Pineapples And Olives as a party appetizer, or with Zucchini Salad With Thai Hot Chili Dressing for a weeknight dinner.
1. You can also serve Rich Chocolate Brownies to end your delicious meal.
