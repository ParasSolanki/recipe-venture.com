### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Sambar Powder.
- 2 Mango (Raw) - cut into big chunks with the skin.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 tablespoon Jaggery.
- 1/4 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pacha Manga Pachadi Recipe, start by boiling the raw mango in tamarind water, jaggery, and turmeric powder in a saucepan over a medium heat.
1. Boil till the raw mango becomes soft & cooked, once that is done add in the sambar powder, salt and give it a stir.
1. Leave the Pacha Manga Pachadi to boil till it thickens up.
1. Once it thickens, turn off the heat.
1. In a tempering pan, add oil and place over medium heat; once it is hot add in mustard seeds allow it to crackle.
1. Once it crackles, add curry leaves, sprinkle the hing and add in the dry red chillies, saute till the aroma is released and then pour it over the Pacha Manga Pachadi.
1. Check the taste and adjust the salt and spices accordingly.
1. Serve the Pacha Manga Pachadi along with steamed rice, Mixed Vegetable Sambar Recipe and Kurukku Kaalan Recipe for south indian lunch or dinner.
