### Ingredients

- Nutralite Garlic & Oregano Spread - as required.
- 2 tablespoon Whole Wheat Flour.
- Water - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 Potato (Aloo) - finely grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 3 tablespoons Gram flour (besan).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Potato Onion Cheela recipe, add finely grated potatoes for 5 minutes in cold water.
1. Take a bowl, add gram flour and whole wheat flour.
1. Add red chilli powder, cumin powder, black pepper powder and salt to taste.
1. Add water and make a smooth batter.
1. Take grated potato out from cold water and add it to the gram and whole wheat batter.
1. Mix it well and keep it aside.
1. Heat one tablespoon Nutralite Garlic & Oregano Spread in a nonstick pan, spread one portion of potato and onion mixture on the pan thinly and cook, turning sides until crisp and properly cooked.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve Potato Onion Cheela along with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney, Tomato Chutney or any other chutney for your breakfast or with Masala Chai during your tea time break.
