### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Ghee - for cooking.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 250 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese).
- 8 Whole cashews.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Fresh cream.
- 3 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lehsuni Methi Paneer recipe, soak the cashews in a bowl with 1/4 cup water for about an hour.
1. After an hour, grind cashew along with ginger, garlic and green chillies and the soaked cashew water make a smooth paste and keep aside.
1. In a big bowl, add cashew ginger garlic and chilli paste, fresh cream, curd, garam masala, cardamom powder salt and whisk well to form a smooth mixture.
1. Heat ghee in a pan over medium heat; Add the cashew cream mixture, the chopped Paneer cubes and simmer for about 5 minutes.
1. Add water to adjust the consistency of the mixture while it's simmering.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to taste accordingly.
1. Finally stir in the kasuri methi, cover the pan and turn off the heat.
1. Allow the flavours of methi to get infused into the Lehsuni Paneer Methi sabzi.
1. Transfer the Lehsuni Methi Paneer into a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Lehsuni Methi Paneer Recipe along with Smoked Makhani Dhaba Style Dal, Jeera Rice, Tomato Onion Tadka Raita and Garlic Naan for a house party.
