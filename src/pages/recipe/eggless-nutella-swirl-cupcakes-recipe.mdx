### Ingredients

- 100 grams Nutella Spread.
- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 tablespoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 60 grams Butter.
- 3 tablespoons Condensed Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/2 cup Milk - lukewarm.
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1 1/2 teaspoon Vinegar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Eggless Nutella Swirl Cupcakes recipe, firstly preheat the oven to 180℃.
1. Grease cupcake tin with some butter or cooking oil.
1. Take a big bowl and sift flour, baking powder, baking soda twice together in it.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now in a large bowl, add butter, condensed milk, vanilla essence and sugar together and whisk them until combined or gets creamy texture.
1. Now mix wet and dry ingredients together with the help of luke warm milk.
1. Now add vinegar and mix well.
1. Take prepared cupcake tin or moulds and fill each cup about 2/3 full with the batter.
1. Drop 1 teaspoon of nutella to each and swirl it around a few times with a toothpick.
1. Bake them for 10 to 12 minutes.
1. Check the cupcake by inserting a toothpick in the centre of the cake.
1. If it comes out clean that means cupcake is ready and if not then bake it for another 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Let the cake cool down for 3 to 4 minutes and then serve.
1. Serve Eggless Nutella Swirl Cupcakes along with Cucumber Cheese sandwich and Masala Tea during tea time break.
