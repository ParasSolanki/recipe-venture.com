### Ingredients

-  1 tablespoon paprika powder.
-  1 cup all purpose flour (All Purpose Flour).
-  salt - as per taste.
-  2 tablespoons water - cold.
-  4 tablespoons butter.
- 200 gm Chhedar Cheese - finely tightened.

### Instructions

1. To make Cheese Crackers Recipe, first add butter, cheddar cheese and mix in a bowl.
1. Now add refined flour, paprika powder, salt and mix it well.
1. After that add 1-2 tablespoons of cold water and take it.
1. Dump this dough into big balls and rape it with Kling rape.
1. Keep it in the fridge for an hour.
1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees.
1. Take out the dough from the fridge and roll it 1/4 inch thick.
1. Cut into square shape.
1. Now make a hole in the middle of each cracker with the help of a fork.
1. Place parchment paper on the baking sheet and spread the crackers over it.
1. Bake for 15 to 17 minutes or until golden.
1. Remove the baking tray and let it cool.
1. Serve after cooling.
1. Serve the Cheese Crackers recipe with Masala Tea, Ginger Tea or Filter Coffee for an evening snack.
