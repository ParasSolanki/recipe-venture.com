### Ingredients

- 1 sprig Curry leaves - roughly torn.
- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 40 grams Ginger - peeled and grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a small bunch finely chopped.
- 1 Mango (Raw) - peeled and grated.
- 3 cups Cooked rice.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Manga Inji Sadam, first get all the ingredients ready - by grating the mangoes and ginger.
1. Using a day old cooked rice also helps in making the Manga Ingi Sadam faster.
1. Heat a wok or a heavy bottomed pan over medium heat.
1. Add the mustard seeds and the urad dal and roast them until they crackle and the dal is golden brown.
1. Add the curry leaves, green chilies, grated mango and ginger and saute until the mango softens for about 4 to 5 minutes.
1. Once the mango softens, add the turmeric powder, cooked rice and salt.
1. Stir all the ingredients together until well combined.
1. Check the salt and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and serve.
1. Serve the Ingi Mangai Sadam along with a bowl of yogurt or buttermilk for a lunch box or even along with your main course meal.
