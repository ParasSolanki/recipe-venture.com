### Ingredients

- 2 inch Ginger - (adjust).
- 4 teaspoons Honey - (optional).
- 1/2 Lemon - squeezed.
- 2 cups Water.
- 12 Tulsi (holy basil).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Ginger-Holy Basil Tisane - Adrak Tulsi Chai Recipe, wash and clean the ginger thoroughly.
1. Then grate it without peeling.
1. The peel of ginger is rich in nutrients.
1. Take water in a saucepan and bring it to boil.
1. Add the grated ginger and boil for 2-3 minutes again.
1. Then keep on simmering for another 3 minutes.
1. Roughly tear the tulsi (holy basil) leaves and put into the simmered ginger-water.
1. Turn off the heat, cover the saucepan and let the ginger-holy basil tisane be on boil for 2-3 minutes.
1. Next, strain in serving cups.
1. Drizzle honey, squeeze lemon juice and give a quick stir.
1. Serve hot.
1. Drink Ginger-Holy Basil Tisane - Adrak Tulsi Chai Recipe on empty stomach.
