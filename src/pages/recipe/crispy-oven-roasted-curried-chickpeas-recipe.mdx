### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon Dry ginger powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 teaspoon Garlic powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 tablespoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Crispy Oven Roasted Curried Chickpeas Recipes, firstly rinse the chickpeas and soak it in enough water overnight.
1. Once its soaked add them to a  pressure cook and pressure cook for 2 whistles.
1. Turn off the heat and let the pressure release naturally.
1. Once the pressure cooker releases all the pressure, drain all the water from chickpeas.
1. Pat them dry with a kitchen towel and make sure they are completely dry and has no moisture.
1. In a wide bowl add all the spice powder, salt, olive oil and chickpeas.
1. Toss them well to ensure all the spices are coated well on the chickpeas.
1. Preheat the oven to 200 degree centigrade.
1. Line a baking tray with good quality parchment paper.
1. Transfer the masala coated chickpeas to the baking tray.
1. Line them up in single layer spreading it evenly all over the tray.
1. Roast them in an oven for about 45-55 minutes or until it turns crunchy.
1. After 30 minutes check the chickpeas, if they are still undone keep it for another 10-15 minutes.
