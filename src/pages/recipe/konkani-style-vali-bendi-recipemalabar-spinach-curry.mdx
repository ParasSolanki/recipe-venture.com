### Ingredients

- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 3 Dry Red Chillies.
- Salt - to taste.
- 18 grams Tamarind.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut.
- 1 cup Spinach Leaves (Palak).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Konkani Style Vali Bendi Recipe, we will heat a skillet to dry roast the dry red chillies.
1. Once done grind it along with coconut and tamarind to a smooth paste by adding some water.
1. Heat a pressure cooker with oil, add pound garlic and allow it to sizzle.
1. Add chopped spinach leaves and saute for few seconds till it wilts.
1. Add ground paste and about 1/2 cup water and salt.
1. Pressure cook for around 2 whistle.
1. Serve the Konkani Style Vali Bendi Recipe along with steamed rice with a dollop of ghee on top and Konkani Style Mooga Ghushi Recipe by the side to enjoy your everyday lunch.
