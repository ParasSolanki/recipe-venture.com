### Ingredients

- 2 Thai Red chilli (Birds Eye Chilli) - slit.
- 1/4 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 1/4 cup Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Tomato Ketchup.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 1/2 cup Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - chopped.
- 200 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cut in cubes.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chilli Paneer Recipe, mix corn flour with ¼ cup of water.
1. Stir in the soya sauce, ketchup, chilli sauce and salt into the corn flour mixture.
1. Heat oil in a wok or frying pan on high heat; add garlic, ginger, thai red chillies, spring onions and stir fry for a few seconds.
1. Add in the bell peppers and sauce on medium heat until tender.
1. Once the peppers are tender, stir in the corn flour mixture to the above and cook for a few minutes until the chilli sauce thickens.
1. At this stage stir in the paneer cubes, turn the heat to high and saute for a few minutes until well combined with the sauce.
1. Garnish the chilli paneer with spring onion leaves and extra green chillies if you like it spicy.
1. Serve the Chilli Paneer along with other Indo Chinese Dishes like a Chilli Coriander Fried Rice and Roasted Eggplant in Szechuan Sauce.
