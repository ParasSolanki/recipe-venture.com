### Ingredients

- 2 Lemons - juiced.
- 10 Chicken - legs.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - for basting.
- 1/2 teaspoon Parsley leaves.
- 1/2 tablespoon Onion powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 2 Red Chilli Paste - roasted.

### Instructions

1. To make Mediterranean Inspired Spicy Grilled Chicken Recipe, clean the chicken legs and pat dry them.
1. In a big mixing bowl, put all the spices- roasted red chilli paste, onion powder, chilli flakes, cumin powder, oregano, parsley leaves and the ginger garlic paste and give it a mix.
1. In the bowl, mix the chicken pieces with all the spices, and marinate preferably overnight for a great flavour.
1. Or you can marinate at least for 3-4 hours.
1. Preheat a barbeque grill and grill the chicken till the chicken is evenly grilled.
1. Or you can bake the chicken in the ovenOven MethodYou can bake it in a convection oven.
1. Preheat the oven at 425 degree F for about 10 minutes or till the oven indicator shows ready.
1. Meanwhile prepare a baking sheet and line it with a parchment paper.
1. Arrange the marinated chicken on the parchment paper on baking sheet and brush the chicken with olive oil.
1. Bake it for 20-25 minutes or till done.
1. Serve Mediterranean Inspired Spicy Grilled Chicken Recipe as a main dish along with a Tabbouleh Recipe, and end on a sweet note with Aish el Saraya | A Lebanese Bread Pudding on a weekend with your family and friends.
