### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2-1/2 tablespoon Jaggery.
- 2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 teaspoon Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- Turmeric powder (Haldi) - a pinch.
- 3/4 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Shimla Mirch Ki Launji  recipe, heat the oil in a broad non-stick pan, add the nigella seeds, fennel seeds and green chillies and sauté on a medium flame till the aromas of the seeds comes through and the chilies are cooked.
1. .
1. Now add the capsicum and sauté on a medium flame for a few more seconds.
1. Add the turmeric powder, coriander powder, chilli powder and dried mango powder, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 1 minute.
1. Add 1/2 cup of water, jaggery and salt, mix well and cook on a medium flame for 4 to 5 minutes or till the capsicum turns soft, while stirring occasionally.
1. Serve the launji immediately or store in an air-tight container in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 days.
1. Serve Shimla Mirch Ki Launji along with Tawa Paratha, Dal Palak and Homemade Yogurt for a weekday lunch.
