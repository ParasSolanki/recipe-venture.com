### Ingredients

- 2 Sweet Potatoes - skin peeled and cut into chunks.
- 1 cup Spinach - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Dried Thyme Leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spinach and Sweet Potato Bombs Recipe, we will boil the sweet potato in a pressure cooker with water for about 4 whistle till they are soft.
1. Heat a kadai with oil, add chopped garlic and saute until they soften.
1. Add chopped onions and saute them till they turn translucent.
1. Add spinach leaves and saute till the spinach leaves leave out water and completely dries up.
1. Add all the seasonings mentioned above including thyme leaves, dried oregano, red chilli flakes and black pepper powder.
1. Add in the cooked sweet potato and sprinkle salt according to your palate.
1. By using potato masher, mash them well and combine the mixture together.
1. Switch off the heat and allow it cool down.
1. Shape them into medium sized balls and keep it aside.
1. Heat a kuzhi paniyaram pan with little oil in each cavities.
1. Place the kofta balls in the pan's cavities; drizzle the kofta's with a little oil and pan fry them until golden brown on both sides.
1. You will have to flip the kofta balls so they fry on all sides evenly.
1. You can serve it with a dollop of cream on top and some toasted nuts.
1. Serve the Spinach and Sweet Potato Bombs Recipe along with Tzatziki Recipe - A Greek Yogurt Dip and White Bean Salad with Onions and Bell Peppers by the side.
