### Ingredients

- 1 cup Milk.
- 1 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 cup Jackfruit Raw (Kathal) - chopped finely.
- 1 cup Brown Rice.
- 2 teaspoon Cashew nuts - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kerala Style Chakka varattiyathu Ari Payasam Recipe, we will first boil the jackfruit.
1. Boil the chopped jackfruit with 1/2 cup water in a saucepan over medium heat.
1. Leave it on medium-low heat for about 20 minutes, till the jackfruit become soft and turns pulpy.
1. Switch off the flame, and allow it to cool down.
1. Transfer this into a a mixer-jar and grind it into a smooth paste.
1. Heat a saucepan, add in the brown rice, milk, jackfruit puree, and 1 cup water, cardamom pod and allow it to boil on medium-low heat.
1. You can keep stirring till the milk thickens and rice is boiled.
1. This is a slow process that will take sometime.
1. the brown rice that needs to be fully cooked.
1. Check the Chakka varattiyathu Ari Payasam after 25 - 30 minutes to see if its done.
1. If ts cooked, switch off the heat and serve.
1. Serve the Kerala Style Chakka varattiyathu Ari Payasam Recipe after a nice Kerala meal like Kerala Avial Recipe and Kerala Style Egg Roast Curry and Malabari Parotta .
