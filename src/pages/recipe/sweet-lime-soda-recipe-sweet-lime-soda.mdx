### Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons sugar.
-  black salt or rock salt - as per taste.
-  pepper powder - a pinch.
-  1 cup soda.
-  peppermint - as per use.
-  1 teaspoon cumin powder.
-  1 cup water.
-  ice - According to use.
-  1/4 cup lime juice.

### Instructions

1. To make sweet lime soda, mix lemon juice, water, sugar, cumin powder, black salt, black pepper powder in a bowl and mix until the sugar dissolves.
1. Syrup is ready.
1. Add ice chunks, mint leaves, lemon pieces in glass, add syrup, then add soda and serve immediately.
1. Suggestion - Use sugar as per your taste.
1. You can also use salt or sedha salt in place of black salt.
1. Serve white lime soda with mathri and biscuits in the evening.
