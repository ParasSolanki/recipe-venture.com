### Ingredients

- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1 tablespoon Butter - chilled and cut into small pieces.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera) - roasted.
- 1/4 cup Lukewarm Water.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Indian Style Jeera Biscuit Recipe, preheat the oven to 180 C.
1. Mix self raising flour, salt and cumin seeds in a mixing bowl.
1. Add in the chopped butter and mix all together till you get a crumb like mixture.
1. You can use a food processor to do this.
1. Once you get the crumb texture, knead it into a medium-soft dough using lukewarm water.
1. Cover the dough with a with a wrap or lid and keep it aside for 15 minutes.
1. Divide the mixture in 5 big balls and roll out each of them to 4-5 mm thickness.
1. Use your favorite cookie-cutter and cut them into shapes.
1. Place a parchment paper on baking tray and arrange all the Jeera Biscuit  onto it.
1. Bake the Jeera Biscuits in preheated oven at 180 C for around 20 minutes.
1. Take them out of the oven and place them on cooling rack.
1. Store the Jeera Biscuits in an air-tight container and serve it as desired.
1. Serve Indian Style Jeera Biscuit along with hot Masala Chai for your tea time break.
