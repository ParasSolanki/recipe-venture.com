### Ingredients

- 1 cup Black Rice.
- 1/2 cup Palm sugar - or jaggery.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 3 cups Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chettinad Kavuni Arisi Sweet recipe, wash the rice and soak adding lots of water for about 8 hours or overnight.
1. Drain the water and add the rice to a pressure cooker.
1. Pour 3 cups of water to the rice, drained water can also be used.
1. Pressure cook for 5 to 6 whistles.
1. Once the pressure seizes, open the lid of cooker.
1. Add palm sugar or jaggery along with cardamom powder to the rice.
1. Mix and keep cooking in medium flame by mashing with a ladle.
1. Add grated coconut to it and mix well.
1. Cook until it becomes thick.
1. Remove from flame and add in ghee.
1. Mix and serve warm.
1. Serve Chettinad Kavuni Arisi Sweet after your simple meal of Vegetable Sambar, Steamed Rice and Elai Vadam on a weekday.
