### Ingredients

-  oil - as per use.
- 1 cup cheese - tightly Chaat masala powder.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  3 green coriander - finely chopped.
-  1 rice flour.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Vegetarian Malai Kebab Recipe, prepare all the ingredients first.
1. Now in a mixing bowl add cottage cheese, potato, milk powder, green chillies, ginger, onion, garam masala powder, chaat masala powder, coriander leaves, rice flour, salt and mix well.
1. Now take a little mixture from it and make small round round kebabs.
1. Press it so that it becomes flat.
1. Now heat oil on a pan.
1. Keep the kebabs in it and cook till it turns golden brown from both the sides.
1. Remove and serve to soak the oil on a kitchen paper after cooking.
1. Serve the Vegetarian Malai Kebab Recipe for the starter with coriander mint chutney and vinegar onions.
