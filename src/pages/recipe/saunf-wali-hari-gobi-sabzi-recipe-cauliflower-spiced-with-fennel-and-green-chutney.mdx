### Ingredients

- 1 Cauliflower (gobi) - cut into florets.
- Salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 Ginger.
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Saunf Wali Hari Gobi Sabzi Recipe, get all the ingredients and keep them ready.
1. If you don't have Green Chutney, click here to get the recipe.
1. Heat a teaspoon of oil in a heavy bottomed pan; add the fennel seeds, ginger and bay leaves.
1. Stir fry for a few seconds.
1. Add the tomatoes, turmeric powder, coriander powder, chili powder and give it a stir.
1. Next add the yogurt, green chutney and salt.
1. Give it a stir.
1. Add the Cauliflower into the Chutney Masala and stir well to combine into the masala.
1. Once combined, cover the pan and cook until the cauliflower is well cooked.
1. This will take about 5 to 8 minutes on medium heat.
1. Make sure you keep stirring it intermittently until done.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and serve.
1. Serve the Saunf Wali Hari Gobi Sabzi Recipe along with Tawa Paratha and Boondi Raita Recipe Spiced With Black Salt for lunch or dinner.
