### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1/4 teaspoon Garlic powder - or 4 cloves garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 150 grams Kale.
- 1 cup White beans - cooked.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 tablespoon Tahini - or greek yogurt.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Kale White Bean Hummus Dip, add the white beans and kale to a blender and give it a quick pulse for everything to mix.
1. To this add the greek yogurt, cumin powder, fresh garlic cloves/garlic powder, salt and lemon to taste.
1. Blend well till you get a creamy consistency.
1. If you need more liquid to blend, add more tahini paste or greek yogurt.
1. Serve chilled with baked pita chips or fresh veggies.
1. You can also use it as a spread for sandwiches and wraps.
1. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for 2-3 days.
1. (Because of the veggies, this won't stay fresh for as long as a traditional hummus)Serve Kale White Bean Hummus Dip with Pita bread and Baba Ganoush as an appetizer for your house parties.
