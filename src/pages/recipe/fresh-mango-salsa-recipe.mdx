### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 Mango (Ripe) - peeled and diced.
- 1 pinch Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the fresh Mango Salsa, combine the mangoes, olive oil, green chili, onion, cumin powder, salt, lemon juice and coriander leaves in a mixing bowl, stir well to combine.
1. Check the salt and seasoning of the Mango salsa and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Serve the Mango Salsa along with Homemade Nachos and another dip Spicy Mexican Salsa as your party starter.
