### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 Fresh fruits - like strawberries or blueberries that are seasonal.
- 2 tablespoon Walnuts - chopped.
- 2/3 cup Chia Seeds.
- 2 teaspoon Honey.
- 2 tablespoon Raisins.
- 2 cup Almond Milk (Badam Milk) - or whole milk.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Almond Milk Chia Seeds Pudding With Fresh Fruits Recipe, in a mixing bowl combine, the chia seeds with milk, raisins, walnuts, vanilla essence, cinnamon powder, honey.
1. Fill dessert glass cups with the mixture.
1. Cover them with a cling film and refrigerate overnight.
1. Garnish with raisins and chopped walnuts and fresh seasonal fruits and serve Almond Milk Chia Seeds Pudding With Fresh Fruits.
1. Serve the Almond Milk Chia Seeds Pudding as part of a breakfast meal alongside Poached Eggs Recipe With Arugula Fig Walnut Salad.
