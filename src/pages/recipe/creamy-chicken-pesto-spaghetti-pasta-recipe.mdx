### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Walnuts.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Pepper Corns.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 50 grams Basil leaves.
- 4 Chicken breasts - boneless skinless.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 300 grams Spaghetti Pasta.
- 1/2 cup Sun Dried Tomatoes.
- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Creamy Chicken Pesto Pasta Recipe, heat a sauce pan with enough water.
1. Sprinkle salt in order to increase the cooking process.
1. Add the spaghetti pasta into the boiling water, and wait till it softens.
1. Cook until it becomes al dente (just cooked) and strain it under running water to stop the cooking process.
1. Drizzle olive oil into the pasta, toss it well and keep it aside.
1. The next is to make the basil pesto.
1. Into a mixer grinder, add the basil leaves, garlic, olive oil, walnuts, parmesan, pepper and salt.
1. Blend to make a smooth pesto mixture.
1. Add a little water if required.
1. Squeeze in some lemon juice into the pesto.
1. Take out the sun dried tomatoes from the jar.
1. And cut them into small bite size pieces.
1. Set them aside.
1. Cube the chicken breast.
1. Also mince some garlic and set aside.
1. In a wide pan, add some olive oil and add minced garlic to the pan and give it a toss.
1. Add chicken and cook till it has turned white from pink and is roasted well.
1. Season with salt and black pepper according to your taste.
1. Into the saucepan, add the basil pesto, boiled pasta and the chopped sundried tomatoes.
1. Toss the Creamy Chicken Pesto Pasta together and stir fry on medium heat until all of them come together.
1. Check the salt to taste and adjust accordingly.
1. Turn off the heat and transfer the Creamy Chicken Pesto Pasta to a serving bowl.
1. Serve Creamy Chicken Pesto Pasta Recipe with Garlic Bread and a Rich Dark Chocolate Mousse Recipe for a healthy weekend dinner.
