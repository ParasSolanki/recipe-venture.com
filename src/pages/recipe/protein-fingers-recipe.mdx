### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Garlic - minced.
- 3 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon Onion powder.
- 100 grams Chicken - boneless and skinless breast piece with angular slits.
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/4 teaspoon Paprika powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Sage.
- Salt - as per taste.
- 100 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cut in shape of a finger snack.
- 100 grams Tofu - cubed.
- 1/4 teaspoon Garlic powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Protein Fingers recipe, make a marination with olive oil, garlic, cumin powder, salt, pepper, paprika, red chilli powder, onion powder, garlic powder, oregano, sage and lemon juice.
1. Apply marinate on the protein and keep it for at least 20 minutes or overnight in the refrigerator.
1. Grill the protein logs on a hot grill pan or flat griddle until slightly charred.
1. If grilling chicken, you will need to cover it and cook initially on both sides until the juices run out and chicken is cooked.
1. Remove the lid and char the chicken skin on high heat for a couple of more minutes.
1. Cut the cooked chicken into thin slices.
1. Serve the protein fingers warm with sour cream or salsa.
1. Serve Protein Fingers along with Dill Leaves & Parmesan Dip during your dinner time or serve it as a snack in your house parties.
