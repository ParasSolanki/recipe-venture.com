### Ingredients

- 3 Mango (Ripe).
- 1 teaspoon Tamarind Paste.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - to garnish.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Asafoetida (hing) - A pinch.
- 10 Curry leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 3 Green Chillies - slit.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mamidi Pandu Pulusu, firstly peel the ripe mangoes and keep aside.
1. In a pan, place oil and add mustard seeds and let them crackle.
1. Once it crackles, add asafoetida, curry leaves and green chillies and saute it for a minute.
1. After a minute, add the peeled mangoes and saut√© till its a little until it becomes a little soft.
1. Add red chilly powder and a cup of water, tamarind paste, salt and cover it until it comes to a boil.
1. Once the water starts evaporating, add another cup of water and boil again.
1. At the end of it, you should be left with nice thick Mamidi Pandu Pulusu.
1. After 2-3 minutes, switch off the gas and the Mamidi Pandu Pulusu/ Mango Pulusu is ready to be served.
