### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Butter - optional.
- 1 Avocado - skin removed and sliced.
- 2 Red Romaine lettuce - washed and towel dried.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Tomato - sliced.
- 1/2 cup Homemade Basil Pesto - fresh basil pesto.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread - multigrain.
- 1 teaspoons Black pepper powder - crushed.
- Cheese - shredded cheese or cheese slices as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Avocado Tomato Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe, butter the bread slices (if using) and toast them lightly on a grill pan.
1. Spread the pesto evenly on 2 slices.
1. Now arrange the tomato slices on the pesto layer.
1. Next arrange the sliced Avocado.
1. Sprinkle some pepper and salt on it.
1. Once it is done, top the Avocado with cheese slice or slices (or shredded cheese).
1. Arrange the lettuce leaves on top of the cheese slice and cover it with another bread slice.
1. Grill the sandwich on both sides till the cheese just starts melting and both the sides become brown.
1. Serve Avocado Tomato Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Banana And Mango Smoothie or Cocoa Banana Almond Date Smoothie.
