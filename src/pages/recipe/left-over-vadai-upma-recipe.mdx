### Ingredients

- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 6 Medu vada.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger - finely chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Left Over Vadai Upma recipe, you can either use ready made medu vadas or make them at home b y following this recipe- Medu Vada Recipe (Traditional Fried Urad Dal Vada Using Vada Maker).
1. Break the vadas with your hands and make it appear like crumbs.
1. Heat a wok/kadhai on medium flame, add the oil, once the oil is hot, add hing, mustard seeds, bengal gram dal, red chilli and saute well.
1. Add green chillies, onions and let it fry for a minute.
1. Add onion, salt, turmeric and let it cook well.
1. Add curry leaves and let it cook for a minute.
1. Add the crumbled vadai and mix well.
1. Let it cook well for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Garnish with coriander leaves.
1. Serve Left Over Vadai Upma with South Indian Coconut Chutney for breakfast and a cup of Bellada Coffee Recipe With Milk (Karnataka Style Filter Coffee With Jaggery).
