### Ingredients

- 1 cup Red grapes.
- 1 Celery - cut into medium sized.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 2 tablespoon Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 10 Walnuts - crushed.
- 1 tablespoon Fresh cream - lot fat.
- 1 Apple - cut into cubes.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Waldorf Salad Recipe, keep all the ingredients ready in a bowl.
1. Add cream and yogurt in, sprinkle with salt and pepper and mix well till everything is well coated.
1. You can serve it as it is or store it in the fridge and serve cold.
1. Serve the Waldorf Salad Recipe along with beetroot wellington and a glass of cocktail by the side to complete your meal.
