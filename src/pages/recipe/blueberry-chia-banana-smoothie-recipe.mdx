### Ingredients

- 1 cup Blueberries - fresh or frozen.
- 1 teaspoon Chia Seeds.
- 1/4 cup Badam (Almond).
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 1 Ripe Bananas.
- 1 cup Tender coconut water.
- 1 cup Ice cubes.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Blueberry Chia Banana Smoothie first, in a blender add blueberry, banana, chia seeds and almonds, blend them to a paste.
1. Now add honey, coconut water, and ice cubes and give one more blend.
1. Your Blueberry Chia Banana Smoothie is ready to be served.
1. Pour the smoothie into a glass and add a scoop of Vanilla ice cream if you want.
1. Serve Blueberry Chia Banana Smoothie during breakfast along with Scrambled Egg Sandwich With Cucumber.
