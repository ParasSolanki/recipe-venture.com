### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped for garnish.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1 cup Rice.
- 3 Potatoes (Aloo) - cut into big cubes.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Aloo Matar Masala Khichdi Recipe, wash the rice and keep it soaked in water till you add it into the recipe.
1. Wash, de-skin, cut the potatoes into big cubes and keep it immersed in a bowl of water until further cooking.
1. Heat ghee in a pressure pan, add the whole spices - bay leaf, black and green cardamoms, cinnamon, cloves and let it release its flavours into the ghee.
1. Add the cumin seeds and let it splutter.
1. Add the sliced onions, green chilies and saute until the onions turns translucent.
1. Drain the potatoes from water and add it to the pressure pan along with peas and saute for a minute.
1. Add the coriander powder, cumin pepper and red chilli powder and mix well.
1. Add the drained and soaked rice, about 5 cups of water, season with salt and bring it to a rolling boil.
1. Close it with the pressure pan lid and cook for 4 to 5 whistles, or till done.
1. Switch off the heat, let the pressure release on its own before opening the lid.
1. Garnish with coriander leaves use a fork to carefully mix the khichdi.
1. Serve the Aloo Matar Masala Khichdi Recipe along with Cauliflower & Cucumber Raita Recipe and Papad for a simple yet delightful weeknight dinner.
