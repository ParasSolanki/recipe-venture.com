### Ingredients

- 6 Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- Salt - as required.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 1/4 cup Green Moong Dal (Whole).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Green Moong Dal Soup, wash the Green moong dal and soak it in water for 4 hours or overnight.
1. Next day, add it to pressure cooker and cook for 3 whistles.
1. Once done, mash it or puree with hand blender or mixer adding a cup of water.
1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds.
1. Once the cumin seeds sizzles, add asafoetida and saute for a minute.
1. Now pour the mashed dal along with a cup of water, required salt, slit green chilli and crushed pepper.
1. Mix well and boil in low flame for 5 minutes.
1. Switch off the flame and add lemon juice.
1. Garnish with Coriander leaves.
1. Mix and serve hot.
1. Serve Green Moong Dal Soup along  Sweet Potato & Methi Stuffed Paratha Recipe & Raw Papaya, Apple & Carrot Salad Recipe for a wholesome weeknight Indian dinner.
