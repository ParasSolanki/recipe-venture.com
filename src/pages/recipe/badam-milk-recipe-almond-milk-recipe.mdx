### Ingredients

-  1 tsp saffron.
-  1 liter milk.
- 1 cup almonds - soak.
-  2 cardamom - break.
-  1 cup sugar.

### Instructions

1. To make almond milk, first cut and cut the peel of almonds.
1. Now pour them into the mixer grinder and grind them.
1. Keep the paste separately, put milk in a saucepan and keep it for boiling.
1. After boiling, add almonds, cardamom, sugar, saffron and cook until thickened.
1. Serve cold or hot.
1. Serve almond milk recipe with potato poha for breakfast.
