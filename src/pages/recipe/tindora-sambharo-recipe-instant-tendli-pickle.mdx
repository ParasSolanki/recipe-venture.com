### Ingredients

- 1 cup Tindora (Dondakaya/ Kovakkai) - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Split Methi Seeds (Methi Kuria).
- Lemon - juice extracted.
- 1/2 teaspoon Split Mustard Seeds (Rai Kuria).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tindora Sambharo Recipe - Instant Tendli Pickle, combine all the ingredients including the carrot and capsicum pieces in a bowl.
1. Stir well until all the ingredients are well combined.
1. Store the Tindora Sambharo Recipe in the refrigerator and use as and when required.
1. Serve Tindora Sambharo Recipe with dals, parathas or rice.
1. Note: Gingely oil/Sesame oil or Mustard oil can be used for better taste.
