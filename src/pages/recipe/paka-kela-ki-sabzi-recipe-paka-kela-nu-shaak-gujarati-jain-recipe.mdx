### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 3 cups Ripe Bananas - cubed.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Paka Kela Nu Shaak; prepare all the ingredients and keep them ready.
1. Heat oil in a pan on medium high; add the mustard seeds, cumin seeds and curry leaves and allow it to crackle.
1. Add the asafoetida, turmeric powder, red chilli powder and give it a stir.
1. Stir in the banana and the besan into the above seasoning and stir gently until the gram flour and condiments gets well coated around the ripe banana, for about 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Turn off heat and transfer to a serving dish.
1. Serve Paka Kela Ki Sabzi along with of Phulka, Gujarati Dal and a bowl of dahi.
1. Note: The Paka Kela Ki Sabzi has to be prepared just a few minutes before you are ready to serve your meal.
1. When it stays for a longer time, the bananas tend  to lose their texture and the dish can get too soggy.
