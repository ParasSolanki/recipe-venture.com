### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 Drumstick - cut into 1 inch.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Mangalorean cucumber - cut into small pieces with the skin.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut.
- 1 tablespoon Jaggery.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 5 Dry Red Chillies.
- 4 cloves Garlic - roughly chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Maskasangi Magge Koddel Recipe, prep all the ingredients and keep them aside.
1. We will first begin to roast the ingredients to be ground into a masala  in "Masala To roast and grind"In a small skillet, roast red chillies and coriander seeds until well roasted.
1.  Grind it along with coconut, add a little water to make a smooth paste.
1. Heat a pressure cooker with oil, add some crushed garlic and saute for 10 seconds.
1. Add the mangalore cucumber and drumsticks and the tamarind water.
1. Add in the ground masala mixture, jaggery, chilli powder and salt and about 1/2 cup of water.
1. Cover the pressure cooker and cook for 2 to 3 whistles.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Check the salt and seasonings and adjust to suit your taste and serve warm.
1. Serve the Maskasangi Magge Koddel Recipe with steamed rice or Mangalorean Neer Dosa and Chettinad Pepper Chicken Dry Recipe.
