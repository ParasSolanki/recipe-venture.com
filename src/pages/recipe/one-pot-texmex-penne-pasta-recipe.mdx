### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- Salt - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - crushed.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Garlic powder.
- 2 cups Penne pasta.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- Milk - as required.
- 1 tablespoon Parsley leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese - grated.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - as required.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Paprika powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the One Pot Tex-Mex Penne Pasta recipe, Heat olive oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add the crushed garlic and saute for a few seconds.
1. Add the capsicum and onions and let it cook for about 2 to 3 minutes or until it gets soft.
1. Once the vegetables are soft, add the red chilli powder, paprika powder, cumin powder, garlic powder and the required salt.
1. Mix everything properly and let it cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. After 2 to 3 minutes, turn the heat low and add the required milk.
1. Mix well and stir continuously for about 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes add the raw pasta and parmesan cheese and let it cook till the penne is cooked properly.
1. Once it is done, garnish it with parsley and serve hot.
1. Serve One Pot Tex-Mex Penne Pasta along with Whole Wheat Oats and Pesto Bread Recipe or a Fresh Garden Salad Recipefor a weekend family dinner.
