### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 cups Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 1/4 cup Corn flour.
- 2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 3/4 cup Beer - chilled.
- 3/4 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 2 teaspoon Pizza seasoning.
- 15 Prawns.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 2 teaspoon Garlic powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cooking soda.
- 4 teaspoons Salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder - or paprika.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Beer Batter Prawns recipe, first let us clean the prawns.
1. Wash the prawns, discard the heads and devein.
1. Remove the cover by leaving the tip of tails intact.
1. Then make a slit on the back of the prawn till it sits flat.
1. It should look similar to a butterfly.
1. Marinate the prawns by applying black pepper powder, red chilli powder and salt and keep aside for at least 30 minutes.
1. Now let us make the beer batter.
1. Take a mixing bowl and add all purpose flour, cornflour and other powders.
1. Now add beer little at a time and whisk to make thick batter.
1. Add salt to taste and mix well.
1. Now heat a kadai and add oil.
1. Hold the prawn on the tail and dip it in the batter.
1. Drain away the excess batter and gently drop the batter coated prawn in oil.
1. Deep fry in batches till it turns golden brown for about 3-4 minutes by turning on both sides.
1. It is ready to be served.
1. Serve Beer Batter Prawns along with other appetisers like Vegetarain Hariyali Malai Kebab and Crispy Chicken Pakoda for your house parties.
