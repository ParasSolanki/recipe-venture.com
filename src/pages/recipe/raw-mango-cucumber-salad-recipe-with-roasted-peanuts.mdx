### Ingredients

- 1 Mango (Raw) - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Cucumber - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sweet Chutney (Date & Tamarind).
- 1/2 teaspoon Paprika powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Raw Mango & Cucumber Salad Recipe, keep all the ingredients ready.
1. In a large mixing bowl, add the raw mango, cucumber, peanuts, coriander leaves and mint leaves.
1. Add all of the dressing ingredients like the sweet chutney, paprika and salt into a small bowl and whisk to combine well.
1. Drizzle the dressing over the top of the Raw Mango & Cucumber Salad and toss well.
1. Check for seasonings and adjust if required.
1. Transfer the Raw Mango & Cucumber Salad into a serving bowl and serve as a side dish with meals.
1. Serve the Raw Mango & Cucumber Salad Recipe along with Spinach Rice Recipe and Nawabi Kofta Curry Recipe for weekend lunch.
