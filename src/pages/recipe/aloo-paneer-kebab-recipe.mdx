### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/4 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- Salt - to taste.
- Sunflower Oil - for shallow frying.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 3 Potato (Aloo) - boiled.
- 200 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Aloo Paneer Kebab Recipe, get prep with all the ingredients first, including boiling potatoes till soft.
1. Mash boiled potatoes, and paneer together.
1. Mix all the other ingredients and give it a good mix.
1. Shallow FryHeat 2 tablespoons of oil in a shallow pan.
1. Make large lemon sized balls and roll them in some breadcrumbs and press them gently between palms.
1. Gently place them in the pan and shallow fry them on both sides till they are golden and crisp.
1. BakingAlternately for a healthier option, you can even bake them at 180 degrees Celsius in a preheated oven for about 15 minutes on each side.
