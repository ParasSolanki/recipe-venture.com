### Ingredients

- Sugar - to taste if required.
- 1/2 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1/2 cup Blueberries.
- 1/3 cup Mango (Ripe) - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Blueberry-Mango Marble Froyo Bars Recipe, pour all the ingredients of blueberry froyo in a blender and make smooth puree.
1. Keep aside.
1. Pour all the ingredients of mango froyo in the blender and make a smooth puree.
1. Marble Freezing Method:Line a loaf pan with parchment paper and spread blueberry smooth puree evenly inside.
1. Top with 5-6 tablespoon of mango smooth puree on it.
1. Use a clean bread knife to gently swirl on the puree.
1. Cover with the cling film and freeze overnight or until solid.
1. Cut into bars, once froyo gets frozen till solid.
1. Enjoy Blueberry-Mango Marble Froyo Bars Recipe as a dessert or as a summer coolant during evening.
