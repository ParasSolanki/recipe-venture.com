### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Walnuts.
- Basil leaves - one bunch.
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- 1 tablespoons Black pepper powder.
- Pasta  Fusilli.
- 1 tablespoon Avocado - mashed.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 3/4 cup Mozzarella cheese - grated.
- Kale - one bunch.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese.
- 1 tablespoon Green Chilli Paste.
- Salt - for seasoning.

### Instructions

1. To begin Healthy Kale Pesto Pasta Recipe, bring a pot of water to a rolling boil and add the fusilli pasta.
1. Add a tablespoon of oil and a teaspoon of salt.
1. Cook until the pasta is cooked through.
1. Drain the water from the cooked pasta and cool the pasta under running water so that the pasta stops cooking.
1.  Keep aside.
1. Now to make the pesto, in a food processor add roughly chopped Kale leaves, basil leaves, walnuts, lemon juice, black pepper powder, olive oil, green chilli paste, garlic and parmesan cheese.
1. Blend until it becomes a smooth green paste.
1. Place a heavy bottomed pan, on a low to medium flame.
1. Add in the pesto mixture and cooked pasta.
1. Add in the grated mozzarella cheese and give them all a good toss.
1. Kale pesto pasta is ready to serve, serve it warm or let it cool in refrigerator and serve as a pasta salad.
1. Serve Kale Pesto Pasta along with Whole Wheat Rosemary Focaccia Bread and Mushroom soup for a sumptuous Italian Style Sunday lunch.
