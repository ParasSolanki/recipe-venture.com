### Ingredients

- 1 cup Chenna (Homemade Cottage Cheese).
- 1 Cucumber - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 2 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 1/2 Watermelon - cut into triangles.
- 10 Pistachios - crushed.
- 1/2 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Watermelon, Chena Cucumber With Soy Dressing Recipe, prep all the ingredients and keep it ready.
1. Mix in all the ingredients for the dressing in a bowl and add the water melon and cucumber.
1. Mix well.
1. Serve it on a plate and place some crumbled chena over it and sprinkle with mint leaves and pistachios.
1. Serve the Watermelon, Chena Cucumber With Soy Dressing Recipe along with quiche or tossed Mexican rice.
