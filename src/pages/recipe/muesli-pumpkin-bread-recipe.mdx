### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Jaggery.
- 1 teaspoon Dry ginger powder.
- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1/2 cup Muesli.
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1/2 cup Kaddu (Parangikai/ Pumpkin) - shredded.
- 1/2 cups All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 2 teaspoons Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Muesli Pumpkin Bread Recipe by pre heating the oven at 200 degree Celsius for about 10 minutes.
1. Keep your loaf pan ready by layering it with parchment paper.
1. Now in a mixing bowl, add in all the dry ingredients and mix it well.
1. Then slowly add in the egg whites and oil along with the vanilla essence.
1. Add in the grated pumpkin, spice powders, jaggery and butter milk.
1. You will get quite a thick batter.
1. Add in a pinch of salt and mix well.
1. Gently pour into your loaf pan and hit it so that the batter is evenly spread.
1. Bake it in the oven for about 45 minutes till it is done.
1. You can insert a knife to check if the bread is cooked, if it comes cleanly then it is done.
