### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cups Milk.
- 1 cup Sugar.
- 1-1/2 cups Badam (Almond).
- 2 tablespoons Ghee.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Badam Burfi Recipe, soak the almonds overnight and peel the skin or put almonds in boiling water for 10 minutes.
1. Peel the almonds after it cools down.
1. Grind the almonds with milk to a fine paste in a mixer grinder.
1. Mix the almond paste and sugar and transfer it to a kadhai.
1. Keep the gas on high heat and keep stirring.
1. Add few spoons of ghee and keep stirring until the mixture starts to leave the side of the pan.
1. (this might take around 30 minutes)In the meanwhile, grease a plate with ghee and keep it aside.
1. Pour the mixture in until the greased plate, spread evenly and wait for it to cool down completely.
1. Once it cools down, cut the burfi into pieces and store in an airtight container.
1. Serve Badam Burfi Recipe as a sweet dish after a meal of Gujarati Dal, Aloo Simla Mirch Ki Sabzi and Phulkas with friends and family for a weekday dinner.
