### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 3 tablespoons Corn flour.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Cheese.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 teaspoon Mixed Herbs (Dried).
- 2 teaspoon Mixed Herbs (Dried).
- 1/2 cup Spinach Leaves (Palak) - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 cup Ricotta Cheese - or homemade paneer.
- 8 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 cups Homemade tomato puree.
- 2 sprig Basil leaves - roughy torn.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Ricotta Spinach Balls in Tomato Basil Sauce Recipe we will first make the Tomato Basil SauceTo make the Tomato Basil SauceHeat a skillet with oil on medium-high flame, once the oil is hot add the garlic, and saute until it turns into a light brown colour.
1. To this add the roughly torn basil leaves, and turn off the flame.
1. Now add the tomato puree, mixed herbs, salt, sugar, black pepper powder and red chilli powder.
1. Give it a good mix and allow the tomato basil pasta sauce to come to a brisk boil.
1. Once it comes to a boil, turn the heat to low, simmer the tomato basil pasta sauce for 8 to 10 minutes until the flavors comes through.
1. Once done, transfer to a cast iron skillet and set aside.
1. To make the Spinach Ricotta BallsIn a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, processed cheese, spinach leaves, onion, mixed herbs, red chilli flakes, black pepper powder, cumin powder, salt and cornflour.
1. Mix well until well combined.
1. Make sure the addition of cornflour absorbs the moisture present in the mixture.
1. If needed add some more cornflour, depending on your spinach ricotta ball mixture.
1. Grease both your palms with oil, take a tablespoon full of the ricotta spinach ball mixture and roll into a ball.
1. Set aside.
1. Heat a paniyaram pan on medium-high heat, with a few drops of oil in each of the cavities, drop these ricotta spinach balls and lightly cook them on all sides until it turns into brown in colour.
1. Remove from the heat and directly place them in the skillet on the bed of the tomato basil sauce and serve.
1. Serve Spinach Ricotta Balls in Tomato Basil Sauce along with Herbed Butter Rice Recipe With Rosemary & Thyme and Steamed Vegetables With Chilli Lime Butter Recipe followed by a dessert of Spiced Apple Crumble Recipe with Greek Yogurt.
