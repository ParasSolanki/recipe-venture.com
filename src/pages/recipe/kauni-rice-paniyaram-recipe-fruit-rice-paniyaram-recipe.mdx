### Ingredients

- 1 cup Black Rice.
- Health Mix - (optional).
- 2 tablespoons Whole Wheat Flour.
- Sunflower Oil - for frying in paniyaram pan.
- 6 tablespoons Palm jaggery - powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kavuni Arisi Paniyaram, wash and soak the rice in enough water for about 4 hours.
1. Once done, drain the rice from water and add it to a mixer.
1. Add 1/2 a cup or required amount of drained water to the rice and grind into smooth batter.
1. Transfer the batter into a mixing bowl and add wheat flour and powdered palm and powered palm jaggery to it.
1. You can also add 2-3 tablespoons of health mix or sathu maavu to the mixture, this step is optional.
1. Mix everything together into dosa batter consistency.
1. Heat a paniyaram pan adding required amount of oil.
1. Once the pan is hot, ladle the batter into all holes and cook in medium flame until golden.
1. Put the lid of the pan on so that the paniyarams cook/steam.
1.   Open the lid in intervals of a 30 seconds to check if the paniyarams are cooked.
1. Now, flip to the other side using a spoon and cook for 2-3 minutes until golden on all sides.
1. Remove from pan and repeat for remaining batter.
1. The healthy and tasty Kavuni Arisi Karupatti Paniyaram is ready.
1. Serve Kavuni Arisi Paniyaram at breakfast or at tea time as healthy snack along with a hot cup of Ginger Tea.
1. You can also serve as a side dish along with your South Indian Meal of Mixed Vegetable Sambar, Chow Chow Thoran and Steamed Rice.
