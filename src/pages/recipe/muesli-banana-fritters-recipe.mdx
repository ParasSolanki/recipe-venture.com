### Ingredients

- Butter - as required.
- 1/4 cup Honey.
- 2 Nendra Pazham Banana (Ripe) - sliced in rounds or long angles.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 cup Muesli.
- 2 tablespoon Cocoa Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Muesli Banana Fritters Recipe into a bowl add the muesli, honey, cocoa powder into a bowl.
1. Slice the bananas.
1. Heat a flat skillet or a grill pan,and grease the pan with some butter.
1. Dip the bananas in the muesli mix and place it on the skillet, cook on a medium heat until both sides are golden brown and crispy.
1. Once done transfer the Muesli Banana Fritters onto a serving plate and serve.
1. Serve the Muesli Banana Fritters Recipe as a dessert with Tender Coconut Ice Cream Recipe and a drizzle of date syrup on top.
