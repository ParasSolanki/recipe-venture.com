### Ingredients

- 2 Lady fish.
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish).
- 20 grams Tamarind.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Onion - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Goan Lady Fish Curry Recipe, clean and cut the fish into 2-3 pieces.
1. Wash and keep aside.
1. Marinate by applying turmeric powder, red chilli powder, salt and keep it aside for a minimum of 30 minutes.
1. Separately cut the radish into 2 inch long fingers and boil them separately.
1. The next step is to make the gravy.
1. Grind, grated coconut, turmeric powder, tamarind, peppercorns, coriander and red chillies by adding 1/2 cup water to make a thin gravy in a mixer grinder.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, add oil and fry the chopped onions till they turn brownish.
1. Add the fish and cook on medium flame for about 2 minutes.
1. Pour the gravy and stir.
1. Add 1/2 cup of water in the same.
1. Add the radish and boil for 10 minutes more by covering the utensil.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve Goan Lady Fish Curry either for lunch or dinner with Steamed Rice, Rava Fried Prawns, Salad and pickle and enjoy a delicious Goan meal.
