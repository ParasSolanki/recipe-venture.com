### Ingredients

- Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - for the crust.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera) - roasted.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 8 Whole Wheat Brown Bread - slices.
- Red Chilli powder - to taste.
- Butter - as required for greasing the breads.
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 4 Sweet Potatoes - boiled .
- 1/4 cup Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - roasted.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Healthy Aloo Roll Recipe (Baked Bread Potato Roll) by pre heating the oven to 200 C and grease a baking sheet with butter.
1. Combine the mashed potatoes, bell peppers, onions, green chillies, coriander, cumin powder, chaat masala powder, salt, juice from one lemon and the pounded peanuts.
1. Stir to combine all the ingredients well.
1. Using a rolling pin roll the bread slice to stretch it a bit, this we do so we can roll the bread over the filling with ease.
1. Spoon a teaspoon of the filling towards the centre, roll it up tightly; press and seal the edges and cut the excess away.
1. Proceed the same way with the remaining slices of bread to make the bread rolls.
1. To get crusty top we will dip the rolls in the melted butter, then roll this over the sesame seeds and place it on the greased baking sheet.
1. Proceed the same way with the remaining rolls and place them on a greased baking sheet.
1. Place the rolls in the preheated oven and bake for about 15 to 20 minutes until the crust turns golden brown in colour.
1. Serve the Healthy Aloo Roll Recipe (Baked Bread Potato Roll) along with Date Tamarind Chutney or Spicy Strawberry Chutney.
