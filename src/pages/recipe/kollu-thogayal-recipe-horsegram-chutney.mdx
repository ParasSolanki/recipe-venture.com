### Ingredients

- Water - as required.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- Salt - to taste.
- 3 Horse Gram Dal (Kollu/ Kulith).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 1 tablespoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- Turmeric powder (Haldi) - a pinch.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 tablespoon White Urad Dal (Split).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kollu Thogayal recipe, firstly heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan/kadhai.
1. Add mustard seeds.
1. Once the mustard splutters add asafoetida, curry leaves, dry red chilli [broken in half], chana dal and urad dal.
1. Stir well and keep the heat on low.
1. Once the dals are roasted, add a pinch of turmeric powder and the horsegram.
1. Fry until it gives a lovely nutty aroma [keep the heat on low to avoid burning the lentils]Turn off the heat and stir in the freshly grated coconut.
1. Once cooled, grind into a thick 'paste' with a very little water.
1. Add salt to taste and give it a final churn in the mixer.
1. It is ready to be served.
1. Serve Kollu Thogayal along with Homemade Soft Idlis or Ghee Roast Dosa and Filter Coffee for your morning breakfast.
