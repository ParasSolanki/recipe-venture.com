### Ingredients

- Ice cubes - Some if needed.
- 1 cup Mixed berries - (blueberry.
- 1 Lemon - sliced.
- 2 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 3 cups Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lemon-Cherry Berry Spa Water recipe, add all ingredients to a large glass or into a pitcher.
1. Cover the lid and let it chill overnight in the refrigerator.
1. Drink Lemon-Cherry Berry Spa Water instead of normal water to detox your body and keep your skin hydrated.
