### Ingredients

- 2 cucumber - peel and chop.
-  3 tablespoons curd.
-  1/4 tsp oil - Follow the experiment.
-  1 green chili - finely chopped.
-  1 teaspoon mustard.
-  1/4 teaspoon asafoetida.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  3 tablespoons peanuts - roast and peel.
-  coriander - as per use.
-  1/2 cup coconut - tighten.
-  1 sprig curry leaves - chop.

### Instructions

1. To make Khamang Cucumber recipe, firstly take a big bowl.
1. Add cucumber, coconut, coriander, salt and mix well.
1. Now add yogurt and mix again.
1. Keep aside.
1. Heat the oil in a tempering pan for tempering.
1. Add mustard seeds, asafoetida and cook for 10 seconds.
1. Now add green chillies, curry leaves and cook for 10 seconds.
1. Now add this tempering to the Khamang Cucumber and mix.
1. Finally add peanuts and mix well.
1. Serve Serve Khamang Cucumber with Khandeshi Dal, Bharali Bhendi and Phulka for the day's dinner.
