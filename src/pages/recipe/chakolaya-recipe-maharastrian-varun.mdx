### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- Water - as required.
- 1/4 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 3/4 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 1/4 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 cups Whole Wheat Flour.
- 2 Kokum (Malabar Tamarind) - (soaked in water for some time and remove the extract).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Onion - small and chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Chakolaya Recipe ( Maharashtrian Varan Phal) first, In a bowl mix wheat flour, salt, ajwain, oil and make a stiff dough.
1. Then in a pressure cooker cook Toor dhal with garlic for 3-4 whistles.
1. After the dal is done mash it well and a separate pan heat oil.
1. Add mustard seeds, after they splutter add turmeric, red chili, coriander powder and sprinkle some water.
1. Add this tempering to the mashed dal with this add the kokum extract and  2-3 cups of water to make it slurryBoil the dal for few minutes in low heat.
1. Till then divide the dough into equal parts and roll it on a rolling board.
1. Give horizontal and vertical cuts to make square or diamond shapes .
1. keep the square cuts on a plate .
1. Do this for all divided dough.
1. Once the dal starts boiling slide on the prepared square cuts in the dal.
1. Cook the dhokli's in the dal for 15 mins in medium flame or till they are cooked.
1. Once they are cooked, serve them hot with ghee simmered and garnished with coriander leaves and some chopped onions.
