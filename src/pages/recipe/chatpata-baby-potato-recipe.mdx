### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - as required.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/2 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1/4 tablespoon Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 3 tablespoons Water.
- 12 Baby Potatoes - boiled and peeled.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Tamarind Paste.
- 1/2 tablespoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chatpata Baby Potato Recipe, heat a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Once done, add in the coriander seeds, cumin seeds, fennel seeds, black peppercorn and dry red chillies.
1. Roast them for few seconds.
1. Add all roasted dry masala in grinder and turn them into a coarse powder.
1. Heat oil in a wok/kadhai.
1. Add asafoetida and after few seconds, add ginger garlic paste.
1. Saute it properly.
1. This will take about 1 to 2 minutes.
1. Add baby potato and cook them till the colour of baby potatoes turn into golden brown.
1. Meanwhile, mix tamarind paste and sugar in water and add into the potatoes.
1. Add salt to taste and the roasted dry masala.
1. Mix and cook the Chatpata Aloo for two more minute and serve hot.
1. Serve Chatpata Baby Potato along with Panchmel Dal, Lauki Raita and Pudina Tawa Paratha for a weekday meal.
