### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cloves (Laung).
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 3 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Cauliflower (gobi) - florets.
- 3 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dry ginger powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1/4 cup Mustard oil.
- 1-1/2 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 2 teaspoons Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- Ratan jot (alkanet) - infused in oil.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 12 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Kashmiri Phool Rogan Josh Recipe, wash cauliflower and cut them into medium size florets.
1. Allow to dry.
1. In a mixer, add all the ingredients mentioned under “For marinating” and grind them into fine powder.
1. In a bowl, add the chopped cauliflower florets and the grounded powders.
1. Combine them together.
1. Marinate the florets and set it aside for about 1 to 1.
1. 5 hours.
1. Heat oil in a non-stick pan on high flame.
1. Once the oil is smoking hot, add cinnamon stick, cardamoms, cloves and fennel seeds.
1. Cook until the seeds sizzle.
1. Add peppercorns and cook for about a minute.
1. Reduce the flame and add the marinated florets to the spices in the pan.
1. Combine them wellSeason it with salt.
1. Add hing and dry ginger powder.
1. Give them a stir.
1. Now, add the red chili powder and combine them well.
1. If you are adding ratan jot, add at this stage.
1. Heat oil in a small pan.
1. Once the oil is warm, add the root and allow it to infuse in the oil for an hour.
1. Add the infused oil to the florets at this stage.
1. The root is added to the curry for the rich red color.
1. Some prefer to use saffron.
1. Infuse saffron in warm milk and add it to the curry.
1. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, add thick curd and beat them with a whisk.
1. Add the whisked yogurt to the florets and mix them well.
1. Lower the flame and cook the Kashmiri Phool Rogan Josh Recipe in low flame till the florets are cooked thoroughly and oil separates from it.
1. Adjust salt if needed.
1. Enjoy this delicious Kashmiri Phool Rogan Josh Recipe with Homemade Butter Naan Recipe (Soft Yogurt Bread), Saffron Pilaf Recipe (Kesar Pulao) and Burani Raita Recipe.
