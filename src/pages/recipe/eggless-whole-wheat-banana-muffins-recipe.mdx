### Ingredients

- 3/4 cup Caster Sugar.
- 1 Pinch Salt.
- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1/2 cup Cashew nuts - chopped.
- 4 Ripe Bananas - mashed.
- 3/4 cup Milk.
- 1/2 cup Sunflower Oil.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Eggless Whole Wheat Banana Muffins, firstly sift the flour, baking powder, powdered sugar and salt and mix them in a bowl.
1. Keep it aside.
1. In an another bowl, mash the bananas by hand or with a fork.
1. Add vegetable oil and milk and whisk well.
1. You can use hand mixer too for mixing.
1. Add cashew-nut pieces and mix everything well.
1. Pre-heat the oven for 5 Minutes at 175 degree C in convection mode.
1. Line the muffin tray with cupcake covers and fill them with the prepared batter.
1. Fill only till 3/4th of the cupcake cover and leave some space as the muffins will rise.
1. Bake them for about 25-30 minutes.
1. Keep checking after 20 minutes that the muffins are ready or not.
1. To check if the muffin is baked, pierce a toothpick at the centre of the muffin.
1. If the toothpick comes out clean, then it is baked properly.
1. Otherwise, bake it for another 5 minutes.
1. Serve Eggless Whole Wheat Banana Muffins with hot Masala Chai and Grilled Spicy Potato sandwich for your tea time parties.
