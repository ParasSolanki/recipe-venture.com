### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 2 Stalks Spring Onion Greens - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Whole Egg.
- 1 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 3 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 2 Fresh Red chillies - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thai Style Mung Bean Fritter Recipe we will first soak the yellow mung bean in water for at least 1 hour.
1. It is preferable to soak it overnight as the texture may vary.
1. Grind the mung to a coarse mixture.
1. Transfer it into a bowl.
1. Add all the remaining ingredients and mix well.
1. Heat a Kuzhi Paniyaram pan with oil, once it is heated up add a spoon of the mixture and cook them on either side.
1. Keep flipping them so that it will evenly browns on all the sides.
1. For the chutney grind all the ingredients into a mixer by adding about 1/4 cup water and check for salt and serve it in a bowl.
1. Serve the Thai Style Mung Bean Fritter Recipe along with Thai Basil Vegetarian Fried Rice With Pineapple and thai curry.
