### Ingredients

- 1 cup Rice flour.
- Mixed nuts - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Cashew nuts - powdered.
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 2 cups Water.
- 1/2 cup Sugar.
- 1 liter Milk.
- 1 tablespoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Khira Gaintha Recipe - Rice Dumplings In Sweetened Milk, soak the rice in water for about 5 to 6 hours.
1. Then wash and drain the water from rice and dry them under a fan and keep it aside.
1. Grind the rice to a fine mixture.
1. You can use the rice powder also for this recipe.
1. Boil water in a deep pan.
1. Add a tsp of oil and salt to this.
1. Now gradually add the rice flour to the water and keep stirring.
1. Make sure there arenâ€™t any lumps.
1. Lower the flame, cover the pan with a lid and cook it for five minutes.
1. Transfer this mixture to a plate.
1. Let it cool.
1. Once cooled, knead it with your fingers so that it becomes a soft dough.
1. Make small balls out of it.
1. Boil some water and drop these rice balls in boiling water for 4 to 5 minutes.
1. Take out and keep aside.
1. You can also steam these balls.
1. Boil the milk on low flame until milk thickens.
1. Add sugar, Cardamom powder and the cashew nut powder .
1. It will thicken your milk .
1. Now one by one drop the rice balls into the boiling milk and continue stirring.
1. Cook for 1 more minute and turn off the heat.
1. Garnish with chopped pistachios and almonds.
1. Serve chilled.
1. Serve Khira Gaintha Recipe - Rice Dumplings In Sweetened Milk as a dessert after Lobia Kebabs Recipe, One Pot Spicy Vegetable Pulao Recipe with Coconut Milk, Kela Anar Raita Recipe, Butter Naan Recipe.
