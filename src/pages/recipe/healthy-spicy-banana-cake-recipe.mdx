### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Chia Seeds.
- 2 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/2 cup Canola Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 3/4 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1/2 cup Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 1 teaspoon Nutmeg powder.
- 3/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1-1/2 Ripe Bananas.
- 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower seeds - and few more for sprinkling on top.
- 3 tablespoon Walnuts - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Healthy Spicy Banana Cake Recipe, get the all ingredients ready.
1. Preheat oven at 180 degrees C.
1. Grease a 9 inch baking loaf tin with oil.
1. Sift  the flour along with baking powder, baking soda and salt into a mixing bowl.
1. Add cinnamon powder and grated nutmeg.
1. In another mixing bowl beat sugar and oil.
1. Later add curd once sugar is dissolved.
1. In the mixer put in banana and chia seeds and blend them to a puree.
1. Add this puree in the oil sugar curd mixture and mix well.
1. Add vanilla essence.
1. Now add the dry ingredients in the wet ingredients' bowl in parts.
1. Cut and fold the mixture.
1. If the mixture looks too dry add little milk at a time.
1. Cake batter using whole wheat flour is thicker than that of all purpose flour.
1.   Add sunflower seeds and chopped walnuts in the batter.
1. Pour the batter in the greased baking pan.
1. Tap the baking pan to avoid air bubbles.
1. Sprinkle Sunflower seeds on the top and bake in preheated oven for about 35-40 minutes.
1. Check the readiness with the toothpick.
1. Toothpick comes out clean is the indication that cake is baked.
1. Cool the cake on wire rack.
1. Serve Healthy Spicy Banana Cake Recipe along with Fresh Figs Strawberry and Banana Smoothie Recipe for an evening snack.
