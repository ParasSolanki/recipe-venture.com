### Ingredients

- Lemongrass - (1 strand).
- 3 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds - crushed.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 teaspoon Black tea - leaves.
- 6 Tulsi (holy basil).
- 4 Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 1 teaspoon Sugar - (optional).
- 2-1/2 cups Water.
- 1/2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Lemon Ginger Spice Detox Tea Recipe, boil water in a saucepan and add lemon grass strand into the water and allow it to boil for few seconds.
1. Now add crushed ginger, crushed cardamon and cinnamon to it and allow it a quick boil and simmer the gas for 5 minutes.
1. Add tea leaves along with mint and tulsi leaves, and allow the tea to boil for another 2 minutes.
1. You will now see the color of tea changing to golden.
1. Turn off the gas and strain the tea and sip it on empty stomach for best results.
1. You can serve Lemon Ginger Spice Detox Tea Recipe during evenings with healthy snacks as well.
