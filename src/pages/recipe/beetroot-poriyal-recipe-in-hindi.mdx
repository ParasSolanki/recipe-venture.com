### Ingredients

- 2 beetroot - cut into small pieces.
-  1 tablespoon coconut - Tighten.
-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  1 teaspoon white urad dal.
-  1/2 teaspoon mustard.
-  1/4 teaspoon asafoetida.
-  Salt - As per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  1/2 cup small onion - finely chopped.
-  4 curry leaves - break.
-  Oil - As per use.
-  1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder .

### Instructions

1. To make the beetroot poriel, first steam the beetroot.
1. In a pressure cooker, add beetroot, salt and 2 tablespoons of water.
1. Cover the cooker and cook till it comes to 2 cities.
1. After coming to the city, keep the cooker under water and remove the pressure.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now heat the takal in a pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, urad dal and curry leaves.
1. Cook until lentils turn golden.
1. Now add onions, green chilies and cook it.
1. After 3 to 4 minutes add asafoetida, turmeric powder, beetroot, salt and cook for 2 minutes.
1. After cooking, turn off the gas and garnish with coconut.
1. Serve the beetroot poriyal with kirai sambar and rice for dinner.
