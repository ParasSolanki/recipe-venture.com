### Ingredients

- 6 Garlic - finely chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - as per taste.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali).
- Lemon wedges.
- 2 tablespoons Sriracha sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - toasted.
- 1 Lemon - juiced.
- Spring Onion Greens - chopped.
- 4 tablespoons Thai Red Curry Paste.
- Onion - sliced.
- 200 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cut to small squares.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Thai Style Paneer Recipe, first we will marinate the paneer.
1. .
1. In a mixing bowl, add the Thai red curry paste, sriracha sauce, Lemon, garlic, green chillies, salt, pepper and oil.
1. Stir well to combine.
1. Add in the paneer batons and carefully mix in the marinade.
1. Please be gentle to ensure the paneer doesn't break.
1. Let it sit for 30 minutes in the refrigerator.
1. Soak the skewers in some water and keep aside.
1. When ready, get the paneer out of the fridge.
1. Heat up your grill pan or outdoor grill, brush 2 tablespoons oil on the pan.
1. Grill the marinated paneer for 2-3 minutes on both the sides.
1. Don't overcook or it will become rubbery.
1. While grilling, make sure to baste it with the remaining marinade or at least some oil or melted butter.
1. Take the thai style paneer off the grill as soon as you get grill marks and transfer onto a plate.
1.  Garnish the Thai paneer with sesame seeds, panuts, spring onion greens, coriander leaves.
1. Place a few lemon wedges and onions on the side and serve hot.
1. Serve Sweet And Spicy Thai Style Paneer Recipe along with  Chilli Coriander Fried Rice and Roasted Eggplant in Schezwan Sauce.
