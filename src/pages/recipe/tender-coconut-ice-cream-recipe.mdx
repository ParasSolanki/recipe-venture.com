### Ingredients

- 1 cup Fresh cream.
- 1/4 cup Sugar - granulated.
- Honey - for glazing (Optional).
- Ice cream cone - (Optional).
- 1 cup Tender coconut meat - + Little extra.
- 1/2 cup Condensed Milk.
- 1/2 cup Tender coconut water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tender Coconut Ice Cream recipe, in a grinder or blender, grind tender coconut meat along with tender coconut water to make a puree.
1. Now add the full fat cream, sugar and the condensed milk into the same jar and beat everything together until well combined.
1. Chop the extra tender coconut into small chunks and ix it into the the tender coconut ice cream.
1. Your ice cream is all ready now.
1. Pour this tender coconut ice cream mixture in a freezer safe bowl with a tight lid and freeze it for minimum 5-6 hours.
1. Once the ice cream is frozen remove the bowl out, scoop the tender coconut ice cream into the serving bowl.
1. Drizzle some honey over it and a few broken ice cream cone pieces and enjoy.
1. Serve Tender Coconut Ice Cream as a dessert after you simple meal of Mixed Vegetable Sambar, South Indian Coconut Chutney and Ghee Masala Dosa.
