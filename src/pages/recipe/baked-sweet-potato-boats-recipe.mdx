### Ingredients

- 5 Pickled Jalapenos - picked.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Sweet corn - steamed and cooled.
- 1 Tomato - seeded and finely diced.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Apple cider vinegar - or white vinegar.
- 1 tablespoon Water.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Sweet Potatoes.
- 1/4 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely diced.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - as needed.
- Red Chilli sauce - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 cup Cashew nuts - soaked for an hour.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1 tablespoon Apple cider vinegar - or white vinegar.
- 1/2 Onion - finely diced.
- Black pepper powder - a pinch.
- 1 cup Black beans - cooked.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - finely diced.
- 1 clove Garlic - finely minced.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon zest - optional but preferred.
- 1-2 teaspoons Red wine.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Baked Sweet Potato Boat's Recipe, first in a baking tray grease the sweet potatoes with olive oil and bake in a 200 degree preheated oven for 45-60 mins.
1. In a small jar add cashews, apple cider or white vinegar, lemon juice,lemon zest, water and salt.
1. Now in a small mixing bowl add the apple cider vinegar , red wine and olive oil and whisk continuously until combined.
1. Add red chilli powder, cumin powder, dried oregano, raw sugar, pepper, clove to the viniger and red wine mixture and whisk it thoroughly.
1. In a large Mixing bowl add the beans and the chopped vegetables.
1. Drizzle the dressing and mix it into the salad.
1. Using a fork create a cavity into the cooked sweet potato and fill the salad to the brim and drizzle with sour cream and Hot Sauce.
1. Serve the delicious and healthy Baked Sweet Potato Boast's for lunch or early dinner.
1. Notes:Vegan sour cream can be easily substituted with regular Dairy Sour creamAdd 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika (if available)and reduce red chilli powder to 1/2 a Teaspoon.
1. Cold avocado slices make a great addition.
1. The Baked Sweet Potato Boat's can be served for a fresh summer lunch or for early dinner.
