### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 1 tablespoon Cocoa Powder.
- 2 tablespoons Peanut Butter.
- 1 cup Milk.
- 4 Dates - pitted and chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Cranberries - or raisins.
- 1/4 cup Wheat Bran.
- 4 tablespoons Walnuts - chopped.
- 1 Ripe Bananas - sliced.
- 3 Ripe Bananas - chopped into chunks.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chocolate Banana Smoothie Bowl Recipe into a tall jar of the blender add the ripe bananas, milk, peanut butter, oats, cocoa powder and soft pitted dates.
1. Blend well to make a smooth puree.
1. Once all the ingredients are blended well transfer the Chocolate Banana Smoothie into a smoothie bowl.
1. Top the Chocolate Banana Smoothie with chopped walnuts, banana slices, wheat bran flakes and few cranberries or raisins.
1. Serve the Chocolate Banana Smoothie Bowl immediately or chilled.
1. Serve Chocolate Banana Smoothie Bowl Recipe on its own for breakfast to give you a healthy start to your day.
