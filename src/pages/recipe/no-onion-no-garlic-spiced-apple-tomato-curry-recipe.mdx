### Ingredients

-  1/2 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  1 lentil sugar.
-  oil - as per use.
- 2 apples - peel and chop.
-  2 sprigs of coriander - finely chopped.
-  Salt - or kala namak (as per taste).
-  1/2 teaspoon red chili powder.
-  1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  2 tomatoes - puree or grated.
-  1 bay leaf - break .

### Instructions

1. To make apple and tomato vegetable, first heat oil in a pan.
1. Add bay leaves and lentils and cook for 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add tomato puree and all the spices.
1. Cook it for 10 minutes.
1. After 10 minutes, add chopped apples to it and mix.
1. Cook it for 10 minutes until the apple is cooked.
1. After cooking, turn off the gas and serve hot.
1. Serve the apples and tomato vegetables along with dal tadka and roti for the day's dinner.
