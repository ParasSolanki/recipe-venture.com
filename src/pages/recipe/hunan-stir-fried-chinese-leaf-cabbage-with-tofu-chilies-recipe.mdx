### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Homemade Hoisin Sauce.
- 250 grams Tofu - cubed.
- 2 sprig Onions - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Onion - cut into squares.
- 4 Dry Red Chilli - cut into 1 inch.
- 4 cloves Garlic - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 2 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - sliced into diamonds.
- Black pepper powder - for seasoning.
- 1 Chinese cabbage - chopped into big squares.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Hunan stir fried Chinese Leaf cabbage with Tofu & Chilies Recipe by heating a sauce pan with oil, add ginger and garlic, sauté till it is light brown.
1. Then add all the vegetables along with dry red chilies, give it a stir, cover it with a lid and cook it for few minutes.
1. Once the vegetables are cooked and little crunchy, add in all the sauces along with cut tofu and coat it evenly, season it well with salt and pepper.
1. Serve the Hunan stir fried Chinese Leaf cabbage with Tofu & Chilies Recipe with Dan Dan Noodles to enjoy a comforting meal.
