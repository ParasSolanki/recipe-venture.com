### Ingredients

- 2 carrots - cut into 1/2 inch sticks.
-  2 cups cabbage.
-  1/2 teaspoon celery.
-  2 bay leaves.
-  2 cinnamon.
-  7 whole pepper.
-  2 cardamom.
-  1 cup green peas.
-  2 green peppers - slit.
-  mustard Oil - as per use.

### Instructions

1. To make Carrot Cabbage Pea Sabzi Recipe, prepare all the ingredients first.
1. Now heat mustard oil in a pan.
1. Add celery, bay leaves, cinnamon, cardamom and cook for 10 to 20 seconds.
1. Now add green chillies, carrots and cook for 1 minute.
1. After 1 minute, add cauliflower, salt, some water, cover the pan and cook until the cauliflower becomes soft.
1. After the cabbage becomes soft, add the peas, cover and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Turn off the gas and serve.
1. Serve the Carrot Cauliflower Matar Sabzi Recipe along with Dal Fry and Phulka for dinner.
