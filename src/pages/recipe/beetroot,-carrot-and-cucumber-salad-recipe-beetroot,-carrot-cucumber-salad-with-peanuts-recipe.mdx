### Ingredients

-  4 tbsp peanuts.
- 2 Chakundar - Tighten.
-  1 tsp Black pepper powder.
-  1 green chilli - chop.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  4 sprig coriander - chop.
-  1 tsp chaat masala powder.
-  1 cucumber - cut out the seeds.
-  2 carrots - Tighten.
-  1 tsp lemon juice.

### Instructions

1. To make Chakundar, Carrot and Cucumber salad recipe, prepare all the ingredients first.
1. In a mixing bowl, add chakundar, carrot, cucumber, peanuts, green chillies, coriander leaves, lemon juice, black pepper powder, chaat masala powder, salt and mix well.
1. Remove and serve in a bowl.
1. Serve the Chakundar, Carrot and Cucumber Salad recipe with punchy lentils, gourd elder greens and phulka.
