### Ingredients

-  4 curry leaves.
-  2 sprigs of green coriander - chopped.
- 1 cup coconut - grated.
-  2 green chillies - chopped.
-  salt - or rock salt (as per taste).
-  1/4 cup peanuts - roasted.

### Instructions

1. Prepare all the ingredients to make coconut peanut sauce.
1. One by one, grind all the ingredients in the mixer grinder and grind them.
1. Remove and serve in a bowl.
1. Serve coconut peanut chutney with rava idli, carrot idli or dosa of your choice.
