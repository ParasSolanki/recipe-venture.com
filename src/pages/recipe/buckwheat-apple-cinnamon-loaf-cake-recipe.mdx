### Ingredients

- 100 gram Butter - soft low fat.
- 5 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 1 Apple - peeled.
- 11/4 cup Buckwheat Flour (Kuttu Ka Atta).
- 1/2 cup Milk - low fat.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1/2 cup Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar) - (alternatively icing sugar).
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 2/3 cup Icing Sugar.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Baking powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Buckwheat Apple Cinnamon Loaf Cake recipe, sieve all dry ingredients and keep it aside.
1. Preheat the oven to 180.
1. Mix brown sugar, cinnamon and keep it aside.
1. In a bowl, add butter and sugar and blend it until creamy.
1. Add eggs, 1 at a time and blend until pale yellow in colour.
1. Add vanilla extract and mix.
1. Now add dry ingredients and milk alternately.
1. Using cut and fold method mix well.
1. Pour the half batter in greased loaf pan, add half of apples evenly all over.
1. Sprinkle half of cinnamon sugar on top of apple.
1. Now pour remaining batter and add rest of apple on top.
1. Sprinkle remaining Cinnamon sugar on top.
1. Bake for 25 to 40 minute until done.
1. Once it is done, allow it to cool completely.
1. Cut and serve these delicious cakes.
1. Serve Buckwheat Apple Cinnamon Loaf Cake along with Espresso Coffee and Shredded Chicken Cheese Sandwich for your tea party.
