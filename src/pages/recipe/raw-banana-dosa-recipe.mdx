### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut.
- 1 Raw Banana - boiled and cut into small pieces.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 cups Idli Dosa Batter.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Raw Banana Dosa Recipe, make the dosa batter ready by following the recipe link.
1. Pressure cook the raw banana with water and turmeric powder for about 4 whistles until it becomes soft, remove the skin and chop them into small pieces.
1. Grind the raw banana along with coconut by adding little water to a smooth paste.
1. Add the paste to the dosa batter and mix well.
1. Check the salt levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Heat a skillet on high heat; Pour a ladleful of batter in the center and hold the ladle and spread the batter to form an uttapam.
1. Drizzle about a teaspoon of oil around the edges and towards the insides.
1. Cook the dosa on both sides, until you noticed it has browned a little and has slightly crispy edges.
1. To make it extra crispy, cook the dosa on a medium heat and preferably on an iron skillet.
1. Iron skillets maintain heat and brings out a perfect texture to the dosa.
1. Serve the Raw Banana Dosa Recipe along with south Indian Chutney or a Tomato onion chutney recipe to enjoy your breakfast.
