### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Active dry yeast.
- 1 Egg yolks.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 teaspoons Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds).
- 1 tablespoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder -.
- Milk - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoons Spinach - puree.
- 2 cups Vivatta Maida.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spinach Buns recipe, blanch spinach in boiling water and puree it in a mixer.
1. In a mixing bowl, add flour, salt, sugar, instant yeast, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, kasuri methi, garam masala, spinach puree and mix well.
1. Then add yolk, olive oil, and mix.
1. Slowly add water make a soft dough.
1. Cover and keep it for it to double.
1. Punch out air and cut into six parts.
1. Make a smooth ball with each piece and keep it in a baking pan/tray.
1. Allow it to double.
1. When doubles brush with milk and sprinkle nigella seeds over it.
1. Bake it in a pre-heated oven at 180 deg C for 22-25 minutes till it browns on top.
1. Cool and serve.
1. Serve Spinach Buns along with Akuri and Adrak Chai for your weekday breakfast.
