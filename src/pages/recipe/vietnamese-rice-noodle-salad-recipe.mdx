### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 1/2 tablespoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 3 Onions - thinly sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 2 teaspoons Lemon juice.
- 3 cups Water.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger - crushed.
- 1 tablespoon Garlic - crushed.
- 1/2 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - cut into juliennes.
- 1/3 cup Spring Onion Greens - cut lengthwise.
- 100 grams Rice noodles - thin variety.
- Salt - to sprinkle.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 1 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1/2 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 150 grams Tofu - cut into rectangles.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 English Cucumbers - sliced into ribbons lengthwise.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vietnamese Rice Noodle Salad Recipe, we will get all the elements ready.
1. To cook the rice noodles Heat a saucepan with 3 cups of water along with oil and salt on high flame.
1. Once the water comes to a rolling boiling, reduce the flame to medium and add the rice noodles.
1. Allow them to cook for about 4-6 minutes.
1. Turn off the heat, and drain the water, transferring the rice noodles into a sieve.
1. Run the rice noodles under cold water and ruffle the rice noodles, ensuring they don't stick to each other.
1. Set aside.
1. For the fried onions In a skillet heat oil and butter on medium-high flame, add the sliced onions, along with salt and sugar.
1. Fry the onions, stirring occasionally, until they attain a deep brown colour.
1. this will take about 10 mins.
1. Once they achieve a lovely brown colour, turn off the flame and place the onions son an absorbent paper.
1. To make the dressingIn a small mixing bowl, combine the crushed ginger, garlic, red chilli flakes, vinegar, soya sauce, honey, brown sugar, salt and lemon juice and mix well.
1. Allow it to chill in the fridge.
1. To pan roast the tofu Heat a pan with a few drops of oil on medium heat, place the cut tofu rectangles, sprinkle some salt and saute until it gets a light brown colour.
1. To assemble the Vietnamese Rice Noodle SaladIn a mixing bowl, combine the carrots and cucumber, along with salt some and lemon juice and allow it to sit for about 10 minutes.
1. Next, to the bowl of carrots and cucumbers, add rice noodles, pan fried tofu, fried onions, peanuts, coriander leaves, spring onion greens and the chilled salad dressing and toss the Vietnamese Rice Noodle Salad well.
1. Serve Vietnamese Rice Noodle Salad Recipe along with Vietnamese Rice Paper Rolls Recipe and Chicken And Shrimp Bánh Xèo Recipe (Vietnamese Stuffed Crepe) for a complete meal.
1. You can serve Vietnamese Pitaya & Calamansi Sorbet Recipe (Dragon Fruit & Small Lime Sorbet) as dessert, to finish of the meal with.
