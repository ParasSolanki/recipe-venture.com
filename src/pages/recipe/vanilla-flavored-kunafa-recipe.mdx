### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour - dissolved in 2 tablespoon milk.
- 1 tablespoon Sugar.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 100 grams Butter - melted.
- 1/2 cup Sugar.
- 200 grams Kunafa pastry.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Vanilla Flavored Kunafa recipe, start by making sugar syrup.
1. In a medium saucepan, combine sugar and water.
1. Let it boil, stirring, until sugar has dissolved.
1. Keep aside to cool to make sugar syrup.
1. To make cream, in a pan, add milk and sugar together.
1. Let it come to a boil.
1. Add cornstarch mixture to the milk and keep stirring, till the mixture thickens, then remove from the heat.
1. Add vanilla essence and keep aside to cool, to prepare cream.
1. Instruction For KunafaCut the kunafa thread into small pieces with hands.
1. Pour melted butter, 1 teaspoon sugar and vanilla essence over the kunafa threads and mix well with your hands.
1. Put half of the kunafa in the cake pan and press well with your fingers.
1. Spread all the cream over kunafa and add the other half of the kunafa over the cream.
1. Now bake vanilla flavored kunafa in a preheated oven at 180 degrees Celsius, for 20 minutes or until it turns golden on the top.
1. Take out the kunafa from oven and after 10 minutes left to cool, flip it on a plate.
1. Now pour cooled sugar syrup on the hot kunafa and leave it for 7 to 8 minutes to let kunafa absorb all the sugar syrup.
1. Now garnish vanilla flavored kunafa with crushed pistachios and serve hot after a delicious and wholesome dinner with Lucknowi Murg Biryani or Hyderabadi Vegetable Biryani Recipe.
