### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Caster Sugar.
- 3 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 4 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - cut into small cubes.
- 1/2 cup Broccoli - cut into tiny florets.
- 1 cup Broken Wheat (Dalia/ Godumai Rava).
- 1 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - soaked for 8 hours and boiled.
- 1/8 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 3/4 cup Feta Cheese - crumbled.
- 1/3 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped.
- 3 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - cut into small cubes.
- 2 Onions - thinly sliced.
- Salt and Pepper - for seasoning.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Greek Style Broken Wheat Salad with Chickpeas Fresh Mint & Feta Recipe by cooking the broken wheat in a pressure cooker with 1 ½ cups of water for about 2 whistle.
1. Release the pressure naturally, drain the water and toss it over olive oil and place it on tray, spread.
1. So that it separates.
1. Keep all the ingredients ready, and blanch the carrot and broccoli in hot water till it is cooked and drain it.
1. In a large mixing bowl add all the ingredients and toss it over.
1. Check for seasonings and add if it is required for more.
1. Serve the Greek Style Broken Wheat Salad with Chickpeas Fresh Mint & Feta Recipe for breakfast or as a Light Dinner meal.
