### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 300 grams Spinach - washed and finely chopped.
- 1 pinch SSP Asafoetida (Hing).
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Padpe Uppukari Harive Soppu Palya Recipe, heat a kadai with oil on medium flame, add the mustard seeds and allow it to splutter, next add the urad dal, a pinch of hing, dry red chillies and curry leaves as well.
1. Allow the curry leaves to crackle.
1. To this add chopped garlic and onions, saute till it the onions turn translucent.
1. Finally, add the chopped spinach leaves and cook the Harive Soppu Palya.
1. Saute well till the spinach leaves wilts down.
1. Once done, stir in the grated coconut, salt to taste and mix Harive Soppu Palya well.
1. Check the taste and adjust the seasonings accordingly.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. Transfer the Harive Soppu Palya to a serving bowl and serve warm.
1. Serve the Padpe Uppukari Harive Soppu Palya Recipe along with Steamed Rice,  Dali Thoy Recipe (Karwar Style Toor dal Curry) and Cabbage Palya Recipe for a complete meal.
