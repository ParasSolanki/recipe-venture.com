### Ingredients

- 1 cup Black Eyed Beans (Lobia) - cooked.
- 1 Baguette - sliced.
- 2-3 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- Lemon juice - from one lemon.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - for toasting the baguettes.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon zest.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Black Eyed Bean And Lemon Bruschetta, you will first have to have the cooked black eyed beans.
1. Once you have the cooked beans, allow it to cool before you proceed with the recipe.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the cooked black eyed beans, along with the olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt and pepper.
1. Stir in the lemon zest and mint leaves to the mixture and stir to combine.
1. Check the salt and pepper and adjust to suit your taste and keep this bean topping aside.
1. In our next step we will grill the baguette in the oven.
1. Preheat the oven to 200 C.
1. Slice and brush the with olive oil.
1. Arrange the olive oil baguettes on a baking sheet and grill in the oven until golden and crisp.
1. Once toasted, remove the baguettes from the oven and arrange them on a serving platter.
1. Spread a spoonful of black eyed bean mixture over each slice and serve.
1. Serve Black Eyed Bean and Lemon Bruschetta along with Roasted Tomato and Pumpkin Soup for a weeknight dinner.
