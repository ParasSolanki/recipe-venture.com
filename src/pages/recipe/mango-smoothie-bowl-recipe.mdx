### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Dessicated Coconut - grated.
- 1/2 cup Kiwi - sliced.
- 1/2 cup Watermelon - scooped.
- 1 teaspoon Pumpkin seeds.
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 cup Papaya - freshly cut into chunks.
- 2 cups Mango (Ripe) - freshly cut into chunks.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mango Smoothie Bowl recipe, blend Mango, Papaya and Yogurt for 10 to 20 seconds in a blender.
1. Pour the Mango Smoothie into a bowl and top it with watermelon scoops, kiwi slices, coconut and pumpkin seeds.
1. Serve Mango Smoothie Bowl on its own for a Morning breakfast along with Mushroom Masala Cheese Omelette.
