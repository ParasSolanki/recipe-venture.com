### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Chia Seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/4 cup Spinach Leaves (Palak) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried basil leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Britannia Cream Cheese - softened.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 cup Walnuts - finely chopped.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - peeled and grated.
- 1 clove Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Pimiento-stuffed green olives - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Savory Carrot Spinach Sandwich Spread Recipe wash, peel carrots and grate it in a bowl.
1. Wash spinach in water to get rid of any dirt particles.
1. Finely chop spinach leaves and transfer to the same bowl as carrot.
1. Similarly fine chop olives, pecans and garlic clove and add to the bowl of chopped vegetables.
1. Now add cream cheese, salt, pepper, basil, chia seeds to the vegetables.
1. Stir with the spoon, add olive oil to get the smooth spreading consistency.
1. Adjust seasoning as per your taste.
1. The Savory Carrot Spinach Spread is ready to be served as a spread on sandwiches, wraps or even as a dip for parties.
1. The sandwiches made from Savory Carrot Spinach Spread also make great school lunch box additions for kids.
