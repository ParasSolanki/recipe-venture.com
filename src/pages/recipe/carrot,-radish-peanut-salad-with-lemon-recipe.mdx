### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Dry ginger powder.
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 3 tablespoon Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - pound.
- 2 Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish) - grated.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 2 Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Carrot, Radish & Peanut Salad with Lemon Recipe, we will keep the vegetables grated and ready in a bowl.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine carrots, radish, green chilli, toasted peanuts, cumin powder, ginger powder, lemon juice andcoriander leaves.
1. Toss the salad well and serve.
1. Serve the Carrot, Radish & Peanut Salad with Lemon Recipe along with Thepla, Dal Palak and Dahi Bhindi Recipe by the side.
