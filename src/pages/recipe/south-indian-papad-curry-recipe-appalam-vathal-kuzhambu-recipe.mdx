### Ingredients

-  1 teaspoon horse-powder.
-  3 teaspoons sesame oil.
-  tamarind - as big as lemon.
-  1 teaspoon mustard.
-  1 tablespoon sambar powder.
-  1 pinch Asafoetida.
-  1/2 teaspoon gram lentils.
-  1 curry leaves.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder.
- 4 papad - urad dal.
-  1 dry red chili.

### Instructions

1. To make South Indian Papad Curry, dip tamarind in 2 cups of hot water and keep aside.
1. Heat 2 teaspoons of sesame oil in a pan on medium heat, and add mustard seeds and gram and wait for it to crackle.
1. Now add asafoetida, red chili and curry leaves and let it cook for 30-40 seconds.
1. Add chopped papads and fry them in oil.
1. Now add tamarind water, turmeric, horse and salt and cook until the tamarind is raw.
1. Finally add a teaspoon of oil and let it come to a boil, then turn off the gas.
1. South Indian Papad Curry is now ready, serve it with rice and chow chow kootu at dinner.
