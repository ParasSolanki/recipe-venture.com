### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Corn flour - dissolved in 2 tablespoon water.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 3 Garlic - crushed.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Spring Onion Greens - chopped.
- 150 grams Tofu - cubed.
- 1/4 cup Coconut milk.
- 1/2 cup Vegetable stock.
- Black pepper powder - according to your taste.
- 1 teaspoon Sweet and Spicy Red Chilli Sauce (Tomato Chilli Sauce).
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - whites.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Ginger Garlic Tofu curry, heat 1 tablespoon oil in a pan.
1. Add crushed ginger and garlic and cook it till it turns golden brown.
1. Once it is done, add chopped spring onion whites, greens and chilies into the pan.
1. Stir it well.
1. Add chopped carrot and mix it properly.
1. Add turmeric powder, tomato chili sauce and pepper powder.
1. make sure everything is mixed properly.
1. Add soya sauce, vegetable stock and coconut milk and stir everything nicely.
1. Cover the pan and cook it for about 10 minutes.
1. Add tofu cubes and salt according to your taste.
1. Let it cook for about 10 minutes more.
1. Add corn flour dissolved water and cook for another 2 minutes.
1. Switch off the heat and transfer it to a serving bowl.
