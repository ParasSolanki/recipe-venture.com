### Ingredients

- 1 cup Vivatta Maida.
- 1 teaspoon Corn flour.
- 1/4 cup Icing Sugar.
- Salt - a pinch if you are using unsalted butter.
- 1/4 cup Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 packet Britannia Cream Cheese - if you find it difficult to get cream cheese all you can do is take grated paneer add pinch salt n grind it in your mixer.
- 1 Whole Eggs.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice - can add lemon zest as well.
- Salt - a pinch.
- 2 tablespoons Milk Powder.
- 50 grams Butter - at room temperature salted or unsalted.
- 1/4 cup Sugar.
- 25 grams Butter - 25 grams of butter.
- 1 Whole Egg - 2 eggs depending on how many tarts you have prepared.
- 1/3 cup Mozzarella cheese.
- 1 Egg yolks - or 2.

### Instructions

1. To bgein making the Cheese Tarts recipe, lets start with the crust take butter and icing sugar mix well in stand or electric mixer till butter is white n creamy.
1. Add egg and vanilla extract.
1. Mix them egg should mix well with the butter.
1. In the end add maida, milk powder and mix well like a dough just like chapati dough but a bit softer.
1. Wrap it a plastic wrap and rest for 10 minutes.
1. Preheat  your oven at 190 degree Celsius for 10 minutes.
1. Roll the dough n make small circle shouldnâ€™t be too thick or thin just like how you make pooris you can use tart moulds or just take small circles n fix them in your cupcakes tray or moulds.
1. Bake the tarts at 190 degrees for 15 to 16 minutes or till the tarts are lightly brown once they are out of oven they will turn out crispNow comes the filling to mix all the ingredients you can either use double boiler method or just take a non stick pan and simmer it.
1. Add cream cheese, cheese, lemon juice, sugar, vanilla extract mix till cheese melts.
1. Add milk, corn starch and add very little water mix it well and add to the mixture.
1. Give it a nice mix till everything is mixed well.
1. Remove from heat, cool a bit and add eggs one by one and butter mix well and cool the mixture.
1. Once the crust cools down add the filling and brush the filling with egg yolk it gives a nice glaze.
1. Bake once again at 230 degree Celsius for 6 to 7 minutes.
1. Remove from the oven let the tarts cool down.
1. Can store them in fridge or in a container outside at room temperature.
1. Serve Cheese Tarts along with Cafe Latte and Aloo Tikki Stuffed Pita Sandwich for your tea break.
