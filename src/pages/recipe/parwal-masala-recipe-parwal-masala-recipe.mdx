### Ingredients

- 300 gm Parwal - Peel and chop straight.
-  3 tsp watermelon.
-  1 tsp coriander powder.
-  1 / 2 tsp garam masala powder.
-  1 bay leaf.
-  2 tomatoes - chop.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 tsp cumin powder.
-  1 inch ginger - finely chopped.
-  1 tsp red chilli powder.
-  1 tsp oil.

### Instructions

1. To make the parwal masala recipe, first wash and peel the parwal.
1. Cut it in half and keep it aside.
1. In a mixer grinder, add ginger, tomato, watermelon and make a paste.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add parwal and cook till it becomes soft.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now add bay leaves, tomato ginger paste in the same pan and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. After 4 minutes add red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala powder and mix.
1. Allow to cook for 3 minutes and after that add parwal, water and let it cook.
1. Cook for 5 minutes, turn off the gas and serve.
1. Serve Parwal Masala Recipe with Tawa Paratha and Gourd Raita for dinner.
