### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Chocolate sauce.
- 1/4 Cup Caster Sugar.
- 1/2 Cup Heavy whipping cream.
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Instant Coffee Powder.
- 1/4 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/2 Cup Dark chocolate - melted.
- 1/4 Cup Condensed Milk.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Butter.
- 1 tablespoon Icing Sugar.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Eggless Coffee Souffle Recipe With Chocolate Sauce, Preheat oven to 200°C.
1. Heat 1/2 cup milk in a non-stick pan.
1. Add caster sugar and stir till it dissolves.
1. Place refined flour in a bowl, add remaining milk and whisk till smooth.
1. Add condense milk,  Add the sweetened milk and whisk well.
1. Pour this mixture into a deep non-stick pan and cook, stirring continuously.
1. Melt butter in the microwave oven and pour into a bowl.
1. Add this to the pan and whisk well so that there are no lumps.
1. Cook till thick.
1. Switch off heat and transfer into a bowl to cool to room temperature.
1. You can cool it in a refrigerator.
1. Fold the heavy whipped cream into the chocolate custard and add melted dark chocolate, mocha coffee powder and chocolate sauce with a light hand.
1. Grease 2 ramekin moulds and Pour the mixture into them upto ¾ full and bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes.
1. The Eggless Coffee Souffle Recipe With Chocolate Sauce in the ramekin moulds will be ready in 15 minutes.
1. sprinkle some icing sugar on the souffle and enjoy it hot.
