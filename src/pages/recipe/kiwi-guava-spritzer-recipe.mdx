### Ingredients

- 2 Kiwi.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - for garnish.
- 4 Guava - (pink variety) cut into cubes.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger - grated.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 1/4 cup Sugar.
- 3 cups Aerated water (Soda Water).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kiwi Guava Spritzer Recipe, wash and cut the guavas into cubes, likewise de-skin the kiwi cut into cubes and keep them aside.
1. Place the guavas into a blender along with ginger and make a smooth puree out of it.
1. Pass it through a seive to discard the seeds and the residue.
1. Introduce the guava pulp back into the blender, add the kiwi cubes, lemon juice, sugar, 1/2 cup water and make a smooth mix.
1. Just before serving divide the mix in equal part into 6 tall glasses to half, and fill the rest half with the aerated water.
1. Garnish with mint leaves.
1. It is ready to be served.
1. Serve the refreshing Kiwi Guava Spritzer Recipe, to your guests at home for a welcome drink, may be along with Crispy & Spicy Paneer Starter Recipe.
