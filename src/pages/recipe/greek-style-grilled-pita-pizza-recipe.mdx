### Ingredients

- 5 Black olives.
- 1 cup Homemade Hummus.
- 4 Mini Pita Breads.
- 1 teaspoon Fresh Thyme leaves - dried.
- 1/2 cup Feta Cheese - crumbled.
- Whole Black Peppercorns - pound.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - for cooking.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 5 Tomatoes - quartered.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Greek Style Grilled Pita Pizza Recipe, firstly grill the pita bread on a griddle pan with some oil.
1. Turn over and cook both the sides till it is golden brown.
1. In the meantime, cut the tomatoes and add salt, pepper, thyme and olive oil and toss them in a small bowl and keep it aside.
1. In another bowl, crumble the Feta Cheese, add salt, olive oil, thyme, toss the cheese and keep it aside.
1. It is time to assemble the pizza, so for that apply hummus on the pita and spread it over, sprinkle a pinch of red chilli powder, add the tossed tomatoes and feta cheese on the hummus and place the cut olives.
1. Finally, garnish with coriander leaves.
1. Serve the Greek Style Grilled Pita Pizza as a party starter along with Sparkling Strawberry Mocktail or serve it for evening snacks with a cup of Masala Tea or Espresso Coffee.
