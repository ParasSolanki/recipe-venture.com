### Ingredients

- 5 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 3 tablespoons Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Salt - (adjust as per taste).
- 100 grams Fresh Red chillies.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Instant Red Chilli Pickle Recipe, wash the fresh red chillies and wipe them with kitchen towel.
1. De-seed them and Cut them lengthwise.
1. In separate bowl, mix the mustard seeds, fennel seeds, salt, turmeric powder, lemon juice and about half the oil.
1. Add the red chillies into this mixture and stir well.
1. Take a pickle jar, and fill it with this red chilli pickle.
1. Pour remaining oil into jar and let it rest for 2-3 hours.
