### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds - roasted and crushed.
- 4 Dry Red Chilli.
- 2 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- Salt - to taste.
- 3/4 cup Onion - paste.
- 4 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 750 grams Mutton.
- 2 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 tablespoon Ginger - paste.
- 4 tablespoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/4 cup Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Garlic - paste.
- 4 Cloves (Laung).
- 1/2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Rajasthani Mutton Banjara recipe, heat oil in a non stick wok and add bay leaf, pepper, cloves, cinnamon and dry red chilies and sauté for a minute.
1. Add onion paste and fry till raw smell goes off.
1. Now add ginger garlic paste and fry it till its golden in colour and leaves a nice aroma.
1. Add mutton along with curd and mix well.
1. Cook on high till curd water evaporates.
1. Now add coriander powder, red chilli powder, turmeric and mix well.
1. Fry on low heat till oil starts leaving from sides.
1. Add warm water as per requirement and adjust salt to taste.
1. Mix well and pressure cook the mutton till soft.
1. Carefully remove the lid and add crushed coriander seeds all over and give a gentle mix.
1. Serve Rajasthani Mutton Banjara along with Roomali Roti, Phulkas or Steamed rice for a weekend dinner.
