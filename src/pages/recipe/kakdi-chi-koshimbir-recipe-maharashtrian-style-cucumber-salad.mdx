### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - roughly crushed in a mortar and pestle.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 teaspoons Lemon juice.
- 1 Cucumber - peeled and cubed.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kakdi Chi Koshimbir Recipe, in a mixing bowl, combine the cubed cucumbers, coriander, green chilli, grated coconut and give it a good mix.
1. Roughly pound the roasted peanuts using a mortar and pestle and add it to the salad bowl and mix.
1. Season with salt, sugar, lemon juice and mix all the ingredients evenly.
1. Now, heat ghee in a small tadka pan on medium heat, add the mustard seeds, red chilli and let it splutter.
1. Switch off the flame, add the curry leaves, once it crackles, pour this tadka over the cucumber salad and mix well.
1. Serve the Kakdi Chi Koshimbir Recipe along with Maharastrian Kairichi Amti Recipe and Steamed Rice for a simple Maharashtrian meal.
