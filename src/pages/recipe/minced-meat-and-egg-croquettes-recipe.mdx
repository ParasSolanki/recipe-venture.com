### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 2 Whole Eggs - beaten.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/4 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- Salt - Salt to taste.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 4 Whole Eggs - boiled and peeled.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 tablespoon All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- Water - if required.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 500 grams Mutton - minced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Minced Meat And Egg Croquettes recipe, heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add finely chopped onion saut√© until onions turn translucent.
1. Add ginger garlic paste and cook till the raw smell of the paste goes off.
1. Add the spice powders and salt mix well.
1. Add minced mutton and cook for 5 minutes.
1. Mutton will release its juices.
1. I cooked the meat in its own juices without adding any water.
1. Took about 30 minutes to get it fully cooked.
1. Remove from stove and cool completely.
1. Heat oil for deep frying in a deep fry pan.
1. Divide the minced mutton mixture into 4 equal portions.
1. Take one portion and place it in your palm, press and try to get it together.
1. Slightly flatten it and place one full boiled egg in the middle and gather the meat to cover the egg fully.
1. Try to shape it into cylindrical shape.
1. If you find it too hard to handle the meat to shape it, try adding 1 or 2 teaspoon of beaten egg and work with the meat.
1. Roll the croquettes in flour first, then dip it in egg and then roll with bread crumbs.
1. Repeat this for all the meat.
1. Deep fry the croquettes until golden brown in oil.
1. Remove of kitchen paper.
1. Serve Minced Meat And Egg Croquettes hot with Tomato Garlic Chutney or any dipping sauce of your choice.
