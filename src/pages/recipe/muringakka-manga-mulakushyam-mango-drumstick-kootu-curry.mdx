### Ingredients

- 1 Mango (Raw) - peeled & chopped to bite-size.
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Coconut Oil.
- 2 Green Chillies.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2-1/2 tablespoons Water.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 cups Water.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/2 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal) - soaked for 30 minutes.
- 1 Drumstick - chopped to about 1.5 inch.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Muringakka Manga Mulakushyam get all the ingredients ready, so it will be easy to get started.
1. In a pressure cooker, add toor dal, drumstick, mango & 2 cups of water, salt & turmeric powder.
1. Pressure cook dal for 3 to 4 whistles or till soft and mushy.
1.  Let the pressure release completely.
1. In a mixer, add coconut, green chilies, jeera, water, and grind them into fine paste.
1. Add the coconut to the dal, mango & drumstick mixture.
1. Combine all the ingredients well.
1. Simmer the flame and cook the Muringakka Manga Mulakushyam for about 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Adjust the consistency of the Mulakushyam, by adding a little water.
1. Check the salt and adjust to suit your taste.
1. In a tadka pan, add a little coconut oil.
1. Once the oil is hot, add mustard seeds.
1. As soon as oil splutters, add urad dhal, red chilies, and curry leaves.
1. Pour the tadka over the curry and serve steaming Muringakka Manga Mulakushyam.
