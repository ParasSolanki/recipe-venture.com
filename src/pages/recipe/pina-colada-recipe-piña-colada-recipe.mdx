### Ingredients

-  10 pistachios - chopped.
-  30 mL white Bacardi rum - optional.
-  2 tablespoons sugar.
- Ice - 1/2 cup pineapple - peel and chop.
-  1 cup coconut milk.
-  as per the experiment.

### Instructions

1. To make the Peena Colada recipe, firstly put pineapple, ice, coconut milk, sugar and white Bacardi rum in a blender and blend it well.
1. Remove in a glass and garnish with pistachios and pineapples.
1. Serve.
1. With Pina Colada recipe you can serve Aloo Paneer Kebab Recipe and house parties with spicy potatoes.
