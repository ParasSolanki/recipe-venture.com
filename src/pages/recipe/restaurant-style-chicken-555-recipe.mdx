### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- Curry leaves - a few.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil - to cook.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 tablespoon Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).
- 1 teaspoon Rice flour.
- 250 grams Chicken - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Restaurant Style Chicken 555 Recipe, wash the chicken pieces in thoroughly under running water.
1. In a mixing bowl, marinate the chicken with all the spices mentioned- red chilli powder, haldi, garam masala, rice flour, semolina and salt.
1. Mix well to coat the chicken properly and allow it to rest for about 30 minutes under refrigeration.
1. Once the marination is done, heat oil in a shallow frying pan.
1. Once the oil is hot on medium heat, place the marinated chicken in the pan.
1. Place only number of pieces which can fit into the pan in one layer with a little space between them.
1. Do not overcrowd.
1. Also pour the marinated mix in the pan.
1. I made it in two batches and hence had to reserve half of the marinated mix for the next batch.
1. You can fry according to the quantity.
1. Once the chicken are crisp and done, add a few curry leaves and saut√© till leaves become crisp.
1. Serve Restaurant Style Chicken 555 Recipe topped with the leftover masala in pan along with curry leaves over the pieces.
1. Serve Restaurant Style Chicken 555 Recipe along with a meal Mughlai Style Chicken Changezi Recipe and Whole Wheat Onion Stuffed Kulcha Recipe for a complete meal.
