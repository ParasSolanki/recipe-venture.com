### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 3/4 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 1 inch Ginger - roughly chopped.
- 2 cups Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lehsuni Pudina Chutney, firstly keep all the ingredients ready.
1. Next, take a mixer grinder jar and add all the ingredients in it.
1. Add a little water and grind it to a smooth paste.
1. Do not add too much water as we want a thick paste and not watery chutney.
1. Once ready, take it out in a bowl and serve.
1. Serve Lehsuni Pudina Chutney along with Punjabi Aloo Paratha and Tadka Raita for a weekday dinner.
