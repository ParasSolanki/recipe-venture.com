### Ingredients

- 2-3 Green Chilli.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely sliced.
- 1 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - diced.
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 250 grams Semiya (Vermicelli) - MTR Roasted.
- 1/2 inch Ginger.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Biryani Masala.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - handful.
- Green beans (French Beans) - finely chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - as needed.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin the preparation of Vermicelli Biryani, first heat half a teaspoon of oil in a pan and add the ingredients to grind one after the other and slowly saute them on low flame until they slightly wilt and the raw smell goes.
1. Cool and grind to a smooth paste.
1. Keep Aside.
1. In a pan, heat oil, add jeera, once it starts spluttering, add onions, saute till they're translucent.
1. Add the carrot, capsicum, beans and peas, slowly saute them till they're slightly tender.
1. Now add turmeric, garam masala, dhaniya powder, biryani masala and mix till they're well incorporated.
1. Add the ground paste, salt and fry for 2-3 minutes.
1. Add 1 cup of water, and let it come to a boil.
1. Now add the roasted vermicelli and mix them all together.
1. Cook it covered for about 5-7 minutes until the vermicelli has cooked al dente.
1. Sprinkle some coriander leaves and enjoy the Vermicelli Biryani while it is hot.
