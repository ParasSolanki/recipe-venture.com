### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 50 grams Basil leaves - fresh.
- 3 tablespoons Red Wine Vinaigrette.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Tomatoes - sliced 1/4 inch thick.
- Black pepper powder - freshly ground.
- 400 grams Mozzarella cheese - sliced 1/4 inch thick.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Insalata Caprese Salad Recipe, we will first prepare and slice the tomatoes and cheese for the salad.
1. We will next arrange the tomatoes and mozzarella on a large serving platter.
1. Overlap the tomatoes mozzarella and basil.
1. Sprinkle the salt and the coarsely ground black pepper.
1. Drizzle the olive oil and red wine vinaigrette.
1. Serve the Insalata Caprese Salad fresh as an appetizer along with Crispy Eggless Bread Pinwheels Recipe and Orange Mocktail Recipe to begin your Italian Meal.
