### Ingredients

- 1/8 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 teaspoons Ginger Paste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a bunch.
- 1 Lemon - sliced.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 4 Shrimps - large.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Sambal paste.

### Instructions

1. To begin Shrimp Sambal Recipe, firstly clean and wash the shrimps and boil them for five minutes.
1. In a wok, add some sesame oil and fry the shrimps with salt for 3 minutes.
1. Once the shrimps are dried, add the ginger paste, chopped garlic and onions and saute until the onions become soft.
1. Add the sambal paste, and give it a good stir.
1. Add the lemon juice and the same slice of lemon to the wok.
1. Sprinkle some water to cook for another 5 minutes.
1. Stir continuously, to avoid burning and check for salt.
