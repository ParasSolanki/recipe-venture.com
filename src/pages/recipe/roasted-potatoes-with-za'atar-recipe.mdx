### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 6 Potatoes (Aloo) - cut in cubes.
- 3 tablespoons Za'atar.
- 1 tablespoon Paprika powder - or to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Dried Thyme Leaves.
- 4 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 1 tablespoon Sea salt - or to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Sumac.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Roasted Potatoes With Za'atar Recipe first make your spice mix by combining all the ingredients under “To make your own Za'atar” and grind them in a mixer or coffee grinder.
1. Pre-heat oven to 200 degrees C.
1. In a mixing bowl add potato cubes, prepared za'atar mix, paprika, lemon juice and olive oil.
1. Toss well util all the potatoes are well coated with the spice mix and oil.
1. Line a baking sheet with aluminium foil or parchment paper and transfer the potatoes onto the tray.
1. Spread them around and place the tray in the oven to bake for 40 to 50 minutes or until potatoes are golden and crispy.
1. Take them out of the oven.
1. Serve Roasted Potatoes With Za'atar along with Tzatziki or a Peanut Chilli Dipping Sauce for a lip-smacking snack.
