### Ingredients

- 1 cup Basmati rice.
- 1 inch Ginger - crushed.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 2 Stalks Lemon Grass.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Spring Onion Greens - chopped.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Green Chilli Sauce.
- 1 cup Coconut milk.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lemon Grass Flavored Tofu Fried Rice Recipe, cook the basmati rice in a pressure cooker by adding coconut milk and 1/2 a cup of water, chopped lemongrass, crushed ginger and turmeric powder.
1. Pressure cook for about 2 whistle and switch off the heat.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally and rest it aside for some time.
1. Take out the lemon grass and crushed ginger from the pressure cooked rice and mix the rice well with a fork.
1. Heat a wok with some oil, add ginger and garlic that are chopped and saute till they soften.
1. Add chopped onions and saute till they turn translucent.
1. Once that is done you can add chopped bell peppers as well.
1. Sprinkle some salt, chopped green chillies and saute till the bell peppers soften.
1. Add cubed tofu pieces, pepper powder, soy sauce and green chilli sauce and give it a toss.
1. Add the flavored lemon grass rice and toss well into the saut√©ed vegetables.
1. Check for salt and finally add chopped spring onions and serve warm.
1. Serve the Lemongrass Flavored Tofu Fried Rice Recipe along with Gobi Manchurian Recipe and Spring Rolls Recipe to make it a complete meal.
