### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 3/4 cup Seven up.
- Lemon glaze.
- 3/4 lb Butter - softened.
- 2/3 cup Sugar - powdered.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon zest.
- 3 cups All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 3 cups Sugar.
- 5 Whole Egg.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lemon 7 Up Cake recipe, cream butter and sugar together for 20 minutes.
1. Add eggs, 1 at a time, beating after each addition.
1. Add flour and lemon extract.
1. Fold in 7-Up.
1. Pour into well-greased 12-cup Bundt pan.
1. Bake at 325 degrees for 1 to 1-1/4 hours.
1. The next step is to make the lemon glaze.
1. Mix powdered sugar and lemon juice together.
1. Keep it aside.
1. When cake is cooled, dribble with Lemon Glaze.
1. Serve Lemon 7 Up Cake for some special ocassions or for birthday parties.
