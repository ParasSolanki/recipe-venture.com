### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1 tablespoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- Chocolate chips - as required.
- 1 cup Caster Sugar.
- Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini) - as required.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 2 tablespoons Cocoa Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Whole Wheat Chocolate Cinnamon Cupcakes recipe, in a mixing bowl, add milk, oil, vanilla extract and castor sugar and mix it well.
1. In another bowl, sieve whole wheat flour with baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon powder and cocoa powder all together.
1. Now gradually add this mixture into milk mixture and mix it into all combined.
1. Preheat the microwave at 180 degrees.
1. Set the cupcake liners into cupcake mould and pour the batter into liners and sprinkle chocolate chips on top.
1. Bake them for 20-25 minutes into preheated microwave.
1. Let them cool and garnish them with cinnamon powder on top.
1. Serve Whole Wheat Chocolate Cinnamon Cupcakes along with Espresso Coffee and Avocado Egg Sandwich for your next tea party.
