### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Curry leaves.
- 1/4 cup Pumpkin seeds.
- 1/4 cup Watermelon seeds.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 10 Dry Red Chillies - adjustable.
- 1/3 cup White Urad Dal (Split).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Watermelon Seeds And Pumpkin Seeds Podi recipe, first of all keep a big plate ready.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, add the urad dal and roast it till it releases a nice aroma.
1. Switch off the gas and transfer it to the plate.
1. Spread it on one side.
1. Similarly, roast the dry chillies and transfer it to the plate.
1. Next, add the curry leaves and keep a close watch ensuring that they don't turn brown.
1. It should get crispy.
1. Now heat the pan and switch it off.
1. Add one seed at a time and transfer to the plate.
1. Heat the pan again and repeat the process.
1. Take the seeds out in the plate too.
1. Next, add salt and asafoetida in the plate and let all the ingredients cool down for about half an hour.
1. After half an hour, grind the mixture in a jar to get a coarse powder.
1. Your Podi is ready to serve.
1. Serve Watermelon Seeds And Pumpkin Seeds Podi as a side dish along with Steamed Rice, Keerai Sambar and Cabbage Thoran for a weekday meal.
