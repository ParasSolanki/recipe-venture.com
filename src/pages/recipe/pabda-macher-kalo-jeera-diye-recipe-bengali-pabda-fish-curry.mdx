### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- Water - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoons Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Mustard oil.
- 3 Pabda fish.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pabda Macher Kalo Jeera Diye recipe, firstly wash and clean the fish.
1. In a bowl, combine the fish along with salt and 1 teaspoon of turmeric powder and marinade for 30 minutes.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan on medium flame and shallow fry the fish, cover and cook.
1. Flip the fish to the other side and cook them till they change the colour and turn a little crisp.
1. Once cooked, transfer to a plate and keep aside.
1. Now in the same pan, heat oil on medium flame, add the potatoes, salt to taste and a little turmeric powder.
1. Let it cook till the potatoes become crisp and golden in colour.
1. This will take about 8-10 minutes.
1. Once it is cooked, drain on an absorbent paper, and keep it aside.
1. Next in a small mixing bowl, combine turmeric powder, red chilli powder, cumin powder and coriander powder, 1/2 cup of water and let it sit for 5 minutes.
1. This makes the spice mix for the Pabda Macher Kalo Jeera Diye recipe.
1. Next in the remaining oil in the pan, add nigella seeds, chopped tomatoes and green chillies.
1. Cook them till the tomatoes turn soft and mushy.
1. This will take about 5 minutes.
1. Once the tomatoes are soft, add the ginger paste and mix everything well.
1. After about 2 to 3 minutes, add the Pabda Macher Spice Mixture.
1. Adjust the consistency of the curry to your liking, and allow it to boil.
1. Once it starts boiling, add salt, fried fish and and let it cook for about 3-4 minutes minutes.
1. Once done, switch off the heat, add the roasted potatoes and garnish with coriander leaves.
1. Your Pabda Macher Kalo Jeera Diye is ready to be servedServe Pabda Macher Kalo Jeera Diye along with Steamed Rice and Kachumber Salad for a weekday meal.
