### Ingredients

- Salt - salt to taste.
- 6 Whole Eggs.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped as required.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- Cheese - shredded.
- Lemon zest - as required.
- 2 tablespoon Milk.
- 4 tablespoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 1 Avocado.
- 1 Fresh Red Chilli.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - or butter.
- 1 Onion.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 1/4 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 Lemon.

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Mexican Style Omelette With Avocado Dressing, heat and pan and add some oil.
1. Add some onion to the pan and saute them till they are transparent, now add the bell peppers and salt as per taste.
1. Take the pan from flame and keep the mixture aside.
1. Add the avocado dressing to the mixture and also add some shredded cheese on them.
1. Now beat eggs in a bowl,add some milk and whisk them again for a while.
1. Heat pan and add olive oil, as the oil heats up pour the egg mixture and wait until the egg starts to set.
1. Now sprinkle vegetable mixture over the egg and let it cook for 2 minutesRoll up Omelette and remove it from the flame.
1. Your Mexican Style Omelette With Avocado Dressing Recipe Is ready to be served.
1. You can serve them with a toast on the side and some fresh juice to complete your breakfast.
1. You can serve Mexican Style Omelette With Avocado Dressing Recipe with Fresh Orange juice and a slice of bread on the side.
