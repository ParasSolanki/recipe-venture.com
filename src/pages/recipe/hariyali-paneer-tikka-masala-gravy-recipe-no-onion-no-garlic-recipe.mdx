### Ingredients

- 50 grams Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 250 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cut into an inch lengthwise.
- 2 Tomatoes - pureed.
- 20 grams Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Butter - to cook.
- 1/4 cup Fresh cream.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Hariyali Paneer Tikka Masala Gravy ,coarsely grind the fennel seeds, coriander, mint, ginger and green chillies in a blender along with some water.
1. Heat butter in a pan, add spices - cinnamon, bay leaf, and cardamon pods and then the tomato puree.
1. Cook until the raw smell of the tomato goes away.
1. Add the ground green masala ingredients, and the garam masala and chaat masala, saute the masala for a few minutes on low heat.
1. Cover the pan and allow the hariyali masala gravy to cook through.
1. This will take about 4 to 5 minutes.
1. Once done, stir in the cream and paneer.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Simmer for 3 more minutes so the paneer absorbs the flavors of the masala.
1. Once done, turn of the heat and serve.
