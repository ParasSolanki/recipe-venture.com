### Ingredients

- 3/4 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 3 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 250 grams Colocasia root (Arbi).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 teaspoons Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoon Gram flour (besan).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Besan Arbi Roast Recipe, scrub the arbi to get rid of mud and dirt.
1. Pressure cook the arbi with little water and a pinch of salt for 1 whistle (do not over cook).
1. After the first whistle, turn off the heat.
1. Once the pressure releases, open the cooker.
1. Peel the skin off and cut them into half lengthwise and place it in a large mixing bowl.
1. In a wide plate mix besan, red chili powder, coriander powder, salt, turmeric powder, amchur powder, roasted cumin powder.
1. Add this spice powder like the turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chilli powder, amchur powder and cumin powder to the cut arbi and toss to coat well.
1. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a heavy bottomed pan, add the besan arbi in small batches and roast until golden brown in color on all sides.
1. Do this in 2 to 3 batches, add oil as the pan gets dry.
1. Drain the roasted arbi on kitchen towel and place it in a serving platter.
1. In another pan add 1 teaspoon of oil.
1. Add cumin seeds, carom seeds and finely chopped green chilies and saute for 10 seconds.
1. Add this to the Besan Arbi and toss gently.
1.  Sprinkle some coriander leaves and lemon juice on top of the besan arbi and serve.
1. Serve Besan Arbi Roast Recipe with Dal Palak Recipe and Steamed Rice Recipe - Pressure Cooker & Sauce Pan Methods for lunch.
