### Ingredients

- 1 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 tablespoons Caster Sugar - (reduce sugar quantity if your coconut is sweetened).
- 1/8 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 pinch Nutmeg powder.
- 3/4 cup Milk.
- 1/4 cup Fresh cream.
- 2 tablespoons Butter.
- Maple syrup - or chocolate syrup.
- 1/4 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 6 Whole Wheat Brown Bread - (best use French bread).
- 2 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Coconut French Toast with Chocolate Chips Recipe, in a bowl whisk eggs, milk and cream well until frothy.
1. Add vanilla extract, nutmeg powder, sugar and salt whisk again.
1. Cover and leave it for 15 to 20 minutes.
1. Heat a pan and add 1 tablespoon butter to the pan.
1. Dip the bread slice in the custard mix and remove immediately.
1. Gently press the excess liquid.
1. Coat it with shredded coconut on both sides.
1. Cook the slices of bread until golden brown on one side and then turn and cook the other side.
1. Repeat for rest of the slices.
1. Coconut French toast with choco chip and any fresh fruit of your choice immediately.
1. Serve the Coconut French Toast with Chocolate Chips Recipe for breakfast along with a cup of Hot chocolate goes well, you can also serve Watermelon Smoothie Recipe to enjoy it even better.
