### Ingredients

- 250 grams suran - cut into 1 inch squares.
-  1 tsp coriander powder.
-  1 onion - cut straight and thin.
-  1 tsp garlic - finely chopped.
-  1/2 tsp cumin powder.
-  1/2 tsp red chilli powder.
-  1/2 tsp turmeric powder.
-  1 tsp ginger - finely chopped.
-  1/2 tsp garam masala powder.
-  1/2 tsp black pepper powder.
-  1 sprig curry leaves.
-  2 tsp oil.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  salt - taste According.
-  2 green chillies - cut.

### Instructions

1. To make the Sukhi Suran Masala Sabzi Recipe, we will first remove the skin of the suran.
1. Now cut the suran and put it in water and keep it separately.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add curry leaves, cumin seeds and cook until the cumin seeds crackle.
1. Now add onion, ginger, garlic and cook till the onion becomes soft.
1. Now add suran and cook for 1 minute.
1. Add water, salt, cover the pan and cook for 10 minutes.
1. After 10 minutes, add red chili powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, black pepper powder, garam masala powder and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Once cooked, turn off the gas and serve.
1. Serve the Sukhi Suran Masala Sabzi Recipe along with Dal Palak, Gourd Raita and Phulka for dinner.
