### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 2 Onions - sliced and fried until brown.
- 1 cup Cauliflower (gobi) - small florets.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - cubed.
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Potatoes (Aloo) - cubed.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 7 Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- 1 1/2 cup Basmati rice.
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 8 Green beans (French Beans) - chopped 2 inch lengthwise.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Awadhi Style Taheri Recipe (Vegetable Rice Pulao Recipe), firstly heat a pressure cooker, keep it in medium heat, add oil and toast all the whole spices including bay leaf, black cardamom, whole black peppercorns, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon stick till the aroma comes out.
1. Next add in the onions, ginger, garlic and chilies and saute till it is golden brown and translucent.
1. Add in potatoes, carrot, green beans, cauliflower, green beans, onions and saut√© for 5 more minutes.
1. Once done add all the spice powders including red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder and salt.
1. You can also add curd and give it a stir.
1. At this point, you can transfer the soaked rice into the pressure cooker and mix it thoroughly, add  2-1/2 cups water and check for salt.
1. Close the pressure cooker and cook for 3 whistles, once done release the pressure naturally.
1. You can sprinkle some sliced onions that are browned on top.
1. Serve the Awadhi Style Tahari Recipe (Vegetable Rice Pulao Recipe) along with Cucumber Raita for a delicious lunch meal.
