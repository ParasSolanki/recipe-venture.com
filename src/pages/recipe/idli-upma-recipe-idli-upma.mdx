### Ingredients

-  1 tablespoon olive oil.
- 10 Idli - steal.
-  2 sprig green coriander - finely chopped.
-  3 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  1/2 teaspoon mustard.
-  6 curry leaves - finely chopped.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  10 small onions - finely chopped.
-  1 inch ginger - grated.
-  1 teaspoon white urad Lentils.

### Instructions

1. To make idli upma, first heat oil in a pan.
1. Add mustard seeds and let it cook for 10 seconds, after 10 seconds add Urad Dal and cook till it turns brown.
1. After browning, add chopped onions, ginger, green chillies and curry leaves.
1. Cook onion until it becomes soft.
1. After the onion softens, add turmeric powder, idli and salt.
1. Mix them well, after they are done, add green coriander and mix.
1. Turn off the flame and serve.
1. Serve Idli Upma with Fruit Bowl and Masala Tea for breakfast.
