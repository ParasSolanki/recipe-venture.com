### Ingredients

- 500 grams All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/2 tablespoon Dried oregano.
- 2 tablespoons Homemade Pizza And Pasta Sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/2 cup Onions - sliced.
- 5 grams Active dry yeast.
- Red Chilli flakes - as required.
- 1/2 cup Button mushrooms.
- 200 grams Mozzarella cheese - grated.
- 4 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 tablespoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1/2 cup Red Yellow and Green Bell Peppers (Capsicum).
- Dried oregano - as required.
- 1/4 cup Black olives.
- 1/4 cup Baby corn.
- 8 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Butter.
- 1-1/2 cup Lukewarm Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Garlic Crust Vegetable Pizza recipe, firstly mix yeast powder in luke warm water and keep it aside for 15 minutes.
1. In a big bowl, add all purpose flour, garlic, oregano and mix everything well.
1. Slowly slowly add the reqired water and kneada soft dough.
1. Cover it and keep it aside for 15 to 20 minutes.
1. After 20 minutes, add activated yeast, olive oil and kneed for 2 more minutes.
1. Let the dough ferment for 3 to 4 hours.
1. Cover with a lid and put it aisde in a warm place.
1. After 3 to 4 hours, you will see the dough is raised.
1. Take it out of the bowl, cut it into 2 equal size balls and let it rest for 10 minutes.
1. Sprinkle some flour on your working table or platform and knead this dough again for a few more minutes.
1. Again put this dough in a bowl for second fermentation, this time put it at the warm place for an hour only.
1. Pre-heat oven at 200 degree celsius.
1. Apply some oil in your palm and take out the pizza dough from the bowl.
1. Knead it for 2-3 minutes and make a nice round shape pizza base.
1. Transfer the pizza base to a buttered baking tray.
1. Apply Pizza sauce and sprinkle loads of grated mozzarella cheese on it.
1. Now places the sliced vegetables on the pizza which includes onions, bell peppers, mushrooms, baby corn and black olives.
1. Sprinkle some more cheese on the top.
1. Place in the preheated Oven at 200 degree Celsius for 10 minutes.
1. Increase the baking temperature to 220 degrees and bake further for 6-8 minutes until the cheese is melted and bread is soft and done.
1. Once it is done, take out of the oven and serve hot.
1. Serve Garlic Crust Vegetable Pizza along with Burnt Garlic Pasta With Mushroom for your weekend dinner.
1. You can also serve Caramel Apple Trifle to end the meal on a sweet note.
