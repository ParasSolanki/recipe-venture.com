### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1/2 tablespoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1 teaspoon Enos fruit salt.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger - grated.
- 10 Curry leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 3 to 4 Green Chillies - finely chopped (adjustable).
- 1/2 cup Sweet corn.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Veggie Rava Idli recipe, in a large bowl add the semolina/rava , yoghurt and water.
1. Whisk to combine all the ingredients.
1. To the same bowl, add the corn, peas, carrot, green chillies, ginger, coriander and salt to the mixture.
1. Mix well to combine and leave aside.
1. Heat a tadka pan, with oil on medium heat , add the mustard seeds, chana dal, curry leaves and green chilies.
1. Saute for a minute and turn off the flame.
1. Add the tadka to the suji/rava mixture, at this stage also add the enos fruit salt and mix well.
1. Preheat the idli steamer with enough water, Grease plates of the idli mould.
1. Ladle the Veggie Rava Idli batter into each of the cavities and place it in the steamer.
1. Steam for 12-15 minutes.
1. After 15 minutes, check if the idlis are done, by inserting a skewer, if it comes out clean , if its not done cover and steam for a few more minutes.
1. Serve Veggie Rava Idli recipe along with Tomato Garlic Chutney and Beetroot Sambar Recipe and Banana And Mango Smoothie Recipe  for your breakfast or pack it your kids lunch box.
