### Ingredients

- 200 grams Chicken breasts.
- 2 Boiled egg.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 2 cups Water.
- 2 teaspoons Garlic - fried.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Celery.
- 2 Stalks Lemongrass.
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 cup Bean Sprouts.
- 5 Kaffir lime leaves.
- 2 Onions - cut into cubes.
- 1/4 cup Spring Onion Greens.
- 8 cloves Garlic.
- 1 Stalks Lemongrass.
- 1/2 cup Rice noodles.
- Lemon wedges - to serve.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 cup Coconut milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Soto Ayam Recipe/Indonesian Chicken Noodle Meal bowl, keep the elements ready.
1.  Lemon wedgesBoil the Eggs and once boiled, peel and cut into 4 wedges.
1. Dip the bean sprouts in a bowl of hot water for about 2 minutes and drain the water.
1. Keep the bean sprouts aside.
1. To boil the rice noodles Heat a saucepan with water, and bring it to a boil, add the noodles and boil for about 2-3 minutes.
1. Turn off the flame, drain the water and transfer the noodles into a strainer, hold the strainer under running water to stop the cooking process.
1. The noodles should be cooked but not too soft at the same time.
1. To make the spice paste; in a mixer jar, combine the onions, garlic, ginger,  turmeric powder, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, black peppercorns, lemongrass and grind to a fine paste using some water.
1. To make the coconut milk broth; in a soup pot, add the freshly ground spice paste and saute for a good six to eight minutes.
1. Now add the coconut milk, water, fried garlic, salt to taste, lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, spring onions, celery leaves, chicken breast,  lemon juice and bring to a boil.
1. Continue to cook until the chicken breast is well boiled.
1. Turn off the flame, remove the chicken breast and shred it lengthwise, about 1 inch in size.
1. To assemble the Soto AyamIn a bowl, place the boiled noodles, pour the coconut milk broth, place some bean sprouts on the side, shredded chicken, boiled egg wedges, coriander leaves, and lemon wedges and serve hot.
1. Serve this Soto Ayam Recipe/Indonesian Chicken Noodle as a meal in itself.
1. Serve a dessert of Mango Mini Tart with Custard Recipe after this meal for a special weeknight dinner.
