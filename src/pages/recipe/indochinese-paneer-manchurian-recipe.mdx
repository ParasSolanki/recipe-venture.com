### Ingredients

- 300 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cut into cubes.
- 3 tablespoons Soy sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce.
- 4 cloves Garlic - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 2 tablespoons Homemade tomato puree.
- 1 tablespoon Rice Vinegar.
- 1/2 cup Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - (white top part).
- 3 tablespoons All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour.
- Black pepper powder - for seasoning.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - finely chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - for frying.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit lengthwise.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Indo-Chinese Paneer Manchurian recipe, first batter-fry the paneer cubes.
1. To do this, first mix together all ingredients mentioned under “For the batter”, and make a thick batter.
1. Place a wok on the heat oil to fry the paneer.
1. Once the oil is hot, dip each paneer cube into the batter, and drop it gently into the oil ,fry till light golden brown in colour.
1. Repeat with all the paneer cubes and drain them on absorbent paper so that extra oil is soaked up.
1. Then, proceed to make the sauce.
1. Place a large pan/pot on the heat.
1. Warm up a tablespoon of oil.
1. To it, add the spring onions, garlic, ginger and the green chilies and saute together for two to three minutes.
1. Add the fried cubes of paneer, reduce the heat to medium and cover for a minute.
1. Meanwhile, in a bowl whisk together soy sauce, sriracha, vinegar, ketchup, cornflour and water.
1. Pour sauce into the pan, and stir till  Paneer Manchurian comes together.
1. Reduce the heat and stir for another minute or two and you will see the sauce thickening up.
1. At this stage, add salt and pepper to taste, bearing in mind that the soya sauce is salty.
1. Turn off the heat, sprinkle Indo-Chinese Paneer Manchurian with chopped spring onion greens and serve.
1. Serve Indo-Chinese Paneer Manchurian recipe along with Thai Style Noodles With Spinach And Leeks or Chinese Egg Fried Rice for a weekend dinner.
