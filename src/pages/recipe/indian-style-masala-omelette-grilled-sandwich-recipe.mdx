### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 4 Whole Eggs.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 Tablespoon Mustard oil.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- Butter - melted.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Indian Style Masala Omelette Grilled Sandwich Recipe, whisk eggs and salt in a bowl till you start seeing bubbles.
1. Now add chopped green chillies, tomato, onion, black pepper powder and garam masala powder & whisk again.
1. Now heat up a skillet or flat surface pan and heat up mustard oil and tilt the pan from all the sides so the pan is evenly coated with oil.
1. Pour the whisked egg on the heated pan and make sure the egg is evenly spread across the pan.
1. Cook it for a couple of minutes and then flip it in order to cook it from the other side.
1. Make sure the omelette is cooked and its edges turn golden in colour.
1. Turn off the heat and transfer the omelette on a chopping board and slice it into 4 equal pieces.
1. Stuff 2 slices of omelette in between two pieces of bread.
1. Apply some melted butter on the outer surface of the sandwich and grill on a griddle or a grill pan from both sides until it turns crispy & golden.
1. Serve Indian Style Masala Omelette Grilled Sandwich Recipe along with Spiced Pumpkin Smoothie Recipe for breakfast.
1. You can also serve it with Adrak Chai or Filter Coffee.
