### Ingredients

- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 4 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Sambar Powder.
- 500 grams Beetroot - cut into long strips.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Karnataka Style Beetroot Palya Recipe, peel the outer skin of the beetroot, cut it into long thin strips and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan, add the mustard seeds, and let it crackle.
1. Add the asafoetida, red chilli, curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. Add the onions and saute until it turns translucent.
1. Once the onions are translucent, add in the beetroot, season with salt, mix well and cook until the beetroot is half cooked.
1. Add in the sambar masala and, toss well and let the beetroot cook well.
1. Once the beetroot is cooked, pound the grated coconut and garlic cloves using a pestle and mortar, and add it to the pan.
1. Mix well and cook for another 2 minutes and switch off the heat.
1. Serve the delicious Karnataka Style Beetroot Palya Recipe along with Steamed Rice, Keerai Sambar and Elai Vadam for a simple weekday lunch.
