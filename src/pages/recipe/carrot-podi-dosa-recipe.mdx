### Ingredients

- 3 Carrots (Gajjar) - grated.
- Idli Dosa Podi (Milagai Powder) - as needed.
- 2 cups Idli Dosa Batter.
- Sunflower Oil - as needed for cooking dosa.

### Instructions

1. To start making Carrot Podi Dosa, in a small bowl, add grated carrots along with required Idli podi (according to the spice you need), stir well and keep aside.
1. Heat up the dosa griddle on medium heat.
1. Pour a ladleful of dosa batter, spread the batter in a circular motion inside out to form a circular crepe.
1. Drizzle some oil on the sides and allow the dosa to cook a bit.
1. Sprinkle the prepared carrot podi mixture on top of dosa, cover the pan and allow the carrots to steam for a few minutes.
1. Once done, you will notice you have a crisp browned based with steamed carrots.
1. Fold the dosa.
1. Serve the Carrot Podi Dosa along with Chow Chow Sambar and Amla / Nellikai chutney for a filling breakfast.
