### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Date syrup - or any sweetener (optional).
- 1 Kiwi - sliced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Badam (Almond).
- 2 tablespoons Rice flour.
- 1 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 2 Strawberries - sliced.
- Blueberries.
- Honey - to drizzle on top (optional).
- 4 Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- Orange - cut into segments.
- Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt) - as needed.
- 2 Ripe Bananas.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Healthy Fruit Pizza For Breakfast recipe, firstly in a blender, add oats and almonds and blend it into a coarse flour.
1. Add this to a mixing bowl along with rice flour and salt.
1. Now puree the bananas along with date syrup if using.
1. Pour this mixture over the oats mixture and gently mix until combine by greasing your hands.
1. The mixture will be wet, let it rest for 1 to 2 minutes and the oats will absorb excess moisture and it will all come together well.
1. Line a large baking tray with with baking paper, grease your hands with oil and pat the dough on the paper with a thickness between 1/2 centimetre to 1 centimetre.
1. Bake in a 180 degree preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes.
1. Let it cool completely and peel the baking paper gently.
1. Now smear with greek yogurt and place the fruits - strawberries, kiwi, orange, blueberries, mint leaves on top, drizzle with honey if using and cut into slices.
1. Serve Healthy Fruit Pizza For Breakfast on its own for a healthy and delicious breakfast along with a glass of Beetroot Juice Recipe With Cucumber & Pineapple or Homemade Pomegranate Juice with Ginger Recipe to cut down the heaviness while your eating.
