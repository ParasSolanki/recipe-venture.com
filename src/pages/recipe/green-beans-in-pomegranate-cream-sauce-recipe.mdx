### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 5 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1/3 cup Fresh cream.
- 2 tablespoons Coconut milk.
- 3 sprig Spring Onion Greens.
- 250 gram Green beans (French Beans).
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Pomegranate Sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 1/3 cup Fresh Pomegranate Fruit Kernels.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Green Beans In Pomegranate Cream Sauce Recipe, clean, wash and dry green beans.
1. Cut each bean into half lengthwise.
1. Prepare a steamer for steaming and steam the beans in a steamer until it's just about cooked.
1. Remove from the steamer and keep aside.
1. In a pan heat oil and saute chopped garlic until lightly brown in color.
1. Add cream, coconut milk, salt, honey, pomegranate seeds and pepper.
1. Bring the mixture to a brisk boil to combine the flavors and turn off the heat.
1. Fold in the beans in pomegranate cream sauce and mix well so that beans get coated well with the sauce.
1. Garnish with more pomegranate seeds and chopped spring onion greens.
1. Serve warm or cold.
1. Serve the Green Beans in Pomegranate Cream Sauce along with Light And Creamy Broccoli Soup and Garlic Bread with Herb Butter for a weeknight dinner.
