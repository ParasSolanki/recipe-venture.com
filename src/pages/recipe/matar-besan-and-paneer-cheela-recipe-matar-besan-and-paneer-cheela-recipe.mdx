### Ingredients

-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  1/2 tsp turmeric powder.
- 1 cup green peas - boil.
-  1 tsp ginger - chopped.
-  1/2 teaspoon salt.
-  1 cup gram flour.
-  2 oil - as per use.
-  1/2 tsp Cumin powder.
-  1/2 cup cheese - tighten.

### Instructions

1. To make the pea gram flour and paneer cheela recipe, firstly mix green peas, green chilies, ginger, some water in a mixer grinder and make a paste.
1. Now pour this paste in a bowl.
1. Add gram flour, cheese, turmeric powder, cumin powder, salt, some water and mix it well.
1. Now preheat a pan.
1. Put a little mixture in the middle and spread it in a round shape.
1. Add some oil and cook till it turns golden brown from both the sides.
1. Remove and serve in a plate after cooking.
1. Serve the Pea Besan and Paneer Cheela recipe with coriander mint chutney and masala tea for breakfast.
