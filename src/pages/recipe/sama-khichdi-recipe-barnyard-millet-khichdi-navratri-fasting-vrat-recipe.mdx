### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Barnyard Millet.
- 2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- Salt - to taste (rock salt).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - boiled and cubed.
- 1 Bay leaves (tej patta) - torn.

### Instructions

1. We begin making Sama Khichdi Recipe (Vrat Recipe) by dry roasting the barnyard millets in a pan till it turns slight brown.
1. Turn off the flame and allow it to cool.
1. Then in a pressure cooker add 1 teaspoon cooking oil,Once the oil is hot, add green chillies, bay leaves, cinnamon and crushed pepper corns and give it a good stirNext add the chopped potatoes, carrots, tomatoes and saute until for a few minutes until they are slightly soft.
1. Add 2 cups of water for 1 cup of sama rice to get a creamy consistency.
1. Let the water boil and then add the millet and cover it for 5 minutes and let it cook for 2 whistles.
1. Turn of the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with coriander leaves and squeeze in the lemon and give it a mix.
1. Serve your creamy Sama Khichdi with Cucumber Green Chilli Raita with Makhana Moongphali Ki Kadhi to enjoy your meal.
