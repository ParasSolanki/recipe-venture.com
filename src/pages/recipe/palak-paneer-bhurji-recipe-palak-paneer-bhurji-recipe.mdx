### Ingredients

-  oil - as per use.
-  2 cloves garlic - finely chopped.
-  2 tsp mango powder.
-  1 onion - finely chopped.
-  50 grams spinach - finely chopped.
-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  2 tsp red chilli powder.
-  1/2 small Teaspoon cumin.
-  1 tbsp butter.
-  salt - as per taste.
- 200 grams cheese - pinch.
-  2 tsp garam masala powder.
-  1 tomato - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make the Palak Paneer Bhurji recipe, firstly make paneer.
1. You can also buy cheese from outside.
1. Steal the cheese and keep it aside.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add cumin seeds and let it crackle.
1. After the cumin seeds crackle, add ginger, garlic, green chillies, onions and cook them till the onions turn light brown.
1. Now add tomatoes and cook till they become soft.
1. Add the spinach and cook until the spinach is cooked.
1. Now add red chili powder, garam masala powder, mango powder, paneer and mix well.
1. Add the butter and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. After cooking, turn off the gas and serve hot.
1. Serve the spinach paneer bhurji with dal makhni, boondi raita and phulka for dinner.
