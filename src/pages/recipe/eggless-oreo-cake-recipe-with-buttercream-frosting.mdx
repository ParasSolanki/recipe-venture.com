### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Butter.
- 1 tablespoon Milk.
- 2 cups Caster Sugar.
- 24 Oreo biscuits - (2 large packs).
- 7 Oreo biscuits - cut into halves.
- 2 cups Milk.
- 3/4 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- Enos fruit salt - a sachet or 1/4 teaspoon baking powder.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Badam (Almond) - thinly sliced.
- Icing Sugar - for dusting (optional).
- Sugar - (only if required) (I have not added in this recipe).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Oreo Cake Recipe With Butter Cream Frosting, break the oreo cookies into chunks.
1. Blend the oreo cookies to a fine powder and then add the required milk to form a thick batter of cake-batter consistency not too runny, but thick enough to pour into a greased container.
1. Once you are about to pour into the cake baking tray- add the eno and mix it well.
1. Pour the batter immediately into a greased pan, you can use parchment/ butter paper to avoid the cake from sticking at the bottom.
1. Microwave for 4-5 minutes on high.
1. If you are using the OTG/Convection Oven â€“ 180 degrees Celsius for 15 -20 minutes depending on your oven.
1. Buttercream FrostingTo prepare buttercream frosting, mix butter and sugar with a hand mixer for about 2-3 minutes.
1. Add milk, vanilla and continue blending for 2-3 minutes.
1. Adjust milk to achieve a spreading consistency.
1. Once the cake is cooled on a wire rack, apply a generous coat of buttercream frosting.
1. Decorate with almonds and halved oreo biscuits.
1. Serve Oreo Cake Recipe With Butter Cream Frosting as a light dessert after a meal with Ratatouille with Grilled Cottage Cheese Steak or Potato Wedges.
