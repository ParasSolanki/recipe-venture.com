### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cups Cooked rice.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 1/2 cup Spring Onion Greens.
- 2 cups Button mushrooms - cut into quarters.
- 4 cloves Garlic - cut lengthwise for garnish.
- 1 tablespoon Schezwan sauce.
- 2 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Burnt Garlic Mushroom Fried Rice Recipe, heat oil in a skillet on medium flame, add the garlic and saute until it turns into a deep brown colour.
1. This will take 2-3 minutes.
1. To this skillet add ginger and onions and continue to cook for another 2 minutes.
1. At this stage add the mushrooms, green chilli, carrots, vinegar and soy sauce and mix well.
1. Cook until the water from the mushrooms dry out.
1. Once the mushrooms and carrots are cooked, add in the salt, pepper and Schezwan sauce until well combined.
1. Finally add in the rice and spring onion greens and gently toss the Burnt Garlic Mushroom Fried Rice.
1. Turn off the flame.
1. Transfer the Burnt Garlic Mushroom Fried Rice to a serving platter and serve hot.
1. To garnish, heat a tadka pan with a few drops of oil on medium flame, and add the garlic that has been cut lengthwise and fry until it attains aa golden brown colour.
1. Drain on an absorbent paper and garnish the Mushroom Fried Rice with these fried garlic chips.
1. Serve Burnt Garlic Mushroom Fried Rice Recipe along with Sweet Corn Spring Onion Momo Recipe as an appetizer and serve the rice along with Sichuan Style Vegetarian Mapo Doufu followed by a dessert of Peach Sorbet Recipe with Pineapple Puree.
