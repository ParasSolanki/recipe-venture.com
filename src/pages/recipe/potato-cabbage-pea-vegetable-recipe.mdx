### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1 cup Green peas (Matar).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves).
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - for garnishing.
- 2 Potato (Aloo).
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1/2 kg Cauliflower (gobi) - floret.
- 1 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 tablespoon Onion - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Aloo Gobhi Matar Ki Sabzi Recipe, put the florets in a large bowl and cover with hot water.
1. Add a teaspoon of salt and mix well.
1. Keep aside for 10 minutes.
1. Peel wash and cut the potato into small pieces and put potatoes in cold water.
1. Heat the oil in a kadai or a non-stick pan.
1. Add fenugreek seeds, when they splutter, saut√© ginger and onion.
1. Now add the cauliflower florets, peas, potatoes and fry for few minutes stirring occasionally.
1. Cover and cook for 2-3 minutes.
1. Now add turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder and salt stir and mix well all the spices and veggies together.
1. Adjust salt.
1. Cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low flame for 5 to 6 minutes.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. Garnish with coriander, serve Aloo Gobhi Matar Ki Sabzi Recipe hot with chapati or poori.
