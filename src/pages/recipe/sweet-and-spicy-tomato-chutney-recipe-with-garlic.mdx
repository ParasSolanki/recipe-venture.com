### Ingredients

- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1/4 cup Jaggery.
- Salt - to taste.
- Lemon juice - from one lemon.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Cashew nuts - roughly chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 6 Tomatoes - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sweet and Spicy Tomato Chutney Recipe With Garlic, first prep the ingredients according to instructions.
1. In a heavy bottom pan add all the ingredients except sugar, lemon and red chilli powder.
1. Cook it at the medium flame by stirring it continuously until the tomatoes have softened.
1. When the tomatoes soften, add sugar and red chilli powder and continue cooking at medium flame until the tomato chutney thickens.
1. When it thickens turn off the heat, add the lemon and stir.
1. Check the salt, sweetness and spice and adjust to suit your taste.
1. The Sweet and Spicy Tomato Chutney With Garlic is now ready to be served.
