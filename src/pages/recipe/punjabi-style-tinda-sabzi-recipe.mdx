### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder - (or as required).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 4 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 8 Tinda (Apple Gourd) - peeled.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoons Tomato - paste.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Punjabi Style Tinda Sabzi, first heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add the cumin seeds and once they splutter add the garlic, onion and saute till the onion turns translucent.
1. Once done add the chopped tomato, tinde, tomato paste, asafoetida, turmeric powder, salt, red chilli powder and stir.
1. Cook covered till the vegetable becomes soft, stirring once or twice.
1. Add little water if required.
1. When the vegetables are nearly cooked sprinkle cumin powder, coriander powder and garam masala on the sabzi and stir.
1. Cook for about 2 minutes and then turn off the heat.
1. Transfer Punjabi Style Tinda Sabzi and serve hot garnished with coriander leaves.
1. Serve Punjabi Style Tinda Sabzi along with Dal Tadka, Palak Raita and Phulka for a weekday lunch.
