### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1/2 teaspoon Fennel Powder.
- 1 cup Vegetable stock.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried Thyme Leaves.
- 2 cups Lukewarm Water.
- 4-5 Basil leaves - to garnish.
- 2 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 3 cloves Garlic - crushed.
- 1 cup Rice - semi-cooked.
- 1 teaspoon Orange Zest (Rind).
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Provençal Tomato Rice Soup Recipe, heat oil in a Saucepan and saute onions, carrot, garlic, orange zest, thyme and add fennel powder.
1. Once the vegetables start to soften, add tomato paste and water.
1. Mix well till everything is incorporated well.
1. Add in the chopped tomatoes now along with vegetable stock and season with salt, pepper and sugar.
1. As it starts boiling add semi cooked white rice.
1. Close the lid and simmer for around 12-15 minutes until the rice is tender.
1. Pour the soup into bowls, add a swirl of fresh cream and garnish with fresh basil and crushed dry thyme and serve Provençal Tomato Rice Soup Recipe with salad of your choice.
