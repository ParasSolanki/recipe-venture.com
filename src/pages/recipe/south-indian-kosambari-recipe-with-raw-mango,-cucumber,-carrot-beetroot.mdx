### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/4 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1/4 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - soaked in water for 3 hours.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Cucumber - chopped.
- 1/2 tablespoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 1/4 cup Beetroot - grated.
- 1/4 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - small cubes.
- 1/2 cup Mango (Raw) - grated.
- 10-15 Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making South Indian Kosambari Recipe wash and soak the chana dal in water for a minimum of 2-3 hours.
1. Once it is soaked, drain the water completely and grind the chana dal in a mixer till you get a coarse paste.
1. The next step is to prepare the salad.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the coarsely ground chana dal, carrot, beetroot, cucumber, raw mango, chilies and paneer cubes and keep it aside.
1. In a tadka pan, heat oil on the medium heat.
1. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves and asafoetida and allow them to crackle.
1. Once the seeds have crackled add the urad dal and allow it to turn into a nice golden colour.
1. Once done turn off the heat and add it to the Kosambari mixture.
1. Sprinkle some salt and pepper powder and mix  well to combine.
1. Check the salt and adjust the seasoning to suit your taste.
1. Garnish the Kosambari Salad with some grated coconut and serve.
1. Serve South Indian Kosambari Recipe along with Moong Dal Rasam/Pesara Pappu Charu Recipe, Steamed Rice and Capsicum Masala Poriyal Recipe to complete your South Indian meal.
