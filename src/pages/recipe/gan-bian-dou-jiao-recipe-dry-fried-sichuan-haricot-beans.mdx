### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli sauce.
- 3 Spring Onion Greens - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 2 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 400 grams Green beans (French Beans) - trimmed and halved.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Stir Fried Sichuan String Bean Recipe (Gan Bian Dou Jiao Recipe), first prep all the ingredients.
1. Steam the green beans along with salt on high for about 5 minutes until just about done.
1. The beans have to be still crunchy, drain it under cold water in order to stop cooking.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan over medium heat, add the ginger, garlic, and spring onions and saute until the aromas come through.
1. Add honey, salt, soy sauce, Sichuan sauce and the steamed green beans and toss on high heat until the beans are well coated with the spices and flavors.
1. Check the salt and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and serve.
1. Serve the Stir Fried Sichuan String Bean Recipe (Gan Bian Dou Jiao Recipe), along with Chinese Sichuan fried rice or Chinese Vegetable Fried Rice for a delicious weekend dinner.
