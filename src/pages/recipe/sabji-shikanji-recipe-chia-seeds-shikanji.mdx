### Ingredients

- 4 teaspoons Sabza seeds.
-  4 tablespoons sugar - PC.
-  1 cup ice.
-  pepper powder - a pinch .
-  3 Lemon - Ras nikal.
-  cumin powder - a pinch.
-  black salt or rock salt - according to taste.
-  Peppermint - for garnish.
-  3 cups cold water.

### Instructions

1. To make Sabja Shikanji, firstly soak the Sabja Seed in 2 cups of water for 30 minutes.
1. Add 3 cups of water, sugar, lemon juice, black salt, cumin powder, black pepper powder, and mix until sugar dissolves.
1. Add sabja bija and mint leaves and mix.
1. Serve immediately after putting it in glass.
1. Serve Sabja Shikanji during summer.
1. You can also add honey as per taste instead of sugar.
