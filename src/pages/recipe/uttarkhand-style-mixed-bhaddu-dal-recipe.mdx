### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/4 cup White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/4 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 2 teaspoon Fresh cream.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/2 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 3 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 cup Rajma (Large Kidney Beans).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Uttarkhand Style Mixed Bhaddu Dal recipe, firstly soak the urad dal and rajma seperately overnight or for minimum 5 hours.
1. Heat a pressure cooker with water, cinnamon stick, cardamom and lentils, cover it and let it cook till the cooker releases 4 whistles.
1. Take a medium wok, heat 2 teaspoons of oil, add bay leaf, cloves, whole black pepper, turmeric powder and red chilli powder.
1. Let it cook for about a minute.
1. Pour tomato puree and let it cook for another 3 minutes.
1. After 3 minutes, add in the ginger garlic paste.
1. Let it cook till the raw smell vanishes.
1. Next, add the boiled dal to wok and simmer for 8 minutes then add the cream.
1. Meanwhile heat a spoonful of oil in a tadka pan, add coriander seeds, cumin seeds and let it cook for about 15 seconds.
1. Pour it over the dal, mix and serve hot.
1. Serve Uttarkhand Style Mixed Bhaddu Dal along with Bharwa Bhindi and Tawa Paratha for your weekly meal plan.
