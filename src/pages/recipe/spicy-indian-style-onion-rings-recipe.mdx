### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 3 Onions - cut into thick rounds.
- 1/4 cup Gram flour (besan).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 1/2 inch Ginger - peeled and grated.
- 10 Curry leaves.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 2 tablespoons Corn flour.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Spicy Indian Style Onion Rings Recipe, In the mixer grind together the ginger, garlic, red chillies, green chilli and curry leaves with a tablespoon of water into a coarse paste.
1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside until required.
1. Heat oil for deep frying the onion rings over medium heat while you prepare the batter to coat the onion rings.
1. In a mixing bowl, sift together the flour, besan, corn flour, soda along with salt and ginger paste.
1. Whisk gently to mix well.
1. Gradually add little water at a time to make thick batter.
1. The batter should be thick dropping consistency so it coats the onion rings.
1. Add a few thick onion rings into the batter and using a fork, allow them to get well-coated.
1. Using the fork transfer them into the hot oil, fry till golden and crisp.
1. Transfer to a plate lined with oil absorbent paper.
1. Similarly fry the remaining onion rings.
1. Serve Spicy Indian Style Onion Rings Recipe along with Green Chutney or a yogurt dip for a sunday evening teatime snack.
