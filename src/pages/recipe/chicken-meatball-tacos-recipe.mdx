### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 1/2 cup Refried beans.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 cup Cheddar cheese - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 8 to 10 Hard taco shells.
- 250 grams Chicken - minced.
- 1 cup Iceberg lettuce - shredded.
- 2 sprig Spring Onion Greens - as required.
- 1/2 cup Sour cream.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1/2 cup Guacamole.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken Meatball Tacos recipe, firstly we will make the meatballs.
1. To make the meatballs in a mixing bowl, combine the minced chicken along with the green chilli, salt, pepper powder, coriander leaves and  ginger garlic paste.
1. Mix well and bring the mixture together.
1. Now roll them between your palms to make small lemon sized meatballs.
1. Heat oil for deep frying in a deep fry pan on medium heat.
1. Deep fry the chicken balls on low to medium flame until cooked well.
1. Remove from oil and drain on a kitchen towel.

1. Once the cheese is melted, remove the tacos from the oven, spread some shredded lettuce leaves, drizzle some sour cream and some guacamole.
1. Garnish with some spring onions, fresh coriander leaves and serve immediately.
