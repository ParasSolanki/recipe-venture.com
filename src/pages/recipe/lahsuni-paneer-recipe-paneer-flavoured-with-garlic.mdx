### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- Salt - as required.
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - for garnish.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 1 Onion.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 10 cloves Garlic.
- 200 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lahsuni Paneer Recipe, thaw the paneer to room temperature.
1. Cut into small cubes and keep aside.
1. Follow Homemade Paneer Recipe to make paneer at home.
1. To a mixer, add garlic pods, sugar, vinegar, salt and dry red chilli (removing seeds inside) and grind everything into smooth paste in a mixer grinder adding very little water.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, heat some oil (or butter).
1. Add cumin seeds and once it splutters, add chopped onion and saute until translucent.
1. Now add the grounded garlic chilli paste to it and saute well until raw smell goes.
1. Once everything is combined and the oil starts separating, add the paneer cubes.
1. Saute for 2-3 minutes in medium flame until paneer gets coated with masala.
1. Garnish Lahsuni Paneer with coriander leaves and remove from flame.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve freshly prepared Lahsuni Paneer for a weekend meal along with Parathas, Punjabi Dal Tadka and Red Cabbage Carrot, Sprout and Onion Salad for a complete meal.
