### Ingredients

- 1/2 Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cups Mixed vegetables - (beans.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 tablespoons Jaggery - adjustable.
- 5 Kokum (Malabar Tamarind) - soak in hot water and extract juice.
- 8 Sichuan peppercorns.
- Curry leaves - few.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Karwar Style Khatkhate Recipe, wash and pressure cook toor dal with a pinch of turmeric for 2-3 whistles.
1. Once cooked, mash it well and keep aside.
1. Dice the vegetables and cook them with enough water in a pressure cooker for one whistle.
1. Turn off the flame and immediately release the pressure.
1. They should just be cooked.
1. make sure you don't overcook them.
1. Meanwhile, grind coconut, red chillies, coriander powder  and turmeric powder with little water to a smooth paste.
1. Add sichuan pepper and grind the paste again for few more seconds.
1. When the vegetables are almost done, add ground coconut paste and mashed toor dal.
1. Add salt, kokam and jaggery.
1. Mix everything well and bring to boil .
1. Finally prepare the tempering for the curry.
1. Heat oil in a kadhai.
1. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds and curry leaves.
1. Cook for about a minute and then pour it on Khatkhate.
1. Serve Karwar Style Khatkhate Recipe (Mixed Vegetable Curry With Toor Dal & Coconut) along with Karwar Style Lasani Kadhi and Steamed Rice for a weekday meal.
