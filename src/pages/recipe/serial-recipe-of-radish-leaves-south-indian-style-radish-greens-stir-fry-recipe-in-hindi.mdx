### Ingredients

-  1/4 cup tur dal - hot water for 10 minutes Soak in.
-  1/8 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  asafetida - a pinch.
-  2 teaspoons coconut - tighten.
-  1 dry red chilli.
-  1 cup radish leaves - finely chopped.
-  1 sprig curry leaves.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  1/2 inch ginger - tighten.
-  1 teaspoon sesame oil.
- 12 small onions - chopped.
-  1 tomato - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make the porridge of radish leaves, first prepare the sari material.
1. Grind the ingredients given for grinding in a mixer grinder.
1. Now heat the oil in a pan and add the ingredients for tempering.
1. After tempering, add small onions, tomatoes, turmeric powder, salt and cook till the tamar becomes soft.
1. Now add the ground spices and sprinkle some water.
1. Cook on low heat for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Now add radish leaves, mix and cook for next 5 minutes.
1. Serve after 5 minutes.
1. Serve the poriyal of the radish leaves with beetroot sambar, rice and papad for dinner.
