### Ingredients

- 250 grams Baby Potatoes.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped finely.
- 2 teaspoons Gram flour (besan) - optional                                                                       Salt to taste.
- 5 Green Chilli - (bajji variety (light green ones).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 teaspoons Rice flour - optional.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bhaji Miligai And Baby Potato Fry Recipe, pressure cook baby potatoes for 1 or 2 whistles, until cooked al dente.
1. Prep up with all the ingredients meanwhile and cut miligai to big chunks.
1. Once the pressure of the cooker releases, peel off all the potatoes.
1. In a shallow frying pan, heat oil and fry chopped garlic.
1. Add bhajji milagai and let it fry for just about a minute.
1. Add small potatoes and saute after adding curry leaves, salt, and turmeric evenly.
1. You may optionally add besan and rice flour at this stage, if you want to make Bhaji Miligai And Baby Potato Fry Recipe as a crunchy appetizer.
1. Mix everything well and saute lightly until crispy and switch off.
1. Serve Bhaji Miligai And Baby Potato Fry Recipe as a side dish along with Tomato Onion Sambar, Steamed Rice and Elai Vadam for an everyday meal.
