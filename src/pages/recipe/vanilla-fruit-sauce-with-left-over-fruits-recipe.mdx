### Ingredients

- 3 Ripe Bananas.
- 3 Apples.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 1/4 cup Honey dew melon.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1/4 cup Blueberries.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vanilla Fruit Sauce With Left Over Fruits recipe, wash and chop the fruits roughly after peeling the skin.
1. Blend the fruits with enough water in a blender.
1. Transfer the fruit pulp to a pot and add the remaining water, vanilla essence and honey.
1. Let this cook and become thick like any other sauce consistency.
1. Let it cool and store it in a bottle and refrigerate.
1. It can stay upto a week in fridge.
1. Serve Vanilla Fruit Sauce With Left Over Fruits along with Healthy Oats Waffles Recipe and Chocolate Banana Smoothie Recipe for a delicious Sunday breakfast.
