### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Gingelly oil - + 1 teaspoon.
- 2 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 5 cloves Garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 teaspoon Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 20 Pearl onions (Sambar Onions).
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- Tamarind - lemon sized soaked in 3/4 cup water.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Milagu Kozhambu recipe, dry roast all ingredients mentioned under the list ‘for milagu masala powder’.
1. Let the spices cool and grind into powder.
1. Heat gingerly oil in a pan.
1. Add crushed garlic and shallots and saute till the onions turn translucent.
1. Add fenugreek seeds.
1. Extract the juice of tamarind and pour into the pan.
1. Boil the tamarind juice until oil separates from the sides of the pan.
1. Add 4 heaped tablespoon of milagu masala powder along with salt to taste, stir well.
1. Let it cook for 5 minutes on low flame.
1. Meanwhile heat 1 teaspoon oil in another pan.
1. Add mustard seeds and let it splutter.
1. Add curry leaves and a pinch of asafoetida.
1. Add the tempering into the curry.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve Milagu Kozhambu along with Carrot and Beans Thoran and Steamed Rice for a weekday meal.
