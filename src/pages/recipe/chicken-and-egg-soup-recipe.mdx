### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Button mushrooms - sliced.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - sliced.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1 Whole Egg.
- 1 Chicken breasts - cut juliennes.
- 1/2 cup Broccoli - chopped small.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 1/2 cup Chicken stock.
- 1 Leek - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon White vinegar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken and Noodle Soup Recipe heat oil in a saucepan add chicken cut into juliennes, leek, mushrooms, carrot, and broccoli.
1. Cook for 3 minutes in low heat.
1. Now add chicken stock, salt and black pepper powder.
1. Cook over low heat for 10 minutes.
1. Then add white vinegar and allow to cook for some more time.
1. Now break an egg in a small bowl and beat it till the yolk combines with white, add the egg into the soup when it is boiling and cook till egg comes on top.
1. Remove gently in a bowl and serve hot.
