### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cup Milk.
- 1 cup Papaya - frozen.
- 6 Basil leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Honey - optional.
- 2 tablespoon Badam (Almond) - chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Chia Seeds - soaked.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Papaya Basil Smoothie recipe with chia seeds, add all the ingredients except chia seeds and chopped almond into a blender and blend till smooth.
1. Once the mixture is smooth, add chia seeds and chopped almond.
1. Mix it well.
1. After mixing well, pour them in glass and serve chilled.
1. Serve Papaya Basil Smoothie With Chia Seeds on its own or along with Scrambled Egg Cucumber sandwich for a healthy and filling breakfast.
