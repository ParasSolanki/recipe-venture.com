### Ingredients

- 2 cups Basmati rice.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Butter.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1/2 teaspoon Parsley leaves - finely chopped.
- 3-4 Basil leaves - roughly torn.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Buttered Herbed rice,  heat butter on low heat in a pan.
1. Add chopped garlic and fry until they turn golden in colour.
1. Once done add parsley, dried oregano, chilli flakes, garlic chives, basil and toss them well for about a minute.
1. After a minute, add salt and pepper to the herbs.
1. Add the cooked rice to the pan and mix well to combine.
1. Let them cook for 2-3 minutes.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and transfer it to a serving bowl.
1. Serve the Buttered Herbed rice with Smoked Tofu and Grilled Vegetable salad or you can have it alone also.
