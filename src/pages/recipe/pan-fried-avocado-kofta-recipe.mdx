### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Tamarind Water - or lime juice.
- 2 teaspoons Garam masala powder.
- 1 tablespoon Rice flour - If required.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 Tablespoons Sunflower Oil - for shallow frying.
- Sooji (Semolina/ Rava) - or breadcrumbs for dusting.
- 1 Avocado - ripe.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 3 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pan Fried Avocado Kofta recipe, peel the avocado and mesh it well with fork.
1. Heat a skillet and add a teaspoon of oil.
1. Fry grated carrots on medium high heat for about a minute and remove from the heat.
1. Take a bowl and add in the cooked carrot, chopped onions, grated ginger garlic, green chilies and coriander leaves.
1. Add in all the spices, sprinkle some salt, add in tamarind water and lemon juice.
1. Mix everything well.
1. If the mixture is too soggy and cannot make Koftas, add a tablespoon of rice flour and mix so that the moisture gets absorbed.
1. Take slightly bigger then ping pong size ball of the mixture on your palm and start shaping them into oval.
1. Keep them aside on a plate.
1. Take some semolina or breadcrumbs in a plate and dust all the Koftas well, coating with semolina on all the sides.
1. Heat a skillet and add oil.
1. Place all the prepared kebabs on top keeping some space in between each.
1. Let them fry on medium heat for about 5 minutes.
1. Turn them over and fry on the other sides.
1. Once they look all browned and crisp, Remove on a plate and serve these crisp kofta hot as an appetizer.
1. Serve Pan Fried Avocado Kofta along with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney and Masala Chai for your evening break.
