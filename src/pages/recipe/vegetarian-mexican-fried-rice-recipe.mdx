### Ingredients

- 4 cloves Garlic - minced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cup Cooked rice.
- 1 cup Rajma (Large Kidney Beans) - soaked and boiled.
- 1 cup Sweet corn - boiled.
- 1 tablespoon Cayenne Pepper - or red chilli powder (adjust according to your taste).
- 1/2 cup Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 3 tablespoons Butter.
- 1/2 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 1/2 cup Tomato - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Tomato Ketchup - or tomato puree.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vegetarian Mexican Fried Rice recipe, heat a heavy bottomed pan on high flame and add butter.
1. Add garlic and onion and cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
1. Then add green pepper and tomatoes and let it cook for another 3 minutes.
1. Next add corns and beans and give a nice stir.
1. Add seasoning at this stage.
1. Add salt, red chilli powder, tomato ketchup and vinegar.
1. Cook for another 3 minutes.
1. Finally add rice and mix it well.
1. Let it cook for just 2 minutes.
1. Mexican fried rice is ready to serve.
1. Serve Vegetarian Mexican Fried Rice with Bean Spinach And Corn Quesadilla and a glass of wine for a perfect weekend Mexican lunch or dinner.
