### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/4 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 2 tablespoons Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 1/2 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Jaggery - powdered.
- 1 Kokum (Malabar Tamarind).
- 1 cup Green Moong Sprouts - steamed.
- 3/4 cup Whole Wheat Flour.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dal Dhokli Recipe With Sprouted Moong Dal, we will first make the dal.
1. To Make The DhokliIn a large mixing bowl, combine the wheat flour, gram flour, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, salt , and coriander leaves.
1. Add water little by little and make a firm and smooth dough.
1. Dust the dough in flour and roll out the dough into a large circle and cut the dough into desired shape (either into strips or into a flower using a cookie cutter).
1. Keep the dhokli aside.
1. To Make Dal DhokliInto a pressure cooker, add the sprouts, turmeric powder, salt, cinnamon stick, kokum , jaggery, salt and 2 cups of water.
1. Close the pressure cooker and pressure cook for two whistles.
1. Turn off the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Once we open the pressure cooker, add in the lemon juice and coriander leaves, check the salt and spices.
1. Add 1 cup of water to the dal and then turn the heat back on to high heat until it come to brisk boil.
1. Once the dal comes to a boil, add the dhokli into the dal and allow it to simmer and get cooked in the dal.
1. This will not take more than 5 minutes to cook the dhokli.
1. Adjust the consistency of the Dal Dhokli, by adding more water if required.
1. In the meantime, in a tadka pan heat a tablespoon of ghee.
1. Add the mustard seeds, cumin seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Once it crackles, add the green chilies, curry leaves and add to the add.
1. Finally squeeze in the lemon and stir in the coriander leaves and serve.
1. Serve the Dal Dhokli With Sprouted Moong Dal topped with melted ghee and Kachumber Salad, papad, for a simple sunday lunch.
