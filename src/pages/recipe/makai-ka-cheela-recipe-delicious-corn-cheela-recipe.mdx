### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a small bunch finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 2 cups Sweet corn.
- Sunflower Oil - for making chillas.
- 3 tablespoons Gram flour (besan).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Makai Ka Cheela Recipe, peel the corn sheath, separate the corn kernels from the cob and keep aside.
1. Now, make a coarse mixture of the sweet corn/ makai using a mixer and transfer it in a bowl.
1. Into the bowl, add the besan, chopped onion, green chilli, grated ginger, season with salt.
1. Mix everything adding little water at a time - the consistency of the Makai ka cheela batter should be a little thick.
1. Now heat a skillet on medium flame, brush it with a little oil, pour a ladle full of the prepared Makai Ka Cheela mixture, spread it as your would spread a dosa and let it cook for a minute.
1. Once the bottom of the cheela till it turns a little brown, flip over and cook the other side for another minute.
1. Serve the cooked Makai ka cheela in a plate and proceed to prepare cheelas with the remaining batter.
1. Serve Makai Ka Cheela along with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney & Filter Coffee for breakfast or as an after school snacks for kids.
