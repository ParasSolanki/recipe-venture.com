### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- Salt - to taste.
- 20 Curry leaves.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Jhure Jhure Aloo Bhaja recipe, wash and peel potatoes.
1. Use a Mandolin Slicer to grate them into thin sticks.
1. Alternatively you can cut the potato in long thin stripes.
1. Now soak grated potatoes in water for 10 minutes.
1. Now drain the water completely.
1. Spread the potato strips on a clean kitchen towel to absorb water on their surface.
1. Once the moisture is completely absorbed, transfer those strips to a plate.
1. Heat a kadai on a medium flame and add oil for deep frying.
1. Once the oil is hot, put few strips of potatoes in oil and fry till they turn golden brown.
1. Take them out and place them on an absorbent paper.
1. Similarly fry the remaining potato strips.
1. Deep fry the curry leaves in the same oil and keep aside.
1. Sprinkle some salt and chilly powder and mix well.
1. Adjust the spice level according to your taste.
1. Sprinkle some curry leaves and mix well.
1. You can also add dried herbs kasoori methi instead of curry leaves.
1. You can also add some crushed black pepper for some additional zing.
1. Serve the Jhure Jhure Aloo Bhajas hot as a tea time snack or as a side dish with Bengali style Cholar Dal  and Jeera Rice or with Bhog Khichuri for a comforting bengali meal.
