### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli sauce - (optional).
- 1/2 tablespoon Rice Wine Vinegar.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 4 tablespoon Vegetable stock.
- 5 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 250 grams Green beans (French Beans) - strings removed.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli - broken into pieces.
- 2 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - toasted.
- 2 teaspoon Corn flour.
- 1 inch Ginger - julienne.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chinese Style Green Beans, first heat the oil in a wok.
1. Add the ginger and garlic and saut√© for 1-2 seconds.
1. Add the red chilies and then the beans.
1. Stir fry for 1-2 minutes and then add a tablespoon of the stock.
1. Cook, them covered for 1-2 minutes, till the beans are just tender but still crunchy.
1. Sprinkle salt and add the soy sauce, vinegar, and the chili garlic sauce (if using).
1. Mix well.
1. In a small bowl, mix the corn flour with the rest of the vegetable stock and add it to the beans.
1. Stir so that the sauce coats all over the beans.
1. Let it cook for another 3 minutes.
1. Garnish with the sesame seeds and serve immediately.
1. Your Chinese Style Green Beans Ready to be served with some Thai Jasmine Sticky Rice.
