### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Wheat Berries/ Whole Wheat.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - to serve.
- 6 Saffron strands.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 2 cups Rice - soaked for 30 minutes.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- 250 grams Green beans (French Beans) - chopped small.
- 2 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - chopped small.
- 1 cup Kala Chana (Brown Chickpeas) - soaked overnight and pressure cooked till done.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vegetable Handi Biryani Recipe, we will first keep a pressure cooker on a medium flame.
1. Add ghee, once it starts to melt add all the spice.
1. Add your soaked rice with 1 cup milk and 3 cups of water.
1. Sprinkle little salt and pressure cook it for 2 whistle.
1. Heat a sauce pan with oil, add onions, garlic, ginger and saute until it softens.
1. Add carrots and green beans and saute until the vegetables are cooked.
1. Add tomatoes and sprinkle little salt so that the tomatoes get cooked quickly.
1. Once the tomatoes become mushy add all the spice powders and give it a toss.
1. Finally add curd and cooked chana and mix well.
1. Cook until all the masala comes together.
1. Season it well with salt.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat the milk and saffron mixture in a microwave oven till it the milk is luke warm.
1. Mix water with whole wheat flour to form a firm dough and keep it ready.
1. Take out a Handi or a casserole, add the channa masala mixture at the bottom, layer it with your flavored rice.
1. Pour saffron flavored milk on top and sprinkle with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Cover it with a lid.
1. Roll the dough to form a long cylindrical rope.
1. Cover the edges of the lid tightly with the dough.
1. Make sure the heat does not come out.
1. Cook in simmer or low heat for about 15 minutes and then serve.
1. Serve the Vegetable Handi Biryani Recipe with a cucumber raita, carrot salad by the side to enjoy your Biryani.
