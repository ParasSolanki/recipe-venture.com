### Ingredients

- 2-3 cups Spinach - roughly chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 pinch Garam masala powder.
- Sunflower Oil - to grease.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 5-6 Cashew nuts - slit (optional).
- 3 cups Idli Dosa Batter.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Healthy Spinach Idli Recipe (Steamed Spinach Savory Cake), get ready with all ingredients.
1. Wash the spinach leaves and keep aside.
1. Blanch them in hot water for 3 minutes.
1. When cooled, puree them in a mixer/blender.
1. Pour a cup of idli batter in a mixing bowl and transfer the spinach puree into the bowl, and add salt, red chilli powder, garam masala and mix well with a fork.
1. Grease the idli molds in idli steamer using oil, put a spoonful of the batter into idli molds, add a piece of cashew nut on top of the batter and steam for 10 to 12 minutes or till the idlis are cooked.
1. Cool a bit and de-mould the Steamed Spinach Savory Cakes with the help of spoon, or smooth side of a knife.
1. Serve Healthy Spinach Idli Recipe (Steamed Spinach Savory Cake) with Kadale Chutney or Thakkali Vengayam Sambar Recipe (Tomato Onion Sambar) for a nice combination for breakfast.
