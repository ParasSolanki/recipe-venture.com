### Ingredients

- 1 sprig Curry leaves - roughly torn.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 4 Drumstick - cut into 1-1/2 inch size pieces.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds).
- 3 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 6 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- Red Chilli powder - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Jaggery.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini) - broken.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta) - roughly torn.

### Instructions

1. To make the Saragva Nu Shaak Recipe, we will first cook the drumsticks in a pressure cooker or a steamer.
1. Heat the oil in a pressure cooker over medium heat; add the kalonji seeds, ginger curry leaves, bay leaves and cinnamon stick.
1. Sauté for a few seconds and add the chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, red chili powder, salt , jaggery and saute the tomatoes until they soften just a bit.
1. Add the drumstick and 1/4 cup of water and cover the pressure cooker.
1. Pressure cook the Saragva Nu Shaak for about 3 to 4 whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Once the pressure has released, open the cooker, give the Saragva Nu Shaak a stir.
1.  Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Stir in the chopped coriander leaves and transfer the Saragva nu Shaak to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve the Saragva Nu Shaak along with Gujarati Dal or Gujarati Kadhi along with a hot Phulkas for lunch or dinner.
