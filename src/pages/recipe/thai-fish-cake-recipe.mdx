### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 3 Onions - finely sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Fish sauce - thai.
- 1 tablespoon Thai Red Curry Paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoon Paprika powder.
- 300 grams Basa fish - fillets.
- 2 Thai Red chilli (Birds Eye Chilli) - finely chopped (if you don't find birds eye chillies.
- 1 Whole Egg.
- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Garlic - paste.
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 1 tablespoon Galangal - paste (if you don't find.
- Sunflower Oil - for frying (as required).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thai Fish Cake recipe, make a paste of the fish fillets by placing it in a mixer jar till you reach a very fine minced texture.
1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
1. Now add egg, garlic paste, salt, galangal paste, spring onions, coriander leaves, birds eye chillies, thai fish sauce, thai red curry paste, lime juice, paprika into the mixer jar and  blend until smooth.
1. In a mixing bowl gently mix together this freshly ground paste with the fish paste.
1. Shape the fish cake mixture into small patties, by rolling them between your palms and slightly flattening them and lightly dust with cornflour.
1. Refrigerate for 15-30 minutes.
1. Heat the oil in a frying pan on medium-high heat and fry the fish cakes until crisp and brown, turning every time to cook evenly.
1. Remove from the oil using a slotted spoon and set aside to drain on kitchen paper.
1. Serve hot with a sweet chilli dip.
1. Serve Thai Fish Cake along with a hot cup of Masala Chai or Espresso Coffee for your tea time break.
