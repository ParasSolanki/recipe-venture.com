### Ingredients

- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.
- 400 grams Broccoli - cut into florets.
- 4 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 2 cups Fresh coconut - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Tamarind - lemon size.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Broccoli & Vegetable Coconut Curry Recipe, we will first make the Broccoli Curry Masala.
1. In a small pan; roast the dry red chillies, fenugreek, coriander, cumin and black pepper until you can smell the roasted aromas.
1. Add these to a small blender jar, along with the coconut, tamarind and turmeric powder.
1. Grind the spices adding very little water to make a smooth curry paste.
1. The curry paste is now ready.
1. Keep aside.
1. In the next step; heat a teaspoon of oil in a pan; add the chopped onions, broccoli and red bell peppers and saute for a couple of minutes until the onions are soft and tender.
1. Add the curry paste and saute for a couple of more minutes.
1. Stir in salt and add about 1-1/2 to 2 cups of water.
1. You essentially want a thick gravy for the Broccoli Curry; hence add water accordingly.
1. Give all the ingredients a good stir.
1. Check the salt levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Give the mixture a quick boil only when you are ready to serve.
1. Serve the Broccoli & Vegetable Coconut Curry, along with Neer Dosa or Kerala (Lachha) Paratha or even plain steamed rice.
