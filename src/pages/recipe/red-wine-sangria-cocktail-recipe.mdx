### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Ginger Juice.
- 1 Orange - thinly sliced.
- 1/8 cup Apple juice.
- 1/2 cup Strawberry Vodka.
- 1/2 cup Pomegranate juice.
- 1/2 cup Strawberries - fresh.
- 1/8 cup Lemon juice.
- 1/4 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 1 Lemon - thinly sliced.
- 2 cups Ice cubes - plus more for serving glasses.
- 750 ml Red wine.
- 1/4 cup Ginger Ale.
- 1/4 cup Sugar syrup.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Red Wine Sangria Cocktail Recipe, in a large pitcher stir in the wine, strawberry vodka, pomegranate juice, apple juice, lime juice, ginger juice, simple syrup, ginger ale.
1. Add the Ice cubes, sliced strawberries, mint leaves ,crush them in your palm before adding, lime slices, orange slices and stir well.
1. Strain the Red Wine Sangria Cocktail into a cocktail glass filled with fresh ice.
1. Serve Red Wine Sangria Cocktail along with Baked Pasta with Cottage Cheese and Spinach for a weekend brunch.
