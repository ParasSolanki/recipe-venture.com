### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 3 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 3 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled and mashed.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Onions - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 3/4 cup Green peas (Matar) - steamed.
- 100 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Green Peas, Potato and Paneer Tikki Recipe, first combine all the ingredients in a large mixing bowl, except the oil.
1. Combine all the ingredients well, mash the green peas lightly with your hands as you mix.
1. Check the salt and spice levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. The next step is to shape the tikkis into medium size discs.
1. Divide the Green Peas Potato Paneer Tikki mixture into equal size portions and shape them into 1/2 inch thick discs.
1. Once you have shaped them into discs, the next step is to cook the tikkis.
1. Heat oil in a large skillet or pan and shallow fry the tikkis for 10-12 minutes on each side till golden brown and cooked through.
1. Once the Green Peas, Potato and Paneer Tikki have cooked through, remove them from the pan and drain the excess oil in a kitchen paper towel.
1. Arrange the Green Peas, Potato and Paneer Tikkis on a serving platter and serve it along with Green Chutney and Date Tamarind Chutney for the festivals like Diwali or as an appetizer for parties.
1. Serve the Green Peas, Potato and Paneer Tikkis, along with a Tetley Lemon & Honey Green Tea and stay light, fit and healthy.
