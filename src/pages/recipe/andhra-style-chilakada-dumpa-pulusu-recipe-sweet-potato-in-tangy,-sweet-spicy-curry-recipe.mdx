### Ingredients

- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped for garnish.
- 1/8 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Tamarind Water.
- 2 cups Sweet Potatoes.
- 2 tablespoons Jaggery.
- Salt - to taste Oil for cooking.
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Sambar Powder.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1/4 cup Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Andhra Style Chilakada Dumpa Pulusu Recipe (Sweet Potato in Tangy, Sweet & Spicy Curry Recipe) by heating the pressure cooker with oil, crackle curry leaves and add the shallots and green chilies.
1. Saut√© for few minutes till it is translucent.
1. Then add the tomatoes, smash them and cook for few more minutes.
1. Add the jaggery and rest of the spice and cook for some more time till it is all smashed and becomes a paste.
1. Then finally add the sweet potatoes, give it a toss.
1. Pour in the tamarind water and check for seasoning.
1. Pressure cook the curry for about 4 whistle, and release the pressure naturally once it is done.
1. In order to temper the seasoning, heat a small skillet with oil, crackle mustard seeds, cumin seeds, curry leaves and add the Hing.
1. Add this tempering to the curry once it opened and give it a toss.
1. Serve the Andhra Style Chilakada Dumpa Pulusu Recipe (Sweet Potato in Tangy, Sweet & Spicy Curry Recipe) with steam rice or just eat it along with Chapathi for your Lunch meal.
