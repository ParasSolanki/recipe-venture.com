### Ingredients

- 2 cups Beetroot - grated.
- 1/2 cup Dates - (adjust).
- 1-1/2 Almond Milk (Badam Milk) - or coconut milk (freshly extracted).
- 2-3 teaspoons Ghee - or extra virgin coconut oil.
- 3 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds - freshly ground.
- 8 Pistachios - blanched and sliced.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Beetroot Halwa Recipe (With Vegan Option), soak dates in hot water for 20 minutes, drain excess water into a bowl and grind this into a smooth paste by using the same soaking liquid if you need to add.
1. In a thick bottomed pan, heat the Ghee/coconut oil, and saute grated beets for 3-5 minutes or until fragrant.
1. Now add almond milk/milk/coconut milk and cook covered until all the liquid evaporates, stirring in between on low heat, if unattended it can easily catch the bottom.
1. This should take between 8-12 minutes.
1. Now add the date paste and cardamom powder, mix well until combined and cook on low heat till it all comes together and excess moisture is absorbed, 5-8 minutes approximately.
1. Top Beetroot Halwa Recipe (With Vegan Option) with pistachios and enjoy either warm or chilled.
