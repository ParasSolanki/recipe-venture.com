### Ingredients

- 1 cup Fresh cream.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 200 grams Basil leaves.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 1/4 cup Walnuts.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 300 grams Spaghetti Pasta - or any other pasta.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Black olives - pitted & sliced.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes - (adjust).
- 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Spaghetti Pasta Recipe in Basil Pesto Sauce, fill a large pot with water and bring it to boil.
1. Add the pasta to the boiling water along with some salt and cook the pasta in the boiling water.
1. Cook until the pasta is al dente (just cooked with a firm bite) , this will take about easy 10-12 minutes of your time.
1. Drain the boiled pasta in a colander and rinse with cold water.
1. Rinsing with cold water is an important step to stop the cooking process of pasta so it will continue to have a firm bite and remain "Al Dente".
1. Once done,  drizzle some olive oil on top and set aside until required.
1. In to a blender, add basil, walnuts, garlic, salt, cumin powder, chilli powder, water and olive oil.
1. Blend all of the ingredients together to get a smooth basil pesto.
1. Into a wok, add 1 tablespoon of oil, add the cream, milk, basil pesto sauce, the cooked pasta, black olives and saut√© on high heat until all the pasta it coated with the sauce, for about 4 to 5 minutes.
1. Check the salt and pepper and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Serve the Spaghetti Pasta in Basil Sauce topped with chili flakes and Parmesan Cheese immediately for dinner or even a Sunday brunch.
1. You can serve Spaghetti Pasta in Basil Sauce Recipe with Avocado, Grapefruit and Walnut Salad Recipe, and a dessert like Eggless Coffee Souffle Recipe With Chocolate Sauce for brunch.
