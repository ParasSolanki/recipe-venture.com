### Ingredients

- 1 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 2-3 teaspoons Horse Gram Dal (Kollu/ Kulith).
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/4 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4-5 teaspoon Broken Wheat (Dalia/ Godumai Rava).
- 1 cup White Urad Dal (Whole).
- 2 teaspoon Rice.
- 1 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 1/2 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 2-3 teaspoons Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1/2 cup Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Menthe Hittu Recipe (Roasted Fenugreek Chutney Powder), get all the ingredients handy.
1. In a frying pan/ kadai dry roast each ingredient separately in a medium low flame till they are crisp and aromatic.
1. Take care not to burn anything.
1. Let all the ingredients cool to room temperature.
1. Grind them to a very smooth powder along with some salt in a mixer grinder.
1. Thatâ€™s it, tasty Menthe hittu bursting with flavours with roasted fenugreek is ready.
1. Store it in air tight containers and use when required.
1. Tastes best up to 6 months.
1. The Menthe Hittu Recipe chutney powder can be served with plain hot rice or goes along well with chapatis or dosas.
