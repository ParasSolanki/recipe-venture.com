### Ingredients

-  1/2 cup wheat flour - 1 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  2 tablespoons curd.
-  2 teaspoons oil.
-  1 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  1 green chili - finely chopped.
-  1 teaspoon mustard.
-  1 teaspoon cumin powder.
-  1 small Tablespoon oil.
-  1 sprig curry leaves.
-  1 teaspoon sugar.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  2 teaspoons sesame seeds (white).
- 2 cups carrots - grated.
-  1-1 / 2 cups gram flour.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida.
-  1 tablespoon lemon juice.

### Instructions

1. To make carrot vadi, first wash and peel the carrot.
1. After peeling, tighten the carrots and take them out in a bowl.
1. In the same bowl add gram flour, wheat flour, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder and add some water and take it.
1. Soft dough.
1. To make the vadi, sprinkle some water over the flour.
1. Take a little mixture in your hand and make a roll like a round cylinder.
1. Put the water in a saucepan to boil as per use.
1. Add the vadis and cook on high heat for 15 to 20 minutes.
1. Once cooked, take out the vadi and keep it aside to cool.
1. After cooling, cut it into small pieces.
1. Now for tempering, heat 2 teaspoons of oil in a pan.
1. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds and let it splutter.
1. After that add asafoetida, curry leaves, green chillies, sesame seeds and cook for 30 seconds.
1. Add vadis to it and mix well.
1. Serve.
1. Serve the Carrot Vadi Recipe with Masala Tea or Ginger Tea for the evening breakfast.
