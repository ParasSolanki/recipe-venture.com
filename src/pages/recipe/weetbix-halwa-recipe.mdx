### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Palm sugar.
- 4 Weet bix - biscuits.
- 4 teaspoons Ghee.
- Water - as required.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1/3 cup Mixed nuts - (almonds.
- pinch Saffron strands.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the WeetBix Halwa recipe, if you are using almonds, soak for 2 hours else this step can be ignored.
1. Remove the skin from almonds.
1. Blend the nuts with saffron and less than 1/2 a cup of water.
1. Blend to a nice paste.
1. Take a microwave safe bowl and brush it with ghee.
1. Crush the weetbix separately with hands.
1. Add a teaspoon of ghee and mix well.
1. Microwave for 30 seconds.
1. Take out and mix well.
1. Microwave for another 30 seconds.
1. By this time you would get a nice aroma.
1. If you are not getting then take out, mix well and keep 30 seconds again.
1. Do not keep beyond 1-1/2 minutes.
1. Add the nuts-saffron mix, remaining water, palm sugar and cardamom with this weet bix.
1. Mix well.
1. The water gets absorbed fast but do not worry.
1. Add 1 teaspoon ghee on top.
1. Microwave for a total of 4 minutes.
1. Take out every 2 minutes and mix well.
1. Adjust the sugar taste, (if you want to do so) during these intervals.
1. After 4 minutes take out and add a teaspoon of ghee.
1. The halwa would become thick by itself in 5 minutes time for the heat it has.
1. Serve either hot, warm or cold.
1. The consistency would remain the same.
1. Serve WeetBix Halwa as a dessert after your Festive Diwali Meal or just after a light dinner of Paneer Pav Bhaji on a weekday.
