### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Strawberries - or any berries.
- 1 teaspoons Vanilla Extract.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Cocoa Powder.
- 3 tablespoons Chocolate chips - or chopped dark chocolate.
- 1/2 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1/2 cup Rolled Oats.
- Salt - a pinch.
- 1 tablespoons Mixed fruits.
- 1 Mixed nuts.
- 1 tablespoon Chocolate sauce - optional.
- 2 tablespoons Honey.
- 1-1/2 tablespoons Chia Seeds - + 1 teaspoon.
- 1 tablespoon Peanut Butter.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Choco Berry Chia Parfait recipe, blend strawberries, 1/2 a cup of milk, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla and 1 tablespoon honey in a blender and pour it into a bowl.
1. To this add 1-1/2 tablespoons of chia and mix well and set aside in the fridge for 15 minutes.
1. After 15 minutes, mix this again until well combined and remove any lumps if any and let it soak for atleast 2 hours or overnight.
1. In an another bowl mix oats, cocoa powder, 1 teaspoon of chia seeds, pinch of salt and chocolate chips.
1. In the same bowl, add 1/2 cup of milk, greek yogurt, 1 tablespoon honey, nut butter, 1/2 a teaspoon vanilla and mix them all together and refrigerate for a minimum 2 hours or overnight.
1. In a clean jar layer the chia pudding alternating with Chocolate oats and drizzle with chocolate sauce, fruit and nuts.
1. Serve Choco Berry Chia Parfait on its own for a healthy and delicious breakfast.
1. You can also have it as an evening snack.
