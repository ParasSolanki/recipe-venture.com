### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Dry ginger powder.
- 1 teaspoon Palm sugar - (Panakalkandu).
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 Tulsi (holy basil).
- 2 cups Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kashayam Recipe, prep all the ingredients and keep them handy.
1. Add water into a sauce pan, tear the tulsi leaves and give it a brisk boil.
1. Once the color of the water changes, add the black pepper powder, ginger powder, palm sugar (Panakalkandu), boil for a few more minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Serve the Tulsi Kashayam hot and have it 2 to 3 times a day to get maximum relief from cough and cold.
