### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 cup Whole cashews.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves) - crushed for garnish.
- 4 Tomatoes.
- 3 tablespoons Fresh cream.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kaju Butter Masala Recipe, wash and blanch the tomatoes in enough water, once cool, remove the skin and make a smooth paste along with 8 cashews using a mixer grinder.
1. Keep aside.
1. Heat butter in a heavy bottomed pan, add the whole cashews and fry until they turn golden and remove them into a plate.
1. Keep aside until further use.
1. To the same pan add the bay leaf, ginger garlic paste and saute for a minute.
1. Add the tomato cashew puree, green chilli and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Add the red chilli powder, 2 cups water, season with salt and let it simmer for 5 minutes until the gravy becomes thick and comes together.
1. Add the fried cashews, garam masala powder and simmer for 5 minutes so that the cashews become a little soft.
1. Add the cream and mix well.
1. Simmer for another 2 minutes and switch off the heat.
1. Slightly crush the kasuri methi between your palms and add it as a garnish over the prepared Kaju Butter Masala.
1. Serve the Kaju Butter Masala Recipe, along with Butter Naan, Tandoori Aloo, Salad for a delicious Weekend Lunch.
