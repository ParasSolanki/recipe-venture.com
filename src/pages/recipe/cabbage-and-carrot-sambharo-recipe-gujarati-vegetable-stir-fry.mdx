### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 3 Carrots (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 3 cups Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - shredded.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 3 Green Chillies - slit lengthwise.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cabbage and Carrot Sambharo Recipe, heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan on medium heat.
1. Add the mustard seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Once it crackles, add the asafoetida, green chilies, curry leaves and saute for a few seconds.
1.  At this stage add the turmeric powder and the chopped vegetables, salt and stir to combine all the ingredients well.
1. Turn the heat to low, cover the pan and allow the vegetables to cook in its own steam for about 3 to 4 minutes, until just about cooked.
1.  You want the vegetables to remain little firm and crunchy and not too soft.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and the Cabbage and Carrot Sambharo is ready to be served.
1. Serve Cabbage And Carrot Sambharo along with Palak Ragi & Oats Wheat Thepla Recipe or Beetroot & Sesame Thepla Recipe for a simple mean.
