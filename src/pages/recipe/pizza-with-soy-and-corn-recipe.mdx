### Ingredients

- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 1 cup Sweet corn.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 2 tablespoons Homemade tomato puree.
- 1 pinch Sugar.
- 1 cup Soy granules.
- 1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese - shredded (adjust).
- 3/4 tablespoon Butter - for roasting the pizza base.
- Salt - as per taste.
- 3 Tomatoes.
- Black olives - few.
- 1-2 Cherry tomatoes - to garnish (optional).
- 2 Onions - thinly sliced.
- Red Chilli flakes - as per taste.
- 1 Pizza base - (homemade/store-bought).
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Pizza With Soy And Corn Recipe, the following steps are to be followed.
1. First, boil the soy granules for 10 minutes and strain them with fresh water.
1. Pressure cook the American corn in sufficient water till 1 whistle or till soft but not mushy and keep aside.
1. Take a wok/kadai add a teaspoon oil in it.
1. Let it heat.
1. Now add 1 chopped onion and salt as required and sauté them till it turns translucent in color.
1. Keep the fried onions aside.
1. In the same wok, add another onion and sauté it till semi cooked.
1. Now add soya granules and sauté for a minute or two.
1. After a minute or two, add boiled corns and stir well.
1. Next, add salt, red chili powder, oregano flakes and tomato ketchup.
1. Stir it well for 5 to 7 minutes or until cooked and keep aside.
1. Instruction for Pizza SauceTake the tomatoes along with garlic cloves and grind them.
1. Now take a wok/kadai, add some oil and add the tomato paste to it, on a medium flame.
1. Add sugar, salt, chili flakes and oregano flakes in the sauce.
1. Keep stirring till it thickens to semi-pouring consistency.
1. Keep aside.
1. Instruction for PizzaTake a tawa and butter it.
1. Now put the pizza base on the tawa and roast it.
1. When the base starts turning golden, flip it and roast on the other side.
1. When the base turns little crispy, close the flame, and take off the base from tawa.
1. Now start layering with pizza sauce first, and spread it on the base.
1. Then spread prepared soy granules and corns on it.
1. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on the top and lastly garnish it with your fried onions.
1. Sprinkle oregano flakes.
1. Microwave on high for 2 to 3 minutes (till the cheese starts melting).
1. Alternately, you can also close a lid on the tawa for 2-3 minutes to achieve this.
