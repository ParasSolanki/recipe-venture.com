### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 1 cup Vivatta Maida.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - coarsely pounded.
- Water - to knead.
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 2-1/2 tablespoons Ghee.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the mathri get all the ingredients ready and handy.
1. Take a mixing bowl add the maida, ghee, ajwain and mix it well with your hands till the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
1. Add water gradually and mix till the flour comes together to form a firm and soft mathri dough.
1. Knead the mathri dough it lightly and put it back into the bowl, cover it with a clean damp cloth and let it rest for half an hour.
1. Heat oil for deep frying on a medium heat.
1. Divide the mathri dough into very small portions.
1. You will get about 20 mathris.
1. Roll the mathri dough into 75mm thickness or the desired thickness you like.
1. Make holes in them using forks, to avoid Mathri from puffing up while frying.
1. Drop the mathri dough into the hot oil and deep fry till the Mathri on low to medium heat until they turn golden brown in color, flipping it once or twice.
1. Drain the oil and put them on kitchen towel/ tissues.
1. Sprinkle chaat masala over the mathris and store them in airtight containers.
