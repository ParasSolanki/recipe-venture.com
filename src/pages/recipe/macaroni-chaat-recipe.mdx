### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - or Spicy Peanuts/Congress Kadlekayi.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- Lemon juice - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Sweet Chutney (Date & Tamarind).
- 1 cup Macaroni pasta - cooked.
- 1/4 cup Sweet corn - steamed.
- Sev - Fine one.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Macaroni Chaat recipe, in a large mixing bowl add the cooked macaroni.
1. Add onion, tomato, corn, coriander leaves, spicy peanuts, date tamarind chutney, green chutney, chat masala, red chilli powder, lemon juice and salt.
1. Mix well such that everything is combinedMix everything well and adjust the spices accordingly.
1. Transfer Macaroni chaat to a serving plate.
1. Garnish with little fine sev and coriander leaves and serve.
1. Serve Macaroni Chaat along with Masala Chai and Vegetable Dal Pakora for your tea time break.
