### Ingredients

- 250 grams Mutton.
- 8 cloves Garlic.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 2 Onions.
- 6 Green Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Poppy seeds.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo).
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 3 Tomatoes.
- 4 Cloves (Laung).
- 2 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kerala Style Mutton Stew Recipe, clean and wash the mutton pieces and keep them aside.
1. Dice the onion, chillies, and tomatoes into small pieces.
1. Peel and dice the potatoes into four pieces and keep it aside.
1. Grind the coriander seeds and poppy seeds separately to a fine paste.
1. Grind the ginger and garlic together.
1. Next add onions, chilies, tomatoes, spices and salt to the mutton and give it a good stir so that mutton pieces are coated properly.
1. Heat the oil and add spices including poppy seeds, cinnamon, cloves and coriander seeds.
1. Cook it for few minutes and make sure that spices are not getting burnt.
1. Add the mutton and fry it for a while.
1. Add just sufficient water to cook the mutton; cook till the time mutton pieces become tender and cooked.
1. Next, add the grated coconut & chopped coriander leaves.
1. Cook it for 7 to 9 minutes on low heat and serve it hot.
1. Serve Kerala Style Mutton Stew along with Kerala Pumpkin Pachadi and Kerala Parotta for a weekday meal.
