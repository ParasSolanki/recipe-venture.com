### Ingredients

- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- Salt - a pinch.
- 5 tablespoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Veechu/Ceylon Parotta Recipe, we will add all the ingredients into a mixing bowl including all purpose flour, whole wheat flour, curd, salt except oil.
1. Add little water slowly and mix to form a soft and sticky dough.
1. Add a tablespoon of oil and knead it a little more and rest for some time outside.
1. To make the Veechu/Ceylon paratha, heat a roti tawa on a medium heat and leave it side.
1. Pinch out a little dough from the kneaded dough, add some oil on your working place.
1. Spread the dough with your palm and fingers.
1. Spread to form a thin sheet, it is okay if there are gaps.
1. Fold the dough to form an envelope.
1. Then fold it again from the sides as well to form a small folded napkin.
1. Place this on the roti tawa and cook on both sides by drizzling some oil till it is toasted well.
1. Serve the Veechu/Ceylon Parotta Recipe along with Sri Lankan Egg Curry or a Sri Lankan Chicken Curry to enjoy the dish for your Sunday lunch.
