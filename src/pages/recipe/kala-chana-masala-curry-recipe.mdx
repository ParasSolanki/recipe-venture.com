### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 3/4 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Water - as required.
- 3 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 tablespoon Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 2 cups Kala Chana (Brown Chickpeas) - soaked overnight.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 2 Tomato - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kala Chana Masala recipe, firstly boil the soaked black chickpeas in a pressure cooker until the cooker release at least 3 whistles.
1. When they are soft, drain the water and keep them aside.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds and hing.
1. Let the mustard seeds crackle.
1. Once it is done, add the tomatoes and green chillies and let it cook for about 5 minutes.
1. Meanwhile mix the whole wheat flour properly with 3 tablespoons water.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Add the black chickpeas in the pan.
1. Next, add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, salt and the whole wheat flour mixture.
1. Mix everything properly and let it cook for about 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Once done add the lemon juice and garnish it with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve Kalal Chana Masala along with Phulkas and Gujarati Dal for a perfect weekday meal.
1. You can also pack this in your lunch box along with Pudina Tawa Parathas.
