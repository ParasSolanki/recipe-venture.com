### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 300 grams Banana Stem - cut into small dices and soaked in buttermilk.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vazha Thandu Poriyal Recipe, firstly pressure cook the banana stem along with the buttermilk, salt and turmeric powder for about 1 whistle.
1. Release the pressure naturally and drain the water out.
1. Heat a skillet with oil.
1. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves and let the mustard seeds crackle.
1. Once done, add the cooked Banana stem and mix everything properly.
1. After a minute, add in the green chili, salt to taste and give it a mix.
1. Let it cook for about 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes, add in the grated coconut and give it stir fry for about 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes, switch off the stove and it is ready to be served.
1. Serve the Vazha Thandu Poriyal Recipe along with Steamed Rice, Parappu Rasam and Elai Vadam to enjoy your Lunch meal.
