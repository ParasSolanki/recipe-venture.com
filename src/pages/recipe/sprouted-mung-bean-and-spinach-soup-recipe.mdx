### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 cup Spinach Leaves (Palak) - (with stems) washed and chopped.
- 3/4-1 cup Green Moong Sprouts.
- 1 Tomato - blanched.
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil - or ghee or butter.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 Lime Wedges.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 2 sprigs Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - or mint leaves to serve.
- 1 pinch Red Chilli powder - or flakes.
- 1-2 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sprouted Mung beans and Spinach Soup Recipe, first heat oil or ghee or butter in a saucepan.
1. Add the onion, garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes.
1. Add the ginger, chilli powder and cumin powder.
1. Mix and add the mung beans.
1. Add 2-2 1/2 cups of vegetable stock and cook covered for 5-6 minutes.
1. Add the tomato and season with salt.
1. Mix well.
1. Add the spinach leaves and cook for 2-3 minutes.
1. Serve Sprouted Mung beans and Spinach Soup Recipe in individual bowls, garnished with lime juice and coriander/mint leaves.
1. Serve the Sprouted Mung Bean and Spinach Soup Recipe as a starter to your sunday meal before Mexican Sizzler Recipe and end it with a Butterscotch Marshmallow Mousse Recipe.
