### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 2 tablespoon Gond (Edible Gum).
- 2 tablespoon Jaggery - powder.
- 5 Badam (Almond) - sliced.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Goondh Paani Recipe ( Sweet Gum Tree Almond Drink Recipe) by heating ghee in a Flat nonstick pan and frying the gum tree for about 10 minutes.
1. Fry until the gum tree becomes opaque and increases in size.
1. Then later slowly add 1 cup water along with jaggery and keep mixing until the Gumtree slowly dissolves in water.
1. Once water thickens add the sliced almonds and give it a stir.
1. Your Gondh Paani (Sweet Gum Tree Almond Drink) is ready to be served hot.
