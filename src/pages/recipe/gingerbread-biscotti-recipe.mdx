### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Molasses - or dark corn syrup or honey or melted jaggery.
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Baking powder.
- 2 cups All Purpose Flour (Maida) - (more if required).
- 3 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar) - ½  cup brown sugar.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 2 teaspoons All spice powder.
- 2 Whole Eggs - lightly whisked.
- 1/4 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 3 tablespoons Butter.
- Hazelnut - handful.
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Dry ginger powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Gingerbread Biscotti first preheat the oven to 180 degree celcius.
1. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
1. Mix the whole wheat flour and all purpose flour, ground ginger ,allspice powder, baking powder and salt.
1. In another mixing bowl whisk together the sugar, oil and butter until combined.
1. Add molasses (or corn syrup or honey or liquid jaggery), eggs to the sugar and butter bowl and whisk again.
1. Stir in the dry ingredients to the  and mix till it is completely incorporated and it forms a stiff dough .
1. Add little flour if it is very soft.
1. Divide the dough into two parts.
1. Shape each portion into a rough log about 8-9 inches.
1. Bake for about 20-22 minutes till it is firm to touch.
1. Time may vary depending on the oven and size of logs.
1. Cool the logs on the baking sheet.
1. Using a sharp serrated knife carefully cut them into about ½ inch slices.
1. Place the slices on cookie sheet or sheets and bake on each side for about 10-12 minutes or till the cookies dry out.
1. Cool completely on a wire rack.
1. Store Gingerbread Biscotti recipe in an airtight container and serve during tea timeServe Gingerbread Biscotti recipe with Kesar Chai ,Coffee , hot chocolate or wine.
