### Ingredients

- Water - as required.
- 1 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 1/2 tablespoon Ginger - paste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - crumbled.
- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few.
- 1/2 tablespoon Garlic - paste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 Star anise.
- 1/4 cup Homemade tomato puree.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Paneer Bhurji Gravy recipe, heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, bay leaf, red dry chilli, black cardamom, green cardamom, star anise and hing.
1. Once the mustard seeds splutter and the spices release a nice aroma, add the finely chopped onions.
1. Cook them till they turn soft and translucent.
1. Add the ginger garlic paste and let it cook for, once done add the finely chopped tomatoes and tomato puree.
1. Cover with lid and let it cook it for about 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Once the tomatoes are cooked and start coming together add the spices including red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala and salt.
1. Stir well to combine and let it cook for 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes, add the crumbled paneer and the required water.
1. Mix everything properly and let it simmer for three to four minutes and then turn off the heat.
1. Once it is done, garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
1. Serve Paneer Bhurji Gravy along with Bharwa Karela and Pudina Tawa Paratha for a delicious lunch or dinner on a weekday.
