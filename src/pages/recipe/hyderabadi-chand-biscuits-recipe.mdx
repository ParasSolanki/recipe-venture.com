### Ingredients

- 125 gram Butter (unsalted).
- Baking powder - a pinch.
- 10 grams Milk Powder.
- 200 grams All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- Salt - a pinch.
- 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 75 gram Sugar - powdered.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Hyderabadi Chand Biscuits recipe, preheat oven to 180 degree.
1. In a bowl, add butter, powdered sugar and cream.
1. Mix everything properly.
1. Next, add all purpose flour, milk powder, baking powder, vanilla essence.
1. Crumble the mixture and mix it well like a dough.
1. Using a rolling pin, roll the Chand Biscuits dough into a thin sheet and cut it into chand (crescent) shape using a round cutter (I used a steel tumbler).
1. Transfer the Hyderabadi Chand Biscuits into baking tray and bake for about 15 to 20 minutes until you notice them getting a golden crust.
1. Keep a watch on the Chand biscuits and make sure they don't get over baked.
1. Serve Hyderabadi Chand Biscuits Recipe along with a cup of Hot Masala Chai or Punjabi Style Espresso Coffee for evening snacks.
