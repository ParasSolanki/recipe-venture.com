### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons Soy sauce.
- 1 Cauliflower (gobi) - grated.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - cubed.
- 1 cup Tofu - cut into small cubes.
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar) - (optional).
- 2 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 3 tablespoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - cubed.
- 6 sprig Spring Onion Greens - minced.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the 15 minute Cauliflower Fried Rice Recipe by grating the cauliflower and prepping the vegetables first.
1. Heat a sauce pan with oil, add garlic and onions and saut√© for few minutes, then slowly add in all the vegetables and saute till it is cooked.
1. Finally add the cauliflower along with all the sauces and the seasonings and give it a stir fry.
1. Serve the 15 minute Cauliflower Fried Rice Recipe along with Chilli Paneer Recipe (Indian Chinese) to enjoy your meal.
