### Ingredients

- 1 scoop Vanilla Ice cream - (small scoops).
- 2 tablespoons Tea Monk Oolong Tea.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 1 cup Aerated water (Soda Water).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Ice Cream Tea Float, into a teapot, add a cup of boiling water.
1. Add in the Tea Monk Oolong Tea and allow it to brew for two minutes.
1. Once it has brewed, strain to filter the tea.
1. Allow the tea to cool or refrigerate for about half an hour.
1. Pour the brewed oolong tea into a serving glass.
1. Add 1 cup of aerated soda water.
1. Finally add a scoop of vanilla  ice cream and top with more soda water as needed.
1. Serve the Ice Cream Tea Float Recipe as a Party drink along with Mexican Vegetable Nachos or a Murg Malai Kebab to enjoy it with.
