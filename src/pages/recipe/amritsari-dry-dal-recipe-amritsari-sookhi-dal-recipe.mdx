### Ingredients

-  1/2 cup coriander leaves Finely chopped salt - 1/2 tsp red chilli powder.
-  asafetida - a pinch.
- 1/2 cup black urad dal (split).
-  1/2 tsp turmeric powder.
-  1 tbsp oil.
-  1/4 cup coriander - finely chopped.
-  1/2 tsp celery.
-  2 onions - cut into thin and straight.
-  1/2 Teaspoon cumin.
-  3 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  1/2 inch ginger - tighten.
-  1/2 tsp coriander powder.
-  2 tomatoes - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make Amritsari dry dal recipe, first wash the urad dal 3 to 4 times and soak it in water for 30 minutes.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add celery, cumin, asafoetida and cook till the cumin seeds crackle.
1. Now add green chili, garlic, onion, salt and cook till the onion becomes soft.
1. After the onion becomes soft, add tomato, red chilli powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder and cook till the tomato becomes soft.
1. Now add urad dal and some water.
1. Cover the pan and cook till the lentils are cooked.
1. Add a little water as it is a dry pulse.
1. Turn off the gas, garnish with green coriander and serve.
1. Serve the Amritsari Dry Dal Recipe with Tawa Paratha and Bundi Raita for dinner.
