### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoon Dates - (dried ones) finely chopped.
- 3 tablespoons Pumpkin seeds.
- 1/2 tablespoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Wheat Flakes (Cereal).
- 3 tablespoon Dried raspberries.
- 3 tablespoons Raisins - (sultana).
- 3 tablespoon Badam (Almond).
- 3 tablespoon Walnuts.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 1/2 teaspoon Nutmeg powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 3 tablespoon Pine nuts.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Homemade Granola Mix Recipe with Oats & Wheat Flakes, heat a wok or a heavy bottomed pan over medium heat and gently roast the oats until they turn crisp and lightly golden.
1. Once roasted, remove from the pan and keep aside.
1. Heat the vegetable oil in the same pan over medium heat.
1.  Add all the nuts and seeds and roast them for about 5 minutes.
1. Once the nuts and seeds are roasted, add the dried dates, raspberries and sultanas along with the honey and vanilla essence.
1. Roast for another couple of minutes.
1. Finally add in the tossed oats, wheat flakes, cinnamon and nutmeg powderUsing a spatula mix everything thoroughlyOnce they are cooled down the Homemade Granola Mix with Oats & Wheat Flakes is ready.
1. Store them in an airtight container for a few weeks.
1. Serve the Homemade Granola Mix with Oats & Wheat Flakes crunchy for breakfast along with fresh milk & fruits or as an evening snack for kids or can be even used as a topping for smoothies.
