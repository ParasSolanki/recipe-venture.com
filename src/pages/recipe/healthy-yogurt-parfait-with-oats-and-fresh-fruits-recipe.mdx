### Ingredients

- 1 Apple - chopped.
- 4 Prunes - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Raw Banana - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 1/2 cup Coconut milk.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 1 tablespoon Pistachios - pound.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Healthy Yogurt Parfait with Oats and Fresh Fruits Recipe by cooking the oats first, heat a sauce pan add all the ingredients and mix, stir till it thickens and keep it aside to let it cool down.
1. Whisk in the yogurt and honey mixture in a separate bowl till it smooth and thick and keep it aside in the fridge till you assemble.
1. To assemble, take any jar, layer it with oats mixture, then add chopped apple, and layer it with yogurt, add some chopped banana, again add the yogurt layer and top it up with prunes and pistachios.
1. Serve the Healthy Yogurt Parfait with Oats and Fresh Fruits Recipe as a breakfast recipe to start your morning in a healthy way.
