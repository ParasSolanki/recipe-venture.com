### Ingredients

- 1-1/4 cup Cashew nuts - soaked for 6 hours.
- 700 grams Strawberries - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 cup Coconut milk - thick.
- 1/2+1/2 cup Maple syrup - (honey can also be used if not vegan).
- pinch Salt.
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour - or arrowroot powder.
- 4 tablespoons Balsamic Vinegar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dairyfree Balsamic Strawberry Ice-cream recipe, in a bowl add balsamic vinegar, 1/2 cup of honey and arrowroot powder and whisk until combined without any lumps.
1. Combine this mixture and chopped strawberries in a saucepan and cook until mushy and bubbly, for approximately 8 minutes.
1. Let it cool completely.
1. Drain the cashews to remove excess water.
1. In a blender add drained cashews, 1/2 cup maple syrup, pinch of salt, 1 cup coconut yogurt and the cooled strawberry mixture and blend until completely smooth.
1. This can be done in batches.
1. Pour this into a container and cover it with a cling wrap and chill overnight.
1. Set it out on the bench top for about 5 minutes and scoop it out to serve.
1. Serve Dairy free Balsamic Strawberry Ice-cream as a dessert after a delicious dinner of Paneer Pav Bhaji.
