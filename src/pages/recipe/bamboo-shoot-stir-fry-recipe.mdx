### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 cup Red Yellow and Green Bell Peppers (Capsicum) - julienned.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - finely chopped.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 2 cups Bamboo shoots - julienned.
- 6 Button mushrooms - or cremini mushrooms.

### Instructions

1. To start making Bamboo Shoot Fry, Heat up a cast iron skillet on medium heat.
1. Add olive oil when its hot, add chopped spring onions and saute for 2 minutes.
1. Add bamboo shoots and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Add chopped sweet peppers and mushrooms with required salt, cook until all the vegetables are cooked but crispy.
1. Sprinkle chilli flakes and stir for a minute.
1. Check salt and serve Bamboo Shoot Stir Fry Recipe with with phulka or as a side dish with rice and sambar.
