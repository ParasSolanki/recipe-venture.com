### Ingredients

- Drumstick Leaves (Moringa/Murungai Keerai) - one bunch.
- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 cup Tamarind Water.
- 5 cloves Garlic - crushed.
- 2 teaspoon Rasam Powder.
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 tablespoons Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Murungai Keerai Rasam Recipe wash the tur dal and pressure cook the dal along with some water for 3 whistles and turn off the flame.
1. Now allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Open the lid of the pressure cooker and mash it.
1. Place a pan on a medium flame and add the drumstick leaves into it along with two tablespoons of water.
1. Cover it with a lid and cook until the leaves wilt.
1. Turn off the flame and set aside.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, add the tamarind water and let it boil until the raw smell of tamarind goes away.
1. Add the cooked tur dal and stir well.
1. Add in the cooked drumstick leaves.
1. Reduce the flame and let it simmer for 2 minutes.
1. Now add salt, asafoetida, rasam powder, cumin powder and pepper powder and mix well.
1.  Once the rasam comes to a boil and starts frothing, turn of the flame.
1. In a tadka pan, add a teaspoon of oil and wait for it to heat.
1. Add in the mustard seeds, cumin seeds and let it crackle.
1. Once they have crackled add the curry leaves and garlic and  turn off the flame.
1. Add this tempering to the rasam and mix well.
1. Serve Murungai Keerai Rasam Recipe along with Steamed Rice, Carrot Poriyal and a dollop of Ghee.
