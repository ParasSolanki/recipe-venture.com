### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 2 Green Chillies.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1-1/2 cup Vivatta Maida.
- 1/4 cup Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 tablespoon Sugar.
- 1/2 cup Button mushrooms - finely chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - as required (finely chopped).
- 1/8 cup Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Lukewarm Water.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 tablespoon Active dry yeast.
- 3/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Rose Buns With Stuffed Mushroom recipe, in a medium mixing bowl, mix sugar with luke warm water.
1. Then add yeast to the sugary warm water and keep aside for 10 minutes.
1. After 10 minutes, the mixture should turn frothy and have risen well.
1. Take another broad bowl.
1. Add all purpose flour, oil, salt along with the frothed yeast mixture.
1. Gather to get a smooth dough.
1. No need to knead much.
1. Keep covered with a wet cloth for 1-2 hoursor till doubled in size.
1. Meanwhile, start preparing the stuffing.
1. Heat oil in a frying pan.
1. Add in finely chopped onion and saute till they turn soft.
1. Then add ginger garlic paste and cook for some more minutes.
1. Add finely chopped mushroom, turmeric powder, finely chopped green chilies, red chili powder and salt.
1. Mix everything well, cover and cook over medium heat for about 10-15 minutes or until all liquid has evaporated.
1. Add chopped coriander leaves and give a quick stir.
1. Once done, remove from the heat and allow the stuffing to cool completely.
1. After 1-2 hours, the dough will be doubled in size.
1. Now sprinkle some all purpose flour on a flat surface, punch down the dough and knead on the floured surface until smooth with the help of your palm.
1. Divide the dough into 6 number of small balls.
1. Take one ball and roll out to about 5" diameter.
1. Place a round cookie cutter of 1.
1. 5" diameter in center without pressing down.
1. Make four diagonal slits, leaving 1.
1. 5" center area.
1. Now take out the cookie cutter.
1. Place 1 tablespoon of stuffing in the center.
1. Brush a bit of butter on the edges of rolled dough.
1. Take one of the sections and cover the side of the filling with it.
1. Next take the opposite side's section and cover the other side of the filling with it.
1. Press a little on the edges so that it stick to each other.
1. Next similarly take and cover other two sections and finally give to a rose shape.
1. Repeat for rest of the dough balls.
1. Preheat oven to 180 degree C.
1. Place all the roses on a baking tray lined with butter paper.
1. Brush those with milk and sprinkle some white sesame seeds.
1. Bake the roses for 18-20 minutes at 200 degree C or until the tops are golden.
1. Take out from oven and brush them with melted butter.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve Rose Buns With Stuffed Mushroom along with Masala Chai and Orange Lemon Tea Cake for your next tea party.
