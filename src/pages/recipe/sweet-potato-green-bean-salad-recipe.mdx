### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 15 Green beans (French Beans) - steamed and halved.
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice - freshly squeezed.
- 1 Red onion - finely chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 10 Cherry tomatoes - halved (or 2 large tomatoes.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 250 grams Sweet Potatoes - boiled.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sweet Potato & Green Bean Salad Recipe, first get all the ingredients ready.
1. Boil the sweet potatoes ahead of time, peel and refrigerate.
1. Steam the green beans using a steamer for just about 4 minutes and turn off and allow it to cool.
1. Finally combine all the ingredients in a large mixing bowl, check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Refrigerate the Sweet Potato & Green Bean Salad or serve immediately.
1. Serve Sweet Potato & Green Bean Salad Recipe along with a soup like Sweet Corn Vegetable Soup Recipe or French Onion Soup Recipe for lunch.
