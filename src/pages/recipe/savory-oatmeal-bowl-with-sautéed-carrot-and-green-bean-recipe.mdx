### Ingredients

- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 1/2 cup Water.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4 Cloves (Laung) - garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 40 grams Rolled Oats Or Instant Oats - 40 grams.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - sliced diagonally.
- 50 grams Green beans (French Beans) - sliced diagonally.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Savory Oatmeal Bowl with Sautéed Carrot and Green Bean Recipe by heating a flat skillet with some oil, crackle the cumin seeds.
1. Add in the chopped vegetables and saute.
1. You can add some salt and saute as it will fasten the process.
1. Next add in all the spice powders and saute for another 5 minutes till the raw smell goes away.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now to make the oats, take a sauce pan, add in the oats and water and by using whisk keep stirring till it thickens.
1. Make sure it does not form a lump.
1. This will take you about 5 minutes.
1. To plate, take a wide bowl, add in your oats mixture and drop you’re topping on top and serve.
