### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 200 grams Fresh Figs - cut into quarters.
- 1/4 teaspoon Nutmeg.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Fresh Fig Compote Recipe, heat a small skillet on medium-low heat, add butter.
1. Once the butter has melted, add the figs.
1. To the skillet add the demerara sugar, honey and nutmeg powder.
1. Give it a good stir and cook until the figs, lose shape and become slightly mushy.
1. Once the Fig Compote has turned mushy, turn off the flame and allow the compote to cool.
1. Transfer the cooked Fresh Fig Compote into a sterilised jar and store it in the refrigerator for 3-4 days and use as desired.
1. Serve Fresh Fig Compote with some No Bake Steamed Bread Pudding Recipe for a delicious warm dessert with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
