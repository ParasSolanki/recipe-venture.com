### Ingredients

- 2 cups Hung Curd.
-  1/4 tsp red chilli powder.
-  1 tsp cumin powder.
-  2 radish - take cas.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 green coriander - little.
-  1 green chilli - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. Prepare all the ingredients to make radish raita.
1. Now pour yogurt in a bowl and beat well.
1. Now add radish, red chilli powder, cumin powder, green chili, green coriander, salt and mix.
1. Serve.
1. Serve Radish Raita Recipe along with Paneer Butter Masala and Phulka for your dinner.
