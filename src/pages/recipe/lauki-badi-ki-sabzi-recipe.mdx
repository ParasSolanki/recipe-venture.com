### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 Bottle gourd (lauki) - peel the skin and chopped.
- 3 cloves Garlic - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 tablespoons Milk - or more if required.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Badi (wadi).
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1/4 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lauki Badi Ki Sabzi recipe, firstly roast the badi in a pan until it turns brown.
1. Take it out and keep it aside to cool down.
1. Now heat oil or ghee in a wok/kadhai and add cumin seeds, mustard seeds and asafoetida.
1. Once the mustard seeds splutter, add in the green chillies, asafoetida and ginger garlic paste.
1. Add in the chopped lauki and cook till it becomes soft and tender.
1. Once it is done add in the roasted badi and give it a stir.
1. Add in the masalas including red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala and the required salt.
1. Add in the required milk.
1. Don't add too much milk because the bad becomes soft very fast.
1. Cook till the badi becomes soft.
1. Stir in the coriander leaves and transfer the Lauki Badi ki Sabzi to as serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Lauki Badi Ki Sabzi along with Panchmel Dal and Pudina Tawa Paratha for a weekday lunch or dinner.
