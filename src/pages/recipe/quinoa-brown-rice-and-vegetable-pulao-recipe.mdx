### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 cup Quinoa.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - peeled and diced.
- 1 cup Brown Rice - soaked in water.
- 1/2 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil - or ghee.
- 1/4 cup Green beans (French Beans) - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 2 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 3 cloves Garlic - grated.
- 4 cups Hot water.
- 1/2 cup Cauliflower (gobi) - florets.
- 1/4 cup Lemon juice.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - diced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 cup Coconut milk.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Quinoa Brown Rice Pilaf, place a heavy bottom pan on medium heat.
1. Add a teaspoon of oil or ghee.
1. Once it is medium hot, add onions, garlic, ginger, green chilies and fennel seeds.
1. Saute them for a minute.
1. Add the chopped vegetables - cauliflower, potato, carrot, green beans and green peas.
1. Saute the vegetables on medium flame for about 5-7 minutes until they soften.
1. Add garam masala powder, turmeric powder, black pepper powder and salt to taste.
1. Grate ginger and garlic to it.
1. After a minute add the chopped green chilli.
1. Add 2 cups boiling water and coconut milk.
1. Mix all the ingredients well and bring the mixture to a boil.
1. Once the water comes to a boil, turn the heat to medium.
1. At this stage, drain the soaked rice.
1. Add quinoa and rice into the boiling mixture, turn the heat to low, cover the pan and cook on low heat until all the water is absorbed and  Quinoa Brown Rice Pilaf is cooked.
1. At this stage, the quinoa and rice should be perfectly cooked.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and allow it to remain covered for about 5 to 10 minutes before you can serve.
1. Once ready, open and fluff it up with a fort and transfer the Quinoa Brown Rice Pilaf in a serving plate.
1. Stir it with freshly chopped coriander and lemon juice and serve.
1. Serve Quinoa Brown Rice Pilaf with Burani Raita Recipe (Garlic Based Yogurt) or Mint And Pomegranate Raita Recipe for a comforting weeknight dinner or lunch.
