### Ingredients

- 1 flower cabbage - cut.
-  oil - as per use.
-  2 sprig heart leaves.
-  pepper powder - according to taste.
-  2 tsp walnuts - roast.
-  4 tbsp milk.
-  salt - according to taste.

### Instructions

1. To make the Roasted Colflower Heart Soup recipe, firstly cut the cauliflower.
1. Wash it well and keep it separately.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add cauliflower, salt and cook until the cabbage becomes light brown.
1. Let it cool after cooking.
1. After it cools down, put it in a blender and make a paste.
1. Pour this cabbage paste in a saucepan with milk or water and let it boil for 1 minute.
1. Now add the leaf leaves and cook for another 2 minutes.
1. Pour into a serving bowl and garnish with walnut and olive oil.
1. Serve the Roasted Colflower Heart Soup recipe before your daily dinner or serve it with a salad for dinner.
