### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta) - torn.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 3/4 cup Masoor Dal (Whole).
- 1/2 cup Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini) - broken.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a small bunch chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Masoor Moong Phali Ki Dal Recipe, first prep all the ingredients and keep them ready.
1. Heat oil in the pressure cooker, add the ginger, onion and saute until the onions soften.
1. Add the tomato, chilli, bay leaf, masoor dal, peanuts, turmeric powder, garam masala powder and salt to taste.
1. Add 2-1/2 cups of water and pressure cook for 2 whistles.
1. After the couple of whistles, turn the heat to low and simmer for 4 to 5 minutes.
1. Turn off the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Heat ghee in a tadka pan for seasoning; add the cumin seeds, cinnamon and allow the flavors to infuse for a few seconds.
1. Open the cooker, and transfer to a serving bowl.
1. Stir in the chopped coriander leaves and the lemon juice.
1. Check the salt and adjust to taste.
1. Finally pour the seasoning and serve.
1. Serve the Masoor Moong Phali Ki Dal along with Tawa Paratha, Cabbage Sabzi and Raita for a wholesome lunch or dinner.
