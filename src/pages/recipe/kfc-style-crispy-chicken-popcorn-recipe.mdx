### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Dried Thyme Leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 250 grams Chicken breasts - boneless.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 1 Whole Egg.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 2 teaspoon Garlic - minced.
- 1 teaspoon Rosemary.
- 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the KFC Style Crispy Chicken Popcorn, wash the chicken thoroughly, cut into small cubes.
1. To marinate the chicken, in a mixing bowl, combine chicken, minced garlic, lemon juice, oregano, thyme, rosemary, chili flakes and salt.
1. Alternatively if you do not have these herbs available individually, you can use 3 teaspoons of mixed herbs or any herbs of your choice.
1. Let this sit for about 20 minutes.
1. In the meanwhile, heat a griddle on medium-high flame, toast the slices of bread until crispy.
1. Allow it to cool.
1.  In a mixer jar, grind the toasted bread until you get coarse bread crumbs.
1. Transfer the bread crumbs to a bowl, add cumin powder and garam masala mix and set aside.
1. In another bowl, break the egg, add milk to the eggs and whisk lightly, set aside.
1. Place the maida in a bowl and keep it ready.
1. Heat a paniyaram pan on medium-high flame until steaming hot.
1. Put a little oil in each of the cavities.
1. Arrange the components in the sequence of - chicken, egg, maida, bread crumbs.
1. To coat the chicken popcorns, first start by dipping the marinated piece of chicken in egg, then roll in maida, dip it in egg once again, and finally roll it in the bread crumbs.
1. Drop the chicken popcorns in each of the cavities and allow to cook and become crispy.
1. After 3-4 minutes, flip the chicken on to the other side.
1. Allow to cook.
1. Drain on a kitchen towel so that it absorbs the excess oil.
1. Serve hot.
1. Alternatively, you can also deep fry the Crispy Chicken Popcorn in a frying pan until the popcorns turns golden in colour.
1. Serve KFC Style Crispy Chicken Popcorn as appetizer with Cottage Cheese Dip Recipe With Pepper and Olives or Tzatziki Recipe - A Greek Yogurt Dip or Sriracha Hummus for your house parties or a potluck party.
