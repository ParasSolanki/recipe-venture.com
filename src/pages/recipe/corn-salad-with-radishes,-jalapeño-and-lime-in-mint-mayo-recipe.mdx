### Ingredients

- 2 Pickled Jalapenos - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 4 tablespoons Del Monte Mint Mayo.
- 1 cup Sweet corn - steamed.
- 4 Red baby radish - thinly sliced.
- 1 teaspoon White pepper powder.
- 2 teaspoons Lemon juice.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Corn Salad With Radishes, Jalapeño and Lime in DelMonte Mint Mayonnaise recipe, boil corns and keep aside.
1. Transfer the boiled corns to a plate or bowl.
1. AddAdd DelMonte Mint Mayonnaise, salt and pepper, give it a mix.
1. Now add radish slices, Jalapenos, coriander leaves, mix.
1. Serve Corn Salad With Radishes Jalapeño And Lime In Mint Mayo Recipe immediately as an evening snack or as side dish.
