### Ingredients

- 1 cup Sweet corn.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1 Whole Egg.
- 1 teaspoon Thai Red Curry Paste.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli sauce.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thai Style Curried Corn Fritters Recipe, add the corn kernels into a mixer and keep some aside for the extra crunch to your fritter.
1. Blitz the remaining corn to a smooth mixture in a mixer jar.
1. Transfer it into a bowl and add the whole corn kernels along with rest of the ingredients -coriander leaves, thai red curry paste, baking powder, egg, salt, red chilli sauce and mix well.
1. Heat a Kuzhi Paniyaram pan with a few drops of oil in each of the cavities, once it is heated up add a spoon of the mixture and cook them on either side.
1. Keep flipping them so that it will evenly browns on all the sides.
1. Serve the Thai Style Curried Corn Fritters with a Peanut Chilli Dipping Sauce and glass of Carrot Pineapple Orange Juice by the side as your evening snack.
