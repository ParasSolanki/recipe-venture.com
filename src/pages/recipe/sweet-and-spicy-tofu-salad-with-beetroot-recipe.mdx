### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1 Beetroot - diced and steamed.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few.
- 2 Tomatoes - de-seeded and finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 tablespoon Maple syrup - or honey.
- 1/2 tablespoon Balsamic Vinegar.
- 1 cup Tofu - dices.
- 1/2 tablespoon Light soy sauce.
- 1 pinch Red Chilli flakes.

### Instructions

1. To begin making this Sweet And Spicy Tofu Salad With Beetroot, we will first steam the beetroots in a steamer.
1. Cut the beets to desired sizes and place it in a steamer.
1. Steam until cooked well.
1. Once the beets are steamed, set them aside to cool completely.
1. In a salad mixing bowl combine the onions, tomatoes, the steamed beets and green chillies and keep this aside.
1. We will next prepare the dressing for the salad.
1. Mix the balsamic vinegar, maple syrup(or honey) and soy sauce in a small bowl and whisk together.
1. To prepare the salad, spread the cut tofu out on a the serving plate.
1. Scatter the steamed beetroot and onion mixture pieces around it.
1. Pour the prepared salad dressing over the vegetables, sprinkle the coriander leaves and serve.
1. Serve the Sweet And Spicy Tofu Salad with Beetroot for a light weeknight dinner along with a soup and toasted bread.
