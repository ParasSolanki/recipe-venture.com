### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1/4 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1-1/4 cup Water.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Black Gram Chainsoo Recipe, first heat a Kadhai and dry roast the dal for few minutes until you can smell the aroma.
1. Set aside.
1. Once it is cooled down, grind the dry mixture to a fine powder.
1. Heat a heavy bottomed kadhai with oil, temper with red chilli and pepper corns.
1. Add all the masalas including coriander powder, cumin powder, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, salt with 2 tablespoons water and fry for a minute.
1. Add the powdered dal, mix it up and then add water.
1. Cook on medium to low flame stirring occasionally until thickens for 20-30 minutes.
1. Once it is done, switch off the gas and serve hot.
1. Serve Uttarakhand Style Black Gram Chainsoo along with Steamed Rice and Spicy Baby Potatoes for your weekday lunch.
