### Ingredients

- 180 grams Dark chocolate.
- 1/2 cup Butter (unsalted).
- 4 Whole Eggs - at room temperature.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/2 cup Cocoa Powder - unsweetened.
- 1/2 cup Caster Sugar.
- 1/3 cup Dark rum.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Flourless Chocolate Rum Cake recipe, preheat your oven to 180 C / 350 F.
1. Grease and flour a 7 inch round tin.
1. Line the bottom with parchment (optional).
1. Chop the chocolate and butter into small pieces.
1. In the microwave or on stove top , melt together until smooth.
1. Whisk in the sugar.
1. Whisk in the eggs, one by one, followed by the vanilla extract and rum.
1. Whisk in the cocoa until smooth.
1. Pour into pan and bake for 28- 30 minutes until a toothpick inserted in the centre comes out with few moist crumbs.
1. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, unmould carefully and cool completely on a wire rack.
1. Serve Flourless Chocolate Rum Cake as a dessert after your meal or serve it during tea time to your guests with a cup of Masala Tea or Coffee.
