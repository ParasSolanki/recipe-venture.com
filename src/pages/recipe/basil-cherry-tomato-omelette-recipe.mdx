### Ingredients

- 4 Basil leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Cherry tomatoes - halved.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - to drizzle.
- 2 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Basil & Cherry Tomato Omelette Recipe, tear the fresh basil leaves roughly.
1. Next, cut the cherry tomatoes in half and keep them aside.
1. Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl.
1. Now add salt and pepper into the eggs & beat well with a fork until fully combined.
1. Next place a small non-stick frying pan or skillet on a low heat to warm up.
1. Add 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil to the pan and turn the heat up to high.
1. Add the cherry tomatoes into the heated pan and fry for 1 minute.
1. Turn the heat down to low and sprinkle over the basil leaves.
1. Carefully pour in the eggs, then tilt the pan to spread them out evenly.
1. Using a fork, swirl the eggs around the pan a little.
1. Now pour in the eggs, then tilt the pan to spread them out evenly.
1. Using a fork, swirl the eggs around the pan a little.
1. Cover it for few seconds and then turn off the heat.
