### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Guava - cubes (optional).
- 500 ml Champagne - or Sparkling wine.
- 1 cup Guava juice - chilled.
- 1/4 cup Papaya - cubes (optional).
- 1 Lemon - juiced.
- 1 cup Papaya juice - chilled.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Guava And Papaya Mimosa Drink Recipe, get all the ingredients handy and ready.
1. Pour equal quantity of guava and papaya juice into serving glasses.
1. Squeeze equal quantity of lime and orange juice into it.
1. Top the glass with sparkling wine/ champagne.
1. Garnish with few chunks of papaya/ guava if required.
1. Serve Guava And Papaya Mimosa Drink Recipe chilled, along with party appetizers like Assorted Cheese Platter Served With Fruits & Crackers and Protein Fingers Recipe.
