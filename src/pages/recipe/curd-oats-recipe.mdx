### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 3 tablespoons Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1-1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - beaten to a smooth consistency.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Curd Oats Recipe, we first need to cook the oats in plain water.
1. Bring a cup of water to a rolling boil in a saucepan, add the oats and season with salt, mix and simmer for 3 minutes until the oats is cooked.
1. Turn off the heat and keep aside to cool.
1. Take a bowl, add curd and beat the thick curd to the silky smooth texture.
1. To the curd add the finely chopped ginger and green chilli and the cooled oats.
1. Mix well to combine.
1. If the curd oats is too thick, add a little milk or water to bring it to the desired consistency.
1. To prepare the tadka, heat oil in a tadka pan on low heat, add the chana dal and mustard seeds and let it splutter.
1. Once the mustard seeds have spluttered add the curry leaves and asafoetida and once the curry leaves crackle turn off the heat.
1. Add this tadka over the prepared curd oats and mix well.
1. Season with salt if required and serve.
1. Serve this delicious Curd Oats Recipe for a light lunch or early supper along with Kachumber salad and Pisarna Manga Recipe -Instant Raw Mango Pickle to make it a complete meal.
