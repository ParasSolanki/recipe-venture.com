### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 sprig Spring Onion Greens - finely diced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Hot Dog Bread.
- 2 teaspoons Mint Leaves (Pudina) - ground into a paste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 2 teaspoon Fresh orange juice.
- 1 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - loosely torn.
- 3 Carrots (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Orange Zest (Rind).
- 4 tablespoons Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Crunchy Carrots In Hot Dog Buns, we will first make the yoghurt mint spreadIn a small bowl, whisk together  yoghurt, mint paste, lemon juice and salt to tasteTo make Orange-lemon dressingIn another big bowl whisk together the lemon juice, orange juice, orange zest, pepper, olive oil and salt to taste.
1. Set aside.
1. Now, heat a small pan on low flame.
1. Add olive oil and add the spring onions and grated carrots.
1. Toss it for about a minute  on high heat and switch off the flame.
1. Add this carrots and spring onion mix to the Orange-lemon dressing bowl and give it a good toss.
1. Now heat the same pan and toast the half cut hot dog buns and keep aside.
1. To arrange this, take the toasted hot dog, spread a little bit of the yoghurt mint mix, place a small portion on the carrots and the spring onion mix tossed in the orange-lemon dressing, place a few roughly torn coriander leaves on top.
1. Pour some more of the yoghurt mint sauce as required.
1. Serve it as the evening tea time snack or for a healthy supper with a hearty broccoli soup on a rainy evening and end it with a classic Tiramisu Recipe (A Classic and Quick Italian Dessert).
