### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.
- 1 Yellow Zucchini - thinly sliced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Rosemary - dried.
- 1 teaspoon Butter.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 2 Cheese - slices.
- 2 cloves Garlic - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried Thyme Leaves.
- 2 Baguette.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- Mustard sauce - as required.
- Lemon juice - of 1 lemon.
- 150 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cut in 1 inch size thickness.
- 3/4 cup Iceberg lettuce - roughly torn.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Grilled Sub Sandwich Recipes With Paneer & Roasted Vegetables, first let’s make the marinade.
1. In a clean large mixing bowl, add finely chopped garlic, dried oregano, dried thyme, dried rosemary, red chilli flakes, lemon juice, 1 tablespoon olive oil, salt and pepper.
1. Mix them all well.
1. Next we will roast the vegetables.
1. Heat a teaspoon of oil in a heavy bottomed pan or a wok, add the sliced vegetable, sprinkle some salt and roast them until lightly softened.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. Add paneer slices and roasted veggies to the marinade and gently toss them well, cover and keep it aside for 5 minutes.
1. Next, heat and grease the grill pan (if using cast iron grill pan, brush it with little olive oil).
1. Place the marinated paneer slices on the grill pan and grill it on high heat for 2 minutes on each side.
1. Take out the grilled paneer from the pan and keep it aside in a bowl.
1. The next step is to assemble the Sub Sandwich with Paneer & Roasted Vegetables.
1. Next, slice the submarine bread horizontally.
1. Apply butter on both the halves of the bread.
1. Toast  both the halves of the bread on same grill pan until slightly golden brown and crisp.
1. Once the bread is toasted, remove from the heat.
1. Place the toasted bread on a flat surface.
1.  Place a cheese slice on lower half of the bread and make an even layer of iceberg lettuce.
1. Place the grilled vegetables and the grilled paneer pieces on the top.
1. Top it with dollops of mustard sauce and the remaining marinade from the bowl.
1. Cover the sandwich with the other half of the bread and serve.
1. Serve Grilled Sub Sandwich With Paneer & Roasted Vegetables along with Zucchini Salad Recipe With Thai Hot Chili Dressing and a delicious Apple Almond Date Smoothie  for your weekend Brunch.
