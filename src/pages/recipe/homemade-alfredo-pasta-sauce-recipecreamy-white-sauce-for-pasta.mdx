### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Britannia Cream Cheese - if not available.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 2 cloves Garlic - crushed.
- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
- Fresh Herbs - Basil or Parsley.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Homemade Alfredo Pasta Sauce Recipe by heating a sauce pan with butter and saute the chopped garlic.
1. Once the garlic is browned lower the heat to medium, add the milk along with cream cheese, parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, mixed herbs and give it a stir.
1. Keep stirring till u get the desired Alfredo sauce consistency.
1.  Turn off the heat and use the Creamy White Sauce to make your pasta.
1. If you are using it for storing purposes, cool the sauce and pour it into an airtight glass container.
1. Store the Creamy White Sauce-Alfredo Pasta Sauce in a refrigerator for about a week and use it for making pastas and pasta salads.
