### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 3 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 3 tablespoons Water.
- 1 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose).
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 3 tablespoons Gram flour (besan).

### Instructions

1. To begin with Cabbage Besan Fry, firstly chop the cabbage into fine thin pieces and set them aside.
1. Add the salt, chilli powder and ginger-garlic paste to the cabbage.
1. Mix everything well.
1. Add besan and coriander leaves to it and give it a stir.
1. Add little water to the mixture and mix well so that the cabbage is coated with besan/chickpea powder.
1. Next, heat oil in a wide pan and add the cabbage to it.
1. Fry it well till the raw smell of the besan goes away.
1. Once the besan starts to get into little chunks, cover the lid of the pan and let it cook covered for about 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes, remove the lid and fry it on medium flame until the moisture leaves away.
1. Serve Cabbage Besan Fry with Panchmel Dal, Steamed Rice and Phulkas for a perfect meal.
