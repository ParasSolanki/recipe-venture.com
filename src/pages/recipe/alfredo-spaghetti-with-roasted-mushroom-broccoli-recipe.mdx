### Ingredients

- 4 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 1 cup Broccoli - cut into small florets.
- 1 cup Classic Alfredo Pasta Sauce.
- 1 cup Button mushrooms - quartered.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Alfredo Spaghetti with Roasted Mushroom & Broccoli Recipe by making the sauce first, so check our recipe to make the Classic Alfredo Pasta Sauce Recipe.
1. In saucepan, heat water to boil the pasta.
1. Once it start to boil add the spaghetti and cook it till it is Al Dante.
1. Then drain the water and drizzle olive oil and toss it over in order to stop cooking.
1. Heat a saucepan with oil, saute the garlic and then add the vegetables.
1. Saute till it is cooked.
1. Pour in the Pasta sauce.
1. Season it well.
1. Add milk in order to adjust the consistency of the sauce.
1. Finally add the cooked pasta and toss it over for few seconds, check for seasoning again and serve.
1. Serve Alfredo Spaghetti with Roasted Mushroom & Broccoli Recipe along with Toasted Garlic Bread.
