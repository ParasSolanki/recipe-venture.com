### Ingredients

- Ranch Sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- Lettuce leaves - as required.
- 1/2 Cup Cheese - grated.
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 2 Onion - diced.
- 1 Tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 5 Cherry tomatoes - diced.
- 2 Chicken breasts - thoroughly washed & cleaned.
- 4 Tortillas.
- 1 Cucumber - diced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken Ranch Wraps recipe, firstly marinate the chicken breast with olive oil, Italian seasoning, red chilli flakes, salt, mix well and keep it for 15 minutes.
1. Heat a grill pan on medium flame, place the chicken breast and cook for a 6-8 minutes on each side.
1. Make sure the chicken is well cooked from all sides.
1.  Let it rest for 10 minutes, then cut into small strips.
1. Heat a tawa on medium flame, cook the tortillas for a minute on each side.
1. stack them and set aside.
1. Now lay the tortilla sheet on a working surface, first arrange lettuce leaves.
1. Then add chicken pieces, cherry tomato, onion, cucumber & add ranch sauce on the top.
1. In the end, sprinkle black pepper powder & top it with shredded cheese.
1. Fold the sheet and make into a wrap.
1. Put it on a hot grill pan and cook for few minutes and serve it hot.
