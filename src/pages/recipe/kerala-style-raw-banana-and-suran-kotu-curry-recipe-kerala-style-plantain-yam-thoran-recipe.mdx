### Ingredients

-  3 cloves garlic.
-  1 green chili.
- 1/2 cup raw banana - chopped.
-  1 cup coconut - grated.
-  1/4 tsp whole black pepper.
-  1/2 teaspoon red chili powder.
-  1/2 cup black gram.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  1/2 cup suran - chopped.
-  1/2 2 tsp cumin seeds.
-  1/2 tsp mustard.
-  1 tsp oil.

### Instructions

1. To make Kerala Style Raw Banana and Suran Kotu Curry Recipe, first peel the raw banana and suran and cut them into small pieces.
1. In a saucepan, pour both of these with 1/4 tsp turmeric powder, salt and water and let it soften.
1. Cook till Keep it aside.
1. Pour black gram in a cooker with water as per use and cook it for 30 minutes.
1. Allow the pressure to drain automatically and close the cooker.
1. In a mixer grinder, add coconut, cumin, green chili, black pepper, a little water and grind them.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now add suran, raw banana, coconut paste, salt, a little water in a pan and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Add coconut in a pan and cook till it turns brown.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat oil in a tempering pan.
1. Add the mustard seeds, dry red chillies, curry leaves and cook until the mustard seeds crackle.
1. Add this tadka and brown coconut curry and mix.
1. Turn off the gas after a minute and serve.
1. Serve Kerala Style Raw Banana and Suran Kotu Curry Recipe with Kerala Beetroot Pachadi for dinner.
