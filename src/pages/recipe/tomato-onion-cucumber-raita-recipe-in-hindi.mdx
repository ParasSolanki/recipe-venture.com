### Ingredients

-  2 cucumber - peel and chop.
-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
- 2 cups Hung curd - whisk.
-  1 teaspoon cumin powder.
-  1/2 teaspoon mustard.
-  2 tomatoes - finely chopped.
-  salt - According to taste.
-  1 teaspoon oil.

### Instructions

1. To make tomato onion and cucumber raita, first cut the sari vegetables and keep them separately.
1. Now beat curd in a bowl and add salt, cumin powder, chopped vegetables and mix.
1. Now we will put a temper on it.
1. Take a Tadka Pen and add mustard and let it cook for 10 seconds.
1. Now put it in Tadka Raita, mix and serve.
1. Serve tomato onion and cucumber raita along with dal fry, gourd elder greens and phulka for the day's dinner.
