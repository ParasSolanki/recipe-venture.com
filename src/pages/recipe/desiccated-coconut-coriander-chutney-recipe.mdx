### Ingredients

- 3 Green Chillies.
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1/2 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 tablespoons Dessicated Coconut.
- 3 cloves Garlic.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Desiccated Coconut Coriander Chutney Recipe, get all the ingredients ready.
1. Add all ingredients in a mixer jar and grind it to a fine paste with the help of a water as needed.
1. Transfer this to a serving bowl.
1. Serve Desiccated Coconut Coriander Chutney Recipe as a dip with Cheese Crackers Recipe for a movie evening at home.
