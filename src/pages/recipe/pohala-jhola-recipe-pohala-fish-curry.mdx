### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 8 cloves Garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 4 Green Chillies.
- 2 Tomatoes.
- 3 tablespoon Mustard oil.
- 2 Onions - red ones.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3 cups Water.
- 300 grams Pohala fish.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pohala Jhola recipe, clean fish and give marks on it with knife.
1. Add turmeric and salt and keep aside for 5 minutes.
1. Make a paste of garlic, onion, green chili and grind the tomatoes coarsely.
1. Heat oil in a cast iron pan and fry fish on both side until they are done.
1. It will take 3 to 5 minute.
1. Take them out, add onion masala paste in the same pan and fry for a minute.
1. Add tomato paste, salt and fry for another 2 minutes until it's done.
1. Add water and let it boil for 2 minutes more.
1. Add cumin powder and mix well.
1. Immediately add fried fishes to it and cook for 2 minute at low flame.
1. After 2 minutes, switch off the stove and it is ready to serve.
1. Serve Pohala Jhola with hot steamed rice for a delicious weekday lunch or dinner.
