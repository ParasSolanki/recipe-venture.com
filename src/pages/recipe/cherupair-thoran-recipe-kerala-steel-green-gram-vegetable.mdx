### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Green Moong Dal (Whole).
- 3 Green Chillies.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 2 teaspoons Coconut Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped for garnish.
- Curry leaves - few.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cherupayar Thoran Recipe, wash the green gram and pressure cook it for 3 whistles by adding enough water and salt.
1. Crush garlic and green chilli in a mortar pestle.
1. Mix it with the grated coconut.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat a heavy bottomed pan with coconut oil.
1. Once he oil is hot, add mustard seeds and urad dal.
1. Once they splutter add cumin seeds, dry red chilli and curry leaves.
1. Give a quick fry.
1. Add onion, turmeric and salt in the pan.
1. Saute the mixture till the onion becomes soft and translucent.
1. Add the coconut mixture and saut√© it well till raw smell goes.
1. Add the cooked green gram and mix it well.
1. Keep on the flame on low for 2-3 minutes.
1. Switch off and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve Cherupayar Thoran hot.
1. Serve Cherupayar Thoran Recipe along with Keerai Sambar, Steamed Rice, Elai Vadam and Beetroot Pachadi for a weekday meal.
