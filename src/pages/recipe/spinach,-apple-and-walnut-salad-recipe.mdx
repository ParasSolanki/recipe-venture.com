### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 Apple - cored and cut into thin slices.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Dijon Mustard.
- 4 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower seeds.
- 1/2 cup Walnuts - roughly chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Honey.
- 3 cups Baby spinach.
- 1/2 cup Raisins.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spinach, Apple and Walnut Salad recipe, we will firstly make the Honey Mustard dressing.
1. In a mason jar or any bottle with a tight fitting lid, add all the ingredients of the dressing.
1. Close the lid tightly and shake the bottle to blend the dressing well.
1. The next step is to make a salad.
1. In a large salad bowl, toss together spinach, walnuts, raisins and apples slices.
1. Drizzle with the honey-mustard dressing, toss to coat evenly and serve immediately.
1. Serve Spinach, Apple and Walnut Salad along with Minestrone Soup for a weekday dinner.
