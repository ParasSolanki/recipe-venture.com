### Ingredients

- 6 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 teaspoon Poppy seeds.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 6 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1/2 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 Star anise.
- 1 teaspoons Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 inch Mace (Javitri).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chettinad Masala Powder Recipe, heat a skillet on low-medium, add the whole spices - coriander seeds, cumin seeds, red chillies, carom seeds, star anise, cardamom , cinnamon, cloves, fennel seeds, peppercorns, fenugreek seeds, poppy seeds, mace until fragrant.
1. This will take about 8-10 minutes.
1. Turn off the flame.
1. Once the roasted Chettinad masala powder ingredients has cooled down to room temperature, transfer to a mixer-jar and grind until you get a fine powder.
1. Store the Chettinad masala powder in an airtight container and can be used for upto 3-4 months.
