### Ingredients

-  1 tomato - chopped.
- 1 cup chana dal.
-  1 onion - finely chopped.
-  1 tsp turmeric powder.
-  1 tsp red Chili powder.
-  1/4 tsp asafoetida.
-  1 tbsp ghee.
-  1 tsp cumin seeds.
-  2 dry red chillies.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 tsp green coriander.
-  1 cup fenugreek - cut into pieces.

### Instructions

1. To make chana methi dal recipe, first soak chana dal in 3 cups of water for 30 minutes.
1. Now wash and chop the fenugreek with water.
1. Heat the oil in a pan.
1. Add fenugreek to it and cook till the fenugreek becomes soft.
1. After the fenugreek becomes soft, turn off the gas and keep it aside.
1. Now heat the oil in a pressure cooker.
1. Add asafetida to it and cook it for 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add onions and cook till they become soft.
1. After the onion becomes soft, add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt along with tomatoes and let it cook.
1. Now add chana dal with water.
1. Turn off the cooker and cook till 6 to 8 cities come.
1. Let the pressure drain automatically.
1. Open the cooker, mash the lentils and add fenugreek to it.
1. Mix and take out in a bowl.
1. Now for tempering, heat the ghee in a tempering pan.
1. Add cumin seeds, dry red chilies and let it cook for 15 seconds.
1. Add the tadka to the dal and mix.
1. Serve Chana Methi Dal Recipe along with Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita, Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita and Phulka for a day meal.
