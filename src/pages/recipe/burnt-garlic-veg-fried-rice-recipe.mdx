### Ingredients

- 3 sprig Spring Onion Greens - for garnish.
- 2 Carrot (Gajjar) - diced.
- 2 tablespoons Red Chilli sauce.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Green bell peppers - diced.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - diced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chilli vinegar.
- 1 cup Cooked rice.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Green Chilli Sauce.
- 9 cloves Garlic - diced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Burnt Garlic Veg Fried Rice Recipe, pressure cook the rice in a pressure cooker with  2 cups of water for about 2 whistle and switch it off.
1. Heat a wok with oil, add garlic, ginger and allow it to soften.
1. Add onions and saute till they turn translucent.
1. Add carrot and cook for about 10 minutes till the carrot soften.
1. Add all the bell peppers and saute till they become soft.
1. Add all the Chinese sauces along with salt and pepper.
1. Mix well, add your cooked rice into the wok and stir fry them till the rice is well coated.
1. Check for salt and sprinkle with spring onions and give one last toss.
1. In a small pan, heat the oil and add the diced garlic.
1. Fry till they turn deep brown in color.
1. Pour half the burnt garlic in the rice and mix well.
1. Just before serving, garnish with the rest of burnt garlic and spring onions.
1. Serve the Burnt Garlic Veg Fried Rice Recipe along Chinese chicken Manchurian and salad to enjoy your Sunday lunch.
