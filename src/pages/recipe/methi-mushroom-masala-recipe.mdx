### Ingredients

- 1/4 teaspoon Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- 3/4 cup Lukewarm Water.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1 tablespoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 200 grams Button mushrooms.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger - paste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 teaspoon Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1/4 cup Homemade tomato puree.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Methi Mushroom Masala Recipe, firstly wash the mushrooms, in a bowl mix with curd, red chilli powder and ginger paste.
1. Marinate for 5 minutes.
1. Soak the leaves in water for 5 minutes, strain and set aside.
1. Heat a small wok with oil, fry the mushrooms and remove after 2 minutes.
1. In the same pan, add bay leaf, kalonji and ginger and garlic paste.
1. Fry for 1 minute.
1. Fry the onions for few minutes or till they become soft.
1. Next, add tomato puree, red chilli powder and turmeric powder.
1. Let it cook for about a minute.
1. After a minute, add warm water, salt and garam masala powder.
1. Let it boil to a semi thick consistency, now add the mushrooms and soaked and strained dry methi leaves.
1. Serve hot immediately with a dash of lime juice and red chilli on top.
1. Serve Methi Mushroom Masala Recipe along with Palak Raita and Whole Wheat Lachha Parathas for weekly dinner.
