### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1 tablespoon Coconut milk - for garnishing.
- 1 tablespoon Date syrup.
- 1 Dried Figs - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 tablespoon Pistachios - sliced.
- 3 Strawberries - sliced.
- 1-1/2 cups Cauliflower (gobi) - cut into florets.
- 3 teaspoons Cocoa Powder.
- 1 tablespoon Walnuts - chopped.
- 1 cup Coconut milk.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Grain Free Chocolate Porridge recipe, add 1 cup of coconut milk and cauliflower florets to simmer in a saucepan.
1. Cook them for 10 minutes or until they become tender.
1. Using an immersion blender, blitz the cauliflower to a grainy consistency.
1. Alternatively, you can even cool it completely and use the regular blender.
1. Empty the contents into a mixing bowl and add in the cocoa powder, date syrup, salt, cinnamon and mix everything until it is combined well.
1. Once done, serve it in a bowl and top it with fruit and nuts and the reserved coconut milk on top.
1. Serve Grain Free Chocolate Porridge in the morning to start your day in a healthy and delicious way.
