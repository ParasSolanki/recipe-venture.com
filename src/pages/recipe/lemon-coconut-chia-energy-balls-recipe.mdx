### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Chia Seeds.
- 1/2 Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Dates - pitted.
- 1/2 cup Badam (Almond).
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - unsweetened and shredded  + Â½ cup shredded coconut (for dusting the balls).
- 1/2 teaspoon Lemon zest.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lemon Coconut Chia Energy Balls recipe, add dates in the food processor and process them to break into small pieces.
1. Add almonds, chia seeds, lemon juice and process them until they are combined togetherAdd lemon zest and process them until they come together as a dough.
1. Scoop using a spoon and roll them into balls.
1. Dust the rolled balls with the shredded coconut.
1. Place in an airtight container and store in refrigerator for about a month.
1. Serve Lemon Coconut Chia Energy Balls as a tea time snack or pack it in your kids snack box.
