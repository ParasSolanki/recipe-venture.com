### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- Maple syrup - or honey as needed.
- 1/4 cup Coconut Sugar - raw sugar.
- 2/3 cup Soy milk - cow's milk+ 2 tablespoons if needed.
- 2 teaspoons Baking powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 6 tablespoons Soy milk.
- 2 tablespoons Chia Seeds.
- 1/4 cup Coconut Oil - melted.
- 2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract.
- Fresh fruits - to serve as needed (fruit of your choice).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Healthy Oats Waffles recipe, In a small bowl add chia seeds and 6 tablespoons of soy milk, mix well and set aside for 10 minutes to form a chia egg.
1. In a blender, add oats and grind it to a flour like consistency.
1. In a jug add melted coconut oil, chia egg and soy milk and mix well.
1. In a mixing bowl add oat flour, baking powder, salt, coconut sugar and mix well.
1. To this pour the mixed wet ingredients and mix well until combined.
1. Add the extra 2 tablespoons of milk if the mixture is too thick.
1. Now heat the waffle iron, grease it with some coconut oil and make waffles.
1. Serve it on a bed of your favourite fruit and drizzle with maple syrup or honey.
1. Serve Healthy Oat Waffles along with Maple Syrup, Cold Coffee Smoothie or a hot cup of Masala Tea.
