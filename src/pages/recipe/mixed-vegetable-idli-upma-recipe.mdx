### Ingredients

- 6 Curry leaves - finely chopped.
- 10 Idli - cut into diced.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Green beans (French Beans) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - a small bunch finely chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 10 Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - finely chopped (or 1 large onion).
- 1 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 3 Green Chillies - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mixed Vegetable Idli Upma Recipe, heat a teaspoon of oil in a heavy bottomed pan; add in the mustard seeds and allow it to crackle for a few seconds.
1. Then add in the split urad dal and allow it to roast and turn a light brown in color.
1. Once the dal is roasted we will add in the chopped onions, then the ginger, green chillies and curry leaves.
1. Saute on medium heat until the onions are transparent.
1. Add the carrots, capsicum and beans - sprinkle salt and roast the vegetables on medium heat.
1. You can optional sprinkle some water and cover the pan and steam cook the vegetables.
1. Once tender we will add in the turmeric powder and the cut idlis and some salt to taste.
1. Sprinkle a little water so the idlis don't get too dry, you want them to be moist and flavorful when the Mixed Vegetable Idli Upma is done.
1. Stir all the ingredients well.
1. Turn the heat to low, cover the pan and allow the Mixed Vegetable Idli Upma to simmer for about 3 to 4 minutes.
1. After 3 to 4 minutes, add in the chopped mint leaves and stir.
1. Transfer the Mixed Vegetable Idli Upma to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve the delicious Idli Upma along with Roasted Garlic Tomato Chutney Recipe and top of this breakfast Kumbakonam Filter Coffee Recipe.
