### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 500 grams Baby Potatoes.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1-1/2 tablespoons Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- Sunflower Oil - as needed.
- 1 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chatpate Aloo Recipe With Sesame Seeds, take all the baby potatoes in a saucepan with water and boil well till done.
1. You can also choose to pressure cook them for just one whistle in sufficient water and run under cold water for immediate release of pressure.
1. Once the potatoes are cooked al dente, peel them all and put aside.
1. Now, in another small bowl add all the spices -salt, red chili powder, turmeric powder, jeera powder, amchur powder and garam masala powder and mix them well with a spoon, and keep aside.
1. In a kadai, lightly roast sesame seeds and keep them aside.
1. In the same kadai, heat 2-3 tablespoons of vegetable oil into it for frying the boiled baby potatoes.
1. Once the oil is hot enough, add potatoes gently and fry them well.
1. You need to cook them till they get a nice light brown color.
1. Once the potatoes are cooked well, keep them aside on a plate and pat with tissue paper to absorb excess oil and let it cool down for 5 minutes.
1. Take the fried potatoes in a bowl again and put the whole masala into the bowl and mix everything thoroughly together, taking care that the potatoes are firm and not mushy.
1. Cooking the potatoes help the prepared masala to coat well which enhances the taste .
1. Garnish it with few freshly chopped coriander leaves and Serve it like that.
1. Serve Chatpate Aloo Recipe With Sesame Seeds along with Punjabi Dal Tadka, Butter Naan Recipe and end your meal with a Refreshing Mint Lassi Recipe.
