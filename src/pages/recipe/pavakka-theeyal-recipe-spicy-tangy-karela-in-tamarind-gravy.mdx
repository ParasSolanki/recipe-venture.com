### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Onions.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 8 Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 6 Dry Red Chillies.
- 5 Curry leaves.
- 1 cup Fresh coconut.
- 1/2 cups Water.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1/4 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1 tablespoon Coconut Oil.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 3 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds) - a pinch.
- 2 Karela (Bitter Gourd/ Pavakkai).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pavakka Theeyal recipe, firstly cut the bitter gourds into semi circles and soak them in required salt water.
1. After 15 minutes, squeeze the bitter gourds and keep it aside.
1. The next step is to make the masala for Bitter Gourd curry.
1. Add coconut, coriander seeds and dry red chillies into a heavy bottomed pan and dry roast till the coconut turns dark brown.
1. Then reduce the flame and add turmeric, fenugreek seeds, peppercorns and curry leaves.
1. Let it cook for 1 more minute.
1. After a minute, add coconut oil and switch off the heat.
1. Add this gravy mixture into a grinder, add a little water and grind it into a smooth paste.
1. Heat some more oil the same pan and add the cut bitter gourd.
1. Stir fry them till the bitter gourd roundels become crispy.
1. Once the bitter gourd becomes crispy, add onions and cook them till they turn soft and brown.
1. Add the grounded curry paste, tamarind juice, jaggery and 1-1/2 cups water.
1. Mix everything properly.
1. Add salt in the curry and let it cook for about 5 to 6 minutes.
1. Once it is ready, switch off the heat.
1. Next, to make the tadka for curry, heat oil in a tadka pan.
1. When the oil is hot, add mustard seeds, curry leaves and dry red chillies.
1. Let it cook for about 30 seconds and add it to the curry.
1. Give it a mix and it is ready to be served.
1. Serve Pavakka Theeyal along with Palak Raita and Phulka for a weekday lunch with your family.
1. You can also pack it in your Lunch box.
1. You could also serve it along with Steamed rice, Snake Gourd Thoran Recipe (Turai Thoran) and drink a glass of Spiced Butter Milk With Coriander And Ginger Recipe with the meal.
