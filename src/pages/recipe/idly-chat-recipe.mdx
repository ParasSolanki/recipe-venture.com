### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- 1 Lemon - juiced.
- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Idli Dosa Podi (Milagai Powder).
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black Salt (Kala Namak).
- 20 Idli - mini.
- 1/2 cup Sev.
- 1/4 cup Sweet Chutney (Date & Tamarind).
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Idli Chaat Recipe, rub a little chutney powder on both sides of the mini idlis and keep them aside.
1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a shallow pan.
1. Place the idlis on the pan and shallow fry for about 30 seconds from both the sides.
1. Remove from pan and arrange it on a serving plate.
1. Add about 1 teaspoon thick curd on each idli pieces and sprinkle finely chopped onions and sev on the top.
1. Drizzle about 1 to 2 teaspoon of dhaniya pudina chutney and date tamarind chutney on top of the idlis.
1. Once done garnish with finely chopped coriander leaves on the top.
1. Sprinkle a little black salt and chaat masala powder on the idli chaat and squeeze a little lime juice all over and serve.
1. Serve Idli Chaat as a tea time snack with a hot cup of Masala Chai  or  South Indian Filter Coffee .
