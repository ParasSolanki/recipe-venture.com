### Ingredients

- 4 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 teaspoon Tomato - paste.
- 11/2 cup Water.
- 5 teaspoon Ginger - paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 5 teaspoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 700 grams Lamb ribs.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 6 Onion - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 3 teaspoon Fresh cream.
- 3 Tomatoes.
- 3 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - Few.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 10 Garlic.

### Instructions

1. To make Hyderabadi Laziz Lamb Handi, first marinate the lamb ribs with salt, kashmiri, chilli powder, half ginger paste, curd and keep for 30 minutes.
1. Heat a copper Handi with ghee and garlic for 1 minute.
1. Add the meat and cook it for about 5 minutes on high flame.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Add onion, ginger paste, bay leaf, whole garam masala, and fry till half caramelized.
1. Now add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, salt, tomato, tomato paste and cook for about 10 minutes.
1. After 10 minutes, add the lamb meat and water.
1. Cover and cook until the meat is fully cooked.
1. Extra dry the gravy water if more than one-fourth water is left.
1. Check for salt.
1. Add garam masala powder, lime juice, cream and coriander leaves for garnish.
1. The roasted meat brings out the flavor of fat and that contributes to the satisfy along with Tawa Paratha or Jeera Rice for a weekend lunch or dinner.
