### Ingredients

- 3 teaspoons Baking powder.
- 3 Green Chillies - or Jalapenos chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoons Sugar.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 Whole Egg - whisked.
- 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 cup Buttermilk.
- 1 tablespoon Butter - + 2 more tablespoons (or sunflower oil).
- 1/2 cup Sweet corn.
- 1 cup Makki Ka Atta (Yellow Corn Meal Flour) - or corn flour.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Mexican Cornbread Recipe, first preheat the oven to 180 degree Celsius.
1. Brush a 9 inch non-stick baking pan with 1 tablespoon oil or melted butter.
1. In a mixing bowl, add the flours, baking powder, chopped chillies, onion, salt and sugar.
1. In another bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, 2 tablespoon oil or butter, sweet corn and egg.
1. Mix the dry ingredients with the wet till fully incorporated and a thick consistency is obtained.
1. Pour in the prepared pan and bake for about 20-22 minutes (timing may change depending on the pan size and oven efficiency) till the bottom is brown and a tester inserted comes out clean.
1. Serve Mexican Cornbread Recipe with a Mexican menu of Mexican Black Bean Corn Soup and Baked Mexican Rice and end the meal with dessert like Creme Caramel Flan.
