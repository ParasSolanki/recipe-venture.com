### Ingredients

- 4 Strawberries - cut into round slices.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 4 Dried raspberries - chopped.
- 4 Kiwi - cut into round slices.
- 6-7 Choco Thins.
- 2 tablespoons Sugar.
- 200 grams Mascarpone Cheese.
- 2 tablespoons Honey.
- 1 Ripe Bananas - cut into round slices.
- 6-7 Blueberries - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mascarpone Fruit Dessert recipe, in a large bowl, add Mascarpone cheese and whisk using an electric whisk or hand whisk or blender.
1. Whisk till we get stiff peaks.
1. Now, add honey and vanilla extract and whisk again for a couple of minutes.
1. Place a cookie cutter on a plate and start layering the fruits of your choice.
1. Add whipped mascarpone cheese in every alternate layers.
1. Stop once you have reached the top of cookie cutter else the whipped mixture would fall.
1. Place this plate in the freezer to set for 3 to 4 hours.
1. Once the whipped cheese dessert has set, gently remove the cookie cutter from the top.
1. Decorate it with cut fruits and choc√≥ thins on the top and sides.
1. Serve Mascarpone Fruit Dessert as a sweet dish after your delicious meals or you can even serve this to your sudden guests.
