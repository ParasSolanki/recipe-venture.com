### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- Sev - finer one.
- 1 Lemon - squeezed.
- 3 tablespoons Del Monte Mint Mayo.
- 25 Papdi puris.
- Black Salt (Kala Namak) - to taste.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Anardana Powder (Pomegranate Seed Powder).
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Party Sev Puri Recipe Filled With Potato & Pomegranate Mint Mayo, first prep all the ingredients and keep them ready.
1. In a large mixing bowl, add potatoes, pomegranates, chili, Del Monte Mint Mayo, onion, half of the coriander leaves, lemon juice and black salt.
1. Mix them all very well.
1. Check the salt and seasoning and adjust according to taste.
1. Arrange 5 to papdi puris on each serving plate, place a spoonful of mixture on the papdi puris and top it with sev.
1.  Garnish with coriander leaves and serve immediately.
1. Make the delicious Party Sev Puri Recipe Filled With Potato & Pomegranate Mint Mayo and serve it at a chaat party along with Moong Sprouts Usal , Hariyali Pav Bhaji and Orange Mocktail  or as a evening tea time snack.
