### Ingredients

- 2-1/2 cups Coconut milk.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few sprigs.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1/4 cup Roasted Gram Dal (Pottukadalai).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 8 Green Chillies - adjustable.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Curry leaves - few.

### Instructions

1. Start preparing Kobbari Chaaru Recipe (Coconut Milk & Dal Sambar) by preparing coconut milk.
1. Scrape the white flesh of half of the freshly opened coconut and grind in a blender using around 2 cups of water.
1. Strain with a sieve to extract coconut milk.
1. Grind the roasted chana dal along with green chillies into a fine paste in a Mixer adding water as required and set aside until required.
1. Now heat a Kadai with oil.
1. Add mustard, cumin seeds and curry leaves.
1. Let them splutter for few seconds.
1. Add turmeric, water, coconut milk, salt and bring this to boil.
1. As soon as it starts boiling, stir in the ground dal paste and mix well.
1. Make sure that there are no lumps, by stirring while you add the paste.
1. Bring the mixture to boil and at this stage, it resembles kadhi.
1. Lastly, sprinkle with coriander leaves and turn off the heat.
1. Scrumptious Kobbari Chaaru Recipe (Coconut Milk & Dal Sambar) is ready to be served for lunch with steamed rice and fryum (Akki Peni sandige) or just Phulka Recipe (Roti/Chapati) - Puffed Indian Bread.
