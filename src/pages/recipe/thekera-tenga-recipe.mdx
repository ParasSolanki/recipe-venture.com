### Ingredients

- 4 Green Chillies - roughly chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoons Lemon juice - or 3-4 thekera pieces soaked in 1/4 cup of water.
- 1 teaspoon Panch Phoran Masala.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard oil.
- 4 Potatoes (Aloo) - thinly sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Rice flour - or 1 mashed potato.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thekera Tenga recipe, Heat a wide pan with oil and shallow fry the potato pieces and set them aside.
1. Heat oil and temper with ingredients given 'for tempering'.
1. Add 1-2 cups of water, salt and turmeric powder .
1. Mix rice flour with one tablespoon of water (or 1 mashed potato) and add it to the water mixture.
1. When the mixture starts to thicken, add lemon juice ( or thekera water ) and  the roasted potatoes.
1. Cook over low flame for another 4-5 minutes and switch off.
1.  Garnish with cilantro leaves and serve it along with flatbreads or rice.
1. Serve Thekera Tenga along with Steamed Rice for a weekday dinner.
