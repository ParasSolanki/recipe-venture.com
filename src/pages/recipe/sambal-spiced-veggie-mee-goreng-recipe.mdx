### Ingredients

- 10 Dry Red Chillies - (5 Byadgi :5 Guntur).
- 1 Onion - finely sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Sugar.
- 1 Stalk Lemongrass - thinly sliced.
- 2 teaspoons Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3 Baby corn - julienned.
- 6 Green Chillies - (remove seeds for a milder sambal).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - julienned.
- 200 grams Wheat Noodles.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 5 cloves Garlic - sliced.
- 2 tablespoons Sambal paste.
- 1/2 cup Tamarind Water.
- Sunflower Oil - as needed.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely julienned.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sambal Spiced Veggie Mee Goreng Recipe, start with making the Sambal paste first.
1. Blend onions, chili's, garlic, lemongrass, tamarind and dried chilies into a paste, using a mixer.
1. Add about 3 teaspoon oil in a pan.
1. Once the oil is hot, add the paste and saut√© until aromatic.
1. Now add the turmeric powder and saute on low heat for about 10-15 minutes until the raw smell goes.
1. Add tamarind paste, salt and sugar and cook till the oil separates from the pan and floats to the top.
1. To Make Sambal Spiced Veggie Mee GorengCook noodles as per packet instructions and drain the water.
1. Allow it to cool.
1. Heat oil  in a wide bottomed pan/ wok, add onions and saute until they turn translucent.
1. Then add the carrots, baby corn and capsicum and fry till they turn slightly soft.
1. Add the sambal paste and saute for 2-3 minutes and then add salt, noodles.
1. Mix well to combine.
1. Turn off the heat and transfer it to a bowl.
1. Enjoy Sambal Spiced Veggie Mee Goreng Recipe while it is hot! Serve Sambal Spiced Veggie Mee Goreng Recipe for dinner along with Thai Grilled Pineapple Recipe.
