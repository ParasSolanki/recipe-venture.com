### Ingredients

-  3 curry garlic - 1 teaspoon cardamom Powder.
-  1/4 cup cream - (Amul cream is used here).
-  1 onion - chopped.
-  1 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin powder.
-  2 cups tomato puree.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 tablespoon Kasuri methi.
-  1 inch ginger.
-  2 teaspoons honey.
-  1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder.
- 250 g Paneer - home made.
-  Oil - to cook.
-  1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To make Paneer Makhani, first prepare tomato puree to make Paneer Makhani.
1. Now make a paste of onion, garlic and ginger in a grinder.
1. When oil is heated in a pan, fry the onion paste till it is cooked well.
1. Now add turmeric powder, cumin powder, cardamom powder, garam masala and fry it for 2 minutes.
1. Now when cooked well, put tomato puree or tomato paste in it and cook till it comes to a boil.
1. When onion and tomato paste and other spices are cooked, add cream, red chili powder, salt, kasoori methi and paneer to it.
1. Let it cook for 3 to 5 minutes.
1. When cooked, taste the spices and salt according to your taste.
1. Now take out Paneer Makhani in a bowl for survey.
1. You can serve this spicy Swadistha Paneer Makhani with Naan or Kulcha and Burani Raita.
