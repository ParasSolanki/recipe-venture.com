### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 Stalk Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 400 grams Button mushrooms.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar) - steamed.
- 5 Cashew nuts.
- 2 cups Homemade tomato puree.
- 1 cup Soy Chunks (Nuggets).
- 1 tablespoon Poppy seeds.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dhingri Matar Recipe, we will first make a paste of the ginger and garlic, adding a dash of water if required.
1. Keep asideMake a paste of poppy seeds and cashew nuts, adding just enough water to make a paste.
1. Keep aside.
1. Soak the soy chunks in warm water for about 15 minutes and keep aside.
1. Heat the oil in a pan; add the ginger and garlic paste and saute until well absorbed into the oil and turned light golden brown.
1. Add the tomato puree and turmeric powder and saute for a few minutes.
1. Stir in the peas, soy chunks, mushrooms and simmer until the mushrooms are tender.
1. Add the garam masala, cashew nut-poppy paste, salt, chilli powder.
1. Simmer for about 5 minutes and bring the Dhingri Matar to a quick boil.
1. Check the salt and spice levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Turn off the heat and stir in the chopped coriander leaves.
