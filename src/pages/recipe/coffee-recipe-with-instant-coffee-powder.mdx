### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 teaspoons Instant Coffee Powder - (adjust the strength to desired taste).
- 2 cups Milk.
- 2 teaspoons Sugar - (or to taste).
- 2 tablespoons Fresh cream - (optional).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Coffee Recipe With Instant Coffee Powder, get all the ingredients ready.
1. Use of cream is optional.
1. But adds a definite creamy taste and texture to the coffee if used.
1. Pour milk into two microwave safe serving cups, and add sugar.
1. Microwave on full powder for a minute.
1. Take the cups out, stir well and microwave again for a blast of 30 seconds if you find the coffee is still colder.
1. Once it is hot, remove the cups from the microwave and add the instant coffee powder and give a nice stir till it dissolves.
1. Once done, top it up with cream and give a stir to combine.
1. Serve Coffee Recipe With Instant Coffee Powder along with healthy coffee time snacks like Methi Khakra Recipe (Gujarati Savory Crispy Flat Bread), Indian Spicy Masala Cookie Recipe (Khara Biscuit), Phool Makhana (Healthy Roasted Lotus Seeds Snack) or more.
