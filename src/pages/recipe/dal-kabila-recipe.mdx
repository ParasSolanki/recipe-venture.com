### Ingredients

-  3 long.
-  1 cup white urad dal.
-  1/2 inch ginger - finely chopped.
-  1/4 tsp red chilli powder.
-  1 onion - finely chopped.
-  1 tsp coriander powder.
-  1 tsp whole pepper - crush it.
-  1 tbsp ghee.
-  1 green chili - chopped.
-  3 dry red chillies.
-  1 inch cinnamon.
-  1 tomato - Finely chopped.
-  1/4 tsp turmeric powder.
-  1 tsp cumin seeds.
-  3 sprig green coriander - chopped Take 3 cups of water.
- 1 tsp ghee.
-  1/4 tsp garam masala powder.
-  3 long garlic - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make dal kabila recipe, first wash the urad dal thoroughly and soak it for 2 hours.
1. After 2 hours, heat the ghee in a pressure cooker.
1. Add dry red chilli, cinnamon, long and roast it on low heat for 1 minute.
1. Now add onions, green chilies and cook the onions to become soft.
1. Add tomatoes, lala chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder and cook it for 2 minutes.
1. Now add urad dal, 3 cups of water and close the cooker.
1. Cook till 5 to 6 come to the city.
1. Turn off the gas and let the pressure come out automatically, open the cooker and take out in a bowl.
1. For grating, heat the ghee in a pan.
1. Add cumin, black pepper and cook until the cumin seeds crackle.
1. Add it to the lentils and mix.
1. Serve Serve dal kabila recipe with spinach ragi and oats vet thepla and khamang cucumber for dinner.
