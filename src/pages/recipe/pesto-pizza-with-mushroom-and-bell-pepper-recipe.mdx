### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns - coarsely pounded.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Walnuts - toasted.
- 1 cup Button mushrooms - sliced.
- 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese - grated.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese - grated.
- 1 Whole Wheat Pizza Dough - recipe.
- 250 grams Basil leaves - with stems removed.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pesto Pizza With Mushroom and Bell Peppers Recipe, make the pizza dough ready and keep it by following our instructions in the recipe link.
1. To make the basil pesto, we will first have to grind all the ingredients together.
1. Place the walnuts, garlic, basil leaves, salt, and pepper in the food processor and churn until coarse.
1.  With the processor running, slowly pour the olive oil into the bowl through the feed tube and process until the pesto is thoroughly pureed.
1. Add the Parmesan and puree it for a minute.
1. Keep it aside as you move on to saute the mushrooms.
1. Heat a sauce pan with oil, add mushroom and saute them until it softens and all the water is evaporated.
1. To make the pizza, roll the dough using a rolling pin to form a circle, you can roll it as thin you want to get a thin crust or you can keep it thick as well.
1. After rolling, dust some flour on to the pan, carefully lift the rolled dough using both your hands and place it on the pan.
1. Spread the pesto sauce evenly over the dough, add all the toppings including mushroom, bell pepper and top it with cheddar cheese.
1. Put the pizza in the oven and bake them for about 25 minutes until the crust is browned a little.
1. Serve the Pesto Pizza With Mushroom and Bell Peppers Recipe along with a Cream of Celery Soup for dinner.
1. In the end, serve Summer Fruit Tart to end the meal.
