### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Water - as required.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 250 grams Bottle gourd (lauki) - grated.
- 2 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 2 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 2 Onion - paste.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1/2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Aal Ke Kofte recipe, firstly we will begin with preparing the koftas.
1. Mix all the ingredients mentioned under ‘for koftas’ in a mixing bowl.
1. Mix them properly.
1. Add a little water to bring the mixture together.
1. Once it is done, make round shaped pakoras out of it and deep fry them in a deep frying pan.
1. When they turn brown from both the sides, take them out to on a kitchen towel to absorb the extra oil.
1. Follow the same process for the reaming mixture and keep them aside.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan and add cloves, badi elaichi, tej patta and dal chini.
1. After a minute add the ginger garlic paste and cook it tell the raw smell goes from the mixture.
1. Once it is done add the onion paste and let it cook for 5 to 8 minutes.
1. After 5 to 8 minutes, add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder and the required salt.
1. Let it cook for 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes, bring the flame to low and add the curd.
1. Mix well and add the required amount of water.
1. Mix well and let it cook till the gravy gets a little thick.
1. Once it is done, add the fried kofta and let it cook for 2 to 3 minutes more.
1. After 2 to 3 minutes, switch off the stove and garnish it with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve Aal Ke Kofte with Lauki Raita and Tawa Parathas for a perfect weekday meal.
