### Ingredients

- 4 Strawberries - other fruits are also optional.
- 4 Badam (Almond) - slivered .
- 1 cup Mango (Ripe).
- 1 Ripe Bananas.
- 10 Cashew nuts - soaked for 30 minutes.
- 2 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 5 Dates.
- 2 teaspoons Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Buckwheat Flour (Kuttu Ka Atta) - kuttu ka dalia.
- 3/4 cup Milk - almond milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mango Buckwheat Porridge recipe, wash the buckwheat groats thoroughly using a mesh sieve as they are very slimy after soaking.
1. Make sure that water runs clear free from any slime after washing.
1. Drain the excess water and add this to a blender/mixer jar.
1. Add all the other ingredients like the almond milk, lemon juice, cashew nuts, ripe bananas, dates and mangoes.
1. Blend till smooth and and few tablespoons more milk if you need a thinner porridge, serve in bowls.
1. Garnish Mango Buckwheat Porridge with strawberry, badam and mint leaves and serve.
1. Make Mango Buckwheat Porridge while you are holding a fast or a breakfast powering you for the rest of the day.
1. You can also accompany it with Bermuda Grass Ginger & Pepper Juice or Beetroot, Amla And Pudina Juice Recipe.
