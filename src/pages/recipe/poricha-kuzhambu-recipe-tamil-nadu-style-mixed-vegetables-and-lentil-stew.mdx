### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Green beans (French Beans) - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/2 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1/2 cup Cauliflower (gobi) - cut into small florets.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/2 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal) - cooked.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Rice - soaked.
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Poricha Kuzhambu, wash and soak the toor dal for 30 minutes and then pressure cook using a cooker for 3 whistles and keep aside.
1. Make a smooth paste of grated coconut, soaked raw rice, cumin seeds and red chillies using a hand blender and keep aside.
1. Now take a big wok, add all the vegetables, 1/2 glass of water, turmeric powder, salt and cook until the vegetables are well cooked in medium flame.
1. Add the cooked dal to the vegetables, water to your required consistency and bring it to a rolling boil.
1. Now, add the ground spice mix, season with salt and let it simmer for 10 minutes to thicken and switch off the flame.
1. Heat a small tadka pan on medium heat, add the mustard seeds and urad dal and let it crackle.
1. Add the curry leaves and let it splutter and pour this tadka over the Poricha Kuzhambu.
1. Serve the Poricha Kuzhambu along with steamed rice, Manathakkali Kai Vathal Kuzhambu, Carrot and Beans Poriyal Recipe and potato roast for a full course Tamil Nadu style lunch menu on the weekend.
