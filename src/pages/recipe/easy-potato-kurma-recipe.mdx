### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 3 Potato (Aloo).
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 2 Tomatoes.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Easy Potato Kurma Recipe, get ready with all the ingredients.
1. Pressure cook potatoes for two whistles; once boiled, remove skin and cut them into cubes; Keep aside.
1. Grind all the ingredients mentioned under "To Grind" and ground them into a fine paste in a mixer.
1. Heat oil in a medium frying pan.
1. Add cinnamon, cloves, bay leaf and finely chopped onion.
1. Cook until they become transparent.
1. Add ginger garlic paste and saut√© for few minutesAdd chopped tomatoes and cook until they become sauce.
1. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder and cook until the spice powders are blended well with the onion-tomato mixtureAdd cubed potatoes and toss them well with the spicy mixture in the pan.
1. Add the grounded coconut paste and a cup of water.
1. Cook the kurma until the potatoes are absorbed in the spices and required amount of consistency is attained.
1. Prepare this easy potato kurma along with Whole Wheat Lachha Paratha and delight everyone in the family with this delicious curry.
