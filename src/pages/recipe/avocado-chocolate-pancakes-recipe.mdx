### Ingredients

- Ghee - as required.
- 3 tablespoons Honey.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 Avocado.
- 1/2 Whole Wheat Flour.
- 2 tablespoons Cocoa Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Avocado Chocolate Pancakes recipe, combine wheat flour, baking powder, salt and cocoa powder in a large bowl.
1. Mix well.
1. Now add honey, vanilla essence and milk to the flour mixture.
1. Whisk well.
1. Add the mashed avocado and stir until combined into a thick batter.
1. Heat a pan, drizzle oil and pour ladle full of batter into it.
1. Do not spread and cook covered for few minutes.
1. Gently flip to the other side and cook until it gets well cooked.
1. Arrange the pancakes in a plate, dust powdered sugar or add honey or chocolate syrup.
1. Serve Avocado Chocolate Pancakes along with a hot cup of Espresso Coffee and a Fruit Bowl for your weekend breakfast.
