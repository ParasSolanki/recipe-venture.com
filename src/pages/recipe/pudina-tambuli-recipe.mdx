### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1-1/2 cup Buttermilk.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 6 Curry leaves.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - broken.
- 3 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pudina Tambuli recipe, clean and  wash mint leaves .
1. In a pan add few drops of ghee and roast jeera, pepper and green chilli.
1. Fry for a minute.
1. Add the pudina leaves and saut√© for 2-3 minutes or till the leaves turn soft and change their colour.
1. Remove from the flame and keep aside to cool.
1. Grind leaves, spice mix, coconut and salt to a very smooth paste in a mixer by adding little water.
1. Pour the mixture to a bowl and add buttermilk/curd.
1. Mix well.
1. Adjust the consistency as required.
1. The consistency should be thin .
1. Heat the remaining ghee in a small pan.
1. Add mustard seeds and jeera ,when it splutters add curry leaves.
1. Pour the tempering on the thambli.
1. Serve Pudina Tambli in room temperature with hot steaming rice and Keerai Sambar or relish as such as a drink.
