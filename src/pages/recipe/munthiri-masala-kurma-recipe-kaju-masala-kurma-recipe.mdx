### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 2 tablespoons Ghee - as required.
- 1 cup Whole cashews.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Sambar Powder.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Munthiri Masala Kurma Recipe, heat ghee in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add 5-6 cashews and fry for 2-3 minutes till they are medium brown in colour.
1. Once done, keep it asideIn a blender take remaining cashews and grind to a fine powder and keep aside.
1. In the same blender, add chopped onions, ginger, garlic and make it a fine paste without adding water.
1. Heat ghee in a heavy bottomed pan, add the onion ginger garlic paste and saute for a minute until the raw smell goes away.
1. Add the curry leaves, cashew powder, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, garam masala powder and sambar powder.
1. Saute for a minute until it forms a good masala like consistency.
1. Once done, add the tomato puree and a cup of water and let the Munthiri Masala Kurma Recipe simmer for a good 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust accordingly.
1. Finally transfer the Munthiri Masala Kurma to a serving bowl and top it with the roasted cashew nuts.
1. Serve Munthiri Masala Kurma Recipe along with Palak Raita, Tawa Paratha and Satvik Carrot Sprout Salad for your weekday meal.
