### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 Guava - ripe and diced.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Jaggery - powdered.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Amrood Ni Kari Recipe (Parsi Style Dry Spiced Guava Curry Recipe) by heating a pressure cooker with oil, temper the mustard seeds and asafoetida.
1. Let the seeds splutter for some time.
1. Later add the cut guava and shallow fry them, add the remaining spice and 1 cup of water.
1. Adjust salt and check for seasoning .
1. Cover and let it cook till the guavaâ€™s are soft.
1. You can cook the guava's in the pressure cooker for about 3 whistles.
1. Once done let the pressure release naturally.
1. Serve your sweet and spicy Guava Ni Kari Recipe (Parsi Style Spiced Guava Curry Recipe) along with hot  lunch meal.
