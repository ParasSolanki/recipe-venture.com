### Ingredients

- 2-1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 2 tablespoons Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cubed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making  Kasuri Methi Paneer Sabzi Recipe, in a non-stick stir fry pan, add cooking oil and heat it over medium heat.
1. Once the oil is hot, add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
1. Then add finely chopped onion and saute till  they become transparent; Add tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
1. Add the masala powders, red chilli powder, garam masala and saute for a minute.
1. Crush methi leaves with your hand and stir them well into the pan .
1. Add required amount of salt.
1. Add the paneer pieces to the spicy mixture in the pan.
1. Combine Kasuri Methi Paneer Sabzi them well and simmer for about 3 - 4 minutes.
1. Do not saute often after adding paneer, as the paneer pieces may break and crumble.
1. Switch off the flame and add garnish Kasuri Methi Paneer Sabzi with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve Kasuri Methi Paneer Sabzi with Roti/Phulka or Chapati/Tawa Paratha.
