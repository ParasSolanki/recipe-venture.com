### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoons Lemon zest.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds - crushed.
- 5 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Paprika powder.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 4 Chicken breasts - pieces.
- 4 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Garlic.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mexican Lime Chicken recipe, firstly clean, wash the chicken breasts, make 1 inch slits and keep aside.
1. In a large mixing bowl, combine olive oil, lime juice, lime zest, garlic paste, coriander seeds, salt to taste, paprika, cilantro.
1. Add chicken breasts and marinate for minimum 1 hour in the refrigerator.
1. Heat a grill pan on the stove top and add spray/brush some oil.
1. Place the marinated chicken on it and grill from both sides till the chicken is cooked soft and juicy.
1. Transfer the grilled/roasted chicken on a serving platter and serve with a dash of lime juice all over.
1. Serve Mexican Lime Chicken along with some Baked Mexican Rice for a perfect weekend dinner.
