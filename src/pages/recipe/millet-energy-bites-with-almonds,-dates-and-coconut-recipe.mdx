### Ingredients

- 1 cup Dates.
- 1/2 cup Ragi Flour (Finger Millet/ Nagli).
- 1/2 cup Honey.
- 1/2 cup Bajra Flour ( Pearl Millet).
- 1 cup Badam (Almond) - whole.
- 1 cup Dessicated Coconut.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Millet Energy Bites with Almonds, Dates and Coconut recipe, firstly dry roast the ragi and bajra flour in a wide pan over medium heat.
1. After about 3 to 5 minutes, the flours will be aromatic.
1. Remove from the heat and let it cool completely.
1. In a mixer grinder, add the millet flours, dates, almonds and coconut flakes.
1. Give it about 8 to 10 pulses, until the almonds, dates and coconuts and all shredded down uniformly.
1. Add honey now, and give it quick pulse until the mixture is crumbly and starts to gather into a dough.
1. Remove and mold them into lemon sized balls.
1. They can be stored at room temperature.
1. Chilling it in the fridge will keep if fresh for up to two weeks.
