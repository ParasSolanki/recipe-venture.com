### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - toasted.
- 2 tablespoons Dried Figs - chopped.
- 1 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - julienned.
- 1/4 cup Amaranth Seeds (Rajgira) - lightly toasted.
- 1 cup Beetroot - julienned.
- 2 tablespoons Basil leaves.
- 2 tablespoons Tahini - for dressing.

### Instructions

1. To make the Carrot Beet Amaranth Super Salad Recipe first combine the beets and carrots in a salad bowl.
1. Drizzle in the tahini and extra virgin olive oil to the beets and carrots.
1. Top it with the chopped dried figs, chopped basil leaves and toss it all very well.
1. Toast the amaranth and sesame seeds next.
1. To do this, put them in a non stick pan, and saute them lightly over a low-medium flame.
1. Ensure you do not have the heat too high, so that they do not burn.
1. Set them aside to cool.
1. Once the amaranth is cooled down, add it to the above beet and carrot salad mixture only when you are ready to serve.
1. This is to ensure that they donâ€™t get soggy and lose the crunch.
1. Check the salt and seasoning and adjust to suit your taste.
1. The Carrot Beet Amaranth Super Salad is ready to be served as a meal by itself for a delicious Dinner along with a warm toasted bread and soup.
