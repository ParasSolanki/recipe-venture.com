### Ingredients

- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- Salt - to taste.
- 18 grams Tamarind.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 tablespoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 cup Black Eyed Beans (Lobia) - soaked in water for 2 hours.
- 1 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 cup Shallots.
- 2 cloves Garlic - crushed.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Cloves (Laung) - garlic.
- 1 cup Coconut milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mangalorean Style Lobia Gassi Recipe, in a Kadai/Pan, add 1 teaspoon ghee.
1. Roast coriander seeds, cumin seeds, dried red chilies, peppercorns one by one until fragrant.
1. Once roasted remove from pan and set aside to cool.
1. Into the same pan, add sliced onions, garlic, and roast until onions start to turn brown.
1. Add fresh grated coconut, tamarind ball, and continue to roast until coconut start to turn golden brown.
1. Remove from the pan and set it aside.
1. After roasting all the, and the spices and onion-coconut mix has cooled, add them all to a blender and blend into smooth paste by adding little water.
1. Heat a pressure cooker with oil, add the ground masala paste and cook until oil/ghee separates from the sides of the pan.
1. Add in your soaked lobia, turmeric powder and salt, add about 1/2 water and cook the lobia for 3 whistle.
1. Remove and add the coconut milk and stir well, check for salt and adjust according to your palate.
1. To temper, heat a kadai, add mustard seeds, hing, curry leaves, garlic and dry red chilies and let it crackle for few minutes and pour it over the gassi and serve.
1. Serve the Mangalorean Style Lobia Gassi Recipe along with Mangalorean Neer Dosa Recipe (Savory Rice & Coconut Crepe) to make a good meal for your Sunday lunch.
