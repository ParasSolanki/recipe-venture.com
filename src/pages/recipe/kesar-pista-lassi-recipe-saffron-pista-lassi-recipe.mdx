### Ingredients

- 3-4 Saffron strands.
- Ice cubes - a few.
- 1 teaspoon Pistachios.
- 1/4 cup Fresh cream.
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - (low fat).
- 1 tablespoon Sugar - or honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kesar Pista  Lassi Recipe, add the dahi, pista, saffron, cream and sugar into a mixer grinder.
1. Blend to make a delicious Kesar Pista Lassi.
1. Its your personal preference if you would like the lassi to be smooth or little chunky.
1. Once done transfer the Kesar Pista Lassi into a serving glass add a few ice cubes and garnish with saffron strands and serve the Kesar Pista Lassi chilled for the summers.
1. Serve the Kesar Pista Lassi Recipe along with Peshawari Naan, Malai Kofta for a special weekend lunch or dinner.
