### Ingredients

-  oil - as per use.
-  2 tsp lemon juice.
-  4 cloves garlic.
-  3 sprig mint.
-  2 green chillies.
-  5 sprig green coriander.
- 250 grams chicken - chopped.
-  2 tbsp Hung curd.
-  1/2 tsp Ginger - grated.
-  chaat masala powder - as per use.
-  salt - to taste.

### Instructions

1. To make Coriander Mint Chicken Tikka recipe, first clean and wash the chicken thoroughly.
1. Grind it small and make a hole with the help of fork.
1. Keep it separately.
1. To make the marination spices, add green coriander, mint, Hung curd, green chillies, ginger, garlic, salt and make a paste in a mixer grinder.
1. Add chicken to it and mix well.
1. Keep it in the fridge for 1 hour.
1. Now heat oil in a grill pan.
1. Add marinated chicken to it and mix well.
1. Cook well on all sides.
1. A light brown layer will start to form on the chicken and the hakiyan will also become soft.
1. Once cooked, drain in a bowl, add chaat masala, lemon juice and mix well.
1. Serve coriander mint chicken tikka recipe with yogurt mint chutney and vinegar onion for your house parties.
