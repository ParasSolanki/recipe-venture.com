### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Poppy seeds.
- 1/4 cup Rolled Oats Or Instant Oats - 40 grams.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 1/2 cup Water.
- 1 cup Button mushrooms - sliced.
- 1/2 Green peas (Matar).
- 1 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Savory Oatmeal Bowl with Chettinad Mushroom and Green Peas Stir Fry Recipe by roasting the spices for the chettinad masala and grinding into a smooth powder.
1. Next to stir fry the mushrooms, take a pan with oil add the chopped ginger and garlic, allow them to fry for few minutes.
1. Then add in the onions, saute till it becomes translucent and slight brown.
1. Next add in your chopped mushroom and saute till it releases all the water and cooks well.
1. At this point you can add in your ground masala and green peas and mix it well.
1. Stir fry till the raw smell of the masala goes away and keep it aside.
1. Now to make the oats, take a sauce pan, add in the oats and water and by using whisk keep stirring till it thickens.
1. Make sure it does not form a lump.
1. This will take you about 5 minutes.
