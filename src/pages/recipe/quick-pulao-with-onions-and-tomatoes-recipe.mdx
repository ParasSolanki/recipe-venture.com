### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- Ghee - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 Lemon - juice.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 2 cups Rice - cooked.
- Few sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Quick Pulao With Onions And Tomatoes, firstly prepare all the ingredients.
1. Heat ghee in a heavy bottomed pan and add cumin seeds.
1. Once it splutters, add the green chillies and curry leaves.
1. After a minute, add the onions and cook till they become soft and translucent.
1. Add the tomatoes and cook for a about a minute or two.
1. Add the leftover rice, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, salt and cook for about 3 to 5 minutes.
1. Once done, switch off the stove, put lemon juice on the quick pulao, mix and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Pair Quick Pulao With Onions And Tomatoes along with Burani Raita or any Raita of your choice and a roasted Papad.
1. It can be packed for picnics or lunch boxes.
