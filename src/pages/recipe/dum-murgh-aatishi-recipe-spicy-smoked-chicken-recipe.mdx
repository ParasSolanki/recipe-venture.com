### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Poppy seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1/2 teaspoon Anardana Powder (Pomegranate Seed Powder).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 4 Onions - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Fresh cream.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 1 inch Coal - for dum.
- 500 grams Chicken - Boneless.
- 8 Whole cashews.
- 1 teaspoon Char Magaz.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 2 tablespoons Ghee.
- 1/2 teaspoons Garam masala powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dum Murgh Aatishi Recipe, firstly add 1/2 tablespoon ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, lemon juice, salt to taste, anardana powder, cumin powder and mix everything well.
1. Next, add chicken and mix so that the chicken is properly marinated with the masala.
1. Cover and keep it aside for 3 to 4 hours for marination.
1. Meanwhile add khus khus, cashew nuts, char magaz and fennel seeds in a mixer grinder and make a paste out of it.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Once it is hot, add the ginger garlic and onions and saute until the onions become caramelised.
1. Once it gets caramelised, grind to make a smooth paste and keep aside.
1. The final step is to make the Dum Murgh Aatishi.
1. In a pan; add the ghee and dry red chillies.
1. Stir in the brown onion paste, poppy seeds paste, tomato pur√©e, garam masala powder, red chilli powder and keep stirring till oil leave from side.
1. This will take about 4 to 5 minutes.
1. After 4 to 5 minutes, add in the boneless marinated chicken and let it cook for about 3 to 4 minutes.
1.  Once the chicken is cooked properly, add in the cream to the chicken mixture.
1. After 2 minutes, switch of the heat.
1. Heat a coal directly on the gas flame.
1. Once it is red, add it in a small bowl.
1. Place this bowl in the middle of the heavy bottomed pan of the chicken.
1. Put a teaspoon of ghee over the coal and cover the pan.
1. The coal will smoke and add the smoky flavor to the chicken.
1. Once the smoke has subsided, remove the coal from the bowl and transfer the Dum Murgh Aatishi to a serving bowl.
1. Serve Dum Murgh Aatishi along with Garlic Naan, Jeera Rice, Burani Raita and Pudina Pyaz Kachumber Salad for a weekday meal.
