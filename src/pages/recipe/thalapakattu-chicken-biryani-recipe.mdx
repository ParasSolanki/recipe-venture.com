### Ingredients

- 3 Dagad phool (/pathar phool/black stone flower).
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Star anise.
- 3 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1-1/2 cup Water.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoons Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped.
- 1 cup Seeraga (Jeeraga) Samba rice.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 2 teaspoons Garlic - minced.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1/2 kg Chicken - cut into medium sized pieces.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Sunflower Oil - to cook.
- 3 Mace (Javitri).
- 3 Green Chillies - slit.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Thalapakattu Chicken Biryani Recipe, wash and clean the chicken thoroughly.
1. Wash and soak the seeraga samba rice in water for a minimum of 30 minutes.
1. In a mixer-jar, combine the cumin seeds, fennel seeds, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, mace, black stone slower and star anise blender and powder it coarsely.
1. Set aside.
1. Heat oil in a pressure cooker on medium flame, add in onions and green chillies.
1. Saute it till the onions are translucent.
1. Add in ginger and garlic paste and saute for a minute.
1. Add the tomatoes and mix well, now add salt, turmeric, chilli powder, and coriander powder.
1. Mix everything well.
1. Add the ground biryani masala and mix well.
1. Add the chicken and saute well for 5 to 10 minutes or till the chicken is at least half way done, for about 5-7 minutes.
1. At this stage, add the rice to the pressure cooker, add one and half cup water.
1. Cover and pressure cook for 2 whistle on high and simmer for 5 minutes.
1. Open the lid once the pressure has released naturally.
1. Serve Thalapakattu Chicken Biryani Recipe with Burani Raita Recipe for a hearty weekend meal.
