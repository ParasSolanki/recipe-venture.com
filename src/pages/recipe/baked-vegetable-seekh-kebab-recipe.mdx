### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1-1/2 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- Lemon wedges - for garnish.
- Onion - chopped for garnish.
- 2 tablespoons Cashew nuts - powdered.
- 1/2 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- 1/2 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 3 tablespoon Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Gram flour (besan).
- 1/4 tablespoon Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds - powdered.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled.
- 6-8 Green beans (French Beans) - finely sliced.
- 3 Green Chillies - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Baked Vegetable Seekh Kebab Recipe, firstly we will preheat the oven at 180 degrees for 10 minutes.
1. Grease a baking tray with oil.
1. Heat oil in a non-stick pan.
1. Add cumin seeds and saute until fragrant.
1. Add green chillies and saute for a minute.
1. Add carrot and saute for one 2-3 minutes.
1. Add french beans, cabbage and green peas and saute for 2-3 minutes.
1. Add turmeric powder, mix well and cook till it combines.
1. Transfer this mixture into a mixture into a mixer jar and grind coarsely.
1. Set aside.
1. Add gram flour in the same pan and sauté until fragrant.
1. Add cashew nut powder and sauté.
1. Reduce heat, add ground vegetables, mix well and saute for 2-3 minutes till the raw smell disappears.
1. Transfer this mixture into a bowl.
1. Add breadcrumbs, salt, crushed peppercorns and cardamom powder.
1. Add garam masala powder, potato and mix well.
1. Divide this mixture into large equal portions and wrap them around in the shape of satay sticks tightly to get a seekh kabab.
1. Place the sticks on the greased baking tray and put the tray in preheated oven.
1. Cook the recipe for 10-12 minutes, turning twice to the other side every 4-5 minutes.
1. Vegetable Seekh Kebab can be served as a starter along with Green Chutney, Pickled Onions and lemon.
1. This serves as a great party starter or as the appetiser for a full-fledged weekend meal.
