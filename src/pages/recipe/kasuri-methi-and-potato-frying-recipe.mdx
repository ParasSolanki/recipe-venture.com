### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 cups Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled and roughly mashed.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoons Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves) - (adjust).
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 2 teaspoons Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Ginger - grated and ground.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1/2 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 cup Tomatoes - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kasuri Methi Aur Aloo Rasedar Recipe, first boil the potatoes using a pressure cooker.
1. Once done, peel and roughly mash the potatoes keep a few pieces intact.
1. Heat ghee in a wok on medium heat, add cumin seeds, fennel and methi seeds and let it splutter.
1. Add the dried fenugreek leaves (kasuri methi), and let its aroma release into the oil.
1. Add the ginger paste, slit green chillies and saute until the raw smell goes away.
1. Add the chopped tomatoes and saute until its half cooked.
1. Add the coriander, turmeric, cumin and red chilli powder and saute for 2 minutes.
1. Now, add the roughly mashed potatoes and two cups of water and mix well.
1. Season with salt, aamchoor powder and simmer the mixture for 5 to 6 minutes.
1. Once the gravy comes together and reaches your desired consistency, switch off the flame.
