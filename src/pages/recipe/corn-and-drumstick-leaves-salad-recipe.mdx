### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 cups Drumstick Leaves (Moringa/Murungai Keerai).
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery - powdered.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 100 grams Del Monte Whole Corn Kernels.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Corn and Drumsticks Leaves Salad Recipe, first wash the corn kernels and clean the drumstick leaves.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, add some oil and heat it on medium flame.
1. Once the oil is hot, add cumin seeds and mustard seeds and turn to a low flame.
1. Once the mustard seeds splutter add the corn kernels and saute for 2 minutes.
1. Now add in the turmeric powder, chilli powder, salt  and give it a stir until the masalas are combined with the corn.
1. Next add in the drumstick leaves and saute until everything is well combined.
1. Adjust the salt, jaggery powder and cook on a low flame.
1. Once the drumstick leaves have shrunk and corn kernels are soft, turn off the flame.
