### Ingredients

-  1/3 cup peanuts - roast.
-  1 tablespoon parsley - finely chopped.
-  2 cloves garlic.
-  1 tablespoon oil.
-  salt - as per taste .
- 2 guava.
-  2 green chillies.
-  1 teaspoon good.
-  4 curry leaves.
-  1 tablespoon black urad dal (split).
-  1 tablespoon lemon juice.

### Instructions

1. To make guava chutney, first peel and chop the guava.
1. In a microwave pan, add 1/3 cup of water and salt.
1. Let it cook for 10 minutes and then cool.
1. Remove the seeds from the guava and mash it.
1. Now add guava, green chillies, peanuts, parsley, garlic, lemon juice, good, salt and paste into a mixer grinder.
1. Now heat oil in a small pan for tempering.
1. Add black urad dal, curry leaves and let it cook for 10 seconds.
1. Add this tempering to the chutney and serve.
1. Serve guava chutney with kirai sambar, beetroot thoran and rice.
