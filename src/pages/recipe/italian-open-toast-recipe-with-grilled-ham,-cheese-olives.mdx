### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Dill leaves - fresh.
- 1/4 cup Black olives - halved.
- 2 Swiss cheese - finely sliced.
- Salt - to taste.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 6 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 1/4 cup Homemade Pizza And Pasta Sauce.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - for cooking.
- 6 Chicken ham.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Italian Open Toast Recipe With Grilled Ham, Cheese & Olives first get all the ingredients ready.
1. Toast the slices of bread in the toaster until crisp.
1. Once the toast is done, smear the butter and place it on a baking sheet.
1. Heat a teaspoon of oil in a skillet; add the ham slices and grill them until cooked and lightly browned on both sides.
1. Smear the pasta sauce over the bread slices, place the slice of ham, sprinkle some dill leaves, olives and finally place a slice of cheese over the toast.
1. Preheat the oven at 180 degree for grilling and melting the cheese.
1. Place the Ham Open Toasts in the oven and bake until the cheese melts.
1. This will take about 6 to 8 minutes.
1. Once the cheese melts, remove from the oven and serve hot.
1. Serve the Open Toast Recipe With Grilled Ham, Cheese & Olives for a high protein breakfast along with a glass of Mango Tofu smoothie.
