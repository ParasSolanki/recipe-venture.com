### Ingredients

- 3 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- Ghee - for cooking.
- 3 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 500 grams Mutton - with bones.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Peshawari Karahi Gosht Recipe, we will begin by placing a pressure cooker on a medium flame and adding some ghee.
1. Add garlic, ginger and dry red chillies allow it to saute for few minutes.
1. Add in chopped tomatoes, sprinkle salt and turmeric and saute till the tomatoes become soft and mushy.
1. Once the tomatoes are cooked you can add the mutton pieces and sprinkle the required amount of salt and saute till the mutton pieces get browned.
1. Add curd and 1/2 cup of water and pressure cook the mutton for at least 6 whistles.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally and sprinkle coriander leaves and serve hot.
1. Serve the Peshawari Karahi Gosht Recipe can be made any day along with some hot phulka or tandoori roti and jeera rice.
