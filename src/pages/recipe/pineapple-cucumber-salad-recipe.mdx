### Ingredients

- 2 cups pineapple - chop.
-  1/4 tsp black pepper powder.
-  2 tbsp coriander - chop.
-  3 cucumber - chop.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/4 tsp lemon zest.
-  1 tsp lemon juice.

### Instructions

1. To make Pineapple Kukumber Salad Recipe, first prepare all the ingredients and keep them aside.
1. In a large mixing bowl, add pineapple, cucumber, coriander, lemon zest, lemon juice, salt, pepper powder and mix well.
1. Serve.
1. You can also prepare this salad and keep it in the fridge and serve cold.
1. Serve Pineapple Cucumber Salad Recipe with Lemon Coriander Soup and Broccoli Cheese Mayo Sandwich.
