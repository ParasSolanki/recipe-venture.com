### Ingredients

- 5 Shallots - (pearl onions).
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 cups Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 2 teaspoons Coconut Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1-1/2 cups Water.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 3 Garlic - cloves.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Moru Kachiyathu Recipe (Kerala Style Seasoned Buttermilk), add the curd into a big bowl and whisk it to a silky texture.
1. To this add water and whisk it to get to a semi thick buttermilk consistency.
1. Heat coconut oil in a wok, add the mustard seeds and let it crackle.
1. Add the methi seeds, curry leaves, red chillies and let it splutter.
1. To this add the finely sliced shallots, garlic and saute until the onions turn translucent.
1. Add the chopped ginger, green chillies, turmeric powder and flash fry it.
1. Keep the heat to low, and slowly add the buttermilk into the wok.
1. Keep stirring, until the buttermilk get just warm and starts to bubble.
1. Make sure you don't bring it to a boil to avoid curdling.
1. Switch off the flame, season with salt and mix well.
1. Serve the Moru Kachiyathu Recipe (Kerala Style Seasoned Buttermilk) along with Mixed Vegetable Sambar, Steamed Rice and Beetroot Thoran to make it a perfect simple meal for a weekday lunch.
