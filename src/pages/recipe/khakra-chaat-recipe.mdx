### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/4 cup Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- 1/4 cup Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Cucumber - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/4 cup Green Moong Sprouts.
- 1/4 cup Tomato - finely chopped.
- 4 Homemade Whole Wheat Khakras.
- 1/4 cup Sev.
- 1/4 cup Sweet Chutney (Date & Tamarind).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Khakra Chaat Recipe, in a large bowl combine all the ingredients for the topping and set aside.
1. Place the khakra pieces on a serving plate.
1. Sprinkle the salad mixture evenly on the khakra.
1. Drizzle the Date Chutney and the Dhania Pudina Ki Chutney over the salad mixture and sprinkle some Sev.
1. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve the Khakra Chaat immediately as a Tea time snack  on a weekend along with Bhel Puri With Raw Mango or Pav Bhajito make a complete meal.
