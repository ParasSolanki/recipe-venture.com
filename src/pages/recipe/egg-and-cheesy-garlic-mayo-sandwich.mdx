### Ingredients

- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 2 tablespoon Nutralite Cheesy Garlic Mayo.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- Â½ teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 3 Whole Eggs - hard boiled.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard sauce.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 Green Romaine lettuce.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Egg and Cheesy Garlic Mayo Sandwich, ensure the eggs are boiled well.
1. (Hard Boil)Roughly chop the entire egg and keep it aside.
1. In a large mixing bowl, add the chopped eggs, Nutralite Cheesy Garlic Mayo, mustard sauce, salt & pepper, and lemon juice.
1. Mix well.
1. Place a lettuce leaf on the bread slice and then spread the Egg and Cheesy Garlic Mayo Sandwich mixture on it and close the sandwich with the other slice.
1. Cut the Egg Mayo Sandwich into triangles and serve.
1. Serve the  Egg and Cheesy Garlic Mayo Sandwich with Carrot Pineapple Orange Juice Recipe and some almonds and walnuts for a healthy breakfast.
