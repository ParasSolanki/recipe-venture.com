### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 3 Carrots (Gajjar) - peeled and finely chopped.
- 300 grams Penne pasta.
- 6 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Black olives - or both green & black.
- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese - grated.
- 4 Tomato - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Penne Pasta Recipe In Roasted Carrot Sauce, firstly cook the pasta.
1. In a large pot, add water and bring it to a boil.
1. Once the water has boiled, add a teaspoon of salt.
1. Add the pasta to the boiling water and let it cook in the boiling water until it is al dente.
1. This process should take a good 12-15 minutes.
1. Once the pasta has cooked, drain the water and run the cooked pasta under running water to stop the cooking process.
1. Drizzle some oil on top of pasta and make sure to coat all of the pasta with the oil so that the pasta doesn't stick to each other.
1. The next step is to make the Roasted Carrot SauceHeat olive oil in a heavy bottomed pan over medium heat.
1. Add the crushed garlic and let it cook for about a minute.
1. After a minute, add the chopped carrots, sprinkle some salt and roast the carrots over medium heat until it is soft and cooked.
1. Once the carrots are cooked, add the chopped tomatoes and cook the tomatoes along with the carrots until the tomatoes become soft.
1. Once the tomatoes are soft, add salt, red chilli powder, oregano, and chilli flakes.
1. Simmer the mixture for about 3 to 4 minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Once done, allow the roasted carrots to cool.
1. Once cooled, puree the roasted carrots to get a smooth pasta sauce.
1. Heat a wok over medium heat; add the pureed roasted carrot pasta sauce, the cooked pasta and toss well on high heat to combine.
1. Once done, check the salt and adjust according to taste.
1. Transfer the Penne Pasta In Roasted Carrot Sauce, to a serving bowl, add the chopped olives and parmesan cheese and serve hot.
1. Serve the The Penne Pasta Recipe In Roasted Tomato Carrot Sauce along with Fennel Pesto Pull-Apart Bread Recipe for weekend lunch or dinner along with a glass of Apple And Ginger Cocktail Recipe.
