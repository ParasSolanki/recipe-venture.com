### Ingredients

- Slivered Almonds (Badam) - or pista or cashews.
- 1/4 teaspoon Nutmeg.
- 1 cup Broken Wheat (Dalia/ Godumai Rava).
- 3 tablespoon Ghee.
- 3/4 - 1 cup Jaggery - grated.
- 2 tablespoon Chironji - or any other nuts of choice.
- 2 tablespoon Rice - soaked in water for 30 minutes.
- 3 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 cups Milk.
- pinch Saffron strands.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Maharashtrian Gavachi Kheer Recipe (Whole Cracked Wheat Pudding), first heat ghee in a saucepan.
1. Add the chironji and any other nuts.
1. Saut√© for a minute and take out the nuts onto a plate.
1. In the same pan add daliya.
1. Stir and roast till you get the distinct aroma.
1. Take it off the heat.
1. Cool slightly and pressure cook the daliya and rice with 2 1/2-3 cups of water till it becomes soft, about 3 whistles and 10 minutes on sim.
1. In the saucepan add daliya, coconut and jaggery and heat this mixture till the jaggery dissolves.
1. Add a little water if required.
1. Add the saffron and nutmeg to the milk.
1. Add milk and nuts to the kheer and cook for a few minutes.
1. Serve Maharashtrian Gavachi Kheer Recipe (Whole Cracked Wheat Pudding) hot or cold garnished with slivered nuts.
