### Ingredients

- 200 grams Spaghetti Pasta.
- 1/4 cup Mozzarella cheese.
- Salt - to taste.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - as required.
- 2 cloves Garlic - grated.
- Garlic Butter - to spread.
- 4 Hot Dog Bread - or Bagutte.
- 1/4 cup Cheddar cheese.
- 1/2 cup Tomato Basil Pasta Sauce.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cheesy Spaghetti Sandwich Recipe, first you will need to keep the get the tomato basil pasta sauce ready.
1. You can either use store bought sauce or make the sauce at home using this recipe - Tomato Basil Pasta Sauce and Pizza Sauce Recipe.
1. We will then cook the spaghetti pasta along with salt in boiling water until al dente.
1. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it from the hot water and run it under cold water immediately to stop the process of cooking.
1. Drain the pasta from its water and drizzle a bit of olive oil to prevent it from sticking to each other.
1. Keep the pasta aside.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, add in a tablespoon of olive oil and the grated garlic.
1. Saute for a few seconds andNow add the cooked spaghetti to this pan along with the tomato basil pasta sauce and stir wellOnce the pasta is well coated with the sauce, turn off the heat.
1. Next, slit the submarine bread horizontally.
1. Apply butter on both the halves of the bread.
1. Toast both halves of the bread on a griddle over medium heat until slightly golden brown and crisp.
1. Once the bread is toasted, remove from the heat.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the cheddar cheese and mozzarella cheese and set aside.
1. Place the toasted subway bread on a flat surface.
1.  Place some tomato basil spaghetti on lower half of the bread and top it generously with cheese on the top.
1. Cover the sandwich with the other half of the breadTo lightly met the cheese, place this Cheesy Spaghetti Sandwich in the microwave for 1 minute on high heat.
