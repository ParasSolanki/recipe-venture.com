### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 2 tablespoon Fresh coconut.
- 4 Dry Red Chilli.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- 1 tablespoon Tamarind Water.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Horse Gram Dal (Kollu/ Kulith).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies - cut equally.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 2 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1 cup Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - peeled.
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Hurali Kalu Chutney Recipe - Horse Gram Chutney Recipe by heating the kadai with little oil.
1. Add in the jeera seeds, curry leaves, dry red chillies and coriander seeds.
1. Let it splutter.
1. Once the seeds have spluttered, slowly add in the pearl onions and garlic and saute till it is translucent.
1. Once that is done you can add the horse gram and saute till it puffs up.
1. Once done turn off the heat and leave it to cool down.
1. Then grind the saut√©ed ingredients along with jaggery, coconut and tamarind into a smooth paste.
1. For the tadka, heat oil in a small tadka pan and splutter mustard seeds.
