### Ingredients

- 6 sprig Basil leaves - torn.
- 1 cup Rajma (Large Kidney Beans) - Soaked in water for 8 hours.
- 4 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Rosemary.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Feta Cheese - crumbled.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kidney Beans, Crumbled Feta, Basil Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette Recipe, pressure cook the soaked in kidney beans with water and cook for 5 whistle.
1. Release the pressure naturally and drain the water.
1. Add all the lemon dressing and toss it well.
1. Add torn basil leaves and sprinkle with crumbled feta and serve.
1. Serve the Kidney Beans, Crumbled Feta, and Basil Salad with Lemon Vinaigrette along with some pita bread and hummus to make a complete meal.
