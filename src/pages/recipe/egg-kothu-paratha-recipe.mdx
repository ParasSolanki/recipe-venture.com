### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Lemon - juiced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon SSP Asafoetida (Hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder - or egg curry masala.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 6 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves - torn.
- 3 Whole Eggs.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 cups Leftover roti - torn into 1 inch bits.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Egg Kothu Paratha Recipe, into a preheated pan add oil and mustard seeds and allow the seeds to crackle.
1. Once done add the asafoetida,onions, garlic, ginger, green chillies, curry leaves and saute until the onions soften and turn golden brown in colour.
1. After the onions have become golden brown add the tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chilli powder and saute until the tomatoes soften.
1. Once done crack three eggs and add to the onion tomato masala.
1. Stir well until the eggs becomes a scramble.
1. After the eggs are done add the torn leftover roti.
1. Sprinkle salt to taste and stir well for three to four minutes.
1. Once all the ingredients are well combined add juice from one lemon and chopped coriander leaves.
1. Stir well to combine and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer the Egg Kothu Paratha  into a serving plate and serve hot.
1. Serve Egg Kothu Paratha Recipe for breakfast or a quick dinner with Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita Recipe.
