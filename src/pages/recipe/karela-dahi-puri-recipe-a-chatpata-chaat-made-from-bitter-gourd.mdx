### Ingredients

- 4 Karela (Bitter Gourd/ Pavakkai) - sliced into rounds.
- 1/4 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1/2 cup Sev.
- 1/4 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1/4 cup Aloo Bhujia - (optional).
- 1 teaspoon Black Salt (Kala Namak) - (adjust to taste).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- Dates - few.
- 1 tablespoon Sweet Chutney (Date & Tamarind).
- Sunflower Oil - as needed.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder - (adjust).
- 2 teaspoons Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Karela Dahi Puri Recipe, heat about 2 cups of water in a saucepan and add all the sliced bitter gourd in it and boil for about 2 minutes or until semi cooked.
1. Strain the karela slices out of water and keep aside.
1. In a mixing bowl, add the gram flour, red chili powder, black salt, coriander powder and asafoetida.
1. Toss the bitter gourd slices into this mixture.
1. Sprinkle some water if needed and mix till it is coated well.
1. Heat oil in a pan for shallow frying and fry the spiced bitter gourd in batches until done and crisp.
1. Whisk the yogurt along with chaat masala powder and keep aside.
1. Arrange the pan fried karela slices in a plate.
1. Drizzle the spiced chatpata yogurt, tamarind chutney on each slice.
1.  Spread bhujia & sev, dates and garnish with chopped coriander and serve.
1. Serve Karela Dahi Puri along with a hot cup of Masala Tea or Filter Coffee for evening snacks.
