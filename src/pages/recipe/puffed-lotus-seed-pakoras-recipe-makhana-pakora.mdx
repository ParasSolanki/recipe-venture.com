### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves - chopped.
- 100 grams Phool Makhana (Lotus Seeds) - roasted in ghee.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Puffed Lotus Seed Pakoras Recipe, we will first make the pakora batter.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine all the ingredients together -lotus seeds, besan flour,  onion, green chilli, cumin seeds, red chilli powder,  turmeric powder, salt, coriander leaves,curry leaves.
1.  Add water little at a time, to make a thick pakora batter.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Preheat the Kuzhi Paniyaram Pan on medium heat; add a few drops of oil into each cavity.
1. Spoon the Lotus Seed Pakoda batter into the cavity and pan-fry them until it is browned and crisp on both sides.
1. You can optionally add just a little more oil to give it additional taste which also makes the pakora a lot more crispy.
1. Proceed the same way with the remaining Lotus Seed pakora Batter.
1. The process of cooking pakora in a pan preserves nutrition and the addition of Lotus seeds giving an added taste and nutrition for this classic teatime snack.
1. Arrange the Lotus Seed pakoda on a serving platter, sprinkle some chaat masala, place some pickled onions by the side and serve it as a snack or as an appetizer for parties.
1. Serve the Puffed Lotus Seed Pakoras Recipe along with hot chai with a dipping sauce for your evening tea.
