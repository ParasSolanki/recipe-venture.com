### Ingredients

- 1 Cup Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 Tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 Tablespoon Tamarind.
- 15 cloves Garlic.
- 1 Cup Dry Red Chillies.

### Instructions

1. To make Peanut Garlic Chutney Powder Recipe, dry roast peanuts and remove skin.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat the kadai, add a spoon of oil and roast red chillies till they turn a little dark colour.
1. Keep them aside.
1. In the same kadai, roast cumin seeds and tamarind.
1. Need not add oil to it.
1. Allow them to cool.
1. In a blender jar, combine red chillies, cumin seeds and tamarind.
1. Grind them to make a coarse powder.
1. Add salt, peanuts and again grind them.
1. Lastly, add garlic cloves and press whip or pulse button in the mixer jar.
1. Store this powder in an air tight container.
1. This powder lasts for 15 days to one month.
1. Serve Peanut Garlic Chutney Powder Recipe with steamed rice and sambar or smear a layer of ghee and sprinkle Peanut Garlic Chutney Powder and roll up Rajasthani Kaddu Aur Aloo Ki Sabzi Recipe in a tawa paratha.
