### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Green Chilli.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves - 2 sprigs curry leaves.
- 2 cup cups Water - 2 cups Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Curry Leaves Buttermilk Recipe, keep the curd ready.
1. You can either use store bought curd or make fresh curd at home -  learn How To Make Homemade Yogurt (Curd)In a mixer-jar, combine the curry leaves, salt, black pepper powder, and green chilli and grind to a coarse paste.
1. Next add the curd and water and blend it one more time, until the Curry Leaves Chaas is well combined.
1. Pour into a glass and serve chilled.
1. Serve Curry Leaves Buttermilk Recipe along with a healthy meal of Iyengar Style Vendhaya keerai Pulao Recipe, Onion Bajji Recipe, and South Indian Kosambari Recipe With Raw Mango, Cucumber, Carrot & Beetroot for a complete meal.
