### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) -  cut into thin strips.
- 2 teaspoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - cut into thin strips.
- 2 teaspoons Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - white.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - cut into thin strips.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 teaspoon Mixed Herbs (Dried).
- 1 Green zucchini -  cut into thin strips.
- 3 tablespoons Barbeque sauce.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Zucchini Salad with Barbecue Sauce Dressing Recipe, cut all the vegetables into thin strips.
1. Combine them all and allow it to chill in the refrigerator.
1. To make the Barbecue Sauce Dressing, in a bowl combine, the barbeque sauce, lemon juice, chilli flakes, olive oil and salt.
1. Mix the dressing well.
1. In a mixing bowl, toss the vegetables with the Barbecue Sauce Dressing and serve it in a bowl.
1. Garnish with mixed herbs and sesame seedsServe this Zucchini Salad with Barbecue Sauce Dressing Recipe along with Beetroot Gazpacho Soup Recipe and Chicken And Cheese Sandwich With Avocado Recipe for a light summer meal.
