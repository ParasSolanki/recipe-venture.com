### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Baking soda.
- Water - as required.
- Curry leaves - Few sprigs.
- 1-1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 3 Dry Red Chillies.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - Few sprigs.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Onions - chopped.
- 6 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 4 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 Bottle gourd (lauki) - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Lauki Ke Gatte Recipe, firstly add all the ingredients under ‘For dumpling dough’ including bottle gourd, whole wheat flour, green chillies, garlic, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, salt, coriander powder, coriander seeds, coriander leaves and baking soda in a mixing bowl.
1. Add some water and mix till everything comes together.
1. Do not knead the dough too much.
1. Pinch medium sized balls from the dough.
1. Using the palms of your hands shape them into elongated dumplings.
1. If they are big enough cut them into half to make small dumplings.
1. Steam the dumplings for about 12 to 15 minutes or till a knife inserted comes out clean.
1. Once it is done, the next step is to temper the dumplings.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan and add all the ingredients mentioned under ‘for tempering’ which includes cumin seeds, curry leaves, mustard seeds, asafoetida and dry red chillies.
1. Once the mustard seeds starts crackiling, add the chopped onions.
1. Saute the onions till they become translucent.
1. Once it is done, add the steamed dumplings in to the pan.
1. Mix everything properly.
1. Cook for another minute or two.
1. Once done, switch off the heat and serve hot.
1. Serve Lauki Ke Gatte on its own or with Gujarati Kadhi for a light dinner meal.
1. You can also serve it as a snack with Green Chutney.
