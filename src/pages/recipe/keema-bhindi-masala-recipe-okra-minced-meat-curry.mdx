### Ingredients

- 250 grams Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra) - cut into 1/2 inch pieces.
- 8 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoons Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 teaspoons Garam masala powder.
- 1 tablespoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Mustard oil.
- 500 grams Chicken - cut into 1/2 inch pieces.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Keema Bhindi Masala Recipe - Okra & Minced Meat Curry, first we will roast the bhindi.
1. To make roasted BhindiInto a pan add mustard oil.
1. Once the oil is hot add the bhindi and sprinkle salt to taste.
1. Stir to combine.
1. Keep the ladle in the pan and cover with the lid.
1. Placing the ladle inside the pan helps to keep the lid ajar.
1. This also helps the bhindi to cook well at the same time not get sticky.
1. Saute at regular intervals to cook the bhindi on all sides.
1. Once the bhindi is well roasted and cooked through turn off the heat and keep aside.
1. To Mince ChickenTo mince the chicken attach the chopping blade into the Preethi zodiac master chef jar.
1. Add the chicken pieces to the jar and close the jar with its lid.
1. Pulse to make minced Chicken.
1. Once done remove the minced chicken into a bowl and keep aside.
1. To make the Keema MasalaInto a pan add oil and once the oil is hot add the onions, green chillies, ginger, garlic.
1. Saute until the onions soften and turn golden brown in colour.
1. Once done add the chicken keema and salt to taste.
1. Saute on high heat for 3-4 minutes until the chicken in half cooked and changes colour.
1. At this stage add the tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, garam masala and stir well to combine.
1. Add 1/4 cup water.
1. Check the salt and add more if required.
1. Cover and cook the keema till the chicken is completely cooked.
1. This will take about 5 minutes.
1. After the chicken is cooked stir in the roasted bhindi and kasuri methi.
1. Toss to combine well.
1. Garnish with coriander leaves and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer the Keema Bhindi Masala into a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Keema Bhindi Masala Recipe - Okra & Minced Meat Curry hot with parathas or poori and Carrot Cucumber Tomato Salad with Lemon and Coriander  for a satisfying meal.
