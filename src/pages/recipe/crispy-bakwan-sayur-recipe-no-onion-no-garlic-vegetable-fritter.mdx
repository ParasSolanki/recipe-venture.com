### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.
- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - shredded.
- 250 ml Water.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - shredded.
- 1 tablespoon Rice flour.
- 3 tablespoon All Purpose Flour (Maida).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Crispy Bakwan Sayur Recipe (No Onion No Garlic Vegetable Fritter), in a mixing bowl, combine all purpose flour, rice flour, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, salt, sugar, and ground pepper.
1. Add water and mix well, make sure there are no lumps.
1. Add shredded cabbage and shredded carrot in the batter and mix well.
1. Pour enough oil in a pot for deep frying.
1. Once a bit of batter dropped into the oil bubbles immediately, the oil is ready.
1. Use a small ladle and drop a ladleful of vegetables mixture into the hot oil.
1. Deep fry until the fritter is crispy and turns golden brown on both sides, remove and drain on a wire rack/paper towel.
1. Repeat until all batter is used up.
1. Serve Crispy Bakwan Sayur Recipe (No Onion No Garlic Vegetable Fritter) immediately with some Thai red chilies, or a combination of roasted tomato sauce and chili sauce during evening.
