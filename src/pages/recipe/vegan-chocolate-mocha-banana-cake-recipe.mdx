### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1/2 cup Chocolate chips.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 teaspoon Instant Coffee Powder.
- 1/2 cup Ripe Bananas - mashed.
- 1/3 cup Sunflower Oil.
- 1 cup Water - (adjust).
- 1 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 1/4 cup Cocoa Powder.
- 1-2/3 cups Whole Wheat Flour.
- 3/4 cup Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Vegan Chocolate Mocha Banana Cake Recipe, get prepped with all the ingredients.
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit or 180 degrees Centigrade.
1. Prepare an 8 inch or 9 inch pan; grease and flour or line with aluminum foil and then grease and flour.
1. Keep aside.
1. Mash bananas and gather the ingredients for the cake.
1. In a large bowl, add whole wheat flour, sugar, cocoa powder, coffee powder, baking soda and salt.
1. Mix well with a wire whisk or sift the mixture well.
1. To this, add oil, vanilla essence, vinegar, and 3/4 cup water.
1. Stir well with a silicone spatula or spoon.
1. Fold in mashed bananas.
1. If you feel the batter is a bit thick, add more water; up to 1 cup to get a medium thick pouring consistency.
1. Pour into prepared pan and smoothen to level the top.
1. Sprinkle with chocolate chips.
1. Bake in preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
1. Remove from oven and place on cooling rack.
1. After 10 minutes, remove cake from foil and cool on wire rack completely.
1.
