### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/4 teaspoon Black Salt (Kala Namak).
- Salt - as required.
- 2 Chicken thighs.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/2 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 3 Charcoal - small pieces.
- 2 tablespoons Mustard oil.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger - paste.
- 2 tablespoons Ghee.
- 4 tablespoons Mustard oil.
- 1 tablespoon Garlic - paste.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Smoked Tandoori Chicken Recipe, clean, wash and make 1 inch slits on Chicken thighs on both sides and keep aside.
1. Mix all the ingredients mentioned in 'marination' in a large bowl to make a smooth paste.
1. Add chicken thighs in it and rub the marinade well all over the pieces.
1. Cling wrap the bowl and refrigerate it for minimum 3-4 hours.
1. (Overnight marination gives much more amazing results)Grill Pan MethodHeat some mustard oil in a grilling pan and place the marinated chicken thighs and allow then to cook till they get well cooked.
1. Carefully flip the tandoori chicken and cook from another side as well.
1. Brush some ghee or mustard oil in top while the chicken is grilling.
1. Ones the tandoori chicken is soft, juicy, charred and nicely cooked, transfer it in a broad, wide base bowl.
1. Oven MethodPreheat the oven to 210 C for about 10 minutes.
1. Once the oven is heated, place the marinated chicken in the oven in the middle rack.
1. Bake the Tandoori Chicken for half the time on one side, drizzle more ghee or oil in between and then turn it over and slather some of the masala from the pan and even it out.
1. Bake until the masala and top of the tandoori chicken looks cooked and absolutely well done.
1. For the Smoking the Tandoori Chicken Place the Tandoori chicken on the sides of the bowl and a small steel bowl covered with aluminium foil in the centre.
1. Take a piece of charcoal and hold it on fire with the help of a tong.
1. As soon as the charcoal starts burning and is red hot place it in the small bowl.
1. Take 1 tbsp of ghee over the charcoal and close the lid such that all the smoke remain inside the bowl of tandoori chickenKeep the lid closed and open only when you are ready to serve the Tandoori Chicken.
1. Serve Stove Top Grilled And Smoked Tandoori Chicken as a starter at your house parties with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney and some Pickled Onions.
