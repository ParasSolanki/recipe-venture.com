### Ingredients

- Salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon Kala jeera.
- 2 teaspoons White Urad Dal (Split) - +1/2 teaspoon for tadka.
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 30 grams Tamarind - soaked and pulp extracted.
- 40 Grams Ginger - peeled and roughly chopped.
- pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 2-3 Dry Red Chillies.
- 40 grams Jaggery.
- 1/8 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - optional.
- Sunflower Oil - as needed.
- Curry leaves - few.
- 6-7 Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin the preparation of Allam Pachadi, add oil in a small pan, and add jeera, pepper, methi seeds, red chilli and urad dal and roast until Fragrant and colour changes.
1. Add this in a blender along with ginger, jaggery, tamarind pulp, salt and 1 tablespoon of water and blend this till it achieves a smooth texture and dish it out into a bowl.
1. Now add some oil in the same pan and make a tadka with mustard, hing, curry leaves and red chillies and spoon it on top of the chutney.
