### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Jaggery - grated.
- 8 Curry leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Tomato Gojju (South Indian Style Tomato Curry Recipe), first heat oil in a wok.
1. Add the mustard seeds.
1. Once they splutter, add the cumin seeds, hing, turmeric powder, curry leaves and green chillies.
1. Saute for a few seconds and then add tomatoes, jaggery, and salt.
1. Cook covered for 3-4 minutes.
1. Cook Tomato Gojju uncovered for 3-4 minutes more or till the tomatoes soften and some of the water evaporates.
1. Serve Tomato Gojju with Roti or Rice along with Paruppu Podi.
