### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Tamarind Paste.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Onion - medium.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 3-4 Green Chilli.
- 1/2 inch Ginger.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 500 grams Prawns - tiger prawns.
- 7 cloves Garlic.
- 2 Tomatoes.
- 1 tablespoon Coconut Oil.
- 1/2 cup Coconut milk - first extract.
- 1/2 cup Coconut milk - second & third extract.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Prawns Coconut Curry Recipe, begin by making a paste of fennel, pepper, ginger and garlic by adding a little water.
1. Heat a Kadai or meen chatty with oil.
1. Once hot, splutter mustard seeds.
1. Add the above paste and saute till the raw smell leaves.
1. Add curry leaves, green chilli and chopped onions.
1. Saute till onions turn translucent.
1. Add in chopped tomatoes and cook till tomatoes turn mushy.
1. Add in tamarind paste.
1. Add coriander powder and chilli powder and mix for a minute.
1. Add second and third milk extract of coconut.
1. Add salt and bring it to a boil.
1. Add cleaned and deveined prawns.
1. Cook for 4-5 minutes.
1. Now add first milk extract of coconut and mix well.
1. Do not boil.
1. Once it is heated enough take it off flame and garnish prawns coconut curry recipe with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve Prawns Coconut Curry with hot steamed rice.
