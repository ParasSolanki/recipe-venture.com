### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Garlic - finely chopped(or garlic paste).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 teaspoon Cajun spice mix.
- 2-3 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 1/2 cups Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - soaked in water for 7-8 hours ( overnight).
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Roasted Chickpeas, first, Pre-heat the oven to 200 deg Celsius.
1. Brush some olive oil on a non-stick baking sheet.
1. Drain the chickpeas till all the water is removed.
1. Use a kitchen towel to soak off the excess moisture.
1. Whisk together the olive oil, garlic, and salt, in a small bowlToss this together with the chickpeas in a mixing bowl.
1. Spread the chickpeas in an even layer on the baking sheet.
1. Bake till they just start browning about 20-25 minutes.
1. While they are being baked, stir to mix using a spatula once or twice, to ensure even baking.
1. Sprinkle the seasoning and mix so that it evenly coats the chickpeas.
1. Bake Roasted Chickpeas for another 10-12 minutes.
1. Once done remove form the oven and allow to cool before serving.
1. Serve on its own or with chopped onions,herbs, with some mulled apple juice as a snack or just with some Masala Chai Recipe - Indian Spiced Tea to enjoy your evening time.
