### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - split.
- 5 Cashew nuts - broken roughly.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Enos fruit salt.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil - to grease the Idli Plates.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/4 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- 2 teaspoons Ghee.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Godhumai Idli Recipe (Instant Whole Wheat Flour Idli), first get all the ingredients ready and keep aside.
1. Heat a pan over low heat and dry roast the wheat flour till they turn fragrant.
1. Keep stirring continuously to roast the flour uniformly and avoid burning.
1. Transfer the flour to a another vessel and allow it to cool.
1. Heat the pan again with ghee and add the ingredients given under ' seasoning' in the order given.
1. Allow them to roast and crackle and set it aside.
1. Whisk together yogurt and 1 cup of water to make a buttermilk.
1. Add buttermilk to the flour and using a hand whisk, stir to form a batter adding more water if required to make a thick batter.
1. Once you have a thick batter, add the tempering to the batter and mix well.
1. Add salt ( just a little) and allow the batter to rest for 20-30 minutes.
1. In the meantime, grease the Idli plates and preheat the steamer.
1. Once you are ready to steam the idlis, add 1/2 teaspoon of enos fruit salt and stir the batter.
1. The batter will become light and fluffy.
1. Spoon the batter into the greased plate and steam the Idlis for 15 -20 minutes until done.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and allow it to rest for 5 minutes.
1. Remove Godhumai Idli from the plates and serve.
1. Serve these healthy Godhumai Idlis along with Tomato Onion Chutney, Thakkali Vengaya Sambar or Idli Milagai Podi for breakfast or dinner.
