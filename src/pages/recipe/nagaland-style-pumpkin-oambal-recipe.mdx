### Ingredients

- 1 Tamarind - half the size of lemon.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 300 grams Kaddu (Parangikai/ Pumpkin).
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - deseeded.
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Green Chilli - cut into 2 pieces.
- 1 tablespoon Raisins.
- 1 tablespoon Jaggery.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin the preparation of pumpkin Oambal, in a microwave safe bowl, combine 1/2 cup water and tamarind.
1. Microwave for about 30-40 seconds.
1. You can also combine water and tamarind in a pan and bring the mixture to a boil.
1. Once it cools down a bit, grind in a mixer/grinder.
1. Strain this mixture and set aside.
1. Wash and peel the pumpkin.
1. Remove its seeds and cut it into cubes.
1. In a wide pan, add pumpkin cubes and tamarind water.
1. Cover with a lid and cook for about 10 minutes or till pumpkin gets cooked and becomes soft.
1. Mash it with the help of a vegetable masher.
1. Keep aside.
1. In a pan heat oil and add dry red chilli and mustard seeds.
1. When mustard seeds crackle, add green chilli, raisins, pumpkin mixture and salt.
1. Mix well and add jaggery.
1. Let it cook for about a minute.
1. Remove from flame.
1. Serve this sweet and sour Pumpkin Oambal along with phulkas or parathas, Tomato Onion Gravy Sabzi and Spicy Paneer Pulao With Vegetables for a weekday lunch.
