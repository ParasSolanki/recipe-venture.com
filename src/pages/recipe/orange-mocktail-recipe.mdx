### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Sprite - or 7 up or club soda or ginger ale.
- Salt - to taste.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - few.
- 1 cup Fresh orange juice.
- Ice cubes - as required.
- Sugar - as required (optional).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Orange Mocktail, firstly mix the orange juice with lime/lemon juice in a mixing bowl.
1. Take two glasses and put some ice cubes in it.
1. Pour the juice mixture in the glass till it is half full.
1. Pour some sprite or 7up in the same glass and a pinch of salt.
1. Crush some mint leaves from hand and add in the juice.
1. Add some more ice cubes if needed.
1. Serve Orange Mocktail along with Cheese Stuffed Aloo Bonda and Sabudana Vada for your house parties.
