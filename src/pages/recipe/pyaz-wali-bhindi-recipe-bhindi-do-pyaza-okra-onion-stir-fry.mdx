### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Coriander (Dhania) Powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini) - broken.
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 2 tablespoon Mustard oil - or Sunflower oil.
- 500 grams Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra).
- 2 teaspoons Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 3 Onions - thinly sliced.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder - (or more to taste).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Pyaz Wali Bhindi Recipe Bhindi Do Pyaza, first wash bhindi in water.
1. Pat dry with the kitchen towel.
1. To cut okra first remove the stem of the bhindi and then cut into thin strips of 4 pieces.
1. Keep aside until ready to use.
1. Heat the oil in a heavy-bottomed saucepan over medium heat.
1. Add the ajwain seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Add the cinnamon stick, bay leaf and saute for a few seconds.
1. Add the thinly sliced onions, turn the heat to low and saute the onions until they soften.
1. At this stage add in the chopped lady's finger, sprinkle salt, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder and amchur powder.
1. Stir until all the ingredients get well combined.
1. Cover the pan with the lid slightly ajar.
1. Keep the heat on low and let bhindi do pyaza cook until it turns soft and tender.
1. This process will take about 15 minutes on medium heat.
1. Ensure to keep stirring the bhindi do pyaza a few times in between so it gets cooked evenly and towards the end you will notice the onions have slightly caramelised as well.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and check the salt and spices and adjust to taste accordingly.
1. Transfer the Pyaz Wali Bhindi/ Bhindi Do Pyaza to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve the Pyaz Wali Bhindi/ Bhindi Do Pyaza Recipe along with Parathas and Kadhi Pakora for a delicious lunch or dinner.
