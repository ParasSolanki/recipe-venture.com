### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 tablespoon Rice Wine Vinegar.
- 4 Spring Onion Greens - bulbs and greens chopped.
- 1 cup Green beans (French Beans) - finely chopped.
- 3 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Soy granules - soaked in hot water and drained.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 4 tablespoons Corn flour - mixed in 1/2 cup water to seal edges.
- 1 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - chopped finely.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder - crushed.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 1 cup Cauliflower (gobi) - chopped finely.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 12 Spring Roll Wrappers.
- 1 Pinch Sugar.
- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 1 cup Tofu - chopped into small pieces (1 inch strips).
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Soya Spring Rolls recipe, first heat the sesame oil in a wok/kadhai.
1. Add the chopped onion bulbs, garlic and ginger.
1. Saute for a few seconds and then add the vegetables.
1. Cook till the vegetables soften.
1. Add the soy granules, coloured pepper, green chillies, soy sauce, sugar and vinegar.
1. Mix and add the onion greens, salt and black pepper.
1. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes more.
1. Take it off the heat and cool.
1. Place one spring roll wrapper on a flat work surface.
1. Cover the rest of the wrappers with a kitchen towel.
1. Spread about 2 tablespoons stuffing on one side of the wrapper towards you.
1. Place one tofu piece over it.
1. Fold the corner over the filling and roll tightly.
1. Tuck in from both the sides and roll again.
1. Apply some thick flour and water mixture to seal the open end of the roll.
1. Make all the rolls and keep them covered.
1. Deep fry the spring rolls in batches in a deep fry pan, on low to medium heat till golden brown.
1. Drain on an absorbent paper to absorb the excess oil.
1. Serve Soya Spring Rolls as an appetiser or a tea time snack with Peanut Chilli Dip or Cottage Cheese Dip Recipe with Pepper and Olives.
