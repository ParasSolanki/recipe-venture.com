### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Masoor Dal (Whole).
- 1/2 cup Rocket leaves - chopped(optional use more spinach if you don't have).
- 1 cup Spinach - chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Butter.
- 1/2 cup Dry white wine.
- 1/4 cup Milk.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2-3 Basil leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1/4 cup Fresh cream.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin with the One Pot Creamy Spinach Lentils first, in a heavy bottom pan heat butter and add chopped onions to it, Sauté till the onions are translucent and add the washed lentils to the same.
1. Sauté for 2-3 minutes and add water to just cover the lentils, cover and cook for 45mins - 1 hour till the lentils are full cooked (you can alternatively use a pressure cooker to fasten this process)Add wine, spinach, rocket and tomato at this stage and let it simmer for 5-6 minutes.
1. Add chilli, basil leaves, cream and milk.
1. Bring to a boil and turn of the gas.
1. Let the lentils stand for 15-20 minutes to absorb flavours before serving.
1. Serve the One Pot Creamy Spinach Lentils for a sunday brunch  with some garlic bread on the side and Piña Colada Recipe (Pineapple with Coconut Milk Mocktail) for a refreshing drink.
