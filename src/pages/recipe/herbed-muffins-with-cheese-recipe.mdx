### Ingredients

- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Baking powder.
- 140 gram Buttermilk.
- 1/2 teaspoon Pizza seasoning.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 50 gram Mozzarella cheese - grated.
- 40 gram Butter - melted.
- 1/4 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1/2 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mixed Herbs (Dried).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Herbed Muffins With Cheese Recipe, preheat Oven to 150-160 degrees Celsius.
1. Mix all dry ingredients in a large Mixing bowl (flour, rawa, baking powder, baking soda, herbs, pizza seasoning, chilli flakes, and salt).
1. Mix all wet ingredients in a small mixing bowl (melted butter, buttermilk, and mozzarella).
1. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
1. Divide herbed muffin batter evenly into the greased Muffin tin or paper cups.
1. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the largest muffin comes out clean.
1. To make Herbed Muffins With Cheese Recipe more interesting, top it up with swirls of homemade mayonnaise, required oregano and chilli sauce and garnish with carrot and capsicum slices and serve.
1. Serve the Herbed Muffins With Cheese Recipe along with Tzatziki dip or Corn Pineapple Salsa Salad to enjoy the muffin.
