### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 2 Green Chilli.
- 400 grams Chicken - cut into pieces.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 2 cup Water.
- 1-1/2 cup Onion.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 teaspoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - beaten with a little water.
- 1/4 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken Korma recipe, firstly clean and wash the chicken pieces.
1. Drain the water completely and add it in a bowl along with pepper powder, kashmiri red chilli powder, cooking oil, salt and ginger garlic paste.
1. Mix everything well and keep it aside for about 30 minutes.
1. Add onions, salt, ginger and garlic pods in a pressure cooker with some water and pressure cook it for 1 whistle.
1. Let it cool down and add it in the mixer grinder.
1. Make a fine paste out of it and keep it aside.
1. Heat a heavy bottom pan with oil, add green cardamom.
1. After 10 seconds, add in the marinated chicken pieces and cook it for about 10 minutes.
1. The chicken will turn white from inside once it is cooked.
1. Take it out in a bowl.
1. In the same pan, add some more oil.
1. Add shahi jeera, green chillies and cook for about 10 seconds.
1. Next, switch the heat low, add in the onion paste, salt to taste and curd.
1. After a minute, switch the heat high and cook for about 6 to 7 minutes.
1. Once the chicken and gravy is properly cooked, sprinkle the garam masala powder and coriander leaves.
1. Switch of the gas, cover it and let it rest for 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
1. Serve Chicken Korma Recipe along with Pakki Mutton Biryani, Garlic Naan and Burani Raita for an Indian weekend dinner or for your main course during house parties.
