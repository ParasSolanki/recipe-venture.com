### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Curry powder.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cups Quinoa - cooked beforehand.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garlic - finely diced or crushed.
- 1/8 teaspoon Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 1 cup Onions - chopped.
- 2 Whole Eggs - whisked in a bowl.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - diced.
- 1 cup Brown Rice - cooked beforehand.
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1/2 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - finely diced.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Curry Fried Quinoa Rice Recipe, mix the cooked quinoa and brown rice in a bowl.
1. Break down the lumps with your hands and mix it well.
1. If required, grease your hands with a little vegetable oil.
1. Set it aside.
1. Heat a little oil in a pan and swirl it around the pan to coat.
1. Add the whisked egg and scramble vigorously to break it up into small bits.
1. When the eggs, are cooked, transfer them to a plate.
1. In the same wok, add the remaining oil.
1. Once it is warm, add garlic and curry leaves and saute it for 30 seconds.
1. Add the onions and peppers in the wok and saute it for another 30 seconds.
1. After 30 seconds, add the remaining vegetables which are peas and carrots.
1. Saute the vegetables on high heat for about a minute or two until they vegetables are half cooked.
1. Add the curry powder, soy sauce, salt, sugar and pepper.
1. Stir and cook for about 30 seconds.
1. Add the quinoa rice mixture and stir fry until well combined.
1. Lastly, Add the scrambled egg and combine.
1. Turn off the heat and transfer it to a serving plate.
1. Serve the Curry Fried Quinoa Rice with Pumpkin raita or Lauki raita.
