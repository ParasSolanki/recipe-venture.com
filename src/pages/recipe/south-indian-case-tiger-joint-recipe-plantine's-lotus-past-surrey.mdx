### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 teaspoon Sambar Powder.
- 1 tablespoon Tamarind Paste - (adjust).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 teaspoons Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 5 Shallots - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 2 teaspoons Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1/2 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 2 Tomatoes - roughly chopped.
- 2 Raw Banana - cut into cubes.
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery - powdered.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Vazhakkai Puli Kootu Recipe, peel the skin of the raw banana's and cut them into cubes and place them in a bowl of water to avoid discolouration.
1. Dry roast the channa dal, cumin, fennel seeds until golden brown.
1. Add coconut to this mix and roast till brown.
1. When the mixture gets cool, grind them into a smooth paste adding little water using a mixer grinder.
1. Now heat sesame oil in a pan, add the mustard and fenugreek seeds and let it crackle.
1. Add the asafoetida, red chilli and curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. Add the sliced shallots and fry until it turns slightly brown.
1. Add the drained raw banana cubes and tomatoes and saute for a minute.
1. Add the sambar powder, season with salt, add half a cup of water, mix and cook with a lid on until the raw banana is well cooked on low heat.
1. This will take around 10 minutes.
1. Add the ground spice mix, tamarind pulp and jaggery, adjust the salt and mix well.
1. Simmer for 5 minutes.
1. Once the dish comes together and becomes slightly thick, switch off the flame.
1. Serve the Vazhakkai Puli Kootu Recipe along with Steamed Rice, Beetroot Garlic Lemon Rasam and Papad for a simple yet delicious weeknight dinner.
