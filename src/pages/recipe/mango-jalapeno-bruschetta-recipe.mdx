### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Garlic - minced.
- 3 Mango (Ripe) - peeled.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 4 tablespoons Pickled Jalapenos.
- 1 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - to garnish.
- 1 Baguette.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mango Jalapeno Bruschetta Recipe, we need to first make the Garlic Yogurt.
1. To make the Garlic Yogurt In a mixing bowl, combine the hung yogurt with some salt, lemon juice and minced garlic, whisk well until smooth.
1. To assemble the Mango Jalapeno BruschettaSlice the baguette into 1/2 inch thickness, arrange the slices on a serving platter.

1. Garnish the Mango Jalapeno Bruschetta with mint leaves and serve.
