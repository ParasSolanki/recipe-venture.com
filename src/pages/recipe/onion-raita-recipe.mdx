### Ingredients

-  2 sprig green coriander - finely chopped.
-  2 sprig mint - finely chopped.
-  1/4 cup water.
- 1/2 cup yogurt.
-  1 onion - cut thin and straight.
-  salt - as per taste 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder.
-  1 green chilli - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make onion raita recipe, first cut the onion thin and straight.
1. Finely chop green chili, mint and coriander.
1. Now add yogurt, salt, cumin powder to a mixing bowl and mix well.
1. Now add water, onion, green chillies, mint, coriander and mix well as per the experiment.
1. Serve.
1. Serve Onion Raita Recipe with Ambur Star Chicken Biryani or Chettinad Mint Potato Biryani and Chilli Salon.
