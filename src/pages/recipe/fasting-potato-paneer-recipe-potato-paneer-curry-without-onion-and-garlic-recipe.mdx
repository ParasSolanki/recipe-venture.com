### Ingredients

-  1 teaspoon Cumin Powder - Baked.
-  2 inch Ginger - Grated.
-  5 Tomatoes.
- 7 Potatoes - Chopped.
-  1 teaspoon Red Chilli Powder.
-  350 gms Cheese - Chopped.
-  Salt - As per taste.
-  oil - Green coriander - Finely chopped according to use.
-  1 teaspoon Coriander Powder.
-  2 Green Chillies.

### Instructions

1. To make Aloo Paneer Curry, firstly grind tomatoes, ginger and green chillies in a blender.
1. Keep it separately.
1. Heat the oil in a pressure cooker and add grated ginger to it.
1. Cook it for 1 minute.
1. Add tomato puree to it and cook for at least 10 minutes.
1. Now add red chili powder, coriander powder and salt.
1. Cook it well for the next 5 minutes.
1. Add potatoes and cook for 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes add 2 cups of water and roasted cumin seeds.
1. Close the cooker and cook till one city comes.
1. After coming to a city, turn off the gas and let the pressure drain.
1. Open the cooker and let the curry boil.
1. Add pieces of pannaer, cook for 2 minutes and garnish with green coriander.
