### Ingredients

- 1 Garlic - crushed.
- 1 tablespoon Onions - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2-3 tablespoons Del Monte Mint Mayo.
- 2-3 tablespoons Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - crushed.
- 10-12 Button mushrooms - cleaned.
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 Carrot (Gajjar) - peeled and grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Mushrooms Stuffed With Carrots And Mint Mayo Recipe, first pre- heat the oven to 190 degree Celsius.
1. Lightly grease a mini muffin pan or baking sheet with few drops little oil.
1. Chop the mushroom stems.
1. In a small pan, heat the oil.
1. Add garlic, onion and sauté for a few seconds.
1. Add the carrots and chopped mushroom stem.
1. Sprinkle some salt and sauté for 1 minute or till the carrots and mushrooms sweat out.
1. Take it off the heat.
1. Add black pepper and Del Monte Mint Mayo.
1. Using a spoon fill the mushroom caps with the carrot-mayo mixture.
1. Sprinkle crushed groundnuts over it.
1. Bake for 10-12 minutes till the mushrooms are cooked and filling slightly browns.
1. You can alternatively pan fry the mushrooms using little oil.
1. Serve Mushrooms Stuffed With Carrots And Mint Mayo Recipe hot as an appetizer or as an evening snack.
