### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 Gram flour (besan).
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- Water - as required.
- 1 tablespoon Baking powder.
- Butter - to smear.
- 1/2 Cup Onion - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1/2 Tablespoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 6 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 1/4 cup Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1/4 All Purpose Flour (Maida).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Eggless Bread Omelette Recipe, take a mixing bowl, add besan, all purpose flour, red chilli powder, salt to taste and chaat masala.
1. Add water and make a thin batter.
1. Add baking Powder and mix it in the batter.
1. Heat a non stick pan, add one tablespoon of butter .
1. Once butter is melted, add chopped onions, green chillies, chopped coriander and chopped tomato, saute it for a few seconds.
1. Add one ladle or one and half (according to pan size), spread the batter.
1. Let it cook for few seconds, place one bread in middle and then flip bread again.
1. This is for the bread to coat with the batter.
1. Let the omelette cook from one side and then flip it along with the bread to other side.
1. Once it is cooked properly, tuck on the omelette side .
1. Eggless Bread Omelette is ready to serve.
1. Repeat Same process for other bread slices.
1. Serve Eggless Bread Omelette Recipe with Spiced Pumpkin Smoothie Recipe for breakfast.
