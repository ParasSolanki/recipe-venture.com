### Ingredients

- 10 Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Green Chillies.
- Salt - to taste.
- 8 Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - roughly chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Coconut Oil.
- 2 Raw Banana - boiled and diced.

### Instructions

1. To begin with, the Raw banana Thoran first, cut the raw banana into half and boil it in the pressure cooker for 3 to 4 whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Once cooked, through, peel the skin and cut into dices.
1. Blend all the ingredients "to be ground" into a coarse paste and keep aside.
1. In a heavy bottom pan, add mustard seeds and allow it to cracke.
1. When they crackle add dry red chillies, curry leaves, onions and garlic.
1. Saut√© until the onions soften.
1. Add the cooked banana, the ground coconut mixture and give it a stir.
1. Add the salt to taste and stir fry for about 3 to 4 minutes until the raw banana thoran is well incorporated.
1. Once done, serve warm.
1. Serve the Raw Banana Thoran along with Steamed Rice, Beetroot Sambar and Yogurt for a comforting meal.
