### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Parsley leaves - chopped.
- 4-5 Button mushrooms - coarsely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese - grated.
- 1/2 Onion - chopped.
- 3 teaspoon Britannia Cream Cheese.
- Black pepper powder - as per your taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese - grated.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Spinach - chopped.
- 1-1/2 tablespoons Water - or more if needed.
- Salt - as per your taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 5 tablespoon Butter.
- 5 Basil leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 Whole Eggs.
- 1 Egg white - beaten for egg wash.
- 2 cups All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 5 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2-3 clove Garlic - minced.
- 1 tablespoon Chives - chopped.

### Instructions

1. For the ravioli dough: To begin making the raviolis, put 1 teaspoon olive oil, water and whole eggs in a mixing bowl and blend it properly.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now take a different bowl and put flour and salt in it and make a well in the centre.
1. Now pour the egg mixture into the well and mix it properly until it forms a dough.
1. Put the dough on a lightly floured surface and knead it for 5-10 minutes, until it becomes smooth.
1. Wrap dough tightly with plastic wrap, and set it aside to rest.
1. For the stuffing: While the dough is resting, We will begin making the filling for the raviolis.
1. Heat 1 teaspoon of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
1. Add the garlic and onions and cook it for 2 minutes until tit becomes soft.
1. Now add the mushrooms and cook it for another 10 minutes till the mushrooms becomes soft.
1. Switch off the heat and let it cool down.
1. Now to begin making the Mushroom Stuffed Ravioli With Burnt Butter Sauce, beat the cream cheese in a bowl until it becomes soft.
1. Now put the mushroom mixture in the bowl and add parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, spinach, chives, parsley and cayenne powder.
1. Sprinkle some salt and pepper according to your taste and keep it aside.
1. Now roll the pasta dough and make raviolis using a pasta roller or by your hands.
1. Cut circles using a large (3.
1. 5-3 inch) cookie cutter and coop about 1 tablespoon full of the filling onto the center of the pasta.
1. Brush the pasta lightly with the egg white and then cover with a second piece of pasta, pinching the edges to seal.
1. Cut the sealed ravioli with the cookie cutter one more time to get a uniform shape.
1. Indent the edges lightly with a fork and place the finished ravioli on a floured baking sheet, and repeat the process with the remaining pastas and the filling.
1. Now fill a large vessel with slightly salted water and bring it to a boil on high flame.
1. Now put the raviolis in the boiling water and let them cook for 3-4 minutes until it floats on the top.
1. For the sauce: To make the sauce, melt the butter in a skillet over high heat for about 4-5 minutes.
1. Now put the chopped garlic and cook it until it becomes brown.
1. Switch off the heat and add the basic and all the seasonings.
1. In the same skillet, add the cooked raviolis and toss it for another 30 seconds and itâ€™s ready to eat.
1. Garnish Mushroom Stuffed Ravioli With Burnt Butter Sauce with fresh cheese, blistered tomatoes and a little more of the sauce, and serve hot along with Pizza Margherita.
