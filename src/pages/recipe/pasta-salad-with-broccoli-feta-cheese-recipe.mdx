### Ingredients

- 250 grams Macaroni pasta.
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 cup Cherry tomatoes - cut into half.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoons Whole Black Peppercorns - freshly pounded.
- 1/2 cup Broccoli - cut into small florets.
- 1/4 cup Feta Cheese - crumbled.
- 4 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 2 Stalks Parsley leaves - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Pasta Salad With Broccoli & Feta Cheese Recipe,To Cook the PastaIn a large pot, add water and bring it to a boil.
1. Once the water has boiled, add a teaspoon of salt.
1. Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook until the pasta is cooked till al dente (just cooked).
1. This process should take a good 12-15 minutes to get an al dente pasta.
1. Once the pasta has cooked, drain the water and run the cooked pasta under running water to stop the cooking process and prevent the pasta from sticking to each other.
1. Drizzle some oil on top of pasta and make sure to coat all of the pasta with the oil so that the pasta doesn't stick to each other.
1. To Make the Pasta Salad With Broccoli & Feta Cheese RecipeCook the broccoli florets in a pressure cooker with 2 tablespoon of water for 1 whistle and immediately remove the pressure under running tap water, this is done to keep the broccoli fresh and tender.
1. In a small mixing bowl, prepare the dressing by adding parsley leaves, oregano, lemon juice, crushed peppercorns, salt along with olive oil.
1. Whisk well until it combines.
1. Take a large mixing bowl, add cooked pasta (cold/at room temperature), veggies - broccoli, cherry tomatoes,crumbled feta cheese and dressing to it.
1. Toss everything together gently and adjust the seasoning.
1. Serve Pasta Salad With Broccoli & Feta Cheese Recipe Roasted Vegetable Pizza Pockets, Cream of Celery Soup and Yogurt Panna Cotta to make a complete weekend dinner menu.
