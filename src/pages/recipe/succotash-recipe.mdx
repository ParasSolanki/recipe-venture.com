### Ingredients

- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 2 Spring Onion Greens - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Rajma (Large Kidney Beans) - cooked.
- 1/2 cup Lima Beans - cooked.
- 2 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Corn Kernels - steamed.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Butter (unsalted).

### Instructions

1. To start making Succotash, in a medium sauté pan, melt the butter over medium heat.
1. Add the minced garlic, sauté for a few seconds.
1. Next add onions, along with white part of spring onions and cook for 2 minutes or until they are translucent.
1. Next, add the red bell pepper and sauté until the peppers are well roasted.
1. Once the peppers are roasted, add the corn kernels, red kidney beans, lima beans, salt and pepper and stir fry until all the ingredients are well combined and the beans have absorbed the flavors.
1. You can additionally add herbs like basil, coriander or parsley to enhance the flavor of the Succotash Recipe.
1. Serve Succotash as a salad or a side dish for dinner along with a side of Garlic Bread with Herb Butter.
