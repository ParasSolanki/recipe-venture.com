### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 2 Onion - finely chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 cup Spinach.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- 4 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Masala Palak Bhurji Recipe by heating a pan with oil, splutter the ajwain seeds, add in the onions and bell peppers saute till the onions become translucent.
1. Then add in the tomatoes and add the spice powders along with salt and pepper.
1. Give it a stir.
1. Add the chopped spinach and wet the spinach and cook till it wilts.
1. Then finally crack one egg at a time.
1. Start mixing the egg with the masala that is been saut√©ed.
1. Mix well and combine all the ingredients together.
1. Check for seasoning once done, and serve.
1. Serve the Masala Palak Bhurji Recipe along with toasted bread and a Chickoo Banana Date Smoothie for your healthy breakfast.
