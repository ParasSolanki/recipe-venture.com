### Ingredients

- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 Tomato.
- 1 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1 tablespoon Rasam Powder.
- 1/2 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal) - cooked.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 cloves Garlic - crushed.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - roasted.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf) - crushed.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Roasted Bell Pepper Rasam Recipe we will first roasted the green bell pepper on a flat skillet, or grill till it is completely roasted on the outside.
1. Transfer to a bowl, allow it cool down and cut open the green bell pepper and deseed it completely, cut into small pieces and set aside.
1.  In a saucepan, combine the bell peppers, tomatoes, curry leaves and water, bring to a rolling boil on high heat.
1. Turn off the flame Strain the liquid and allow the bell peppers, curry leaves and tomatoes to cool down and blitz it in a grinder to a smooth paste.
1. Now heat the tamarind water along with 1 cup water in a big saucepan, add in your ground pepper and tomatoes and the rasam powder along with garlic, fennel seeds, peppercorns.
1. Allow this to boil.
1. Next pressure cook the dal along with 1/3 cup of water for about 3 whistles.
1. Turn off the flame and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Open the pressure cooker and transfer the dal into to the boiling bell pepper mixture.
1. Let the rasam boil for another 10 more minutes.
1. To make the tempering, heat a small pan with oil on medium heat, crackle the mustard seeds, curry leaves and asafoetida, and add in dry red chillies.
1. Pour it over the rasam and serve.
1. Serve the Roasted Bell Pepper Rasam Recipe along with steamed rice, Sweet Corn Poriyal and Mushroom Thoran Recipe for a complete meal.
