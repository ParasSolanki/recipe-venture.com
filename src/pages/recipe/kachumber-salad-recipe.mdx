### Ingredients

-  coriander leaves - little.
-  1 onion - finely chopped.
-  black pepper powder - According to taste.
-  1-1 / 2 teaspoon lemon juice.
-  1 teaspoon cumin powder - roast.
-  2 tomatoes - chopped.
-  1 green chili - finely chopped.
-  salt - as per taste.
- 3 cucumber - peel and chop.
-  1/4 cup pomegranate.
-  mint - little.
-  1 carrot - peeled and chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make the Kachumbar salad, mix cucumber, onion, green chillies, tomatoes, carrots and pomegranates in a mixing bowl.
1. Mix well.
1. After mixing, add lemon juice, black pepper powder, cumin powder, salt and mix well.
1. Now add green coriander, mint and mix well.
1. Serve Serve the Kachumbar Salad with punchy lentils, Aloo Bhindi Ki Sabzi and Phulka for dinner.
