### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 inch Ginger.
- 2 Tomatoes - ripe and pureed.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 Garlic.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/4 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 30 grams Tamarind.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - halved.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 3 cups Mixed vegetables - chopped and steamed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tarkari Ni Kari recipe, grind all the ingredients given under 'for the ground masala paste' to a smooth paste adding water and set it aside.
1. Heat a wide pan with oil and temper with ingredients given under tempering.
1. Once tempered add the tomato puree, turmeric powder, coriander powder, chilli powder, sugar, salt and allow it to cook for 3 to 4 minutes over low flame.
1. Add the steamed vegetables and the the ground masala paste and cook for another 5 to 7 minutes and switch off.
1. Your simple yet wholesome Parsi Style Tarkari Ni Kari is ready.
1. Serve Tarkari Ni Kari along with Steamed Rice Or Whole Wheat Lachha Paratha for a weekday lunch or dinner.
