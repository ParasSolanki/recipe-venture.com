### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - diced.
- 1 cup Broccoli - cut into florets.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 4 cups Vegetable stock.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Paprika powder.
- 1 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - chopped (red).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1-1/2 cups Cauliflower (gobi) - cut into florets.
- 3 Garlic - minced.
- Cheese - grated.
- 2 teaspoon Italian seasoning.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Broccoli And Cauliflower Soup Italian Style recipe, chop broccoli & cauliflower into small florets and then blanch them.
1. Next heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a deep pan and add onion & garlic in it.
1. Cook for a couple of seconds.
1. Now add diced carrots into the pan and keep cooking for another 2 minutes and then add tomatoes, vegetable stock and all the seasoning which includes red chilli flakes, black pepper power, salt, italian seasoning and paprika.
1. Keep cooking for 2 minutes and then add blanched broccoli & cauliflower florets.
1. Cook until soup thickens and then taste the soup to adjust the seasoning.
1. Serve the soup hot top with grated cheese and fine chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve Broccoli And Cauliflower Soup Italian Style along with some Garlic Bread for a healthy and light winter dinner.
