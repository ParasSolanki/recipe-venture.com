### Ingredients

- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/3 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 3/4 cup Caster Sugar.
- 1/4 cup Almond Meal (Badam Powder).
- 1/2 cup Buttermilk - (or milk+1/2 tablespoon vinegar).
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 teaspoon Fresh orange juice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 3 tablespoon Orange marmalade jam - slightly warmed and then cooled.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 2 Whole Eggs.
- 4 tablespoon Orange marmalade jam - to drizzle/brush on the cake.
- 3/4 cup Whole Wheat Flour.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Orange Marmalade Tea Cake, preheat the oven to 180 degree celcius.
1. Prepare two 5 inch round pan or one 9 inch round pan or one 9 inch loaf pan.
1. Sieve the flours, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a mixing bowl.
1. Set it aside.
1. In another mixing bowl, beat the eggs till fluffy.
1. Add in the sugar, oil, marmalade, orange extract and whisk till blended properly.
1. Fold in the dry ingredients with the wet, alternating with the addition of buttermilk.
1. Pour the batter in the prepared pan and bake for about 30-35 minutes or till a cake tester comes out clean (time will vary depending on the oven and size of pan).
1. Carefully invert the cakes after 5 minutes onto a cooling rack and cool.
1. (Run a knife around the edges of the pan to loosen the cake from the sides).
1. Drizzle the Marmalade over the cakes when it is warm and not hot.
1. Serve Orange Marmalade Tea Cake with a hot cup of coffee or Masala chai.
