### Ingredients

- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 cup Wheat Berries/ Whole Wheat.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1/4 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Gehun ki Bikaneri Khichdi Recipe, we will soak the gehun (whole wheat) in water for at least 8 hours or overnight.
1. Once soaked, drain the water.
1. Blend the wheat in a mixer to a coarse mixture.
1. Add the soaked wheat, moong dal, and all the other remaining ingredients into the pressure cooker.
1. Add 3-1/2 cups of water, add the salt to taste, cover the cooker, and allow it to cook until you hear 3 to 4 whistles.
1. Turn the heat to low after 3 to 4 whistles and allow it to cook on low heat for another 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Heat a pan for preparing the tadka ,once done pour it over the Khichidi and serve warm.
1. Serve the Gehun ki Bikaneri Khichdi along with Marwadi Gatte ki Kadhi Recipe for a wholesome lunch or dinner.
