### Ingredients

- 2 inch Ginger.
- Water - as required.
- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- 8 to 9 Green Chillies.
- Salt - to taste.
- 5 to 6 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1/2 cup Rice flour.
- 1/2 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 1/2 cup White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/2 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 3/4 cup Whole Wheat Flour.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Andhra Style Multi Lentil Garelu (Vada Recipe), dry grind the dals coarsely and add rice flour.
1. Mix thoroughly.
1. Add enough water and make a thick vada/garelu batter along with ginger and green chillies in a grinder.
1. Add salt, chopped curry leaves, coriander leaves and wheat flour to the prepared batter.
1. Adjust water only if required.
1. The batter needs to be really thick like dough to shape them.
1. Heat oil in a deep frying pan.
1. Apply a little oil on to a parchment or butter paper, take a golf ball sized dough, pat with your fingers on the greased parchment paper to make disc and make a hole in center.
1. Carefully drop it in hot oil and fry till beautiful golden on both sides, flipping them as required.
1. Repeat to make other garelus.
1. Drain excess oil on a kitchen towel.
1. Serve Andhra Style Multi Lentil Garelu with Chutney.
