### Ingredients

-  pepper powder .
-  Oil - or ghee.
-  1 tsp turmeric powder.
-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  1 tsp cumin powder.
- 1-1 / 2 cup wheat flour.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  coriander leaves - a little.
-  1 inch ginger - grated.

### Instructions

1. To make Green Coriander Paratha Recipe, first of all add wheat flour, cumin powder, turmeric powder, coriander, green chillies, ginger, salt, black pepper to a bowl and mix it well.
1. Now slowly add water and flour Knead until soft.
1. Apply some oil on it and cover it and keep it aside for 10 minutes.
1. Now take some flour from the dough and make round balls.
1. Dust in the dry flour and take a round ball.
1. Place a tawa on it for heating.
1. Now place the paratha over it, add some ghee and roast it.
1. Turn over and apply some ghee and roast it well from the other side as well.
1. Serve.
1. Make the remaining parathas as well.
