### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 Onion - sliced and caramelised.
- 2 sprigs Spring Onion Greens - chopped.
- 1 Fresh Red chillies - slit diagonally.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 2 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 Stalk Lemon Grass - slit diagonally.
- 4 tablespoons Black Rice.
- 100 grams Tofu - cubed.
- 2 Whole Eggs.
- 2 sprigs Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thai Style Khao Tom Recipe, we will first make the broth, Heat 4 cups of water with black rice, lemon grass, onions, ginger and garlic.
1. Boil it in low heat for 25 minutes.
1. This will be your main broth for your soup.
1. In the mean while keep the egg to boil with shell, you can check our recipe on how to boil an egg.
1. Heat a sauce pan with oil and add the onions and saut√© them until it is brown and keep it aside.
1. Check if the black rice is cooked in the broth, the rice will little crunchy so do not overcook it.
1. The black rice will take about 30 minutes to cook.
1. Add the soy sauce, spring onions, coriander leaves and check for salt.
1. To garnish, add the broth to a serving bowl, place the peeled boiled egg, add caramelized onions, fresh tofu and garnish it with some more coriander leaves and serve.
1. Serve the Thai Style Khao Tom Recipe as a one dish meal for your morning breakfast as a healthy start and accompanied with Thai Grilled Pineapple or a simple Thai Som Tum salad by the side.
