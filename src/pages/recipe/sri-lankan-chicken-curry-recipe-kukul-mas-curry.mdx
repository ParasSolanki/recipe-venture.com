### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Pepper Corns.
- 3 Dry Red Chillies.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 300 grams Chicken - curry cut pieces.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 250 ml Coconut milk.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 2 Onions - thinly sliced.
- 4 Cloves (Laung).
- 4 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sri Lankan Chicken Curry Recipe - Kukul Mas Curry, thoroughly wash the chicken pieces and set aside.
1. The first step is to make the Sri Lankan curry powder.
1. In a small pan; add all the ingredients for the spice powder including coriander seeds, cumin seeds, whole black pepper corns, mustard seeds, cloves, cardamom pods, dalchini, saunf, dry red chillies, cinnamon stick and roast on medium heat for 3 to 4 minutes until you can see the spices crackling and the aromas coming through.
1. Once it is roasted, switch off the heat and let it cool down.
1. Once it is cooled down, add it in the mixer grinder and make a powder.
1. Your Srilankan Powder is ready.
1. In a pressure cooker, heat oil on medium flame.
1. Add ginger, garlic, onions, red chillies and curry leaves.
1. Saute for a good 4-6 minutes, until it turns into a deep brown colour.
1. To this add the Sri Lankan curry powder, mix well.
1. Add the chicken and 2 cups of water.
1. Close the lid and pressure cook the Kukul Mas Curry for 2 whistles.
1. Turn off the heat, allowing the pressure to release naturally.
1. Once the pressure is release, open the lid, mix in the coconut milk into the Kukul Mas Curry and simmer on low flame for 5 minutes.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and transfer the Kukul Mas Curry to as serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Sri Lankan Chicken Curry Recipe (Kukul Mas Curry) hot with Dhaba Style Spicy Ghee Rice Recipe Made From Whole Spices or Veechu Parotta Or Ceylon Parotta Recipe and Pickled Onions Recipe for a completely satisfying meal.
