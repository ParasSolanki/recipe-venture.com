### Ingredients

- 4 Tortillas - or Large Roti.
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 3 Onions - thinly sliced.
- 1/2 cup Curry leaves.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Enos fruit salt.
- 1/4 cup Rice flour.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 3 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 1 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 1 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- 4 tablespoons Veeba Carrot Cucumber Sandwich Spread.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Tomato - jullienned.
- 1 Lemon juice - juiced.
- 1 Cucumber - julienned.
- Sunflower Oil - for pan frying.
- Chaat Masala Powder - for seasoning.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Onion Pakora Roll Recipe, prep all the ingredients and keep it ready.
1. *First we will make the Green Chutney for filling in the wraps:Add the coriander leaves, mint leaves, curry leaves, peanuts, ginger, green chillies, sugar, lemon juice and salt, into a mixer grinder and grind to a smooth paste in a blender, adding very little water.
1. Once ready, keep it aside.
1. *To make the Onion Pakora:In a large mixing bowl, add all the ingredients to make a onion pakora batter - rice flour, besan, onion, ajwain, green chillies, coriander leaves, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, enos fruit salt and regular salt to taste.
1. Mix well to combine.
1. Add little water at a time to make a smooth thick onion pakora batter.
1. Check the salt and spice and adjust to suit your taste.
1. We will be using a Kuzhi Paniyaram Pan to make the pan fried onion pakoras.
1. Preheat the Kuzhi Paniyaram Pan on medium heat.
1. Add a few drops of oil into each of the cavities.
1. Spoon a portion of the onion pakora mixture into the cavities and allow the pakoras to cook on medium heat until browned and crisp on both sides.
1. Flip the pakoras around as you are cooking so it cooks evenly on all sides.
1. Once done, place the Pan Fried Onion Pakoras on a platter.
1. To assemble the Onion Pakora Roll, place the tortilla on a preheated pan with a little oil to warm them a little.
1.  Transfer the tortilla onto a flat surface.
1. Spread the Carrot Cucumber Sandwich Spread over the tortilla, place the onions, tomatoes, cucumber and onion pakora in the center of the tortilla.
1. Sprinkle some chaat masala over the pakoras and drizzle green chutney and roll into a wrap.
1. Serve the Onion Pakora Roll Recipe along with a hot cup of Adrak Chai for an evening snack or even make it for a quick weeknight dinner.
