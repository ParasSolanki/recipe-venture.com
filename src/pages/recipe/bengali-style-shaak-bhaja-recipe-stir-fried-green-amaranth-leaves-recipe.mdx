### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Poppy seeds.
- 1/2 cup Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - roasted.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 3 Garlic - cloves.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 300 grams Green Amaranth Leaves.
- Mustard oil - for cooking.

### Instructions

1. We begin making Bengali Style Shaak Bhaja Recipe (Stir Fried Green Amaranth Recipe) by washing the leaves thoroughly 3 times.
1. Chop the leaves and cook it with a half cup of water in a vessel on high heat for about 2 minutes.
1. In an another Kadai heat oil and add peanuts and fry on medium heat.
1. And then remove it.
1. With the remaining oil temper red chili, poppy seeds, ginger, and garlic.
1. Then later add the peanuts and the cooked leaves with cumin powder.
1. Adjust the seasoning with salt.
1. Serve your Shaak Bhaja (Stir Fried Green Amaranth leaves) with crispy and layered Lachha Paratha.
