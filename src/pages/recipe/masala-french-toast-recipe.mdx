### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread - slices.
- 1/4 cup Milk.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 4 tablespoons Butter.
- 2 Whole Eggs.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.

### Instructions

1. To prepare the Masala French Toast, first make the topping by chopping the onion, tomato, and green chili.
1. Keep it aside in a bowl.
1. Next cut each slice of bread diagonally into two triangles and keep aside.
1. In a deep and wide bowl whisk the eggs along with milk, salt, red chili powder and black pepper for few minutes or till combined well.
1. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.
1. Dip one triangle of bread in the egg mixture until its evenly coated.
1. Using a slotted spoon transfer the egg coated bread slice to the preheated skillet.
1. Cook until evenly browned on both the sides, by flipping over alternatively.
1. Once well toasted, transfer the French Toast to a wire rack.
1. Cook the remaining bread slices the similar way.
1. Once all the toast are cooked, arrange them in a serving platter.
1. Place equal amount of onion mixture on each french toast.
1. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and finally sprinkle chaat masala on the top.
1. Serve Masala French Toast warm, along with green chutney and a cup of tea/coffee for the evening snacks or breakfast.
