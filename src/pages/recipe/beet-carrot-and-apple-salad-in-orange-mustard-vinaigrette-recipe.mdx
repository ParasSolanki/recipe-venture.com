### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh orange juice.
- Salt - to taste.
- 5 Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 1 tablespoon Mustard sauce.
- 1 Beetroot - spiralized to thin noodles.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 6 Walnuts.
- 1 Apple - cored and spiralized to thin noodles.
- 1/2 teaspoon Orange Zest (Rind).
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - spiralized to thin noodles.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - toasted.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Beet Carrot and Apple Salad in Orange Mustard Vinaigrette Recipe, wash, peel the skin of beetroot and make spiralized noodles and keep aside.
1. Likewise, make the spiralized noodles of carrot and apple and keep aside.
1. Now, take a small bowl and whisk together all the ingredients listed in the vinaigrette till viscous and keep aside.
1. Take a big bowl, add the spiralized beet, carrot, apple, add the prepared vinaigrette.
1. Place a portion of the salad on the serving plate, garnish with walnuts, mint leaves and toasted sesame.
1. Serve the Beet Carrot and Apple Salad in Orange Mustard Vinaigrette Recipe for light dinner on its own or with a Roasted Tomato and Pumpkin soup for a weeknight dinner.
