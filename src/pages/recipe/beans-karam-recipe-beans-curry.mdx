### Ingredients

- 1 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 Tamarind - lemon ball sized.
- 500 grams Green beans (French Beans).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 10 Curry leaves.
- 2 Onions - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Jaggery.
- 2 tablespoons Dessicated Coconut.
- 1 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Byadagi Dried Chillies.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 tablespoon Poppy seeds.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Beans Karam recipe, roast coriander seeds, cumin seeds, chillies, dry coconut, poppy seeds and fresh coconut in a pan with little oil.
1. Grind this mixture with tamarind extract, jaggery, 1/2 an onion and salt into a paste in a mixer grinder.
1. Cook the beans till tender in a pan with some water.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan, add mustard seeds and let them crackle.
1. Next, add hing, curry leaves, 1-1/2 onions with a pinch of salt and let it cook until transparent.
1. Add turmeric powder, beans and saute for a minute.
1. Add the ground masala paste to the pan and cook for 5 to 6 minutes while stirring in between.
1. Add a cup of water and bring this to a boil.
1. Adjust the seasoning and serve hot.
1. Serve Beans Karam Recipe along with Phulkas, Steamed Rice and Puli Manga Kuzhambu Recipe for a complete meal.
