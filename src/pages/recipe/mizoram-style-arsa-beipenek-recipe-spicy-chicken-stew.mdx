### Ingredients

- 10 Dry Red Chilli.
- 500 grams Chicken - with bone.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 6 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Mustard oil - cooking.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mizoram Style Arsa Beipenek Recipe, wash the chicken pieces thoroughly with water.
1. Marinate the chicken with chopped onions, ginger, garlic, turmeric powder and little bit of salt.
1. Leave the chicken for at least 30 minutes.
1. Heat a kadai with oil and add the marinated chicken into the hot oil.
1. Fry them for at least 25 minutes.
1. Add about 1 1/2 cup of water, salt, dry red chilies and cook the chicken in the pan for about 20 more minutes.
1. In a separate bowl mix flour and water to form a thick paste and keep it ready, once the chicken is cooked you can add the wheat flour mixture and start stirring till it thickensOnce the gravy thickens, check for salt and garnish it with coriander leaves.
1. Serve the Mizoram Style Arsa Beipenek along with steamed rice, tawa paratha to enjoy your Sunday meal.
