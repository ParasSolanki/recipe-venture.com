### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoon Raisins.
- 1 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1/4 cup Britannia Cream Cheese.
- 12 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - parsley or any herb of choice (optional).
- 8 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cream Cheese Carrot Raisin Sandwich Recipe,spread cream cheese on one side of all 4 bread slices.
1. Spread grated carrots and then raisins on the top.
1. Place the bread slices together to form Cream Cheese Carrot Raisin Sandwich and serve.
1. Serve Cream Cheese Carrot Raisin Sandwich Recipe with Papaya & Banana or Strawberry & Oats Smoothie for your morning breakfast or your  Kids Lunch Box.
