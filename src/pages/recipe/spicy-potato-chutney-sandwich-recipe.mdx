### Ingredients

- 3 Green Chillies.
- 2 Tomatoes - thinly sliced.
- 1 Beetroot - boiled and sliced.
- Water - as required.
- 1/2 cup Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- Chaat Masala Powder - for taste.
- 1 Onion - sliced very thinly.
- Salted butter - softened for sandwich.
- 12 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 4 Potatoes (Aloo) - peeled and sliced thick.
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 4 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 1 Cucumber - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Potato Chutney Sandwich Recipe, first you need to prepare the mint chutney.
1. To do that, grind together all the ingredients listed under â€œFor green chutney" including green chillies, coriander leaves, mint leaves, lemon juice, salt and water in a mixer-grinder, into a smooth paste.
1. You can also use this recipe to make a Mint-Coriander chutney.
1. Next, stack the slices of bread and carefully trim the edges with a knife.
1. Generously apply butter and green chutney on each slice of bread.
1. Now, assemble the fillings with layers of vegetables, with chaat masala sprinkled in between.
1. Begin with cucumber and potato slices with chat masala in between.
1. Then place the second slice of bread on top of the potatoes, with the butter and green chutney side facing up.
1. Place 4 slices of tomato, 2 slices of onions, and 2 slices of beetroot on top of the second slice, followed by a sprinkling of chaat masala on top.
1. Next place the third slice of bread with the buttered side facing downward.
1. Press the Potato Chutney Sandwich down so it holds together all the layers.
1. Then gently cut it diagonally, into four equal parts.
1. Serve the Potato Chutney Sandwich Recipe along with a cup of refreshing cup of Masala Chai and a Fruit Bowl for your breakfast.
