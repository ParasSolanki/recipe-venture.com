### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- Coconut Oil - for cooking.
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1 cup Coconut milk.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 500 grams Shrimps - cleaned and deveined.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Pearl onions (Sambar Onions).
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kerala Style Chemmeen Moilee Recipe - Prawn Curry, marinate the shrimps with turmeric powder and lemon juice and keep it aside for at least 15 minutes.
1. Heat a kadai with oil, add cinnamon, cardamom pods, pepper corns and allow it to sizzle.
1. Add whole shallots and saute until they turn slightly brown.
1. Once done add chopped ginger, garlic and saute until they soften.
1. Now add the tomatoes and salt to taste.
1. Do not mash them completely.
1. Cook till the tomatoes just turn soft.
1. Once done add coconut milk along with turmeric powder and salt and bring it to a boil.
1. Stir in the sugar and slowly drop in the shrimps and cook in a medium heat for about 20 minutes till the shrimp are cooked.
1. Once done turn off the heat and transfer the Kerala Style Chemmeen Moilee to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve the Kerala Style Chemmeen Moilee Recipe along with Steamed Rice or kerala parotta and Cabbage Thoran.
