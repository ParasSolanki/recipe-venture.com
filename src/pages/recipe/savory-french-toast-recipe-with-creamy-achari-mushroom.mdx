### Ingredients

- Nutralite Garlic & Oregano Spread - as needed.
- 1/4 Cup Fresh cream.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread - brioche bread preferred.
- 1/4 teaspoon Whole Black Pepper Corns - coarsely pounded.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1/4 Cup Milk.
- 3 Whole Eggs.
- 1 Onion - finely Chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Achari Mayo.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- 200 Grams Button mushrooms - sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Savory French Toast Recipe With Creamy Achari Mushroom, first prep all the ingredients and keep ready.
1. Melt Nutralite Garlic & Oregano Spread in a stir fry pan; add the onionS and saute until softened and lightly browned.
1. Add the chopped green chillies and sliced mushrooms, sprinkle some salt and saute until the mushrooms become soft.
1. A this stage add the Nutralite Achari Mayo, give it a stir and turn off the heat.
1. Keep the Creamy Achari Mushroom aside.
1. For the Savoury French Toast, in a mixing bowl; break the eggs, add milk, cream, salt and pepper.
1. Whisk to combine well until smooth.
1. Dip the bread slices in the egg mixture and allow the slices to get well soaked.
1. Melt the Nutralite Garlic & Oregano spread in a preheated pan; place these egg dipped bread slices in the pan and cook on medium high heat until the French Toast is golden brown and all of the egg mixture is cooked through and absorbed well into the bread.
1. Once done, place the French Toast on a serving platter, spoon the creamy Achari Mushroom filling on the top and serve hot.
1. Serve the Savory French Toast Topped With Creamy Achari Mushroom along with a cup of coffee for a wholesome high protein weekend breakfast.
