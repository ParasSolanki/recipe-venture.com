### Ingredients

- 100 grams Amla (Nellikai/ Gooseberry) - deseeded and finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds) - roasted and powdered.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Nellikai oorugai/Amla Sambhariyo Recipe (Instant Gooseberry Pickle Recipe) by de-seeding the gooseberries and keeping it aside.
1. Heat oil in a small skillet, splutter seeds, hing and red chili powder and add this to the gooseberries along with the rest of the ingredients and mix well.
1. Serve the Nellikai oorugai/Amla Sambhariyo Recipe (Instant Gooseberry Pickle Recipe) along with Sambar and some steamed rice.
