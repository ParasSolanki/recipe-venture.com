### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - Few sprigs.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Rice flour.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 tablespoons Corn flour.
- 1 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Cauliflower (gobi) - chopped into florets.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chettinad Style Chilli Gobhi Recipe, in a large pot, boil water and blanch cauliflower florets for one minute.
1. Drain and keep aside.
1. Take a bowl and whisk together corn starch, rice flour, red chilli powder, salt, pepper powder, ginger garlic paste and curd.
1. Dip blanched cauliflower florets in it and deep fry them in oil or fry them using the air fryer.
1. In a small pan, heat the ghee for tempering and add mustard seeds, curry leaves, red chillies, green chillies and fry for 2 minutes.
1. Pour over fried gobhi and serve hot garnished with a few coriander leaves.
1. Serve Chettinad Style Chilli Gobhi as a tea time snack along with a cup of Filter Coffee or as starter or as a side dish to a South Indian Sapad.
