### Ingredients

- 2 cups All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 2 teaspoons Sugar - 2 teaspoon sugar.
- Ghee - for layering.
- Salt - to taste.
- 35 grams Cashew nuts - ground to paste.
- 1 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).
- Sunflower Oil - to knead.
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 2 teaspoons Poppy seeds.
- 2 teaspoons Active dry yeast.
- 200 ml Milk.
- 50 ml Fresh cream.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Awadh Style Bakarhani Roti Recipe ( Thick Spiced Flat-Bread Recipe ) preheating the oven at 180-degree celsius for about 10 minutes.
1. Mix all the ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
1. Knead slowly to make a soft chapati dough, you can knead for about 5 minutes in order to get the right consistency.
1. Once done roll it into a small ball and cover the dough with a damp cloth and keep it in a warm place for about 2 hours.
1. Once the dough is raised after two hours , divide the dough into small equal balls and roll it on a board about 1 cm thin.
1. Apply ghee and sprinkle some dough on top and spread it evenly over the surface.
1. Then fold the dough in half and repeat the process of applying ghee and flour on the surface of the dough.
1. Once done fold over to form a triangle.
1. The again apply some more ghee.
1. Roll the dough to 2 cm thin and then from one of the long sides of the triangle start rolling the dough tightly till you reach the other side.
1. Pinch the open edges to secure it neatly.
1. Once it is rolled you will get a conical shaped dough,  start pressing the dough from the wide part of the cone by placing the thin end of the cone onto your palm resulting in a circular shape.
1. When the circular shape is made you can use your hand to press the dough to form the desired thickness , about  1/2 an inch.
1. Once it flattened, you can poke holes on one side to make the dough cook quickly.
1. Grease the pan on which you are placing the dough and cook the bread inside the oven for about 2 minutes on each side.
1. Make sure you flip the bread after every 2 minutes.
1. You can also cook the bread on a flat nonstick tawa on each side for about 4 minutes on each side.
1. Serve the Awadh Style Bakarhani Roti Recipe ( Thick Spiced Flat-Bread Recipe ) with Sultani dal to enjoy a rich Sunday lunch.
