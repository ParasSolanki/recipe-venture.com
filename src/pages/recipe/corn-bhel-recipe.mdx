### Ingredients

- 1 Potato (Aloo) - boiled.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1 cup Sweet corn - boiled.
- 2 tablespoons Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Sweet Chutney (Date & Tamarind).
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- Sev - to garnish.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Corn Bhel recipe, take a mixing bowl and mix corn kernels, onion, potato, green chillies and coriander leaves well.
1. Add both date tamarind, dhaniya pudina chutney and mix everything properly.
1. Next add chaat masala and salt to taste.
1. Sprinkle some sev and some more fresh coriander.
1. Mix everything and serve immediately.
1. Serve Corn Bhel along with Masala Chai for your evening snack.
