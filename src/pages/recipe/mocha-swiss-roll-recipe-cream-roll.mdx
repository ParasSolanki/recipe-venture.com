### Ingredients

- 1 Tablespoon Hot water.
- Butter - for greasing.
- 2 Tablespoons Instant Coffee Powder.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 3 Whole Eggs.
- 1 Cup Heavy whipping cream.
- 1/2 Cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 4 Tablespoons Caster Sugar.
- 1/2 Cup Sugar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Mocha Swiss Roll Recipe (Cream Roll), preheat oven to 350 degree Fahrenheit.
1. Sift all purpose flour (maida) in a bowl.
1. Take a big bowl and beat the eggs with hand mixer on high, till it becomes thick and fluffed up for about 2-3 minutes.
1. Add hot water and again beat the mixture for few more seconds.
1. Add sugar and keep beating until sugar is nicely combined, for just a couple of minutes.
1. Cut and fold the sifted flour in this wet mixture.
1. Add vanilla extract and again fold it.
1. Line up 13 x 9 inches (or accordingly) cake tray with aluminium foil and grease the foil with butter.
1. Pour the prepared cake batter into the tray and give a tap to it so that it spreads evenly popping away any air bubbles.
1. Bake for about 15 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center, comes out clean.
1. Once baked, Just flip the cake over a cooling rack and allow to cool down completely while you prepare your whipped cream spread.
1. In a mixing bowl, take in heavy whipping cream and sugar.
1. Beat using electric beater on low for a few seconds and gradually increase the speed and beat until the soft peaks are formed.
1. Add instant coffee powder and continue beating till you get stiff peaks.
1. Stop beating at this point.
1. Once the cake is cooled, flip it on a greased aluminum foil with smooth side down and spread the whipped cream evenly.
1. Gently roll it over starting from one end.
1. Wrap the cake with aluminium foil and refrigerate for about an hour.
1. Remove the cake from refrigerator and with a knife, cut it into pieces and serve.
1. You can serve Mocha Swiss Roll Recipe (Cream Roll) along with Cinnamon Spiced Orange Iced Tea Recipe on a quiet evening with family.
