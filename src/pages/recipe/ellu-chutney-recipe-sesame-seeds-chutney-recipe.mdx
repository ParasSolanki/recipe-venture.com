### Ingredients

- Curry leaves - a few.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 20 grams Tamarind - soaked in water.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 cup Black sesame seeds.
- 5 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 tablespoon White Urad Dal (Split).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Ellu Chutney (Sesame Seeds Chutney Recipe), soak tamarind in a very little water.
1. Dry roast urad dal until lightly roasted and browned.
1. Stir in the black sesame seeds, dry chillies, fresh grated coconutRoast for a few minutes.
1. In a mixer grinder, add sesame seeds, tamarind with water, red chillies, urad dal & salt to taste.
1. Grind it to a smooth, thick paste.
1. Transfer the chutney to a serving bowl.
1. In a tadka pan, heat oil on low flame and add mustard seeds and allow to splutter.
1. Immediately add curry leaves and allow to crackle for 5 seconds or more.
1. Pour this tempering on the chutney mix lightly and serve Ellu Chutney (Sesame Seeds Chutney Recipe) with your favorite idli / dosa recipe.
