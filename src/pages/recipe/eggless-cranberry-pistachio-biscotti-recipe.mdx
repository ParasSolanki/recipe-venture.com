### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Pistachios - unsalted.
- Salt - to taste.
- 5 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida) - plus more for dusting.
- 5 tablespoon Sugar.
- 1/4 cup Cranberries - dried.
- 1 teaspoons Baking powder.
- 2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cranberry Pistachio Biscotti RecipeAdd the flour, baking powder and salt in a bowl and mix well and keep aside.
1. In another bowl, add oil and sugar, whisk till it is creamy, and add in the cranberry and pistachios.
1. Now add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and combine till it forms a sticky dough.
1. Cover the dough using a cling film and refrigerate for 10 minutes.
1. After 10 minutes take the dough out of the fridge.
1.  Dust your hands with enough flour,roll the dough into a long log shape.
1. About 5 centimeters wide.
1. Place the log directly on a butter paper placed on a baking tray.
1. Bake the dough for 25 minutes till it forms a golden crust on the top.
1. Remove the dough and let it cool for 10 minutes.
1. After the dough is cooled a little use a serrated knife and cut the log into half-inch thick biscuits.
1. Do not use the sawing motion as it will crumble the Cranberry Pistachio Biscotti.
1. Turn the cut edges on the tray, keep the biscuits close enough as they won't spread.
1. Bake it again, for 10 more minutes and take it out, turn the cookies over and bake it again for 10 more minutes.
1. Serve the Cranberry Pistachio Biscotti Recipe for special festive seasons or just relish as a Tea Time snacks with some Ginger Cardamom Chai.
