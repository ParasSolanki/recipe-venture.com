### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cups Rice.
- 1 Onion - finely sliced.
- Parsley leaves - small bunch.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dry ginger powder.
- 4 Chicken - de skinned & diced.
- 3 cups Chicken stock.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 4 Cloves (Laung) - powdered.
- 3/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon All spice powder.
- 1/2 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - soaked overnight & boiled.
- 2 tablespoons Butter.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Spicy Moroccan Rice With Chicken Recipe, we will first make the spice mix.
1. In a mixer jar, combine the cumin, ground ginger, salt, black pepper, cinnamon stick, coriander seeds, red chilli powder, ground allspice, ground cloves and grind to a fine powder to make the Moroccan Spice Blend.
1. Mix the chicken pieces with about 2-3 tablespoons of Moroccan spice blend and a little water and keep aside for 30 minutes.
1. Heat butter in pressure cookeron medium heat and add sliced onion.
1. Saute until soft and transparent.
1. Add the marinated chicken pieces.
1. Saute and cook for about five minutes in medium heat.
1. Take the uncooked rice and add to the chicken mix.
1. Toss for a couple of minutes and add chicken broth and chickpeas.
1. Pressure cook until one whistle and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Serve Spicy Moroccan Rice With Chicken Recipe as a one pot dinner with Pomegranate Mint Cocktail Recipe and a Rich Chocolate Cheesecake Recipe as dessert.
