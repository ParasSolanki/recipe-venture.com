### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- Slivered Almonds (Badam) - for garnish.
- 250 grams Green beans (French Beans) - ends trimmed and cut in half.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies - broken into pieces.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoon Dessicated Coconut.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 7 Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the green beans salad recipe, first steam the green beans for 4 to 5 minutes till they are just cooked and are still crunchy.
1. Cool them down.
1. Toast the desiccated coconut in a small pan for a minute or till it just starts to turn brown.
1. Cool and add the coconut to the green beans in a mixing bowl.
1. Heat oil in the heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add the mustards seeds and once they splutter add the cumin seeds.
1. Add the chillies and saut√© for a few seconds.
1. Take the pan off the heat and add the hing and curry leaves.
1. Add this tadka to the green beans.
1. Add salt, lemon juice and toss everything together.
1. Keep for half an hour, garnish with the almond flakes and serve.
1. Serve Green Beans Salad along with Penne Pasta Arrabiata and end it with Mascarpone Fruit Dessert Recipe for a delicious weekend dinner.
