### Ingredients

- 8 Garlic - minced.
- 2 tablespoons Del Monte Tandoori Mayo.
- 1 cup Broccoli - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 15 Button mushrooms - chopped.
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1/2 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - julienne.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spicy Mushroom And Broccoli Stir Fry Recipe Flavored with Tandoori Mayo, prep all the ingredients and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a kadai/wok over medium heat.
1. Once the oil gets heated, add onion and stir fry till it turns pinkish.
1. Stir in the minced garlic and saute for a few seconds.
1. Stir in the bell peppers, broccoli, mushrooms and tomatoes.
1. Sprinkle some salt and saute until the vegetables have just about cooked through.
1. Once the vegetables have cooked through, stir in the Del Monte Tandoori Mayo, red chili powder and salt to taste.
1. Saute until all the ingredients are just about combined.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and serve.
1. Serve the Tandoori Mushroom And Broccoli Stir Fry as a side dish along with tortillas or even phulkas for dinner.
