### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 1 cup Basil leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Spirulina.
- 1/2 cup Badam (Almond) - blanched .
- 1/2 cup Tofu.
- 2 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/2 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Spirulina and Mint Basil Pesto Recipe by prepping all the ingredients and keeping it ready.
1. Add all the ingredients into a blender and blitz it into a smooth paste according to your consistency, check for seasoning.
1. Serve the Spirulina and Mint Basil Pesto Recipe along with Spirulina and Mint Basil Pesto Pasta Recipe to enjoy your lunch meal.
