### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- Mustard oil - as required to deep fr.
- 1/2 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - washed and soaked in water for 4 to 5 hours.
- 1/5 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoons Green peas (Matar).
- 2 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Basmati rice - washed and soaked in water for 4 to 5 hours.
- 3 Green Chillies - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Dhuska, grind rice and chana dal with water to a idli/dosa batter consistency.
1. Now add green peas, green chillies, salt, turmeric powder and chopped coriander.
1. Mix well.
1. Heat oil in a pan.
1. When the oil is hot, pour some batter directly in the oil with the help of a laddle.
1. Deep fry both the sides till it changes the colour.
1. Transfer them on a paper towel.
1. Repeat the process with with remaining batter.
1. Serve Dhuska along with Aloo Sabzi and Dhaniya Pudina Chutney for lunch on a weekend.
