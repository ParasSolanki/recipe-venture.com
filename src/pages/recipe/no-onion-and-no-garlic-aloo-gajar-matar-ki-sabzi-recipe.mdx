### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste (kala namak if using for a fast).
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - peeled and cubed.
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - as required for garnish.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - peeled and cubed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the No Onion And No Garlic Aloo Gajar Matar Ki Sabzi recipe, Heat oil in a kadhai/wok.
1. When oil gets heated add mustard seeds.
1. After mustard seeds flutter, add cumin seeds.
1. After cumin seeds flutter, add asafoetida and mix.
1. Add curry leaves and fry for 2 minutes.
1. Add the slit green chillies.
1. Now add the cubed potatoes, cubed carrots and green peas.
1. Add 1 cup water and simmer on low flame with closed lid till carrots and potatoes get boiled.
1. This would take around 10-15 minutes.
1. Open the lid and add turmeric powder and red chilli powder and mix well.
1. Add salt to taste and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve No Onion And No Garlic Aloo Gajar Matar Ki Sabzi with Panchmel Dal and Phulka for a weekday lunch or dinner.
