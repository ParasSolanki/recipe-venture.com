### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 cups Cooked rice.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 Green bell peppers - chopped.
- 2 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 cup Green Moong Sprouts.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sprouted Green Moong Bhat Recipe, soak the rice in water for about 10 minutes.
1. Wash the rice thoroughly and keep it aside.
1. Heat a pressure cooker and cook the rice with 2 cups water for about 2 whistle and switch off the heat.
1. All the pressure to release naturally and keep it aside.
1. Heat a kadai with oil, add cumin seeds and allow it to splutter for few seconds, add cinnamon stick, cardamom and cloves.
1. Allow the aroma to be released.
1. Add chopped onions, green chilli, ginger and garlic chopped and saute them until it softens.
1. You can add chopped bell peppers at this point along with all the spice powders which includes red chilli powder, turmeric powder and coriander powder.
1. Saute well till the raw smell goes away.
1.  Add the sprouted mung beans and mix well.
1. You can add the cooked rice and gently toss the masala over the rice till every grain is coated well.
1. Serve the Maharashtrian Green Moong Bhat with a healthy raita like Spinach Raita or just a simple Mint and Pomegranate Raita to enjoy your meal.
