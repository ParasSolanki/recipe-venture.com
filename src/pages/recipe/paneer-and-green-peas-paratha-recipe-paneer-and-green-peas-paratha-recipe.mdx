### Ingredients

-  1 tsp coriander powder.
-  1 cup wheat flour.
-  1 tsp turmeric powder.
-  1 tsp mango powder Powder.
- 1/2 cup green peas - make paste.
-  water - lukewarm.
-  cut 1 inch ginger - 1 green chili - chopped.
-  2 tsp red chili powder.
-  oil - according to use.
-  salt - according to taste.
-  1 cup cheese - crumble.

### Instructions

1. To make paneer and green pea paratha recipe, prepare all the ingredients first.
1. To make paratha flour: Now add wheat flour, salt, oil and mix in a bowl.
1. Add a little water and knead the dough.
1. Cover and keep aside.
1. To make the masala: Heat the oil in a pan.
1. Add cumin seeds and cook for a few seconds.
1. Now add ginger, green peas and cook half.
1. Now add cottage cheese, red chilli powder, coriander powder, mango powder, salt.
1. Mix well and keep aside.
1. Let it cool down.
1. Now take a little piece of flour and roll it out.
1. Place the mixture of paneer peas in the middle and close it from all sides.
1. Now coat it with dry flour.
1. Take the vine again.
1. Only fat, or else spices can come out.
1. Make all such parathas.
1. Heat a griddle.
1. Put paratha on it and apply ghee on top.
1. Cook till it turns golden brown from both sides and turn off the gas.
1. Serve Paneer and Hare Matar Paratha Recipe with Coriander Mint Chutney and Tadka Raita for an evening snack.
