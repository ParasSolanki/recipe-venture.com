### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder - ground.
- 1-1/2 tablespoons Paprika powder.
- 1/4 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 4 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 1 tablespoon Dried oregano.
- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 500 grams Boneless chicken.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 1 Onion - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Tomato Ketchup.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Dried Thyme Leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Shish Tawook recipe, in a mixing bowwl, combine the yogurt, paprika, garlic, ginger, dried oregano, dried thyme, olive oil,  lemon juice, tomato paste, ground pepper powder and Salt to taste.
1. Whisk everything properly and next, add the chicken cubes, capsicum and onions and mix well to coat all the chicken pieces.
1. Cover the mixing bowl and marinate the chicken pieces over night in the fridge.
1. Next day, fire up your outdoor grill or preheat the oven at home.
1. Immerse skewer sticks in water for about 15 minutes.
1. If you are using metal skewers rod, you can just go ahead with the next step.
1. Skewer the chicken cubes along with cubed capsicum and cubed onions.
1. Pour the remaining marinate over the skewed chicken.
1. Place on hot grill and grill until meat begins to brown.
1. Turnover and grill the other side too.
1. Once meat is grilled on both sides, remove from grill.
1. This will take about 7-10 minutes.
1. Serve Shish Tawook Recipe with Whole Wheat Pita Bread and Tzatziki Dip for a perfect weekend lunch with your friends and family.
