### Ingredients

- Lemon wedges - or slices to garnish.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 2 teaspoons Assam tea leaves.
- Honey - to taste (optional).
- 1 Lemon - juiced.
- 2 cups Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Cinnamon Spiced Black Lemon Tea Recipe, keep all the ingredients handy.
1. In a carafe or saucepan, heat water on high heat with cinnamon and bring it to a rolling boil.
1. Once the water comes to boil, add the assam tea leaves and squeeze in lemon juice and switch off heat.
1. Keep the container covered for a minute.
1. Stir in honey, if you are using.
1. Strain the tea, pour to tea cups and adorn it with a lemon slice/wedge to serve.
1. Serve Cinnamon Spiced Black Lemon Tea Recipe along with a Ginger Brandy Cookie Recipe, Tea Sandwich Recipe With Beets, Radish, Avocado and Arugula or Mumbai Style Spicy Murmura Recipe during evening.
