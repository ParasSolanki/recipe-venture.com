### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - cut into small pieces.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 cup Basmati rice - cooked.
- 1 cup Fish - cut into half.
- 2 tablespoons Dry coconut (kopra).
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Sunflower Oil - as needed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Assamese Fish Fried Rice Recipe, wash and cut fishes into 1 inch pieces.
1. In a bowl mix coriander powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder and ginger together.
1. Heat around 1-2 tablespoon oil in a pan, add the masala mix and fry till raw smell goes off.
1. Do not burn the masala.
1. Place the fish pieces in the pan and stir careful without breaking them.
1. Place the lid and let the fish cook in its own steam for 5 minutes on low flame.
1. Remove the fish carefully and place them in a plate or bowl.
1. Now in the remaining oil, add bay leaf, chopped onions and saut√© till onions are translucent.
1. Add desiccated coconut and fry till raw smell goes off.
1. Now add lemon juice, peas and carrots and fry for another couple of minutes.
1.  Add cooked rice and mix well such that the flavours incorporate and infuse completely.
1. Now add fried fish chunks into the rice, give a quick stir.
1. Garnish with fresh spring onion greens or coriander and serve hot! Serve Assamese Fish Fried Rice on its own or pair it with a Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita for your everyday meal.
