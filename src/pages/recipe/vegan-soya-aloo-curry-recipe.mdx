### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 4 Potatoes (Aloo) - cubed.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves) - crushed.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1 Onion - chopped finely.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 cup Soy Chunks (Nuggets) - cooked in water.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 tablespoon Garlic - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Vegan Soya Aloo Curry, firstly boil enough water in a pan, add the tomatoes, let it sit, remove the skin and chop them finely.
1. Heat oil in a Kadhai, add the asafoetida powder, cumin seeds, fry it.
1. Add the grated ginger, chopped garlic, chopped onions, cook until the onions turns translucent.
1. Once it is done, add the potatoes, all the spice powders and salt.
1. Cook everything for few minutes and then add the chopped tomatoes, slit opened green chillies and cook it for another 2-3 minutes.
1. Finally add the soya chunks, cook for few minutes, add water as per need.
1. Serve Vegan Soya Aloo Curry with Whole Wheat Lachha Paratha or Achari Masala Puri.
