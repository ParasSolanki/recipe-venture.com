### Ingredients

- 1 cup Spring Onion Greens - roughly chopped.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Salt - as needed.
- 1 Mango (Raw) - roughly chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Raw Mango and Spring Onion Chutney, firstly cut and wash spring onion leaves in a mixer grinder jar.
1. Add raw mango pieces, whole red chilies, cumin and sugar.
1. Now add a little water and grind them all together to make a thick chutney.
1. Add oil and blend again for few seconds .
1. Adjust salt according to your taste.
1. Use Raw Mango And Spring Onion Chutney Recipe as a spread for your sandwiches or you could also serve it along with Chicken Tikka and Pickled Onions Recipe along with some chilled beer.
