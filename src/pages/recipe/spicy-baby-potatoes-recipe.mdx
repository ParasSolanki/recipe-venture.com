### Ingredients

- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves - optional.
- 3 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 12 Baby Potatoes.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spicy Baby Potatoes recipe, pressure cook the baby potatoes and keep aside to cool.
1. Peel off the skin and collect the boiled potatoes in a bowl.
1. Heat oil in a wok/kadhai and add dry red chilies, curry leaves, cumin and let them crackle.
1. Now add all the spices (coriander powder, red chili powder, amchur, cumin powder, garam masala powder and turmeric).
1. Saute it for few seconds, don't burn it.
1. Add the baby potatoes and mix well with the spice mix.
1. Fry for 6-7 minutes in medium flames till everything incorporates well and the flavors gets along with the potatoes.
1. Adjust salt as per your taste and add some lemon juice and mix again.
1. Turn off the heat, add lot of chopped coriander and give a quick stir.
1. Serve Spicy Baby Potatoes along with Phulkas and Panchmel Dal for a weekday lunch or dinner.
