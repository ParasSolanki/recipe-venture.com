### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Jaggery.
- 1 teaspoon Rasam Powder - or red chilli powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 10 Curry leaves.
- 4 to 5 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 cup Tamarind Paste.

### Instructions

1. To begin with, the Hunase Gojju Recipe, take deep pan and heat oil in it.
1. As the oil heats up add mustard seeds, fenugreek, curry leaves, hing and chillies.
1. Saute them thoroughly.
1. Add the tamarind pulp, jaggery and rasam powder to the pan and let it cook for 2 minutes.
1. Now add 2 cups of water to the mixture and let it boil for about 15 minutes so that all the flavours are combined.
1. And salt and taste according to your taste.
1. The gojju will have a tangy, spicy and sweet taste, Serve it with rice and potato curry.
