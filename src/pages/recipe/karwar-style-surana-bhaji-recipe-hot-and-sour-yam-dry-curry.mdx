### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery - pound.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 10 Dry Red Chillies.
- pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 500 grams Elephant yam (Suran/Senai/Ratalu) - peeled and cut very small.
- 1 cup Tamarind Paste.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Karwar Style Surana Bhaji Recipe - Hot and Sour Yam Dry Curry by heating a skillet with oil.
1. Splutter the mustard seeds for few seconds.
1. Then add asafoetida, turmeric powder, dry red chillies and fenugreek seeds.
1. Roast them for few seconds and grind it into a smooth mixture using a mixer grinder.
1. Heat the pressure cooker, add little oil and fry the yam pieces, add the ground spice mixture, then add the tamarind pulp and jaggery.
1. Toss it well and coat it evenly.
1. Check for seasoning as well.
1. Pressure cook the yam for 4 whistles, let the pressure get released naturally.
1. Pour it into a serving dish.
1. Serve the Karwar Style Surana Bhaji Recipe - Hot and Sour Yam Dry Curry as a side dish along with Karwar Style Varan Kadhi Recipe and Steamed Rice for your everyday meals.
