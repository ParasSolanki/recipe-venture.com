### Ingredients

- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 teaspoon Panch Phoran Masala.
- Salt - to taste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 4 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1/2 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 2 tablespoons Mustard oil.
- 1 tablespoon Fresh cream.
- 4 Indian butter fish.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Creamy Indian Butter Fish Recipe, first clean the fish and dry it.
1. Marinate with little salt and keep aside for 2 minutes.
1. Take a medium bowl and whisk together cream, turmeric powder, punch phoren masala, kashmiri red chilli powder, water and salt.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Place the fish in frying pan and fry lightly both sides for two minutes.
1. Pour the whisked creamy sauce on top of the fishes so to coat them entirely.
1. Keep the green chillies on fishes.
1. Take extra care not to break the fish while flipping as it is extremely delicate to work with.
1. Cover and cook on low flame for 5 minutes.
1. Check the salt and adjust the taste.
1. Sprinkle few coriander leaves and it is ready to be served.
1. Serve Creamy Indian Butter Fish with Bengali Style Tetor Dal and Steamed Rice for a weekday meal.
