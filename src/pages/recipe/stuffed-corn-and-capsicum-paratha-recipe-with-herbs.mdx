### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 2 cups Red Yellow and Green Bell Peppers (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Mixed Herbs (Dried).
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1/2 cup Baby corn - thinly sliced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 4 Phulka - (Made of Vivatta whole wheat flour chakki atta).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Stuffed Corn and Capsicum Paratha Recipe with Herbs, heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan or a wok, add the onions and garlic and sauté on a high flame for a minute.
1. Add baby corn and capsicum and sauté on high flame for another 2 minutes until they become soft and tender.
1. Once soft and tender, add the chilli flakes, mixed herbs, salt and mix everything well.
1. Sauté on a high flame for another 30 seconds.
1. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and keep aside to cool for 5 minutes.
1. Place a portion of the mixture in the centre of the roti, fold 2 opposite corners of the roti over the filling, overlapping them slightly.
1. Now fold the remaining 2 sides to seal the stuffing well.
1. Heat a skillet or roti tawa and cook the envelope using oil, till they are golden brown in colour from both the sides.
1. Repeat with the remaining rotis and stir-fry to make 3 more envelopes.
1. Serve Stuffed Corn and Capsicum Paratha Recipe with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney or any other chutney of your choice.
