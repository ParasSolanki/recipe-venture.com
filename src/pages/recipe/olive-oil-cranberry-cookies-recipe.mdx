### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 cup Vivatta Maida.
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Baking powder.
- 1/4 cup Milk.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/4 cup Cranberries.
- 1/2 cup Sugar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Olive Oil & Cranberry Cookies Recipe, preheat the oven to 180 degree Celsius for 10 minutes.
1. Combine the flour, wheat flour, baking powder, sugar, salt first.
1. Mix all the dry ingredients well.
1. Add olive oil and cranberries and mix well.
1. You will get a crumble texture.
1. Slowly add milk and bring all the mixture together and bind them.
1. Make small balls out of the cookie mixture, make sure they are holding together and then flatten them.
1. Place the cookies on your cookie tray and bake them in the oven for 20 minutes.
1. Serve the Olive Oil & Cranberry Cookies Recipe along with a cup of hot chocolate or masala chai to enjoy evening time with bliss.
