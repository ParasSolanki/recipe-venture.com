### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Milk.
- 1 cup Vivatta Maida.
- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 3/4 cup Caster Sugar.
- 3 tablespoons Cocoa Powder.
- 1 cup Broccoli - chopped finely.
- 1/2 cup Sunflower Oil.
- Badam (Almond) - a handful.
- 2 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Broccoli Chocolate Muffins Recipe, grate broccoli and keep it aside.
1. Sieve all the dry ingredients in a bowl.
1. In a separate bowl, whisk in eggs well, then add sugar, milk and oil.
1. Mix well with the help of a hand mixer.
1. Gradually add the dry ingredients now and blend it well.
1. Once done, finally add broccoli and mix along with almonds.
1. Adding almonds is optional but this increases the taste of muffins.
1. Preheat the oven to 180 C.
1. Line up muffin moulds or tray with paper muffin cups.
1. Pour the batter till about 2/3 in each of the cavity.
1. Bake it for 16-20 minutes or until it is cooked.
1. Take off the muffin pan from oven once done and allow them to cool a bit.
1. Serve Broccoli Chocolate Muffins Recipe when they are still warm along with Fresh Figs Strawberry and Banana Smoothie Recipe during evening.
