### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 pinch SSP Asafoetida (Hing).
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 Beetroot - grated.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 teaspoon Mustard oil.
- 1 cup Green Amaranth Leaves - chopped.
- 3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 10 Baby Potatoes.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Beetroot, Aloo & Amaranth Leaves Sabzi Recipe, pressure cook the baby potatoes in water for  2 whistle.
1. Let the pressure release naturally and then open the lid and peel the potatoes.
1. Keep aside.
1. Heat a kadai and add some oil.
1. Once the oil is hot add mustard seeds, hing, cumin seeds and allow it to splutter for few seconds.
1. After the seeds have spluttered add the garlic, green chillies and onions and saute till onions are soft.
1. Once the onions have softened add the baby potatoes and roast for two minutes.
1. Now add the masalas, haldi, coriander powder, chilli powder and stir well to combine.
1. Once the masalas are well combined into the potatoes add the grated beetroot, salt to taste and stir.
1. Add in the Amaranth leaves and combine everything well.
1. Close the pan with a lid and cook till the leaves and beetroot are nearly done.
1. Open the lid and stir in the garam masala and cook till the beetroot and amaranth leaves are cooked.
1. Turn off the heat and transfer the Beetroot, Aloo & Amaranth Leaves Sabzi into a serving owl.
1. Serve the Beetroot, Aloo & Amaranth Leaves Sabzi Recipe along with phulka, Green Moong Dal Makhani and Sweet Potato & Green Bean Salad by the side to make it a rich and delicious meal.
