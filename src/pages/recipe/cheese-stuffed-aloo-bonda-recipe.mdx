### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- 50 grams Mozzarella cheese - cut into 1/2 inch pieces.
- 1 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- Sunflower Oil - as needed.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled and mashed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cheese Stuffed Aloo Bonda Recipe, heat oil in a frying pan, add mustard seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Once the seeds have crackled add the onions and saut√© for a couple of minutes till onions are soft and light golden in colour.
1. Once done add ginger, green chillies, asafoetida, turmeric and salt.
1. Saute for a minute and add the mashed potatoes.
1. Stir in the coriander leaves and combine.
1. Turn off the heat and allow the potato mixture to cool down.
1. Once the potato mixture has cooled down divide it into 8 portions.
1. While shaping, flatten the potatoes in the palm of your hand , place a piece of the Mozzarella cheese in the center and cover it to make a ball.
1. Shape the bonda into round balls and keep aside.
1. Combine gram flour, turmeric powder, salt to taste in a bowl.
1. Add a little water at a time to make a thick batter which coats the back of a spoon.
1. Make sure to whisk the batter well until light and fluffy.
1. Keep aside.
1. Preheat a kuzhi paniyaram pan.
1. Add a few drops of oil into each cavity.
1. Dip and coat each of the potato cheese balls in the gram flour batter and gradually place it into the cavity.
1. Cover the pan and allow the bonda to steam.
1. Once you notice the batter is cooked, uncover the pan and cook all sides by flipping and turning.
1. Once the Cheese Stuffed Aloo Bonda turns golden brown in color on all sides, transfer the Cheese Stuffed Aloo Bonda onto a plate with an oil absorbent paper and serve hot.
1. Serve the Cheese Stuffed Aloo Bonda along with a spicy Coconut Chutney or Green Chutney  along with a cup of steaming Filter Coffee .
