### Ingredients

- 1 liter Soy milk - or almond milk.
- 1 tablespoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/4 cup Cocoa Powder.
- 60 grams Chocolate Bar - dairy free .
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 1/4 cup Cane sugar - or coconut sugar.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Vegan hot Chocolate Recipe, get prep with all the ingredients first.
1. In a Saucepan add cocoa powder, cane sugar, salt, vanilla and 1/2 cup of soy milk into a Blender and whisk until ingredients have combined smoothly.
1. Now whisk in the remaining soy milk (or almond milk).
1. Turn on the heat and slowly bring it to a boil and turn off the heat and drop the dairy free chocolate (if using) and stir.
1. Alternately, break chocolate and divide into 4 cups (optional), pour the hot chocolate into the cups.
1. Serve vegan hot chocolate recipe with Pretzel salad or Whole Wheat Banana Bread Recipe (with Eggless & Vegan Options) during evening.
