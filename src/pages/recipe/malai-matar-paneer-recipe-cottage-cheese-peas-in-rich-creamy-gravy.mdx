### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Yogurt.
- 1 tablespoon Cashew nuts - paste.
- 1 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - paste.
- 1 tablespoon Green Chilli - paste.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander leaves - chopped.
- 1/2 tablespoon Poppy seeds - paste.
- 1/2 cup Onion - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Malai Matar Paneer recipe, in a kadai/wok heat oil and add garlic paste.
1. As it turns light brown, add chopped onion.
1. Fry onion until the onion becomes soft and translucent.
1. Next, add poppy seed paste, green chilli paste, coriander powder, salt, sugar and cook it for about 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes, add green peas and yogurt.
1. Cook the same for about 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Once the green peas are soft, add paneer cubes and give a stir.
1. Add 1/2 cup of warm water and let it cook for 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes, add cashew nut paste and cook for about 7 to 8 minutes.
1. Check seasoning and garnish it with some chopped coriander leaves and give a stir.
1.  Malai Matar Paneer is ready to serve.
1. Serve Malai Matar Paneer along with Lauki Raita, Kachumber Salad and Phulkas for a weekday meal with your family.
