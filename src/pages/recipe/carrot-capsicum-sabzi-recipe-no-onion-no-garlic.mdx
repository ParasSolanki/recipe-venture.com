### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - cut into squares.
- 1 1/2 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 4 Carrots (Gajjar) - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Carrot & Capsicum Sabzi Recipe, heat a kadai with oil, add mustard seeds and ajwain and allow it to splutter for few seconds.
1. Add a pinch of hing and chopped carrots and saute till they turn soft.
1. This will take about 5 to 10 minutes to cook.
1. Once that is done add the chopped bell peppers and sprinkle some salt and keep saut√©ing until the bell peppers are cooked.
1. Add all the spice powders like coriander powder, garam masala powder, red chilli powder, amchoor to the vegetables and mix well.
1. Sprinkle some water and cover it with the lid and allow the masala to cook for at least 10 minutes on medium heat.
1. Check for salt in the Carrot & Capsicum Sabzi  before your turn off the heat and serve hot.
1. Serve the Carrot & Capsicum Sabzi Recipe along with Gujarati Dal Recipe, phulka and raita to make it a complete meal.
