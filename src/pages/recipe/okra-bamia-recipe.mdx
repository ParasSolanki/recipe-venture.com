### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 Garlic - minced.
- 4 Tomatoes - blanched.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 13 Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Okra Bamia recipe, first heat oil in a saucepan and let it heat.
1. Add the onion and garlic and saut√© till the onion becomes translucent.
1. Add the coriander powder, chilli powder and okra and stir fry for a minute.
1. Add about 1 cup of water to the okra and add salt.
1. Bring it to a boil and cook for another 2-3 minute.
1. Now add the tomato paste and stir it, cook the mixture with the lid on top at a low heat.
1. Wait till the okra is cooked and see to that the sauce is thick.
1. Add the lime juice and give it a good mix.
1. Butter Garlic Naan or Saffron Pilaf can be served with Bamia Okra Recipe.
