### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pudina Dahi Chutney Recipe, grind the dhaniya and pudina leaves along with green chilli and ginger.
1. Add water if required, grind to form a smooth paste.
1. Once that is done add the ground dhaniya pudina paste into the dahi.
1. Add enough salt and mix to form a light green creamy chutney.
1. Transfer into a bowl and garnish it with mint on top.
1. Serve the Pudina Dahi Chutney Recipe along with Dahi Ke Kebab ,Chicken Tikka Recipe to enjoy your starters.
