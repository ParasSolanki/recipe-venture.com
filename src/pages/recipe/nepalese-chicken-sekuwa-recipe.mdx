### Ingredients

- 1/2 Tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 3 Green Chillies - minced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 Cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 Chicken breasts.
- 1/2 Tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 Sichuan peppercorns - (timur).
- 1/2 Tablespoon Garam masala powder.

### Instructions

1. Making Nepalese Chicken Sekuwa Recipe requires overnight marination for best results so you can plan this dish in advance.
1. Thoroughly wash and clean the chicken.
1. Next cut them into 1 inch cubes.
1. In a mixing bowl, marinate the chicken along  with yogurt, salt, garam masala powder, turmeric powder, Chopped coriander leaves, black pepper powder, ginger garlic paste,timur, green chillies and lemon juice.
1. Cover the bowl with a cling wrap and let it marinate in the fridge overnight.
1. Once the marination is over, chicken is ready for cooking.
1. So preheat your oven at 180 C.
1. Rub some vegetable oil on the skewers and start putting the chicken cubes on the skewers, making sure there is some gap between the chicken pieces so that they properly cooked.
1. Grill them for good 20-25  minutes in your oven, to check if it is cooked, ensuring it not overcooked or burnt.
1. Chicken Sekuwa is ready now and if you like to give them a roasted look then you can add cook them for a couple of minutes on direct flame.
1. Once the chicken is ready brush them with some melted butter.
1. Serve Nepalese Chicken Sekuwa Recipe hot with some onion & lemon pieces along with Pudina Chutney Recipe and another skewer like Sweet Potato Pineapple Skewers In Spicy Sweetish Peanut Dip Recipe for appetizer.
