### Ingredients

- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Cheese.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Spinach - roughly chopped.
- 1 cup Sweet corn - steamed.
- 5 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - or tofu.
- 2 cups Whole Wheat Flour.
- Sunflower Oil - oil for cooking.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Corn and Spinach Whole Wheat Calzone Recipe, first get all the ingredients ready.
1. To begin making the dough for the spinach roti - in a wok, add a teaspoon of oil and stir fry the spinach until it wilts down.
1. Once the spinach wilts down, turn off the heat and allow it to cool.
1. once cooled, puree the spinach.
1. In a large mixing bowl, add the pureed spinach, the whole wheat flour, salt and add little water at a time to make a smooth firm dough.
1. Drizzle a little oil and knead again to make a smooth dough.
1. Divide the dough into 8 to 10 equal portions and shape into balls.
1. Keep aside.
1. To make the corn filling for the calzone, heat oil in a pan and add green chillies and capsicum and stir fry until the capsicum becomes soft and tender.
1. Once soft and tender, add the remaining filling ingredients and stir well to combine.
1. Check the salt and adjust to suit your taste.
1. To make the calzones, roll the dough ball into a flat circle of 3 inch diameter by dusting it in flour.
1. Place a tablespoon of the filling on one side of the rolled dough.
1. Now fold the other to make a calzone which is half moon shaped.
1. Take a fork and press on all sides to seal the edges and make a design.
1. Place this calzone on a hot tava on simmer.
1. Add a little oil on both sides and cook until the dough is cooked through and brown on both sides and lightly crisp.
1. Once done, remove from the skillet and serve.
