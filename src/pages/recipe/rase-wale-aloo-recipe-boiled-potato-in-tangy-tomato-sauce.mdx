### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4 Tomatoes - pureed.
- 1/4 Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 6 Potatoes (Aloo) - freshly boiled (roughly chopped).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera) - roasted.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Red Chilli powder - as per taste.
- Salt - as per taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Rase Wale Aloo recipe, in a heavy bottomed pan heat oil and temper the jeera and hing.
1. Add tomato puree and cook till all the moisture reduces.
1. Add all the masalas and sugar.
1. Cook for another 30 seconds.
1. Add the potatoes and add 1 glass water (250ml) and let it cook and reduce further to a thick consistency.
1. Mash some of the potatoes in the gravy to get that typical rase wale aloo.
1. Serve Rase Wale Aloo along with Puri or Pudina Tawa Paratha for lunch or dinner.
