### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Kala Chana (Brown Chickpeas) - soaked overnight.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Onions - sliced.
- 500 grams Ridge Gourd (Turai/ Peerkangai) - sliced.
- 2 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard oil.
- 1 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Chana Turai/Nenua Chana Sabzi Recipe (Ridge Gourd with Black Chana Recipe) by soaking the chana overnight or for 8 hours in water.
1. Heat a kadai with oil, splutter the mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds and hing.
1. Add chopped onions and fry them till it is translucent.
1. Add in the green chillies after few minutes and Give it a stir.
1. Next add all the red chilli powder, turmeric powder and coriander powder and mix well.
1. Now at this point add the soaked channa along with the water and mix.
1. Boil it over for few seconds till the water is all evaporated.
1. Then add the chopped ridge gourd and mix well.
1. You can also Â½ cup of water and close it and cook.
1. Once you remove the lid add the mango powder and cumin powder and mix well.
1. Saute till the water is all evaporated and serve hot.
1. Serve the Chana Turai/Nenua Chana Sabzi Recipe (Ridge Gourd with Black Chana Recipe) along with Hot Phulka or steamed Rice as your healthy Lunch.
