### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- Ghee - for cooking.
- 1/2 inch Ginger - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera) - roasted.
- 1 Tomato - blanched and pureed.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1/2 cup White Urad Dal (Whole).
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - cubed.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Bengali Style Dhokar Dalna Recipe (Spiced Lentil Cakes in Tomato Gravy Recipe) by soaking the dal for at least three hours.
1. Then into the grinder, add all the ingredients along with the dal and little water and grind it into a smooth rice batter consistency.
1. Pour the batter into the Dhokla Pan and steam it for about 30 minutes.
1. Till it is firm and not sticky.
1. Place it outside and let it cool.
1. Cut into diamonds and pan fry them using a shallow frying pan till it is brown and crisp on either side.
1. Next for the gravy blanch the tomatoes in boiling water over a sauce pan for about 2 minutes.
1. Then remove the skin and blitz it in a mixer to a smooth puree.
1. Heat a kadai with oil and add ingredient along with the ginger that are mentioned in the seasoning list.
1. Let it sauté for few minutes.
1. Then add the tomato puree, along with all the spice and sauté till the raw smell goes away.
1. Add 1 cup water and let it simmer for about 20 minutes.
1. Once done add in the cakes and can served by sprinkling chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve the Bengali Style Dhokar Dalna Recipe (Spiced Lentil Cakes in Tomato Gravy Recipe) along with Hot Phulka to enjoy your meal.
