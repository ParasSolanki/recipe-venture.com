### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 4 cups Green Amaranth Leaves - tightly packed.
- 1/2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 4 Green Chillies.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 10 cloves Garlic.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - dry roasted and powdered.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thotakura Vepudu Recipe, clean, wash and roughly chop the amaranth leaves.
1. Keep aside.
1. Dry roast the sesame seeds on medium heat, powder it using a mixer grinder and keep aside.
1. Now, heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan, add the mustard seeds, cumin seeds, urad dal and let it crackle.
1. Add the dried red chillies, curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. Roughly pound the garlic cloves and green chillies using a pestle and mortar and add it to the pan.
1. Saute for a minute till the garlic turn light brown.
1. Add the turmeric powder, chopped amaranth greens and mix well.
1. Wait for the greens to wilt.
1. Season with salt and let the green completely dry out.
1. This will take around 5 minutes.
1. Turn off the heat, sprinkle the sesame powder on top and mix well.
1. Serve the Thotakura Vepudu Recipe along with Phulka/Steamed Rice, Palak Pappu and Avakaya Pickle for a delicious Andhra Style weeknight dinner.
