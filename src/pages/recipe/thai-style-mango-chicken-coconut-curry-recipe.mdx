### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 Mango (Ripe) - cut into cubes.
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Dabur Homemade Coconut Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 2 Onions - diced.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - cut into jullienes.
- 2 Stalks Lemongrass - chopped.
- 5 Dry Red Chillies - soaked in water for 15 minutes.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/3 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 1 inch Galangal - (Indonesian ginger) or regular ginger.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - cut into juliennes.
- 2 Chicken breasts - washed.
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 4 Kaffir lime leaves - or Lemon Leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thai Style Mango Chicken Coconut Curry Recipe, keep all the ingredients ready and we will first make the Yellow Thai Curry Paste.
1. For the Yellow Thai Curry PasteIn a mixer-jar combine, the lemongrass, galangal or ginger, onions, dry red chillies, kaffir lime leaves, garlic, coriander leaves, cumin seeds, turmeric powder and salt.
1. Grind to a fine paste along with 1/4 cup of water.
1. Transfer the Yellow Thai Curry Paste to a bowl and set aside.
1. Getting ahead to make the Thai Style Mango Chicken Coconut Curry Recipe Heat a pan on medium heat, add oil, once the oil is hot add in the ginger, garlic and saute for about 40 seconds, or until the garlic turns into a light brown colour.
1. To this add onions and continue to cook for another 2 minutes, until the onions start turning brown.
1. At this stage add in the freshly ground thai yellow curry paste, continue to cook for about a minute, to this add 1/2 cup of water, salt and the chicken strips.
1. Cover and cook the chicken until almost done.
1. This will take about 5-7 minutes.
1. Open the lid, now add the soy sauce, red bell peppers, and the green bell peppers and give it a good mix.
1. Finally reduce the flame add in the coconut milk, red chilli flakes and the mango cubes.
1. Add in the coriander leaves and once the Thai Style Mango Chicken Coconut Curry comes to a single boil, turn off the flame.
1. Transfer the Thai Style Mango Chicken Coconut Curry to as serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Thai Style Mango Chicken Coconut Curry Recipe along with Thai Jasmine Sticky Rice Recipe along with a drink of Thai Ice Tea Recipe, followed  by a dessert of Khao Neeo Mamuang - Thai Sweet Sticky Rice With Mango.
