### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 Tomatoes - pulp removed and cut lengthwise.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 cup Lettuce leaves - torn into small pieces.
- 1/4 cup Feta Cheese.
- 1 English Cucumber - cut lengthwise.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Summer Lettuce Salad, prep all the cucumber, tomatoes an lettuce and keep it ready in a salad bowl.
1. In another bowl, mix olive oil, lemon juice, honey, salt and pepper and whisk till it combines.
1. Drizzle the lemon dressing over the cut vegetables and toss well.
1. Plate the tossed salad over a serving plate and add crumbled feta over the top and serve.
1. Serve the Summer Lettuce Salad with Cucumber, Tomato & Feta Recipe along with Chicken Shawarma and Zucchini fritters to create and delicious dinner.
