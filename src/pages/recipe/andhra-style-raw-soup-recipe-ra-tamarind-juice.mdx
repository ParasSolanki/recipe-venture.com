### Ingredients

- 4 cloves Garlic - crushed.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- 2 cups Tamarind Water.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Green Chilli - smoked.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Onions - sliced.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pachi Pulusu, we will first roast the chilies in a pan till it is completely roasted.
1.  Chop the chillies very finely and transfer it to a bowl.
1. Add the tamarind water, coriander leaves, jaggery, onions and check for salt.
1. Balance out the flavor by adding little jaggery if it is too tangy.
1. Heat a small tempering pan with oil, add mustard seeds and cumin seeds and allow it splutter for 10 seconds.
1. Add curry leaves, hing, red chillies, and crushed garlic and leave the curry leaves to crackle for 10 seconds.
1. Pour this over the pulusu and serve.
1. Serve the Pachi Pulusu Recipe along with hot steamed rice, palya, and Gor Keri Recipe by the side to relish your lunch meals.
