### Ingredients

- 2 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 tablespoon Cashew nuts - halved.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 1/2 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Green beans (French Beans) - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Spinach Leaves (Palak) - finely chopped.
- 2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Tofu - cut into cubes.
- 12 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 4 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1/2 cup Spring Onion Greens - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Basmati rice.
- 1 cup Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves) - finely chopped.
- 4 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 6 Cloves (Laung).
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Tofu Green Pulao Recipe, first wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes and then strain the water using muslin cloth and keep it aside.
1. Meanwhile in a blender combine spinach leaves, methi leaves, coriander leaves, green chillies and blend till it forms a smooth puree.
1. Add oil and ghee in a pressure cooker, once hot; add cumin seeds, bay leaves, black cardamom, cloves, green cardamom, cinnamon stick.
1. After they begin to roast, add tofu cubes, cashew and fry for about 20 seconds.
1. Now add capsicum, green peas, french beans, finely chopped spring onions and soaked double beans.
1. After a while add in the basmati rice and stir fry for a minute.
1. Add the prepared green puree and let it cook for a minute.
1. Add 2 to 3 cups of water and mix everything well.
1. Close the cooker with a lid and let it cook till the cooker release 2 to 3 whistles.
1. Once done, let the pressure release normally.
1. Open the lid, servie it in a bowl and garnish it with chopped coriander leaves.
1. This Tofu Green Pulao can be served with Burani Raita or any other raita of your choice for your weekday lunch or dinner.
