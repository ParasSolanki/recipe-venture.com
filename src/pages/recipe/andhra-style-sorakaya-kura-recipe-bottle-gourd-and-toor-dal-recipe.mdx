### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 cups Bottle gourd (lauki) - cut into small pieces.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 2 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal) - soaked for 30 minutes.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sorakaya Kura Recipe we will keep a pressure cooker on medium heat, add oil and leave it get hot.
1. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds and allow it splutter for 10 seconds.
1. Add curry leaves, hing, and leave the curry leaves to crackle for 10 seconds.
1. Add in the chopped tomatoes and saute well till it becomes mushy.
1. Add the turmeric powder, red chilli powder, pepper powder, salt, green chilli, chopped bottle gourd and soaked toor dal.
1. Give the ingredients a stir and add 2 cups of water.
1. Cook it for 4 whistle.
1. Allow the pressure to release by itself and open the lid, give it a stir and check for salt and serve.
1. Serve the Sorakaya Kura Recipe along with Tawa Paratha ,or Phulka along with a Carrot And Beans Thoran to go along with your meal.
