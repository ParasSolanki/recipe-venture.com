### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Fennel Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - as per your taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 3 Pomfret fish - medium sized.
- 2 tablespoon Butter - melted.
- 8 Curry leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 tablespoons Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tawa Fry Crispy Pomfret Recipe, wash and clean the pomfret fish properly and make gashes on both sides using a knife.
1. Now, we need to marinate fish so add turmeric powder, salt, lemon juice and crushed peppers and rub it properly on the fish.
1. Let it sit for 15 minutes.
1. Next step includes making a smooth paste by mixing curd, red chilli powder, fennel powder, finely chopped coriander leaves and curry leaves.
1. Now rub this masala paste on the fish making sure to push the masala inside the gashes.
1. Marinate the fish for 1 hour in refrigerator.
1. Heat a non stick pan and add melted butter into it.
1. Put the pomfret on the pan and fry for 8 to 10 minutes on one side in medium flame.
1. Carefully flip the fish and cook on the other side too for 8 to 10 minutes.
1. Make sure to not overcook the fish otherwise it may become dry.
1. Drain the excess butter using a paper towel.
1. Serve it hot.
1. Serve Tawa Fry Crispy Pomfret along with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney or any other chutney of your choice for your house parties or a weekend dinner.
1. You can also serve it as a side dish with your meal.
