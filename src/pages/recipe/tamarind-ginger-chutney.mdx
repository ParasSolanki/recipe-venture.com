### Ingredients

-  3 tbsp jaggery.
-  1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds.
-  1/4 tsp red chilli Powder.
-  1/4 tsp asafoetida.
-  2-3 curry leaves - finely chopped.
-  1/4 tsp turmeric powder.
-  2 tbsp green chillies - cut.
-  1/4 tsp rye.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  2 tbsp ginger - cut.
- 1 cup tamarind paste - as much as lemon.
-  1 tbsp sesame oil.

### Instructions

1. To make the Puli Engi recipe, first heat the oil in a saucepan.
1. Add the mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds and cook for 10 seconds.
1. Now add asafoetida, curry leaves, ginger, green chilli and cook for 30 seconds.
1. Now add turmeric powder, tamarind paste, red chilli powder, jaggery, salt and cook for 1 minute.
1. Now boil in this mixture Come on After boiling, reduce the heat and let it cook for 15 minutes.
1. Turn off the flame and serve.
1. Serve Puli Engi with ghee roast dosa and filter coffee for breakfast.
