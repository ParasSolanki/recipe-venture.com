### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens).
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar).
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum).
- 1 Fresh Red Chilli.
- 1 teaspoon Dark soy sauce.
- 1 Onion.
- 1 Celery.
- 2-1/2 cup Vegetable stock.
- 2 inch Ginger - sliced.
- 1/4 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 1/2 cup Mixed lentils.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Asian Carrot Ginger Lentil Soup recipe, wash the lentil and soak for 5 minutes.
1. Cut the vegetables in chunks and the scallion into long thin strips.
1. Heat a medium saucepan with oil, ginger, red chilli and sesame seed.
1. Let it cook for about 15 seconds.
1. After 15 seconds, add the celery and onions fry for a minute.
1. After a minute, add rest of the vegetables.
1. Mix it properly and let it cook for another minute.
1. After a minute, add the water and lentil.
1. Let it fully cook until the grains start to break.
1. Add soya sauce and salt.
1. Pour the soup in a big soup bowl and garnish with scallions on top.
