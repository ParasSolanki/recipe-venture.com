### Ingredients

-  1 onion.
-  4 bread.
-  3 whole black pepper.
-  3 teaspoon sambar Powder.
-  salt - 1 teaspoon oil.
-  as per taste.
- 1 cup rice - cooked.
-  1/2 inch lentil sugar.
-  2 long.
-  5 eggs.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  1 teaspoon ginger garlic paste.

### Instructions

1. To make egg and bread fried rice, firstly chop the onion finely.
1. Now break the eggs and put them in a bowl and beat.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Now add cinnamon, long and cumin seeds.
1. Add onions after 15 seconds and cook till they become soft.
1. Now add ginger garlic paste and let it cook for 1 minute.
1. Now give egg solution to it.
1. Cook like scrambled eggs.
1. Now add black pepper powder, sambar powder and salt.
1. Mix well and cook until the eggs are cooked.
1. Now add bread pieces to it.
1. Cook for 2 minutes and add rice.
1. Mix well and let it cook for 3 minutes.
1. Garnish with green coriander and serve hot.
1. Serve egg and bread fried rice with avocado raita for dinner.
