### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Panch Phoran Masala - roasted and powdered.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- 2 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera) - roasted and powdered.
- 2 Potato (Aloo) - peeled and cut into cubes.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Tomato - big sized.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 200 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cut into cubes.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chanar Dalna recipe, grind roasted cumin and grated ginger in a mortar pestle.
1. Mix red chilly powder and turmeric in a tablespoon of water and set it aside.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan heat oil, add cubed potatoes and fry till the edges turn brown.
1. Set it aside.
1. Add Paneer cubes to the same oil and fry till they turn gold.
1. Set it aside.
1. Add bay leaf and dry red chilly to the same pan.
1. Add cumin and ginger paste and saute till it turns brown.
1. Add red chilly and turmeric paste and mix it well, till the raw smell is gone.
1. Add chopped tomato and cook till it is cooked well and oil separates.
1. Add Paneer and Potato cubes to this.
1. Cook for a minute.
1. Add 2 cups of water and cook till the potatoes are soft.
1. Add powdered paanch phoran and give it a good mix.
1. Boil till the gravy till it reaches the desired consistency and turn off the heat.
1. Serve Chanar Dalna with steamed rice and Bengali Luchi and Watermelon Smoothie Recipe for a weekend brunch.
