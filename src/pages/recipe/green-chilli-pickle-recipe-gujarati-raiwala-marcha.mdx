### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoons Salt - (adjust).
- 1/2 cup Yellow mustard seeds - coarsely crushed.
- 20 Green Chillies - (bhavnagari variety.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 cup Lemon - cubed.
- 4 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Green Chilli Pickle Recipe (Gujarati Raiwala Marcha), in a mixing bowl, combine the split mustard seeds, salt, turmeric powder, hing, 1 teaspoon of oil and mix well.
1. Rub the bhavnagari chillies in a little of the prepared mixture and place them on a big plate.
1. Sprinkle the remaining mixture evenly over them along with the remaining 3 teaspoons of oil and lemon cubes and toss gently.
1. Fill them in a glass jar and keep it aside for atleast 1 hour.
1. Serve or store Green Chilli Pickle Recipe (Gujarati Raiwala Marcha) in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
