### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- Salt - to taste.
- 5 cloves Garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 1 Whole Egg.
- 5 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 Fresh Red chillies - sliced.
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 8 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 1 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - crumbled.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thai Style Paneer Toast Recipe, we will first grind the ingredients in the list to a coarse mixture.
1. Keep a teaspoon aside to mix into your egg and mix the reaming into the paneer crumble.
1. Add the egg into a bowl and also the masala that was kept aside and beat well.
1. Take the bread slices and cut them diagonally to form two triangles.
1. Add paneer crumble on top of each slices.
1. Heat a flat tawa with a drizzle of oil, carefully dip each toast in the egg mixture and place it on the tawa with the crumble side on top first.
1. After 2 minutes carefully flip on to the other side and cook for another 2 minutes and serve hot with some sesame seeds, chopped red chili and coriander leaves.
1. Serve the Thai Style Paneer Toast with a bowl filled with fruits and a glass of Cantaloupe Pomegranate and Mint Juice Recipe by the side.
