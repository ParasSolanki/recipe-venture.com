### Ingredients

- 1/4 teaspoon Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Punjabi style mango pickle - use only the masala and not the pieces.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 500 grams Kaddu (Parangikai/ Pumpkin) - cut into 1 inch cubes.
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Achari Kaddu Ki Sabzi, combine the pumpkin along with 1.
1. 4 cup of water and salt and pressure cook for 1 whistle.
1. Turn off the flame.
1. Drain the remaining water and transfer the pumpkin into a bowl.
1. Heat a skillet with oil on medium flame, add the mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, fennel seeds, onion seeds and cumin seeds, allow them to sizzle.
1.  Add a pinch of asafoetida, then add the ginger, garlic and green chilli and saute for few seconds Sprinkle some salt, and add the other masalas- red chilli powder, amchur powder, turmeric powder and garam masala.
1. Add in only the masala/gravy from the Punjabi pickle.
1. Finally add in the cooked pumpkin cubes, coriander leaves and give Achari Kaddu Ki Sabzi a good stir.
1. Serve the Achari Kaddu Ki Sabzi Recipe along with Phulka and Dal Banjara Recipe - Langar Wali Dal by the side to enjoy your lunch meal.
