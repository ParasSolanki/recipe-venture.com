### Ingredients

-  1/2 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  1 tablespoon oil.
-  1 teaspoon cumin powder.
-  2 cardamom.
-  2 tomatoes.
-  1 cup water.
-  2 long.
- 15 small potatoes.
-  1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  1/2 teaspoon Salt.
-  1-1 / 2 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  1 inch lentil.

### Instructions

1. To make Chhoti Aalur Dum, first put the potatoes in a pressure cooker with water and salt.
1. Cook until 2 cities come.
1. After coming to city 2, let the pressure drain.
1. Remove the skin of the potatoes and keep them aside.
1. Now take a blender, add tomatoes and grind it.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add cardamom, long and cinnamon.
1. Allow to cook for 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add potatoes and mix well.
1. After 1 minute add tomato puree and add all the spices.
1. Mix, add 1 glass of water and mix.
1. Cook till the potatoes are cooked and then add salt as per taste.
1. Allow to cook for 1 minute and then serve hot.
1. Serve Chhoti Aalur Dum with Bengali lentils and Bengali leek for dinner.
