### Ingredients

- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Mustard sauce.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- Red Chilli flakes - as needed.
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 1 pinch Garlic powder.
- Iceberg lettuce - small bunch.
- 1 Lemon - juiced.
- 1/4 cup Sweet corn - steamed.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - finely chopped.
- 200 grams Bean Sprouts.
- 2 teaspoons Honey.
- 100 grams Star fruit - roughly chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - toasted.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bean Sprout & Corn Salad Recipe, we will first light cook the bean sprouts by tossing it in a wok.
1. Heat a teaspoon of oil in a wok on medium heat; add in the spring onions, the red peppers, and the bean sprouts.
1. Stir fry on high heat for a couple of minutes until just about cooked.
1. Turn off the heat and transfer the sprouts to a large salad bowl and allow it to cool.
1. Add in the remaining salad ingredients- star fruit, roughly chopped, steamed sweet corn and green salad leaves.
1. Cover and keep the bowl in the fridge.
1. Prepare the dressing for the salad, by combining all the ingredients in a bowl - lemon juice,  mustard sauce, sesame oil, soy sauce, honey, toasted sesame seeds, chilli flakes, chopped red chilli, garlic powder and salt to taste, whisk it well.
1. Add the dressing and toss the salad.
1. Check for the salt and spice levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Serve Bean Sprout & Corn Salad Recipe with Israeli Couscous Minestrone Soup Recipe and Classic Grilled Tomato Cucumber Chutney Sandwich Recipe for a filling dinner.
