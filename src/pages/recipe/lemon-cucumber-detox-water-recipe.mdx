### Ingredients

- 8-10 Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 1 liter Chilled water.
- 1 Cucumber - sliced.
- 1 Lemon - sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lemon Cucumber Detox Water, mix all ingredients together in a Pitcher.
1. Allow it to steep in the refrigerator for a minimum of 3 hours before consuming it.
1. The Lemon Cucumber Detox Water can also be prepared and kept in the refrigerator overnight.
1. Lemon Cucumber Detox Water can be prepared and refrigerated overnight and consumed the next day as well with  Sweet Mixed Fruits and Granola Breakfast Wraps Recipe.
