### Ingredients

- 1/2 tablespoon Cocoa Powder.
- 1 tablespoon Coconut milk.
- 1 cup Almond Milk (Badam Milk) - hot and frothed.
- 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 tablespoon Beetroot juice - freshly extracted.
- Coconut Sugar - as per taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Beet Latte, in a mug add beet juice, cinnamon powder, vanilla extract, coconut milk, coconut sugar and cocoa powder.
1. Give a good whisk to this mixture and make sure that lumps are not formed.
1. Now gradually add the hot almond milk and keep stirring.
1. Pour into the a serving mug or glass and serve hot.
1. Serve it with chocolate buckwheat pancakes for breakfast or during tea time.
