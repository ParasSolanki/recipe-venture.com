### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 tablespoon Red Wine Vinaigrette.
- Salt - to taste.
- 10 Fresh Figs - quatered.
- 1 cup Green beans (French Beans) - cut into 2 inch lengthwise.
- 1/2 cup Lemon juice.
- 2 tablespoon Tabasco Original - Hot Sauce.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower seeds.
- Black pepper powder - for seasoning.
- 2 cups Arugula leaves - roots removed and washed.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Arugula & Fig Salad Recipe by washing the arugula leaves well with water and drain all the water and keep it aside.
1. Next heat a small skillet, add the figs and let it sizzle for few minutes and a tablespoon of red wine vinaigrette and toss it over.
1. Cook for 2 more minutes and set aside.
1. Do the same thing for green beans as well.
1. Then to make the dressing squeeze in the lemon juice and whisk in all the other ingredients till it it emulsifies.
1. Drizzle half of it on the arugula leaves and toss over, check for seasonings.
1. Drizzle the remaining over the green beans and figs.
1. To serve carefully place the tossed arugula leaves on a serving plate and drop in the figs and green beans and sprinkle few sunflower seeds as well.
1. Serve the Arugula & Fig Salad Recipe as a healthy appetizer during any party occasion.
