### Ingredients

- 1 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 3 Dry Red Chillies.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1 Chow chow (chayote squash) - diced.
- 30 grams Tamarind.
- 2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To make this Chow-Chow Kothamalli Chutney Recipe| Chayote Coriander Chutney, clean, peel and dice the chayote and get ready with the other ingredients.
1. In a saucepan, heat oil, add urad dal, channa dal, and mix them well.
1. After they turn golden brown, add chillies and sauté.
1. Add the diced chow-chow and sauté them till they are cooked but firm.
1. Add tamarind to chow chow and sauté.
1. After the chow chow is cooked, turn off the flame and let it cool.
1. In a mixer, add coriander leaves, chow-chow mixture, and salt to taste.
1. Grind to a smooth chutney.
1. Add water only if needed.
1. If needed you can temper them with some mustard seeds, urad dal, and curry leaves.
1. Serve Chow-Chow Kothamalli Chutney Recipe| Chayote Coriander Chutney with idlis, onion rava dosa recipe or as an accompaniment with full course lunch.
