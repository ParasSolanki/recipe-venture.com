### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 300 grams Elephant yam (Suran/Senai/Ratalu) - cut into thin slices.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Senai Kizhangu Varuval Recipe (Spicy Chettinad Style Yam Stir Fry Recipe), by mixing all the spice powder including turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder with yam in a small mixing bowl.
1. Check for seasonings.
1. Heat a flat bottomed pan, add a tablespoon oil for shallow frying the yam slices, fry the yam slices for 2 minutes on each side till its brown and crisp.
1. Once done in the same oil add some curry leaves and let it splutter, then add in the shallow fried yam chips and give it a toss.
1. Serve the Senai Kizhangu Varuval Recipe as a side dish for Thandu Keerai Sambar and Steamed Rice for lunch box meal.
