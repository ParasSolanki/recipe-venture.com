### Ingredients

- 2 cloves Garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Butter.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- Salt - a pinch.
- 1 cup Broccoli - 10 florets approximately.
- Black pepper powder - a pinch.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Broccoli Puree for babies and toddlers, make sure to washt the broccoli florets thoroughly.
1. Add half a cup of water to a pressure cooker and add the broccoli florets to it.
1. Add a pinch of salt to it and close the lid.
1. Let 2 whistles out and release the pressure immediately.
1. Releasing the pressure immediately, allows the broccoli to retain its rich green color and nutrients.
1. In a pan, add a teaspoon of butter.
1. Once the butter melts, add chopped garlic and saute until the raw smell goes away.
1. Next add the broccoli florets, and saute for 2 minutes.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. Lets the broccoli cool down.
1. Once the broccoli is cool, add them to a mixer and grind until its a smooth puree.
1. Serve Broccoli Puree with Garlic and Butter to your babies/toddlers over 7 months for lunch or dinner meal.
