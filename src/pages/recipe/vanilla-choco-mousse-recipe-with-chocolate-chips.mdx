### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 100 grams Heavy whipping cream.
- 2 tablespoon Chocolate chips.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 + 1 teaspoon Instant Coffee Powder.
- 200 grams Chocolate Cake - plain or frosted.
- 100 grams Chocolate ganache.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Vanilla Choco Mousse Recipe with chocolate chips start by cutting the Chocolate Cake into small pieces or crumbling it with hand.
1. Here's how ro make a Eggless Easy Chocolate Cake Recipe - Vegan OptionTake a bowl and add vanilla extract to whipped cream.
1. This makes the vanilla cream.
1. Keep asideTake a saucepan and heat water in it on medium flame.
1. After water gets hot add 1 teaspoon coffee powder and 1 teaspoon sugar to it.
1. When sugar gets dissolved turn off the flame and allow it to cool completely.
1. Now take a glass and first put a thin layer of vanilla cream followed by the layer of cake.
1. On the layer of cake and put little bit coffee syrup (around 1/2 teaspoon per layer of chocolate cake).
1. Now put a layer of vanilla cream followed by a teaspoon of chocolate chips.
1. Continue this process and make about 4 more layers.
1. Lastly, sprinkle coffee powder with the help of a sieve.
1. Set this glass for 1 hr in the fridge.
1. Vanilla Choco Mousse With Chocolate Chips is now ready to serve.
1. Serve Vanilla Choco Mousse Recipe as a dessert after a meal of Lemony Pasta Recipe With Roasted Shrimps or Penne Pasta Recipe In Creamy Cauliflower Sauce with Cheese Garlic Bread Recipe.
