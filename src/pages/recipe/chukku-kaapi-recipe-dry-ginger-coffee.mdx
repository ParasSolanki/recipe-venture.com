### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons Jaggery - (adjust to taste).
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4 cups Water.
- 20 Tulsi (holy basil).
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt - or to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Dry ginger powder.
- 1 tablespoon Instant Coffee Powder.
- 4 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chukku Kaapi Recipe (Dry Ginger Coffee), in a saucepan, boil enough water.
1. Add in jaggery once the water is boiling and stir till it dissolves.
1. Once jaggery is dissolved, add in dry ginger powder, cumin seeds, coffee powder, black pepper and boil for about 4 minutes to make the brew even and effective.
1. Now add Tulsi (holy basil) leaves and let the mixture boil for 2-3 minutes.
1. Once done add cardamom powder, a bit of salt and switch off heat.
1. Strain the Chukku Kaapi into serving cups and serve hot in mornings and evenings to get rid of common cough and cold.
