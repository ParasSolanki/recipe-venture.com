### Ingredients

- 500 grams Carrots (Gajjar) - diced.
- 250 grams Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish) - diced.
- Lemon juice - to taste.
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 1 Watermelon - peeled and cubed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Watermelon Carrot Radish Juice Recipe, first prepare the fruits and vegetables.
1. You can either use a juicer or a blender to make the juice.
1. If you use a blender, then you will have to strain the juice to consume.
1. Place the watermelon, carrots and radish in the juicer and blend to extract the juice.
1. Once done, pour it into a glass jar, stir in a pinch of salt and squeeze some lemon to taste and serve.
1. Serve Watermelon Carrot Radish Juice in the morning along with Grilled Rajma Masala Sandwich breakfast to give your day a refreshing start.
