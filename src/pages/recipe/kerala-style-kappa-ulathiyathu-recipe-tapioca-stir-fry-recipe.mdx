### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Shallots - chopped.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Tapioca root.
- Coconut Oil - for cooking.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 2 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kerala Style Kappa Ularthiyathu Recipe, firstly cut the tapioca and boiling them in a pressure cooker with water and salt for about 3 whistle.
1. Once done release the pressure naturally and drain the water and peel off the skin, cut them into big chunks and keep it aside.
1. We begin by heating kadai with oil, crackle the mustard seeds, and curry leaves and dry red chillies.
1. Then add in the onions and ginger and fry them till it is translucent, you can add the turmeric powder.
1. Once that is done, add in the boiled tapioca and salt and fry them till it evenly coated.
1. Cook for 2 minutes, switch off the gas and serve hot.
1. Serve the Kerala Style Kappa Ularthiyathu Recipe along with Kerala Style Fish Curry Recipe or Chettinad Muttai Masala Recipe and Kerala Parotta for an everyday meal.
