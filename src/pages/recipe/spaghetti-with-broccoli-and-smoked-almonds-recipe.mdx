### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 8 Badam (Almond) - slices (roasted and smoked).
- 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese - grated for garnish (optional).
- 3 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 3/4 teaspoon Lemon juice - or to taste.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped or red chillies.
- 250 grams Spaghetti Pasta - cooked as per package instructions and drained12 Broccoli florets.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spaghetti with Broccoli and smoked almond, first heat oil in a pan.
1. Add garlic, chillies and saut√© for a few seconds.
1. Toss in the broccoli florets and spaghetti.
1. Season with salt and squeeze some lime juice over it.
1. Mix everything.
1. Serve hot topped with almond slivers and parmesan cheese (if using).
1. Serve Spaghetti with Broccoli and smoked almonds with Pizza Margherita for quick weeknight meals.
