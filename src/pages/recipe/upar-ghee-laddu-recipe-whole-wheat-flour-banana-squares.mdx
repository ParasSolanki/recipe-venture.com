### Ingredients

- Water - for kneading.
- 1/4 cup Ghee.
- 2 tablespoons Caster Sugar - (powdered sugar) (adjust).
- 2 Ripe Bananas - peeled and chopped.
- 1/3 cup Jaggery.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 2 Cups Whole Wheat Flour.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Uparghi Laadu Recipe, combine the whole wheat flour, oil and ghee in a deep bowl and knead into a stiff dough adding water gradually.
1. Dough should be firm, hard and stiff.
1. It should not be soft and sticky.
1. Divide the dough into 4 equal portions and make balls out of it.
1. Dust a little flour and roll out the dough into a circle of medium thickness like that of a bhakhri.
1. Prick with the help of a fork or make small cuts with a knife.
1. Traditionally bhakris for this ladoo are cooked on an earthen tawa.
1. But if we don’t have one, we can make them on a non stick tava/griddle.
1. Heat a tawa and place the bhakri.
1. Cook on a low to medium flame from both sides till golden brown spots appear on it.
1. While cooking, press it with the help of a flat spoon or spatula.
1. Make all the 4 bhakris like this.
1. Let them cool down.
1. Once it cools down, make small pieces with hands and put them into a mixer jar and grind.
1. Once it is coarsely crushed, add jaggery and grind again to make a smooth mixture.
1. Take out the mixture in a wide bowl and add chopped bananas to it.
1. Mix well.
1. Heat ghee in a small pan.
1. Now add melted ghee and mix everything well by hand.
1. Be sure jaggery and ghee are blended well with the mixture.
1. In a greased plate, spread the mixture evenly.
1. Sprinkle powdered sugar on it.
1. Cut into square or diamond shaped pieces with a knife.
1. Uparghi Laadu Recipe  can be stored for 1-2 days at room temperature and 2-3 in refrigerator.
