### Ingredients

- 1 inch Ginger - cut into juliennes.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Rice.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1 cup Coconut milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Malaysian Nasi Lemak Rice Recipe, wash the rice thoroughly.
1. In a pressure cooker, add the washed and drained rice, water, coconut milk, salt and ginger and mix well.
1. Close the pressure cooker and pressure cook for 3 whistles and turn off the flame.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally, open the cooker fluff up the rice with a fork.
1. Transfer the Malaysian Nasi Lemak Rice to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Malaysian Nasi Lemak Rice along with Malaysian Vegetable Curry Recipe for a simple Malay meal.
