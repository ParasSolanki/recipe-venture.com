### Ingredients

-  2 tablespoons mustard oil.
- 1/2 kg small potatoes - boil and peel.
-  1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  1/4 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  2 tomatoes - chopped.
-  2 onions - chopped.
-  1/2 inch ginger.
-  coriander leaves - chopped Take (as per use).
-  1/4 teaspoon Sugar.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  1/4 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  2 green chillies - chopped.
-  1/4 teaspoon garam masala powder.

### Instructions

1. To make Chhotu Alur Dum, first heat mustard oil in a pan.
1. Add cumin seeds and let it cook for 10 seconds.
1. In a blender add onion, tomato, green chilli, ginger and make a paste.
1. Pour this mixture in the pan with salt, sugar and mix.
1. After 1 minute, add red chili powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder and mix.
1. Let it cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Now add potatoes, garam masala and mix.
1. Allow to cook for 7 to 10 manutas.
1. Turn off the gas and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve Chhotu Alur Dum along with Cholar Dal and Luchi for dinner.
