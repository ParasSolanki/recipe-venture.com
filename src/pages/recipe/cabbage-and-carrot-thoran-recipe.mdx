### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/2 Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - finely chopped.
- 4 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 3 Carrots (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 6 Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To prepare this easy Cabbage And Carrot Thoran Recipe, wash and prepare carrots and cabbage first.
1. In a bowl, add grated carrots and chopped cabbage.
1. Add onions, green chilies, cumin powder, turmeric powder and required salt to the vegetable mixture.
1. Toss them well so that they are mixed well together.
1. In a kadai, add oil and heat over a medium flame.
1. Add mustard seeds once the oil is hot.
1. Once the mustards splutter, add curry leaves and the vegetable mixture.
1. Cook the mixture over the medium flame with the lid covered.
1. This may take 10-12 minutes.
1. Once the vegetables become tender and cooked, add grated coconut to the mixture.
1. Combine them well and cook with the lid open for about 3 minutes till the dish is cooked.
1. Switch off the flame.
1. Serve the carrot and cabbage thoran with hot boiled rice, Cheriya ulli sambar and fried papad for a perfect meal.
