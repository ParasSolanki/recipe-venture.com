### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Ginger - paste.
- 1/4 cup Onion - paste.
- 4 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 tablespoon Melon seeds - paste.
- 1 teaspoon Nutmeg powder.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 2 tablespoon Onion - paste.
- 500 grams Mutton.
- 2 tablespoon Cashew nuts - paste.
- 2 tablespoon Poppy seeds - soaked in 4 tablespoon water.
- 1 teaspoon Mace (Javitri) - crushed.
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 3 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 4 tablespoon Ghee.
- Salt - as needed.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - paste.
- 4 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 4 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mutton Rezala recipe, In a large mixing bowl, add all the ingredients under ‘For Marinade’ along with mutton and mix well, cover it with a cling wrap and keep the bowl in refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
1. Heat ghee in a pressure cooker and add all the ingredients under 'Tempering' and let them to splutter.
1. Add the marinated mutton pieces into the cooker and fry for 3-4 mins on high flame.
1. Now add around 1.
1. 5 cups of water and mix well.
1. Bring it to boil, close the lid and pressure cook for 4-5 whistles or until the mutton is half done.
1. Grind soaked poppy seed and blanched cashew and melon seeds to make individual paste.
1. Heat rest of the ghee in a pan and add left over onion paste and sauté until raw smell goes off and colour changes to golden.
1. Pour the poppy seed paste, melon seed paste and cashew paste and continue to sauté for another 2-3 mins till ghee starts leaving the sides.
1. Add nutmeg powder and crushed mace.
1. Now add mutton mix and fry for 5 mins.
1. Cover the lid and continue cooking until mutton is nicely done.
1. Add sugar & salt and adjust seasoning as per your taste.
1. Once mutton is done add a dollop of ghee on the top and serve this delicious Mutton Rezala along with Whole Wheat Lachha Paratha and Onion Salad for a weekend meal with your family.
