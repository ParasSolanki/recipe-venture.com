### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 6 Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra).
- 1 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - broken into half.
- 6 Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - quartered.
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 2 Tomato - roughly chopped.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Andhra Style Vendakaya Charu Recipe (Ladies Finger Rasam Recipe) by placing  a pressure cooker on medium flame with ghee and the mustard seeds and allow them to splutter, next add  cumin seeds, fenugreek seeds, dry red chillies, curry leaves and allow them to crackle.
1. Next add in the chopped onions, and garlic pods.
1. Saute till it is translucent.
1. Then add in the tamarind water,  sugar, salt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, asafoetida, coriander powderand chopped tomatoes and okra.
1. Stir well.
1. Pressure cook the rasam for about 3 whistles, turn off the flame and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Serve the Andhra Style Vendakaya Charu Recipe (Ladies Finger Rasam Recipe) with  Chettinad Beans & Cauliflower Poriyal Recipe, hot steamed rice with a teaspoon of ghee to enrich the taste.
