### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 tablespoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- Salt - to taste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few.
- 3 Sweet Potato - boiled or roasted and mashed.
- 1 tablespoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1/4 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 2 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 1 tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Sunflower Oil - to shallow fry.
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar) - boiled and mashed.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Sweet Potato & Peas Cutlet Recipe, take a bowl and mix mashed peas and cooked sweet potato.
1. Add ginger garlic paste, green chillies, and fresh coriander, mix well.
1. Add all the dry ingredients, chaat masala, salt, chilli powder, roasted cumin powder, dry mango powder, garam masala.
1. Add lime juice and mix everything well.
1. Also, mix corn flour and water in a small bowl.
1. Shape the mixture into medium size patties.
1. Dip the patty in corn flour mixture and roll into the breadcrumbs, making sure that the bread crumbs cover the cutlet well.
1. Repeat for a batch of 3-4 patties at a time.
1. Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry them on both sides on low heat until golden brown.
1. Serve Sweet Potato & Peas Cutlet Recipe hot with dhaniya pudina chutney and dates and tamarind chutney or roasted tomato sauce.
