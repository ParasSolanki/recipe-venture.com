### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoon Butter.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 2 Tomatoes - sliced.
- Dried oregano - as required.
- 2 Cheese.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Grilled Tomato and Cheese Sandwich Recipe by spreading the butter evenly on both the side of the bread.
1. First place the bread on the sandwich toaster, then place the tomato, sprinkle the seasoning over it and place the cheese slice over it and then cover it.
1. Toast the sandwich till you get grill marks over the bread.
1. Serve the Grilled Tomato and Cheese Sandwich Recipe along with some glass of juice like Gajjar Lauki Juice Recipe (Bottle Gourd Carrot Juice) to kick start your breakfast with a satisfying meal.
