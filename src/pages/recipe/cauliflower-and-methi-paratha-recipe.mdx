### Ingredients

- 1 cup Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 cups Cauliflower (gobi) - grated/finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 5 Garlic.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/2 Onion - finely chopped.
- Ghee - for smearing.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Cauliflower And Methi Paratha Recipe, firstly heat oil in a kadhai.
1. When the oil gets heated, add cumin seeds.
1. Once the cumin seeds starts crackling, add finely chopped onion and fry till onion turns pinkish and soft.
1. Add finely chopped garlic and green chilies and stir till the raw smell of garlic goes away.
1. Once it is done, add grated cauliflower and let it cook for another 10 minutes.
1. Now add the finely chopped methi leaves and stir till the methi leaves are cooked.
1. Add turmeric and red chili powder and stir.
1. Finally, add salt according to your taste, mix and switch off the gas.
1. Let the stuffing cool down.
1. While the stuffing cools down, make the dough for the parathas.
1. Add salt and oil to wheat flour and mix.
1. Mould it into a soft yet firm dough by adding water little at a time.
1. Cover the dough with muslin cloth for 30 minutes.
1. Make small balls from the dough of equal size.
1. The size should be a little bigger than the table tennis ball.
1. Roll the dough ball into a little bit of dry wheat flour and roll it into small thick flat round shape.
1. Place 2 tablespoon of the stuffing on it and close from all sides.
1. Dust it in dry whole wheat flour and roll it into bigger round shapes, just like a chapati.
1. If you see any air bubbles while rolling, simply pat it gently with your fingertips.
1. Then apply 1 teaspoon ghee on the frying pan and place the paratha on it.
1. After 2-3 minutes, apply 1 teaspoon ghee on the upper side and turn.
1. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes more.
1. Serve Cauliflower And Methi Paratha Recipe with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney and Pumpkin Raita.
