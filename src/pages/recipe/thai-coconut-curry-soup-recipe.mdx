### Ingredients

- 3 Onion - chopped.
- 2 cups Coconut milk.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Poppy seeds.
- 3 cups Vegetable stock.
- 3 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 Stalk Lemon Grass - crushed.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 3 Green Chillies - (Thai variety).
- 2 Kaffir lime leaves - crushed.
- Raisins - a few.
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Salt - (adjust to taste).
- 2 cups Wheat Noodles - or rice noodles boiled.
- 1 teaspoon Coconut Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar - (optional).
- 2 tablespoons Cashew nuts.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thai Coconut Curry Soup Recipe, heat the coconut oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add in the bay leaves, ginger and garlic to the hot oil and fry on low heat.
1. Add in the crushed lemon grass and kaffir lime leaves at this stage and fry it for another minute.
1. Next, add in the green chilies and chopped green onions together with the vegetable stock to the pan.
1. Cover the lid and bring all the ingredients to a boil, reducing the flame thereafter and allowing it to simmer for another 20 minutes.
1. Now, add in the coconut milk to the pan to thicken the soup, the ground spice powder and salt and bring all of them to a boil again.
1. Finally, pass the entire pan contents through a sieve to remove the chunkier bits and you will be left with a smooth soup.
1. Put the curry soup back again in the pan and boil this till it reaches a good consistency.
1. At this stage, put in the boiled whole wheat noodles or rice noodles to the soup.
1. Serve Thai Coconut Curry Soup garnished with raisins, accompanied with toasted bread on the side.
1. Or pair Thai Coconut Curry Soup Recipe with Spicy Chilli Garlic Noodles Recipe or Thai Basil Vegetarian Fried Rice With Pineapple and Spicy Thai Red Chillies Recipe.
