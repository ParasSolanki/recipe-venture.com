### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Spirulina.
- 1 cup Papaya.
- 1 teaspoon Flax seeds - toasted.
- 2 teaspoons Alfalfa Powder.
- Tulsi (holy basil) - for garnish.
- 1 cup Soy milk.
- 1 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Wheat Grass Powder.
- 2 tablespoons Honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Super Food Green Smoothie Recipe, first prep all the ingredients and keep them handy.
1. Add all the ingredients except the flaxseed and tulsi into a blender and make a smooth puree.
1. Add water or more soy milk if required to adjust the consistency of the smoothie.
1. Pour the Super Food Green Smoothie into glasses, garnish with flax seeds and serve.
