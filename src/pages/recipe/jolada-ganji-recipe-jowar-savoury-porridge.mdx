### Ingredients

- 1 sprig Curry leaves - roughly torn.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice - (skip if you are using buttermilk).
- 1-1/2 cup Water - or buttermilk.
- 1/2 cup Jowar Flour (Sorghum).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 3 Green Chillies - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Jolada Ganji recipe, In a mixing bowl, add the jowar flour, salt and water or thin buttermilk.
1. Mix well without any lumps.
1. Heat a heavy bottomed kadai or pan with oil and temper with ingredients given under ' to temper'.
1. Once tempered, add the jowar flour mixture and keep stirring over low flame.
1. The mixture will thicken and turn glossy.
1. Switch off.
1. Serve Jolada Ganji along with Mixed Vegetable Sambar for a weekday dinner.
