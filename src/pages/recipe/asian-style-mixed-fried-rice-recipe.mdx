### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 1/4 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - cut in very small cubes.
- 1/4 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - cut in small cubes.
- 1 tablespoon Dark soy sauce.
- 1/4 cup Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 3 tablespoon Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - coarsely crushed.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Spring Onion Greens - chopped.
- 2-1/2 cup Rice - cooked.
- 1 tablespoon Rice Vinegar.
- 1/4 cup Sweet corn - steamed.
- 1 tablespoon Peanut Butter.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Asian Style Mixed Fried Rice, firstly heat oil in a wok.
1. Add chopped garlic and fry until the raw smell of garlic vanishes.
1. Add chopped spring onion white bulbs and toss for about 30 seconds.
1. After 30 seconds, add carrots and corn, toss well for another 30 seconds.
1. Add green pepper, peanut butter, soya sauce, red chili paste, rice vinegar and mix well.
1. Saute it for about 40-50 seconds and make sure everything is mixed properly.
1. Add spring onion greens and season with salt and pepper.
1. Once it is done, add 2 tablespoon of crushed peanut, mix well.
1. At the end, add the cooked rice and toss it well.
1. Garnish this delicious rice recipe with some spring onion greens and crushed peanuts.
1. Serve Asian Style Mixed Fried Rice with Vegetarian Thai Red Curry and Thai Spicy Corn & Green Papaya Salad.
