### Ingredients

- 1/2 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - julienned.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Cheese - grated.
- 1 Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish).
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 2 tablespoons Butter.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Radish Sandwich recipe, firstly grate the radish and keep aside.
1. Tightly hold the grated radish in your fist and extract the water from it and discard.
1. To a mixing bowl, add grated radish, black pepper powder, chat masala, finely chopped green chilli and salt and mix well.
1. Now let us assemble the sandwich.
1. Take a bread slice, apply some butter to it.
1. Spread grated radish mixture on this.
1. Top this with 3-4 capsicum juliennes.
1. Sprinkle grated cheese all over it.
1. Close this with other slice of bread.
1. Similarly assemble the other sandwiches.
1. Grill the sandwiches in a grill pan for 5 minutes or till the bread slices appear brownish and crispy.
1. Cut it into bite sized pieces and serve hot.
1. So make this yummy Radish Sandwich and serve it with Tomato Garlic Chutney, Dhaniya Pudina Chutney or any other chutney of your choice.
