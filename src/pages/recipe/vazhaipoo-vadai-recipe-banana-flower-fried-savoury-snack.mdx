### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 1/4 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 1/4 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf) - coarsely pounded.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Banana flower (Vazhaipoo) - soaked in buttermilk.
- 3-4 cloves Garlic - pound.
- 1/4 cup Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. We begin Vazhaipoo Vadai Recipe (Banana Flower Fried Savoury Snack Recipe) by soaking the chana dal and toor dal in hot water for half an hour.
1. Next, we move on to prepping the Banana Flower.
1. Peel the banana flower petals and remove the white small florets inside.
1. Keep a container with buttermilk ready in order to add the used florets.
1. From each banana floret, the stamen(kaalan) and the thin white coloured cover portions must be removed and should be soaked in the buttermilk.
1. Once the dal is soaked, drain the water and coarsely grind it in a blender.
1. You should be able to see a few broken pieces of the dal.
1. Keep aside.
1. Drain the buttermilk from the banana flower and roughly chop it.
1. Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl, including the ground dal and the chopped banana flower.
1. Check the taste, adjust the salt if required.
1. Heat oil for deep frying in a deep frying pan and shape the vada into small flat discs and deep fry until browned and crisp.
1. Serve your hot crispy Vazhaipoo Vadai Recipe (Banana Flower Fried Savoury Snack) with a sweet Semiya Payasam.
