### Ingredients

- 1 cup Spinach - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 100 grams Cheese.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - half boiled and grated.
- Black pepper powder - for seasoning.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Hash Browns With Spinach & Cheese Recipe, mix all the ingredients in a mixing bowl.
1. Check for the seasonings.
1. Do not mash them hard be gentle with the dough.
1. Divide them equally into small size and press lightly and shape them into patties.
1. Heat a flat pan with little oil and place the hash browns and toast it on both the sides for about 5 minutes.
1. Serve your crispy Hash brown With Spinach & Cheese Recipe with some toasted bread and tomato ketchup for a perfect light Mulled Apple Juice Recipe.
