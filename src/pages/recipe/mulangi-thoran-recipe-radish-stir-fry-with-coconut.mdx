### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Green Chillies.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 8 Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 2 Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish) - julienned and steamed.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Coconut Oil.
- Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mulangi Thoran Recipe (Raddish Stir Fry with Coconut), we will first steam the radish in a vegetable steamer and keep it ready.
1. Blend all the ingredients "to be ground" including fresh coconut, green chillies, curry leaves, ginger, salt into a coarse paste and keep aside.
1. In a heavy bottom pan, add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and allow it to crackle.
1. When they crackle, add curry leaves.
1. Add the cooked radish, the ground coconut mixture and give it a stir.
1. Add turmeric powder, salt to taste and stir fry for about 3 to 4 minutes until the Radish thoran is well incorporated.
1. Once done, serve warm.
1. Serve the Mulangi Thoran Recipe along with Steamed Rice, Dal Palak Recipe or Mango Rasam Recipe to complete your lunch plate.
