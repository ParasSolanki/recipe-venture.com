### Ingredients

- 2 Tomatoes - diced.
- 2 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - diced.
- 2 Beetroots - peeled and diced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Beetroot Carrot Tomato & Ginger Juice Recipe, prep all the ingredients and place them in the Juicer or a Mixer Grinder.
1. If using a Juicer, add water to the pulp to extract until the last bit of the juicer and serve.
1. If you are using a Mixer Grinder, blend adding water to make a smooth paste.
1. Strain, add water to adjust the consistency of the juice and serve.
1. Give this delicious Beetroot Carrot Tomato & Ginger Juice Recipe a try and serve it along with Breakfast Oatmeal Raisin Muffin Recipe for your breakfast.
