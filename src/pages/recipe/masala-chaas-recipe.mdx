### Ingredients

-  5 mint leaves - finely chopped.
-  1-1 / 2 cup water - cold.
-  1 green chili - finely chopped Take 1/8 teaspoon black pepper powder.
-  1/4 teaspoon cumin powder.
- 3/4 cup curd.
-  asafetida - a pinch.
-  1/8 teaspoon black salt or rock salt.
-  1/8 teaspoon ginger - grated.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  coriander leaves - a little.
-  1/4 teaspoon chaat masala powder.

### Instructions

1. To make Masala Chas recipe, firstly add curd, cumin powder, chaat masala powder, black salt, ginger, green chillies, black pepper powder, asafoetida, salt, cold water in a bowl and blend it well with the help of blenders.
1. After blending, add the Chas to the gloss.
1. Add coriander and mint to top and mix.
1. (If you want, you can also grind the mint and make pulses.
1. Serve Masala Chas with Rajasthani Dal Baati Churma and Mirchi Chili for dinner.
1. You can drink it even after eating it during the day.
