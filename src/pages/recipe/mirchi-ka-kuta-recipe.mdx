### Ingredients

- 20 Green Chillies.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Lemon - juiced.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Mirchi Ka Kuta Recipe, In a pestle and mortar, crush the mirchi and keep it in a bowl.
1. Grind the mustard seeds and saunf in a grinder or pestle and mortar and keep it aside.
1. Once the mirchi is crushed, to the same bowl, add ground mustard seeds and sauf by adding a little oil.
1. Mix it well and add 2 fresh lemon juice into it.
1. Keep in sun for 2 days.
1. you can eat it immediately also, but pickled version tastes yummier.
1. Serve Mirchi Ka Kuta Recipe along with Lauki Thepla Recipe and a bowl of homemade curd.
