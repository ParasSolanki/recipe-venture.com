### Ingredients

-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds .
-  oil - as per use.
- 1/2 cup gram flour.
-  1 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  5 sprig green coriander - finely chopped.
-  1 green chili - finely chopped.
-  1 teaspoon mustard.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  5 cloves garlic - peel and chop finely.
-  1 cup of water - lukewarm.
-  2 onions - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make junka, first heat the oil in a pan.
1. Add the mustard seeds and cumin seeds and cook for 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add garlic to it and cook till it turns brown.
1. After that add onion and green chillies and cook till the onion becomes soft.
1. After softening, add red chili powder, gram flour and cook on medium heat.
1. After 2 minutes add salt and lukewarm water and keep stirring.
1. Cook for 10 minutes until all the water runs out.
1. Beat the pan and cook for 5 minutes.
1. Remove the lid from the pan and cook for another 5 minutes.
1. Add some more oil in between.
1. Mix and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve junka with dal tadka, khichia churi and phulka for the day's dinner.
