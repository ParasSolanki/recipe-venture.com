### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 2 teaspoons Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 2 cups Dried Green Peas (Vatana).
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1/2 cup Sev - thin.
- 2 cups Pani Puris - fried.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Masala Puri Chaat Recipe, wash and soak the dried green peas overnight.
1. In the morning wash again, drain and pressure cook with 2 cups water for 3 whistles until they are well cooked and keep aside.
1. Now, to prepare the masala puri masala, dry roast the coriander seeds, cumin, fennel and dried red chillies until aromatic.
1. Once cool, make a fine powder using a mixer grinder.
1. Keep the cooked green peas back on heat, add the freshly prepared spice powder to the boiled green peas, season with salt and slight mash them with the back of the ladle.
1. If the masala is too thick, add water to your desired consistency, check the seasoning and simmer for a few minutes and switch off the heat.
1. To assemble the Masala Puri Chaat Recipe, take a plate, roughly crush a handful of crunchy fried pani puris, pour a couple of ladle full of the prepared hot green peas masala, pour a teaspoon of green chutney.
1. Garnish with few teaspoons of chopped onions, tomatoes, sev, coriander, squeeze the juice of 1/4th lemon on top and serve.
1. Serve the Masala Puri Chaat Recipe as an evening tea time snack with a hot cup of Masala Chai.
