### Ingredients

- 2 Seaweed Sheets.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - cut into thin strips.
- 1 Avocado - cut into thin strips.
- 1/2 cup Rice Vinegar.
- 1 tablespoon Sugar - (Adjust according to your preference).
- 1 cup Cooked rice.
- 1 Cucumber - cut into thin strips.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vegetarian Sushi recipe, cut the vegetables into thin strips.
1. Add salt and mix thoroughly.
1. Keep aside.
1. In a mixing bowl, take rice vinegar, sugar, add rice to it and mix.
1. Take one seaweed and spread rice mixture over it in a thin layer.
1. Don't cover the edges.
1. Arrange the vegetables in a single line.
1. Don't overcrowd.
1. Start rolling it.
1. Press it so that the roll gets tightened.
1. Cut the roll into half first with a sharp knife.
1. Don't put pressure on knife while cutting.
1. Just make back and forth motion.
1. Cut them into 1/2 inch thick pieces.
1. Serve Vegetarian Sushi with soy sauce.
1. Serve Vegetarian Sushi as a side dish along with Spicy Vegetable Noodles and Chilli Paneer Recipe for a weekend lunch or dinner.
