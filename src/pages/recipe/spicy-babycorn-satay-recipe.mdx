### Ingredients

- 2 clove Garlic - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 10 Baby corn.
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spicy Baby corn Satay Recipe, first steam the baby corns over high heat in a steamer for about 3 to 4 minutes, until just about cooked.
1. Once cooked firm, turn off the heat and keep aside.
1. In a bowl mix combine all the ingredients for the marinade and make a thick paste.
1. Add the baby corns to this and evenly coat them with the mixture.
1. Allow the baby corns to marinate in the mixture for at least 20 minutes.
1. Once the baby corns have marinated well, it's time to toss them in a pan, so the the corn get cooked and coated well with the marinade.
1. Heat a skillet pan on medium high heat.
1. Add a tablespoon of oil in the skillet and place the baby corns along with marinade into the pan and toss them in the oil and heat until all the marinade has thickened and the baby corn has cooked through.
1. Once done, remove the baby corn satay from the pan, insert skewers or picks through the center, place it in a platter and serve.
1. Serve the Baby Corn Satay along with a peanut dipping sauce as an appetizer for parties.
