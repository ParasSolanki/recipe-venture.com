### Ingredients

- 1 pinch Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Curry leaves.
- 1/2 cup Dill leaves - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 6-7 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut.
- Ginger - small piece.
- 1/2 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - optional.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Nuchinunde / Nucchina Unde Recipe , wash and soak dals in water for about 2-3 hours.
1. Drain water.
1. In a mixer, pulse the lentils coarsely along with green chillies and ginger without adding any water.
1. In a bowl, mix together ground lentils, dill leaves, coriander , curry leaves, coconut, hing & salt.
1. Heat water in a steamer.
1. Grease idli molds with oil.
1. Make oval shaped dumplings and place them in the greased idli stand.
1. Steam cook for 10-15 minutes.
1. When done, serve hot / warm Nucchinunde / Nucchina Unde Recipe with majjige huli / kadale chutney recipe or with tea during evening.
