### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 Beetroot - thinly sliced like juliennes.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - thinly sliced like juliennes.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - coarsely pounded.
- 2 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 2 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 5 tablespoon Red Wine Vinaigrette - (juice of one lemon).
- 3 tablespoons Feta Cheese - crumbled.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Simple Winter Salad With Beet, Carrots & Feta Recipe by prepping the ingredients in a large mixing bowl and keep it aside.
1. Add ice cubes on the vegetables to keep it fresh and crisp for the salad.
1. In a small mixing bowl, add all the ingredients for dressing and whisk it till it combines.
1. Adjust the salt and pepper and check for the seasoning.
1. Add the dressing to the vegetables and add the sunflower seeds and toss it over.
1. Add the tossed vegetables on a  serving plate.
1. top it up with feta cheese.
1. In a small bowl, crumble the feta cheese and mix it up with olive oil and oregano and top the served salad with some crumbled feta cheese.
1. Serve the Simple Winter Salad With Beet, Carrots & Feta Recipe as a light appetizer for a party along with Paneer And Grapes Platter Recipe - Healthy Party Appetizer.
