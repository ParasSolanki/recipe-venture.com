### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Dried oregano.
- 2 tablespoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Dried Thyme Leaves.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - roughly chopped- to be ground.
- 1 Lemon - juice from the lemon.
- 2-1/2 cups Cooked rice.
- 10 Pickled Jalapenos - sliced.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Jalapeno Cilantro Lime Rice, Keep your rice cooked and ready for the recipe.
1. You can check out the link on How to cook rice in a pressure cooker.
1. Add freshly washed coriander leaves into a mixer and grind it into a coarse paste and keep it aside.
1. Heat a kadai with oil, once hot add ginger and garlic and saute till they become soft.
1. Add in chopped onion and saute till they turn translucent.
1. Once the onion changes the colour to light brown, add in the sliced jalape√±os and a give it a light mix.
1. You can add cooked rice as well.
1. Sprinkle all the seasonings, red chilli flaxes, thyme, oregano, and ground coriander paste and salt to taste.
1. Toss the rice well and mix until you notice that all the grains have changed to a bright green colour.
1. Squeeze in the lemon juice, check the salt and seasoning and adjust according to taste.
1. Turn off the heat and transfer the Jalapeno Cilantro Lime Rice into a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve the Jalapeno Cilantro Lime Rice along with Jamaican Stew,  Delicious Herbed Hassel back Potatoes and a glass of wine to make a delicious continental meal.
