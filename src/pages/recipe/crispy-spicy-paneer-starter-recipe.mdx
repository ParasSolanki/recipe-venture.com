### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Achari masala.
- 500 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese).
- 12 Papads - (urad dal & pepper).
- Water - as required.
- 3 teaspoons Salt.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoons Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 teaspoons Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds).
- 1/2 cup Corn flour.
- 4 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- 2 teaspoons Mustard seeds.
- 2 teaspoons Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 6 teaspoons Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- Sunflower Oil - for frying.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Crispy & Spicy Paneer Starter Recipe, get ready with all the ingredients and start with making achar masala.
1. Pulse the spices in the mixer-grinder to get a coarse mix for the pickle.
1. Wash and pat dry the Paneer.
1. Cut the Paneer into 1/2" thick and 2" long cuboid.
1. 500 gram paneer will yield approximately 24 pieces.
1. Marinate paneer with a tablespoon of pickle mix, juice of half a lemon and salt only if required.
1. In the meantime, spread the black pepper urad dal papad on a kitchen towel and keep under open sun or fan for 45 minutes to an hour, or till the papads are dry.
1. Pulse papad in a Mixer-grinder and make a coarse powder.
1. Heat the oil for frying in Deep bottomed pan.
1. Make a thick paste of cornflour, a pinch of salt, and water.
1. Dip the marinated paneer in cornflour paste and roll it on the black pepper urad dal papad powder.
1. Put 5-6 pieces of prepared paneer for frying at a time.
1. Fry till golden brown.
1. Serve Crispy & Spicy Paneer Starter Recipe with roasted tomato sauce or Dhaniya Pudina Chutney and Masala Chai Recipe - Indian Spiced Tea as an appetizer during get-together or as a monsoon snack during tea time.
