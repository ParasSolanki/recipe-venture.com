### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 3 Carrots (Gajjar).
- 5-6 Curry leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard seeds.
- 8 Dry Red Chillies.

### Instructions

1. To begin with carrot Chutney, soak the dry red chillies and mustard seeds in just enough water for 1 hour or more.
1. Chop the carrots very finely (do not grate them).
1. Make a fine paste of the red chilly and mustard in a mixer grinder.
1. Add 3/4th of the carrots and give it one quick blend.
1. Do not make it into a paste.
1. In a pan, heat the oil for tempering in a pan.
1. Add mustard seeds and let it crackle.
1. Add curry leaves and let it crisp up.
1. Once it is done, add the ground carrot and chopped carrot in the pan.
1. Add 1/4 cup of water to the pan and add salt and jaggery and boil on a low flame, till the oil separates.
1. Once it is cooled down, store it in an air-tight jar.
1. Serve Carrot Chutney with Kanchipuram Idlis, Phulkas or Neer Dosa.
