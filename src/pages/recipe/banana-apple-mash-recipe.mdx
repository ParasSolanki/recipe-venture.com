### Ingredients

- 1 Ripe Bananas - finely chopped.
- 1/2 Apple - finely chopped without peel.
- 1-1/2 cup Milk.
- 1 pinch Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- Honey - to taste.
- 1/2 pinch Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Banana Apple Mash Recipe, clean and wash apple, peel off its skin.
1. Chop it finely.
1. In a saucepan, put the apple pieces inside add a bit of water and bring it to boil.
1. Once the apple is cooked, take it off heat, take out apples into a bowl.
1. Add banana, and mash very well with the back of the spoon until mushy.
1. Mix the mashed banana and cooked apple well together and mash it until they are well combined.
1. Serve this Banana and Apple Mash to your baby/toddler from 6 months onwards.
