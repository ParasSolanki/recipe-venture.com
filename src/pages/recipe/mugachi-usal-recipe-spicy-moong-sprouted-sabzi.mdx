### Ingredients

- 1 cup Green Moong Sprouts.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.
- Water - as required.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mugachi Usal recipe heat a flat skillet with oil, splutter the mustard seeds and jeera.
1. Then sprinkle a pinch of hing.
1. Place the moong sprouts in the pressure cooker.
1. Add the salt, turmeric powder, and salt and 1/4 cup of water and pressure cooker for 3 whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Heat oil in a skillet pan over medium heat.
1. Add the mustard and cumin and allow it to crackle.
1. Stir in the curry leaves, green chillies, ginger, coconut and cooked moong sprouts.
1. Stir well until combined.
1. Once the mugachi usal is combined well, check the taste and adjust the salt accordingly and turn off the heat.
1. Serve Mugachi Usal along with Phulkas, Punjabi Bhindi Kadhi and  Kachumber Salad Recipe With Cucumber, Onion & Tomatoes for a weekday lunch or dinner.
