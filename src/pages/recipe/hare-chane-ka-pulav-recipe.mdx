### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 cup Hara Chana.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few sprigs finely chopped.
- 1 cup Rice.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - peeled and cut into small cubes.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice - Juice from half lemon.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 2 Onion - thinly sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Hare Chane Ka Pulav, firstly heat ghee in a wok.
1. Add cumin seeds, cloves, red dry chillies and let it cook for about 15-20 seconds.
1. After 15-20 seconds, add onions and green chillies and let it cook till the onions become soft and translucent.
1. Once it is done, add in the potatoes for about 2 minutes on medium heat.
1. After 2 minutes add in the hare chane.
1. Cover the lid and cook it till the potatoes are half cooked.
1. Next, add the rice, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, lemon juice and salt.
1. Mix everything properly and cook it for abut 2-3 minutes.
1. Add the required water, cover the lid and let it cook on medium flame till the water is absorbed.
1. If the rice is still not cooked, add more water and cook it for few more minutes.
1. Once done, switch off the heat and garnish it with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve Hare Chane Ka Pulav with Burani Raita for a healthy and light meal.
