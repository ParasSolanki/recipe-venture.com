### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes - or black pepper powder.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh orange juice.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1 cup Black beans - soaked in water for 7-8 hours.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - peeled and chopped.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - as required or oregano leaves for garnish (optional).
- 3 teaspoons Dried oregano - or fresh one.
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes - or black pepper powder.
- 2 teaspoons Dried oregano - or fresh leaves.
- 3 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 3 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 5 cloves Garlic - grated or finely crushed.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Cumin powder (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the black bean soup with Mojo sauce first cook beans till they become soft.
1. You can either pressure cook them or cook them in a saucepan on the stove top.
1. In a saucepan, heat the oil.
1. Add garlic and onion.
1. Saut√© till the onion becomes translucent.
1. Add capsicum and carrot.
1. Stir and cook covered for 2-3 minutes.
1. Add some water if required.
1. Add the tomatoes, beans, cumin, oregano, chilli flakes and salt.
1. Add some water and bring it to a boil.
1. Mash some of the beans with a ladle to thicken the soup.
1. (You can blend some beans in a food processor if you want a thick soup)Serve in individual bowls, topped with a spoonful of mojo sauce and garnished with herbs.
1. For the mojo sauce: Whisk together all the ingredients and use as required.
1. Serve Black Bean soup with Mojo on its own or with baked Tortilla chips for a delicious light meal.
