### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - as required.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh cream.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera) - crushed.
- 200 grams Button mushrooms - chopped.
- 1 Broccoli - cut into florets.
- 2 teaspoons Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mushroom and Broccoli Soup recipe, wash the broccoli and mushrooms.
1. Cut broccoli into florets and slice mushroom into pieces.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat oil in a pressure cooker.
1. Once hot, add coarsely crushed cumin and whole black peppercorns.
1. Saute for a minute.
1. Now add broccoli and saute for a minute.
1. Add mushrooms and saute until it starts leaving water.
1. Pour 2 cups of water and add required salt.
1. Stir well and pressure cook for 2 whistles.
1. Once the pressure releases, open cooker and allow the Mushroom and Broccoli Soup to cool a bit.
1. Now using hand blender, mash and blend everything until smooth.
1. Pour the Mushroom and Broccoli Soup in soup bowl and add a tablespoon of fresh cream on top.
1. Garnish with coriander leaves, crushed pepper and serve hot.
1. Serve Mushroom and Broccoli Soup as a starter before your simple meal of Panchmel Dal, Aloo Bhindi Ki Sabzi and Phulka for a weekday meal.
