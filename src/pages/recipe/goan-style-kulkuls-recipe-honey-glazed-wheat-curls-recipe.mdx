### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Caster Sugar.
- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 Whole Egg.
- 1/4 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 2 cups Whole Wheat Flour.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Coconut milk.
- 1/2 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Goan Style Kulkuls Recipe (Honey glazed wheat Curls Recipe) by mixing all the ingredients in a small bowl to a soft dough.
1. Rest it for 10 minutes by covering it with a damp cloth.
1. Once it is rested it is time to make those small curls.
1. Take a small piece from the dough, keep a fork next to you and press the dough gently on the back of the fork.
1. Once the dough becomes flat, from one side start slowly rolling to join the other end, making sure the shape of the stripes remain.
1. Once you take the rolled curl out of the fork, gently roll it again by using both your palm making sure the curl stays intact and shape is not destroyed.
1. Heat a kadai with enough oil to fry these curls, once it is heated, add the curls by batches and fry them till it is brown.
1. Once its fried and it cools down, drizzle hone and toss it over.
1. Serve the Goan Style Kulkuls Recipe ( Honey glazed wheat Curls Recipe) as a Party Snack or just as a snack after School.
