### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1 tablespoon Spirulina.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 1/4 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 1/2 cup Muesli - Saffola Lite Delight.
- 4 Prunes - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Whole Wheat Muesli Pancake with Spirulina Recipe by mixing all the ingredients in a mixing bowl.
1. Mix well till it combines.
1. Heat a nonstick flat skillet, add a ladle of the pancake and flatten it evenly in the shape of a thick pan cake.
1. After 2 minutes, flip it over carefully and cook it again on the other side for 2 more minutes till it is brown enough.
1. Serve the Whole Wheat Muesli Pancake with Spirulina Recipe with some chopped strawberries and almond flaxes as a Healthy Breakfast Meal.
