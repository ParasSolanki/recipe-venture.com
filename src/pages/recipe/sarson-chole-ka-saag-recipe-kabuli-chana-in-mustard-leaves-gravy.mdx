### Ingredients

- 300 grams Mustard greens - washed and finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 150 grams Spinach Leaves (Palak) - washed and finely chopped.
- 1/3 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - soaked over night.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 50 grams Bathua leaves - washed and finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Ghee.
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Makki Ka Atta (Yellow Corn Meal Flour).
- White Butter - to serve.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sarson Chole Ka Saag Recipe, firstly wash and soak the chana overnight.
1. To cook the Chole for the Sarson Chole Ka SaagNext morning, transfer the chole along with the water into a pressure cooker with some salt, close the pressure cooker.
1. Pressure cook for 6-8 whistles and turn off the flame.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally before you open the pressure cooker.
1. How To Cook Legumes in Pressure CookerDrain the excess water from the chana and set aside.
1. To cook the Saag for the Sarson Chole Ka SaagIn a pressure cooker, add the finely chopped sarson leaves, palak leaves and bathua leaves, along with 1/4 cup of water, salt, green chillies and garlic.
1. Close and pressure cook for about 3 whistles and turn off the flame.
1. Once the pressure releases naturally, open the lid and sprinkle the corn meal over it, using a potato masher, mash the saag well.
1. To make the Tadka for the Sarson Chole Ka SaagHeat a kadai with ghee on medium flame, to this add the finely chopped ginger and garlic and saute for about a minute.
1. To this add the finely chopped onions, and cook until it turns into a deep brown colour.
1. Once the onions are brown, add in the chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
1. Mash the onion- tomato mixture with the back of the ladle.
1. At this stage, add all the dry masalas - salt, red chilli, turmeric powder, cumin powder, garam masala, and coriander powder.
1. Mix well and add the pre cooked greens, and chole and simmer for a few minutes until the Sarson Chole Ka Saag comes to a boil.
1. Transfer the Sarson Chole Ka Saag to a serving bowl and serve with a dollop of white butter.
1. Serve Sarson Chole Ka Saag Recipe along with Makki Ki Roti Recipe - A Classic North Indian Corn Roti, Gajar Shalgam Ka Paani Wala Achar Recipe - Carrot And Turnip Pickle, White Butter and Jaggery by the side to complete winter dinner.
1. You could also serve Sarson Chole  Ka Saag as brunch along with Puri Recipe - Learn to Make Soft Puffed Puris At Home, and  Boondi Raita Recipe Spiced With Black Salt.
