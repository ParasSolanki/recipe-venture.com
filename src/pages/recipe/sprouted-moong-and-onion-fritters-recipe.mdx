### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Rice flour.
- Red Chilli powder - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Green Moong Sprouts.
- Sunflower Oil - to deep fry.
- 1/2 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 2 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Sprouted Moong And Onion Fritters Recipe, prep all the ingredients.
1. Take a large mixing bowl, add all the ingredients except oil and mix well with water to make a thick batter.
1. Make sure you add water little by little to prevent lumps.
1. Heat oil in a kadai.
1. Once the oil is hot, drop spoonfuls of batter into oil and deep fry them until golden brown and crisp.
1. Drain the fritters on a kitchen towel once done.
1. Serve Sprouted Moong And Onion Fritters Recipe along with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney Recipe (Green Chutney) and tomato ketchup over Ginger Cardamom Chai (Spice Infused Indian Style Milk Tea) during evening with Tea Sandwich Recipe With Beets, Radish, Avocado and Arugula.
