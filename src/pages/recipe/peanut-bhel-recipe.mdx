### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Cucumber - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 300 grams Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Sev - thin variety.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Peanut Bhel Recipe, we first start with making the spiced peanuts.
1. Heat oil in a pan, add the peanuts and roast till they pop and turn brown, keep sauteing on medium heat.
1. You can opt to remove the outer layer.
1. Add in the red chilli, turmeric, roasted cumin, chaat masala and salt and mix well.
1. Saute for couple of minutes.
1. The spiced peanuts are ready.
1. To make the Peanut Bhel Recipe, take a big bowl, add the chopped onion, tomato, cucumbers, coriander leaves and mix well.
1. Add in the spiced peanuts, lemon juice mix well.
1. Check for the seasoning of salt.
1. Divide the bhel into 4 plates, garnish with more coriander leaves, sev and serve.
1. Serve the Peanut Bhel Recipe as a party appetizer or as an evening tea time snack along with a cup of Adrak Chai Recipe (Indian Style Ginger Tea ).
