### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 2 Turmeric leaves - Long ones.
- 3/4 cup Jaggery - adjustable.
- 2 tablespoon Ragi Flour (Finger Millet/ Nagli).
- 1/4 cup Rice flour.
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 1/4 cup Jowar Flour (Sorghum).
- 1/4 cup Dry Fruits.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Paatolyo, first mix together the jaggery, grated coconut, cardamom powder, salt, ragi flour and oats and cook in a kadhai till jaggery melts and the entire mixture thickens.
1. Add the dry fruits to the mixture and mix it properly.
1. Keep aside to cool down.
1. Meanwhile, wash and dry the turmeric leaves.
1. Mix together the rice flour, jowar flour and salt and make a thick spreadable paste using water as required.
1. Grease a steamer plate or keep a thin moistened cloth over it.
1. On the upper side of one turmeric leaf, spread a layer of the flour paste so as to cover the entire leaf leaving a border of about a centimetre.
1. Spread some filling lightly on one vertical half.
1. Fold the other half of the leaf lightly over the filled half and seal the edges giving a little pressure.
1. Place on the steamer plate and steam for 15 minutes.
1. Serve with some homemade ghee.
1. Serve Paatolyo as a dessert after your Goan meal of Goan Chana Ros, Tambdi Bhaji and Steamed Rice.
