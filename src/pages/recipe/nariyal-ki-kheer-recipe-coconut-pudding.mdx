### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Whole cashews - chopped.
- Saffron strands - a pinch.
- 3/4 cup Sugar - adjustable.
- 1 Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Badam (Almond) - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1 tablespoon Pistachios - chopped.
- 1 liter Milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Nariyal Ki Kheer Recipe, crush and soak the saffron strands in 1 teaspoon warm milk.
1. In a thick bottom pan, bring the rest of the milk to boil on a medium heat.
1. You need to stir in continuously to avoid any sticking to the base.
1. Keep stirring till the milk is reduced to just half of the original amount.
1. Now add grated coconut to milk and stir it firmly until it again starts boiling.
1. Cook on low medium heat for about 15-20 minutes or till the coconut is cooked well.
1. Now add sugar, cardamom powder, saffron, almonds, cashews and pistachios.
1. Stir it well and cook it for 5-7 minutes further, or till the nuts are cooked.
1. Garnish it with some remaing dry fruits and serve warm or chilled.
1. Serve Nariyal Ki Kheer Recipe as a dessert with Puri Recipe, Peshawari Chole Recipe, and Satvik Carrot Sprout Salad.
