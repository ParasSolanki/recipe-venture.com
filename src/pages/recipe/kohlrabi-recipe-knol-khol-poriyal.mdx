### Ingredients

- 3 cups Kohl rabi - peeled and sliced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Salt - for taste.
- 1 teaspoon Black Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 Tomato - diced.
- 2 tablespoons Dessicated Coconut.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion.
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Curry leaves - few.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kohlrabi recipe, take kohlrabi and peel the outer fibrous part and cut into small squares.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. When heated, add cumin seeds and mustard seeds.
1. When they splutter, add a teaspoon of black urad dal.
1. Saute until the dal tuurns brown.
1. Now add in curry leaves, dry red chillies and turmeric powder.
1. After 1 minute, add sliced onions and fry till the onion becomes soft.
1. Add tomato slices and cook till they become little soft.
1. Add fresh peas followed by kohlrabi slices and mix everything.
1. Now close the lid and cook for some more time mixing at regular intervalsAdd spices - coriander powder, red chilli powder and salt as per taste.
1. Add dry dessicated coconut, mix everything and cook for few minutes by closing lid.
1. Cook till kohlrabi slices become soft.
1. Turn off the heat and garnish with coriander leaves.
1. Serve Kohlrabi along with Dal Tadka and Steamed Rice for a weekday meal with your family.
