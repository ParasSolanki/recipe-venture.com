### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - crushed.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 10 Shrimps - peeled and deveined.
- 1/2 cup Tomato - crushed.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Mustard oil.
- 1 cup Star fruit - cut into small pieces.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Misa Maas Kordoi Tenga Recipe (Shrimp With Star Fruit Curry), rub salt and turmeric powder on shrimps.
1. Heat oil in a wok/frying pan & add shrimps and just fry for 2 minutes.
1. Take out and keep aside.
1. On same wok add dried red chili and mustard seeds, as when start crackling add garlic paste, fry for a bit.
1. Add star fruit pieces.
1. After star fruit is tender add 1/2 a cup water, turmeric powder, sugar and salt.
1. Let it cook for about 5 minutes.
1. Add fried shrimp and crushed tomato and cook for another 10 minutes.
1. Switch off the flame.
1. Serve Misa Maas Kordoi Tenga Recipe (Shrimp With Star Fruit Curry) hot with warm steamed rice.
