### Ingredients

- 1 Apple - chopped.
- 2 cups Chamomile tea - brewed.
- 1 cup Almond Milk (Badam Milk).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1 tablespoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Apple Tea Latte recipe, bring the cinnamon, milk and honey to a simmer in a small sauce pan over medium heat.
1. Meanwhile in a blender, combine the brewed tea and the apple.
1. Blend until smooth.
1. Pour the tea and apple mixture into 2 mugs and top with the milk mixture from the sauce pan.
1. Sprinkle some cinnamon powder on top and serve.
1. Serve Apple Tea Latte along with Choco Swirl Cookies Recipe or Orange Zested Shortbread Cookies for your tea time break.
