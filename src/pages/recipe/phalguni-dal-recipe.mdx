### Ingredients

-  2 teaspoons mustard oil.
-  2 cloves garlic - grated.
-  1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds.
- 1/4 cup lentils - wash.
-  asafetida - a pinch.
-  1 cup onion - finely chopped.
-  1/2 teaspoon Kalonji seeds.
-  1/2 teaspoon aniseed.
-  1 teaspoon mustard.
-  1/2 inch ginger - grated.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  2 green chillies - Cut.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  1/4 cup green peas.
-  1 teaspoon garam masala powder.
-  1 tomato - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make phalguni dal, first put dal in a pressure cooker.
1. Add tomatoes, onions, ginger and garlic to it.
1. After this, add 1-1 / 2 cups of water.
1. Melt, close the cooker and cook till 3 cities come.
1. After coming to 3 cities, turn off the gas and let the pressure drain automatically.
1. Open the cooker and mash the lentils.
1. Now heat mustard oil in a pan.
1. Add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds, fennel seeds, cumin seeds and kalonji.
1. Add green chilies, mix and let it cook for the next 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes add asafetida and green peas.
1. Let it cook for next 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes add masoor dal, garam masala and salt.
1. Mix and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve phalguni dal with brinjal bhaja and rice for dinner.
