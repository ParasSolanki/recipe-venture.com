### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - pealed and diced.
- 1 Karela (Bitter Gourd/ Pavakkai) - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - for seasoning.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Karotkhabi Kanghou Recipe, heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add cumin seeds, when it starts crackling add chopped onion.
1. Cook onion until soft and tender.
1. Once the onions are soft and tender, add chopped green chilies, karela and potato.
1. Let it cook for about 2 to 3 minutes.
1. After 2 to 3 minutes, add salt, turmeric powder and red chili powder.
1. Mix everything well.
1. Switch the heat to medium, cover and let it cook till the vegetables are cooked properly.
1. Once it is done, switch off the heat.
1. Serve Karotkhabi Kanghou along with Tomato Onion Sambar and Steamed Rice with a dollop of ghee for your everyday meal.
