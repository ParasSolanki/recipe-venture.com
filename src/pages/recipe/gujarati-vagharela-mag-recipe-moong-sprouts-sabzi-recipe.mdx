### Ingredients

- 1 sprig Curry leaves - roughly torn.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Lemon - juice from the lemon.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a small bunch.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Green Chillies - chopped or slit.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Green Moong Sprouts.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Gujarati Vagharela Mag Recipe - Moong Sprouts Sabzi,  you need to have the moong sprouts ready.
1. Place the moong sprouts in the pressure cooker along with 2 tablespoons of water, a pinch of salt and pressure cook for 2 whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Heat oil in a pan on medium heat; add the cumin seeds and allow them to crackle.
1. Stir in the green chillies, curry leaves ginger and turmeric powder, sauté for a few seconds.
1. Add the cooked moong sprouts and stir gently to combine all the ingredients.
1. Taste and adjust the salt to suit your taste.
1. Stir fry the Vagharela Mag for about a minute and turn off the heat, Finally squeeze the lemon juice, add the coriander leaves and give it a gentle stir.
1. Serve the Gujarati Vagharela Mag Recipe - Moong Sprouts Sabzi along with Tawa paratha to make a rich, wholesome and nutritious breakfast.
