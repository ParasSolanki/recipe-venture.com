### Ingredients

- 1/2 Black pepper powder.
- 400 grams Sweet Potato.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/3 cup Walnuts - pine nuts.
- 1/2 Lemon juice.
- 1/2 Dried oregano.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli flakes.
- 1/2 Chia Seeds.
- 1/2 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 1/2 Apple cider vinegar.
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 1-2 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 1-1/4 cup Oats Flour.
- 2 cups Basil leaves.
- Sunflower Oil - to cook.
- 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Basil Pesto Bombs Recipe, to make basi pesto, wash the basil leaves and drain the excess water and dry them by placing them on kitchen towels.
1. Now place all the ingredients of the basil pesto except olive in a food processor/ blender and blend them coarsely while slowly incorporating oil while blending or adding oil in intervals.
1. Store this in an air tight container in the refrigerator for a couple of hours.
1. Wash, peel and chop the sweet potato and let it steam cook until it is completely soft.
1. Let this cool to room temperature.
1. In a bowl, add chia seeds and add 1-1/2 tablespoons of water and let it swell for 10-15 minutes.
1. Now in a large mixing bowl add sweet potato and mash it completely add 1 cup of oat flour, chia mixture, apple cider vinegar, oregano, garlic, salt, chilli flakes and mix them all together to form a dough.
1. Let this rest for 15 minutes and check for the stickiness.
1. It should not stick to your hands.
1. If the dough is sticky add the remaining 1/4 cup oat flour and mix well.
1. Now pinch a lemon sized dough, flatten it and spoon 1 teaspoon of basil pesto into the centre and seal all the sides to form a ball.
1. Repeat with the remaining dough mixture.
1. Cook them on a paniyaram pan/mini dutch pancake pan by drizzling very little oil on a low to medium heat by flipping in between, to get an even colour.
1. Serve basil pesto bomb recipe with your favourite salad.
