### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Tamarind Paste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - to garnish.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 4 Small Brinjal (Baingan / Eggplant) - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Onions - chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Roasted Gram Dal (Pottukadalai).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Poppy seeds.
- 500 grams Avarekai (Broad Beans).
- 2 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Curry leaves - a handful.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 2 Mutton legs - chopped to 2 inch.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- Gingelly oil - as needed.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 4 Cloves (Laung).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Attukal Mochai Kuzhambu Recipe | Mutton Leg-Field Beans Gravy, prep with all the ingredients required.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed kadai.
1. Add jeera, saunf, cinnamon, cloves, bay leaf.
1. Once they are aromatic, add 2 chopped onions and saute till translucent.
1. Add poppy seeds, gram, tomatoes and saute till tomatoes are cooked.
1. Add grated coconut, switch off heat and allow to cool.
1. Once cooled, grind this into a smooth gravy, with salt & a little water if required.
1. In another kadai, heat a little gingelly oil, add chopped onion (1), chopped brinjals, field beans/avarekalu, and saute well.
1. Add water till the veggies are immersed and cover the kadai with its lid.
1. Meanwhile, also pressure cook goat leg for 15 whistles or till the leg meat is tender.
1. Once the pressure releases, add this meat with stock to the veggies in kadai and bring to boil.
1. Add the ground masala paste and enough water to make it a thin gravy.
1. Adjust salt and spices accordingly.
1. Once it starts boiling add tamarind extract and cook it covered over low flame for about 15 minutes.
1. In a tadka pan, heat a teaspoon oil and temper with mustard, curry leaves, 1 chopped onion, and hing.
1. Garnish with finely chopped coriander leaves and serve Attukal Mochai Kuzhambu Recipe | Mutton Leg-Field Beans Gravy with steamed rice/ Jeera rice or lachha paratha.
