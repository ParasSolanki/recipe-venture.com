### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- 2 teaspoons Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 3 Tomatoes - deseeded.
- 1/2 Lemon - juiced.
- 1 tablespoon Chicken masala.
- Salt - to taste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 2 Onions - roughly chopped.
- 500 grams Chicken - chopped to bite size.
- Sunflower Oil - to cook.
- 1 tablespoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chicken In Tomato Onion Gravy Recipe, take onions, green chillies and tomatoes (de seeded) in a mixer jar and grind to a smooth paste.
1. In a pressure cooker, add the onion-chilli and tomato paste, curd, salt, chicken masala powder, lemon juice and ginger garlic paste.
1. Stir to combine.
1. Add the chicken pieces and about half cup of water and close the lid of the pressure cooker and cook on high flame for about 3 whistles or till the chicken is cooked till tender.
1. Once the pressure is released on its own, heat a bit of oil in a kadai and once the oil is hot, transfer the gravy of the cooked chicken from the pressure cooker.
1. Retain the cooked chicken in the pressure cooker.
1. you may do this by refraining the chicken with a slotted spoon while pouring the gravy.
1. Cook for a few minutes while stirring, till the gravy starts bubbling and thickening.
1. Once the gravy is thickened to desired consistency, add the chicken pieces to the kadai, close a lid and cook on a low flame for about 5 minutes or till the flavours seep in a bit more in the chicken pieces.
1. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and serv.
1. Serve Chicken In Tomato Onion Gravy Recipe along with Homemade Butter Naan Recipe (Soft Yogurt Bread) or Jeera Rice Recipe - Cumin And Ghee Flavored Rice and Pickled Onions for a complete meal.
