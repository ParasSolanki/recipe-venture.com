### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Red onion -  finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - grated.
- 3 to 4 tablespoons Classic Mayonnaise (With Egg).
- 1 Green Chilli - or jalapeno.
- 1/4 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Vinegar - or lemon juice.
- 1 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - finely chopped or shredded.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder - (optional).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Crunchy Creamy Coleslaw Recipe, finely chop the cabbage, green chilli (optional), onions, bell pepper and grate the carrot.
1. Mix all the coleslaw vegetables in a bowl.
1. Add the mayonnaise, vinegar or lemon juice and black pepper powder (optional).
1. Mix well with a spoon.
1. Taste the coleslaw salad for a balance of spice, salt, sugar and tang.
1. Adjust accordingly.
1. Cover the Crunchy Creamy Coleslaw and refrigerate for at least 1 hour for the flavors to blend well.
1. The Crunchy Creamy Coleslaw Recipe goes great with Moroccan Chicken Burgers With Feta And Carrot Recipe or in a Tofu & Veggie Coleslaw Sandwich Recipe or as a side salad with any meal.
