### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - coarsely pounded.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1 cup Black Eyed Beans (Lobia) - soaked.
- 2 Celery - stalks.
- 3 Cloves (Laung) - garlic.
- 2 sprig Fresh Thyme leaves.
- 1/4 cup Homemade tomato puree.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Greek Style Μαυρομάτικα Recipe (Black Eyed Beans with Celery and Thyme Recipe) by soaking the black eyed peas in water for 2 hours.
1. Heat a pressure cooker and add oil, sauté the garlic and onions till it is translucent.
1. Add the herbs and celery which is chopped and sauté for some more time.
1. Then add the tomato puree and salt, sauté till it is cooked and the water gets evaporated.
1. Finally add the black eyed beans and coat it nicely with the masala and pressure cook it for 4 whistle.
1. Serve the Greek Style Μαυρομάτικα Recipe (Black Eyed Beans with Celery and Thyme Recipe) along with the Broken Wheat/ Bulgur to make it a healthy high protein meal.
