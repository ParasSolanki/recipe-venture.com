### Ingredients

- 100 grams Cheddar cheese - shredded (optional).
- 1 Cucumber - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 tablespoon Achari Mayo.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 4 Soft Taco Shells - or hard taco shells.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 400 grams Tofu - crumbled.
- 1/2 cup Sweet corn.
- 1 teaspoon Tabasco Original - Hot Sauce.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mexican Taco Recipe With Spicy Tofu Crumble, first prep all the ingredients and keep them ready.
1. Mix the tomatoes, onion, cucumber and toss it along with salt and pepper and keep aside.
1. Heat olive oil in a pan; add the crumbled tofu, corn and achari mayo along with tabasco sauce and toss them together on high heat until it is well combined.
1. Check the salt to taste and adjust accordingly.
1. Toss the onions, tomato and cucumber into the tofu corn crumble and keep aside.
1. To assemble the Mexican Taco Recipe With Spicy Tofu Crumble, warm the soft tacos on a skillet for a fe seconds.
1. Place it on a platter, spoon the achari tofu corn crumble on the tacos and serve immediately.
1. Serve your healthy Mexican Taco Recipe With Spicy Tofu Crumble along with Mexican Vegetarian Bean & Cheese Enchiladas Recipe, Cinnamon Spiced Orange Iced Tea Recipe, followed by a dessert of Mexican Style Capirotada Recipe-Bread Pudding Recipe.
