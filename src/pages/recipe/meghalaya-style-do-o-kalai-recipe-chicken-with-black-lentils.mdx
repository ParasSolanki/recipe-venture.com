### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Garlic - minced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 150 grams Black Urad Dal (Whole) - soaked overnight.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cooking soda - or baking soda.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder - Red chilly powder( 1 to 2 tbsp).
- 500 grams Chicken - (Preferably with skin).
- 3 tablespoons Ginger - grated.
- 3 Green Chillies - chopped (adjust).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 teaspoons Lemon juice.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Meghalaya Style Do O Kalai Recipe soak the black lentils overnight.
1. Start by grinding the onions, ginger, & garlic together in a small grinder jar to a smooth paste.
1. Add a couple of teaspoons of water only if required.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Take a pressure cooker and heat a little cooking oil in it.
1. On a medium heat, add the green chillies and saute for a few seconds.
1. Add onion-ginger-garlic paste and saute continuously so that the paste does not get burnt.
1. Once the raw smell disappears from the onion paste, add in the curry cut chicken and saute till well coated.
1. Once the chicken is well coated with the paste, add the red chilli powder.
1. Mix it well till the red chilli powder coats the chicken evenly.
1. Add the soaked urad dal lentils, salt to taste and cooking soda to the pressure cooker and mix.
1. Now close the lid of the pressure cooker and cook on a low heat for a couple of whistles till the lentils are tender and soft and mushy to form like a gravy and the chicken is cooked as well.
1. After a few whistles turn off heat, allow the pressure of the cooker to release naturally.
1. Once the pressure is released, remove the lid and add in the lemon juice.
1. Transfer the Do O Kalai into a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Do O Kalai Recipe with Yellow Pumpkin Pulav Recipe or Manipuri Style Tan Ngang Recipe, Manipuri Style Alu Kangmet Recipe and Narikol Pitha Recipe for a complete meal plate.
