### Ingredients

- Strawberries.
- Ripe Bananas - chopped to cubes.
- Kiwi - chopped to cubes.
- Red grapes.
- Mango (Ripe) - chopped to cubes.
- Watermelon - chopped to cubes.
- Lemon juice - to taste.
- Papaya - chopped to cubes.
- Cheese Cubes.
- Fresh raspberries - or any other Berries of choice.

### Instructions

1. To make the Fruit Kebabs recipe, toss the fruits and cheese in lemon (lemon).
1. Make sure you use the seasonal fruits to make the recipe more colourful and taste divine.
1. Keep the skewers ready, I like to use tooth picks too.
1. Thread the fruits and cheese into the skewers and serve.
1. Serve Fruit Kebabs Recipe as an appetizer for parties along with Assorted Cheese Platter Served With Fruits & Crackers and Strawberry Lemonade Recipe With Grand Marnier.
