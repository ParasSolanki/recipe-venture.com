### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 3 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 500 grams Button mushrooms.
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Pav bhaji masala.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 2 teaspoons Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tawa Mushroom Recipe, heat a kadai with oil.
1. Add ginger and garlic, allow it to soften.
1. Once done, add chopped onions and cook till they turn translucent.
1. Add chopped tomatoes and sprinkle with little salt so that the tomatoes become mushy and soft.
1. At this point you can add all the spice powders including red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, and saute well.
1. Add chopped mushrooms and cook until the water is all evaporated.
1. Check for salt and add if required.
1. Cook until the mixture becomes semi dry.
1. At the end add chopped coriander leaves and mix well.
1. Serve the Mushroom Do Pyaza Recipe along with Tawa Paratha and Panchmel Dal or Phulka, Curd Rice to make it a complete meal.
