### Ingredients

- 1 cup Cherry tomatoes - cut into halves.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 tablespoons Balsamic Vinegar.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 Avocado - cut into cubes.
- 1 cup Strawberries - cored and sliced.
- 10 Basil leaves - rolled and cut into thin strips.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - toasted.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Strawberry and Avocado Caprese Recipe, take a big bowl add in the sliced strawberries, cubes avocado, halved cherry tomatoes and toss it with olive oil, salt and sugar.
1. Just before serving, add in the balsamic vinegar and mix well.
1. Garnish with toasted sesame seeds.
1. Serve the Strawberry and Avocado Caprese Recipe as a snack or a a part of your brunch party menu, to add colour and new flavours.
