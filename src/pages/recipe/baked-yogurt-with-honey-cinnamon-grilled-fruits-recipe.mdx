### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 cup Fresh cream.
- 1 cup Pineapple - sliced.
- 4 Strawberries - sliced.
- 1 cup Condensed Milk.
- 1 Mango (Ripe) - sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 3/4 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 Pears - sliced.
- 2 Plum - sliced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 2 Peaches - sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Baked yogurt with honey cinnamon grilled fruits, first pre-heat the oven to 160 degree CelsiusAdd the yogurt to a mixing bowl and whisk.
1. Add the cream, condensed milk and vanilla extract if using.
1. Whisk till everything blends well.
1. Pour the mixture into 5-6 ramekins.
1. Make a water bath by pouring water in a baking tray till it is half filled.
1. Place the ramekins in the water bath and bake for about 18-20 minutes or till the mixture is just set.
1. Cool and refrigerate for a couple of hours.
1. To make the topping, heat and brush a grill pan with the butter.
1. Place the fruit slices on the pan and grill for 1-2 minutes, till you see grill marks on the slice.
1. This time may change depending on the fruit used.
1. Whisk the honey and cinnamon in a small bowl.
1. Brush it on the fruit slices.
1. Take the fruits slices off the heat.
1. Cool completely and place them on top of the cold baked yogurt and serve Baked yogurt with honey cinnamon grilled fruits as a Dessert.
