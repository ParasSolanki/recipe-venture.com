### Ingredients

- 1 cup Phool Makhana (Lotus Seeds) - chopped.
- Ghee - as needed.
- 1 tablespoon Whole cashews - slivered.
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1 cup Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Raisins - 1/4 for garnish.
- 1 cup Semiya (Vermicelli) - (Fine Banarsi Sewai).
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1/4 cup Badam (Almond) - slivered.
- 2 tablespoons Dry coconut (kopra).
- 1 cup Khoya (Mawa).
- 1-1/2 cup Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kimami Sewai Recipe, heat a pan on a slow flame and roast dry sewai till it turns dark brown.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Make sure to avoid over-roasting the delicate Sewai.
1. In the same pan, heat ghee and fry the fox nuts/makhana till it gets crunchy.
1. It will take 5 to 6 minutes on low medium heat.
1. After 5 to 6 minutes, add dry fruits and saute it for about 2 minutes.
1. Lastly, add coconut as it burns quickly, be very careful.
1. Fry altogether and keep aside.
1. In a deep pan, add sugar, khoya, milk, water and mix it well.
1. Put it on the flame and keep stirring till the first boil.
1. Slow down the flame to cook the sugar syrup till it gets thicker and you feel heavy while stirring.
1. Add 1/2 cup water or milk to the thick syrup and give another boil.
1. Add roasted sewai, dry fruits makhana coconut mixture and cook on a slow flame for about 4-5 minutes and switch off.
1. Sprinkle green cardamom powder and mix well.
1. Cover the lid and leave for 10 minutes.
1. Switch off the heat, give it a mix and garnish with almonds, cashews and raisins.
1. Serve Kimami Sewai Recipe along with a festive lunch of Mirchi Ka Salan Recipe, Lugai Gosht Recipe, Butter Garlic Naan Recipe and Pakki Mutton Biryani.
