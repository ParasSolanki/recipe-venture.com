### Ingredients

-  1/4 tsp sugar.
-  1 cup wheat flour.
- 1 cup all purpose flour (all purpose flour).
-  1/4 cup wheat bran.
-  1/2 tsp salt.
-  1-1 / 4 tsp active dry yeast.
-  1 cup water - lukewarm.

### Instructions

1. To make Goan Poi recipe, first of all add maida, wheat flour, active dry yeast, sugar, salt in a bowl and mix it well.
1. Now slowly add water and knead the dough.
1. Apply some oil on top and keep it aside to double for 2 hours.
1. Now knead the dough once again.
1. Take the dough in 12 parts.
1. Sprinkle the flour in the wheat bran and keep it on a baking sheet.
1. Keep it aside for 1 hour.
1. Now flatten them slightly with your hand.
1. Place on a baking sheet and preheat the oven at 220 Â° C for 10 minutes.
1. Put the poi in the oven and cook it for 6 to 8 minutes.
1. Do not keep long because the poi becomes hard.
1. Serve.
1. Serve Goan Poi Recipe with Goan Style Mushroom Shagotti or Aloo Vindaloo for dinner.
