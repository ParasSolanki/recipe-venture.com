### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Jaggery.
- 4 cups Water.
- 3 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds - pounded.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/4 cup White Urad Dal (Split) - soaked for 30 min.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Ulundhu Kanji Recipe | Urad Dal Porridge, wash and soak urad dal in water for about 30 minutes.
1. After soaking time, drain the urad dal and add into blender along with cardamom.
1. Add little water and grind into smooth paste.
1. Boil water in a pan, add jaggery to the water and dissolve it.
1. Strain to remove impurities.
1. Keep it again in flame and boil the jaggery water in low flame.
1. Add the urad dal paste to the boiling water and keep stirring.
1. Cook for 4-5 minutes in medium flame by stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
1. Once it becomes thick and smooth like porridge, remove from flame and add grated coconut to the kanji.
1. Mix and serve Sweet Ulundhu Kanji Recipe | Urad Dal Porridge warm.
