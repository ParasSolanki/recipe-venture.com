### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 15 Baby Potatoes.
- 250 ml Water.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 2 Tomatoes.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Cloves (Laung).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chotti Aloor Dum recipe, In a pressure cooker, boil baby potatoes with a pinch of salt for about 2 whistles.
1. Once the pressure is released, remove the skin from the potatoes and set it.
1. In a blender, add chopped tomatoes and grind them into a fine paste.
1. In a heavy bottomed pan, add oil and heat over medium flame.
1. Add cardamom, cloves and cinnamon stick to the oil and roast them for few seconds.
1. Now, add baby potatoes and toss them evenly in the oil.
1. Gently add the tomato puree followed by cumin powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder and turmeric powder.
1. Combine them well and add a glass of water to the mixture.
1. Cook them on a medium flame stirring them occasionally until the potatoes are blended well with the spices.
1. Season the gravy with the required amount of salt.
1. Remove the gravy from the flame and serve hot.
1. Chotti Aloor Dum is a versatile dish and can be served along with Bengali Luchi and Boondi Raita for a delicious meal.
