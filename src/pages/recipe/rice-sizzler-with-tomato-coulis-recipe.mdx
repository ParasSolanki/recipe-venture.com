### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped into cubes.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil - mixed with 4 tablespoon water to give it a sizzling effect..
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Butter.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Basmati rice - soaked in water for about an hour.
- 2 tablespoons Green peas (Matar).
- 8-10 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 4 Tomatoes.
- 1/2 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - chopped into cubes.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - Handful.
- 1 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 cup Potato (Aloo) - wedges.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Rice Sizzler With Tomato Coulis recipe, firstly we will prepare the tomato coulis sauce.
1. Dip the tomatoes in a saucepan of hot boiling water for about 20-30 seconds.
1. After 20-30 seconds, peel of the skin of the tomatoes and chop them very finely.
1. Heat the oil in a pan and sauté the tomatoes with garlic for about 10 minutes on low heatOnce it is done, add salt, pepper and butter.
1. Blend the mixture in a mixer to make a puree out of it and keep it aside.
1. The next step is to make the rice.
1. Heat oil in a kadhai and add cloves, bay leaf, cinnamon, cardamom, black elaichi, black pepper corns and cumin seeds.
1. Saute them for about a minute.
1. After a minute, add onion and sauté them again for another minute.
1. Now add green chili, carrot, green peas, basmati rice and double the volume of water.
1. Cover and cook it on low heat till the rice is soft.
1. Preheat the sizzle plate in the oven for about 30 minutes.
1. The next step is to combine and serve it on a sizzler plate.
1. Drizzle some butter and spread cabbage leaves.
1. Fill a small round cup with the rice and invert it in the centre of sizzler plate.
1. Pour the half tomato coulis over rice.
1. Mix vegetables with the remaining sauce and cook for two minutes in a pan.
1. Arrange saucy vegetables and potato wedges at the side of the hot plate and sprinkle some coriander leaves and serve.
1. Make Rice Sizzler With Tomato Coulis for a light dinner during your busy weekdays and enjoy it with your family.
1. You can optionally add some stuffed grilled tomatoes by the side for an addition twist.
