### Ingredients

- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- 2 tablespoons All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 4 cloves Garlic - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 2 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 2 teaspoon Tandoori masala.
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 300 grams Button mushrooms - quartered.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mushroom 65 Recipe, wash the mushrooms, pat dry and quarter them and keep aside.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the cornflour, all-purpose flour and salt.
1. Make a thick batter by adding little water.
1. Add water using a tablespoon little by little to make a thick batter.
1. Add the quartered mushrooms in the batter and keep aside.
1. Heat oil for deep frying in a Kadai/ Wok on medium high heat.
1.  Add a few mushrooms at a time and deep fry them until browned and crisp.
1. Drain on paper towels and keep the mushroom aside.
1. In another Kadai/ Wok, heat a tablespoon of oil on medium heat.
1. Add the ginger, garlic, green chilies and curry leaves and saute for a few minutes.
1. Add the fried mushrooms, the black pepper powder and tandoori masala powder.
1. Stir fry until the mushrooms get well coated with the spices.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Stir in the chopped coriander leaves and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer to a serving bowl and serve.
1. Serve the Mushroom 65 (Indian Style Spicy Mushrooms with Chillies) as an appetizer for parties.
