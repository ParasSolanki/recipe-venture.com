### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - skin peeled and cut into medium sized pieces.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds) - optional.
- 250 grams Flat Green Beans - ends cut and chopped into half.
- 2 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 tablespoons Poppy seeds.
- Mustard oil - for cooking.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bengali Style Sheem Poshto Recipe, first prepare all the vegetables and keep it ready.
1. Grind the poppy seeds with green chilli along with little water to a smooth paste.
1. Heat a kadai with mustard oil, add mustard seeds, onions seeds and allow them to crackle for 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add the flat beans and fry them till they soften a little.
1. Later, add in your chopped potato and fry again for another 10 minutes by closing it with a lid.
1. Once the potatoes are lightly cooked, add the ground poshto paste along with turmeric powder, salt, sugar and little bit of water.
1. Mix well, and saute them for another 10 minutes until the gravy thickens and you get a dry curry.
1. Serve the Bengali Style Sheem Poshto Recipe along with Bengali Style Cholar Dal Recipe and Steamed Rice to enjoy your everyday meal.
