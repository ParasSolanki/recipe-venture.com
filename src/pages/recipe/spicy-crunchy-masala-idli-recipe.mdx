### Ingredients

- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3 tablespoons Tomato Ketchup.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 cup Tomato - finely chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - to taste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.
- 10 Idli - cut into strips.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 cup Onions - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Spicy Crunchy Masala Idli Recipe, Heat a tablespoon of oil in a heavy bottomed pan and stir fry them until they are lightly crisp.
1. Transfer to a bowl and keep aside.
1. In 2 teaspoons of oil the same pan; add in the onions, capsicum, green chillies and saute them on medium heat until tender.
1. This takes about 5 to 7 minutes.
1.  Add in the chopped tomatoes, salt, pepper, turmeric powder, chilli powder and stir well.
1. Saute until the tomatoes turn soft.
1. Finally stir in the tomato ketchup, the fried idli's the chopped coriander and mint leaves.
1. Stir fry on high heat until well combined just for about a minute.
1. Turn off the heat and transfer the Spicy Crunchy Masala Idli Recipe to a serving bowl and serve hot topped with more date chutney or tomato ketchup.
