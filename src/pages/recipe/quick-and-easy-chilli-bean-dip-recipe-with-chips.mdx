### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera) - roasted ground to a paste.
- 1 Baked beans - Can.
- 2 sprig Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Britannia Cheese Spread - Mexican Chilli.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - for cooking.
- 3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Quick And Easy Chilli Bean Dip Recipe With Chips, heat the oil in a pan, add the onions and the garlic and saute for a few seconds.
1. Add the baked beans, cumin powder, chili powder, and mix well and cook on a medium heat.
1. Use a potato masher and coarsely mash the baked beans until you get a dip like consistency.
1. Stir in the Mexican Chili Cheese Spreadz which not only adds the the richness but also taste.
1. You will notice the creamy texture the cheese spreadz has given this dip.
1. Stir well and Adjust the salt according to your taste and voila the Creamy Chilli Bean Dip is ready.
1. Serve the Quick And Easy Chilli Bean Dip along with tortillas chips as an appetizer for parties.
