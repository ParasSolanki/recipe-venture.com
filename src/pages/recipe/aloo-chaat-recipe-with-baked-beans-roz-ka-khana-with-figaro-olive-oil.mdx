### Ingredients

- Whole Wheat Brown Bread - 8 slices.
- Sweet Chutney (Date & Tamarind).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Sev - for topping.
- Salt - to taste.
- Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - for toasting the bread.
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- Anardana Powder (Pomegranate Seed Powder) - for serving.
- Baked beans - as needed.
- Chaat Masala Powder - for seasoning.
- 4 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled and mashed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Aloo Chaat with Baked Beans recipe, we first have all the ingredients ready.
1. First, have the homemade baked beans ready.
1. Click here to see the recipe of how to make homemade baked beansIn a bowl, add the boiled potatoes, sprinkle salt, cumin powder, chaat masala powder, a tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil and some chopped coriander leaves.
1. Using a fork, mash the potatoes well and check the seasoning and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Keep the mashed potato mixture aside.
1. Apply extra virgin olive oil on the bread slices and cut them into half.
1. Spoon the potato mixture over the bread slices and spread it evenly on the top.
1. Preheat the skillet on medium heat and place bread with potatoes on the baking tray and cook it on both sides like a patty.
1. Once you it getting crisp and golden, you can keep it aside.
1. In a bowl, toss the onions, some chaat masala and chopped coriander leaves.
1. Keep this aside.
1. Now when you are ready to serve, warm the beans and spoon the baked beans on each of the mashed potatoes.
1. Sprinkle them with the onion mixture and finally top it with crisp sev.
1. Serve the Aloo Chaat with Baked Beans recipe immediately as a teatime snack or as an appetizer for your parties.
