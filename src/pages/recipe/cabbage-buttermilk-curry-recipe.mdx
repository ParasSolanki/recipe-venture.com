### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 4 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 3 tablespoons Gram flour (besan).
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 1 Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - medium sized.
- Sunflower Oil - for tadka.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Cabbage & Buttermilk Curry Recipe, prep up with all the ingredients needed.
1. Heat oil in a Kadai on a medium flame and add mustard seeds.
1. Once mustard seeds splutter, add asafoetida, salt, and turmeric.
1. Add chopped cabbage and saute for 2 minutes.
1. Blend all the ingredients of Buttermilk curry well with a wire whisk, transfer them to a pan.
1. Let the buttermilk mixture boil and once done, mix it with cabbage curry and switch off the flame.
1. Garnish with coriander and Cabbage & Buttermilk Curry Recipe goes well with hot chapati, phulka or plain cooked rice.
