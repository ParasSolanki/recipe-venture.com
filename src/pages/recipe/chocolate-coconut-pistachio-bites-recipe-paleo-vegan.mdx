### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/3 cup Almond butter - or Peanut BUtter.
- 3/4 cup Dessicated Coconut - (flakes).
- 1/3 cup Maple syrup - or Honey.
- 1 cup Pistachios.
- 1/2 cup Cocoa Powder - (or chocolate protein).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Chocolate Coconut Pistachio Bites Recipe (Paleo & Vegan), process the unshelled pistachios in a food processor into a coarse powder.
1. Process the coconut flakes in a food processor into a fine flour.
1. Transfer the coconut flour into a large bowl and add the other ingredients - cocoa powder, almond butter, vanilla extract with maple syrup being last.
1. If the batter is too wet, add more coconut flour; if the batter is too dry, add maple syrup a little extra.
1. Mix well and roll with your hands into small round bites.
1. Freeze the bites in the airtight container or just refrigerate to retain its freshness.
