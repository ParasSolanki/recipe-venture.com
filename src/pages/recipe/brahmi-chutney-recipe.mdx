### Ingredients

- Curry leaves - a few.
- 1 cup Brahmi leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 12 grams Tamarind - (grape size).
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1-1/2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 2 teaspoon Coconut Oil.
- 4 - 6 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Brahmi Chutney Recipe, clean and wash brahmi leaves.
1. Heat a kadai with coconut oil, add urad dal, chana dal, red chillies, curry leaves and hing.
1. Fry them in oil till the urad dal and chana dal turns golden brown in color.
1. Allow them to cool to room temperature.
1. Transfer roasted and cooled masala to a blender / mixer.
1. Add in salt , tamarind, coconut and brahmi leaves.
1. Grind to a smooth consistency by adding little water.
1. Brahmi Chutney Recipe is a simple nutrient dense dish  that can be relished with rice, Homemade Crisp Ghee Roast Dosas, Carrot & Cheese Filled Idli Recipe, Jolada Roti Recipe (Jowar Bhakri) etc.
