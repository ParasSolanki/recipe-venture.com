### Ingredients

- 2 Sweet Potatoes - cut into 1 inch cubes.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1-1/2 tablespoons Soy sauce.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garlic powder.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Peanut Butter.
- 1 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 Pineapple - cut into cubes.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- Lemon juice - to taste.
- 1 Lemon - juiced.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Sweet Potato Pineapple Skewers In Spicy Sweetish Peanut Dip Recipe, soak wooden skewers in water for 15-20 minutes, to start with.
1. Partially cook sweet potato in a microwave for about 3-4 minutes, or grill them till cooked al dente, while basting with olive oil.
1. In a mixing bowl, take olive oil, salt, lemon juice, chilli powder, soya sauce and garlic powder.
1. Throw in the sweet potato cubes and pineapple cubes.
1. Mix everything well .
1. Allow to marinate for 10 minutes.
1. Meanwhile preheat oven for 10 mins at 180C.
1. Skew all the marinated cubes into a skewer.
1. Brush the leftover marinade.
1. Grill the sweet potato pineapple skewers, flipping every 5-6 minutes, for a total of about 20 minutes, until the sweet potatoes are tender.
1. As its getting grilled prepare the spicy peanut dip.
1. Mix all the ingredients mentioned for peanut sauce, in a mixing bowl.
1. The spicy sweetish peanut dip is ready.
1. You can serve Sweet Potato Pineapple Skewers In Spicy Sweetish Peanut Dip Recipe along with other skewers like Paprika Chicken Skewers Recipe and Grilled Tofu in Spicy Plum Barbecue Sauce for a weekend house party for starters.
