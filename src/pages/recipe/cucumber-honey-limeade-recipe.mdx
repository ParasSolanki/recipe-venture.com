### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Salt - (adjust).
- 2 tablespoons Honey - (adjust).
- Ice cubes - to serve.
- 1/4 cup Aerated water (Soda Water).
- 1 Cucumber - juiced and strained.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 2 tablespoons Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Cucumber Honey Limeade Recipe, peel a cucumber and cut into small pieces.
1. In a blender blend cucumber, strain in a strainer and extract the juice.
1. In a mixing jar add honey, lemon juice, salt, cucumber juice and water.
1. Mix it well.
1. Pour in a serving glass living 1/4 space for club soda.
1. Fill with chill club soda and add ice cube.
1. Cucumber Honey Limeade is ready to serve and enjoy.
1. Serve Cucumber Honey Limeade Recipe as an energy drink on a summer evening with Tea Sandwich Recipe With Beets, Radish, Avocado and Arugula or Grilled Tofu Satay Recipe (Wrapped in Lettuce with Peanut Dipping Sauce).
