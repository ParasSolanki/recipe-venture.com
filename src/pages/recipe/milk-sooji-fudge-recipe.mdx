### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Ghee.
- 1 tablespoon Slivered Almonds (Badam).
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1/2 cup Fresh cream.
- 2 cups Sugar.
- 1 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Milk Sooji Fudge Recipe, take a thick bottom wok, add milk and sugar.
1. Allow the milk to boil on medium flame with continuous stirring.
1. Take another pan, heat ghee, add fine sooji and fry well.
1. Add the fried rava to the sugar milk mixture and continue to stir.
1. Now add the thick fresh cream, cardamom powder to this and stir further.
1. Here, fresh cream can be replaced with little more of ghee.
1. At a point, the mixture starts leaving the sides of the pan.
1. Now pour this mixture into greased tray and garnish with almond pieces.
1. Allow it to cool and after they cool cut into pieces.
1. Store these milk sooji fudges in airtight containers.
1. Serve Milk Sooji Fudge Recipe along with Masala Chai Recipe - Indian Spiced Tea when there are guests at home.
