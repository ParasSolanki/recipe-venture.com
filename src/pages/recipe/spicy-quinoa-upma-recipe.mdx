### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/8 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - as per your taste.
- 1 cup Quinoa - organic.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger - finely grated or chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar) - steamed.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Green Chillies - slit lengthwise.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Spicy Quinoa and Oats Upma Recipe, dry roast the quinoa in a large pan for about 3 to 5 minutes on medium heat.
1. Once they get a roasted aroma, set them aside.
1. In the same heavy bottomed pan, add a tablespoon of olive oil on medium-low heat,  once the oil is hot, add ginger, cumin seeds, curry leaves and green chilies.
1. When the cumin seeds begin to sizzle, add onions and saute it until the onions turn translucent and become soft.
1. Add the turmeric powder, tomatoes and peas to the onions and saute it for a couple of minutes.
1. Sprinkle salt according to your taste and pour in 2 cups of water to the tomato onion mixture and bring the water to a brisk boil.
1. Once the water is boiling, add the roasted quinoa and allow them cook for about 4-6 minutes on medium heat.
1. Stir gently on low heat to get rid of any lumps.
1. Cover the Quinoa upma with a lid and allow it cook for another 10 minutes until all the water is absorbed and the quinoa is cooked.
1. After 10 minutes, turn off the heat and garnish the Spicy Quinoa Upma with chopped coriander leaves.
1. You can optionally sprinkle some lemon juice and serve warm.
1. Serve the Quinoa Upma along with Coconut Chutney, a glass of Green Apple Spinach Juice for a wholesome breakfast.
