### Ingredients

- 220 ml Milk.
- Black pepper powder - (optional).
- Honey - or sugar to taste.
- 1 inch Fresh turmeric root - or 1 teaspoon turmeric powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Turmeric Milk (Haldi Doodh), bring the grated turmeric root/ haldi and milk to boil for a couple of minutes.
1. Once the turmeric milk boils, turn off the heat and allow the turmeric milk mixture to rest for a couple minutes.
1.  This will bring out the essential medicines and color from the turmeric.
1. After a couple of minutes of resting, strain the turmeric milk from the turmeric root into a glass, stir in sugar / honey and black pepper and serve the Turmeric Milk while still hot.
1. The Turmeric milk/ Haldi Doodh / Golden Milk is a great home remedy for cough and colds and you have give to children as young as 1 year.
1. Note: If you are using turmeric powder, you just have to boil the milk with the powder, add in the sugar/ honey and serve as it is.
