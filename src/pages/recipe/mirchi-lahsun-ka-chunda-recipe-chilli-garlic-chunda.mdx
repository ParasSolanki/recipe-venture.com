### Ingredients

- 6 Green Chillies.
- 15 cloves Garlic - crushed and chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 tablespoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To prepare this easy Mirchi Lahsun Ka Chunda Recipe (Chilli Garlic Chunda), heat oil in a pan.
1. Add cumin seeds and asafoetida .
1. Add garlic and saute it for 1 minute.
1. Add green chillies and saute it for 1-2 minutes or till soft and cooked.
1. Add salt to taste and lemon.
1. Cook for a few more seconds.
1. Mirchi Lahsun Ka Chunda Recipe (Chilli Garlic Chunda) is ready to be served with weekend Rajasthani meal.
