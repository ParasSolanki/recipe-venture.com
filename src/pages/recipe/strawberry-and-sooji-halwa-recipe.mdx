### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Mixed nuts - sliced.
- 1-1/2 tablespoons Ghee.
- 1 cup Hot water.
- 1/4 cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).
- 1/4 cup Sugar.
- 4 tablespoons Strawberry Compote.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).

### Instructions

1. We begin making Strawberry And Sooji Halwa Recipe with heating ghee in a kadai.
1. Once the ghee melts, add semolina and roast till it starts giving a nice aroma and the color changes to light golden brown.
1. You can use less ghee to roast semolina, too.
1. Now add the hot boiling water (or milk if using) slowly and keep stirring the halwa.
1. Add sugar and cardamom powder and mix well.
1. Once the sugar mixes completely, cover the kadai and let the halwa simmer for 2 more minutes.
1. Now add strawberry puree and cook for another 2 minutes or till done.
1. Transfer the halwa into a serving bowl and garnish with the sliced almonds and cashewnuts.
1. Halwa is ready to serve.
1. Serve Strawberry And Sooji Halwa Recipe as a party dessert along with Gujarati Badshahi Pulao Recipe - A Rich Preparation Of Rice, Vegetables, Nuts And Spices.
