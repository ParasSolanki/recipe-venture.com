### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Sambar Powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 cups Cooked rice.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- Ghee - or oil as required.
- 1 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - shredded.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Spicy Cabbage Rice recipe, firstly heat oil in a kadai.
1. Add cumin seeds, green chillies and let it cook for 15 seconds.
1. Next, add in the sliced onions and cook till they become soft and translucent.
1. Add in the shredded cabbage, salt and turmeric powder.
1. Cook till the cabbage becomes soft.
1. Once it is done, add in the cooked rice, red chilli powder and the sambar powder.
1. Mix everything well.
1. Cover for 2 minutes.
1. Open the lid, give it a stir and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
1. Serve Spicy Cabbage Rice recipe with raita of your choice.
