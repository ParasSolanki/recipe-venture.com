### Ingredients

-  1 teaspoon Rye.
-  Salt - as per taste.
-  12 green beans - finely chopped.
-  1 onion - finely chopped.
-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
-  3 tablespoons Ghee.
- 1 cup semolina.
-  1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (split).
-  1 Lemon juice.
-  1 inch ginger - tightly.
-  Oil - As per use.
-  1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida.
-  5 curry leaves - finely chopped.
-  1 carrot - peeled and finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make Sabzi Rava Upma, first of all steam the carrots and green beans with water.
1. After cooking, drain the water and keep aside.
1. In a saucepan; Boil 3 cups of water with salt and sugar and once it comes to boil in water, keep it aside.
1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat.
1. Add the mustard seeds, urad dal and let it cook for 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add onion, ginger, asafoetida, curry leaves and green chillies.
1. Cook onion until it becomes soft.
1. Add semolina to it and roast.
1. Cook till it gets roasted well.
1. Take care not to let semolina turn brown.
1. Slowly pour hot water into the mixture and stir it with the help of a spoon.
1. Reduce the flame, cover the pan and allow the sub to cook.
1. Cook for 10 minutes.
1. Don't forget to move between bitches.
1. Finally add ghee, boiled vegetables and lemon juice.
1. Cook for a while and then serve hot.
1. Serve Sabzi Rava Upma with coconut chutney and filter coffee for breakfast.
