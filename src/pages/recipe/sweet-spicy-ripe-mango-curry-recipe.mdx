### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Tamarind Paste.
- 3 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 3 Mango (Ripe) - peeled and diced.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Sweet & Spicy Ripe Mango Curry Recipe, firstly peel the mangoes.
1. Heat oil in a wide pan over medium heat.
1. Add all the mustard, cumin, methi and fennel seeds and let them crackle.
1. Add whole chillies and add rest of the masalas.
1. Next, add the peeled Mangoes.
1. Saut√© on low heat for two minutes.
1. This way mangoes will absorb the flavours of all the masala.
1. Now, add 2 cups of water and the tamarind paste.
1. Cover the pan and let the Sweet & Spicy Ripe Mango Curry to cook on low heat for about half an hour.
1. The flavors will seep in beautifully.
1. Switch off the flame and check the salt and spices and adjust accordingly.
1. Serve the Sweet & Spicy Ripe Mango Curry Recipe with steamed rice for a delicious lunch or dinner.
