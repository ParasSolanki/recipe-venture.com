### Ingredients

- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 2 cups Green Amaranth Leaves - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 4 tablespoons Gram flour (besan).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thotakura Undalu Recipe, add chopped amaranth leaves in a bowl add all the ingredients mentioned next.
1. Add little water and mix to form stick mixture.
1. Using your hand take little of the mixture and hold it tightly to form a ball.
1. Do the same for the rest of the mixture.
1. Heat a kuzhi paniyaram pan with oil.
1. Once it is hot place the balls in each cavity and fry them till they become crispy.
1. Take them out and drain oil over a paper napkin.
1. Heat a flat skillet with oil, Add sesame seeds and curry leaves and allow them to splutter for few seconds.
1. Add dry red chilies and hing, let them crackle for 2 seconds, add fried amaranth balls and coat the the tempering well and serve hot.
1. Serve the Thotakura Undalu Recipe along with Thakkali Rasam Recipe , steamed rice and Carrot Poriyal Recipe or just plain with Thakkali sadam.
