### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Caster Sugar.
- Saffron strands - a pinch.
- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 3 tablespoon Caster Sugar - to garnsih.
- 2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini) - to garnish.
- 1 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 3 tablespoon Water.
- 75 gram Butter - (at room temperature).
- 1 Apple - cut into to very thin slices.
- 40 gram Butter - to brush.
- 1-1/2 cup Water.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Apple Roses Recipe With Homemade Puff Pastry Sheets (Eggless), start by preparing the puff pastry dough.
1. In a bowl, add butter (at room temperature) and whisk it with a fork.
1. Mix saffron, castor sugar and cinnamon powder in a bowl and keep it ready for later use.
1. Add all-purpose flour in a Mixing bowl.
1. Add required water to make a smooth but tight dough.
1. To prepare the apple roses now, add apple slices, sprinkle lemon and water into a glass/ceramic microwave safe bowl and microwave it on medium for 4 minutes or until cooked.
1. To soften the apple slices while making the roses, cool the apple slices to room temperature.
1. Now roll the dough out to make long rectangular sheet and cut strips of 8-inch long and about less than an inch wide.
1. Apply butter and sugar cinnamon powder on it.
1. On the upper half of the stip, arrange the apple slices side by side to make half of the sheet overlapped with arranged apple slices.
1. Now fold bottom part of the sheet once.
1. Apply more butter and sugar cinnamon powder on it.
1. Now roll the sheet with arranged apple slices gently, to make a beautiful rose!Repeat the process to make other eggless apple roses as well.
1. Preheat Oven to 200 degrees Celsius for 10 minutes.
1. Meanwhile you can Repeat the process to make other eggless apple roses as well.
1. To bake, arrange the roses on a parchment paper lined Baking sheet and bake for 30-35 minutes or until the edges appear brownish and crisp.
1. Serve it these delicious Apple Roses Recipe With Homemade Puff Pastry Sheets (Eggless) with more cinnamon-sugar powder sprinkled on them, during tea time with Masala Chai or Coffee.
