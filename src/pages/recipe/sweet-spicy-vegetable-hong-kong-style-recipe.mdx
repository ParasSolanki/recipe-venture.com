### Ingredients

- 3 Dry Red Chillies - halved.
- 1-1/2 cups Vegetable stock.
- 1/4 cup Green beans (French Beans) - cut into inch pieces.
- 1/2 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - cut into chunks.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - cut into chunks.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli sauce.
- 2 inch Ginger - grated.
- 2 tablespoons Soy sauce.
- 2 tablespoons Corn flour - mixed with 1/4 cup water.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 teaspoon Chilli vinegar.
- 200 grams Tofu - cut into cubes.
- 3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - for seasoning.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - cut into chunks.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sweet and Spicy Vegetable Hong Kong Style Recipe heat the oil in a wok on high heat add the garlic, ginger, green bell peppers and the carrots and green beans.
1. Stir fry on high heat for a few minutes until you have tender-crisp vegetables.
1. Once done add the chillies, tofu and stir-fry for 2 more minutes on high heat.
1. After two minutes add the soy sauce, chilli sauce, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper and mix well.
1. Add the cornflour paste and the vegetable stock; simmer until the sauce thickens.
1. Turn off heat and serve Sweet and Spicy Vegetable Hong Kong Style hot.
1. Serve the Sweet & Spicy Vegetable Hong Kong Style Recipe along with Sichuan fried Rice for a delicious asian dinner.
