### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 3 tablespoon Coconut milk - (optional) to give a creamy texture.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 1 cup Black Eyed Beans (Lobia) - soaked for 2 hours.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mexican Style Frijoles Refritos Recipe, pressure cook the soya beans with water for about 4 whistle till it softens and is cooked.
1. Heat a sauce pan with oil, add onions and saut√© until it softens.
1. Add in the cooked soya beans along with the stock.
1. Using a potato masher, start mashing the beans to form a coarse paste.
1. Add in the coconut milk, salt and mix well.
1. Cook until it thickens and place it in a bowl and drizzle some olive oil so that it does not dry up.
1. Serve the Mexican Style Frijoles Refritos Recipe along with Nachos, Tortillas and Burritos to complete your meal.
