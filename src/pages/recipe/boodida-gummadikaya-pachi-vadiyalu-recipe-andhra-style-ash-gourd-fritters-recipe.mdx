### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Green Chillies - (adjust).
- 1-1/2 cups Vellai Poosanikai (Ash gourd/White Pumpkin) - finely chopped.
- 1 pinch Cooking soda.
- 2 cups White Urad Dal (Whole) - soaked for 4-5 hours.
- Sunflower Oil - to deep fry.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Boodida Gummadikaya Pachi Vadiyalu (Andhra Style Ash Gourd Fritters Recipe), soak the split black gram dal in water for 4-5 hours.
1. Drain the water and In mixer grinder, grind the soaked dal along with green chilies to a fine paste.
1. Ensure that you use minimal amount of water to make a thick batter.
1. Transfer the batter in a bowl and add the diced ash gourd pieces.
1. Add salt and a pinch of baking soda.
1. Mix well.
1. Heat a small pan, pour oil for frying.
1. Once the oil is heated, keep on medium flame.
1. Apply little water to your palm.
1. Now take a morsel of this batter and pat it into a round shape on the moistened palm.
1. Drop this into hot oil, slowly.
1. Take care not to touch the hot pan.
1. Fry the ‘pachi vadiyalu’ until they turn golden brown.
1. Remove from oil and drain on kitchen towel.
1. Serve these Boodida Gummadikaya Pachi Vadiyalu (Andhra Style Ash Gourd Fritters Recipe) with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney Recipe (Green Chutney) along with Masala Chai Recipe in the evening.
1. Or this fritter can also be served with hot rice and Beetroot Sambar Recipe (A Tangy Beetroot Lentil Curry).
