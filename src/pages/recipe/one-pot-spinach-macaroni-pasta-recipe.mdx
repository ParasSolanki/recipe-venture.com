### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 cups Macaroni pasta.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
- 1-1/2 cup Spinach - tightly packed.
- 3 tablespoon Butter.

### Instructions

1. To begin with One Pot Spinach Macaroni, cook macaroni as per the packet instructions in a saucepan.
1. Once cooked, drain the water from the macaroni.
1. Into the same pot add butter and mix well.
1. Now add finely chopped spinach and saut√© it along with macaroni until spinach gets cooked.
1. Add milk, salt and pepper powder and mix again.
1. Finally, add cheese stir and turn off the flame.
1. Serve One Pot Spinach Macaroni as a one dish meal for a light dinner along with Fennel Pesto Pull-Apart Bread Recipe to make a delicious meal.
