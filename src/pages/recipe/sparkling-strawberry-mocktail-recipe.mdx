### Ingredients

- Aerated water (Soda Water) - as required.
- Honey - as a sweetner.
- 15 Strawberries.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - few.
- 1/2 cup Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sparkling Strawberry Mocktail recipe, wash the strawberries well and blend along with mint leaves and lime juice using 1 cup water.
1. For this recipe you can either use lime juice or lemon juice.
1. Also try variation by using orange juice.
1. Strain it well using strainer.
1. Add some honey in a glass.
1. Pour half of the glass with prepared juice and mix well.
1. Pour the remaining half with club soda.
1. Mix well.
1. Garnish with some mint leaves and strawberry.
1. Fill a bottle with the remaining juice and refrigerate.
1. Use whenever required in the same way.
1. Serve Sparkling Strawberry Mocktail along with Paneer Broccoli Soya Ghee Roast Pockets and Salted Caramel Popcorn for yor house party.
