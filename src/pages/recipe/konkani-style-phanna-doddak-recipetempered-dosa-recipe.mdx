### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Mustard seeds.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- Sunflower Oil - for the tadka.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup White Urad Dal (Split) - soaked for 30 minutes.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 2 cups Sooji (Semolina/ Rava) - (idli rava).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Phanna Doddak Recipe, wash and soak the split urad dal for 30 minutes.
1. After half an hour add the urad dal to a mixer grinder and grind the soaked urad dal along with green chillies and ginger to a smooth paste with just enough water.
1. Take the urad dal mixture into a big bowl, add the rice semolina to this and mix well.
1. Season with salt and add water to make a thick dosa batter.
1. To prepare the Phanna Doddak, heat a skillet on medium heat.
1. Add a teaspoon of oil, mustard seeds and few curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. Pour a ladle of the prepared batter over the tadka without spreading it much and let it cook for 2 minutes.
1. Flip over and cook on the other side for another 2 minutes and remove Phanna Doddak into a serving plate.
1. Likewise, prepare the rest of the dosas.
1. Serve the Phanna Doddak Recipe along with Coconut Chutney or Tomato Gotsu Recipe (Spicy & Tangy Tomato Gravy) and hot filter coffee for the breakfast or pack for kids lunch box.
