### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Chocolate chips.
- 1/4 cup Honey.
- 2 tablespoons Flax seeds - ground.
- 1 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 1/2 cup Peanut Butter.

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Peanut Butter And Flax Oats Bar With Chocolate Chip Recipe first, line a pan with foil or parchment paper.
1. Take a mixing bowl and add the oats and ground flax into it.
1. Combine well.
1. Melt the honey and peanut butter together until it’s smooth enough to mix with the oats.
1. You could do this in the microwave for approximately 1 to 2 minutes because it’s faster]Combine the mixture with the oats and flax and mix well.
1. Allow it to cool for some time.
1. Add the chocolate chips and stir well.
1. Pour it into your prepared pan, and store it in the refrigerator/ freezer for a four hours until it’s set enough to cut into squares.
1. Once ready cut them into squares and serve them.
1. Serve the Peanut Butter And Flax Oat Squares With Chocolate Chips as a snack and munch it whenever you are hungry or serve it with some Cantaloupe Pomegranate and Mint Juice.
