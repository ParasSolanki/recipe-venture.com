### Ingredients

-  2 dry red chilli.
-  2 sprig curry leaves.
-  1 teaspoon oil.
-  4 green chillies.
-  1/2 teaspoon mustard.
- Tamarind - a little.
-  1/2 teaspoon gram lentils.
-  200 grams spinach.
-  sugar - a pinch.
-  salt - as per use.

### Instructions

1. To make puli kirai, firstly fry the spinach and wash it well.
1. Now add spinach, green chilies, salt, sugar and mix in a pressure cooker.
1. Add tamarind and some water to it.
1. Cook till 1 city comes.
1. Allow the pressure to drain and keep the spinach separately.
1. Put the spinach mixture in a blender and grind it.
1. Take out in a bowl.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add mustard seeds and let it cook for 10 seconds.
1. After that add chana dal and cook till it turns light brown.
1. Add this tempering to the spinach mixture and mix.
1. Serve Puli Kirai with Beetroot Thoran and Rice for dinner.
