### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 2 Mango (Raw) - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Coconut Oil.
- 5 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Mavinakayi Chutney Recipe, take a small pan, add oil, once the oil is hot, add mustard seeds, and allow it to splutter.
1. Immediately add fenugreek, cumin seeds, asafoetida, and saute well for half a minute.
1. Now add red chilies and saute well for 1 minute.
1. Cool this mixture.
1. Add the roasted mixture along with raw mangoes and salt in a blender or a mixer grinder and grind into coarse paste.
1. Serve the Mavinakayi Chutney Recipe as an side dish with Bele Dosa Recipe and homemade butter.
