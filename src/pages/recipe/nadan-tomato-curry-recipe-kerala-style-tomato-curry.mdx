### Ingredients

- 5 Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - halved.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 5 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Coconut Oil.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Nadan Tomato Curry recipe, heat a pan with oil and temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves.
1. Add the green chillies, tomatoes, turmeric powder, chilli powder and salt.
1. Let the tomatoes cook over low flame, till they become soft and mushy.
1. While the tomatoes cook, grind the ingredients given under 'ground mixture' along with 1/2 cup water .
1. Once the tomatoes are soft, add the ground mixture and cook over low flame for another 3 to 4 minutes and switch off.
1. Serve Nadan Tomato Curry along with Steamed Rice for light dinner or Mangalorean Neer Dosa for breakfast.
