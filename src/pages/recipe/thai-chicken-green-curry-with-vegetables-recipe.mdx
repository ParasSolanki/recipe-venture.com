### Ingredients

- 1/4 teaspoon Nutmeg - ground.
- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - roughly chopped.
- 100 grams Broccoli - cut into florets.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Palm sugar.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - diced thin.
- Salt - to taste.
- 50 ml Coconut milk.
- 1/2 Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 100 grams Baby corn - cut lengthwise.
- 1/2 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 3 Stalks Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - roughly chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - Saffola active for cooking.
- 80 grams Instant Oats (Oatmeal) - saffola veggie twist.
- 100 grams Button mushrooms - sliced.
- 500 grams Chicken - cut into 2 cm pieces.
- 3 Stalks Lemon Grass - pale section only.
- 5 cloves Garlic.
- 50 grams Green beans (French Beans) - cut into 1 inch pieces.
- 8 Basil leaves - torn.
- 2 inch Galangal - or ginger roughly chopped.
- 4 Green Chillies - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thai Chicken Green Curry With Vegetables recipe, firstly we will make the green curry paste.
1. To make the thai curry paste, in a mixer jar combine, spring onions, fresh coriander, galangal or ginger, garlic, lemongrass, coriander seeds, black pepper corns, cumin seeds, ground nutmeg and grind to a paste along with some water.
1. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan; add the freshly ground thai green curry paste and saut√© for a few seconds.
1. Add the chicken and allow it to coat the curry paste.
1. Add 1/2 cup of water, add salt and cover the pan and allow the chicken to cook completely and absorb the thai spices.
1. Once the chicken is cooked, add the coconut milk, oats and 2 cups of water.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust accordingly.
1. Add the vegetables; reduce the heat and cook stirring occasionally for about 10 minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Add the palm sugar, basil leaves and stir well into the curry.
1. Check the taste once again and adjust accordingly.
1. Serve Thai Chicken Green Curry With Vegetables for a weeknight dinner or lunch along with some hot steamed rice and an appetizer like Thai Stir Fried Green Beans Recipe to go along with it.
