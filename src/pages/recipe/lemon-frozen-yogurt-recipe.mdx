### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Sugar - powdered.
- 1/8 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoons Fresh cream.
- 1/4 cup Lemon juice.
- Orange - two wedges.
- Vanilla Extract - few drops.
- 1 teaspoon Orange Zest (Rind).
- 500 grams Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lemon Frozen Yogurt recipe, take a medium bowl and beat yogurt and turmeric powder for about 4 minutes.
1. Into the bowl of yogurt, add cream, powdered sugar, 2-3 drops of vanilla essence and squeeze out the lemon juice.
1. Pour the yogurt mixture in a freezing bowl and freeze it for 2 hours.
1. Take the frozen yogurt bowl out of the freezer and stir well, return to the freezer and keep overnight.
1. Take out of freezer 30-40 minutes before serving.
1. Serve in fancy bowl with few big scoops of Lemon Frozen Yogurt with orange zest on top and a slice of orange for garnishing.
1. Serve Lemon Frozen Yogurt on its own as a snack or serve it with your meal.
