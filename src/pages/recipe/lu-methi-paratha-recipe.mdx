### Ingredients

- 1 cup Potato (Aloo) - boiled and mashed.
- 1/2 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- Water - as required.
- Ghee - as required.
- Salt - to taste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Onion - finely chopped.
- 3 cups Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/4 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/4 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/2 tablespoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves) - chopped.
- Whole Wheat Flour - for dusting.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Aloo Methi Paratha recipe, in a bowl, mix together wheat flour, salt and oil.
1. Add water little at a time and prepare a soft dough just as we make dough for chapati.
1. Grease your hands with oil and knead it well.
1. Cover it and let it rest for some time.
1. Now prepare stuffing the stuffing for the paratha.
1. Mix mashed potatoes, chopped onions, chopped methi, finely chopped green chillies and fresh coriander properly in a bowl.
1. Now add all dry spiced in mixture including salt, red chilli powder, garam masala, coriander powder, chaat masala and mix everything well.
1. Now make even size ball out of paratha dough and roll out the ball in a small circle dusting wheat flour.
1. Add some stuffing in the centre and gather all the corners and seal it well and press it lightly.
1. Dust some wheat flour and roll out the sealed dough into a circle as we do for chapati dough.
1. Donâ€™t apply too much pressure.
1. Heat a tawa, put the rolled out paratha on it.
1. Let it cook little bit on one side.
1. Flip it over to the other side and spread some ghee over the paratha which is half cooked.
1. Flip it again, when the second side is half cooked and spread some more ghee.
1. Flip it gain, do it couple of times to get paratha browned and cooked well.
1. Now topped it with butter and serve hot.
1. Serve Aloo Methi Paratha along with Boondi Raita, Bhindi Raita or any other raita of your choice.
