### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli flakes.
- Salt - to taste.
- 10 to 12 Strawberries - chopped into huge chunks.
- 1/2 teaspoon Honey.
- 1 English Cucumber - sliced.
- 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 3 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 Onion - small sized.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Strawberry Salad Recipe, get all the ingredients handy.
1. Mix all the ingredients for dressing like the extra virgin olive oil,red chilli flakes, salt and honey in a small mixing bowl and let it sit for 5 minsToss in strawberries, cucumber and onion slices in a bowl.
1. Drizzle the dressing over it and give it a good mix.
1. Beat the summer heat with this Strawberry Salad Recipe along with some extra dressing on the side.
1. You can also serve it with some bread to go with it.
1. Serve Strawberry Salad along with Cream of Celery Soup and Whole Wheat Rosemary Focaccia Bread for a light and healthy dinner.
