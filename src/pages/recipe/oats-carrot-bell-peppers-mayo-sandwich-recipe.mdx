### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- 2 tablespoon Classic Mayonnaise (With Egg).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - chopped finely.
- 4 tablespoon Butter - for grilling.
- 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 8 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Oats Carrot Bell Peppers Mayo Sandwich Recipe, firstly mix all ingredients for the coleslaw salad including carrot, red bell pepper, instant oats, mayonnnaise, red chilli flakes, dried oregano, salt and pepper.
1. Check for seasoning according to your palate.
1. Now to grill the bread, take a tablespoon of the filling and place it over one slice bread and spread it evenly.
1. Take one more slice and cover it.
1. Heat a sandwich maker, add half a tablespoon of butter and place the sandwich and then spread half a tablespoon of butter over the bread and grill the bread till it is crispy and golden brown.
1. Serve the Oats Carrot Bell Peppers Mayo Sandwich Recipe along with Papaya Banana Smoothie Recipe and Indian Spicy Masala Cookie Recipe (Khara Biscuit) for your tea parties.
