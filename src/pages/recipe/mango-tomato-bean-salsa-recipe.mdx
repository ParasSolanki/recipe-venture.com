### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 4 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- Kosher salt - as required.
- 2 cups Mango (Ripe) - diced.
- 2 Tomato - diced after removing the seeds.
- 1 cup Rajma (Large Kidney Beans) - boiled.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mango Tomato Bean Salsa recipe, add ripe mangoes, tomatoes, rajma, green chili, mint, lemon juice, and salt as required in a large bowl.
1. Gentle mix the Mango Tomato Bean Salsa  and adjust the seasoning to suit your taste.
