### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 5 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 3 Onion - finely chopped.
- 3 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 2 cup Mutton - minced.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 2 tablespoons Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania) - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 tablespoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mutton Keema recipe, Heat oil in a wide pan and add cumin seeds, bay leaves, peppercorns and cardamoms.
1. Add the chopped onions and saute them till they turn brown in colour.
1. Add garlic and ginger paste, green chillies, tomato puree and mix everything well.
1. Let it cook for about 5 minutes on medium flame.
1. Once it is done, add coriander powder, red chilli powder, cumin powder and salt.
1. Cook for about 5-8 minutes on low flame.
1. After 5-8 minutes, add the minced mutton and salute it for about 2-3 minutes.
1. Add required water and cook till it gets dry.
1. Once the mutton is cooked, switch off the stove and add garam masala.
1. Mix well and garnish it with mint leaves.
1. In Maharashtra, this Mutton Keema is served with pav.
1. You can also serve it with Phulkas or Pakki Mutton Biryani.
