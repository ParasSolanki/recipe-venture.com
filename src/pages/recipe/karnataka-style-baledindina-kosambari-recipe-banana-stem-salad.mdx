### Ingredients

- 1 Banana Stem - skin peeled and cut into small pieces.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 Lemon.

### Instructions

1. To begin preparing the Baledindina Kosambari Recipe soak the dal for an hour and then drain the water.
1. Finely chop the vegetables and add it to the dal in a mixing bowl.
1. Add freshly grated coconut, coriander leaves, salt and lemon juice and mix it well.
1. The next step is tempering.
1. For tempering, heat the oil in a pan and add mustard seeds.
1. Once it crackles, add asafoetida and curry leaves and let it crisp up.
1. Add it to the kosambari and mix everything properly.
1. Serve the Karnataka Style Baledindina Kosambari Recipe along with Chitranna or steamed rice and Karnataka Style Sorakkai Palya Recipe.
