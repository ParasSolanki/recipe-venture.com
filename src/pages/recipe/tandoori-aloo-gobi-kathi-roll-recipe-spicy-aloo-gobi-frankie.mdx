### Ingredients

- 1 Cauliflower (gobi) - cut into florets.
- Salt.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Lemon - juice extracted.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 2 cups Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 12 Baby Potatoes.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Aloo Gobi Kathi Roll, we have to first cook the aloo and gobi.
1. Into a pressure cooker, add the potatoes along with 1/2 cup of water and pressure cook for 3 to 4 whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Peel the skin of the potatoes and keep aside.
1. If you have a steamer, steam the cauliflower florets on high heat for 3 to 4 minutes until just about cooked, but its still firm.
1. You can also pan roast the cauliflower with a little salt until it has lightly cooked.
1. In a bowl add curd 1/2 cup yogurt along with chaat masala, red chilli powder, turmeric powder and salt.
1. Add the baby potatoes and cauliflower florets into the curd mixture and coat the aloo gobi evenly using a spoon.
1. Check for seasoning and leave aside for 15 minutes to marinate.
1.  Now insert the baby potatoes and cauliflowers into a sticks or skewers for roasting on a skillet.
1. Place a skillet on medium heat and add a teaspoon of oil.
1. Add cumin seeds and let them crackle.
1. Place the skewered aloo gobi on the skillet and roast it until you see a few brown spots on the vegetables and see it getting roasted.
1. Roast all the skewers on a low to medium heat and keep aside.
1. *Making the paratha for the Kati RollsIn a bowl mix whole wheat flour, salt and one teaspoon of oil and mix well.
1. Add little water at a time and bring the flour together to form a smooth dough.
1. Cover with a muslin cloth and keep aside for 10 minutes.
1. Make equal portions of the dough and roll it into balls.
1. On a clean surface dust some flour and flatten the ball of dough on it.
1. Roll out the dough into a paratha.
1. Place a roti tawa on medium heat and cook all the parathas with ghee or oil.
1. Keep the cooked parathas aside.
1. In a medium bowl, mix together sliced onions and capsicum slices along with lemon juice, salt and green chillies.
1. *Assembling the Kathi RollsPlace a paratha on foil paper.
1. Place a small portion of the onion and capsicum slices.
1. Next place a skewer of roasted tandoori aloo gobi in the centre.
1. Pull the skewer out and discard.
1. Roll the parathas along with the foil and prepare the rest of the Tandoori Aloo Gobi Kathi rolls the similar way.
1. Serve Tandoori Aloo Gobi Kathi Rolls along with a Dhaniya Pudina Chutney and a cup of Masala Chai for a tea time meal or pack these Aloo Gobi Kathi Rolls for lunch along with a cup of curd.
