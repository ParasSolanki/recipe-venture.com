### Ingredients

- Water - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 teaspoon Rose water.
- 2 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/4 teaspoon Nutmeg powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Saffron strands - soaked in milk for 10 minutes.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 3 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 1-1/2 inch Ginger - julienne.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 teaspoons Garam masala powder.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- 4 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - crushed.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - crushed.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Onion - sauteed and ground.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1 kg Mutton - (I used shanks).
- 1/4 cup Water.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Awadhi Khaas Nihari Recipe, firstly slowly add water into the whole wheat flour and make a tight dough.
1. This dough is to seal the vessel.
1. Once it is ready, keep it aside.
1. Heat oil in a wok/ pan and add whole spices (green cardamom, cinnamon, cloves, black cardamom and bay leaves.
1. Now add the meat and saute for 6-7 minutes.
1. Add salt and turmeric and mix well.
1. Pour in the water, cover the pan and cook.
1. When it comes to a boil, immediately add ginger- garlic paste, coriander powder, red chilli and onion paste.
1. Now add nicely whisked yogurt, rose water , garam masala, nutmeg and cinnamon powder and saffron soaked milk/water.
1. Cover the lid and cook for 2-3 minutes.
1. Now transfer the prepared meat mix in a broad base heavy bottom vessel/ handi.
1. Cover the with a lid and seal it with the wheat dough and leave to cook on slow fire.
1. This may take 1.
1. 5- 2 hours.
1. Once the meat is cooked till tender, garnish with fresh coriander and ginger juliennes.
1. Serve Awadhi Khaas Nihari Recipe hot with roomali roti or butter garlic naan and rice.
