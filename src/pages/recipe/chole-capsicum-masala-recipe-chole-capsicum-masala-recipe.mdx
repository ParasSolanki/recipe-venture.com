### Ingredients

-  1 green capsicum - chopped.
-  1 teaspoon Kasuri methi.
-  1 cup tomato puree.
-  oil - to cook.
-  1 teaspoon cumin powder .
-  2 teaspoons ginger garlic paste.
-  2 teaspoons coriander powder.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  1 teaspoon red chili powder.
- 1 cup Kabuli Channa - soaked boiled.
-  1/2 cup cashew nuts.
-  2 onions - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make chickpea capsicum, soak cashew nuts in water for 12 to 15 minutes.
1. Heat the oil in a skillet, cook the onion till it turns brown.
1. Add ginger garlic paste and cook 1 minute.
1. Add capsicum, and roast for 2-3 minutes.
1. Now add cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric and red chili powder and fry.
1. Keep in mind that spices should not be burnt.
1. Add tomato puree and boiled gram with 2 cups of water.
1. Let it boil for 5 minutes and cook till the spices are mixed.
1. Grind the soaked cashews.
1. Add cashew paste to the curry with salt and cook for 4-5 minutes.
1. Add kasuri methi and close the gas.
1. Serve Chole Shimlamirch Masala with Phulka, Rice and Salad.
