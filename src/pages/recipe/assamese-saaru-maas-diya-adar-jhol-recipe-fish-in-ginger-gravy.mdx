### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies - slit and cut into half.
- 1-1/2 cups Water.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - (optional).
- Salt - as needed.
- Mustard oil - for shallow frying and cooking.
- 10 Fish - small.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 inch Ginger - peeled and grated.
- 1 teaspoon Panch Phoran Masala.
- 1 teaspoon Corn flour - mixed in 1 tablespoon water.

### Instructions

1. To gein making the Assamese Saaru Maas Diya Adar Jhol Recipe (Fish In Ginger Gravy), get prepped with all the ingredients.
1. Heat mustard oil in a pan for shallow frying and meanwhile, rub the fish with 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder and salt to taste such that everything incorporates well.
1. When the oil is hot, shallow fry the fishes and collect them in a plate and keep aside on a plate/bowl.
1. Add a half to one teaspoon mustard oil if required and heat.
1. Once the oil is hot, add bay leaf, red chili, panch phoron and wait till they crackle.
1. Add grated/pounded or minced ginger along with green chilies, turmeric powder and saute till you start getting a nice aroma.
1. Once the ginger is nicely sauteed, add cornflour and water mix and season with salt as needed.
1. Let the curry come to a boil and keep stirring to avoid lumps.
1. Now simmer the gas and add fried fishes into the curry and let them cook for a minute or more.
1. The curry should be really light so add water to make it thin.
1. Once the curry is done, serve Assamese Saaru Maas Diya Adar Jhol Recipe (Fish In Ginger Gravy) along with Steamed Rice and any Salad of your choice for your weekday meal.
1. You can also make Rava Fried Prawns as a side dish with this meal.
