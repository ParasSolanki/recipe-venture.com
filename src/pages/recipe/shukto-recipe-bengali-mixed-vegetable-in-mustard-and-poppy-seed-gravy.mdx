### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 Raw Banana - peeled and diced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Panch Phoran Masala.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - peeled and diced.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 1 Ridge Gourd (Turai/ Peerkangai) - peeled and diced.
- 2 teaspoons Mustard oil.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - peeled and diced.
- 2 teaspoons Poppy seeds.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Karela (Bitter Gourd/ Pavakkai) - small diced.
- 1 Drumstick - cut into 2 inch pieces.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies - broken.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Shukto Recipe, first prepare all the vegetables and the ingredients and keep aside.
1. Place all the vegetables including karela, raw banana, carrot, potato, ridge gourd, drumstick in the pressure cooker, sprinkle a little salt, 1/4 cup of water and cook for 3 to 4 whitles.
1. After 3 to 4 whistles, turn off the heat and let the pressure release immediatlely by placing the cooker under running cold water.
1. Open the lid and keep aside.
1. Make a paste of 1 teaspoon of mustard seeds and 2 teaspoon of poppy seeds along with 1 ot 2 tablespoons of water.
1. Heat mustard oil in a pan, add the ginger, bay leaf, green chillies, red chillies and stir fry for a few seconds.
1. Next, add the turmeric powder, the poppy seed and mustard seed paste, the cooked vegetables, milk, salt and sugar.
1. Stir well, add 1/4 cup of water and give the Shukto mixture a brisk boil.
1. The Shukto will thicken as it is boiling and you can adjust the consistency by adding water or milk.
1. Check the taste and adjust the salt accordingly.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and serve immediately.
1. Serve the Shukto along with Puri, Cholar Dal and Begun Bhaja for a delightful meal.
