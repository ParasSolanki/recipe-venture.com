### Ingredients

- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 tablespoon Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal) - (soak for 20 minutes).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Rajma (Large Kidney Beans).
- 2 tablespoons Black Urad Dal (Whole).
- 1/4 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split) - (soak for 20 minutes).
- 2 tablespoons Pink Masoor Dal (Split).
- 2 teaspoons Garam masala powder.
- 1 tablespoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- 2 tablespoons White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- 2 tablespoons Masoor Dal (Whole).
- 2 teaspoons Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 inch Ginger - cut to julienned for garnish.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 2 tablespoons Green Moong Dal (Whole).
- 2 tablespoons Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas).
- 2 tablespoons Ghee.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Navrang Dal (spicy Mixed Lentils) Recipe soak all the dals except split moong and toor dal overnight.
1. In the morning pressure cook everything together till it's cooked.
1. Mash the cooked dal gently and keep it aside.
1. Heat a big wok on medium flame, pour oil or ghee, a stick of cinnamon and cumin seeds and let it splutter.
1. To this add the onions and saut√© till it turns light brown.
1. Add the ginger garlic paste and cook until the raw smell of the paste goes away.
1. Now add the chopped tomatoes and slit green chilli and cook until the tomatoes are half done.
1. Add the coriander, red chilli, and garam masala powder and give it a mix and cook until the ghee or oil separates out.
1. Now add the cooked mashed dal to this, season with salt, add the required amount of water and let it simmer for ten minutes.
1. Now gently crush the kasuri methi between your palms and put it over the dal.
1. Bring it to a boil, switch off the flame and garnish with the ginger julienned.
1. Your Navang Dal is ready to be served, serve it with Laccha Paratha or Peas Pulao for a weeknight dinner.
