### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 tablespoons Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- 400 grams Green beans (French Beans) - cut into 1 inch pieces.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon SSP Asafoetida (Hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1/2 teaspoon Enos fruit salt.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Fansi Muthia Nu Shaak Recipe, add the green beans into a pressure cooker, with 1/4 cup of water, salt to taste and pressure cook for 2 whistles.
1. Turn off the heat and release the pressure immediately to retain the bright green color of the beans.
1. Open the pressure cooker and keep aside.
1. *To make the Muthia:In a small bowl combine the ingredients for the muthia - Gram flour, kasuri methi, turmeric powder, sugar, asafoetida, enos fruit salt, lemon juice, cooking oil and salt.
1. Combine to make a firm dough by adding little water.
1. Divide the dough into equal small portions.
1. Shape them in ovals and lightly press them down.
1. Preheat a paniyaram pan and add little oil into each cavity.
1. Place the muthia in each cavity and pan fry until golden and crisp on all sides.
1. Once done remove and keep aside.
1. *The next step is to make the Fansi Muthia Nu Shaak:Heat a teaspoon of oil in a heavy bottomed pan over medium heat; add the ajwain seeds,  mustard seeds and allow them to crackle.
1. After the seeds crackle, add asafoetida and the cooked green beans.
1. After a minute, add the coriander powder, red chilli powder, amchur powder, turmeric powder, jaggery, muthia and saute until well combine.
1. Cover and allow it to steam cook for two minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer Fansi Muthia Nu Shaak into a serving bowl and serve.
1. Serve the Fansi Muthia Nu Shaak Recipe along with Gujarati Kadhi, Phulka and Steamed Rice to enjoy your everyday simple lunch.
