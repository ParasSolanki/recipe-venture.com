### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- Salt - to taste.
- 80 grams Rolled Oats Or Instant Oats - powdered.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - few.
- 2 cloves Garlic - grated.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- Red Chilli powder - to taste.
- 250 grams Gram flour (besan).
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf) - pounded.
- 2 Onions - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Oats Besan Cheela Recipe, combine all the ingredients for the Besan Chilla in a large bowl.
1. Gradually add water to make a thick batter.
1. Check the salt and spice levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. The cheela batter will be similar to the consistency of a dosa batter or pancake batter.
1. The batter should be able coat the back of the spoon.
1. The next step is to cook the Oats Besan Cheela in a panPreheat a skillet on medium heat, pour a ladleful of batter on the pan and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin crepe/ dosa.
1. Drizzle some oil around the cheela and cook it until the top does not look raw and you notice the edges are getting brown.
1. Flip and cook on the other side for a few seconds.
1. Once done, transfer to a plate and proceed to make the chillas with the remaining batter.
1. Serve the Oats Besan Cheela along with a Sweet And Spicy Tomato Chutney and a Dhaniya Pudina Chutney for breakfast, a tea time snack or even weeknight dinner.
