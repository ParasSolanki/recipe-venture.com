### Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons Dessicated Coconut.
- 1-1/2 cups Milk.
- 1-1/2 cups Water.
- 4 tablespoons Ghee.
- Mixed dry fruits - as per preference.
- 1/3 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 1/2 cup Sugar - (or rock candy sugar).
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1/2 cup Rice.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chakkra Pongali Recipe (Andhra Style Sweet Pongal), fry the rice & moong dal together in hot ghee until they become aromatic & turn slightly brownish.
1. Add them in a pressure cooker, add the milk, water & cook till  idone (say 3 whistles mostly)Meanwhile heat a kadai with a bit of ghee, and fry the dry fruits till done and keep aside.
1. Also fry the desiccated coconut in ghee in the same kadai for 4-5 minutes until it changes colour.
1. Once the rice mix cools down, keep it on stovetop on medium heat & add the sugar, cardamom powder & keep stirring it continuously.
1. You can even add a clove & a bit of crushed edible camphor for additional flavour.
1. The sugar should melt & the porridge should start thickening in approx 8-10 minutes on medium heat.
1. Once the pongali has thickened, switch off the stove & add the dry fruits & toasted coconut.
1. Serve Chakkra Pongali Recipe (Andhra Style Sweet Pongal) as a dessert along with a meal of Steamed rice, Pappu Charu Recipe (Lentil Soup Style Curry), Nalla Karam Podi Recipe, Beetroot Vepudu Recipe and an Elai Vadam Recipe for ann Andhra inspired meal.
