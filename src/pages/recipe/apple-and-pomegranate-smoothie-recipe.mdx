### Ingredients

- Ice cubes - (optional).
- 2 cups Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 cup Anardana Powder (Pomegranate Seed Powder).
- Honey - to taste.
- 1 Apple - peeled and diced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Apple and Pomegranate Smoothie Recipe, get prep with all the ingredients first.
1. Place all the ingredients in the mixer grinder and grind it to a smooth puree.
1. Add water to adjust the consistency of the smoothie and adjust the sweetness by adding honey if you would prefer it any more sweeter than the natural taste of combined fruitsOnce the Apple and Pomegranate Smoothie is well blended, pour it out in tall glasses immediately as a wholesome drink.
1. Serve the Apple and Pomegranate Smoothie Recipe along with a high protein Breakfast like High Protein Zucchini & Corn Patty Recipe (Fritters) and Paneer Capsicum Bhurji Recipe (Spicy Cottage Cheese Crumble).
