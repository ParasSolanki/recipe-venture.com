### Ingredients

- 250 grams Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra) - washed.
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - roasted.
- Salt - to taste.
- Curry leaves - few sprigs.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Tamarind - big lemon size.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon White Urad Dal (Split).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Karnataka Bendekayi Gojju/Kayirasa Recipe first take a pan and keep on heat.
1. On medium flame dry roast the chana dal and urad dal until golden brown.
1. Add the dry red chillies to the pan and saute till crisp.
1. Cool and keep aside.
1. Grind the above roasted dal with coconut, tamarind, jaggery in a mixer grinder to a smooth paste and keep it aside.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottom pan over medium heat; add mustard seeds and allow it splutter.
1. Add the dry red chillies and curry leaves and give it a stir for a few seconds.
1. Now add the chopped Bendekayi/ Bhindi, sprinkle a little salt and roast until the Bendekayi turns golden brown.
1. Cover the pan with the lid slightly ajar.
1. We keep the lid ajar to prevent the lady finger from getting slimy.
1. Once the Bendekayi soften and is cooked through; stir in the ground Gojju masala, about 2 cups of of water and allow it to simmer on medium flame for about 5 minutes.
1. Check the salt and spices of the Bendekayi Gojju and adjust to taste.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and transfer the Bendekai Gojju/Kayirasa Recipe to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Karnataka Style Bendekayi Gojju/Kayirasa with Steamed Rice, Vazhakkai Varuval  and Elai Vadam for a comforting lunch.
