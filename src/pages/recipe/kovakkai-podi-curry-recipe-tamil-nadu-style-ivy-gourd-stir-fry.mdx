### Ingredients

- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 400 grams Tindora (Dondakaya/ Kovakkai) - thinly sliced vertically.
- 1/2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 tablespoon White Urad Dal (Split).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kovakkai Podi Curry Recipe, dry roast the ingredients mentioned in the spicy lentil mix table and grind it to a coarse mix using a hand blender or Mixer and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a Kadai on medium flame, add the mustard seeds, urad dal and let it splutter.
1. Add the asafoetida, dry red chilli and curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. Now add the sliced ivy gourds and give it a good stir.
1. Sprinkle with a little water, mix and let it cook with the lid closed.
1. This will take around 10 minutes.
1. Once the ivy gourds are cooked yet firm, add the lentil spice mix and stir well.
1. Let the ivy gourds roast on low flame for another 10 minutes, and occasionally keep stirring the mix in between.
1. Switch off the flame once it is done.
1. Serve the spicy Kovakkai Podi Curry Recipe along with Steamed Rice, Tomato Onion sambar, Pepper Rasam and Curd for a full course Tamil Nadu Style lunch.
