### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/8 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1-2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - boiled and mashed.
- 2 teaspoons Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 6 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - washed and finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Gram flour (besan).
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Hari Dhania Ki Sabzi ( Fresh Coriander leaves Dry Curry) firstheat a medium sized pan with 2 teaspoon of sesame oil and temper the mustard seeds, green chillies, cumin seeds and asafoetida.
1. Add the gram/besan flour, turmeric powder and roast it over low heat for 5 minutes till it turns fragrant.
1. Add the chopped coriander leaves and potatoes, salt for taste , then cover and cook for another 4-5 minutes.
1. Sprinkle a little water while cooking so the besan and the potatoes get cooked and get a crumbly texture.
1. Once done, check the taste and adjust the salt and spices accordingly.
1. A very simple and refreshing Hari Dhania Ki sabzi is ready to be served.
1. Hari Dhania Ki Sabzi ( Fresh Coriander leaves Dry Curry) can be served with Phulka, or steamed rice for a weekday meal.
