### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Red cabbage - shredded.
- 1/3 cup Onions - thinly sliced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Iceberg lettuce - shredded.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 1/3 cup Tomatoes - deseeded and thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Minty Summer Salad Recipe With Red Cabbage Lettuce Onions And Tomatoes, cut the vegetables lengthwise and set aside.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the shredded red cabbage, lettuce, thinly sliced onions and tomatoes and mix well.
1. The salad tastes best when it is served chilled.
1. Chill the vegetables in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
1. Just before serving season the salad with some salt, honey lemon juice.
1. Toss well and serve.
1. Serve Minty Summer Salad Recipe With Red Cabbage Lettuce Onions And Tomatoes along with Beetroot Gazpacho Soup Recipe and Roasted Vegetable Panini Sandwich With Feta Cheese Recipe for a refreshing summer meal.
1. Finish the with a Strawberry Banana Sorbet Recipe.
