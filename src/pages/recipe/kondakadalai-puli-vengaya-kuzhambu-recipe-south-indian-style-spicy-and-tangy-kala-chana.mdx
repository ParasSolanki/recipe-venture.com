### Ingredients

- 1 sprig Curry leaves - roughly torn.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Sambar Powder.
- Sesame (Gingelly) Oil - for cooking.
- 12 Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - halved.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1/2 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Kala Chana (Brown Chickpeas).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kondakadalai Puli Vengaya Kuzhambu Recipe, first soak the kala chan (kondakadalai) in water for at least 8 hours.
1. Cook the Kondakadalai with salt and water in the Pressure cooker for about 7 to 8 whistles.
1. Turn the heat to low and simmer for another 15 minutes.
1. Ensure that the kondakadalai is cooked well.
1. Once done keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan, add the mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds and allow them to crackle.
1. Add the onions, curry leaves and roast the onions until tender.
1. Add the tamarind water, tomato puree, jaggery, sambar powder, turmeric powder, cooked kondakadalai and salt to taste.
1. Simmer the kondakadalai Kuzhambu for 10 to 15 minutes until the masala get well incorporated into the kondakadalai and the raw taste from the tamarind goes away.
1. Once done, turn off the heat, check the salt and spices and adjust the taste accordingly.
1. Serve the Kondakadalai Puli Vengaya Kuzhambu along with Beetroot Poriyal and hot steamed rice for lunch or dinner.
