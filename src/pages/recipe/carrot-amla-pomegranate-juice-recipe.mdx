### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Fresh Pomegranate Fruit Kernels.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - diced.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 2 Amla (Nellikai/ Gooseberry) - deseeded and chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Carrot Amla Pomegranate Juice Recipe, simply blend all the ingredients into a blender until you get a smooth mixture of the juice.
1. You will find the juice has a lot of texture from the pomegranates and the fibre from the carrots.
1. Serve it as it is to get the maximum benefits from all three.
1. Serve the Carrot Amla Pomegranate Juice for breakfast or even as a snack during the day.
1. This can be given to kids as well.
