### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 8 cloves Garlic.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 Onions.
- 6 Green Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 3/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- Mustard oil - for deep frying the fish and 2 tablespoon for the curry.
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 inch Ginger - paste.
- 2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 500 grams Bhetki fish - (asian sea bass).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Doi Bhetki Recipe, clean and wash fish pieces well.
1. Add 1 teaspoon turmeric powder, 1 teaspoon red chili powder and salt to taste.
1. Rub this spice mix well on both sides of the fish.
1. Cover and marinate for 30 minutes.
1. Grind onions, green chilies, ginger, and garlic into smooth paste adding little water using a mixer grinder.
1. Heat oil in a wide pan for deep frying.
1. Fry few pieces of fish at a time on medium flame on both sides.
1. Remove the fish from the pan and drain in on paper towel.
1. Now, add 2 tablespoon of the same oil in which fish was fried into a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add cloves, cardamom and bay leaf.
1. Add the onion-chilli-ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell of the paste is gone.
1. It will take about 10 to 15 minutes.
1. Now add all the spice powders including turmeric powder, cumin powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, black pepper powder into the pan and cook for about 3 to 5 minutes.
1. Add 1 cup water, mix well and bring it to boil.
1. Now add whisked yogurt, salt to taste and mix again.
1. Bring it to boil on high flame.
1. Simmer and cook for 10 minutes.
1. Add fried fish into the curry and cook for another 5 minutes.
1. Turn off flame and serve hot.
1. Serve Doi Bhetki Recipe along with hot Steamed Rice and Begun Bhaja for a perfect and satisfying weekday meal.
