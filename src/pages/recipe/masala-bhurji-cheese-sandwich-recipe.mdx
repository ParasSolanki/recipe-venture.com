### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 2 Cheddar cheese - sliced.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread - slices.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - or butter as required.
- 2 Whole Eggs.
- 3 sprigs Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Masala Bhurji Cheese Sandwich, firstly we will make the bhurji to stuff in the sandwiches.
1. Heat oil in a pan/wok and add the finely chopped onions and green chillies, cook till it turns soft and translucent.
1. Now add the tomatoes, salt, black pepper powder and coriander leaves.
1. On the other side break the eggs and beat them.
1. After a minute, add the beaten eggs and stir it continuously, don't overcook the eggs.
1. Switch off the stove and keep the bhurji aside.
1. Now in a  grill pan, add butter.
1. Put two slices of bread and place the cheese slices on it.
1. Add the bhurji mixture and cover it with another bread slice.
1. Grill the sandwich till it becomes brown and crisp.
1. Serve the Masala Bhurji Cheese Recipe for breakfast with a glass of Mulled Apple Juice or Muskmelon (Kharbuj) Juice Recipe for a healthy breakfast.
