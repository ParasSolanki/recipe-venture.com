### Ingredients

- 500 grams Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra) - half inch slice.
- 2 inch Ginger.
- 3 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- Salt - to taste.
- 3/4 Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - beaten till smooth.
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Dahi Ma Bheeda Recipe (Dahi Bhindi - Parsi Style Okras Cooked In Yogurt Recipe) by washing the okra and wipe them thoroughly, cut the okra into half-inch slice and sprinkle some salt and keep it aside.
1. Heat oil in a non-stick pan, add the cut okras and shallow fry them till it is crispy.
1. Once done remove them.
1. In the same pan add the ginger, garlic and saut√© for few minutes.
1. Then add the spices and fry till the raw smell of the spice goes away.
1. Lower the heat and gradually add the beaten curd to the pan, mix well.
1. Later add the crispy okras and simmer it for 2 minutes.
1. Serve the hot the Dahi Ma Bheeda Recipe (Dahi Bhindi - Parsi Style Okras Cooked In Yogurt Recipe) with steam rice or Khichdi Kadhi and Batata Nu Shaak ( The Classic Comfort Food of India).
