### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Dark Chocolate Chips.
- 1/4 cup Butter - melted.
- 2 tablespoons Caster Sugar - for garnish.
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 Whole Egg.
- 2 tablespoons Butter - to grease the pan.
- 1 pinch Cayenne Pepper - powdered.
- 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/4 cup Cocoa Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sea salt.
- 1/4 cup Sugar.
- 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Æbleskivers Recipe, take a big bowl add the melted butter and sugar and whisk till the sugar is melted.
1. Break the egg and whisk well.
1. Add the all purpose flour, oatmeal flour, unsweetened cocao, cinnamon, sea salt, cayenne pepper and mix well.
1. Add the milk and whisk it to a pourable pancake mix consistency.
1. Add in the vanilla extract, dark chocolate chips and fold in gently.
1. Now, heat the paniyaram pan, brush it with little butter on each cavity.
1. Pour a spoon full till half on each cavity and cook for 5 minutes.
1. Once the bottom is cooked, slowly flip over and cook the other side for another 5 minutes and remove the Æbleskivers and keep aside.
1. Likewise, prepare the rest of the Æbleskivers.
1. Once the Æbleskivers are slightly cold, dust the powdered sugar over it and serve.
1. Serve the Æbleskivers Recipe-Danish Chocolate Pancake Puffs along with a glass of hot milk or a Chikoo Banana Date Smoothie as an after school snack for the kids or as a light dessert at your next home party to surprise your guests.
