### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Jaggery.
- 1 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 2 cups Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra).
- 1 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Sunflower Oil - as needed.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mangalorean Style Bendekayi Huli Koddel Recipe - Ladies finger Dal without Coconut Recipe boil the dal in a pressure cooker with water 1 cup water, green chilli and turmeric powder for about 3 whistle.
1. Release the pressure naturally and keep it aside.
1. In the mean while dry roast the ingredients that are mentioned in the sambar powder list.
1. Into a preheated pan first add the urad dal, saute the dal on medium heat till it turn light brown in colour.
1. Once done add the mustard seeds, cumin seeds, cinnamon stick, dry red chillies, coriander seeds and asafoetida.
1. Roast till the aromas come out.
1. Turn off the heatOnce done cool it down and grind it into a coarse mixture and keep it aside.
1. Heat a shallow frying Skillet, add tamarind water, jaggery and ladies finger and boil till the stickiness of the vegetable goes away.
1. Add the cooked dal and ground spice mixture.
1. Bring it to a boil.
1. Check for salt and adjust.
1. For the tempering, heat a small tempering kadai and splutter the mustard seeds,cumin seeds, urad dal, dry red chilli, hing and curry leaf.
1. Pour it over the Mangalorean Style Bendekayi Huli Koddel and add the chopped coriander leaves for garnish.
1. Serve the Mangalorean Style Bendekayi Huli Koddel Recipe along with steam rice or Phulka for lunch or dinner.
