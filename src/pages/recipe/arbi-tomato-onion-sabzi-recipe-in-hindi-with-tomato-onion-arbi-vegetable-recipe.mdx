### Ingredients

- 12 arbi - finely chopped.
-  3/4 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  oil - as per use.
-  asafetida - a pinch.
-  1 onion - chopped.
-  3/4 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  2 tomatoes - chopped.
-  3/4 teaspoon Red chili powder.
-  2 dry red chillies.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  3/4 teaspoon garam masala powder.
-  1 teaspoon ginger garlic paste.

### Instructions

1. To make tomato onion curry arabi vegetable, first wash and soak the arbi in salted water for 10 to 15 minutes.
1. Drain and wash again.
1. Dry it Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add arbi to it and cook till it becomes golden.
1. Remove it on a paper towel and keep it aside.
1. Heat a little more oil in the same pan.
1. Add dry red chillies, asafetida, ginger, garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
1. Now add onion and cook till the onion becomes soft.
1. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt, coriander powder and garam masala powder.
1. After 1 minute add tomatoes and green chilies and cook it for 3 minutes.
1. Now add arbi to it and mix well.
1. Cover the pan and cook it for 6 to 8 minutes.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve the tomato onion arbi vegetable along with boondi raita and phulka for dinner.
