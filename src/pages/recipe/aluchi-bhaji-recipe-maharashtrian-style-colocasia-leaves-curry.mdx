### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 teaspoons Jaggery - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - soaked in hot water for 30 minutes.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1/4 cup Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - soaked in hot water for 30 minutes.
- 7 Dry coconut (kopra) - thinly sliced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 50 grams Colocasia Leaves (Arbi) - leaves along with tender stemscleaned and finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Aloochi Bhaji recipe, get all the ingredients ready and keep them aside.
1. Soak the channa dal in water for about 1 hour.
1. Add the chana dal and peanut along with a pinch of salt and 1/4 cup of water in the pressure cooker and cook until you hear 3 to 4 whistles.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. Keep aside.
1. Add the mustard seeds and once they splutter add the cumin seeds.
1. Add hing, turmeric powder, green chillies and the chopped aluchi leaves and stems, amchur.
1. jaggery, salt, coconut pieces and mix well.
1. Saute until the colocasia leaves soften and get cooked well.
1. Once cooked, add the cooked chana dal and the peanuts and give it a brisk boil.
1. Once done, check the salt and adjust to taste and turn off the heat.
1. Serve Aluchi Bhaji along with Phulka, Matki Chi Usal and Kanda Kairichi Chutney for a delicious sunday lunch.
