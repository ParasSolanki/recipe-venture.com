### Ingredients

- 2 Mango (Ripe) - peeled and cut.
- 1 cup Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Saffron strands.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Raw Mango Murabba Recipe, peel the mangoes and cut the in cubes and set aside.
1. In a saucepan, add the sugar & 1/2 cup water and make a thin syrup.
1. Once the syrup starts boiling, add the mango cubes, add the saffron strands & cinnamon powder, simmer on low flame, until the mango pieces are cooked.
1. This will take 5 minutes more.
1. Remove the Raw Mango Murabba from the flame & let the murabba cool.
1. Once cooled, store the Raw Mango Murabba Recipe in a glass jar or steel jar.
1. The Raw Mango Murabba Recipe can be stored for more than 6 months in the fridge & can be enjoyed with hot Spicy Yogurt Chapati Recipe or Whole Wheat Bread Recipe With Oatmeal And Flaxseeds.
