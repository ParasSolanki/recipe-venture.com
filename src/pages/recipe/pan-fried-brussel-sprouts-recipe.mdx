### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 300 grams Brussel sprouts.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garlic salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mixed Herbs (Dried).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Pan Fried Brussels Sprouts Recipe, clean the brussels sprouts and cut them into halves and keep aside.
1. Heat olive oil in a skillet, add the finely chopped garlic cloves and saute until the oil becomes fragrant.
1. Add in the brussels sprouts and let it char on one side, this will take around 5 minutes.
1. Now, flip over and let the other side char the similar way.
1. Add in the pepper powder, mixed herbs, season with garlic salt and mix well.
1. Saute for another 5 minutes on medium heat and switch off the flame.
1. Serve the Pan Fried Brussels Sprouts Recipe for a light dinner along with a hearty Tomato Noodle soup.
