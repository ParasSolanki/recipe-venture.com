### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 4 teaspoons Ghee.
- 4 teaspoons Gingelly oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Thonnai (dried leaves).
- 1/4 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- Cashew nuts - Few.
- 1 cup White Urad Dal (Whole).
- 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 tablespoons Sabudana (Tapioca Pearls).
- 1 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 3 cups Idli Rice.
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thonnai Idli Recipe recipe, soak the items mentioned under ‘For the Idli Batter’ for 6 to 8 hours except salt.
1. Grind them slightly coarse than regular idli batter in a mixer grinder.
1. Take a mix and grind cumin seeds & pepper into a coarse consistency.
1. Take a tadka pan, add ghee and gingelly oil.
1. Once heated add hing and mustard and let it splutter.
1. Add urad dal, bengal gram, ginger, cashew, cumin & pepper powder and curry leaves and let it get cooked.
1. Mix it with the idli batter.
1. Let the idli batter ferment for 6 to 8 hours or overnight.
1. Make sure the thonnai is cleaned with a wet cloth.
1. No oil needed to be applied.
1. Once fermented, pour it into the 3/4th of Thonnai.
1. Arrange them in a pan which can fit into pressure cooker or idly pan.
1. Make sure the idlis are tightly arranged one next to each other.
1. If idlis are loosely arranged the thonnai will open up easily.
1. Steam the idlis for 20 minutes, take out and let it cool for just 5 minutes.
1. Pack Thonnai Idli in your lunch box along with South Indian Coconut Chutney or Tomato Onion Chutney.
1. You can also have it for a tasty and healthy breakfast.
