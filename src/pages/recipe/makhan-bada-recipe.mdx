### Ingredients

- 1/4 teaspoons Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 2 tablespoons Pistachios - chopped.
- Baking powder - a pinch.
- 1 tablespoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - Yogurt – 1 tablespoon.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 1 cup Sugar.
- 1/6 cup Ghee - for dough.
- 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- Ghee - to deep fry (as required).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Makhan Bada recipe, In a bowl, add maida, ghee, baking powder and mix it with your hands.
1. Now add yogurt and make a dough like poori dough.
1. Don’t add water and leave it for 10-15 minutes.
1. Heat pan, add sugar, green cardamom powder and water and make one thread sugar syrup.
1. Now divide the dough in small equal portions (i.
1. e.
1. size of small lemon).
1. Round them, press each ball from the center with the help of finger.
1. Heat ghee in a kadhai and deep fry the ball until golden brown and crisp.
1. Drain on an absorbent paper.
1. Soak badas in sugar syrup for 30 minutes.
1. Take them out in a serving plate.
1. Garnish with the chopped pistachios.
1. Serve Makhan Bada as a dessert to your family after your festive meal of Aloo Gobhi Ki Sabzi, Boondi Raita and Puri.
