### Ingredients

-  1/4 cup green onion.
-  4 bud garlic.
-  2 tsp ginger teriyaki sauce.
-  1 tsp pepper powder.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 inch ginger.
- 1-1 / 2 cup rice.
-  1 / 2 tsp Lemon juice.
-  1 tbsp sesame oil.

### Instructions

1. To make Ginger Teriyaki Rice Recipe, first heat sesame oil in a pan.
1. Now add ginger, garlic, green onions and cook for 1 minute.
1. After 1 minute, add salt, black pepper powder, ginger teriyaki sauce and mix well.
1. After 1 minute add rice to it and mix again.
1. Turn off the gas after 2 minutes and drain it in a serving bowl.
1. Serve Ginger Teriyaki Rice with One Pot Creamy Spinach Dal or Chicken in Creamy Mushroom Sauce, Spinach Mushroom Stir Fry.
1. Finally, you can also serve coffee ice cream.
