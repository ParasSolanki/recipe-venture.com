### Ingredients

- 120 ml Heavy whipping cream.
- 250 grams Digestive biscuit.
- 175 grams Butter.
- Dark chocolate - curls for garnish.
- 2 Ripe Bananas - sliced.
- 1/4 cup Caster Sugar.
- 375 grams Condensed Milk.
- 2 tablespoon Butter - melted.
- 75 grams Caster Sugar.

### Instructions

1. To begin Banoffee Pie recipe, Using a food processor pulse the biscuits roughly.
1. Now add melted butter not too hot and mix it properly until it resembles wet sand.
1. Add the biscuit crumbs equally on the pie molds and press firmly to form the shape of a pie.
1. Place the pie molds in freezer for half an hour.
1. Now to make the caramel sauce, use a pressure cooker, add 1/2 cup water and place the whole after removing the paper wrapper.
1. Pressure cook it on a medium heat , with a whistle for about 40 minutes.
1. Release the pressure naturally and do not open until it is completely been released.
1. Allow it to cool down and then open it.
1. Now to make the whipping cream, into the bowl add cream and sugar and whip in the medium speed till it is fluffy and stands up straight.
1. Remove the pies from the refrigerator and place chopped bananas on it.
1. Drizzle a generous layer of caramel sauce from the can.
1. Top it with whipped cream and garnish with chocolate curls.
1. Place in the refrigerator until served.
1. Serve the Banoffee Pie during your tea time with hot cup of coffee or just as Dessert after your dinner.
