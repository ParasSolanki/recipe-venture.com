### Ingredients

- 1 cup Pinto Beans - soaked in water overnight.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - Handful.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 1 Avocado - peeled.
- 1 Chipotle peppers - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Adobo Sauce.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pinto Bean Salsa Salad recipe, first pressure cook the pinto beans for one whistle in a pressure cooker and 10 minutes on low heat (or till they are just soft).
1. Drain and cool completely.
1. In a mixing bowl combine the onions, tomatoes, avocado, chipotle pepper, lime juice, coriander leaves, and the cooked pinto beans.
1. Toss the salad well and serve Serve Pinto Bean Salsa as a salad or a party starter/snack along with Tortilla chips or as a side dish with Naked Burrito Bowl.
