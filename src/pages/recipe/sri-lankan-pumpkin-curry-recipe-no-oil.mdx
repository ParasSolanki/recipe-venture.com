### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 5 cloves Garlic.
- 500 grams Kaddu (Parangikai/ Pumpkin) - cut into medium chunks.
- 1 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 8 Shallots - finely sliced.
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- 2 teaspoons Mustard seeds.
- 1 cup Coconut milk.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Sri Lankan Pumpkin Curry Recipe, wash, de-seed and cut the yellow pumpkin along with the skin to medium square pieces and keep aside.
1. Make a coarse paste of the garlic and mustard seeds using a mortar and pestle and keep aside.
1. Heat a heavy bottomed pan, add the pumpkin squares, sliced shallots, slit green chilli, cinnamon stick, fenugreek seeds, turmeric powder, pepper powder, red chilli powder, sugar and a cup of water.
1. Season with salt and simmer until the pumpkin is half cooked, occasionally stirring in between.
1. This will take around 8 minutes.
1. Now, add the coarse garlic, mustard mix and cook until the pumpkin is well cooked yet firm.
1. Add the coconut milk and bring it to a rolling boil and switch off the flame.
1. Serve Sri Lankan Pumpkin Curry Recipe along with Steamed Rice, Sri Lankan Style Sambol and Elai Vadam for a weekday meal.
