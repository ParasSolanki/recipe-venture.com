### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - diced.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - paste.
- 1 teaspoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves) - crushed.
- 2 Tomatoes - blanched.
- 250 grams Tofu - firm.
- 2 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 5 to 6 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoons Anardana Powder (Pomegranate Seed Powder).
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - paste.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - as required.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - diced.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 3 to 4 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kadai Tofu Sabji, first make the dry masala.
1. In a small pan, dry roast the coriander seeds, peppercorns, fennel seeds, cumin seeds, red chillies, tejpatta and black cardamom.
1. Cool and coarsely grind it in a blender along with the Anardana and dry mango powder.
1. Now we will make the Kadai Tofu SabjiIn a kadai/wok, heat the oil.
1. Add the cumin seeds and let it splutter.
1. Once done add  the onion and the  ginger and garlic paste and saut√© for a few seconds till the onion becomes translucent.
1. Once the onions have turned translucent add  the turmeric powder, tomato puree and cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
1. Add 2 to 3 teaspoons of the freshly ground masala and kasuri methi, mix well to combine.
1. Once done add the capsicum and stir fry for a few seconds.
1. Add the Tofu cubes, sprinkle salt and sugar.
1. Mix nicely so that the masala coats the Tofu cubes.
1. Cook for another minute.
1. Garnish with coriander leaves and transfer Kadai Tofu Sabji to a serving bowl,it is ready to be served.
1. Serve Kadhai Tofu Sabji with Pudina Lehsun Laccha Paratha, Dal Palak and Red Cabbage Carrot, Sprout And Onion Salad for a wholesome lunch.
