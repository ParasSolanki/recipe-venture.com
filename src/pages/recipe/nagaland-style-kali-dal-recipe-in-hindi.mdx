### Ingredients

-  1 tablespoon oil.
- 1/2 cup black urad dal (split).
-  baking soda - pinch.
-  1/2 teaspoon Panch Foran Masala.
-  2 green chillies - 2 green chillies.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  2 dry red chilies - 2 dry red chilies.
-  Ginger - As per need (ginger flakes).
-  2 bay leaves - 2 bay leaves.

### Instructions

1. To make Nagaland style black lentils, first wash and soak the lentils for 30 minutes.
1. Cook the dal with 1 cup of water, green chillies (cut in the middle), baking soda and cover the lid.
1. Add a little more water as needed and cook.
1. The pulses should not melt completely.
1. Boil until the water dries.
1. Add oil to the pan.
1. Add mustard seeds to the oil.
1. Rye tadak added red dry chillies, bay leaves and panch poran.
1. Add this tadka to the dal.
1. Serve hot lentils with rice garnished with ginger flakes.
1. Serve Nagaland style black lentils with cumin casserole and boondi raita for dinner.
