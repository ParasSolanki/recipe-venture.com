### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Pepper Corns.
- 2 teaspoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - boiled.
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Lemon juice.
- 5-6 Curry leaves.
- 1-1/2 Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 2 Tomatoes.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 clove Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Chana stock - (water in which channa was boiled).
- Salt - as per required.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Kabuli Chana Rasam, grind tomatoes to a paste and dilute it with 2 cups of water.
1. Add turmeric powder, hing and salt to this, transfer to a saucepan and bring to a rolling boiling till the raw smell vanishes.
1. To make the fresh rasam masala, in a mixer-jar combine black peppercorns, cumin seeds, curry leaves and dried red chillies.
1. Grind to a coarse masala.
1. Mash coarsely the boiled chana and add the freshly ground rasam masala, boiled chana and the 1 cup of chana stock and boil until the rasam froths up.
1. Heat one teaspoon of ghee in a tadka pan, add mustard seeds and allow it to splutter.
1. .
1. Once it splutters, add finely chopped garlic pieces and curry leaves.
1. Pour this tempering over the boiling rasam.
1. Turn off the flame.
1. Serve Channa Rasam with steamed rice with a dollop of ghee and Elai Vadam, Chow Chow Pasiparuppu Poriyal Recipe (Chayote Squash And Moong Stir Fry) and Pudina Buttermilk Recipe.
