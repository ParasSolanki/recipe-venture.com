### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Milk - or coconut milk.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 3 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Ghee - or oil.
- 6 Whole Eggs - whisked.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Anda Bhurji Recipe - Spicy Indian Style Scrambled Eggs whisk the eggs in a bowl with milk and salt to taste until light and fluffy and keep aside.
1. Into a preheated pan add ghee, the cumin seeds, onions and chopped green chillies and saute till the onions turn slightly caramelized.
1. Once done stir in the tomatoes and saute till they have soften.
1. Once the tomatoes have softened, add the whisked eggs.
1. Turn the heat to low and keep stirring the eggs until it scrambles.
1. It is important to cook on low heat for light and fluffy textured Anda Bhurji.
1. Once the Anda Bhurji is cooked, stir in the chopped coriander leaves and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer the anda bhurji into a serving plate and serve hot.
