### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- Salt - to taste.
- Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1 tablespoon Peanuts.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin (Jeera) seeds.
- 4 Green Chillies.
- Sev - for garnish.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Fresh Pomegranate Fruit Kernels - for garnish.
- 1/2 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1/2 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1 Lemon - juiced.
- Sugar - a pinch.
- 4 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled.
- 1/2 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Green Chutney Aloo Chaat Recipe, wash and put the potatoes in a pressure cooker with enough water and close the lid.
1. Place the pressure cooker on heat and pressure cook the potatoes for three whistles.
1. Turn off the heat and let the pressure release naturally.
1. After the pressure in released discard the water and let the potatoes cool.
1. Once cool, peel the potatoes and chop the potatoes into equal bite size and keep them aside.
1. While we are waiting for the potatoes to cool, we will make the green chutney.
1. Add all the ingredients listed under *For green chutney* - the coriander leaves, mint leaves, ginger,green chillies, salt, sugar, lemon and peanuts to a mixer grinder, add little water and grind the green chutney until smooth.
1. Now we will pan fry the aloo.
1. Preheat a pan and add little oil.
1. Once the oil is hot, add cumin seeds and let it splutter.
1. After the cumin seeds have spluttered, add the potatoes and saute.
1. Add salt to taste and stir fry potatoes till they evenly turn light golden brown on the outside.
1. Add cumin powder, garam masala powder and stir well to combine.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. Once the potatoes have cooled a bit, mix the green chutney with the aloo and combine to coat all the aloo pieces well with the green chutney.
1. Sprinkle chaat masala and combine.
1. Transfer the green chutney aloo chaat into serving bowls.
1. Garnish with pomegranate kernels and sev and serve.
1. You can add a dash of lemon juice to the Green Chutney aloo Chaat if you like.
1. Serve Green Chutney Aloo Chaat Recipe along with Rajma Cutlet (Kidney Beans Patties), Dahi Bhalla Papdi Chaat and Masala Soda Shikanji  for your next chaat party.
