### Ingredients

- 100 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - crumbled.
- 2 cups All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 cup Butter (unsalted) - hard.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 tablespoon Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- 1 cup Butter (unsalted) - at room temperature.
- 100 grams Sweet corn.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spicy Tandoori Paneer Open Puff Recipe by making the butter log first.
1. Use the unsalted butter which hard and cut them into small cubes.
1. Spread them on a cling film and wrap it in such a way the u can roll it flat.
1. Using rolling pin roll the butter into a rectangular shape about 2 centimeter thick.
1. Make sure u do this quickly otherwise the butter will melt quickly.
1. Place the rolled butter in the cling film itself and keep it in the refrigerator for 15 minutes.
1. In the meanwhile make the dough, add all the ingredients in a mixing bowl.
1. Using your hand create a crumble texture with the dough by mixing the flour and butter together.
1. Slowly add water and knead it into a soft dough, keep it in the refrigerator for 10 minutes.
1. Once done take out the dough and start rolling it into a rectangular shape so you can wrap the rectangular butter log with this dough.
1. Once it is rolled, take out the butter and place it in the center, fold in and wrap the butter inside make sure you seal all openings by just pinching the dough.
1. After sealing, gently roll the dough keeping the rectangular shape intact.
1. Keep dusting flour on the dough and rolling it so that it does not stick.
1. Keep edges straight and even.
1. Donâ€™t overwork the dough.
1. Roll it into a long rectangular shape about 20 cm length  to 10 cm  breadth, keep the long length facing towards you, fold the left side to the middle and the right side as well, so that the two edges meet the center.
1. Now fold it like a book and dust some more flour and refrigerate by covering it with a cling film for 10 minutes.
1. Repeat the process around 5 times, make sure you refrigerate the dough every time you do the book folding.
1. Keep the edges straight and do not over work it.
1. In the meanwhile, pre-heat the oven to 220 degrees Celsius and keep it ready for baking.
1. To make the fillingHeat a sauce pan with oil, add onions, ginger and garlic paste and saute until the onions soften.
1. Add tomatoes and the spice powders and saute well till the tomatoes are cooked.
1. Mash them well as you cook.
1. Add bell peppers, crumbled paneer, sweet corn and mix well, at the end add curd and give it a nice mix.
1. Saute until the masala has combined well.
1. To assembleTake the dough out, roll it into a rectangle with 2 centimeter thickness.
1. Cut the big rectangle into small squares about 10 centimeter.
1. Take a heap tablespoon of the filling and place them in the center of each square.
1. Place these puffs over a greased tray.
1. Brush them with olive oil and sprinkle some toasted sesame seeds and bake in the oven for about 20 minutes.
1. Serve warm.
