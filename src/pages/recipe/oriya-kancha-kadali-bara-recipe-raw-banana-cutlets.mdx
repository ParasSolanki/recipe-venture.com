### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 tablespoon Gram flour (besan).
- 4 Raw Banana.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2-3 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.

### Instructions

1. To make Odiya Kancha Kadali Bara Recipe, wash the raw bananas and pressure cook them with water till 2 whistles.
1. Remove from heat and allow them to cool.
1. Then peel the skin of the bananas and mash them well.
1. Now add chopped green chillies, chilli powder, coriander leaves, cumin powder, chat masala, grated ginger and salt to taste.
1. Mix them well to get dough- like consistency.
1. Heat the cooking oil on a non-stick pan.
1. Make flat cutlets and roll them in gram flour or semolina, dust off excess flour.
1. Shallow-fry them on the non-stick pan on both sides on low heat till both sides turn brown.
1. Serve Odiya Kancha Kadali Bara Recipe hot with roasted tomato sauce or Dhaniya Pudina Chutney.
