### Ingredients

- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Milk.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1/4 cup Cashew nuts.
- 250 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cut into small cubes.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Brown cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1 Star anise.
- 1 teaspoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Paneer in White Gravy Recipe, prep all the ingredients and keep them ready.
1. To make a paste of the cashew nuts, add it to the mixer grinder, add milk and blend to make a smooth paste.
1. Heat ghee in a pan; add the green chillies, bay leaf, star anise, badi elaichi, cinnamon stick and saute for a few seconds.
1. Add the kasuri methi, the cashew paste, yogurt, salt and paneer.
1. Give the Paneer in white gravy a stir and give it a brisk boil.
1. Once done, check the salt and adjust according to taste and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer the Paneer In White Gravy to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Sprinkle with more kasuri methi if desired.
1. Serve the  Paneer in White Gravy Recipe along withDal Makhani, Tawa Paratha and Pyaz for a delicious dinner or lunch.
