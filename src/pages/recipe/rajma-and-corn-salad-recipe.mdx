### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 tablespoons Pickled Jalapenos - roughly chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Sweet corn - kernels.
- 1/4 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina) - roughly torn.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 2 cloves Garlic - crushed.
- 1/2 cup Kashmiri Rajma - or regular rajma.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Rajma and Corn Salad Recipe, wash and soak the rajma overnight.
1. Pressure cook the rajma with water for about 8-10 whistles.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. In a pan, boil water on medium flame, add the corn and cook until the kernels swell up.
1. Drain out the water and set aside.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the boiled rajma and corn, chopped mint leaves and jalapenos.
1. For the dressing, in a small bowl,add  salt, pepper, lemon juice, sugar, vinegar, jeera powder, red chili powder, crushed garlic & olive oil.
1. Just before serving, toss in the dressing into the salad.
1. Serve Rajma and Corn Salad Recipe as a side dish along with Panchmel Dal and Phulka for a weeknight dinner.
