### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar) - blanched.
- 1/2 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil - to shallow fry.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bhojpuri Style Bharbhara Recipe, mix all the ingredients except oil into a big bowl.
1. Make a thin batter with water, and season with salt.
1. Heat a skillet on medium heat, grease it with a little oil.
1. Pour a small ladle full of batter like a pancake, and pour a few drops of oil around it, let it cook for about 2 minutes or till the bottom is cooked.
1. Once the bottom is cooked, flip over and cook on the other side for another 2 minutes and take it out on a plate.
1. Likewise, prepare the Bharbhara with the rest of the batter as well.
1. Serve the Bhojpuri Style Bharbhara Recipe along with a fruit smoothie for kids after school snack or along with hot Masala Chai as a tea time snack.
