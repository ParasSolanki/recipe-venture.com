### Ingredients

- 3 Whole Eggs - beaten with a fork.
- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 tablespoon Butter (unsalted).
- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 cup Sweet corn.
- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 1 cup Mozzarella cheese - cut into small cubes.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 cups Jasmine Rice.
- 1/4 cup Parsley leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Parmesan cheese - grated.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Pan Fried Rice Balls Filled With Cheese Recipe, first in a heavy bottomed pot, heat oil over medium heat and add some butter.
1. As the butter melts, add diced onion and saute until soft for about 4-5 minutesAdd rice and give a stir to coat with oil.
1. Now add 5 cups of water to the rice and 1 teaspoon of salt.
1. Cover and cook until water has been completely absorbed by the rice.
1. Stir in the corn and cook covered for another 5 minutes and check if the rice has cooked and absorbed all the water.
1. Allow it to cool.
1. Once cooled, combine parsley and parmesan cheese to the mixture.
1. Make rice balls using hand and stuff each ball with a small cube of mozzarella cheese in the centre.
1. The cheese cube should be totally covered with the rice mixture.
1. Arrange flour in a bowl, 3 beaten eggs in another and breadcrumbs in the third ballCoat the rice balls with all purpose flour.
1. Shake off the excess flour.
1. Dip the balls in the beaten egg.
1. Finally, roll balls in breadcrumbs until it is evenly coated with it.
1. Repeat for the remaining balls.
1. Heat some oil in skillet over medium flame.
1. Once hot add the coated rice balls in batches and cook until they turn golden brown.
1. Your Pan Fried Rice Balls Filled With Cheese Recipe is ready to be served.
1. Serve the Pan Fried Rice Balls Filled With Cheese Recipe with classic Marinara sauce or ranch for evening snack.
