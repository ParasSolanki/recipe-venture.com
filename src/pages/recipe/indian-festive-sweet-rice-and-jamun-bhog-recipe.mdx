### Ingredients

- Saffron strands - for garnish.
- Rose water - a few drops.
- 2 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 cup Sugar.
- 3 tablespoon All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- Saffron strands - a few.
- 1 Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 3 tablespoon Ghee.
- 3 tablespoon Caster Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1/2 cup Govind Bhog Rice.
- 2 teaspoon Ghee.
- 1-1/2 cup Water.
- 200 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese).
- 11/2 cups Water.

### Instructions

1. To make the Indian Festive Sweet Rice And Jamun Bhog recipe, first we will prepare the sweet rice so let's wash the rice and soak for 10 minutes.
1. Heat a large heavy bottomed saucepan with ghee and fry the rice.
1. Add green cardamom and bay leaf .
1. Pour in water, rose water and sugar and let the rice cook on medium to low flame.
1. Once the rice is cooked completely spread on a plate to cool.
1. Drain any excess water in the rice if there is any.
1. Sprinkle Kesar all over the rice.
1. Now let's make the sugar syrup for the jamuns.
1. Take a small saucepan and add both sugar and water and let it boil to a syrup consistency.
1. Finally to  make the jamuns, take a big plate and mash the paneer, sugar powder, cardamom powder, kesar and flour with your palm.
1. The mixture should become smooth.
1. Shape the paneer mixture in mini oval jamuns .
1. Meanwhile, heat ghee in a frying pan and fry the mini jamuns till golden brown in few at a time and transfer in hot sugar syrup.
1. Let it rest for 15 minutes.
1. You can serve Indian Festive Sweet Rice And Jamun Bhog Recipe with  Matar Ki Puri and  Kasuri Methi Aur Aloo Rasedar .
