### Ingredients

- 2/3 cup green peas.
-  oil - as per use.
-  1 teaspoon ginger - grated.
-  1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
-  1 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  1 teaspoon cumin powder.
-  2 cups gram flour.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 teaspoon red chili powder.
-  1 teaspoon garam masala powder.
-  1/2 cup onion - finely chopped.
-  1 tablespoon green chili - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make Bihari green peas stuffed, firstly add gram flour, onion, green pepper, ginger, garam masala, black pepper powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, red pepper powder, and salt to the bowl and mix it with water as required.
1. Keep this mixture thick.
1. Keep the pan spread on the pan.
1. Keep the steak hot on the gas.
1. Spread the mixture on the griddle.
1. Note that if the griddle is not completely heated then the bhabra will stick on the pan.
1. Spread the bhabra on the griddle and reduce the flame.
1. Add oil to the bhabra on a low flame and cook it till it turns brown in color from both the sides.
1. Cook on both sides and serve.
1. Serve Bihari Hare Matar Bharbhara with Coriander Mint Chutney and Masala Tea.
