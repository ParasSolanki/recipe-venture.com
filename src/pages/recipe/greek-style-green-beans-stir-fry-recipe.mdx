### Ingredients

- 300 grams Green beans (French Beans) - cut into.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - coarsely pounded.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Black raisins.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - for cooking.
- 1/2 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 1 cup Onion - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Greek Style Green Beans Stir Fry Recipe, prep all the ingredients and keep them ready.
1. Heat a pan with olive oil on medium heat; add the onions and garlic and saut√© until the onions are lightly browned.
1. Ensure you cook on medium heat so it browns evenly.
1. Once the onions have lightly browned add the tomato puree, crushed peppercorn, chilli flakes and give it a brisk boil for about a minute.
1. Add the green beans to the tomato and onion mixture; sprinkle a little water, salt and cover and cook on low heat till the beans are completely cooked and yet green.
1. You can stir it couple of times in between to ensure the green beans gets well coated with the tomato onion mixture.
1. Once the green beans are cooked through, add the raisins and stir fry the Greek Style Green Beans for less than a minute and turn off the heat.
1. Check the salt and adjust to taste accordingly.
1. Once done, transfer the Greek Style Green Beans to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve the Greek Style Green Beans Stir Fry Recipe along with Moroccan Style Vegetable Stew Recipe and Herbed Butter Rice Recipe With Rosemary & Thyme for a delicious continental meal.
