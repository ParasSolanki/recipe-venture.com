### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 Dry Red Chilli.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- Coconut Oil - for cooking.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 cup Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - peeled.
- 300 grams Chicken breasts - cut into small pieces.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Erode Pallipalayam Chicken Fry Recipe, we will first grind the pearl onions along with garlic in a mixer grinder  and keep it ready.
1. Heat a skillet with oil on medium flame, add dry red chillies and allow it to sizzle for a few seconds, next add the curry leaves and allow it to crackle.
1. Add ground onion-garlic mixture and saute till the raw smell goes away.
1. This will take about 10-12 minute.
1. Once the onion mixture appears cooked add all the spice powders - red chili powder and turmeric powder .
1. Continue tot cook for another 5 minutes.
1. Add chicken pieces and sprinkle some salt and mix well.
1. Keep sauting till the chicken is roasted well.
1. Cook for about 20 making sure the chicken is cooked.
1. Switch off the heat,  garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
1. Serve the Erode Pallipalayam Chicken Fry Recipe along with steamed rice, Ghee,  Karuveppilai Chutney Podi and Chettinad Style Poondu Rasam Recipe  to enjoy your Sunday lunch.
1. Finish this meal with Paal Payasam Recipe as dessert.
