### Ingredients

- 3/4 cup Sugar - evaporated.
- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - shredded.
- Water - as required.
- 6 cups Milk.
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1/4 cup Cashew nuts - crushed.
- 2 cups Rice flour.
- 1/8 cup Badam (Almond) - crushed.
- 1 tablespoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Khira Gaintha recipe, boil water in a pan.
1. Add a teaspoon of oil and a teaspoon of salt to this.
1. Now gradually and continuously add the rice flour to the water and keep stirring.
1. Make sure there arenâ€™t any lumps.
1. Lower the flame, cover the pan with a lid and cook it for five minutes.
1. Take this mixture and turn it on to a wide plate.
1. Let it cool.
1. Once it has cooled just enough knead it with your fingers so that it forms a ball.
1. Make small balls and steam them.
1. In an another deep pan boil the milk and evaporated milk.
1. Simmer in low flame until milk thickens.
1. Add the rest of the sugar, cardamom powder and heat the milk some more.
1. Now one by one drop the rice balls into the boiling milk and continue stirring.
1. You can make a paste with 1 tablespoon of rice powder and add it to the milk to thicken it.
1. Add the coconut in the end along with slivered cashew and almonds.
1. Serve it cool.
1. Serve Khira Gaintha on its own as a delicious dessert after your satisfying meal.
