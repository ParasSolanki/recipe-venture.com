### Ingredients

-  1/2 cup green coriander - finely chopped.
-  1 green chili - finely chopped.
-  3 tablespoons gram flour.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  oil - to brush.
- 4 carrots - tightly.

### Instructions

1. To make Carrot Coriander Pakora Recipe, first tighten the carrots and keep them separately in a bowl.
1. In this bowl add coriander, green chillies, gram flour, salt and mix well.
1. Carrots will release water and this will make it easier for everyone to mix.
1. Now heat a skillet on medium heat and brush it.
1. Now add a tablespoon of mixture and fill it well in small discs.
1. Now add some oil on top and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Cook the bottom portion and flip it over.
1. Cook for 2 minutes.
1. Take it out in the plate and make all the other pakoras in this way.
1. Serve carrot coriander pakoda with spice tea and green chutney for evening snacks.
