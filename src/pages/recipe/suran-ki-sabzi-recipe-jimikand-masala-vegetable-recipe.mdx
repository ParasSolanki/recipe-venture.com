### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- Salt - as per your taste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - for garnish.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 300 grams Elephant yam (Suran/Senai/Ratalu) - cubed.
- 2 teaspoons Ghee.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1/2 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar) - steamed.
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Suran Ki Sabzi Recipe, first wash the suran cubes and mix with lime juice in a bowl and keep it aside for 5 minutes.
1. Take a pressure cooker  add the suran along with salt and 2 tablespoons of water.
1.  Pressure cook for 1 whistle and turn off the heat.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Heat a heavy bottomed saucepan with ghee cinnamon, cloves, cardamom and ginger.
1. Saute until you can smell the aromas coming through.
1. Once done, add the tomato puree and all the remaining masalas.
1. Stir for a few minutes until the masalas get well combined into the tomato gravy.
1. Next, add the cooked suran cubes and dahi and give the Suran Ki Sabzi a brisk boil for 2 minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Check the salt and spices to taste and adjust accordingly.
1. Finally stir in the coriander leaves into the Suran Ki Sabzi and serve hot.
1. Serve this delicious Suran Ki Sabzi along with Rotis/Jeera Rice, Palak Raita and Kala Chana Salad for a simple dinner.
