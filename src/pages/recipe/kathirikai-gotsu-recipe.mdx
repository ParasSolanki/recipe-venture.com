### Ingredients

-  1 tsp rye.
-  1/2 cup tamarind water.
-  1/4 tsp asafoetida.
-  2 tomatoes - chopped.
-  10 small onions - cut.
-  1 / 2 tsp turmeric powder.
-  green coriander - as per use.
-  1 sprig curry leaves.
- 1 eggplant - chopped.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 tsp sambar powder.
-  1 inch ginger - finely chopped.
-  1 tbsp sesame oil.
-  1 tbsp jaggery.

### Instructions

1. To make Kathirikai Gothsu recipe, first heat the oil in a pressure cooker.
1. Add mustard seeds and let it splutter.
1. Now add asafoetida, curry leaves, sambar onion, ginger and cook till the onion becomes soft.
1. After the onion is soft, add brinjal, tomato, tamarind water, turmeric powder, asafoetida, sambar Add powder, salt, jaggery, water and mix.
1. Turn off the cooker and cook until 3 to 4 cities come.
1. Turn off the gas and let the pressure drain automatically.
1. Open the cooker, mix and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve Kathirikai Gothsu recipe with Wayne Pongal and filter coffee for breakfast.
