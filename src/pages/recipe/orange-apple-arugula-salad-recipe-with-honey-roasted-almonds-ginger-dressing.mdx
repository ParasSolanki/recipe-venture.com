### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 10 Badam (Almond) - cut lengthwise.
- 1/2 cup Pickled onions.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh orange juice.
- 1/2 inch Ginger - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Orange - cut into wedges.
- 1 tablespoon Poppy seeds - 1 tablespoon poppy seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Butter.
- 1 Apple - cut into wedges.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 1 bunch Arugula leaves.
- 2 teaspoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil - 2 teaspoon olive oil.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Orange Apple Arugula Salad Recipe With Honey Roasted Almonds & Ginger Dressing, keep all the fresh ingredients ready.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the arugula leaves along with oranges, apples, and pickled onions.
1. Allow it to chill in the refrigerator.
1. To make the Ginger DressingIn a small mixing bowl, combine the lemon juice, freshly squeezed orange juice, honey, olive oil, ginger, black pepper powder, salt and poppy seeds.
1. Mix well and allow it to chill.
1. To make the Honey Roasted AlmondsHeat a skillet with butter on medium flame, add the almonds and fry for 1 minute.
1. Reduce the flame, add the honey and mix it into the almonds.
1. Increase the flame for 30 seconds, mixing the almonds continuously until it gets well coated with honey and is sticky and crisp.
1. Once done, turn off the flame, transfer to a bowl and set aside.
1. Finally to make the salad, toss in the ginger dressing, into the chilled arugula leaves, oranges, apples, and pickled onions.
1. Mix well.
1. Serve Orange Apple Arugula Salad in a platter and garnish with the honey roasted almonds.
1. Serve the Orange Apple Arugula Salad Recipe With Honey Roasted Almonds & Ginger Dressing along with Spinach Garlic Bruschetta Recipe With Red Bell Pepper Sauce, followed by a dessert of Creme Caramel Flan Recipe to complete the meal.
