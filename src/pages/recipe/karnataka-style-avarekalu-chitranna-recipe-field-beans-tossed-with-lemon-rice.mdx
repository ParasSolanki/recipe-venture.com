### Ingredients

- 1 cup Avarekalu / Lilva Beans.
- 1 pinch Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 cups Rice - boiled.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 3 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 2 Onion - chopped.
- 5 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- Ghee - or oil.
- 5 Curry leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Karnataka Style Avarekalu Chitranna Recipe by cooking the rice separately in a pressure cooker.
1. Release the pressure naturally, once that is done add some oil and mix it well.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Cook the avarekalu in a sauce pan with water separately till is soft.
1. Then strain the water and keep it aside.
1. Then heat a kadai with some oil, temper the mustard seeds, urad dal, hing and curry leaves.
1. Once that is done, add the onions and green chilies and saut√© till it is golden brown, and add the boiled avarekalu and saute.
1. Now you can add the boiled rice, switch off the heat, sprinkle the lemon juice, chopped coriander and give it a stir.
1. Check for salt according to your taste.
1. Serve the Karnataka Style Avarekalu Chitranna Recipe (Field Beans Tossed With Lemon Rice) as a delicious Breakfast recipe along with some Mango Pickle.
