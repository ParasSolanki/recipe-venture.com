### Ingredients

- 10 Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- 2 tablespoons Khus syrup.
- 1/2 Cucumber - sliced.
- Ice cubes - as required.
- 4 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 400 ml Aerated water (Soda Water).
- 4 tablespoon Cucumber.
- 8 Lemon - sliced thin.
- 2 tablespoons Sugar syrup.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Khus And Cucumber Mojito recipe, crush cucumber pieces in a mortar and collect the juice and Keep aside.
1. In a pitcher squeeze lemon to extract the juice and add mint leaves.
1. Crush the leaves with a pestle and add sugar syrup, khus syrup and cucumber extract.
1. Stir it and add sparkling water into it.
1. Take a serving glass, add lots of ice in it.
1. Pour in the prepared drink and add slices of cucumber and lemon on top with some mint leaves.
1. Serve chilled!Serve Khus And Cucumber Mojito along with some snacks like Herbed Baby Potatoes and Raw Banana Cutlet for a movie night or when you have some guest at home.
