### Ingredients

-  1 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  5 dry red chillies.
-  1/2 teaspoon mustard.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
- 150 grams tindora.
-  1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  2 Teaspoon oil.
-  2 teaspoons tamarind paste.
-  1 teaspoon oil.
-  3 tablespoons black urad dal (split).

### Instructions

1. To make Dondkai Puli Curry Recipe, first wash the tindora and cut it into 4 parts.
1. Clean the tamarind and soak it in water for 10 minutes.
1. Pour water in a saucepan with tindore and cook until soft.
1. Drain the water and keep the tindore separately.
1. Heat the oil in a saucepan.
1. Add cumin seeds, garlic, dry red chilies and cook for 1 minute.
1. After 1 minute add urad dal to it and cook till it becomes golden.
1. Turn off the gas and let the mixture cool down.
1. In a mixer grinder, add tamarind, turmeric powder, salt, roasted garlic masala, a little tamarind water and grind it.
1. Now add tindora and paste in a bowl.
1. Mix well and heat oil in a tempering pan for tempering.
1. Add the mustard seeds, cumin seeds and let it cook for 15 seconds.
1. Add this tempering to the vegetable, mix and serve.
1. Serve Dondkai Puli Curry Recipe along with rice, kirai sambar, beetroot pachdi and papad for dinner.
