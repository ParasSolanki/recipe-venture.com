### Ingredients

- 5 Garlic - cloves.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Onion - cut straight.
- 1 teaspoon Black Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - torn into 2 pieces.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - required for garnish.
- 1 Cauliflower (gobi) - chopped into small florets.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Pepper Cauliflower recipe, boil water and add cauliflower florets to it.
1. Switch off the heat after 10 minutes and let the cauliflower remain in hot boiling water.
1. The cauliflower should retain crispiness and should not be over cooked.
1. You can even blanch the cauliflower.
1. Heat a kadai/wok and add oil.
1. After oil gets heated, add mustard seeds.
1. After mustard seeds flutter, add red chilli and urad dal.
1. After urad dal turns slightly brown, add curry leaves and chopped garlic.
1. Fry the curry leaves till they turn crispy.
1. Add chopped onion and fry it, till it turns pinkish.
1. Now add the boiled cauliflower florets.
1. Do not add water.
1. Add black pepper powder, cumin powder, red chilli powder and toss.
1. Add salt to taste and toss.
1. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve Pepper Cauliflower along with Panchmel Dal, Rice and Phulkas for a perfect weekday meal.
