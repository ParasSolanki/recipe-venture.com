### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/4 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - little for brushing.
- 1 tablespoon Paprika powder.
- 400 grams Chicken - minced.
- Salt - to taste.
- Iceberg lettuce - Few.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 4 Mini Pita Breads.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger - grated.
- 4 tablespoon Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Feta Cheese - crumbled.
- 1 tablespoon Harissa paste.
- 1 tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 tablespoon Garlic - minced.
- 3 Carrots (Gajjar) - cut into thin strips.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Moroccan Chicken Burgers With Feta And Carrot recipe, into the minced chicken in a mixing bowl, add finely chopped onions, mint leaves, grated ginger, minced garlic, paprika, cumin powder, pepper powder, salt, harissa and lime juice.
1. Mix well until combined.
1. Divide into 4 equal portions.
1. Shape into 1 inch thick patty.
1. Keep aside.
1. Heat up your grill on medium flame.
1. Brush oil over the grill and place the chicken patty over the hot grill.
1. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes on one side and then flip to cook on other side.
1. Make sure chicken is cooked through and browned well.
1. Remove the patty from grill.
1. For carrot slaw, cut carrots into thin strips.
1. Add olive oil, pepper powder, salt and lime juice.
1. Mix well.
1. Lightly grill the pita bread.
1. Cut the pita bread in the middle from one end and leave the other ends intact.
1. Smear hung curd inside the pita bread.
1. Place few lettuce leaves and the chicken patty.
1. Spoon some carrot slaw other the grilled chicken patty and crumble some feta cheese.
1. Serve.
1. Serve Moroccan Chicken Burgers With Feta And Carrot  along with Mulled Apple Juice Recipe followed by a dessert of Rich Chocolate Cheesecake Recipe.
