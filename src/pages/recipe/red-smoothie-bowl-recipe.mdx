### Ingredients

- Kiwi - Sliced or any fruit of your choice.
- 1/2 Lemon - juiced.
- Ice cubes - as needed.
- 100 grams Cucumber - sliced.
- 2 tablespoons Coconut cream.
- 2 Broccoli - cut into florets.
- 1 Beetroot - diced.
- 1 Ripe Bananas - frozen.
- 2 teaspoons Flax seed powder (flax meal).
- 1/2 cup Tender coconut water.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - to garnish.
- 1 Spinach - chopped.
- 1/2 Celery - stick.
- 1/2 cup Mango (Ripe) - diced.
- 4 Strawberries - hulled.

### Instructions

1. To begin with red smoothie bowl, prepare all the vegetables and fruits by washing and chopping.
1. Set aside the coconut cream, mint leaves and sliced kiwi.
1. Add remaining ingredients into a blender and blend till smooth.
1. Serve this in a bowl swirl in the coconut cream, and top it with mint and kiwi.
1. Serve Red Smoothie Bowl on its own for a breakfast or you can even serve it as a smoothie.
