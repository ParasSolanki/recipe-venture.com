### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 1/2 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - soaked overnight and cooked.
- 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 5 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 cup Spinach.
- 2 tablespoon Tahini.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spinach Hummus Recipe we will boil the soaked chickpeas with enough water in a pressure cooker for about 30 minutes.
1. Till it is cooked is soft.
1. In a mixer add all the ingredients and blitz till you get a creamy and not too smooth consistency.
1. Once done, check for salt and serve it in a bowl.
1. Serve the Spinach Hummus Recipe can be had along with pita bread, tortilla chips for a healthy start.
