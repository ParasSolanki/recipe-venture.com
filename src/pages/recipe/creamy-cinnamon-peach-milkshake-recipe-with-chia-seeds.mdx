### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Chia Seeds.
- 5 Peaches - deseeded and sliced.
- 3/4 cup Vanilla Ice cream.
- 3/4 cup Milk.
- 2 tablespoons Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Creamy Cinnamon Peach Milkshake Recipe With Chia Seeds in a mixer jar combine the peaches, sugar and honey, cinnamon and grind it into smooth paste.
1. Open the lid of the jar, add milk, icecream and blitz it once more.
1. Ensure the milkshake is well combined Add ice cubes to the glasses and serve this Creamy Cinnamon Peach Milkshake over it.
1. Garnish with chia seeds.
1. Serve Creamy Cinnamon Peach Milkshake as an afterschool snack to kids.
1. You can also serve it as part of a delicious Sunday breakfast along with Apple Cinnamon French Toast Recipe.
