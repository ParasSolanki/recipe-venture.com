### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Ginger - grated.
- 2 cups Fresh orange juice.
- 2 tablespoons Tea Monk Seiki Peppermint Green Tea.
- 4 cups Chilled water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Orange Green Tea Punch Recipe, in a large saucepan, bring water to a boil and turn off the heat.
1. Add the Seiki Peppermint Green Tea leaves, grated ginger to the boiled water and stir.
1. Let the tea brew for 5 minutes.
1. Strain the tea into a pitcher and discard the tea leaves.
1. Allow the tea to cool and refrigerate for 2 hours.
1. After 2 hours stir in the orange juice.
1. Pour the Orange Green Tea Punch into serving glass, add ice cubes and serve chilled.
1. Serve the Orange Green Tea Punch Recipe along with Roasted Vegetable Pesto Tart or a Indian Spicy Masala Cookie (Khara Biscuit) .
