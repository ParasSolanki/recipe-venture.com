### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese - grated.
- 3/4 cup Parsley leaves.
- 1/2 teaspoon Rosemary.
- 1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese - cubes.
- 1 Onion - chopped finely.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - as required.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 200 grams Mutton - minced.
- 2 cups Homemade tomato puree.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mixed Herbs (Dried).
- 1/2 teaspoon Basil leaves.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Mozzarella & Parmesan Stuffed Meat Balls Recipe, firstly get ready with all the ingredients required.
1. In a bowl, add minced mutton, salt, black pepper powder, chopped onion, green chillies, chopped cilantro, chopped parsley, ginger-garlic paste along with few drops of olive oil and make small balls.
1. Add a cube of mozzarella cheese and grated parmesan cheese in each meatball and smoothen the surface to form round balls.
1. Keep the meatballs in the refrigerator for an hour to firm up.
1. Instruction for SauceTo prepare the tomato puree, first, make a cross slit at the base of tomatoes and boil them till they begin to leave their skin.
1. Making a cross slit eases helps to remove the tomato skin easily when they are piping hot.
1. Grind the tomato pulp discarding their skin, in a mixer jar and the puree, is ready.
1. Pre-heat the oven at 200 degree Celsius for 15 minutes.
1. Meanwhile, in a pan, heat oil on medium flame.
1. Add all the sauce ingredients and bring it to a boil.
1. Instruction for BakingIn the baking tray, pour half the amount of tomato sauce.
1. Above the sauce, place the meatballs one by one.
1. Place the tray in the oven for 15 minutes at 180 degree Celsius to bake.
1. After 15 minutes, take the tray out, turn the meatballs to the other side and pour rest of the tomato sauce on them.
1. Place some mozzarella cubes and parmesan cheese on top.
1. Again place the baking tray in the oven to bake for 20 -25 minutes at 180 degree Celsius.
1. After approximately 20 minutes, take the tray out and check if the meatballs have cooked properly from within.
1. If they are yet to be cooked, keep for another 5 minutes.
1. The cheesy tangy and spicy Mozzarella & Parmesan Stuffed Meat Balls Recipe are ready to be served as snacks or main course with rice, Penne Pasta In Marinara Sauce Recipe or spaghetti of your choice.
