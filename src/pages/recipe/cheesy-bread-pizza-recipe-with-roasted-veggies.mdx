### Ingredients

- 4 Panini Bread - slit into half.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - thinly sliced.
- 1/2 cup Broccoli - cut into small florets.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Homemade Pizza And Pasta Sauce.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - for cooking.
- 2 tablespoons Butter.
- 1 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.
- 1/2 cup Del Monte Cheesy Garlic Mayo.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cheesy Bread Pizza Recipe, preheat the oven to 180 degree Celsius for 15 minutes.
1. Meanwhile, heat a sauce pan with oil and roast the onions, broccoli, carrot and bell peppers separately one by one until they are soft.
1. Ensure the color of the vegetables remain intact.
1. Add salt and pepper for each stage and roast them till it is soft.
1. Smear butter on each panini bread slices evenly, then smear the Del Monte Pizza and Pasta sauce, add a dollop of the mayo and top it up with the roasted vegetables.
1. Place these Cheesy Bread Pizzas on to a baking sheet and bake it in the oven for 10 minutes and serve.
1. Serve the Cheesy Bread Pizza Recipe as a evening snack for kids during the summers along with a glass of Oreo Milkshake Recipe.
1. You can also serve Cheesy Bread Pizza Recipe With Roasted Veggies along with Indian Style Masala Macaroni and Vanilla Choco Mousse Recipe With Chcocolate Chips for your simple and tasty dinner.
