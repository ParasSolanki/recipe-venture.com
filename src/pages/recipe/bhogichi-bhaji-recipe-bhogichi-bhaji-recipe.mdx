### Ingredients

-  5 plum - wash and remove stem.
-  coriander leaves - little.
-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  3 eggplants - chopped.
-  1 tablespoon oil.
-  1 large Spoon peanut powder - bake.
- 1 potato - peel and chop.
-  1 teaspoon goda masala.
-  1 teaspoon mustard.
-  10 avrekalu - cut.
-  1/4 teaspoon Teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  3/4 cup green gram.
-  1/4 teaspoon asafoetida.
-  1 teaspoon lemon juice.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  2 carrots - red.
-  1 tablespoon sesame powder - bake.

### Instructions

1. To make Bhogichi Bhaji, first heat oil in a pan.
1. Add mustard seeds, sesame seeds and let it cook for 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add green chili, asafoetida, goda masala and turmeric powder.
1. After cooking for 1 minute, add potatoes, green gram and avarakalu to it.
1. Mix well and add salt, some water to it.
1. Mix again, cover the pan and let it cook for 10 to 12 minutes.
1. Now add brinjals, carrots and mix it.
1. Add 1 to 2 tablespoons of water and cook the saree till the vegetable is cooked.
1. After the vegetables are cooked, add plum, sesame powder, peanut powder and let it cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. After cooking add lemon juice and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve Bhogichi Bhaji with Khandeshi Dal and Phulka for dinner.
