### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Sriracha sauce.
- 2 tablespoons Classic Mayonnaise (With Egg).
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 6 Whole Eggs - hard boiled.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sriracha Spiced Deviled Eggs, first boil the eggs.
1. To hard boil eggs, place eggs in a pot and fill it with water.
1. Bring it to a boil.
1. Once the water starts boiling, turn off the heat and cover the pot.
1. Let this sit for 12 minutes, after which you can cool the eggs under running water and remove the shells.
1. Once the eggs have cooled, slice each egg in half lengthwise and scoop out the yolk.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine together the egg yolks, sriracha, mayonnaise and pepper powder till well combined and no lumps remain.
1. Scoop a teaspoon of the mixture into the cavity of each egg whiteshell.
1. Alternatively, you can also use an icing tip and a piping bag to pipe the mixture into the egg and make it look prettier.
1. Refrigerate the Sriracha Spiced Deviled Eggs for a couple of hours and serve.
1. Serve the Sriracha Spiced Deviled Eggs as a perfect party appetizer, along with your favorite Mexican Salsa.
