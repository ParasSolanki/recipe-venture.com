### Ingredients

- 5 tablespoon Homemade Hoisin Sauce.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- Water - as required.
- 200 grams Button mushrooms - quartered.
- 2 sprig Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens).
- 2 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 200 grams Tofu - cubed.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Hoisin Tofu & Mushroom Stir Fry with homemade Hoisin Sauce Recipe by making the sauce first for that please check our Homemade Hoisin Sauce Recipe.
1. Then heat a flat skillet with oil, add ginger and garlic.
1. Saut√© the mushroom for few seconds till it is cooked.
1. Then add the tofu and stir fry till it has a charred color.
1. Mix the sauce with cornstarch and water (1 cup) and keep it ready, gradually add the mixture on to the pan and give it a stir.
1. Keep mixing till it thickens.
1. Serve the Hoisin Tofu & Mushroom Stir Fry with homemade Hoisin Sauce Recipe along with Brown Rice.
