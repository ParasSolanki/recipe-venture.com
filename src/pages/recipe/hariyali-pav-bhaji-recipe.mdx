### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Spinach Leaves (Palak) - pureed.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - boiled and mashed.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 5 Curry leaves.
- 1/2 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Butter - as required.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint).
- Pav buns - as required.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped (adjustable).
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar) - boiled.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Pav bhaji masala.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 Bottle gourd (lauki) - boiled and pureed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Hariyali Pav bhaji recipe, firstly heat oil in a wok/kadhai.
1. Add the cumin seeds, mustard seeds, curry leaves, asafoetida and green chillies.
1. Once it is done, add ginger garlic paste and cook for about 15 seconds.
1. Next, add the onions and let it cook till they become soft and transparent.
1. Now add the potatoes and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. After 2 to 3 minutes, add tomatoes, cover the pan and cook for about 5 minutes.
1. Now add the bottle gourd puree, mashed potato and green peas.
1. Add some water and mash it using a masher.
1. Let it cook for about 5 to 6 minutes.
1. Add all the masalas including salt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, garam masala and pav bhaji masala.
1. Cook for about 5 minutes and then add the spinach puree and the coriander paste.
1. Let it cook for about 10 to 12 minutes.
1. The next step is to roast the buns.
1. Heat a little butter on a flat pan, sprinkle some pav bhaji masala and chopped coriander leaves.
1. Now but the pav from between and keep on the same pan and roast from both the sides.
1. Once it is cooked properly, add butter on top and serve hot with pav bhaji buns.
1. Serve Hariyali Pav Bhaji  with Masala Chaas for a perfect weekday meal.
