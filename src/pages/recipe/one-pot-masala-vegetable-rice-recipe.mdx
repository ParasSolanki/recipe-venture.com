### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 cup Rice - washed.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 cups Water - for cooking rice.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Green beans (French Beans) - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Coconut Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making One Pot Masala Vegetable Rice Recipe, prep all the vegetables and keep them aside.
1. In a pressure cooker pan, add one tablespoon of coconut oil and let it warm.
1. Once the oil is warm, reduce the flame to low and add cumin seeds and saute for a minute.
1. Now add in the sliced ginger, chopped cloves of garlic and onions and saute them until the onions turn soft and translucent.
1. Next add in the chopped tomatoes, and saute until them until they turn mushy.
1. Once the tomatoes are mushy, add in all the spices one by one - turmeric powder, red chilli powder and garam masala powder.
1. Stir in the masalas well into the onion and tomato mixture Now add in the washed rice and chopped vegetables - Carrots, beans and cabbage.
1. You can also add peas if you wish.
1. Mix well until all the vegetables and rice gets coated with the masalas.
1. Add 2 cups of water and close the lid of the pressure cooker.
1. Increase the flame to high.
1. Put the weight of the cooker and let out 2 whistles and reduce the flame to low and cook it for 2 more minutes Turn off the flame and let the pressure release naturally.
1. One Pot Masala Vegetable Rice Recipe is ready.
1. Garnish with chopped coriander if you wish.
1. Serve this One Pot Masala Vegetables Rice Recipe along with a raita like Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita or a simple Tadka Raita Recipe for a sumptuous midweek dinner.
