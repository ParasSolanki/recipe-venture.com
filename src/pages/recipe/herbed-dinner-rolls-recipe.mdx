### Ingredients

- 1 Whole Egg - for an egg wash.
- 6 cups Vivatta Maida.
- 2 Whole Eggs - whisked with a fork.
- 1/4 cup Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - toasted.
- 1/4 cup Butter (unsalted) - softened.
- Fresh Herbs - small bunch.
- 1/2 cup Lukewarm Water.
- 1-1/2 cups Milk - lukewarm.
- 3-1/2 teaspoons Active dry yeast.
- 3 tablespoons Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Soft Sesame Herbed Dinner Rolls Recipe; in a large mixing bowl or the bowl of the stand mixer, combine all the dry ingredients; next all the wet ingredients and beat using the paddle attachment until the dough comes together.
1. Keep kneading until you notice all the ingredients get well combined.
1. Now place the dough on a floured surface and knead with a stretch and fold motion until smooth and elastic, about 10-12 minutes.
1. Place the dough in a buttered bowl.
1. Cover and allow it to rest and rise until doubles for about 2 hours.
1. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into 20 portions.
1. Shape each portion either into a ball or any other shape you want it to take.
1. Place them on greased baking sheets about two inches apart.
1. Cover the shaped portions and allow it to rest and rise until doubled for another half an hour.
1. Once risen, brush all the dough portions with egg yolk and sprinkle some sesame on the top.
1. Preheat the oven to 180 C and then bake the shaped and risen dough for about 25 minutes until golden brown.
1. Once the herbed sesame dinner rolls are baked, remove and place them on wire racks to prevent the bread from sweating.
1. You can serve them about 10 minutes after they have rested or cool them completely, wrap them in butter paper and store.
1. Serve Herbed Dinner Rolls Recipe with Light And Creamy Broccoli Soup Recipe and Grilled Mushroom Sandwich Recipe With Herbs.
