### Ingredients

-  1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder.
-  1/2 teaspoon Teaspoon celery.
-  1 teaspoon Red Chilli Powder.
-  1 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  1 Teaspoon Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 3 cups Paneer - cut into pieces.
-  1 cup tomato puree.
-  1/2 teaspoon royal cumin - compress.
-  1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds.
-  2 tablespoons cream.
-  1 teaspoon Kasuri Methi.
-  1/4 teaspoon Turmeric Powder.
-  2 teaspoons garlic - make a paste.
-  Oil - As per use.
-  1/2 cup onion - finely chopped.
-  Salt - as per taste.

### Instructions

1. To make Tawa Paneer Masala, first prepare sari ingredients.
1. Put the paneer in a bowl.
1. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, kasoori methi and salt.
1. Mix and keep aside for 20 minutes.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Fry the paneer on both sides.
1. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes and keep it aside.
1. Pour the remaining oil in a pan.
1. Add cumin and royal cumin to it.
1. Add onion after 15 seconds and cook till it becomes soft.
1. After it becomes soft, add garlic, chilli, coriander powder and mix.
1. Add tomato puree, celery and let it cook for 3 minutes.
1. Between the bitches After 3 minutes add paneer, cream, garam masala and mix.
1. Cook another 2 to 3 minutes.
1. After that turn off the gas and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve Tawa Paneer Masala along with Tawa Paratha and Spinach Raita for day khan.
