### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1/2 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - grated.
- 1/2 cup Green beans (French Beans) - finely chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - for frying.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Green Chilli - chopped (optional).
- 1 cup Hot water.
- 2 tablespoons Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Soy sauce.
- 1/2 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - chopped.
- 1 cup Red cabbage - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 2 tablespoon All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 2 teaspoon Ginger - chopped.
- 1 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- 2 tablespoons Tomato Ketchup.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mixed Vegetable Dry Manchurian recipe, we will firstly begin with making the manchurian balls.
1. Take all chopped veggies into a large bowl, add salt and pepper and mix it well.
1. Now add both flours and mix it well.
1. Heat the oil for frying before making balls.
1. Take small portion of veggie mixture and make roll out of it.
1. Similarly, make all the balls.
1. Carefully, place the balls into hot oil, cook it till golden brown crispy colour.
1. Cook it at medium heat, in very hot oil the balls become hard form outside and undercooked form inside.
1. Remove balls from oil and keep into some kitchen papers.
1. And let it be cool completely.
1. In a small bowl, take 1 cup water and mix 1 tbsp corn starch and dissolve it into water.
1. The next step is to make the manchurian gravy.
1. In skillet, heat the 2 tbsp oil; add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© on high flame.
1. Then add green chilli and again cook for half minutesNow, turn the gas on high flame, add green onion and just cook for 1 minute.
1. Add all the sauces and give nice stir, then add water mixture and stir well.
1. Once sauce started to thicken, switch off the gasAdd manchurian balls and keep stirring till balls absorb the sauces.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve Mixed Vegetable Dry Manchurian along with Spicy Schezuan Vegetarian Noodles for a weeknight dinner.
