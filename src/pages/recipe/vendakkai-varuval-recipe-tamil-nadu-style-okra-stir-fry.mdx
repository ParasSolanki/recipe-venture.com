### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoon Gram flour (besan).
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 500 grams Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra) - cut into one inch pieces.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vendakkai Varuval Recipe, chop the ladies finger and keep it in a bowl.
1. Into the same bowl, add all the masala powders including red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder and amchur powder along with besan and mix well.
1. Sprinkle some water so that it combines well.
1. You need coat the masala evenly over all the pieces.
1. Heat a flat skillet with oil, enough to fry the ladies finger.
1. Slowly add them into the hot oil and fry till they turn golden brown and crispy.
1. Skim the ladies finger onto a paper napkin and serve hot.
1. Serve the Vendakkai Varuval Recipe along with Tirunelveli Style Mor Kuzhambu Recipe, Steamed Rice, and Pisarna Manga by the side to create a healthy meal.
