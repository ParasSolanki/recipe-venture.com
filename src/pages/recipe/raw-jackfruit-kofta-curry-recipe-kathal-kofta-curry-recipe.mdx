### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - paste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 5 Tomato - chopped.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 2 tablespoons All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 3 teaspoon Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 4 teaspoon Sunflower Oil - as required or ghee.
- 500 grams Jackfruit Raw (Kathal) - cut into pieces.
- 2 teaspoon Raisins - chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Fresh cream - as required.
- 2 teaspoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 Onions - optional.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 cup Water.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Raw Jackfruit Kofta Curry Recipe, first clean the jackfruit pieces and then cook for up to 1-2 whistle in a cup of salted water and a cinnamon in a pressure cooker.
1. Switch off the stove and let the pressure release naturally.
1. Once fully cooked strain the water and keep the jackfruit aside to dry.
1. Take a blender, add boiled jackfruit with coconut and green chilli without adding water.
1. Blend it into a paste.
1. Once it is done, add the all purpose flour, salt, cardamom and cumin powder.
1. and mix everything well.
1. Mix everything well and keep it aside to cool down the jackfruit kofta mixture.
1. Now make small flat oval shaped balls with the mixture.
1. Heat a paniyaram pan with oil in each cavity and fry the kathal koftas from both the sides till they turn golden brown.
1. The next step is to make the kofta gravy.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add cumin seeds and asafoetida and saute for about 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add onions, salt and cook till they become soft and translucent.
1. Next add tomatoes, ginger paste cook for about 6 to 8 minutes until the tomatoes are soft.
1. Switch off the heat.
1. Once the tomato mixture is cooled down, blend this mixture with a hand blender to smooth gravy adding a cup of water.
1. Add this kofta gravy back in the wok and switch on the heat.
1. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, cook on medium to low till thick gravy is forming with bubbles on top.
1. Add garam masala powder, salt to taste and coriander leaves.
1. Let this kofta gravy cook for about 6 to 8 minutes.
1. Drop the jackfruit koftas in the gravy, let it soak for few minutes before serving.
1. Finally garnish the Raw Jackfruit Kofta Curry with fresh cream and chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve Raw Jackfruit Kofta Curry Recipe along with Palak Raita, Phulka and Satvik Carrot Sprout Salad for your weekday Indian Vegetarian meal.
