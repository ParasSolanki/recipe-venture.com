### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 2 tablespoon All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/2 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 4 Potato (Aloo).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Aloo Bajia, wash and peel the potatoes.
1.  Cut them into thin slices and immerse them in a bowl of  water till you prepare the batter for the bajia.
1. Heat the oil in a kadai on medium flame.
1. To make the bajji batterIn a mixing bowl, combine besan flour, maida, red chilli powder, ajwain and salt along with 1/3 cup of water.
1. Mix well to a make a smooth batter.
1.  Add a tablespoon of hot oil to this and mix it well.
1. This step will ensure the bajias are crisp.
1. Add few slices of potatoes to the batter and make sure the potatoes are covered with the batter.
1. Once the oil is hot, pick up each potato slice from the batter, remove excess batter and drop them carefully into the oil.
1. Fry them on both sides till it is golden in colour.
1. Drain them and put them on kitchen towel to remove excess oil.
1. Transfer the fried bajias to a serving plate and serve.
1. Serve the Aloo Bajjia along with Green Chutney and Masala Chai Recipe as a tea time snack.
