### Ingredients

- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 2 sprig Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens).
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Tofu - chopped finely.
- 5 Garlic - chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 2 Onions - sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 cup Sweet corn.
- 2 tablespoon Chilli vinegar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cantonese Style Dim Sum RecipeTo make the doughMix all the ingredients to make a dough for the wonton wrappers in a large mixing bowl.
1. Add enough water to get a soft but not a sticky dough consistency.
1. Once done cover it with a damp cloth so that it does not become dry.
1. To make the fillingThe next step is to heat a heavy bottomed pan with oil, add ginger, garlic, onion and corn.
1. Saute them nicely till it is all caramelized and cooked.
1. Later add in the Tofu along with soya sauce and chilli vinegar, give it a stir.
1. Check for seasoning by adding enough salt that is required.
1. Finally add the spring onions, toss it over and rest it aside to cool.
1. To make the cantonese style dimumsNow it is time to roll the wonton wrappers, divide the dough into small ball shaped balls, roll each ball on a board, the dough has to be rolled very thin making sure it does not tear.
1. Roll the dough into a circle shape.
1. Once done, in a small bowl keep some water ready to bind the wonton mixture, Take a teaspoon of the filling and place it in the center of the rolled dough, dab some water on the edges of the dough.
1. Fold the wonton wrapper into half, from one end start making small folds and pinch it in order to seal it tightly.
1. Heat a vegetable gas steamer by adding one cup of water below, apply some oil on the plate where the dumpling are kept, once the water starts to boil, place all the dumplings and cover them.
1. Let it cook for 10 minutes.
1. Serve the Cantonese Style Dim Sum Recipe with Cantonese Style Pan Fried Noodles.
