### Ingredients

- 1/4 teaspoon Ghee.
- Cumin powder (Jeera) - a pinch.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a few chopped (8 months onwards).
- 1/2 cup Water.
- Salt - a pinch.
- Asafoetida (hing) - a pinch.
- 1/4 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- Turmeric powder (Haldi) - a pinch.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Moong Dal Soup or Moong Pani For Babies and Toddlers Recipe, pressure cook the Yellow Moong dal , along with a pinch of salt and half cup of water in a pressure cooker for 3 to 4 whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Once the pressure is released, add it to a mixer grinder and make a smooth puree.
1. The next step is to make the tadka for the Moong Dal Pani/ Soup.
1. Heat ghee in a pan over medium heat.
1. Add the asafoetida, turmeric powder and cumin powder and stir for a few seconds.
1. Pour the pureed dal into the ghee tadka and give it a quick boil.
1. Adjust the consistency of the soup by adding water if required and turn off the heat.
1. Serve Moong Dal Paani/Soup to your baby/toddler from 6 months onwards as a lunch meal or a dinner meal.
1. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve/feed it to your toddlers after 8 months so that they can understand the texture and taste of coriander leaves as well.
