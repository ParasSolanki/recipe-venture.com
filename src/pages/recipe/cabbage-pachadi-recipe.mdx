### Ingredients

-  2 teaspoons oil.
-  2 cups cabbage - chopped.
-  asafetida - a pinch.
-  3 green chillies - chopped.
-  1 tablespoon roasted chana dal.
-  10 curry leaves.
-  salt - 1/2 tablespoon.
-  as per taste Tamarind paste.
-  2 dry red chillies.
- 2 tablespoons til oil.
-  1/2 tablespoon mustard.
-  1/2 cup water.
-  1 tablespoon white urad dal.

### Instructions

1. To make the cabbage cabbage, first heat the oil in a pan.
1. Add urad dal and chana dal to it.
1. Let it cook till it turns light brown.
1. Now add green chillies and curry leaves.
1. Add cabbage and salt in it after 15 seconds.
1. Allow to cook for 8 to 10 minutes and then turn off the gas.
1. After the cabbage mixture cools down, add it to the mixer grinder with tamarind and some water.
1. Grind and take out in a bowl.
1. Now for the tempering, heat the oil in a tadka pan.
1. Add mustard seeds and let it cook for 10 seconds.
1. Add asafetida and mix.
1. Add this tempering to the chutney and mix.
1. Serve the cabbage of paneer with onion uttapam, ghee dosa or masala dosa.
