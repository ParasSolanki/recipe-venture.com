### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- Water - as required.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Fresh green chickpeas.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - cubed.
- 3/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 2 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1 Onion - paste.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lilve Ki Sabzi recipe, firstly heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan and add bay leaf, cloves and elaichi.
1. Saute them for about 15 seconds and add the finely chopped onions.
1. Cook them till they turns soft and translucent.
1. Add the cubed potatoes and cook for another 5 minutes or until they become soft.
1. Next, add in the ginger garlic paste and onion paste.
1. Cook it for 2 to 3 minutes and then add all the masalas including red chilli powder, turmeric powder and coriander powder.
1. Cook it for 2 minutes.
1. Next add curd and the required salt.
1. When the gravy starts leaving the oil from the sides, add the green chickpeas.
1. After 5 minutes, add the required water, mix properly and cover the lid.
1. Let it cook till the gravy thickens and the green chickpeas are cooked.
1. Once it is done, garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
1. Serve Lilve Ki Sabzi with hot steamed rice and rotis for a weekend meal.
