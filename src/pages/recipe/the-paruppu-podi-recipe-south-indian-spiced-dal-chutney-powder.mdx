### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 10 Curry leaves.
- 6 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 1/4 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - roasted.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Paruppu Podi recipe, heat a heavy bottomed pan on medium heat and roast all the ingredients except roasted bengal gram dal and salt, till they turn golden brown color and aroma arises.
1. Once it is done, turn off the heat and transfer to a paper towel to cool completely.
1. Next blend all the ingredients for the Paruppu Podi including the roasted Bengal gram to a fine powder.
1. The Paruppu Podi is now ready to use.
1. Store it in an airtight container.
1. Serve this Paruppu Podi | Lentil Powder with hot white rice and ghee or gingelly oil and sutta appalam (roasted papad) on side.
