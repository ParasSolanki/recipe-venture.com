### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Dried Thyme Leaves.
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Dried oregano.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped onion.
- 2 teaspoons Garlic powder.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a few sprigs finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Onion powder.
- 4 Potatoes (Aloo) - washed.
- 2 teaspoons Paprika powder.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cajun Style Potatoes Recipe, preheat the Oven to 220 degree celsius.
1. Add the ingredients listed for the cajun spice mix into a mixer grinder like the garlic powder, onion powder, paprika powder, dried thyme leaves, dried oregano and salt and pulse to mix all the powders well together.
1. Mix oil and the spice mix in a small bowl.
1. Smear the spice mix with the help of a pastry brush on all the potatoes wedges.
1. Keep aside for 15 minutes to marinate and then arrange them neatly in a greased ovenproof dish/sheet.
1. Bake till the potato wedges are brown and crispy on all sides.
1. This will take about 30-35 minutes.
1. Turn the potato wedges after the first fifteen minutes of the cook to help the cajun potatoes cook evenly on all sides.
1. Serve hot Cajun Style Potatoes Recipe immediately topped with onion and coriander leaves.
1. Serve Cajun Style Potatoes as a tea time with tomato sauce or Tzatziki Recipe - A Greek Yogurt Dip and Irani Chai Recipe.
