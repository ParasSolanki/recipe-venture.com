### Ingredients

- 1 pinch Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Whole Egg.
- 1 cup Rice puttu - or wheat puttu.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black Urad Dal (Split).
- 2 Green Chilli - slit/cut.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Kerala Style Puttu Upma Recipe With Scrambled Eggs, heat oil in a kadai on medium heat, add the mustard seeds and allow them to crackle, next add the cumin seeds and allow them to sizzle.
1. To this add urad dal and saute till it turns into a light brown colour.
1. Now add green chilli, curry leaves, turmeric powder and crack open the egg and scramble it for about half a minute.
1. Once the eggs are nicely scrambled, add the crumbled puttu.
1. Add salt and mix well.
1. Sprinkle some water and mix well.
1. Close with a lid and cook on low flame for a minute.
1. This will make the puttu softer.
1. Serve Kerala Style Puttu Upma Recipe With Scrambled Eggs along with a Cocoa Banana Almond Date Smoothie Recipe for a wholesome breakfast.
