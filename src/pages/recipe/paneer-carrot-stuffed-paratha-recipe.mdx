### Ingredients

- Ghee - or oil for cooking.
- Water - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/4 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina) - small bunch.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese).
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 2 cups Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 cup Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Paneer Carrot Stuffed Paratha, we will first make the dough.
1. Add in the flour and a pinch of salt into a large bowl.
1. Add a little water at a time and knead to make a firm yet smooth dough.
1. Knead well for about 3 to 4 minutes, this will make the parathas soft.
1. Drizzle a teaspoon of oil over the dough and knead for another couple of minutes.
1. Cover and keep aside till we make the Paneer Carrot filling for the paratha.
1. Next, we will combine the paneer, grated carrots,onions, green chillies, mint leaves, cumin powder and salt.
1. Stir to combine the ingredients well.
1. We fill be using raw carrots as a stuffing as they will get cooked while cooking the parathas.
1. Now to begin making the Paneer Carrot Stuffed Paratha, keep some flour aside for dusting.
1. Divide the dough into large lemon side balls.
1. Dust the dough portions in flour, flatten it and roll it into a 3 inch diameter circle.
1. Place the Paneer Carrot filling on half side of the circle and close the paratha by folding it with the other half.
1. Yes its as simple as that.
1. We will have a semi-circle oval shape.
1. Press the sides of the paratha down to seal the edges and gently using the rolling pin  flatten it.
1. Take care, the filling can ooze out if the dough is rolled out too thin.
1. Now to cook the Paneer Carrot Stuffed Paratha, first preheat the skillet on medium heat.
1. Place the rolled paneer carrot paratha on top and allow it to cook on one side.
1. After about 10 to 15 seconds, flip the paratha.
1. At this stage, smear some oil or ghee to cook the paratha.
1. Cook the paratha by flipping over a couple of times until you see golden brown spots on both sides.
1. Proceed the same way with the remaining dough portions.
1. Serve the Paneer Carrot Stuffed Paratha Recipe with sweet lime pickle or Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita.
