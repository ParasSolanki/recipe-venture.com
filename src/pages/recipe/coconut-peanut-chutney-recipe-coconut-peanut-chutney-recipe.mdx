### Ingredients

-  2 sprigs of green coriander - chopped.
- 1 cup coconut - grated.
-  2-4 curry leaves.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  2 green chillies - chopped.
-  1/4 cup peanuts - roasted.

### Instructions

1. To make Coconut Peanut Chutney Recipe, add coconut, green chillies, coriander, curry leaves, peanuts, salt and grind it well in a mixer grinder.
1. Remove and serve in a bowl after grinding.
1. Serve Coconut Peanut Chutney Recipe with Ghee Roast Dosa and Tomato Onion Sambar for breakfast.
