### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 300 grams Tinda (Apple Gourd) - peeled and cut into wedges.
- 1 tablespoon Jaggery.
- 1 teaspoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves) - for garnish.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Khatti Meethi Tinda Masala Recipe, firstly first prepare all the ingredients and keep them ready by the side.
1. Heat oil in the pressure cooker, add the ajwain seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Add the ginger and tomatoes and saute until the tomatoes soften.
1. Add the cut tinda (apple gourd), and the remaining ingredients including green chillies, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, amchur, jaggery and salt except the kasuri methi.
1. Add 1/4 cup of water, cover the pressure cooker, cook for three whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Once the pressure releases, open the cooker and stir in the kasuri methi.
1. Check the salt and seasonings and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Transfer to a serving bowl and serve warm.
1. Serve the Khatti Meethi Tinda Masala along with Methi Thepla, Boondi Kadhi and Mango Pickle for a cosy sunday lunch or weeknight dinner.
