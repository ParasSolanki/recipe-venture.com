### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- 1 cup Kala Chana (Brown Chickpeas) - soaked overnight and  cooked.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 3-4 Sun Dried Tomatoes - (optional).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 2 tablespoons Tahini.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 8-10 Button mushrooms - chopped finely.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - as needed.
- 1/2 cup Onions - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1/2 Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Byadagi Dried Chillies.
- 1/2 Black pepper powder.
- 2 Green zucchini.
- 1/2 cup Kala Chana (Brown Chickpeas) - overnight soaked & cooked.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- 2 tablespoons Parsley leaves.
- 1/2 cup Tomatoes - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To cook these Zucchini Boats With Chickpeas In Tomato Hummus Recipe, get prep with all the ingredients including soaking of chickpeas overnight and pressure cooking them until done.
1. Take 1/2 cup cooked chickpeas and drain out excess water using a towel.
1. In a mixing bowl, add chickpeas, lemon juice, salt, chilli powder and 1 tablespoon of oil and leave it on the counter for half an hour to marinate well.
1. Bake this in a 150 degrees Celsius preheated Oven for 45 minutes by stirring in between to ensure an even bake.
1. Set this aside.
1. In a pan, heat a teaspoon of oil, add cumin and red chillies and fry until fragrant.
1. Take it out onto a plate.
1. Now add some more oil and add 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes and cook it until it is reduced and water evaporated.
1. Let this cool down to room temperature.
1. In a food processor/ blender add all the ingredients for the spicy tomato hummus except the olive oil and chickpeas.
1. Once the other ingredients are blended, add chickpeas and olive oil and blend until smooth.
1. Slit the zucchinis into half lengthwise and scoop out the flesh in the middle to create a cavity.
1. Preheat the oven to 170 degrees Celsius and bake the zucchinis (cut side facing upwards) with a drizzle of oil for 15 minutes and rest it on the kitchen counter.
1. Prepare all the remaining ingredients and cutting the vegetables.
1. Also cut the scooped out zucchini into tiny pieces and set aside.
1. In a pan heat some oil add garlic, onions and a pinch of salt and cook for a minute, now add chopped tomatoes and let it soften.
1. Add the chopped mushrooms and chopped zucchini from the boats.
1. Let this cook until everything softens and comes together.
1. Season with oregano, parsley, chilli flakes, pepper and salt.
1. Let this mixture cool down.
1. Now fill each boat with hummus, and the saut√©ed veggies and bake for a further 8-10 minutes on 170 degrees Celsius.
1. Serve Zucchini Boats With Chickpeas In Tomato Hummus Recipe topped with crunchy baked chickpeas along with Watermelon Margarita and Bruschetta with bell peppers and mint coriander recipe to welcome your guests, or during snack time.
