### Ingredients

- 1-1/2 cups Water.
- 2 tablespoons Orange liquor - or any alcohol of choice (optional).
- 1/2 cup Sugar.
- 1 Pineapple - chopped/sliced (2 cups).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Preserved Pineapple Recipe, in a saucepan, start boiling water.
1. Cut the pineapples required as well.
1. Add sugar to it such that it dissolves when the water boils.
1. Bring the water-sugar mixture to a rolling boil, and add the Pineapple chunks.
1. Stir well and bring to another boil.
1. (You can also place all the three ingredients in a vessel and place inside a pressure cooker and cook for 1 whistle)Take off heat and leave to cool for 15 minutes.
1. Prepare a clean glass bottle by wiping it thoroughly with a clean cloth or kitchen towel.
1. Add the liquor at this stage if using.
1. Add liquor to the Pineapple slices and mix well.
1. Ladle the Pineapple slices with the syrup into the bottle and cover the container with a clean kitchen towel till it comes to room temperature.
1. Put the lid on once the pineapple comes to room temperature and refrigerate this!Use Preserved Pineapple Recipe as required.
1. You can also wash off pineapple under running water to drain excess sugar and use them as well, when making savoury dishes.
