### Ingredients

- 1 Kiwi - sliced into roundels.
- 10 Apricots.
- 7 Dates - chopped and deseeded.
- 5 tablespoons Anardana Powder (Pomegranate Seed Powder).
- 250 grams Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - (drain up the whey).
- 1/2 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/4 cup Walnuts - chopped.
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 1/4 cup Badam (Almond) - chopped.
- 1-1/2 cup Honey.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 3 cups Instant Oats (Oatmeal).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the 8 Ingredient Sugar Free Granola Parfait recipe, mix up the olive oil and honey in a mixing bowl.
1. Stir them up.
1. Preheat Oven at 170 Celsius for 10 minutes.
1. Take a bowl, add rolled oats, chopped walnuts and almonds giving it a nice mix.
1. Add cinnamon and salt and stir to combine.
1. Pour in olive oil and honey to the rolled oats mixture.
1. Give it thorough stir to combine.
1. Brush up olive oil to the baking tray and spread up the Granola evenly to avoid any crumbles.
1. Stir in Granola every 10 minutes and bake them for 25 to 30 minutes.
1. After 20 minutes mix chopped Apricots and Dates into the granola.
1. Lookout for Golden brown colour and let it cool.
1. For making parfait, take the short glasses and layer them with granola in both the glasses.
1. Stockup the greek yoghurt leaving place for Granola and pile up the fresh fruits on it.
1. Serve 8 Ingredient Sugar Free Granola Parfait on its own for a healthy and filling breakfast or have it  along with a glass of Plum Ginger Juice Recipe and Muskmelon (Kharbuj) Juice Recipe.
