### Ingredients

- 12 Black olives - sliced.
- 1/2 Watermelon - deseeded & cubed.
- 12 Spinach Leaves (Palak) - (select baby leaves).
- Chaat Masala Powder - to taste.
- 100 grams Feta Cheese - cubed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Watermelon & Feta Skewers Recipe, take a baby spinach leaf, place a watermelon cube on it, feta cheese cube and a sliced black olive and insert a skewer/toothpick through.
1. Repeat the process to make all the skewers.
1. Finally, sprinkle Chaat Masala powder, once all the bites are prepared and serve Watermelon & Feta Skewers Recipe immediately for snack or as a party appetizer.
1. Serve it as an entree to the evening party with skewers spread on a platter with other assorted cheese bites like Pineapple and cheese skewers or Fruit Kebabs and the audience of all ages are sure to love them.
