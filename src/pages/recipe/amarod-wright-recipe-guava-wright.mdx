### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 Dry Red Chilli.
- 2 cups Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - a pinch.
- 1 Guava.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Guava Raita Recipe, chop the ripe guava into small pieces and add it into a bowl filled with curd.
1. Sprinkle little salt and mix well.
1. Heat a tempering pan with oil, add mustard seeds and urad dal and allow it sizzle.
1. Add curry leaves and dry red chillies and leave it to crackle.
1. Sprinkle hing and pour it on top of the raita and serve.
1. Serve the Guava Raita Recipe along with Cabbage Paneer Parathas Recipe ,Gobi Biryani (Spiced Cauliflower Rice) to complete your meal.
