### Ingredients

-  2 teaspoons oil - as per use.
-  2 bud garlic - finely chopped.
- 200 grams Paneer - cut.
-  1 onion - finely chopped.
-  2 chakundar - peel and chop.
-  1 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  1 teaspoon turmeric.
-  1 inch ginger - tighten.
-  3/4 cup coconut milk.
-  red chili powder - as per taste.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  2 green chillies - cut straight.
-  1 teaspoon garam masala powder.

### Instructions

1. To make Chakundar Paneer Curry, first of all, add the Chakundar, Onion, Ginger, Garlic, Green Chilli in a pressure cooker with 1 cup of water, turmeric powder and salt.
1. Cook until the city comes, reduce the heat and 3 to 4 minutes And cook.
1. Turn off the gas and let the pressure drain automatically.
1. After the pressure comes out, add coconut milk, paneer, coriander powder, garam masala powder and let it boil.
1. After boiling, turn off the gas and take it out in a bowl.
1. Serve the Chakundar Paneer Curry with Spinach Raita and Rice / Tawa Paratha for dinner.
