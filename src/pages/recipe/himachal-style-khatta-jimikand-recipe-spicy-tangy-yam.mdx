### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Kala jeera.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds).
- 2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- Mustard oil - for cooking.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 300 grams Elephant yam (Suran/Senai/Ratalu) - chopped small.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1/2 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Himachal Style Khatta Jimikand Recipe, heat a pressure cooker with oil, add all the seeds that are mentioned above including cumin seeds, kalonji, methi seeds, kala jeera and mustard seeds.
1. Allow it to crackle for few seconds.
1. Add in curry leaves and leave to splutter for few seconds.
1. Add ginger and garlic and saute until they soften.
1. Add in onions and cook until they turn golden brown.
1. Add chopped yam along with all the spice powders and salt to taste and give it a stir.
1. Add about 1 cup of water and pressure cook the yam for atleast 3 whistle until it becomes soft.
1. Once done switch off the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally and bring the cooker back on the heat.
1. Open the lid and keep sauteing until the water has all evaporated and the subzi thickens.
1. Serve the Himachal Style Khatta Jimikand Recipe along with Kadhi, Bhakri and Cauliflower & Cucumber Raita by the side for a delicious meal.
