### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut.
- 2 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 4 tablespoon Boiled Peanuts.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Small Brinjal (Baingan / Eggplant) - cut into quarters.
- 2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1/2 cup Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kathirikai Paruppu Kootu Recipe, heat a skillet with oil, add coriander seeds, urad dal and chana dal and allow it sizzle for few seconds.
1. Add peppercorns, dry red chillies and coconut and keep frying them until the coconut turn light brown.
1. Once done, allow it to cool down and grind it into a smooth paste by adding some water while grinding.
1. Heat a pressure cooker with oil, add mustard seeds and curry leaves and allow it crackle for few seconds.
1. Add brinjal and sprinkle with salt and turmeric powder and saute for 2 minutes.
1. Add tamarind water and jaggery and allow the brinjal to cook for at least 5 minutes.
1. Add ground paste later along with whole peanuts and soaked toor dal, add salt to taste, cover it with the lid and pressure cook for 3 whistles.
1. Allow the pressure to release naturally, open the cooker, and transfer to a serving platter.
1. Serve the Kathirikai Paruppu Kootu Recipe along with Moong Dal Rasam/Pesara Pappu Charu, steamed rice and Carrot Poriyal Recipe to make it a complete meal.
