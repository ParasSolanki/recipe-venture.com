### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 cup Muesli.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1/4 cup Milk.
- 1 Whole Egg.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 1/2 cup Jaggery - crumbled.
- 200 grams Strawberries - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Chewy Strawberry Muesli Oats Cookie Recipe by pre heating the oven to 180 degree Celsius for 10 minutes.
1. Then In a bowl add in all the ingredients and mix it well make it a slight wet dough so you can hold it and shape them into cookies.
1. Grease the tray with oil, and place the cookies with an inch apart from each other.
1. Once done Refrigerate it for about 10 minutes.
1. Then bake it in the oven for about 25 minutes till it crisps up.
1. Once baked take them out and grease it with little honey over it and serve.
