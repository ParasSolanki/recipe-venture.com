### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Ginger - minced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Tamarind Paste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - for garnish.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 kg Chicken.
- 4 Green Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Poppy seeds - roasted.
- 2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - minced.
- 1 cup Onion - chopped.
- 4 Cloves (Laung).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Coorg Koli Curry Recipe, marinate the chicken in red chilli powder and turmeric powder and let it sit in the fridge for 30 minutes minimum.
1. Take a  mixer jar and add coriander seeds, cumin seeds, cinnamon, cloves ginger, garlic, green chillies and roasted poppy seeds.
1. Grind them all together, add some water to it.
1. Once done, take a large pan and heat cooking oil on medium flame.
1. Once the oil is hot enough, add the chopped onions and fry till golden brown, on a medium heat.
1. Now turn the heat to low, and add the masala paste which you ground earlier into the pan, stir till the raw smell disappears.
1. Now add the chicken pieces into it, and required salt to taste and cook for about 7-8 minutes on low heat.
1. Once the chicken looks well coated with spices, then add some water.
1. Cover it with a lid and cook the chicken on a low heat for about 15 to 20 minutes or till it gets tender.
1. Once the chicken is cooked, add tamarind extract and cook for 5 more minutes.
1. Switch off and serve it hot.
1. Serve Coorg Koli Curry Recipe Coorgi Akki Roti or Ari Pathiri Recipe for a complete comforting meal.
