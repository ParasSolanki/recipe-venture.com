### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 Onion - finely sliced.
- 3/4 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 4 Green Chillies - slit.
- Asafoetida (hing) - A pinch.
- 1/2 cup Green Moong Dal (Split) - soaked for 3 hours.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 2 cups Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves) - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Tomatoes - finely sliced.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Moong Dal Methi Ki Sabzi, heat oil in a kadhai and add cumin seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Add green chillies and hing and let it cook for a few seconds.
1. After a few seconds, add onion and fry until it becomes soft and pinkish.
1. Add ginger, sliced tomato and cook on high flame for 2 minutes or until the tomatoes get soft.
1. Add soaked and drained moong dal, turmeric, coriander powder and sauté well for a minute.
1. Add half cup of water and salt and mix everything properly.
1. Cover and cook the dal for 7 to 8 minutes or until the dal is cooked well and the water is dried up (Do not overcook or it will get mushy).
1. Add methi and sauté on high heat for 2 to 3 minutes (Do not cover and cook).
1. After 2 to 3 minutes, it is ready to be served.
1. Serve Methi Moong Dal Sabzi with phulkas, jeera rice and Rajasthani dal for a delicious meal.
