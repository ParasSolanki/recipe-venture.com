### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Peanut Butter - or any other nut butter (optional).
- 1 tablespoon Honey - or maple syrup.
- 1/2 Orange - juiced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 cup Ripe Bananas - mashed.
- 1/2 cup Blackberries - or any other berries.
- 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 cup Coconut milk - or regular milk.
- 1/2 cup Poha (Flattened rice).
- 1/2 Orange - segmented fruit.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Avalakki Porridge In Warm Spiced Berries recipe, wash Avalakki properly with water using a colander.
1. In a saucepan bring milk to a gentle boil, add mashed banana and avalakki and cook till soft.
1. Donâ€™t worry if it goes mushy, it will only add a creamy texture.
1. In another saucepan bring orange juice, berries and few orange segments to a boil and cook until all the berries have mushed up and soft add honey, vanilla and pepper powder.
1. Mix well and turn of the heat.
1. Serve the porridge with warm berry-orange mixture, remaining segmented oranges and nut butter for a healthy breakfast.
1. Serve Avalakki Porridge in Warm Spiced Berries on its own for a healthy and wholesome breakfast.
