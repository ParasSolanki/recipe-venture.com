### Ingredients

- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing) - (a generous pinch).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Curry leaves - a few.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a small bunch.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 cup White Urad Dal (Split).
- 4 Green Chillies - slit (adjust to taste).
- Lemon juice - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Ghee.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kathiyawadi Adad Dal Recipe, wash urad dal and add in a pressure cooker, along with about 4 cups of water and turmeric.
1. Pressure cook on medium high heat for a whistle or two, till the dal is almost mushy.
1. Get prep with all other ingredients as well, while the dal is cooking.
1. Once the dal is pressure cooked, allow the cooker to release pressure by itself.
1. Once the pressure in the cooker is released, open the lid and mash the dal with a masher/ladle/hand blender.
1. Keep this aside.
1. In a saucepan, heat cooking oil/ghee on a medium heat.
1. Once hot, add the mustard seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Once they crackle, add the cumin seeds and allow them to sizzle.
1. Immediately add in the hing (asafoetida) and curry leaves and allow the curry leaves to splutter for a couple of seconds.
1. To the saucepan, add ginger garlic paste, and green chillies, fry for about 1-2 minutes on medium heat or till teh raw smell disappears.
1. Add chopped tomatoes, red chilli powder , salt and saute till tomatoes are mushy.
1. Now add the mashed urad dal, remaining salt and turn the heat to high.
1. Bring to a rolling boil.
1. Once everything is combined well, switch off heat, add lemon juice and coriander leaves .
1. Stir well to combine.
1. Serve Kathiyawadi Adad Dal Recipe with Whole Wheat Palak Naan Recipe, Gujarati Lasaniya Batata Recipe  to make a complete weekday dinner menu.
