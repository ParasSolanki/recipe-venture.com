### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Nutmeg powder - (grated nutmeg).
- Dried rose petals - few (optional).
- 2 tablespoon Paprika powder.
- 10 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 teaspoon Star anise - (grated).
- 1 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 10 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mace powder - (or grated mace).
- 1 tablespoon Dry ginger powder.
- 1 tablespoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).

### Instructions

1. To make the Ras El Hanout Recipe (Moroccan Spice Powder), get prepped with all the spices required.
1. Heat a kadai and dry roast every spice till aromatic, separately, with care not to brown or burn any of them.
1. We just want to warm the spices so it releases its amazing fragrant oil from it.
1. Remove from pan immediately and allow each spice to cool.
1. Now into a good blends add all the ingredients roasted along with the other ingredients from the list and blend into powder in a mixer grinder.
1. Store the Ras El Hanout Recipe (Moroccan Spice Powder) spice blend in a clean, dry, airtight container and use as needed.
