### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - coarsely grated.
- 1 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina) - finely chopped.
- 1 Beetroot - coarsely grated.
- 1 1/2 tablespoon Ginger - grated.
- 2 tablespoons Tahini.
- 1/2 cup Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - pounded.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard sauce.
- 1/2 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - shredded.
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 2 cups Red cabbage - shredded.
- 1 teaspoon Tabasco Original - Hot Sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Cabbage Slaw with Ginger Tahini Dressing Recipe by prepping all the ingredients and cut vegetables and keep it ready in a mixing bowl.
1. In another bowl mix all the ingredients for the dressing and whisk well till it combines.
1. Add this dressing over the vegetables and toss it well.
1. Sprinkle the sesame seeds.
1. Serve the Cabbage Slaw with Ginger Tahini Dressing Recipe as an Appetizer for your Sunday Dinner meal.
