### Ingredients

- 300 grams Boneless chicken.
- Parmesan cheese.
- 1 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Parsley leaves - chopped.
- 1 cup Mozzarella cheese - shredded.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 10 Lasagna sheets - ones that need no pre-cooking.
- 1 cup Tomato Basil Pasta Sauce.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Paprika powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken Lasagna recipe, wash and clean chicken well.
1. Add pepper powder, lime juice, salt and paprika powder, mix well in a mixing bowl.
1. Cover and let the chicken marinate for about 1 to 2 hours.
1. Later add olive oil into a pan.
1. And saut√© the marinated chicken pieces on medium flame until cooked well.
1. This will take about 15 minutes approximately.
1. Preheat the oven to 170 degree centigrade.
1. Grease a 9-by-13-inch baking dish with butter well.
1. Place a layer of cooked lasagna sheet in one layer.
1. Add some tomato pasta sauce, sprinkle cheese, layer chicken pieces on top, and sprinkle some more cheese on top of the chicken and some finely chopped parsley as well.
1. Repeat the same step and keep layering until you reach the top of the pan.
1. Bake the lasagna pasta in preheated oven for about 7 to 12 minutes or until cheese melts and top is slightly browned.
1. Let the lasagna rest for about 10 minutes, then scatter the sliced parsley leaves on top, cut into squares and serve.
1. Serve Chicken Lasagna on its own with some Fennel Pesto Pull-Apart Bread Recipe and a glass of wine for a perfect weekend dinner.
