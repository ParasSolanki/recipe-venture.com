### Ingredients

-  1 tsp fennel.
-  3 long.
-  1 star anis.
-  1 tsp khus khus.
- 2 tbsp coriander seeds.
-  1/2 inch mace.
-  1 tsp cumin seeds.
-  1 inch cinnamon.
-  2 cardamom.
-  1/2 tsp celery.
-  6 dry red chillies.
-  6 whole black peppercorns.
-  1 / 4 tsp fenugreek seeds.

### Instructions

1. To make Chettinad Masala Powder recipe, firstly put coriander seeds, cumin, dry red chillies, celery, star anis, cardamom, cinnamon, long, fennel, black pepper, fenugreek seeds, khus khus and mace in a pan.
1. 8 to 8 Roast for 10 minutes.
1. Turn off the flame and let it cool down.
1. Now put it in a mixer grinder and grind it.
1. Pour and store this powder in an air tight container.
1. You can put the Chettinad Masala Powder recipe in an air tight container and store it for 3 to 4 months.
