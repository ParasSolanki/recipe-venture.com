### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoons Jaggery - grated.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 3 Ripe Bananas - cut into thick roundels.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Karwar Style Kelya Sasav Recipe-Ripened Banana Relish by grinding the coconut, jaggery and dry red chillies in a hand blender to a coarse paste.
1. Mix it along with the sliced banana and toss so that it is mixed thoroughly.
1. Serve the Karwar Style Kelya Sasav Recipe-Ripened Banana Relish as a snack after school or even serve it as a side dish along with Karwar Style Kalingana Polo Recipe - Watermelon Rind Dosa as a sweet Breakfast.
