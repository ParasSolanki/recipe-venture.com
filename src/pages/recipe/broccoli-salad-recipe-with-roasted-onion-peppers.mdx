### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - diced.
- 2 teaspoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - cut lengthwise.
- 1 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 1/4 cup Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - cut lengthwise.
- 1 cup Broccoli.
- 2 teaspoons Veeba Harissa Sauce.
- 1 teaspoon Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Broccoli Salad Recipe with Roasted Onion & Peppers, cut the vegetables and make the harissa sauce and keep it ready.
1. You can either use store bought harissa sauce or make it home, using this recipe Homemade Harissa Paste RecipeHeat a wok on high heat, add in the broccoli, onions yellow and red bell peppers.
1. Cook them on high heat and stir continuously for 5-7 minutes until lightly tender.
1. We want the vegetables to be cooked yet crunchy.
1. Turn off the flame.
1. Transfer the Broccoli and Roasted Onion & Peppers to a mixing bowl and set aside.
1. For the DressingIn a small mixing bowl, combine the harissa sauce, brown sugar, vinegar, olive oil and salt.
1. Mix well.
1. Pout this dressing over the Broccoli and Roasted Onion & Peppers and serve at room temperature.
1. Serve this Broccoli and Roasted Onion & Peppers Recipe along with Chicken And Cheese Sandwich With Avocado Recipe and Blueberry and Lemon Iced Tea Recipe for a  complete meal.
