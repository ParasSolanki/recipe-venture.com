### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Sugar - powdered.
- 2 cups Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 4 Apples - cored.
- 1-1/2 teaspoons Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1/4 cup Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 1 teaspoon Nutmeg powder.
- 1/4 cup Butter.
- 1/4 Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 2 teaspoons Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 2 tablespoons Butter.
- 2 teaspoons Sprig Extract of Natural Bourbon Vanilla - a few drops.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spiced Apple Crumble Recipe with Greek Yogurt, we need to make each of the elements and keep them ready.
1. To make the Apple Pie FillingHeat a saucepan on low heat with some butter, add the apple pieces, brown sugar and cinnamon powder and cook on medium heat for 4-6 minutes until the apples are cooked but still have a crunch.
1. Turn off the flame, transfer the cooked apple stew into a bowl and set aside.
1. To make the Greek yogurt In a mixing bowl, combine the thick hung yogurt, along with the vanilla extract and powdered sugar.
1. Whisk until smooth.
1. Allow it to chill in the refrigerator.
1. To make the Spiced crumble Preheat an oven at 200 degree celsius.
1. In a mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour, instant oats, brown sugar, cinnamon powder, nutmeg powder and mix well.
1. To this bowl add the butter and gradually start combining the butter with your fingers, to get a sand like texture.
1. Once you have an evenly distributed mixture, place parchment paper on a baking tray and transfer this crumble mixture on to it.
1. Bake in the preheated oven for 7-10 minutes at 200 degree celsius.
1. Now that it has baked and become slightly crisp, remove and allow it to cool.
1. To assemble the Spiced Apple CrumbleYou can choose to assemble your dessert in a shot glass or a dessert bowl or any other container of your choice.
1. Keep the apple pie filling, greek yogurt, and spiced crumble ready.
1. In a shot glass, put a tablespoon of the apple pie filling, next add a layer of a tablespoon of greek yogurt and repeat with a layer of apple stew, greek yogurt and apple stew again.
1. Your layers should be like this- apple stew- greek yogurt-apple stew-greek yogurt- apple stew and finally top this dessert with the spiced crumble.
1. Serve Spiced Apple Crumble Recipe with Greek Yogurt as a dessert after a meal of Watermelon And Cucumber Gazpacho Recipe, Mozzarella Basil Sandwiches Recipe, and Baked Potato Wedge Recipe.
