### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Baking soda.
- Water - as required.
- 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal) - roasted.
- 1 cup Green Moong Dal (Whole).
- 4 tablespoon Green Moong Sprouts.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 cup White Urad Dal (Whole).

### Instructions

1. We begin making Oats and Green Moong Dal Dahi Vada by soaking the black moong dal and urad dal in water for about 8 hours or overnight.
1. Once that is done grind both dals in the mixer to a smooth paste.
1. You can add the roasted oats to the batter and leave it to rest till you make the dahi mixture.
1. Mix the curd/dahi and water in a bowl, add sugar and whisk it well to a smooth consistency.
1. Leave it in the refrigerator.
1. Next to fry the vada, heat a Kuzhi Paniyaram Pan, take out the batter add salt and baking soda to it and mix well.
1. Add oil and drop in a tablespoon of the dahi vada batter in each of the holes and cover.
1. Let it cook for about 2 minutes and flip it over.
1. Then cook again for about 2 more minutes till it crisps up.
1. Once done take them out and drop it in the dahi mixture and refrigerate for about 20 minutes before serving.
