### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 2 cups Cauliflower (gobi) - cut in florets.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Butter.
- 5 cloves Garlic.
- 1 cup Milk.
- 1/2 cup Water.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 Whole Black Pepper Corns - crushed.
- 1/2 cup Celery - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Roasted Cauliflower Soup Recipe, heat a pan with olive oil on medium flame.
1. Into the pan, add the garlic cloves and celery and saute for about a minute, until the aroma rises.
1. Now add the cauliflower, sprinkle salt and pan roast the cauliflower for 4-6 minutes on high heat.
1. Now sprinkle some water, cover and continue to cook for 5 more minutes.
1. Turn off the heat, and allow it to cool down to room temperature.
1. Transfer to a mixer jar and grind to a smooth puree with some water.
1. Heat a pan with a teaspoon of butter, add the cauliflower puree and bring it to a single boil.
1. Now add the water, milk and black pepper powder and bring the soup to a rolling boil.
1. Garnish with cracked black pepper corns and serve.
1. Serve Roasted Cauliflower Soup Recipe along with Roasted Vegetable Panini Sandwich With Feta Cheese Recipe followed by a dessert of New York Style Baked Cheesecake Recipe for a complete meal.
