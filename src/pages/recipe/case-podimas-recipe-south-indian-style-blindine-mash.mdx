### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 Raw Banana - par boiled and grated thick.
- 1/2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Vazhakkai Podimas Recipe, pressure cook the plantains along with the skin with 1/2 cup water using a pressure cooker for 2 whistles.
1. Once the plantains are cooked, peel its skin, grate is and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan on medium flame, add the mustard seeds, urad dal and let it crackle.
1. Add the curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. Add the onions, green chillies and ginger and saute until the onions turns translucent.
1. Add the grated plantains, season with salt and saute over medium heat for 5 minutes.
1. Once its done, add the grated coconut, give it a good stir and switch off the flame.
1. Serve the Vazhakkai Podimas Recipe along with Steamed Rice and Mixed Vegetable Sambar Recipe for the weeknight dinner.
