### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 cup Water.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1/4 Cup Dry ginger powder.
- 1 tablespoon Palm jaggery.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Karupatti Coffee recipe, take all the ingredients mentioned for spice powder in a blender.
1. Grind it to fine powder.
1.  This can be stored in airtight container and used whenever required.
1. Take a cup of water in a saucepan and add in a teaspoon of spice powder along with palm jaggery.
1. Mix and let the mixture come to boil.
1. Lower the heat and simmer for about 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Strain the coffee in a mug and serve.
1. Serve Karupatti Coffee to your friends and family to cure your cold or just to have it on a cold night.
