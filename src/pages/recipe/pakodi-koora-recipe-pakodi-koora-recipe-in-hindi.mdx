### Ingredients

-  oil - as per use.
-  1/2 cup potato - chopped.
-  1 / 2 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  1/2 onion - finely chopped.
-  2 + 2 large Tablespoon sesame (white).
-  2 tablespoons rice flour.
-  3 green chillies - cut.
-  3 sprigs of coriander - chopped.
-  1/2 cup gram flour.
-  2 teaspoons ginger garlic paste.
- 1/3 cup brinjal - chopped.
-  1/2 tablespoon coriander seeds.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  2 tablespoons peanuts.
-  curry leaves - as per use.
-  1/2 cup cauliflower - chopped.
-  1 + 1 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  1 tablespoon coriander powder.

### Instructions

1. To make Pakodi Kura, first heat the oil in a pan.
1. Now in a bowl add potatoes, cabbage, brinjals, onions, gram flour, rice flour, curry leaves, coriander leaves, sesame seeds, red chilli powder and salt.
1. Add some water and make a mixture of pakodi.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add the pakoris one by one and cook till it turns golden.
1. Now put sesame and peanuts in a pan and roast it.
1. After it is roasted, put it in the mixer grinder with some water and make a paste.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add curry leaves, onion, salt and garlic ginger paste to it.
1. Cook onion until it becomes soft.
1. Now add sesame and peanut paste and keep stirring.
1. Add 3/4 cup water and mix.
1. Now add 3/4 cup water and mix it.
1. After 1 minute, add turmeric powder, coriander powder, red chili powder, pakodi and cook it for 3 to 5 minutes.
1. Garnish with green coriander after cooking and serve.
1. You can serve Pakodi Kura with rice or Lacha Paratha for the day's dinner.
