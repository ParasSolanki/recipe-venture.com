### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - julienned.
- 1 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Broccoli - chopped into small florets.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 Veg Hakka Noodles.
- 6 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 Whole Eggs.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - peeled and julienned.
- 1 tablespoon Spring Onion Greens - chopped for garnish.
- 5 Button mushrooms - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Green Chilli Sauce.
- 1/2 cup Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Egg Hakka Noodles first, boil 5-6 cups of water and add Hakka Noodles to the water when the water starts to boil.
1. Add 1 teaspoon oil and some salt to the noodles while boiling.
1. Boil and cook the noodles according to the instructions given on the pack.
1. Once the noodles is cooked using a strainer, drain the water.
1. Pass running water through the noodles to stop overcooking the noodles.
1. Toss it with some oil to prevent sticking and keep aside.
1. Now for the scrambled eggs, crack and open 2 eggs one by one and add them to a mixing bowl.
1. Add black pepper and salt and beat them well.
1. In a wok, add oil and once the oil is hot add the egg mixture and spread it around by moving the wok in a circular direction.
1. Cook it for 2 minutes and then scramble it till it is cooked properly.
1. Remove to a plate and keep it aside.
1. In the same Kadai/wok, add 2 tablespoon oil and when it gets heated add finely chopped garlic.
1. Saute the garlic for few seconds and add the chopped spring onions, capsicum, broccoli, and carrot saute them till cooked.
1. After the vegetables are cooked add mushrooms and mix well.
1. Cook the mushrooms and let the water evaporate from the mushrooms.
1. Once done add soya sauce and chili sauce and give it a toss, now add black pepper powder and mix well.
1. Add the boiled noodles.
1. salt to taste and toss well to combine.
1. Finally, add the scrambled eggs to the Hakka Noodles and give it a stir.
1. Turn off the heat.
1. Transfer Egg Hakka Noodles to a serving plate.
1. Garnish with spring onion greens and serve them hot.
1. Serve Egg Hakka Noodle Recipe with Vegetable Manchurian Balls In Hot And Spice Garlic Sauce for a complete meal.
