### Ingredients

- 12 Pav buns - (ham burger buns could be substituted).
- 1 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - grated.
- 1 Lemon - juice extracted.
- Salt - to taste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 Lemon - juice.
- Butter - for toasting.
- 1/2 cup Cauliflower (gobi).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 4 Potatoes (Aloo) - peeled and diced.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 1 Onions - finely chopped for serving.
- 2 tablespoons Butter.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Pav bhaji masala.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Paneer Pav Bhaji Recipe, prep all the ingredients and keep it ready.
1. Place the pressure cooker on medium heat.
1. Add the butter and allow it to melt.
1. Once it melts, add the onion, capsicum and garlic and saute until the onions and capsicum soften.
1. Once it softens, add the tomato puree, potatoes, carrots, cauliflower, pav bhaji masala and chaat masala powder.
1. Saute for a few seconds.
1. Add 1/2 a cup of water in a pressure cooker and pressure cook for 5 to 6 whistles.
1. The vegetables have to become very soft and mushy.
1. Turn off the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Once cooked, open the cooker and mash the vegetables well, then add the paneer and mix well to combine.
1. Once done add the coriander leaves and lemon juice and mix well.
1. Check the seasoning and transfer the bhaji into a serving bowl , Sprinkle some onions on the top and keep aside.
1. The final step is to toast the pav buns.
1. Heat a skillet on medium high heat.
1. Spread butter over the sliced pav buns and toast them on the tawa until golden brown and crisp.
1. Serve with the bhaji and enjoy.
1. Serve the Pav Bhaji Recipe along with a Dahi Vada or a Papdi Chaat for a weeknight dinner or a chaat party at home.
1. Your friends and family are sure to love this dish.
