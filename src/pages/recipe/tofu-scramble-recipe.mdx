### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 200 grams Tofu - extra firm.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 Parsley leaves - finely chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tofu Scramble Recipe, heat olive oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add the garlic and onions and saute until the onions are lightly caramelized.
1. Once done add the tomatoes, green chilies and saute until the tomatoes become soft.
1. After the tomatoes are soft stir in the turmeric powder, cumin powder and garam masala powder and saute for a few seconds.
1. Finally stir in the crumbed tofu, add salt and pepper and combine well.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Cover the pan and simmer for a minute and turn off the heat.
1. Finally stir in the chopped parsley leaves.
1. Serve the Tofu Scramble Recipe along with a toasted bread and Muskmelon Juice to kick start your day with a high protein breakfast.
