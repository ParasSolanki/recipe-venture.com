### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - both green and white.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 6 Green beans (French Beans) - finely chopped.
- 2 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Vinegar.
- 2 Whole Eggs - at room temperature.
- 1 cup Basmati rice - cooked.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spicy Schezwan Indo-Chinese Egg Fried Rice Recipe place a wok on the heat, warm some oil.
1. When warm enough, add the garlic, ginger and sauté them on a medium heat for a few seconds, till aromatic.
1. Add the spring onion and the 1/2 carrots and beans, sauté on a high heat for 2 to 3 minutes, until the vegetables get slightly cooked, but retain their crunch.
1. Add the eggs next and sauté for another 1 minute until they get scrambled, before you add the soya sauce, vinegar and 2-3 teaspoons of Schezuan Sauce.
1. Saute this mix for 2 minutes more and finally add the cooked long grain rice.
1. Toss together for 3-4 minutes with the heat on medium, so the rice is well mixed with the spicy sauce and seasoned evenly.
1. Dish the Spicy Schezwan Indo-Chinese Egg Fried Rice out into a serving bowl and garnish with spring onion greens.
1. Serve the Schezwan Egg Fried Rice hot with Sweet and Sour Vegetables with Paneer or Thai Roasted Vegetables with Peanut Coconut Sauce.
