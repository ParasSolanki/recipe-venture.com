### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 30 ml Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- Sunflower Oil - as required for shallow frying.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 2 tablespoons Onion - paste.
- 60 ml Fresh cream.
- 250 grams Chicken breasts - cut tinto 2 inch cubes.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chicken Malai Kebab Recipe, using a fork prick the chicken pieces slightly and transfer to a bowl.
1. Into the bowl with the chicken, add the onion paste, ginger garlic paste, chilli powder, curd, fresh cream, cumin powder, add salt to taste and mix well.
1. Allow this to marinate overnight.
1. Heat a grilling pan on medium heat, add a few teaspoons of oil and place the chicken pieces on a low-medium flame.
1. Flip after a few minutes.
1. Ensuring the chicken is cooked through well.
1. Serve Chicken Malai Kebab Recipe with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney Recipe and Pickled Onions as a party appetizer.
