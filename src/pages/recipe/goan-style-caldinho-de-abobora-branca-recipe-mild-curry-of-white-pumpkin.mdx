### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 200 grams Vellai Poosanikai (Ash gourd/White Pumpkin) - skin peeled and chopped small.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Goan Style Caldinho de Abobora Branca Recipe, pressure cook the pumpkin with water and salt for about 1 whistle till it is just done.
1. Meanwhile grind the ingredients that are mentioned in the list like the coconut, cumin seeds, ginger, garlic and turmeric powder with little water to form a smooth paste.
1. Once the pumpkin is cooked keep it aside.
1. Heat a flat skillet with oil, add onions and saute until they turn translucent.
1. Once done add ground paste and cook for at least 15 minutes till the masala is cooked well.
1. Add cooked pumpkin along with the water and stir well.
1. Check for salt.
1. Sprinkle lemon juice and serve hot with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve the Goan Style Caldinho de Abobora Branca Recipe along with Steamed Rice and Potato Vindaloo  to make a complete meal.
