### Ingredients

- 16 Nilla cookies.
- Decorative sugar (Cake/ Candy Sprinkles) - as required.
- 1 Ripe Bananas - thinly sliced.
- Peanut Butter - as required.
- Nutella Spread - as required.

### Instructions

1. To make the BaNilla Sandwich Cookie Recipe, spread the nutella spread on the inside of all Nilla cookies.
1. Apply peanut butter on slices of banana and roll that side in sprinkles and cover the banana cover with the sprinkles.
1. Place the banana in between the nutella spread nilla cookies and sandwich them by placing another cookie on the top.
1. That's it, the BaNilla Sandwich Cookie is now ready to be served.
1. Serve BaNilla Sandwich Cookie along with a hot cup of Espresso Coffee when you have guests coming over for visit.
