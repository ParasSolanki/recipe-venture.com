### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 cup Dessicated Coconut.
- 8 Spring Onion Greens - chopped (chop away onion bulbs).
- 1/2 cup Tomato - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 cup Boondi.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1/4 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Garlic - paste.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Boondi And Spring Onion Greens Sabzi recipe, heat oil in a non stick wok/kadhai, add cumin seeds and asafoetida.
1. Once cumin seeds starts to crackle, add garlic paste, saute it till raw aroma of garlic goes away.
1. Add coconut powder, saute it for few seconds and make sure coconut powder does not get burnt.
1. Add turmeric powder, dhaniya powder, red chilli powder, garam masala powder and the required salt.
1. Add spring onion green and mix it well, let it cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. After 3 to 4 minutes, add chopped tomatoes and some water.
1. Let it cook for another minute.
1. Add dry mango powder and mix it well.
1. Spread khari boondi over the mixture.
1. Donâ€™t mix it, just spread it and cover the lid for few seconds till boondi gets soft.
1. Now mix it slowly and your Boondi And Spring Onion Greens Sabzi is ready to be served.
1. Serve Boondi And Spring Onion Greens Sabzi along with Panchmel Dal and Tawa Paratha for a weekday meal.
