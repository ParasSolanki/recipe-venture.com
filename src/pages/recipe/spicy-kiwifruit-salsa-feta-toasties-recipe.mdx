### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- Lemon juice - from one lemon.
- 1 Parsley leaves.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 4 Kiwi - peeled and quartered.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - or butter for toasting.
- 100 grams Feta Cheese.
- 10 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 1 teaspoon Tabasco Original - Hot Sauce.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Spicy Kiwifruit Salsa & Feta Toasties combine all the ingredients for the Kiwifruit Salsa in a large mixing bowl.
1. Check the salt and spice levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Refrigerate this filling until the toasties are ready.
1. Toast the bread slices either in an oven or in the bread toaster until golden brown and crisp.
1. Apply some olive oil or butter over the toasts.
1. You can either cut the toasts into rounds using a round cookie cutter or trim the edges and cut them into triangles.
1. Place the toasted bread on the serving platter.
1. Spoon the Spicy Kiwifruit Salsa over the toasted bread.
1. Sprinkle the crumbled feta cheese over the toasties and serve immediately.
1. Serve the Spicy Kiwifruit Salsa & Feta Toasties as an evening snack along with Cold Coffee Smoothie Recipe.
