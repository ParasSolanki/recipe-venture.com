### Ingredients

- 1 cup Chilled water.
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 cup Milk.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 3 tablespoon Assam tea leaves.
- 2 tablespoon Sugar - (adjust).
- 5 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 2 tablespoon Fresh cream.
- 1 Star anise.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Irani Chai Recipe, in a Saucepan, add water, and all the spices (star anise, cardamoms, cinnamon, cloves, and pepper).
1. Allow this to boil for 5 minutes.
1. Add milk and cream and simmer for another 5 minutes.
1. As you leave it for a long time, you get a stronger decoration.
1. Then add in your tea leaves and keep it in the simmer for another 3 minutes.
1. Just before serving strain it over a tea cup.
1. And add sugar and dissolve according to your taste.
1. Irani Chai recipe is ready to be served with Fruitcake cookies, Jeera biscuits or Khara biscuit on the side.
