### Ingredients

- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Mustard sauce.
- 1/2 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/2 cup Fresh cream.
- 1 clove Garlic - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Homemade Egg Free Mayonnaise (Eggless); whisk the cream, lemon juice, garlic, and pepper in a jar and blend using a hand blender until frothy.
1. With the motor running on high, slowly pour in the oil little at a time until the mixture thickens and resembles a soft mayonnaise.
1. Add some salt to taste and store the mayonnaise in a glass jar and store in the refrigerator for about a week.
