### Ingredients

-  5 Gram Nutmeg Powder.
-  25 grams large cardamom.
- 250 grams coriander seeds.
-  25 grams bay leaves.
-  25 grams star anis.
-  50 grams dry red chillies.
-  25 grams mustard.
-  50 grams cinnamon.
-  25 grams cardamom.
-  25 grams whole pepper.
-  25 Gram Poppy Poppy.
-  50 grams long.

### Instructions

1. To make Dhansak Masala Powder recipe, grind coriander seeds, dry red chillies, long, cardamom, cinnamon, big cardamom, whole black pepper, bay leaves, star anis, rye, nutmeg powder, poppy seeds and grind them in a mixer grinder.
1. After the powder is made, put it in an air tight container and store.
1. You can use this spice to create ringing attitude.
