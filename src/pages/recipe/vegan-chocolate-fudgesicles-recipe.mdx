### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Peanut Butter - or almond butter.
- 1/3 cup Date syrup.
- 1-1/2 Ripe Bananas - fully ripe.
- Salt - a pinch.
- 1 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - soaked overnight and cooked.
- 1/4 cup Cocoa Powder.
- 2 cups Soy milk.
- 2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vegan Chocolate Fudgesicles recipe, soak the chickpeas in enough water overnight or for 6 hours.
1. Pressure cook the chickpeas until soft, drain and cool completely.
1. Remove the skin from the chickpeas, this will give a creamy texture to the fudgesicles as well as remove any chickpea flavour if any.
1. Add the chickpeas and the remaining ingredients - peanut butter, soy milk, vanilla extract, date syrup, cocoa powder, banana and salt into a food processor or a blender and blend until creamy and smooth.
1. You can also add in some thick coconut milk or almond milk to give the popsicle a more creamier texture.
1. Fill into popsicle molds and freeze them for at least 4 hours or overnight.
1. Thaw on the counter for 3 to 4 minutes, unmold and enjoy.
1. Serve Vegan Chocolate Fudgesicles as a healthy dessert after your meal of Creamy Mushroom Risotto on a weekday.
