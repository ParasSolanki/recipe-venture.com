### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Corn flour.
- 1/4 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/2 cup Cocoa Powder.
- 3 cups Milk.
- 1/4 cup Butter (unsalted).
- 1/2 cup Dark Chocolate Chips.
- 1/2 cup Sugar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Quick Microwave Chocolate Pudding Recipe, stir together cocoa powder, sugar, corn flour and salt in a large microwave safe bowl.
1. Gradually add milk and whisk till no lumps exist.
1. Place the bowl in a microwave at full power for 8-10 minutes.
1. Pause microwave every 2 minutes to stir the mixture.
1. After 8 minutes, the pudding should have thickened.
1. While it's still hot, whisk in chopped chocolate and butter till pudding is smooth.
1. Pour the Chocolate Pudding mixture into individual pudding bowls.
1. To avoid a skin forming on the top, place a cling film wrap directly on the surface of the pudding bowl and refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
1. Once the pudding is set, serve it chilled with fruits.
1. Serve the Quick Microwave Chocolate Pudding with a dollop of cream as a delightful dessert after a meal of Mini Pita Pizza With Tahini Roasted Vegetables Recipe and Lavender Lemonade Cooler Recipe.
