### Ingredients

- 1 cup Sweet corn -.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Enos fruit salt.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - sour.
- 2 tablespoons Sama Flour (Little Millet).
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1/2 Lemon juice.
- 2-1/2 teaspoon Green Chilli Paste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1 cup Buckwheat Flour (Kuttu Ka Atta).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Buckwheat Corn Dhokla recipe, In a bowl, combine buckwheat flour, sama flour, green chilli paste, salt and fruit salt.
1. Mix them together.
1. Add corn kernels to the flour mixture and keep it aside for an hour.
1. Grease the dhokla plate with oil and pour the buckwheat wet mixture to the plate.
1. Steam in idli cooker for 20 minutes.
1. Buckwheat takes longer time to cook than the usual sooji or besan.
1. Hence, check dhokla often and water in the vessel.
1. Cook until the dhoklas are cooked well.
1. The dhokla is cooked when a toothpick inserted in the center of the dhokla comes out clean.
1. In a small tadka pan, heat oil over medium heat.
1. Add mustard seeds once the oil is hot.
1. Once the seeds splutter, add cumin seeds and sesame seeds.
1. Cook until they turn slightly brownish.
1. Pour the tempering on top of the dhokla.
1. Squeeze lemon at the time of serving.
