### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 2 teaspoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 5 Carrots (Gajjar) - peeled and chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - peeled and chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Lemon wedges.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - cubed.
- 1 Celery - chopped.
- 3 cups Vegetable stock.
- 2 teaspoons Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Carrot Soup recipe, first heat oil in a saucepan.
1. Add the onion and sauté for a minute.
1. Add the coriander powder and sauté for a few seconds.
1. Add the ginger, celery, carrots and potato.
1. Sprinkle some salt and mix everything well.
1. Add 2 cups of stock, cover the pan and cook till the carrots and potatoes becomes soft.
1. Cool it down and puree in a blender.
1. Pour back the puree into the saucepan and heat.
1. Adjust the consistency using the leftover the stock.
1. Add pepper and boil for 1 to 2 minutes.
1. Serve into individual bowls, squeeze some lime juice (if using) and serve immediately.
1. Serve Carrot Soup with Smoked Tofu and Grilled Vegetable Salad for a light and healthy dinner.
