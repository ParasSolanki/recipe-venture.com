### Ingredients

- Puff Pastry Sheet.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 cup Button mushrooms - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 sprig Fresh Thyme leaves.
- 4 tablespoon Fresh cream.
- Whole Black Peppercorns - for seasoning.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Mushroom filled Vol-au-Vent Recipe by making the shape of the vol-au-vent first.
1. So in order to do that keep your puff pastry ready (Click on the link to see how to make the puff pastry).
1. Pre-heat the oven at 200 degree Celsius for 15 minutes and keep it ready.
1. Roll the dough to 1-centimeter thickness, then using a small circular cookie cutter, cut the puff pastry dough into circles.
1. Make extra circles to make the ring for vol-au-vent shapes.
1. Using one smaller circular cutter to make a ring shape with the extra circles.
1. Place these ring on the circular-shaped dough, seal it using milk.
1. Refrigerate for 10 minutes, and brush some milk on the vol-au-vent and bake it for 15 minutes.
1. In the meantime heat a nonstick pan with oil, add the chopped garlic and mushroom and saut√© till all the water is evaporated.
1. Then season it with thyme, chili flakes, salt and pepper.
1. Once done add the cream and give it a stir.
1. Take out the vol-au-vent from the oven and grease honey using a pastry brush and keep it in the oven for 3 more minutes to give it a brown crust.
1. Once done take out the vol-au-vent and rest it outside for 2 minutes and using a spoon, spoon the mushroom mixture in the center.
1. Serve the Mushroom filled Vol-au-Vent Recipe as party appetizers for a festive occasion.
