### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Green Chilli.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 1 cup Green Amaranth Leaves - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 2 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thotakura Majjiga Pulusu Recipe, first grind the coconut with green chilli and coriander leaves with some water to a smooth paste.
1. Heat a flat skillet with oil, add onions and saute well till it is translucent.
1. Once done add amaranth leaves and saute till the leaves are cooked.
1. Add ground coconut paste and curd together and give it a stir.
1. Sprinkle salt and turmeric powder and mix well.
1. Allow it to simmer for 10 minutes and turn off the heat.
1. Heat a small tadka pan with oil, add mustard seeds, fenugreek seeds and cumin seeds and leave it to crackle.
1. Once the seeds have carackled add hing, curry leaves and dry red chillies and allow it to crackle and pour this over the curry and serve.
1. Serve the Thotakura Majjiga Pulusu Recipe with some steamed rice and ghee and Carrot Poriyal Recipe for a comforting lunch.
