### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1 tablespoon Date syrup.
- 1/2 cup Muesli.
- 1 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1 cup Apricots.
- 1 Whole Egg.
- 1/2 cup Milk.
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 1 cup Oats Flour.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Muesli and Oats Waffle with Apricots Recipe by heating your waffle machine for about 10 minutes.
1. Meanwhile mix in the batter for the waffle with all the ingredients mentioned above.
1. Mix well till it combines and becomes a thick batter.
1. Pour a ladle on to your waffle machine and spread the batter evenly and do not over pour it.
1. You can sprinkle few muesli on top.
1. Close and cook till it cooks and becomes brown.
1. Take the waffle out and then place it on a wire mesh.
1. Or you can serve it immediately.
1. Serve the Muesli and Oats Waffle with Apricots Recipe topped up with some date syrup, apricots and apples for your morning breakfast.
