### Ingredients

- 1 Brinjal (Baingan / Eggplant) - diced.
- 1 teaspoon Curry powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 Red Yellow or Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1 cup Spinach - chopped.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - peeled and diced.
- 1 Sweet Potato - peeled and diced.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- Badam (Almond) - a few.
- Raisins - a few.
- 1 Green zucchini - diced.
- 6 Green beans (French Beans) - chopped.
- 2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 cup Fresh orange juice.
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Marrakesh Vegetable Curry Recipe, prepare all the ingredients and keep ready.
1. In a large saucepan, heat some oil, throw in all the veggies (chopped beans, carrot, sweet potato, eggplant, zucchini, capsicum, and onion) and saute for 3-5 minutes of till the veggies are almost done.
1. In another medium pan take 1 tablespoons olive oil, garlic, turmeric, curry powder, cinnamon, salt and pepper and saut√© over medium heat for 3 minutes.
1. Add this sauted spice mix into the veggie mix.
1. Add the remaining ingredients like orange juice, almond, raisin and salt.
1. Mix everything well and simmer for 15 -20 minutes on low flame.
1. Finally add chopped spinach to the curry, let it boil for 5 mins till combined well and switch off.
1. Serve Marrakesh Vegetable Curry Recipe along with cooked couscous or Steamed Rice and a Moroccan Baked Fish Recipe or Spicy Moroccan Eggs Recipe for a complete meal.
