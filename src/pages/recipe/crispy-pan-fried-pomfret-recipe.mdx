### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 5 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Salt - + Extra for pomfret pieces.
- Tamarind - Marble size ball.
- 1 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 1/2 Cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava) - or breadcrumbs.
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 Inch Ginger.
- 1 Pomfret fish - cut into 1 inch size pieces.
- 3 Whole Black Peppercorns.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Crispy Pan Fried Pomfret Recipe, wash Pomfret with water.
1. Drain all the water and sprinkle some salt and keep it aside for 5 minutes.
1. In a mixer grinder, add coriander leaves, fennel seeds, whole black peppercorns, cloves, turmeric powder, dry red chillies, garlic, ginger and tamarind.
1. Grind all the masala with about 1/3 Cup water to smooth batter for fish.
1. Add a teaspoon of salt into the paste and mix well.
1. Apply this batter or paste to pomfret pieces and keep aside for marination for about 10 minutes.
1. Heat a skillet, add oil.
1. Coat the marinated pomfret pieces well with semolina on both the sides.
1. Place them over oil on the skillet.
1. Let them fry on medium heat for about 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Flip to fry on the other side.
1. Once they are all brown and crispy, switch off the heat and remove them out on a serving plate.
1. Enjoy this tasty, crispy pomfret masala fry as an appetizer.
1. Serve Crispy Pan Fried Pomfret along with Prawns Coconut Curry and Steamed Rice for a weekday meal.
