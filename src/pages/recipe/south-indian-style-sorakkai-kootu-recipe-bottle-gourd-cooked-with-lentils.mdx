### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut.
- 2 cups Bottle gourd (lauki) - diced small.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - broken into half.
- 2 Red Chilli powder.
- 1/4 cup Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the South Indian Style Sorakkai Kootu Recipe -Bottle Gourd Cooked with Lentils Recipe add moong dal and the chopped bottle gourd in a pressure cooker with little water.
1. Close the cooker with a lid and cook till the cooker releases 4 whistles.
1. Once it is done, switch off the heat and release the pressure naturally.
1. Add the ingredients mentioned in the list to be ground into a coarse paste, which is fresh coconut, cumin seeds and red chilli powder.
1. Once the pressure is released, add this paste into the cooked mixture and give it a stir.
1. Cook it for 2 minutes and switch off the stove.
1. For the Tadka, heat a skillet with oil and crackle the mustard seeds and urad dal, add the rest of the ingredients and then pour this over the Sorakkai Kootu and serve.
1. Serve the South Indian Style Sorakkai Kootu Recipe (Bottle Gourd Cooked with Lentils Recipe) along with Steamed Rice and Parappu Rasam to enjoy a comforting meal.
