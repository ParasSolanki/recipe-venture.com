### Ingredients

- 2 Carrots (Gajjar).
- 3 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 2 tablespoon Rice Wine Vinegar.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - lightly toasted.
- 1 Red onion - sliced.
- 2 tablespoon Groundnut oil.
- 2 cloves Garlic - finely minced (or chives).
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Honey.
- 2 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder - crushed.
- 1/2 inch Ginger - finely grated.
- 1 Fresh Red Chilli - finely sliced.
- 2 teaspoon Fresh orange juice - or lemon juice.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Carrot Ribbon Salad with Asian Sesame Dressing, first trim the ends of the carrots.
1. Using a vegetable peeler, peel the outer skin and discard it.
1. Keep peeling around the carrots, so that you get thin, long strips or ribbons.
1. In a small bowl or a glass bottle, whisk together the oils, vinegar, honey, black pepper, lime juice, garlic, ginger, salt and red chilli.
1. Make sure that everything is mixed properly.
1. In a salad bowl, toss together the carrot ribbons, sliced onion, coriander, mint and the above mixture.
1. Keep aside for 5 minutes and then garnish it with the sesame seeds.
1. Serve Carrot Ribbon Salad With Asian Sesame Dressing with Broccoli Pesto pasta for a light weekday dinner.
