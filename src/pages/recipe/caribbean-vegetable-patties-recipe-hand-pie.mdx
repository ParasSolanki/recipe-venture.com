### Ingredients

- 2 cups All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to tate.
- 1 cup Mixed vegetables - (beans.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Paprika powder.
- 1/4 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon All spice powder.
- 1/2 cup Chilled water.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/2 cup Butter - adjustable.
- 1 tablespoon Coconut Oil.
- Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini) - a pinch.
- 1/2 teaspoon Curry powder.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- Nutmeg powder - a pinch.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Caribbean Vegetable Patties recipe, for the crust, sift together flour, salt, turmeric and curry powder in a large bowl.
1. Cut butter into small pieces and mix it into flour mixture till it is crumbly.
1. Make a well in the centre and add water.
1. Mix gently until a dough forms, then wrap dough in cling wrap and put in refrigerator for 1 hour.
1. Meanwhile, make the filling.
1. Heat oil in a skillet.
1. Add onion, ginger, garlic, chopped vegetables and cook till they become soft.
1. Add salt, all spice powder, nutmeg powder, cinnamon powder  and cook for a few more minutes.
1. Remove from heat and let cool a little.
1. To assemble patties, remove dough from refrigerator and divide into equal portionsRoll each piece into a 6-inch circle.
1. Brush a little water around the edge of the circle and place about 2 to 3 tablespoons of filling in the middle.
1. Fold in half and crimp the edges with a fork then gently pierce the centre with the fork to allow steam to escape.
1. Apply butter on it.
1. Once all patties are assembled, bake on a lightly oiled sheet pan at 215 degree for 25 minutes or until they're crisp.
1. Serve hot.
