### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali) - crushed or whole or halved.
- Chaat Masala Powder - (optional).
- 2 Oranges - peeled and diced.
- 1 Cucumber - peeled and diced or chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 1/4 cup Fresh Pomegranate Fruit Kernels.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 cup Papaya - peeled and diced into chunks.
- 2 teaspoons Lemon juice - (add more if needed).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Papaya Orange Pomegranate Salad Recipe With Roasted Peanuts, we will first prepare the fruits and keep them ready.
1. In a large bowl add all the ingredients listed and give it a toss with a your fingers.
1. Add the chaat masala if needed.
1. Check for the salt levels, add chaat masala if you like and serve the fruit salad immediately.
1. Serve Papaya Orange Pomegranate Salad Recipe With Roasted Peanuts as a snack or as a light lunch or dinner along with Light And Healthy Spinach Soup Recipe and Garlic Bread With Herb Butter Recipe.
