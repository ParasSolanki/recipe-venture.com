### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 300 grams Parwal - peeled and cut length wise.
- 2 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 3 teaspoons Watermelon seeds.

### Instructions

1. To begin making No Onion No Garlic Parwal Masala Recipe, wash and prep the parwal.
1. Peel the skin out of the parwal.
1. Peel in alternate strips so that the parwal cooks and retains its shape as well.
1. Cut in half and keep aside.
1. Grind ginger, tomato and watermelon seeds into a smooth mixture.
1. Next heat oil in a wok and saute the parwal on medium flame till they are nearly cooked.
1. Remove and keep aside.
1. Now in the same wok add some more oil if required and bay leaf and add the ground tomato and ginger paste and fry till the raw smell of the tomato goes away.
1. Add the dry masalas, red chilli powder, coriander powder, cumin powder and garam masala powder and stir in the Parwal Masala gravy.
1. Add the cooked parwal to the gravy and add some water.
1. Simmer and cook the Parwal Masala for five minutes until the masala is well coated onto the parwal.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer the Parwal Masala to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve No Onion No Garlic - Parwal Masala along with Jain Style Whole Green Moong Dal Subzi, Tawa Paratha and Dangar Pachadi With Grated Carrots.
