### Ingredients

- 3/4 cup Britannia Cream Cheese - at room temperature.
- 1/4 cup Heavy whipping cream - chilled.
- 200 grams Oreo biscuits - (or digestive biscuits).
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 3/4 tablespoon Gelatin - or Agar Agar.
- 1 tablespoon Caster Sugar.
- Hazelnut - chopped/ 4 Ferrero Rocher chocolate.
- 1/4 cup Butter (unsalted).
- 1/2 cup Nutella Spread.
- 2 tablespoons Water - (adjust).

### Instructions

1. *To make the biscuit base:To prepare No-Bake Chocolate Hazelnut Cheesecake Recipe, make the biscuit base - powder the biscuits in a food processor to a fine crumb.
1. Add the melted butter to it and mix until well combined.
1. Place the biscuit crumbs into a greased springform cake tin / small serving glasses.
1. Press down until firm.
1. Then place in the fridge to chill till the filling is ready.
1. *To make the cheesecake filling:Take a small cup and mix the gelatin or agar agar (vegetarian option) with 2-3 tablespoons of water and set aside for 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes, heat it in a microwave for 20-30 seconds until completely melted.
1. Let it cool slightly.
1. In a mixing bowl beat together cream cheese , cream and sugar until smooth and well combined.
1. Add nutella and vanilla essence and continue mixing.
1. Finally, add gelatin mix to the cheese-mix and combine everything together .
1. Spread the cheesecake filling over the prepared base, smooth the top .
1. Cover and leave to set in the fridge for at least 6 hours .
1. To decorate: Once the No-Bake Chocolate Hazelnut Cheesecake Recipe is set, decorate the top with the chopped toasted hazelnuts / ferrero rocher /choco shavings .
