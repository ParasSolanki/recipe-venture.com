### Ingredients

- 2 1/2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 cup Corn flour.
- 300 grams Chicken - cut into small pieces.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- 1 Green bell peppers - sliced.
- 1 Whole Egg.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 cup Water.
- 1 tablespoon Corn flour.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 5 sprig Basil leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Tomato Ketchup.
- 2 tablespoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 2 Fresh Red chillies - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thai Basil Chicken Recipe, thoroughly wash and clean the chicken.
1. In a mixing bowl , combine one egg, to this add corn flour, salt and pepper and mix well.
1. Add the chicken pieces as well and sprinkle some water if the mixture appears too thick.
1. The batter must be of the right consistency-not too thick but not too thin either.
1. esuring the chicken is coated well.
1. To pan fry the chicken, heat oil in a pan on medium heat, place the coated chicken pieces and stir fry till it turns into a nice brown colour.
1. This will take about 8-10 minutes.
1. Remove on an absorbent paper and set aside.
1. To make the sauce.
1. In a mixer-jar, pound the red chilies and garlic into a coarse paste with absolutely no water Mix all the ingredients for the sauce which includes- Red chili sauce, Soy sauce, tomato ketchup and corn flour by adding 1/2 a cup of water into a bowl.
1. Mix well and keep it aside.
1. Heat a saucepan with oil, add mashed garlic and red chillies and saute on low heat.
1. Add sliced bell peppers and saute till they soften.
1. Add in the sauce mixture and stir well.
1. When it starts to thicken, add in the chicken pieces and mix well.
1. Finally add the basil leaves and mix well, switch off the heat and serve warm.
1. Serve the Thai Basil Chicken Recipe (Pad Krapow Gai) along with Chilli Coriander Fried Rice Recipe and Spicy Vegetable Noodles Recipes to make it a complete meal.
