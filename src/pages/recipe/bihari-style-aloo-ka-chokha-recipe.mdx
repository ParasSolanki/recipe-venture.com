### Ingredients

-  1/2 tsp red chili powder.
-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 tbsp mustard oil.
-  2 onions - finely chopped.
- 4 potatoes - boil.
-  4 sprig coriander - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make the Bihari Style Potato Chokha Recipe, first boil the potatoes in a pressure cooker until they come to the city of 5 to 6.
1. After cooking, take out the skin of the potatoes and mash them.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan.
1. Add onion, green chilies and cook till the onion becomes soft.
1. Now add mashed potatoes, salt, red chili powder and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Garnish with green coriander and serve.
1. Serve Bihari Style Aloo Chokha Recipe along with Bihari Litti.
