### Ingredients

- 1 cup Basmati rice.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 2 tablespoons Badam (Almond) - boiled and skin peeled.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 cup Masoor Dal (Whole).
- 1 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt) - for serving.
- 3 Onions - thinly sliced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini) - pound.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder - pound.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Lebanese Style Mujaddara Recipe by parboiling the Masoor dal in a pressure cooker for about 2 whistle and let the pressure release naturally.
1. Remove and keep it aside.
1. Then in the same pressure cooker, heat oil and add the pound pepper and cinnamon sticks.
1. Add the sliced onions and sauté till it is nice and brown.
1. Add all the spice powders and sauté for 10 minutes, once done keep some aside for the garnish.
1. Add the soaked rice and sauté for few more minutes, then transfer the par boiled dal,season it with salt and pepper.
1. Add 2 cups of water and cook it for 2 whistle and simmer in low heat for 10 minutes.
1. Once the pressure has released naturally, toss the rice with some olive oil and garnish it with sauteed onions that was kept aside and with chopped coriander leaves.
1. Serve the Lebanese Style Mujaddara Recipe can be served as a one pot dish for a Sunday lunch.
