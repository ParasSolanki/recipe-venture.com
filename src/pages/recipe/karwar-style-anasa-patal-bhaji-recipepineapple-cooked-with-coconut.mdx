### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 Dry Red Chilli.
- 2 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Jaggery - (adjust according to taste).
- 2 cups Pineapple - chopped.
- pinch Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 2 Kokum (Malabar Tamarind).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Karwar Style Anasa Patal Bhaji Recipe (Pineapple cooked with coconut) by chopping the pineapple by removing the thorns and the inner core.
1. Chop them to medium sized pieces.
1. Heat a pan and dry roast the coriander seeds, dry red chillies till it becomes brown and grind it along with coconut to a smooth paste.
1. In the same pan add the pineapple with Â½ cup water and cook it till it becomes soft, then add the coconut paste, jaggery, kokum rinds and salt.
1. Cook slowly till the water reduces and the masala is well incorporated with Pineapple.
1. Serve the Karwar Style Anasa Patal Bhaji Recipe (Pineapple cooked with coconut) as a side dish along with Gehun Bhajra Thepla Recipe (Spiced Wheat Millet Flat Bread) and Palak Raita Recipe for Lunch meal.
