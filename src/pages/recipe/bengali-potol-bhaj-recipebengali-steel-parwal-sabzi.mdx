### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 250 grams Pointed gourd (Parval).
- 1/2 cup Mustard oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bengali Potol Bhaja recipe, wash and pat dry parwal well.
1. Slit into half length wise and keep aside.
1. In a bowl, add salt and turmeric powder, mix well.
1. Generously rub the turmeric salt mixture all over the parwal and keep aside.
1. Heat mustard oil in a pan.
1. Once the oil is hot add the parwal and saute parwal until browned on all side.
1. Transfer Bengali Potol Bhaja into a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Bengali Potol Bhaja along with Dal Chingri and Steamed Rice for weekday lunch or dinner.
