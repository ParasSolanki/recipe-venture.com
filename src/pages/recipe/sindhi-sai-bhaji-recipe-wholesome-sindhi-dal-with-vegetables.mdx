### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/4 cup Dill leaves - washed and finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Green Moong Dal (Split) - washed and soaked.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Bottle gourd (lauki) - finely chopped.
- 1/3 cup Green beans (French Beans) - finely chopped.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Onions - finely chopped.
- 6 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 cup Spinach Leaves (Palak) - washed and finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1/3 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves) - washed and finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - washed and soaked.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sindhi Sai Bhaji Recipe,  wash and soak the chana dal and split green moong dal for about 30 minutes.
1. Wash and chop all the required vegetable and greens for the Sindhi Sai Bhaji Recipe.
1. In a pressure cooker, heat ghee on medium flame, start by adding the onions, saute for a couple of minutes.
1. Next add ginger, garlic and saute for a couple of minutes.
1. Add the tomatoes and cook until mushy.
1. This will take about 2 minutes.
1. Once it is done, add the remaining ingredients including turmeric powder coriander powder, red chilli powder, raw mango, green chilli, spinach leaves, methi leaves, dill leaves, chana dal, green moong dal, carrot, green beans, bottle gourd etc.
1. Saute it for about 4 to 5 minutes.
1. You can also add other vegetables of your choice such as Brinjal, tinda etc in this Bhaji.
1. Add salt to taste and 3 cups of water, close the lid and pressure cook the Sindhi Sai Bhaji for 15 to 20 minutes.
1. Switch off the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Open the lid and mash the Sai bhaji using a potato masher to bring it to a mushy consistency.
1. Serve this Sindhi Sai Bhaji Recipe with Sindhi Koki (Sindhi Style Flaky Flatbread With Onions) and Sindhi Bhuga Chawal Recipe and Lauki Raita Recipe for a complete Sindhi Rasoi-meal.
1. You could also serve Sindhi Sai Bhaji Recipe along with Jowar Jo Dodo Recipe - Sindhi Sorghum Roti and Gajar Shalgam Ka Paani Wala Achar Recipe for a simple weekday lunch.
