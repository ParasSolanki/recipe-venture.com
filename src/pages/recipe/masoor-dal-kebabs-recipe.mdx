### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - skin peeled and boiled.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Masoor Dal (Whole) - soaked for at least 6 hours.
- 3 teaspoons Garam masala powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Masoor Dal Kebabs Recipe, pressure cook the peeled potatoes separately first in a pressure cooker with 1/4 cup of water, salt an pinch for at least 4 whistle.
1. The potatoes need to be completely soft and easy to mash.
1. Once done, allow the pressure to release naturally and take the potatoes out and place it in a mixing bowl.
1. Once it has cool down you can mash it well.
1. Now cook soaked masoor dal with 2 cups of water, salt to taste for at least 6-8 whistle, once done allow the pressure to release naturally, drain out the excess water and you can use the water that has been drained out for your dals, curries etc, to give your dish a healthy touch.
1. Mash the masoor dal also coarsely and them along with the potatoes and mix well.
1. Heat a sauce pan with oil, add cumin seeds and allow it to crackle for few seconds.
1. Add chopped garlic, ginger and saute until they soften.
1. Add onions and green chilli and saute until the onions turn translucent.
1. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, garam masala powder, salt and give it a stir.
1. Add mashed potato and masoor dal and slowly mix well until all the masala is evenly coated.
1. Keep mixing until the kebab mixture combines together.
1. Leave it to dry and switch off the heat.
1. Once the mixture is cooled down, pinch out a small portion of the mixture and start shaping them into cylindrical shapes.
1. Do the same for the rest of the mixture.
1. Heat a flat skillet with 3 tablespoon oil, add the kebabs slowly and keep turning it over until it crisps up evenly on all the side.
1. Serve the Masoor Dal Kebabs Recipe along with Hyderabadi Vegetable Biryani Recipe,Tomato Onion Cucumber Raita Recipe or it can simply serve with a yogurt dip to enjoy it for your appetizers.
