### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Anardana Powder (Pomegranate Seed Powder).
- 1 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt) - or greek yogurt.
- 1/2 cup Granola.
- 1 Kiwi - peeled and diced.
- 1/4 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 Walnuts - or pecans.
- Honey - for taste.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Granola Yogurt Parfait Recipe with Fresh Kiwi & Pomegranates, first get all the ingredients ready.
1. Whisk the yogurt along with vanilla extract and keep aside.
1. Arrange 2 serving bowls.
1. Spoon some granola to the bottom of the glass bowl.
1. Divide the yogurt and spoon it over the granola.
1. Tossed the cut fruits over the top of the yogurt and finally add the walnuts and honey.
1. Serve the Granola Yogurt Parfait Recipe with Fresh Kiwi & Pomegranates as a healthy breakfast along with glass of fresh Mulled Apple Juice Recipe or Carrot Pineapple Orange Juice Recipe.
