### Ingredients

- 50 grams Badam (Almond).
- Kewra essence - Few drops.
- 3 tablespoons Melon seeds.
- 100 grams Jaggery - powdered or sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- Saffron strands - Few.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Badam Sharbat recipe, soak the almonds in enough water overnight.
1. Soak melon seeds in water for 2-3 hours.
1. Peel off the skin of almonds and blend with melon seeds in a mixer adding 1/4 cup water until you get a smooth mixture.
1. Heat jaggery in 1 cup water in a saucepan.
1. Once jaggery is completely dissolved, add almond milk and cardamom powder.
1. Cook till the mixture is reduced to half.
1. It may take around 15 minutes.
1. Remove from the heat and allow to cool.
1. For Sharbat, use 1 or 2 tablespoons of prepared badam syrup and mix with cold milk.
1. Add few drops of Kewra essence and saffron strands.
1. Mix everything well and serve chilled with ice cubes.
1. Serve Badam Sharbat along with Whole Wheat Flour Chakli and Leftover Roti Cutlet to your guests.
