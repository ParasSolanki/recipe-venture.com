### Ingredients

- 1/2 Ripe Bananas - spotty.
- Strawberries - as needed.
- 1 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 2 tablespoon Sugar.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- Salt - a pinch.
- Butter - or coconut oil to cook as needed..
- 1/2 cup Buttermilk - slightly thick.
- 1/4 teaspoon Baking soda.
- Chocolate sauce - as needed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Oat Banana Pancakes recipe, add oats, baking powder, baking soda, salt, sugar in a mixer/blender and powder this is into a flour.
1. Now add banana, buttermilk and vanilla extract to the mixer jar and blend this along with the oat flour into a smooth batter.
1. Add a few tablespoons more buttermilk if the batter becomes too thick.
1. Now spoon 2 tablespoons of batter on to a hot griddle, cook it on low heat flipping both sides using butter/coconut oil.
1. Serve it with sliced strawberries and chocolate sauce.
1. These pancakes brown very quickly, keep the flame low and let it cook through and flip.
1. Serve Oat Banana Pancakes along with Instant Masala Beans Idli and Masala Chai for breakfast on a weekend.
