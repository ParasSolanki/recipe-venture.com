### Ingredients

- 3 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina) - roughly chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 1 Apples - sliced and cut into quarters.
- 1 Cucumbers - sliced and cut into semi circles.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper corns - crushed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Apple Cucumber Salad Recipe, in a large mixing bowl, combine the cucumbers with the apples, honey, lemon juice, crushed black pepper and salt to taste.
1. Add a few chopped mint leaves and toss well.
1. Keep the Apple Cucumber Salad chill until ready to serve.
1. Serve Apple Cucumber Salad Recipe along with Lemon Coriander Soup Recipe and Cheesy Grilled Sandwich With Smoked Chicken Recipe or Broccoli Paneer Mayo Sandwich Recipe for a complete meal.
