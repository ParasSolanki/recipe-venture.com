### Ingredients

- Ice cubes - optional.
- Vanilla Extract - just a dash.
- 1 Mango (Ripe) - chopped.
- 3 tablespoons Rolled Oats.
- 1 Ripe Bananas - 2 small if it is the Elaichi Banana variety.
- Sugar - optional.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt) - fresh.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Banana And Mango Smoothie Recipe, peel and chop the mango and banana into a blender.
1. Add in the yogurt, rolled oats, vanilla and ice cubes and blend until smooth.
1. Taste the smoothie, if not sweet enough add required sugar and blend again.
1. Pour the Banana and Mango Smoothie into  2 tall glasses and serve chilled along with Paneer Bhurji Wraps for a filling breakfast or just like that on a warm summer afternoon.
