### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- Fresh coconut - Coconut bits for garnish.
- 1 tablespoon Panch Phoran Masala.
- Salt - to taste.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped for garnish.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 inch Ginger - grated.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 2 tablespoons Curry powder - (or garam masala powder).
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Coconut milk.
- Lemon wedges - as required.
- 1 cup Fresh Green Toor Dal (Pigeon Pea).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lentil Dal with Coconut Milk Recipe, start by washing the lentils clean.
1. Pressure cook the lentils for 5-6 whistles in water, salt and turmeric.
1. Wait for the pressure to release naturally.
1. Once done, open the lid and set it aside.
1. Drain and reserve the water.
1. Heat a tablespoon of ghee in a kadhai or deep cauldron/ dutch oven on medium heat.
1. Once hot, add the panch phoran and wait till the seeds crackle.
1. Add the dried red chilies broken in half to it and roast it for a few seconds.
1. Finally stir in the  ginger, garlic, green chillies to it.
1. Saute for 2 mins on medium flame ensuring it doesn't burn.
1. Add the chopped onion to it and all the masala powders, give it a stir for another 3-4 minutes till onions are soft and golden brown.
1. Add the chopped up tomatoes and fry the masala till the tomatoes are mixed in for another 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Add just a dash of water if it sticks to the bottom of the pan.
1. Once the tomatoes soften, add the cooked lentils to the masala and give it a good stir.
1. Add 1 cup of the reserved lentil water to it, and the coconut milk.
1. Check the salt and accordingly.
1. Allow the dal to come to a boil and turn off the heat.
1. Stir in the chopped coriander leaves and add the juice from the lemon.
1. Serve the Lentil Dal with Coconut Milk garnish of coconut bits along with a steaming bowl of steamed rice, pickle and papad for an easy trip down to your grannyâ€™s.
