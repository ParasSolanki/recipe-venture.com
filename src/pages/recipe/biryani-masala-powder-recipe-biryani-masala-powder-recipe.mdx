### Ingredients

-  5 tablespoons coriander seeds.
-  6 cardamom.
-  5 Long.
-  2 tablespoons cumin seeds.
-  2 tablespoons whole pepper.
- 2 bay leaves.
-  1/4 small Spoon Nutmeg Powder.
-  2 tablespoons celery.
-  3 stone flowers.
-  1 large cardamom.
-  3 mace.
-  2 inch Cinnamon.
-  3 star anis.

### Instructions

1. To make Biryani Masala Powder recipe, firstly put coriander seeds, whole black pepper in a pan and roast it well for 10 to 15 seconds.
1. Now add cumin, long, cinnamon and roast it for 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add bay leaves, celery, cardamom, big cardamom, mace, stone flower, star anise, nutmeg powder and bake it for 1 minute.
1. Turn off the gas and allow this mixture to cool.
1. After it cools down, put it in a mixer grinder and grind it into a powder.
1. Use Biryani Masala Powder recipe to make Gobi Biryani or Chicken Biryani.
1. You can serve this biryani with raita of your choice.
