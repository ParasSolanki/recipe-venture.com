### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Dark soy sauce.
- 2 sprig Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 5 cloves Garlic - sliced thinly.
- 1 Bok Choy - sliced big.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - thinly sliced.
- 1 Lotus Stem - thinly sliced.
- 1 cup Button mushrooms - cut into quarters.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Honey.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Fujian Style Green Vegetables Stir Fry Recipe by heating the flat bottomed skillet with oil, add sliced garlic and saute for few seconds.
1. Then add all the vegetables and saute till it is cooked, you can add some salt and water and cover it so that cooking takes place much faster.
1. Once the vegetables are cooked add the remaining ingredients to make the sauce.
1. Give it one last stir-fry and sprinkle some chopped spring onions.
1. Serve the Fujian Style Green Vegetables Stir Fry with Sichuan style Fried Rice to enjoy your Lunch meal.
