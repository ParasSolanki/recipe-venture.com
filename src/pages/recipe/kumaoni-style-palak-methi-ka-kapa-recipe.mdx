### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard oil.
- 1 cup Methi Leaves (Fenugreek Leaves).
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 tablespoon Rice flour.
- 2 cups Spinach.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kumaoni Style Palak & Methi Ka Kapa Recipe, wash the greens in water, drain all the water and chop them finely.
1. Heat a sauce pan with mustard oil over medium heat; add cumin seeds and dry red chillies and allow them to splutter for few seconds.
1. Add the asafoetida and saute for a few seconds.
1. Add the chopped Spinach and Methi leaves into the pan and stir fry until it softens.
1. Once the Palak & Methi becomes soft, sprinkle rice flour, salt, turmeric powder, red chilli powder and sugar and saute the Palak & Methi Ka Kapa on high heat.
1. The rice flour will absorb the excess water and help thicken the Palak & Methi Ka Kapa.
1. Once the Palak & Methi Ka Kapa thickens, turn off the heat.
1. Check the salt and seasonings and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Transfer the Palak & Methi Ka Kapa to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve the Kumaoni Style Palak & Methi Ka Kapa Recipe along with Steamed rice, Punjabi Rajma Masala Recipe and papad by the side.
