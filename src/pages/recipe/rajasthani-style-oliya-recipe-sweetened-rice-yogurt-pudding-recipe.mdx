### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 4 tablespoon Sugar - change according to your taste.
- 1 tablespoon Cashew nuts - chopped.
- 4 tablespoon Milk.
- 4 Saffron strands.
- 1/2 cup Cooked rice.
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Rajasthani Style Oliya Recipe (Sweetened Rice Yogurt Pudding Recipe) by soaking the rice in water for 15 minutes.
1. Then pressure cook the rice for about 2 whistle.
1. Release the pressure naturally and allow it to cool down completely and mash a little.
1. Beat the curd and milk in a bowl to a consistency that you are looking for it can be thick or if you require it to be watery you can add some water.
1. Add the boiled rice, sugar, cardamom powder and mix well.
1. Now to serve just add few strand of saffron over the top and some chopped cashew nuts.
1. Serve the Rajasthani Style Oliya Recipe (Sweetened Rice Yogurt Pudding Recipe) as a main dish during your fasting season along with a glass of Gajjar Lauki Juice Recipe (Bottle Gourd Carrot Juice).
1. You can also serve it after Rajasthani Dal Muthiya Recipe to have a light dessert.
