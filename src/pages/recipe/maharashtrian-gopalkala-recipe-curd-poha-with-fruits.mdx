### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 teaspoon Raw Peanuts (Moongphali).
- 1/4 cup Cucumber - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Anardana Powder (Pomegranate Seed Powder).
- 5 Curry leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - scraped.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 cup Poha (Flattened rice).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Gopalkala Recipe (Curd Poha With Fruits), wash the Poha under running water and allow to drain in a colander.
1. (You can keep the colander on a fitting vessel.
1. In a big mixing bowl, take this drained Poha and mix with chopped cucumber, grated Coconut, pomegranate seeds, curd, salt and sugar.
1. Heat a tadka pan on medium heat and melt the ghee.
1. Add cumin seeds, curry leaves, peanuts and chopped green chilies.
1. Stir fry for a minute and pour this tempering over the Poha and mix well.
1. Served Gopalkala Recipe for breakfast or as an afterschool snack for kids or for breakfast.
