### Ingredients

- 1/2 kg Chicken - minced.
- 1 Inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Star anise.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 3 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 4 Black pepper corns.
- 4 Onions - chopped.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 15 Burger buns - for sliders (small).
- 3 Kashmiri dry red chillies.
- 2 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 3 Cloves (Laung).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 tablespoon Butter.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2-3 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 6 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 1-1/2 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 2 Green Chillies - slit lengthwise.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Indian Chicken Keema Sliders Recipe, firstly roast all the masalas including cumin seeds, cinnamon stick, cloves, black pepper corns, coriander seeds, star anise and Kashmiri dry red chillies at low heat in a flat pan till fragrant.
1. Set it aside.
1. Once cool, powder them or pound in a pestle and mortar.
1. Heat the oil in a pan on medium-high flame.
1. Once the oil is hot and simmering, add the onion, ginger, garlic and green chillies.
1. Keep and eye on it and keep saut√©ing for about 15-20 minutes till the onions moisture and start to turn brown.
1. Reduce the flame to low, add the ground spices.
1. Saute for 20 seconds and add in the onions.
1. Add the chopped tomatoes with their water and keep cooking till the tomatoes soften and turn mushy.
1. If the mixture is burning, add a splash of water.
1. This should take another 10 minutes.
1. Add yogurt in 3 batches.
1. Mixing well every time you add the yogurt.
1. Cook for about 2 minutes.
1. Next add in the chicken keema and with your ladle break any clumps and mix well.
1. Do this step quickly before the chicken starts to cook to avoid lumps.
1. Add water if required, cook the chicken in the spices for about 10-15 minutes till the curry starts to simmer.
1. Once it simmers (bubbles on the surface) cook for another 5 minutes to ensure its done.
1. Finish with juice of one whole Lemon and Coriander Leaves.
1. Toast the slider buns with little bit of butter.
1. Scoop the chicken keema inside and serve.
1. Serve Indian Chicken Keema Sliders along with other starter such as Paneer Tikka and a chilled glass of your favourite Mocktail for house parties.
