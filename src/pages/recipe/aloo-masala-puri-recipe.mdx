### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 1/4 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Potato (Aloo) - boiled and mashed.
- 1/4 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Aloo Masala Puri recipe, firstly add wheat flour to mashed potato along with red chili powder, coriander powder, asafoetida, turmeric powder, carom seeds, fennel seeds, sesame seeds, coriander leaves, salt and oil.
1. Mix them well and add water so as to form a dough.
1. Keep it aside for few minutes.
1. Make small round balls of this dough and roll them in the form of a Puri.
1. Apply oil on the rolling pin and rolling board and carefully roll them in to Puri.
1. Fry them in a deep fry pan and it is ready to be served.
1. Serve Aloo Masala Puri with Aam Ka Achaar and Lauki Raita for breakfast or lunch.
