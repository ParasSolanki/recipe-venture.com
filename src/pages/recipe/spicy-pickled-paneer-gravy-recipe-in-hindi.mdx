### Ingredients

-  1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder.
-  2 onions - grated.
-  1 tablespoon ginger garlic paste.
-  1 Teaspoon Kasuri methi.
-  Few branch green coriander - finely chopped.
-  1 cup tomato puree.
-  1 teaspoon fennel.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin powder.
-  1 teaspoon celery.
-  2 tablespoons pickle - mango pickle with spices.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon turmeric Powder.
-  Ghee - or oil.
- 250 grams Paneer.
-  1 capsicum (green) - cut.
-  1/2 tablespoon red chili powder.
-  water - as per use.

### Instructions

1. To make Achari Paneer, first heat ghee in a pan.
1. Now add onions and cook till they turn brown.
1. Now take a mixer, add pickle, garlic ginger paste, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder and garam masala powder, add some water and grind it.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add celery, fennel and ground spices to it.
1. Let it cook for 2 minutes.
1. After 2 minutes add capsicum to it, mix and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. After 4 minutes, add tomato puree, some water and cover the pan.
1. Let it cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
1. After 7 minutes add paneer, kasuri methi and salt.
1. Mix and let it cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. After 3 minutes, turn off the gas and garnish with green coriander.
1. Serve Achari Paneer with Dal Fry, Phulka and Cumin Rice for dinner.
