### Ingredients

- 12 Badam (Almond) - roasted and chopped.
- 1 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal) - instant one.
- 1/4 cup Ghee - or butter.
- 1 cup Brown Sugar (Demerara Sugar).
- 2 tablespoon Blueberries - dry.
- 1 teaspoon All spice powder.

### Instructions

1. To prepare the Nutty Oats and Berry Truffle Recipe, in a food processor transfer oats and sugar and bringing to a fine crumb.
1. Set aside.
1. Transfer this mixture into a large bowl and add the all-spice powder, blueberries, almonds and mix well.
1. Stir in the ghee or butter and combine well.
1. You will now need to work quickly and shape them into round bite size balls.
1. The mixture will begin to sort of harden, so quickly shape them into small round balls.
1. Roll the oat bites onto slivered almonds and the Nutty Oats and Berry Truffle are now ready to snack on or packed into the snack box for kids.
