### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil - (more to smear).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 3/4 cup Onions - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/3 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 5 Papad - fried and crushed.
- 1-1/2 cups Whole Wheat Flour - kneaded to dough.
- 1/4 teaspoon Garam masala powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Papad Parathas, firstly fry the papads and crushed them in a bowl.
1. In an another bowl, mix finely chopped onions, cilantro, red chilli powder, garam masala, chaat masala, some salt and 1 teaspoon oil.
1. Mix everything well and keep it aside.
1. Mix crushed papads and the onion masala when you are ready to roll the Parathas.
1. Roll out the Paratha into medium roti size and place 1-2 tablespoon of papad stuffing on the centre of Parathas.
1. Close all the edges in a middle and make balls out of it.
1. Roll the stuffed papad ball again and make Paratha.
1. Place the paratha on a heated Tawa or pan.
1. Cook it as you would cook plain paratha.
1. Cook one side with oil or ghee until light golden brown spots appear on one side.
1. Repeat the same process for the naming dough and masala and make all the parathas like this.
1. Serve Papad Parathas with Dhaniya Pudina Chutney and Lauki Raita.
