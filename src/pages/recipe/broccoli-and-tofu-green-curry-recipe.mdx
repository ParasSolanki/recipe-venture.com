### Ingredients

- Vegetable stock - as required.
- 1 inch Galangal - peeled and sliced (or use ginger).
- Salt - to taste.
- Lemon wedges - to serve.
- 250 grams Tofu - cut into cubes.
- 200 ml Coconut milk.
- 4-5 Thai basil leaves.
- 1 Lemongrass - stalk.
- 3 Garlic - minced.
- 3-4 Shallots - chopped.
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - washed and chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Soy sauce - or thai fish sauce.
- 2 Kaffir lime leaves - torn (or use dried leaves).
- 1 Broccoli - washed and cut into florets.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Broccoli and Tofu green curry recipe, first, blend the ingredients mentioned under “Curry Paste” to a fine paste, using a mortar and pestle or food processor.
1. Heat oil in a Kadai and add the curry paste.
1. Sauté for 1-2 minutes till you get a nice aroma.
1. Add the broccoli florets, mix and stir fry for 1-2 minutes.
1. Add the Tofu and stir for other 1-2 minutes.
1. Add 1/2 - 1 cup of the vegetable stock, soy sauce, and coconut milk.
1. Stir and cook for 7-8 minutes till it thickens a little.
1. Adjust the consistency of the Broccoli and Tofu green curry as desired.
1. Serve the green curry recipe hot with cooked Rice and lemon wedges on the side.
