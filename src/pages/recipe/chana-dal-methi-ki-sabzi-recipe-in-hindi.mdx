### Ingredients

-  1/2 teaspoon coriander powder.
-  oil - as per use.
- 1 cup chana dal.
-  1 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  4 curry leaves.
-  1/2 teaspoon asafoetida.
-  water - 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds.
-  1/2 cup fenugreek seeds.
-  1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  1/2 teaspoon mango powder.
-  salt - as per use.

### Instructions

1. To make chana dal and fenugreek vegetable, first boil chickpeas and fenugreek seeds together.
1. Cook till soft.
1. Remove water and keep it aside.
1. Now heat oil in a pan.
1. Add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, asafoetida and curry leaves.
1. After 10 seconds, add gram and fenugreek to it.
1. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, mango powder, coriander powder and salt after 1 minute.
1. Mix all thoroughly and cook for next 3 to 5 minutes.
1. Finally, turn off the gas and garnish the vegetables with green coriander.
1. Serve the gram dal and fenugreek vegetable along with Gujarati kadhi and phulka.
