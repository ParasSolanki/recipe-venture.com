### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 2 teaspoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 3-1/2 cups Vegetable stock.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - cut into thin strips.
- 5 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 1 Red onion - sliced.
- 1 cup Tomatoes - canned.
- 1/2 cup Spinach Leaves (Palak).
- Red Chilli flakes - A pinch (optional).
- 1/4 cup Cheese - plus more for topping (optional).
- 1 cup Pasta  Fusilli - or any pasta you like.
- 1 Green zucchini - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning.
- 1 cup Black beans - drained and rinsed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the One Pot Vegetable Pasta with Beans recipe, heat oil in a skillet add minced garlic and fry for half a minute till it starts turning light brown.
1. Add all the vegetables including onions, tomatoes, bell pepper, zucchini and spinach.
1. Let it cook for about 5 to 6 minutes.
1. Add in the pasta, vegetable stock, seasoning into the pan.
1. Stir, cover, and bring to a rolling boil.
1. Cover the pan, reduce heat to medium-low and cook it for about 12-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
1. There should be 1/4 to 1/2 inch of liquid in the bottom of the pot when it is done.
1. Remove from heat and stir in the black beans and cheese (optional).
1. Let it rest for 5 minutes to warm the beans, melt the cheese, and absorb the excess liquid.
1. Serve One Pot Vegetable Pasta With Beans with Minestrone Soup and a glass of wine for a light and healthy weekend dinner.
