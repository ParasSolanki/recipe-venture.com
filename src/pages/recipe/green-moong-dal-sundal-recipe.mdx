### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 cup Green Moong Sprouts.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Green Moong Dal Sundal Recipe, we need sprouted green moong dal.
1. You can either use store bought ones or learn learn How to make Sprouts at Home.
1. Heat a kadai with oil on medium heat, once the oil is hot, add in the mustard seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Once the mustard seeds have crackled add the cumin seeds and let them sizzle.
1. Once done add asafoetida, slit green chillies and curry leaves.
1. Once the curry leaves splutter for a few seconds, add in the sprouted green moong dal and salt to taste.
1. Mix well to combine and cover and cook the Green Moong Dal Sundal for about 4-5 minutes until the moong sprouts are steamed through and cooked.
1. Once done, open the lid, add in the grated coconut, lemon juice, coriander leaves and give it a stir.
1. Check the salt and adjust to taste accordingly.
1. Turn off the heat, toss the Green Moong Dal Sundal well, and serve.
1. Serve Green Moong Dal Sundal Recipe as an evening snack along with Whole Wheat Flour Chakli Recipe or serve it as part of a meal along with Steamed Rice and Milagu Rasam Recipe and Elai Vadam Recipe for a complete weekday meal.
