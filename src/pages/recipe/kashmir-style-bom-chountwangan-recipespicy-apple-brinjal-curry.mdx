### Ingredients

- 1 Brinjal (Baingan / Eggplant).
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Apple.
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 2 teaspoon Fennel Powder.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Kashmir Style Bom Chount-Wangan Recipe, cut the apple and brinjal to wedges .
1. Fry the apple and brinjal in a pan and keep it aside aside.
1. You can fry them separately as well till it is golden brown.
1. In the same pan, add cumin with the left over hot oil.
1. Then add red chilli powder, fennel seed powder and stir with 1/4 th cup water till the water evaporates.
1. Add the fried apples and brinjal into the pan and mix well.
1. Add garam masala and salt  and keep it in on low flame for 5 minutes and take off heat.
1. Serve Kashmir Style Bom Chount - Wangan Recipe with Spiced carrot pulao recipe or plain rice and Nadru ke kabab.
