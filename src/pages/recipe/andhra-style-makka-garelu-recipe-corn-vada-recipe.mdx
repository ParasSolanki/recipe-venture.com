### Ingredients

- 2 Green Chillies.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- 1/4 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1 cup Sweet corn.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Kalonji (Onion Nigella Seeds).
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Andhra Style Makka Garelu Recipe, blitz the corn in a mixer without water to a very chunky mixture.
1. Transfer to a bowl, add the remaining ingredients including the besan and combine to make a thick garelu mixture.
1. Check the salt and adjust to taste accordingly.
1. Heat oil for deep frying.
1. Once the oil is hot take small portions of the makka garelu mixture, flatten it to make a round disc and slide into the hot oil.
1. Fry until the garelu is browned and crisp.
1. Ensure you fry over medium heat, so it gets cooked evenly from inside out.
1. Serve the Makka Garelu along with hot tea or dip it with a Date and Tamarind Chutney Recipe to enjoy your evening snack.
