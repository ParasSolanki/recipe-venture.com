### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Garlic - crushed.
- 1 tablespoon Dried oregano.
- 1 tablespoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 tablespoon Paprika powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Mint Leaves (Pudina) - (dried).
- 300 grams Chicken Wings - boneless.
- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil - (Extra virgin).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Dajaj Mechwi Recipe, clean the chicken well.
1. Pat dry on a kitchen towel.
1. Place the chicken in a zip lock cover and beat it well.
1. Now mix all spices.
1. Rub olive oil over the chicken.
1. Rub the spice mix all over the chicken.
1. Let it sit in fridge for 30 minutes to marinate.
1. Heat up your grill, if using charcoal grill, fire it up.
1. Now place the marinated chicken pieces on grill.
1. Grill until chicken is cooked, keep flipping at regular intervals.
1. Once grilled, remove Dajaj Mechwi Recipe from pan and serve immediately with crusty bread, Ratatouille with Grilled Cottage Cheese Steak and salad.
