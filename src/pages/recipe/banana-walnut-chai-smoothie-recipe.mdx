### Ingredients

- pinch Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1 tablespoon Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 1/2 Ripe Bananas.
- 1 Apple - chopped.
- 1 cup Milk.
- Ice cubes - few.
- 1 teaspoon Chai Masala.
- 3 Walnuts - halves.

### Instructions

1. To make the Banana Walnut Chai Smoothie, get all the ingredients together.
1. Blend the ingredients for the banana walnut smoothie into a mixer grinder until it forms a smooth drink.
1. Once ready, serve the Banana Walnut Chai Smoothie for a wholesome breakfast along with Chilli Cheese Toast Recipe - Breakfast Bread Pizza.
