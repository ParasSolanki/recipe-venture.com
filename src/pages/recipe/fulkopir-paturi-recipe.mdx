### Ingredients

-  3/4 teaspoon poppy seeds - powder.
-  1/4 cup coconut - grated.
-  1 teaspoon Rye - powder.
-  4 garlic - bud.
-  1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  Sunflower Oil - few drops for greasing.
-  oil - to grease.
-  1/2 banana leaves - steamer To put in.
- 1 cup cabbage - grated.
-  salt - according to taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  1/4 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  2 green chillies - chopped.
-  1 teaspoon mustard oil.
-  1/4 teaspoon sugar.
-  1/2 banana leaves - cook and cut on low flame.

### Instructions

1. To make Phoolkopir Paturi, first wash the cabbage thoroughly and soak in hot water for 2 to 3 minutes.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Now in a mixer grinder, add mustard seeds, poppy seeds, green chillies, coconut, garlic, some water and make a paste.
1. Now in a bowl add cauliflower, ground paste, salt, sugar, turmeric powder, red chili powder, mustard oil, cumin seeds and mix well.
1. Keep it aside for 1 hour.
1. Put a banana leaf in the steamer.
1. Now take small pieces of cabbage mixture and make it with your hands like gatta.
1. Put these gattos in the middle of a banana leaf.
1. Close all sides and seal with a toothpick.
1. Make all the gattos like this.
1. Steam for 12 to 15 minutes.
1. Serve Phulakopir Paturi with rice, phulka and cholar dal for dinner.
