### Ingredients

- 2 cups All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 1/3 cup Butter - softened.
- 2 teaspoons Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking powder.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 3 teaspoons Sugar.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Indian Spicy Masala Cookie Recipe (Khara Biscuit), in a large bowl combine all the ingredients together  and knead to make smooth firm dough.
1. Knead well for about 3-4 minutes.
1. Refrigerate the khara cookie dough covered for about 20 to 30 minutes.
1. Preheat the oven to 180 C.
1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
1. Remove the khara biscuit from the refrigerator and on a lightly floured surface roll the dough out until it has a 2 mm thickness.
1. Using cookie cutters, cut the rolled dough into desired shape and place it on the prepared baking sheet.
1. Place the tray in the oven and bake for about 20 mins or until the edges begin to start during golden brown.
1. Remove the Indian Spicy Masala Cookie (Khara Biscuit) from the oven and allow it to cool completely.
1. The cookies continue to cook as they are cooling.
1. Store them in airtight containers.
1. Notes: You can add Ajwain as well to the above recipe instead of cumin.
