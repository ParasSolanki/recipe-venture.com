### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons Cashew nuts.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/4 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 3 to 4 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 tablespoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 3 tablespoons Fresh cream.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.
- 1-1/2 cup Water.
- 1/2 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 Tomatoes.
- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - cut into thin strips.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a small bunch finely chopped.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1/4 tablespoon Sugar.
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar).
- 1 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - shredded.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Veg Chilli Milli Recipe, soak cashew in 1/4 cup hot water for 20 to 25 minutes.
1. After 25 minutes, drain water and add cashews to  mixer grinder along with ginger and  tomatoes and grind them.
1. to a smooth paste and keep aside.
1. Now we will make the gravy.
1. Heat a wok/kadai and add oil.
1. Add bay leaf, green cardamoms, black cardamoms, cloves, cinnamon and saute till all spices release a nice aroma.
1. Once the spices release a nice aroma add chopped onion and ginger garlic paste, saute on medium flame till ginger garlic aroma goes away and onion turns little brown in colour.
1. Now add tomatoes cashew paste, green chillies, turmeric powder, kashmiri red chilli powder, coriander powder and mix well and saute it on medium flame till the oil separates from masala.
1. Once done add the shredded cabbage, carrots, capsicum and green peas.
1. Mix all vegetables with masala and saute for 5 to 6 minutes.
1. Once the vegetables are half done add water and salt to taste.
1. Mix well and cover the lid.
1. Cook Veg Chilli Milli on medium flame for 10 minutes till vegetables are cooked properly.
1. Add more water if needed but make sure gravy should not be thin.
1. Once vegetables are cooked then add garam masala, fenugreek leaves and sugar.
1. Mix everything well.
1. Bring the Veg Chilli Milli to a brisk boil and add cream, garnish with coriander leaves and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer Veg Chilli Milli to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Veg Chilli Milli along with Boondi Raita and Pudina Tawa Paratha for a weekday lunch or dinner.
