### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3 tablespoons Jaggery - grated (adjust according to the tartness of mangoes).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder - adjustable.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera) - optional.
- 2 Mango (Raw) - peeled.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 2 tablespoons Mustard seeds - powdered.
- 1-1/12 tablespoon Mustard oil.
- 1/2 teaspoons Mustard seeds.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Instant Gor Kairi Lonche Recipe first add the mangoes, jaggery, salt to a mixing bowl.
1. Add the mustard powder over it and mix everything properly.
1. The next step is to temper the Gor Kairi Lonche pickle.
1. In a small pan heat oil.
1. Add the mustard seeds and once they crackle add cumin seeds.
1. Take the pan off the heat, cool a bit and then add hing, turmeric powder and chilli powder.
1. This ensures that the chilli powder will not burn.
1. Pour this hot tempering over the Mango mustard powder mixture.
1. Mix everything together.
1. Spoon the Gor Kairi Lonche into a glass bottle and keep it covered for about 30 minutes for flavours to mingle and allow the the mangoes to release some of its juice.
1. This mango juice along with the salt acts as a preservative.
1. The Gor Kairi Lonche Pickle be stored refrigerated in an airtight container for 3-4 days.
1. Serve Instant Gor Kairi Lonche Recipe along with Maharashtrian Chawli Chi Usal and Bhakri Recipe for a weekday lunch or dinner.
