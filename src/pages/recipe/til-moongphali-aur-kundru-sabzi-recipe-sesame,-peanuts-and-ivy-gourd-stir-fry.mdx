### Ingredients

- 1 pinch Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 2 tablespoons Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - coarsely ground.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 400 grams Tindora (Dondakaya/ Kovakkai) - sliced to roundels.
- 2 teaspoons Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - roasted.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Til Moongphali Aur Kundru Sabzi Recipe (Sesame, Peanuts & Ivy Gourd Stir Fry), wash and thinly slice tindora/kundru and keep aside.
1. Dry roast sesame seeds in a kadai till fragrant (just for a minute or so on medium heat) and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a wok/kadai and add mustard seeds, curry leaves, dry red chillies and let the mustard crackle.
1. Once the mustard seeds have crackled add the sliced kundru and stir well on a high heat for 2-3 minutes.
1. Now add turmeric powder, salt and let the kundru fry on low heat.
1. Once the kundru starts getting tender, add coarselyr grounded peanuts, red chilli powder, coriander powder and give a nice stir.
1. Cover the Moongphali Aur Kundru Sabzi with a lid and simmer and cook for till the kundru is cooked through.
1. Once the Moongphali kundru sabzi is done, add roasted sesame all over and give a quick mix.
1. Serve Til Moongphali Aur Kundru Sabzi along with Palak Dal, Boondi Raita and Phulka for an everyday meal.
