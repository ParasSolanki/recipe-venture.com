### Ingredients

- 2 Tomatoes - pureed.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Rajma (Large Kidney Beans).
- 1 cup Masoor Dal (Whole).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Green Chilli - slit.
- 1 Onion.
- 2 teaspoons Ghee - or clarified butter.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - juliennes for garnish.
- 2 teaspoons Coriander Powder (Dhania).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Masoor Dal Aur Rajma Masala Recipe, wash and soak the lentils overnight and then pressure cook with water and little salt using a pressure cooker for 3 to 4 whistles and keep aside.
1. Now, make a smooth paste of onion and ginger and keep aside.
1. Heat ghee in a pan on medium heat, add the cinnamon stick, cumin seeds and let it splutter.
1. Add in the onion and ginger paste and saute till it turns brown.
1. Add the slit green chilli and tomato puree and saute for 2 minutes.
1. Now, add the dry spices including coriander, red chilli and garam masala powder and saute for couple of minutes till the raw smell goes away.
1. Add the cooked whole masoor dal and rajma to the pan, enough water to your your desired conistency, season with salt and let it simmer for 10 minutes on low flame.
1. Once, the lentils are thick, switch off the flame and garnish with the ginger julienne's.
1. Serve Masoor Dal Aur Rajma Masala Recipe along with Roti's or Steamed Rice for the weeknight dinner.
