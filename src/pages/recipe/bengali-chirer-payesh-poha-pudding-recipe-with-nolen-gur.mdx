### Ingredients

- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 2 1/2 liter Milk.
- 2 tablespoons Rice flour.
- 1 tablespoon Raisins.
- pinch Salt.
- 1/2 cup Poha (Flattened rice).
- 8-10 Mixed nuts - almonds/cashews.
- 1 cup Nolen Gur - or brown sugar.
- 1 pinch Saffron strands - and a little more for garnish.

### Instructions

1. To cook Bengali Chirer Payesh/Poha Pudding Recipe With Nolen Gur, wash poha/chirer with water and strain the poha.
1. Soak the washed and drained poha/chirer in a cup of milk.
1. Also mix 1/2 cup of milk with rice flour and keep aside.
1. Soak saffron in a little milk.
1. Keep aside.
1. Boil the rest of milk and cardamom pods together in a Saucepan and bring to boil.
1. Stir occasionally.
1. Add rice flour mix in boiling milk and stir constantly.
1. Boil about 12-15 minutes till the rice flour is cooked and milk is condensed.
1. Now add milk soaked flattened rice/chirer then add the milk soaked saffron.
1. Cook chirer payesh for 15 minutes more or when flattened rice/chirer is cooked well and milk thickens considerably, add nolen gur or brown sugar.
1. Stir it and cook it till the date jaggery/brown sugar dissolves.
1. Then add chopped almonds/cashews and a pinch of salt, cook for a minute and remove from heat.
1. Pour the Bengali Chirer Payesh/Poha Pudding Recipe With Nolen Gur in a serving bowl, garnish with saffron strands/almonds/cashews and raisins and serve hot or chilled with sumptuous meal of Cholar Dal Recipe/ Aar Macher Jhol Recipe and jeera rice for dinner.
