### Ingredients

- 2 Stalks Celery - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - minced.
- 3/4 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera) - freshly ground.
- 1 cup Tomato - crushed.
- 1 Green zucchini - sliced.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - soaked overnight.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper - ground.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - chopped.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 500 grams Boneless chicken - breasts.
- Strawberries - few.
- 1-1/2 cups Water - (adjust).
- Onion - few slices.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi) - freshly ground.
- 1/2 teaspoon Paprika powder.
- Green zucchini - few slices.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Moroccan Chicken Stew Recipe With Sauteed Zucchini & Onion Dressing, get prep with all the ingredients first.
1. Season chicken with salt and brown in oil in a large sauce pan over medium heat until almost cooked through.
1. Remove chicken from pan and keep aside.
1. Meanwhile pressure cook chickpeas till cooked and soft, for about 5-6 whistles.
1. Saute onion, garlic, carrots and celery in same pan.
1. When it is cooked, stir in ginger, paprika, cumin, oregano, cayenne pepper and turmeric.
1. Stir fry for about 1 minute; then add water and crushed tomatoes.
1. Return chicken to pan, reduce heat to low and simmer for about 10 mins.
1. Add chickpeas and zucchini to pan and bring to boil once again.
1. Cover pan and cook for about 15 mins or until zucchini is cooked through and tender.
1. Saute a few zucchini and onion slices in a separate kadai and keep aside.
1. Serve Moroccan Chicken Stew Recipe With Sauteed Zucchini & Onion Dressing on a bed of Curried Couscous Recipe like it is served traditionally in Africa.
