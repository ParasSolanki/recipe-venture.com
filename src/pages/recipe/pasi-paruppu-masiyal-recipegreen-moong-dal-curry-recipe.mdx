### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Green Moong Dal (Whole).
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 cup Tamarind Water.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 2 teaspoon Ghee.
- 3 cups Water.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Pasi Paruppu Masiyal Recipe soak the green moong dal in 3 cups of water for at least for an hour.
1. Into the pressure cooker, add the soaked green moong dal, salt, turmeric, slit green chillies, tamarind water.
1. Cover the pressure cooker with its weight on.
1. Cook the dal in the pressure cooker, until you here 3 to 4 whistles.
1. Simmer the heat to low and continue to cook for another 10 minutes.
1. After 10 minutes, turn off the heat and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Open the pressure cooker and stir in the chopped coriander leaves.
1. The next step is to give the Pasi Paruppu Masiyal Recipe a tadka.
1. Heat the ghee in a small kadai, add mustard seeds, allow it to crackle; once it crackles add Asafoetida (hing), the red chilies and curry leaves and once the red chillies have roasted turn off the heat.
1. Add this tadka to Pasi Paruppu Masiyal and give it a brisk boil.
1. Check the salt and adjust to your taste and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer the Pasi Paruppu Masiyal Recipe to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Pasi Paruppu Masiyal along with Jolada Roti or Steamed Rice and Kachumber Salad Recipe With Cucumber, Onion & Tomatoes for a simple weekday lunch.
