### Ingredients

- 3 Green Chillies.
- 1/2 Inch Ginger.
- 2 cloves Garlic.
- Salt - to taste.
- 8 Curry leaves.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 Tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 Tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Seeds (Fenugreek Seeds).
- 2 Tomatoes.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 400 Grams Fish fillet - used salmon fillet here.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the South Indian Style Fish Curry Recipe, marinate the fish with turmeric powder and little salt and keep it aside for 20 minutes.
1. Make a puree of tomatoes, cloves, ginger, garlic, chilies and coriander leaves in a mixer grinder.
1. Keep It aside.
1. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
1. Once the oil is hot, add mustard seeds and fenugreek seeds.
1. Once the mustard seeds crackle, add chopped onion and fry till they become soft and translucent.
1. Once the onions are soft, add in the curry leaves and fry for about 10 seconds.
1. After 10 seconds, add the prepared puree and let it cook for about 5 minutes.
1. After 5 minutes, add coriander powder and cumin powder.
1. Mix well and cook for a minute.
1. After a minute, add the marinated fish and mix gently.
1. Cover and cook on low heat for about 10 minutes.
1. Open the cover and check whether fish is cooked.
1. Sprinkle some salt and serve hot.
1. Serve South Indian Style Fish Curry along with Steamed Rice.
1. You can also serve Rava Fried Prawns as a side dish along with it to complete the meal.
