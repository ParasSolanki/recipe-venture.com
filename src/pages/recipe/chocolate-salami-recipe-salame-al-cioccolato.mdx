### Ingredients

- 12 Digestive biscuit.
- 1/2 cup Butter (unsalted).
- 1/4 cup Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 6 tablespoons Cocoa Powder.
- 6 tablespoons Caster Sugar.
- 1/4 cup Cranberries - dried.
- 1/4 cup Pistachios - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chocolate Salami recipe, crush your biscuits roughly into small pieces by hand or food processor.
1. Add the pistachios and cranberries and mix well.
1. Keep it aside.
1. In a saucepan, melt the butter.
1. Add the sugar and whisk until it melts.
1. Add the cocoa and heat while mixing until smooth.
1. Add the milk, vanilla and stir until smooth.
1. Pour this chocolate mixture over the biscuits, cranberries and pistachios, and mix well.
1. Divide the mixture into two halves and place each on cling wrap.
1. Roll each into a log using the cling wrap and flatten the sides (round ends).
1. Refrigerate for at least four hours and unwrap from cling wrap.
1. Dust with icing sugar or cocoa, slice and serve.
1. Serve Chocolate Salami on its own after your scrumptious Italian Meal.
