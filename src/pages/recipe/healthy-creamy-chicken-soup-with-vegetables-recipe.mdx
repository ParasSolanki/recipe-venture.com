### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Sweet Potato - boiled and pureed.
- 300 grams Chicken breasts - boneless.
- 50 grams Green beans (French Beans) - cut into 1 inch pieces.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 clove Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1/4 cup Parsley leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Black pepper powder - crushed.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 2 Stalks Celery - roughly cut into pieces.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - cut into half and diced.
- 4 cups Lukewarm Water.
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar) - steamed.
- 40 grams Rolled Oats Or Instant Oats.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Healthy Creamy Chicken Soup With Vegetables recipe, heat half the water in a large saucepan, add the bay leaves and cinnamon and add the chicken along with salt.
1. Simmer the chicken in the water for about 10 to 15 minutes.
1. Drain the chicken and keep the water as will be using it as chicken stock.
1. Shred chicken into 1/4-inch-wide strips and keep aside.
1. In the same pot, heat a teaspoon of oil, add the garlic, celery and onion.
1. Sauce until the onion softens.
1. Add the carrots, peas, pureed potatoes, beans, salt and pepper and stir for a few minutes.
1. Add the chicken stock and the remaining 2 cups of water, oats and shredded chicken into the pot.
1. Stir well until the soup becomes a little creamy from the oats.
1. Cover the pot and allow the soup to simmer for about 10 minutes.
1. Adjust the salt and pepper to taste.
1. Stir in the chopped parsley, lemon juice and serve.
1. Serve Healthy Creamy Chicken Soup With Vegetables along with Garlic Bread and Spicy Basil Fried Rice.
