### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Coconut Oil - melted.
- 3 tablespoons Coconut flour.
- 1/4 cup Walnuts - chopped (optional).
- 1/2 cup Sweet Potato - pureed or pumpkin puree.
- 1-1/2 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1 Avocado.
- 1/2 cup Honey.
- 1/3 cup Cocoa Powder.
- 1/4 teaspoon Sea salt.
- 1/3 cup Chocolate chips.
- 2 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sweet Potato Avocado Brownie Bites recipe, preheat oven to 350 °F.
1. Line a mini-muffin tin with paper liners or grease with oil.
1. Combine all of the ingredients in a blender, mixer or food processor, except for the chocolate chips.
1. Blend the ingredients just until smooth.
1. Fold in 1/4 cup chocolate chips and optional walnuts.
1. Fill each muffin well with batter.
1. Top with remaining mini-chocolate chips.
1. Bake in oven for 20 minutes.
1. Remove from oven and let cool for about 5 minutes.
1. Transfer muffins to a wire rack to finish cooling before enjoying.
