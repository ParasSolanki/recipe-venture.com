### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2-3 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- Lukewarm Water - as required.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 5-6 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 cup Horse Gram Flour.
- 2 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kulith Shengole, get prep with all the ingredients and mix together the flour, half of the garlic, half of the chillies, a tablespoon oil, and salt in a Mixing bowl.
1. Add warm water to make a stiff, pliable dough.
1. Knead the dough well.
1. Make log shaped dumplings from the dough.
1. Cover and keep them.
1. In a Pan/Kadai, heat the remaining oil.
1. Add the mustard seeds.
1. Once they splutter, add the cumin seeds.
1. Add the rest of the garlic and green chillies.
1. Add about 1-1/2 cups water and bring it to a rolling boil.
1. Drop the dumplings one by one in the boiling water.
1. Cook covered till the dumplings are cooked through (about 7-8 minutes).
1. Serve Kulith Shengole alone, or with Roti, Bhakri, or Hot Steamed Rice for lunch.
