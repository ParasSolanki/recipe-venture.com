### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1 cup Carrots (Gajjar) - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Milk.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1 cup Elephant yam (Suran/Senai/Ratalu) - chopped and boiled.
- 1 cup Raw Banana - chopped and boiled.
- 1 tablespoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Instant Oats (Oatmeal) - for coating.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Oats and Vegetable Kebab Recipe by pressure cooking the yam and raw banana with water, salt and turmeric till it becomes mushy, boil it for about 6 whistles.
1. Release the pressure naturally.
1. Strain any excess water and mash it completely and keep it aside .
1. Heat a kadai with oil, add in the onions, garlic and ginger sauté till the onions become translucent.
1. Then add in the carrot and keep frying till the carrot gets cooked.
1. Then add in all the spice powder mix well and sauté till the raw smell of the chilli goes away.
1.  Add this into the smashed yam and raw banana mixture and mix well, check for seasoning if required.
1. Shape them into medium sized balls and flatten them into a cutlet.
1. Place the oats on a plate.
1. Dip them in the milk and coat it well with oats.
1. Heat a flat pan, drizzle some oil and toast them on medium heat till it becomes golden brown on either side or serve.
