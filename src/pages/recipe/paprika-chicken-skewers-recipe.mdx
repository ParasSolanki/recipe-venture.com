### Ingredients

- 1 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - cubed.
- 4 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 2 tablespoons Paprika powder - (adjust).
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - cubed.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cups Chicken breasts - chopped into small chunks.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - as required.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - freshly ground (adjust).
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - cubed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Paprika Chicken Skewers recipe, get prep with all the ingredients.
1. Take wood skewers and soak them in a bowl of cold water for about 20 minutes.
1. Meanwhile in a bowl, combine cubed chicken, paprika, garlic, salt and 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
1. Mix  well to combine.
1. Cover the bowl and place in refrigerator for about 2 hours.
1. Combine all the cubed peppers in another bowl.
1. Add a little olive oil, pepper and salt and mix them together.
1. Thread the chicken and peppers on wood skewers alternatively and broil in the Oven for about 20 â€“ 30 minutes or until done.
1. Turn the skewers every 5 minutes and brush with olive oil to get an even cooking.
1. Alternately, you can also cook them on tawa/Skillet.
1. Make sure you brush them with olive regularly to help them from not getting burnt and remain moist.
1. Serve Paprika Chicken Skewers as a party appetizer amongst others like Garlic Chicken Cheese Balls Recipe, Cheesy Garlic Broccoli Nuggets Recipe, and Loaded Baked Potato French Fries Recipe.
