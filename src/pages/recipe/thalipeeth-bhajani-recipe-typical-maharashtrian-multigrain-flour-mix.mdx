### Ingredients

- 125 grams Kala Chana (Brown Chickpeas).
- 125 grams Soybeans (Whole Soya dal).
- 250 grams Ragi Seeds.
- 250 grams Rice.
- 125 grams Moth Dal (Matki).
- 125 grams Wheat Germ.
- 125 grams Bajra (seeds).
- 6 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 125 grams Green Moong Dal (Whole).
- 2-3 tablespoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 250 grams Jowar Seeds.
- 125 grams Masoor Dal (Whole).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thalipeeth Bhajani, dry roast all the ingredients one by one in a heavy bottom pan.
1. Roast till lightly browned on low to medium heat until crisp.
1. It takes about 10 minutes to roast each ingredient.
1. Once it is roasted properly, cool the ingredients for Bhajani down completely.
1. Mix and grind to a fine powder in a mixer grinder and store the Thalipeeth Bhajani in an airtight container.
