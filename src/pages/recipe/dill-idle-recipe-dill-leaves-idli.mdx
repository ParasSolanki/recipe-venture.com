### Ingredients

- 6 Curry leaves - torn.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dry ginger powder.
- 2 teaspoon Black pepper powder - crushed coarsely.
- 1 cup Idli Dosa Batter.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 3/4 cup Dill leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 tablespoons Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal) - soaked (for 1 hour).
- Turmeric powder (Haldi) - A pinch.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sabsige Soppina Idli recipe, firstly heat oil in a tadka pan.
1. Add the mustard seeds, cumin seeds and let them crackle.
1. follow it up with the remaining ingredients in the same order.
1. Follow it up with the remaining ingredients in the same order.
1. In a bowl, add the cleaned and chopped dill leaves into the ready to use idli batter along with the soaked channa dal and the prepared tadka.
1. Mix thoroughly, pour them into idli plates and steam for 12-15Mix thoroughly, pour them into idli plates and steam for 12-15 minutes.
1. Serve hot.
1. Serve Sabsige Soppina Idli along with Mixed Vegetable Sambar and Tomato Onion Chutney for your healthy and scrumptious breakfast.
