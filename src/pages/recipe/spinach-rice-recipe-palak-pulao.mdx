### Ingredients

- 200 grams Spinach Leaves (Palak) - rinsed and chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 Tomato - pureed or finely chopped.
- 2 cups Cooked rice.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Sambar Powder.
- 2 tablespoons Ghee - (or sesame oil).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spinach Rice Recipe (Palak Pulao), heat oil in a heavy bottom pan over medium heat.
1. Add cumin seeds, grated ginger and saut√© for a few seconds.
1. Stir in the tomatoes, turmeric powder and saute until the tomatoes have softened.
1. Once the tomatoes have softened, stir in the chopped spinach and salt.
1. Cover the pan and allow the spinach to cook until soft.
1. When you open the pan you will notice that the spinach has released  a lot of water.
1. Turn the heat to high and allow the water to evaporate.
1. Once most of the water is evaporated from the spinach, stir in the cooked rice and sambar powder and combine well.
1. Check the salt and spice levels and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Turn the heat to low, cover the pan and simmer for a few minutes.
1. After a couple of minutes of simmering, you will notice that the rice has absorbed all the spices and has a rich green color.
1. At this stage turn off the heat and transfer the Spinach Rice into a serving plate and serve.
1. Serve Spinach Rice along with Tomato Onion Raita, Tadka Raita or any other raita of your choice.
