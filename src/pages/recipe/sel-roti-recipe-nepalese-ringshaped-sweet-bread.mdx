### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - as required for deep frying.
- 2 tablespoons Sugar.
- 2 tablespoons Ghee.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1/2 cup Rice.
- 2 tablespoons Water - + water for soaking rice.
- 1 tablespoon Rice flour.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sel Roti recipe, wash and soak the rice overnight.
1.  Next day, drain the excess water from the rice.
1. Take drained rice in a mixer jar and blend to a fine paste.
1. Take the paste in a bowl and add sugar, ghee, rice flour and cardamom powder to it.
1. Gradually add about 1-2 tablespoon water to it and whisk well till sugar dissolves completely.
1. Batter should be of a thick consistency.
1. Heat oil in a wide pan.
1. Using a ladle, pour the batter in a circle shape into the hot oil.
1. Reduce the flame to medium and let it cook on one side.
1. Flip it to the other side using a tong.
1. Fry till it gets light golden brown from both sides.
1. Remove and drain on an absorbent paper.
1. Store them in an air tight container once they cool down completely.
1. Sel Roti can be served as a tea-time snack with tea/coffee or other beverages.
