### Ingredients

- 200 grams Button mushrooms - finely chopped.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1/4 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 1/2 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - thinly sliced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli sauce.
- 2 clove Garlic - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Sweet corn.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 10 Raisins - finely chopped.
- 1 Homemade Pizza Dough - divided into 10 lemon sized balls.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - finely chopped.
- 1/4 cup Green peas (Matar).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Roasted Vegetables Pizza Pouch Recipe; heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan; add in the onion and garlic and saute until soft and golden brown.
1. Stir in the remaining vegetables including onion, red bell pepper, button mushrooms, cabbage, carrots, green peas, sweet corn with heat on high, cook them until lightly tender yet crisp.
1. Stir in the soya and chilli sauce with raisins and saute for a couple of more minutes.
1. Turn off the heat and allow the filling mixture to cool completely.
1. Take one ball of the pizza dough, roll it out thin circle and cut them into strips to use as knots for the pouches.
1. You can also use food strings to tie the pouches.
1. Take a portion of the pizza dough, roll it out into a 3 inch diameter circle.
1. Brush some oil at the center and place the filling mixture in the center.
1. Bring the corners together to form a sack and tie it with a strip.
1. Continue the same process with the remaining portions of the pizza dough and filling mixture.
1. Brush with some olive oil on the outside of every pouch.
1. Preheat oven to 250 C for 10 minutes.
1. Arrange the vegetable pouches in a baking tray and bake them for 10 to 15 minutes until the outer crust gets a golden colour.
1. Serve Roasted Vegetables Pizza Pouch with Roasted Tomato Sauce Recipe and with a soup like Cream of Pumpkin Soup Recipe for your light weekend dinner.
