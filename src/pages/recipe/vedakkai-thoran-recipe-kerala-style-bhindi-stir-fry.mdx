### Ingredients

- 10 Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 500 grams Bhindi (Lady Finger/Okra) - slit into half and cut into 1 inch pieces.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vendakkai Thoran Recipe, firstly prepare all the ingredients and keep them ready.
1. Heat a teaspoon of oil in a heavy bottomed wok/kadai; add the mustard seeds, curry leaves and allow it to crackle.
1. Add the chopped onions and saute until the onions become tender.
1. Once the onions are soft and tender, add the green chilies, cut Vendakkai (bhindi) and sprinkle salt.
1. Cover the pan, with the lid slightly ajar and cook the Vendakkai until it is soft.
1. Keep stirring intermittently until it is cooked through.
1. Once the Vendakkai is cooked through, add the turmeric powder, grated coconut and saute for a few more minutes.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Once done, transfer the Vendakkai Thoran to a serving bowl and serve warm.
1. Make the delicious Vendakkai Thoran Recipe and serve it along with Kuruku Kaalan (Raw Bananas in Coconut Curry) and Steamed Rice for a delicious lunch.
