### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- Salt - as required.
- 1/4 cup Rajma (Large Kidney Beans) - washed and soaked overnight.
- 1/8 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/8 cup Double Beans - washed and soaked overnight.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 Onion - sliced finely.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1/2 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1/4 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- Carrot (Gajjar) - peeled and chopped.
- 1/4 cup Potato (Aloo) - peeled and chopped.
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- Green beans (French Beans) - chopped.
- 1 cup Rice - washed and soaked for 10 min.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mixed Beans Vegetable Pulao recipe, pressure cook the rajma in a cooker and double beans with enough water until soft.
1. (Take care not to turn them mushy).
1. Wait till pressure settles.
1. Drain excess water and set it aside.
1. In a heavy bottom cooking vessel, add oil and to it add cloves, cardamom and cinnamon stick.
1. When the spices start to leave their aroma, add sliced onions.
1. Fry till translucent.
1. Now add ginger-garlic paste.
1. Fry for another minute and add boiled rajma and double beans.
1. Saute for a minute or two.
1. After a minute or two, add chopped potato, carrot, green beans, green chilies, garam masala and turmeric powder.
1. Saute for two minutes.
1. Add 4 cups of water to this along with salt.
1. Let it come to boil.
1. Now add rice (by draining the water, in which rice was getting soaked).
1. Gently stir and cook covered on low flame till rice is completely cooked.
1. Sprinkle lemon juice and garnish with coriander leaves before serving.
1. Serve Mixed Beans Vegetable Pulao with Lauki Raita, Beetroot Raita or any other raita of your choice and a roasted papad.
