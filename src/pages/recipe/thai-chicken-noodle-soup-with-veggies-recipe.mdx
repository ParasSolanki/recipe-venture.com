### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Sriracha sauce.
- 100 grams Chicken - shredded.
- Salt - to taste.
- 3 cups Chicken stock.
- 100 grams Rice Vermicelli Noodles (Thin) - boiled.
- 2 Stalks Celery - finely chopped with leaves.
- 30 grams Baby corn - sliced.
- 2 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 5 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - thinly sliced.
- 2 Stalks Lemon Grass - thinly sliced.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - sliced in wedges.
- 1 or 2 Fresh Red chillies - sliced lengthwise.
- 30 grams Green beans (French Beans) - cut length wise.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Thai Chicken Noodle Soup With Veggies recipe, keep the boiled noodles ready to be used.
1. Into a saucepan, add the chicken stock,  garlic, ginger, red chillies, celery stalks, lemon grass, sriracha sauce and bring to a boil on medium heay.
1. Now add gthe chicken, once the chickenhas been boiling for about 5 to 6 minutes and is partially cooked, add the vegetables - babycorn, beans, bell pepper and mix everything well.
1. When the vegetables and chicken are tender, add the boiled noodles and give it one more brisk boil.
1. Turn off the heat, stir in the chopped coriander leaves, lemon juice and serve.
1. Serve the Thai Chicken Noodle Soup With Veggies followed by a meal of  Chinese Fried Rice  and Healthy General Tso's Chicken Recipe for a simple weekday dinner.
