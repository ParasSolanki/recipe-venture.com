### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Garlic - peeled.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 1 Lemon - juiced.
- 8-10 Dry Red Chillies.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lehsun ki Chutney - Spicy Garlic Chutney,combine the garlic, chillies, cumin seeds, lemon juice, water and salt to taste in a blender and blend to make a smooth chutney.
1. Transfer the Lehsun Ki Chutney into a bowl and serve.
1. You can store the Lehsun ki Chutney -Spicy Garlic Chutney in a glass jar and serve as desired.
1. You can also add a little water before serving to make the Lehsun ki Chutney have a little saucy consistency.
1. Store refrigerated for about two weeks.
1. Serve the Lehsun ki Chutney recipe along with Vada Pav for a tea time snack.
1. You can even serve it with hot Gujarati Style Bhakri and   Homemade Curd for brunch.
