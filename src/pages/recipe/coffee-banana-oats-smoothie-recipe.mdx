### Ingredients

- 2 Ripe Bananas - whole ripe.
- 2 cups Milk - (chilled or at room temperature).
- 4 tablespoons Instant Oats (Oatmeal) - instant.
- 1 tablespoon Sugar - optional.
- 1 tablespoon Instant Coffee Powder.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Coffee Banana Oats Smoothie recipe, take milk into mixture jar, add oats and blend them well.
1. You can use chilled milk or milk at room temperature for this recipe.
1. Then add banana, coffee powder, sugar and blend until smooth and creamy.
1. Your smoothie is ready to serve.
1. Serve Coffee Banana Oats Smoothie along with Grilled Rajma Masala Sandwich for a weekend breakfast.
