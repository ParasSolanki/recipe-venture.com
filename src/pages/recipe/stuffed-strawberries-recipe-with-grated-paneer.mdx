### Ingredients

- 6 Strawberries.
- 1/2 Cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - grated.
- 1 pinch Saffron strands.
- 4 Tablespoons Condensed Milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Stuffed Strawberries Recipe With Grated Paneer, have all the required ingredients handy.
1. In a saucepan, add condensed milk and grated paneer together.
1. On a medium heat, cook them both by stirring continually without letting the paneer getting burnt.
1. Keep stirring and cook continually till the mixture gets all dried and leaves the bottom of the pan and begins to lump up.
1. Mine took about 8 minutes to do this part.
1. Add in the saffron strands, mix well and remove the saucepan from heat.
1. Allow to cool to room temperature.
1. Meanwhile you can prep the strawberries as well, if not done already.
1. Wash and pat dry strawberries.
1. With a sharp knife, cut around the top of each strawberry and scoop out the pulp in the middle.
1. Cut off a small portion of the strawberry's sharp bottom, to help them to be in place.
1. Scoop the prepared stuffing once cooled completely, into each hollowed strawberry.
1. Decorate with a few saffron strands or with finely chopped strawberry pulp you scooped out earlier.
1. Serve either chilled or enjoy as it is.
1. Serve Stuffed Strawberries Recipe With Grated Paneer along with other starters like Cheesy Rice Poppers Recipe and Cheese Garlic Bread Recipe for your next house party with family and friends.
