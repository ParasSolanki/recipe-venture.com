### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped for garnish.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 300 grams Ridge Gourd (Turai/ Peerkangai).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 4 Whole Eggs.
- 3 Green Chillies - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Scrambled Eggs With Ridge Gourd Sabzi wash, peel the ridge gourds and dice it.
1. Keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a pan, add the cumin seeds and let it splutter.
1. Add the ginger and green chillies and saute for a minute.
1. Add the onions, curry leaves and saute until the onions turn translucent.
1. Add the ridge gourd, coriander, turmeric, red chilli powder, season with salt and mix well.
1. Let the ridge gourd cook well.
1. This will take around 5 minutes.
1. Now, break the eggs into the pan and mix well.
1. Keep stirring until the eggs and ridge gourd combine together and let the scramble cook well.
1. Once done, switch off the heat and garnish Scrambled Eggs With Ridge Gourd Sabzi with coriander leaves.
1. Serve the Scrambled Eggs With Ridge Gourd Sabzi along with Panchmel Dal and Phulkas for a simplified weeknight dinner.
1. You can also serve this with Bread toast for your healthy breakfast.
