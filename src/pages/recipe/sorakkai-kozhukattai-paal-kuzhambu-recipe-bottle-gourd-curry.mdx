### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 4 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 Bottle gourd (lauki) - cut into small pieces.
- Salt - as per your taste.
- 1/4 cup Milk.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 tablespoons Sugar.
- Water - as needed.
- 2 tablespoons Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1/4 cup Rice flour.
- 2 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Sorakkai Kozhukattai Paal Kuzhambu, first keep the chana dal soaked in warm water for 30 minutes.
1. To make the Kozhukattai Or Rice Flour BallsIn a saucepan, boil 1 cup of water.
1. Once the water has boiled, add rice flour and mix well so that there are no lumps.
1. Rice flour thickens quite quickly so, be sure to continuously stir.
1. Once the temperature of the rice and water mixture is cool enough to handle, make oval/round sized balls of 1/2 inch size - small Kozhukattai and keep aside.
1.  To make the Paal KuzhambuIn a pressure cooker, add the diced bottle gourd along with salt, 1/2 cup water and a pinch of turmeric.
1. Pressure cook the Sorakkai /bottle gourd for 2 whistles and release the pressure immediately by running the pressure cooker under water.
1. Open the lid and keep aside.
1. Place a heavy bottomed pan on medium heat, add the soaked chana dal along with the soaked water.
1. Since the chana dal has soaked in warm water, it will be half cooked.
1. Add the cooked sorakkai/bottle gourd pieces, along with the cooked water When dal gets half cooked add bottle gourd pieces along with the cooked water.
1. Next add the prepared kozhukattai small quantities at a time and let it cook for 10 minutes on a medium flame.
1. Kozhukattais can stick in bottom, if there is not enough water, so adjust water quantity accordingly.
1. When kozhukattai gets cooked add grated coconut to it and stir it well.
1. Add in the sugar, milk and cook it for 2 more minutes and switch off the stove.
1. (Instead of adding plain milk, you can also mix 1 tablespoon f rice flour with milk and add it to a curry to give thicker consistency)For the temperingIn a tadka pan, heat 2 teaspoons of oil.
1. Once the oil is hot, add mustard seeds.
1. When mustard seeds crackle, add urad dal and red chillies, saute until urad dal turns brown.
1. Pour this tadka/tempering to our prepared Pal Kuzhambu and your Sorakkai Kozhukattai Paal Kuzhambu is ready to be served.
1. Serve Sorakkai Kozhukattai Paal Kuzhambu along with Steamed Rice or even Phulkas for a wholesome lunch.
