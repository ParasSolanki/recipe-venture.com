### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Baking powder.
- 2 Apples - diced small.
- 1/2 cup Badam (Almond) - thinly sliced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 3 cups All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 3 Whole Eggs - (For Eggless: 2 cups milk + 1/2 cup curd).
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 1 cup Sunflower Oil.
- 2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 1-1/2 cups Caster Sugar.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Apple Almond Cake Recipe by preheating the oven to 180 degree celsius .
1. Lightly grease a Bundt pan or tube pan.
1. In a bowl, mix together the apples, almonds, vanilla, and cinnamon.
1. In a separate bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
1. Using an electric mixer beat the sugar, oil, and eggs in a large bowl.
1. Add in the dry ingredients and beat until completely combined.
1. Fold in the apple mixture.
1. If you are making it eggless / egg free: instead of the eggs - add the milk and curd.
1. Scrape the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 1 hour and 30 minutes or until a tester inserted into the middle of the cake comes out clean.
1. Allow the cake to fully cool in the pan, about 1 hour, and then turn it out onto a plate.
1. Drizzle the apple cake with some of the honey.
1. The cake can be wrapped tightly and stored in the refrigerator for 3 to 5 days.
1. Serve the Apple Almond Cake Recipe as a Tea Time Snack or as a Party Dessert .
