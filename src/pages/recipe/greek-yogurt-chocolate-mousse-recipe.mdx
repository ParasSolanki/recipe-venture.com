### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Sugar - powdered.
- 2 cups Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1 sprig Mint Leaves (Pudina) - to garnish.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 200 grams Dark chocolate.
- 50 grams Fresh cream.
- 50 grams Dark chocolate - grated to garnish.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Greek Yogurt Chocolate Mousse Recipe, in a mixing bowl, combine the Greek yogurt and powdered sugar, vanilla extract and whisk until smooth.
1.  Allow it to chill in the refrigerator.
1. To make the ganacheHeat a sauce pan half filled with water and allow it to come to a brisk boil.
1. Cut the dark chocolate into small chunks and place them in a mixing bowl.
1. Add the cream into the chocolate.
1. Place the mixing bowl over the sauce pan to melt the chocolate.
1. Stir well until all the chocolate melts and gets well combined to a smooth mixture.
1. This process of melting the chocolate is the double boiler method - it helps temper the chocolate on a medium heat.
1. Mix the ganache vigorously, ensuring there are no lumps left.
1. You should have a smooth velvety ganache.
1. Now allow this ganache to cool down to room temperature.
1. Once the chocolate ganache has dropped to room temperature, gradually fold this into the chilled vanilla flavored Greek yogurt.
1. Make sure it is evenly mixed, with no streaks of the Greek yogurt or ganache visible.
1. Pipe this Greek Yogurt Chocolate Mousse into dessert glasses of your choice, be it shot glasses or martini glasses or even a deep serving dish.
1. Refrigerate the Greek Yogurt Chocolate Mousse for at least 3 to 4 hours and set it well.
1. Garnish with grated chocolate and some mint leaves and serve Greek Yogurt Chocolate Mousse Recipe chilled.
1. Serve this Greek Yogurt Chocolate Mousse Recipe after a meal of Pizza Margherita Recipe, Homemade Tagliatelle Pasta with Capers and Bell peppers Recipe and Ice Tea Sangria Recipe.
