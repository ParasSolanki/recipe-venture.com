### Ingredients

- 5 Whole Eggs - boiled.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Dijon Mustard.
- 1 tablespoon Dill leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper.
- 3 tablespoons Classic Mayonnaise (With Egg).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Deviled Eggs Recipe with Mayo and Mustard recipe, firstly boil the eggs.
1. In a pot, add water enough to submerge the eggs completely.
1. For the eggs to get to a hard boiled stage, it'll take up to 10 minutes in boiling water.
1. Turn off the flameRemove the boiled eggs from the hot water and put it in a bowl of cold water or in a bowl of ice cubes.
1. This will help to stop the cooking process and remove the egg shells easily.
1. De shell the eggs and cut them in half.
1. Scoop out the yolks and put them in a bowl.
1. Into the bowl containing the yolks, add the dijon mustard, mayonnaise, dill leaves and salt.
1. Mix until it gets to a creamy texture.
1. Taste for seasoning.
1. Add this Mayo Mustard Mixture into a piping bag.
1. If you don't have a piping bag, add this to a thick plastic cover (ones that you get your groceries in), cut off the edge.
1. Pipe this creamy Mayo Mustard Yolk mixture into the Egg White halves.
1. Once done, refrigerate the Deviled Eggs Recipe for 30 minutes.
1. Before serving the Deviled Eggs Recipe take them out and garnish with cayenne pepper and freshly chopped dill leaves.
1.
