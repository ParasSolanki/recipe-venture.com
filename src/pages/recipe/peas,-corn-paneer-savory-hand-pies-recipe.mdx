### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Amchur (Dry Mango Powder).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Fennel Powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/2 cup Sweet corn - boiled.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 inch Ginger - chopped.
- 1/2 cup Green peas (Matar) - boiled.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 All Purpose Pie Crust.
- 1/2 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - finely grated.
- 2 teaspoon Green Chutney (Coriander & Mint) - (4 sprig Coriander Leaves + 2 Green  Chillies).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To prepare Peas, Corn & Paneer Savory Hand Pies Recipe, get prep with all the ingredients including boiling corn, peas, chopping ginger and grating paneer.
1. Wrap the pie dough in a cling film and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
1. Coarsely grind peas and corn with ginger, chilli-coriander (Green Chutney) paste, and dhaniya pudina chutney in a Hand mixer.
1. Heat oil in Skillet.
1. Add cumin seeds when heated as well as hing.
1. Add the ground peas and corn mixture and saut√© till the mixture is dry on medium heat stirring at intervals.
1. Add red chili powder, coriander powder, fennel seed powder, garam masala powder, Amchur, salt and black pepper.
1. Add paneer and stir fry to mix well.
1. Switch off gas and let mixture cool down.
1. *To Bake Peas, Corn & Paneer Savory Hand PiesAfter 30 minutes of freezing the dough, preheat Oven to 450 degrees Fahrenheit and line a Baking tray with oven-proof parchment paper.
1. Roll the dough between two parchment paper sheets.
1. Cut into disc shapes of same size.
1. Stuff with filling leaving the edges clean.
1. Apply water to the edges and cover with another piece of circular rolled dough.
1. Seal edges with fork dipped in flour.
1. Give a cross cut in center and Bake in oven for 8-12 minutes rotating tray mid-way through baking.
1. (Baking time depends on temperature of oven as it often varies from oven to oven).
1. Keep a watch over them to prevent over browning.
1. If the dough starts loosening up while rolling it, refrigerate for half an hour till it hardens back.
