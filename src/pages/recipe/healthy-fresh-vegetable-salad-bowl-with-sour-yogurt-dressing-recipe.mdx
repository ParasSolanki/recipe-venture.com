### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Tzatziki.
- 1 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1 cup Tofu - cubed.
- 1 pinch Salt.
- 1 sprig Parsley leaves - chopped.
- 1 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1 cup Cherry tomatoes - sliced.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 1 English Cucumber - sliced.
- 1 Onion - cut into rings.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Fresh Vegetable Salad Bowl Recipe by keeping all the vegetables ready in a mixing bowl.
1. In another bowl whisk in the yogurt, lemon juice, salt and tzatziki to make the dressing.
1. Add this dressing to the vegetable salad and mix well.
