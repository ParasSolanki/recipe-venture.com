### Ingredients

-  2 teaspoons Leaf.
-  1/2 tsp sugar.
-  1/4 tsp turmeric powder.
-  1 tsp panchon masala.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1 tbsp mustard oil.
- 6 tomatoes - finely chopped.
-  1 tsp red chilli powder.
-  1 dry red chili - halved.

### Instructions

1. To make Asamis Bilahir Tok recipe, first heat mustard oil in a pan.
1. After heating the oil, add panch phoran masala, bay leaf, dry red chilli and cook it for 15 seconds.
1. Add tomatoes and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Now add turmeric powder, red chili powder, salt, sugar and cook it.
1. Reduce the flame and cook for 5 to 7 minutes.
1. Turn off the gas and serve.
1. Serve the Asamias Bilahir Tok Recipe with Besan Chila and Masala Tea for breakfast.
