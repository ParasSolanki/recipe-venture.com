### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Honey - optional.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1 Lemon - juiced.
- 1/2 cup Fresh Pomegranate Fruit Kernels.
- 15 Cranberries.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 5 Brussel sprouts - shredded.
- 1 cup Green Moong Sprouts.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bean Sprouts Salad Recipe With Brussel and Pomegranate; we will first get all the ingredients ready.
1. If you are making Green Moong Sprouts at home, follow this recipe.
1. It will a little more than a day to get your sprouts ready.
1. Click here to see the recipe of how to make Green Moong Sprouts.
1. Once you have all the ingredients ready; into a large bowl add all the ingredients and give it a good toss.
1. Check the salt and tanginess and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Serve the Bean Sprouts Salad immediately or refrigerator for a couple of hours and serve chilled.
1. Serve Mung Bean Sprouts Salad Recipe With Brussel Sprouts and Pomegranate along with a warm toasted Cinnamon Sugar Focaccia Bread Recipe or Fennel Pesto Pull-Apart Bread Recipe for lunch, snack or dinner.
