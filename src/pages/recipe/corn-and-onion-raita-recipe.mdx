### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- Water - as required.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Sweet corn - boiled.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 Onion - thinly sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Corn And Onion Raita recipe, first, whisk the curd and salt to taste with the required amount of water in a mixing bowl and keep aside.
1. Heat oil in a small pan.
1. Add cumin seeds, green chillies and onions and saute till the onions become soft and translucent.
1. Once done add the boiled corn, red chilli powder and the required salt and saute for a minute.
1. After a minute turn off the heat.
1. Let the corn cool a bit.
1. Once done add this mixture in the curd, mix it and garnish it with fresh coriander.
1. Serve Corn And Onion Raita along with Vegan Kofta Curry and Phulkas for a perfect weekday lunch or dinner.
