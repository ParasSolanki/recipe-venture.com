### Ingredients

- 3 Green Chillies.
- 6 Tomatoes - cut into strips.
- 1 Onions - cut into strips.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - as required.
- Black pepper powder.
- 2 Carrot (Gajjar) - cut into strips.
- Salt.
- 1 Green zucchini - cut into strips.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried basil leaves.
- 2 cups Spirali pasta - boiled.

### Instructions

1. To begin with Zucchini And Carrot Pasta Recipe, heat oil in a pan and add onions and sauté it till it turns golden.
1. Add chopped tomatoes, salt, dried oregano, dried basil, black pepper powder and mix well.
1. Let the tomatoes cook until they become pasty.
1. Now add sugar and mix it.
1. Your Tomato sauce is ready.
1. Keep it aside.
1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in another pan.
1. Add zucchini, carrot, salt, green chilies and mix well.
1. Add boiled pasta, mix well and sauté it for 2-3 mins.
1. Now add the prepared tomato sauce on top of the pasta and give it a stir.
1. Your Zucchini And Carrot Pasta Recipe is ready to be served you can great some Parmesan chesse on top.
1. You can serve Zucchini And Carrot Pasta with a slice of garlic bread on the side.
