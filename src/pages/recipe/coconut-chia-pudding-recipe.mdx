### Ingredients

- 1 1/2 cup Tender coconut water.
- Honey - optional.
- Mint Leaves (Pudina) - a few.
- 1/4 cup Chia Seeds.
- 2 tablespoon Coconut milk - thick.
- 1 Orange - zest.
- 1/4 cup Blueberries.
- 1 tablespoon Pistachios - crushed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Coconut Chia Pudding recipe, Mix chia seeds and coconut water in a bowl and set aside.
1. After 15 minutes mix again to remove any lumps and now refrigerate this for 3 to 4 hours or overnight.
1. Stir in the coconut milk just before you serve add the berries and the nut powder and zest 1 orange over the pudding and garnish with mint leaves.
1. Enjoy Coconut Chia Pudding with a cup of tea or a glass of juice for a healthy breakfast.
