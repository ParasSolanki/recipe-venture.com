### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli flakes.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cups Cooked rice.
- 1 tablespoon Dried Thyme Leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Butter.
- 3 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Rosemary.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Herbed Butter Rice Recipe With Rosemary & Thyme, keep the cooked white rice ready.
1. In a skillet, heat butter on low flame, add the chopped garlic and saute for a couple of minutes until the garlic turns into a light brown colour.
1. Next add in the rosemary, thyme, chilli flakes and salt.
1. Mix well.
1. Finally add in the cooked white rice and toss the Herbed Butter Rice well.
1. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve.
1. Serve Herbed Butter Rice Recipe With Rosemary & Thyme along with Chicken In Lemon Butter Sauce Recipe,  Sweet Potato Mash Recipe, Broccoli Salad Recipe with Roasted Onion & Peppers and finish off the meal with a dessert of Apple Pie Ice Cream Recipe.
