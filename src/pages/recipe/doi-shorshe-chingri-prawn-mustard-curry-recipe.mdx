### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Poppy seeds.
- 1 Green Chilli.
- Salt - to taste.
- 300 grams Prawns.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 tablespoon Yellow mustard seeds.
- 1/4 cup Water.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 tablespoon Mustard oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Doi Shorshe Chingri - Bengali Prawn Mustard Curry Recipe first clean and devein the prawns and marinate it with salt, turmeric, chilli powder and keep aside.
1. Soak the mustard and poppy seeds in water for ten minutes and grind along with green chillies and water to a smooth paste.
1. Next heat mustard oil in a wok and once the mustard oil is hot add the slit green chillies, lower the heat and  add the mustard paste.
1. Cook for 5 minutes on a medium flame, stirring occasionally.
1. Add the prawns, salt and the beaten curd and cook the Doi Shorshe Chingri on low flame for another 10 minutes.
1. Adjust the consistency of the Doi Shorshe Chingri gravy by adding water.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and transfer the Doi Shorshe Chingri to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Doi Shorshe Chingri with Steamed Rice , Aloo Parwal Sabzi and Mishti Doi Recipe  for a delicious meal.
