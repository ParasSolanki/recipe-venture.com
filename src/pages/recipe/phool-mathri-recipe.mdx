### Ingredients

- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- Salt - according to taste.
- 1 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- Lukewarm Water - for kneading the dough.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Phool Mathri recipe, In a bowl add maida, ajwain, salt and ghee.
1. Mix it and knead a tight dough using lukewarm water.
1. Cover it with a kitchen napkin and keep aside for 10 minutes.
1. Make equal size balls from dough and roll every ball into 4" diameter.
1. For perfect shape we can use a cutter or a lid.
1. Pinch every roll with the help of plucker to give it a phool/flower like shape.
1. Keep pinching in layers.
1. Heat oil in a deep frying pan.
1. Deep fry all mathri on medium flame till golden brown in colour.
1. Take them out on a kitchen towel to absorb excess oil.
1. Crispy and tasty Mathri are ready to serve.
1. Serve Phool Mathri along with Poha Chivda and Masala Tea for your tea time break.
