### Ingredients

- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Snake gourd (pudulangai) - finely chopped (approximately 1 cup).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - broken into pieces.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. To begin the preparation of Potlakaya Perugu Pachadi Recipe, first heat a small pan and add oil.
1. Add the finely chopped snake gourd pieces.
1. Fry for a few seconds.
1. Add salt and cover the pan.
1. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes.
1. Ensure the pieces do not get burnt, by checking in between.
1. Once the pieces are cooked, add grated coconut and mix well.
1. Turn off the flame, and let it cool down.
1. For the seasoning, heat a small pan, add jeera and mustard.
1. Let the mustard seeds splutter.
1. Add red chilli pieces, hing and saute.
1. If you prefer, add turmeric and put off the flame.
1. Transfer the cooked snake gourd pieces and coconut to a bowl.
1. Add curd.
1. Add the seasoning and mix well.
1. Serve this gentle Potlakaya Perugu Pachadi with hot rice for a weekday lunch.
