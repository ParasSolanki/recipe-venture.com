### Ingredients

- 1 cup Walnuts - or choco chips or caramel chips.
- 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1 1/2 cups Chocolate wafers - or coffee flavoured wafer biscuits.
- 1 3/4 cups Condensed Milk.
- 1 1/2 cups Chocolate chips - semi sweet .

### Instructions

1. We begin making the No-Bake Fudge Brownies by melting the chocolate in a saucepan or a microwave.
1. Add the condensed milk, wafer biscuits, half of the walnuts and vanilla essence.
1. Give it a stir and mix well.
1. Pour the fudge mixture into a non stick or lined 8 inch square pan and press it evenly.
1. Press the remaining walnuts on the top of fudge brownies.
1. Allow it to firm up by letting it cool to room temperature.
1. Once it is done, cut the brownies into 2 inch square and chill it in the refrigerator for some more time before serving.
1. Serve No Bake Fudge Brownies as a Dessert after your delicious meal.
