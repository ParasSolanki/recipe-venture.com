### Ingredients

- 1 cup Cabbage (Patta Gobi/ Muttaikose) - thinly shredded.
- 150 grams Veg Hakka Noodles.
- 10 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 4 tablespoons Red Chilli sauce.
- 1 tablespoon Soy sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 6 stalks Spring Onion (Bulb & Greens) - chopped.
- 2 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- Water - required.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 4 tablespoons Tomato Ketchup.
- 2 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- 2 Onion - thinly sliced.
- 4 tablespoons Schezwan sauce.
- 2 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spicy Schezwan Vegetable Noodles Recipe, first cook the noodles as per the instructions on the packet, with enough water, salt and oil.
1. Bring a pot of water to a brisk boil; When it boils, turn the heat down, add the noodles to it and cook for 5-6 minutes or as specified on the packet, till it is cooked al dente.
1. Take care not to over cook the noodles or they may turn mushy.
1. When the noodles are done, drain the noodles in a colander and rinse under cold water to stop the cooking process.
1. Sprinkle a few drops of oil over the noodles and toss well till they are well coated.
1. This will keep the noodles from getting mushy and stuck to each other.
1. Now you can keep them aside for later use.
1. To begin making the spicy seasoning, place a wok on medium heat.
1. Add sesame oil and warm it up.
1. Then add the finely chopped garlic, ginger, onions and green chillies.
1. Saute until the onions soften a little.
1. Add the capsicum, cabbage and carrots, sprinkle a little salt and saute until the vegetables become slightly tender.
1. You can optionally cover the pan to fasten the cooking process of the vegetables.
1. You do not want to over cook them, instead want them to remain crunchy.
1. Once the vegetables are done, add the schezwan sauce, the red chilli sauce, the tomato ketchup and the soy sauce.
1. Toss well to combine along with the vegetables.
1. Once well combined, add the boiled noodles and stir fry on high heat until the noodles get well coated with the schezwan vegetables.
1. =Give it a taste and check if you need more salt and adjust accordingly.
1. Once done, turn off the heat and transfer the Spicy Schezwan Vegetable Noodles to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve the Spicy Schezwan Vegetable Noodles Recipe along with Vegetable Manchurian Balls in Sweet and Spicy Sauce or Korean Stew Recipe With Tofu & Vegetables.
