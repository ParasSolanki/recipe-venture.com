### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 2 teaspoons Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 1 cup Vegetable stock.
- 1 teaspoon Garlic - paste.
- 10 Basil leaves.
- 1/3 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 2 teaspoons Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1/2 cup Arborio rice.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Tomato Basil Risotto Recipe With Grilled Chicken we will first make the risotto.
1. To make the Tomato Basil Risotto Heat a pressure cooker with olive oil on medium flame, add in the garlic, till it turns golden brown.
1. Next, add in basil leaves, and the tomato puree and cook, until it comes to a single boil.
1. To this add the arborio rice and continue to saute for 10-12 minutes.
1. Finally add in the vegetable stock, salt and close the pressure cooker.
1. Pressure cook for 3 to 4 whistles and turn off the heat.
1. Set Tomato Basil Risotto aside and let the pressure release naturally.
1. The rice will continue to cook while the pressure is still present.
1. To make the Grilled ChickenIn a mixing bowl, combine the chicken along with yogurt, ginger paste, garlic paste, lemon juice, garam masala, kashmiri red chilli powder and salt as required.
1. Allow this to marinate for about an hour.
1. Heat some oil in a grilling pan on medium heat, place the marinated chicken and allow then to cook till  well done.
1. Carefully flip the chicken and cook from another side as well.
1. Brush some more oil or butter on top while the chicken is grilling.
1. Once the chicken is soft, juicy, and nicely cooked, transfer to a serving plate.
1. Serve the Tomato Basil Risotto along with the Grilled Chicken by the side and serve hot.
1. Serve Tomato Basil Risotto Recipe With Grilled Chicken with a side of Broccoli Salad Recipe with Roasted Onion & Peppers, a drink of Apple And Ginger Cocktail Recipe followed by a dessert of Tiramisu Recipe (A Classic and Quick Italian Dessert).
