### Ingredients

- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 1 tablespoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoon Active dry yeast.
- 1/2 Red Chilli flakes.
- Lukewarm Water - for kneading.
- 1/2 cup Milk - luke warm.
- 2 tablespoon Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 2 tablespoon Honey - or sugar.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Italian Grissini Recipe- Homemade Breadsticks, take a small bowl and add warm milk, honey and instant dry yeast.
1. Mix them well.
1. Cover the bowl and keep it in a warm and dry place for 7-8 minutes till it becomes foamy.
1. In another mixing bowl, add all purpose flour and whole wheat flour and salt and mix till well combined.
1. Now after 7-8 minutes check on milk yeast mixture if its ready then add it to our dry ingredients slowly and start kneading.
1. Add warm water if required.
1. Keep kneading for 7-8 minutes till dough become soft.
1. Lightly oil a clean large bowl and gather the dough into a ball and place it in bowl.
1. Brush the surface of the dough with olive oil.
1. Cover it with a cling wrap and keep it in a warm place for about 1 to 1-1/2 hours or till it doubles in size.
1. Take the dough out and add carom seeds and chilli flakes to it if you like optional.
1. Knead it for a few seconds till even.
1. Do not knead it for too long.
1. Preheat oven at 200 degrees.
1. Meanwhile, Grease a baking sheet/tray with olive oil.
1. Cut the dough in 16-17 equal pieces and roll each part into thin rope of around 12-13 inches in length and approximately the thickness of a pencil, coat each rope with sesame seeds.
1. Brush some olive oil on a baking tray and place these ropes on it 1/2 inch apart from each other.
1. Bake Grissini ropes for 12-13 minutes, check after 12 minutes to see if done.
1. Bake till crisp and golden brown.
1. Remove Grissini from oven and allow to cool down and serve.
