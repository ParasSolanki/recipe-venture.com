### Ingredients

-  1 tablespoon red chili powder.
-  salt - taste accordingly.
- 1/2 cup carrots - grated.
-  2 cups yogurt.
-  1 tablespoon cumin powder.
-  1 spoon - boil.
-  1/2 tablespoon chaat masala powder.
-  peppermint - for garnish.

### Instructions

1. To make chakundar and carrot raita, first peel the chakundar and cut it into small pieces.
1. Now take a bowl and beat the curd in it.
1. Add cumin powder, red chili powder, chaat masala and salt.
1. Now add grated carrot and chakundar.
1. Mix well and garnish with mint leaves.
1. Serve chakundar and carrot raita with dal fry, potato vegetable and phulka for dinner.
