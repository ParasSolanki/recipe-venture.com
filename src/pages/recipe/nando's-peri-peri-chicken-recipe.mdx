### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 100 grams Nando's Peri Peri Chicken Marinade.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- 2 Fresh Red chillies - chopped (optional).
- 400 grams Boneless chicken.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Nando's Peri Peri Chicken Recipe, add the chicken to a zip-closed bag and pour the marinade over top.
1. Close the top and toss the chicken.
1. Marinate overnight.
1. Heat the oil in a shallow fry pan over medium heat, place chicken pieces in and cook until fully cooked.
1. Turn to the other side and cook till evenly golden brown.
1. Or you can deep fry them instead, as well.
1. This takes about 10 minutes.
1. Stir in coriander and chili and Serve with the lemon wedges.
