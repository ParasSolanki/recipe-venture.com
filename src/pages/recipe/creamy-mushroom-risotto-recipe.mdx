### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Onions - finely chopped.
- 3 tablespoons Fresh cream.
- Salt - to taste.
- Dried oregano - powder.
- 1 liter Vegetable stock.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely minced.
- 15 Button mushrooms - chopped.
- 3/4 cup Cheese - grated.
- 1 cup Arborio rice.
- 2 tablespoons Butter.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Creamy Mushroom Risotto recipe, in a heavy bottomed pan, add a teaspoon of butter.
1. To it add chopped onions and minced garlic.
1. Salute for about 3 to 5 minutes.
1. Add rice to it and mix.
1. Add some vegetable broth to it and bring it to boil.
1. Once they start boiling, reduce the heat and simmer till broth is completely absorbed.
1. Add the remaining stock and simmer until the rice is completely cooked and all the stock is absorbed.
1. If the stock dries up before the rice is cooked add little warm water .
1. Meanwhile, as rice is getting cooked, prepare the veggies.
1. In another pan, take a teaspoon of butter.
1. Throw in chopped capsicums and mushroom.
1. Add a pinch of salt and pepper.
1. Saute in between till the veggies get nicely done.
1. Once the rice is cooked stir in grated cheese, cream and mix till everything combines well.
1. Finally add roasted mushrooms, some crushed pepper and oregano powder give a gentle mix and serve hot creamy mixed vegetables Risotto.
1. Serve Creamy Mushroom Risotto along with Quick Bread Pizza for a weekend dinner with family.
