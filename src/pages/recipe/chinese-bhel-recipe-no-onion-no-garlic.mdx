### Ingredients

- 1 cup Wheat Noodles - boiled and fried.
- 2 Carrots (Gajjar) - thinly sliced.
- 2 tablespoons Red Chilli sauce.
- Salt - to taste.
- 10 Green beans (French Beans) - thinly sliced.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped for garnish.
- Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- 1 tablespoon Tomato Ketchup.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - thinly sliced.
- 2 tablespoons Soy sauce.

### Instructions

1. To begin making No Onion No Garlic Chinese Bhel Recipe , boil the noodles in a saucepan with hot water, a little oil and a pinch of salt.
1. Cook the noodles till half done.
1. In a saucepan, add oil and then add French beans and carrot strips and saute for 2 minutes.
1. Now add green capsicum strips and again saute for 2 minutes.
1. Add cabbage strips, red chilli sauce, soya sauce, tomato ketchup, sugar, and salt, mix it well and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Keep this mixture aside.
1. Deep fry the boiled noodles until golden brown, or air-fry them and once it gets to room temperature, crumble them using your hands.
1. Now add these fried noodles into the vegetable mixture and again saute for a couple of minutes.
1. Garnish Chinese Bhel with coriander leaves and serve immediately.
1. Serve the Chinese bhel  as a great munching option during evenings along with Masala Chai.
