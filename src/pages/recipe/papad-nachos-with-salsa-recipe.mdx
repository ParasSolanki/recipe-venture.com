### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Cheddar cheese - shredded.
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano - or mexican spice mix.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 2 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - Handful or parsley leaves.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder - or chilli flakes.
- 2 Papads - roasted.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Papad Nachos with Salsa, first mix together onion, tomatoes, garlic, red chilli powder, oregano and salt in a mixing bowl.
1. Add the lime juice and mix everything well.
1. Spread this Salsa over the roasted papads.
1. Sprinkle the grated cheese and grill in a hot oven for 1-2 minutes just till the cheese melts.
1. Sprinkle coriander or parsley leaves, sprinkle the Mexican spice mix and serve immediately.
1. Papad Nachos with Salsa can be served as a funky snack for a kiddie party or a potluck.
