### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4 Tomatoes - finely chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 pinch Sugar.
- 2 sprigs Curry leaves.
- 1 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- Salt - or rock salt to taste.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Tomato Curry Leaf Chutney With No Onion No Garlic Recipe by heating the kadai with oil and add all the ingredients.
1. Saute for about 15 minutes till the tomatoes become mushy and the raw smell goes away.
1. Once it has cool down , blend it in a mixer till it becomes a smooth paste.
1. Serve your Tomato Curry Leaf Chutney With No Onion No Garlic Recipe with Rajgira Panner Paratha Recipe to enjoy your morning breakfast.
