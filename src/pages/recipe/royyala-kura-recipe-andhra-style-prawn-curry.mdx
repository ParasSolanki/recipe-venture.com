### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 4 Curry leaves.
- 10 Prawns - Medium Size.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 2 Tomatoes - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1 Cloves (Laung).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Royyala Kura recipe, Firstly make sure that the prawns are clean & deveined.
1. After cleaning using a kitchen towel to pat them dry.
1. Next heat up oil in a pan and add bay leaves, cardamom, cloves, caraway seeds and roast them for few seconds on low heat.
1. Now add the chopped onions, curry leaves and cook them until the onion becomes translucent, .
1. Next add ginger garlic paste and cook the raw smell disappears.
1. Now add coriander powder, chili powder, salt, turmeric powder, garam masala and cook them for few seconds.
1. Next add the cubed tomatoes and cook until tomatoes become soft and mushy.
1. Cook until oil starts to separate from the spices.
1. Time to add the prawns into the pan and give it a good stir.
1. Cover and cook for 2 minutes and then continue to cook the prawns on medium heat without covering.
1. Garnish it with some fresh cilantro and serve.
