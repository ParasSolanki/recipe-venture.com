### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 2 tablespoons Red Chilli sauce.
- 1 cup Rajma (Large Kidney Beans) - soaked overnight.
- 1 teaspoon Dried Thyme Leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- Sunflower Oil - for cooking.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 cup Rice.
- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 cup Soy Chunks (Nuggets).
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cajun Kidney beans Rice with Soya Chunks Recipe soak the kidney beans and keep it ready.
1. You can soak the rice as well 30 minutes before.
1. The soya chunks as well must be soaked in 1 cup of water for 10 minutes.
1. Heat a pressure cooker with oil, add cumin seeds and add the onions, ginger garlic paste and saute well till it translucent.
1. Add the soaked kidney bean along with the water that was soaked in it.
1. Add dried oregano, thyme, red chilli powder, red chilli flakes, sugar, red chilli sauce and allow it to cook for 20 minutes.
1. You will see the beans will soften.
1. Add the soy chunks and rice, stir well till it combines.
1. Add salt and adjust according to your taste.
1. Add 2 cups of water and pressure cook for about 2 whistle and reduce the heat and cook for 5 minutes.
1. Serve hot with chopped coriander leaves over the rice.
1. Serve the Cajun Kidney beans Rice with Soya Chunks can be serve along with a cup of Lauki Raita Recipe and glass of cocktail to enjoy your cozy dinner.
