### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 teaspoons Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil.
- 200 grams Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cut into triangles.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 tablespoons Fresh cream.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 3 Tomatoes - pureed.
- 2 teaspoons Butter.
- 1 inch Ginger - cut into jullienes for garnish.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Restaurant Style Paneer Lababdar Recipe, make a puree of the tomatoes using mixer grinder and keep aside.
1. Heat oil and butter in a pan, add the cumin seeds and let it crackle.
1. Add the onion and saute until it turns translucent.
1. Add the grated ginger, tomato puree and cook until the raw smell goes away.
1. Add the kashmiri red chilli powder, cumin powder, garam masala and mix well.
1. Add half a cup of water and let the gravy simmer for 5 minutes.
1. Now, add the paneer triangles, crushed kasuri methi, mix well and let it simmer for another 5 minutes.
1. Switch off the heat, add in the fresh cream and mix well.
1. Garnish with ginger juliennes and serve hot.
1. Serve the Restaurant Style Paneer Lababdar Recipe along with Paratha, Aloo Gobhi Ki Sabzi and Methi Matar Pulao for the weekend brunch.
