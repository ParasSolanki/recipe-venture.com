### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 to 3 Dry Red Chilli.
- 1 Mango (Ripe) - peel & slice.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 tablespoon Jaggery.
- 1 tablespoons Coconut Oil.
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 3 Green Chillies - slit.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mambazha Pulissery recipe, peel and slice the mango.
1. In a deep pan, add 2 cups of water, mango slices, slit green chillies and turmeric powder and boil till the mangoes are well cooked and soft.
1. In the meantime add the grated coconut, jeera and jaggery along with water into a mixer grinder & grind into a very smooth paste.
1. Once the mangoes are soft, add the ground Pulissery paste to this.
1. After this keep stirring for a while so that the curry does not curdle.
1. Once the Mambazha Pulissery is thick, lower the flame.
1. Beat the yogurt lightly with a fork and add it to the curry.
1. Switch off the flame.
1. Heat oil in a heavy bottomed pan.
1. Add mustard seeds and let it crackle.
1. Add curry leaves and red chillies.
1. Let it crisp up and then add it to the Mambazha Pulissery.
1. Check the salt to taste and adjust accordingly.
1. Serve the Mambazha Pulissery Recipe-Mango Kuzhambu along with Steamed Rice & Kerala Vendakka Thoran Recipe (Okra Stir Fry with Coconut) a for a simple lunch or dinner.
