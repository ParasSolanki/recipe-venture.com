### Ingredients

- 6 Mackerel fish - cleaned and slit.
- 1/2 Cup Goan recheado masala.
- Salt - to taste.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - to shallow fry.
- 1 Cup Sooji (Semolina/ Rava).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Goan Style Recheado Bangdo Recipe | Stuffed Mackerel, remove the head and tail of the mackerels.
1. Slit them in the center all the way from head to tail.
1. If possible, remove the bone or else leave it on.
1. Wash them well for 3-4 times.
1. Apply salt and keep them for 5 minutes.
1. Now stuff in Recheado paste properly inside the Mackerels.
1. Rub a little of the spice paste on the outside of the fish too, on both the sides of the Mackerels.
1. Let them marinade for at least an hour.
1. You can keep them refrigerated overnight to get even more delicious recipe when done.
1. Coat them with semolina on both the sides.
1. Heat a skillet, add a tablespoon of oil.
1. Place these mackerels on top of the oil and let them cook on medium high heat for about 5 minutes.
1. Flip and let them cook on the other side too, till done.
1. Remove when they look all crispy brown on both the sides.
