### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil - for deep frying.
- 1/2 teaspoon Carrot (Gajjar) - grated.
- 1/4 teaspoon Soy sauce.
- 20 Wonton wraps - (3â€³ Square).
- 1/4 cup Britannia Cream Cheese - softened.
- 1/2 teaspoon Sugar.
- 1/4 cup Crab meat.
- 1 tablespoon Onion - finely chopped.
- 1/4 teaspoon Ginger - grated.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Crispy Crab Rangoons, mix all ingredients mentioned under 'for filling' until they are well combined in a bowl.
1. Cover and refrigerate at least for 1 hour, up to overnight.
1. Take one of the wonton wraps and put a teaspoon full of this filing in the center.
1. Using some water, moisten the center of each side.
1. Bring together the adjacent sides and then all together into the middle.
1. Heat oil in a pan to boiling temperature.
1. Fry each of these rangoons both the sides, till crispy.
1. Lay then down on a paper towel to drain excess heat and serve immediately.
1. Serve Crispy Crab Rangoons along with other starters like Rajma Malai Cutlet and Congress Buns for your house party.
