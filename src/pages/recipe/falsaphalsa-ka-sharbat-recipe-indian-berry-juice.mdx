### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped and for garnishing.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera) - roasted.
- Lemon wedges - as required for garnishing.
- 250 grams Falsa - or falsa/phalsa.
- 2 teaspoon Black Salt (Kala Namak).
- 2 tablespoons Sugar - or as per taste.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Falsa/Phalsa Ka Sharbat (Indian Berry Juice Recipe), wash the falsa and then mash with the help of spatula so that the pulp leaves the seeds.
1. Mix falsa, black salt, roasted cumin powder, a little water, and sugar and put the mixture into the mixer grinder to grind to smooth paste.
1. Now take out the mixture, filter it a large bowl and pour the juice into the serving glass with adequate water added as per your preference.
1. Add ice cubes and mint leaves, garnish Falsa/Phalsa ka sharbat (Indian Berry Juice Recipe) with a lemon slice and mint leaves before serving.
1. You can serve this Falsa/Phalsa Ka Sharbat (Indian Berry Juice Recipe) on any warm day as a welcome drink to guests along with snacks like Carrots and Coriander Fritters.
