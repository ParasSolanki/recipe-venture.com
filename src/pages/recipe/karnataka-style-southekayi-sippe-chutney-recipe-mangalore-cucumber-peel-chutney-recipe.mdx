### Ingredients

- 18 grams Tamarind.
- 1 Mangalorean cucumber - Peel removed.
- 4 tablespoon Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 2 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Southekayi Seppe Chutney Recipe (Mangalore Cucumber Peel Chutney Recipe) by peeling the skin of the mangalore cucumber.
1. Keep the flesh for later use.
1. Heat Kadai with oil, fry these chopped peel till it is soft.
1. Then take them out.
1. In the same kadai, add the urad dal, chana dal and coconut and roast them for 2 minutes.
1. Once done grind along with the rest of the ingredients mentioned above including the Mangalore peel.
1. Season it with salt if required and put it into a serving cup.
1. Heat a small kadai, and temper the ingredients by crackling the mustard seeds and curry leaves and pour it over the chutney.
1. Serve the Southekayi Seppe Chutney Recipe (Mangalore Cucumber Peel Chutney Recipe) along with Konkani Style Phanna Doddak Recipe (Tempered Dosa Recipe) for your morning breakfast.
