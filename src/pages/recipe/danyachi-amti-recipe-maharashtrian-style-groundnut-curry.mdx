### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 3 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Ghee - as required.
- 1 cup Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - roasted.
- 2 teaspoon Sugar.
- Salt - to taste (or kala namak when fasting).
- 2-1/2 cups Water.
- 1/4 cup Buttermilk - or 4-5 pieces amsul (dried kokum skin).
- 3-4 Green Chillies - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Danyachi amti recipe (groundnut Amti), first grind the groundnuts to a fine paste in a blender.
1. Use water as required for blending.
1. In a saucepan, heat the peanut paste along with water, salt, amsul (if using) and sugar.
1. Boil for 5-6 minutes until all the raw smell goes away.
1. If using buttermilk, add it once the groundnut amti has boiled.
1. Take it off the heat.
1. In a small pan, heat a little ghee.
1. Add cumin seeds and once they sizzle, add green chillies.
1. Saut√© for a few seconds and add this tempering to the groundnut Amti.
1. Garnish Danyachi Amti Recipe (Maharashtrian Style Groundnut Curry) with chopped coriander leaves to garnish and to side it up with Sabudana Khichdi.
