### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1/2 cup Del Monte Whole Corn Kernels.
- Salt - to taste.
- Water - to knead.
- 1 teaspoon Cooking soda.
- 1/2 cup Broccoli - cut into small florets.
- 1 tablespoon Salt.
- 1 teaspoon Mixed Herbs (Dried).
- Del Monte Black Olives.
- 4 tablespoons Homemade Pizza And Pasta Sauce.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- Black pepper powder - for seasoning.
- Sunflower Oil - for frying.
- 1 Yellow Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Cheesy Tawa Pizza Recipe With Corn & Broccoli, keep all the ingredients ready for the dough.
1. Add about 1/4 cup water, wheat flour, soda and salt and knead it into a soft but not a sticky dough.
1. Rest the dough in the same bowl covered with a cloth and keep it aside till you make your topping.
1. Heat a sauce pan with oil.
1. Once the oil is hot, add the onions, broccoli, and bell peppers one after the other.
1. Add salt and pepper, roast them till it becomes soft.
1. To make the base, take a medium sized ball from the dough and roll it quite thin like chapattis and place it over a heated tawa.
1. Flip it over and cook on either side till your see brown spots on them.
1. Smear tomato sauce on one of the sides, top it up with the cooked onion, broccoli, bell peppers, sweet corn and black olives.
1. Grate some cheese on the top.
1. Cover it with a lid and cook for about 5 minutes till the cheese melts.
1. Take it out and garnish with mixed herbs.
1. Serve the Cheesy Tawa Pizza Corn & Broccoli can be served as an evening snack along with a glass of Banana Almond & Prunes Smoothie for your kids.
1. You can also serve Cheesy Tawa Pizza Corn & Broccoli for Brunch along with Spaghetti Pasta Alla Puttanesca and No Bake White Chocolate Cheesecake.
