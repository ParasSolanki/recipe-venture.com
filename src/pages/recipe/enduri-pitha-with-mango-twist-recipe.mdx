### Ingredients

- 1 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 2 cup Mango (Ripe) - pulp.
- Ghee - as required.
- Banana leaf - as required.
- 2 teaspoon Fennel seeds (Saunf).
- 2 tablespoons Sugar.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon Baking soda.
- 4 cup Rice flour.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Enduri Pitha With Mango Twist recipe, mix the rice flour, mango pulp, baking soda and salt in a mixing bowl.
1. Keep this mixture for fermentation for at least 4 hour.
1. Add sugar if you want more sweetness.
1. Once the mixture is fermented add grated coconut, fennel and mix well.
1. Cut banana leaves into rectangular sizes (more to 8*10 inch size).
1. Take one sheet and put 1/4 tsp ghee and spread well with finger tips.
1. Take 4 to 5 tablespoons batter and place in the middle.
1. Add few mango slices in the centre on top of batter and fold the leaf at centre.
1. In a steamer cook it for around 10 minute.
1. Let it cool a bit and serve hot or cold.
