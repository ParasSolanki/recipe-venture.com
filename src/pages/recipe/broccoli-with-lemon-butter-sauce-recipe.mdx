### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Lemon juice.
- 2 tablespoons Butter - (salted).
- Salt - to taste.
- Fresh Herbs - (I used cilantro).
- 4 Whole Black Peppercorns - ground.
- 1 Broccoli - cut into smaller florets.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Broccoli With Lemon Butter Sauce Recipe, steam cook the broccoli till they are fork tender.
1. Heat a pan with a little water.
1. Add butter, chopped herbs, broccoli, black pepper, salt and lemon juice.
1. Simmer for 2-3 minutes till the broccoli is well coated with the sauce.
1. Switch off flame.
1. Your simple yet flavourful Broccoli in lemon butter sauce recipe is ready to be served with Mexican Lime chicken and bread, or your favorite pasta.
