### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - diced.
- 250 grams Button mushrooms.
- Salt - as per your taste.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- 1 tablespoon Tandoori masala.
- 1 teaspoon Lemon juice.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 2 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - diced.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Pineapple - diced.
- 2 tablespoons Sunflower Oil.
- 3 tablespoons Gram flour (besan).
- 1 teaspoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Pineapple Mushroom Tikka, wash the mushrooms well, deseed the capsicum and cut into squares.
1. Chop onions and pineapple into thin squares - keep aside.
1. Take all the chopped capsicum, onion, pineapple and mushroom pieces in a wide mixing bowl.
1. Add besan flour, curd, salt, ginger garlic paste, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, tandoori masala powder, Kasuri methi, lemon juice and a tsp of oil to it.
1. Mix and marinate so the tandoori masala paste coats well.
1. Cover and refrigerate for an hour.
1. After an hour, mix well once and insert marinated mushrooms, capsicum, pineapple and onions alternatively into wooden skewers.
1. Heat a griddle and sprinkle some oil.
1. Place the skewers and roast Pineapple Mushroom Tikka well in medium flame turning on all sides.
1. Add little oil on top while turning.
1. Once the Pineapple Mushroom Tikka turns golden on all sides, remove from griddle and arrange in serving plate.
