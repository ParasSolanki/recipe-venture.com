### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds) - roasted.
- 1 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 3 cups Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - cubes.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 2 teaspoon Garlic - paste.
- 1/2 cup Onions - finely chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - to cook.
- 1 teaspoon Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 tablespoons Fresh cream.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek Leaves).

### Instructions

1. To start making Tawa Paneer Masala Recipe, first get all the ingredients handy.
1. Marinate paneer with turmeric, chili powder, fenugreek leaves and salt in a bowl.
1. Leave for 20 minutes.
1. Heat oil in a frying pan and shallow fry paneer on both the sides for 2-3 minutes, flipping often.
1. Set aside fried paneer.
1. Heat the remaining oil in a non-stick pan, add cumin seeds and carom seeds.
1. Sauté them for few seconds on medium heat.
1. Add onions and sauté them for 2-3 minutes.
1. Add garlic paste, chilies, and coriander powder and mix them well.
1. Add the tomato pulp, caraway seeds and cook in medium flame for 3 minutes.
1. Stir occasionally.
1. Once the gravy thickens, add paneer, fresh cream, garam masala and combine them well.
1. Adjust spices and let it simmer for 2 minutes.
1. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
1. Enjoy Tawa Paneer Masala with hot rotis/chapatis/Phulkas and raita of your choice to make it a complete meal.
