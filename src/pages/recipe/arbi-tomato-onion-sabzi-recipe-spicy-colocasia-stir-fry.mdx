### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- Salt - as required.
- 3/4 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- Asafoetida (hing) - A pinch (optional).
- 3/4 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 3/4 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 3/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 2 Tomatoes - chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - slit thinly.
- 12 Colocasia root (Arbi) - thinly sliced like fries.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Arbi Tomato Onion Sabzi Recipe, wash and soak in salted water for 10 to 15 minutes.
1. Wash again and use towel to soak water from the arbi sticks.
1. Heat oil in a kadhai and deep fry arbi sticks till they become golden in colour.
1. Drain onto paper towel and keep it aside.
1. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a kadhai.
1. Add whole red chilies, asafoetida, ginger and garlic paste and saut√© it for 1 min.
1. Add sliced onions and stir until they become translucent.
1. Add turmeric powder, salt, red chili powder, coriander powder and garam masala.
1. Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies.
1. Mix well and cook it for 3-4 minutes.
1. Add the Arbi and mix well.
1. Cover it with the lid and cook it for 8-10 minutes.
1. Serve Arbi Tomato Onion Sabzi with phulkas and Rajasthani Kadhi for a weekday meal.
