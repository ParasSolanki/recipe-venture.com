### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Red Chilli powder.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - diced.
- 1/2 tablespoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 tablespoon Garam masala powder.
- 1/2 tablespoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 5 Garlic - chopped finely.
- 1 tablespoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 2 Brinjal (Baingan / Eggplant) - diced (long variety).

### Instructions

1. To prepare the simple Brinjal Capsicum Sabji Recipe, heat a pan with enough oil.
1. Add chopped garlic and hing, sauté it for few seconds.
1. Add brinjal and capsicum.
1. Add all the dry spices, sauté and cover the lid.
1. Let it semi cook by sautéing every few minutes once.
1. Add chopped tomato and let it cook for 5-7 minutes (or until the veggies are desirable cooked).
1. Add salt to taste, sauté and add chopped fresh coriander.
1. Pack this Brinjal Capsicum Sabji Recipe with phulka/ chapati for a light lunch at office or school.
