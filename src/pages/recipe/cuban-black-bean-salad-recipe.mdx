### Ingredients

- 1 Red Yellow and Green Bell Peppers (Capsicum) - finely chopped.
- Parsley leaves - small bunch.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 2 Tomatoes - diced (or cherry tomatoes).
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1 Red onion - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1 cup Sweet corn - steamed.
- 2 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 3 tablespoons Fresh orange juice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Orange Zest (Rind).
- 1 Canned black beans - (canned) drained & rinsed.
- 2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Dried oregano.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cuban Black Bean Salad Recipe, first get all the ingredients ready.
1. If you don't find black beans, you can also cook Rajma (red kidney beans) ahead of time and use it in this salad.
1. In a small mixing bowl, whisk all the ingredients for the mojo sauce until smooth.
1. In a large salad mixing bowl, add all the ingredients for the salad and the mojo sauce and stir well to combine.
1. Check the salt and seasonings and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Serve the Cuban Black Bean Salad along with a bowl of soup and a crusted bread for a weeknight dinner or pack it into your lunch box for work as well.
