### Ingredients

- 2 Kiwi.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- Mixed nuts - chopped.
- 50 grams Caster Sugar.
- 1 liter Milk.
- 1 tablespoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Kiwi Sondesh Recipe, in a saucepan, heat the milk and bring to boil.
1. Add lemon juice and stir it until milk curdles completely.
1. Take it off heat and strain the milk solids with a muslin cloth.
1. Pour some water over the chena to wash off the lemony sourness.
1. Drain away the whey and knead it well for at least 10 minutes.
1. Now add the powdered sugar and kiwi pulp.
1. Mix well to combine.
1. Cook the mix on medium heat till the mixture thickens and starts leaving the sides of the pan.
1. Turn off the flame and let the mixture cool for a while.
1. Roll the mix into small balls and flatten slightly.
1. Garnish with some almonds, pistachios, and raisins.
1. Serve Kiwi Sondesh Recipe as a dessert along with Baked Chevdo Recipe and Pheki Mathri Recipe during evening.
1. This recipe can also be served as a dessert after dinner.
