### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 4 tablespoon Water.
- 2 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 2 cloves Garlic - minced.
- 2 tablespoons Lemon juice.
- Salt - as needed.
- 2 tablespoons Sesame seeds (Til seeds).
- A pinch Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1 cup Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - boiled.
- 1/4 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 2 tablespoons Mango (Raw) - grated.

### Instructions

1. To start making Boiled Peanut Hummus, add all the ingredients to your food processor except water.
1. Pulse couple of times to breakdown all the ingredients.
1. Add water little by little and process until the hummus is coarse or smooth and creamy based on your preference.
1. Tranger the ingredients to a serving bowl, drizzle a little more olive oil (optional) on the top, sprinkle some chili powder or herbs and serve.
1. Serve the Peanut Hummus along with Crispy Lavash or Multi-Grain Crackers as a party appetizer.
1. We can also use them as a spread on toast, bagel or for mediterranean sandwiches.
