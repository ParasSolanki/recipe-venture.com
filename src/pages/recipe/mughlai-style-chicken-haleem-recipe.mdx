### Ingredients

- 300 grams Boneless chicken.
- 1 tablespoon Rice.
- 3 Green Chillies.
- 3 tablespoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 3 tablespoon Broken Wheat (Dalia/ Godumai Rava).
- 3 tablespoon Ghee.
- 1 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 3 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1 tablespoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1 Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 2 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 4 Onions - sliced.
- 2 tablespoon Mint Leaves (Pudina) - chopped.
- 2-1/2 tablespoons Red Chilli powder.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Mughlai Style Chicken Haleem Recipe, dry roast all the spices mentioned under masala list.
1. Grind it into fine powder.
1. Keep aside.
1. Soak chana dal, broken wheat, rice separately.
1. In a saucepot add oil.
1. Add sliced onions, ginger garlic paste, green chilies saut√© until onions turn brown.
1. Remove from stove and keep aside.
1. Add 3 cups water into the same pan.
1. Add chicken pieces, salt and red chili powder.
1. Cover and bring it to rolling boil.
1. Once chicken changes color, add the soaked dal, broken wheat and rice.
1. Cover and cook.
1. Once the chicken is cooked, remove the bay leaf.
1. Start mashing the chicken and dal once chicken gets cooked.
1. Continue mashing and cooking until all the chicken is fully mashed along with dal.
1. Keep adding a little water as and when required.
1. Now add the sauteed onion-ginger garlic-green chilies mix along with roasted spices.
1. Add salt to taste and continue to cook and mash.
1. Once the haleem turns thick and all ingredients are well mashed, add ghee and mix again.
1. Transfer the Mughlai Style Chicken Haleem Recipe to serving bowl, garnish with caramelized onions, fresh coriander leaves and some ghee on top.
1. Serve Mughlai Style Chicken Haleem Recipe topped with caramelized onions and serve as a main course meal with Mutton Keema Samosa Recipe.
