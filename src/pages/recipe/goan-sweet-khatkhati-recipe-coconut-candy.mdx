### Ingredients

- 250 grams Jaggery.
- 1 cup Fresh coconut - shredded (or dry coconut).
- 1/4 cup Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - dry roasted.
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1/4 cup Raw Peanuts (Moongphali) - roasted and crushed.
- Ghee - clarified butter for greasing the plate.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Goan Sweet Khatkhati recipe, grease a plate with ghee and keep it aside.
1. Heat the jaggery in a non stick saucepan on medium high heat.
1. Stir in between.
1. Once the jaggery is melted properly, switch off the heat.
1. Add in all the ingredients like shredded coconut, sesame seeds, crushed peanuts and cardamom powder and mix well.
1. I used store bought roasted sesame, but you can roast the raw sesame on a skillet kept on low flame till they slightly change colour.
1. Let the mixture cool down for 5 minutes.
1. Don't let it cool down completely.
1. Take a tablespoon of mixture on your palm and shape it into a ball and then flatten it by pressing into disc shape.
1. Keep it on a greased plate and repeat for the remaining mixture.
1. Let them cool down completely and then store in the air tight container.
1. Serve Goan Sweet Khatkhati on its own on special occasions to your friends and family.
