### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 teaspoons Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 cups Cauliflower (gobi) - cut into small florets.
- 1 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Cauliflower Pepper Fry (Chettinad Style), heat a pan and pour oil, put the mustard seeds and let it crackle.
1. To this add the asafoetida and a sprig of curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. Add the cauliflower florets to this and give it a stir.
1. Add turmeric powder, season with salt and sprinkle with little water and cook with the lid on top for about 10 minutes.
1. In the meanwhile, make a coarse dry mixture of cumin seeds, peppercorns and dried red chili in a mixer grinder.
1. Once the cauliflower florets are cooked, put this coarse mixture over it and saut√© it for well 2 minutes.
1. Garnish with a sprig of curry leaves and serve Cauliflower Pepper Fry Recipe (Chettinad Style) with steamed rice and Beetroot Garlic Lemon Rasam for a complete meal.
