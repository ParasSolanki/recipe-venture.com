### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 teaspoon Methi Powder (Fenugreek Powder).
- 20 Curry leaves.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Garlic - (whole garlic pods).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 5 tablespoons Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.

### Instructions

1. To begin the preparation of Instant Garlic Pickle, first peel the skin of garlic and cut into slices/ coarsely pound them.
1. Heat sesame oil in a deep pan, add mustard seeds & allow spluttering.
1. Add garlic pieces, chilli powder, turmeric powder and fry till golden brown.
1. Add fenugreek powder, salt, curry leaves and mix well.
1. Once cooled, serve the Garlic Pickle fresh with curd rice or keep it keep it in a dry, airtight container.
