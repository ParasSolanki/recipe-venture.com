### Ingredients

- 1/2 Black pepper powder.
- Orange peel - from 1 orange..
- 1/2 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 2 Cloves (Laung).
- 1 pinch Nutmeg - grated.
- 1 liter Apple juice -.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Spiced Mulled Apple Juice recipe, bring the apple juice to a boil in a saucepan with cinnamon stick, nutmeg, cloves, black pepper powder, ginger and orange peel.
1. Simmer for 8-10 minutes or until the spices have infused into the apple juice.
1. Once done, strain the Spiced Mulled Apple Juice  and serve hot.
1. Serve Spiced Mulled Apple Juice along with some  Jalapeno Potato Cheese Balls Recipe With Four Pepper Cheese Dip and Roasted Mushroom & Caramelized Onion Bruschetta for your next party.
