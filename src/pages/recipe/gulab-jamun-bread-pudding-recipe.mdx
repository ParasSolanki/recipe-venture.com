### Ingredients

- 6 Whole Eggs.
- Saffron strands - few.
- 2 tablespoon Sugar - adjustable.
- 2 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 1 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 3 cups Milk.
- 2 tablespoon Badam (Almond) - powdered.
- 500 grams Gulab jamun - homemade or market ones.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Gulab Jamun Bread Pudding recipe, preheat the oven to 180 C for 10 minutes.
1. Grease a baking dish with baking spray or butter.
1. Line the tray with gulab jamuns just break it with your hands .
1. Mix eggs, milk, sugar, bread, almonds, cardamom powder  and blend everything.
1. Put it on top of the gulab jamun and bake it for 15 to 20 minutes until you see a golden crust on it.
1. Serve Gulab Jamun Bread Pudding as a dessert after your simple meal of Paneer Pav Bhaji.
