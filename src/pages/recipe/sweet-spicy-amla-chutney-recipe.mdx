### Ingredients

- Sunflower Oil.
- 100 grams Amla (Nellikai/ Gooseberry).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Jaggery.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Sweet & Spicy Amla Chutney Recipe, pressure cook the amla along with a little water for three whistles.
1. Once the pressure is released, open the cooker and deseed the alma.
1. Grind the cooked amla in a mixer along with a little water and make into a smooth paste.
1. Heat a kadai with oil, add the cumin seeds, mustard seeds and hing, allow it to crackle.
1. Add red chilli powder and give it a stir, add ground amla paste and mix well.
1. Add jaggery and salt and cook for a few minutes until the mixture comes together.
1. Serve the Sweet & Spicy Amla Chutney Recipe along with Crispy dosa or thalipeeth to enjoy it along with your breakfast meal.
