### Ingredients

- 2 cups Del Monte Chifferi Rigati Pasta - macaroni.
- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried basil leaves.
- Salt - to taste.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - cut into stripes.
- 1/4 cup Mozzarella cheese - grated.
- 1 Red Bell pepper (Capsicum) - cut into stripes.
- 3 Green Chillies - slit.
- 3/4 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1-1/2 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Bell Pepper & Cheese Macaroni recipe, get prepped with all ingredients first.
1. Heat oil in a pan, add onions and sauté it for 1-2 minutes until half done.
1. Add bell pepper and sauté till cooked.
1. This may take 3 minutes.
1. (Adjust level of cooking veggies according to preference)Now add dried oregano, dried basil, black pepper powder, green chilies, and salt.
1. Mix.
1. Add boiled macaroni, mix well and sauté for 2-3 minutes until combined.
1. Transfer it to a microwave safe bowl.
1. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on it.
1. Microwave it for 2-3 minutes on high power.
1. Serve Bell Pepper & Cheese Macaroni recipe with focaccia bread or garlic bread and zucchini fries.
1. Serve with more dips like yogurt dip or sauces as per preference.
