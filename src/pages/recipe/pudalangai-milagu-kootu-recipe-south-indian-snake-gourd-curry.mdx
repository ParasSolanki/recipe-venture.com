### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 1/2 cup Masoor Dal (Whole).
- 1 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 teaspoon White Urad Dal (Split).
- 1/4 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 teaspoon Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 2 Snake gourd (pudulangai).
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 1/4 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 3 Dry Red Chillies - (adjust).
- 1 teaspoon Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 cup Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- Curry leaves - few.

### Instructions

1. To begin with the Pudalangai (Snake Gourd)  Milagu Kootu Recipe, first wash and soak dhal for atleast 30 minutesNow take the snake gourd and clean and chop them into small pieces.
1. In a pressure cooker add dhal and snake gourd along with turmeric powder, hing and salt.
1. Cook them upto two whistles.
1. Cooking for longer time will make the vegetable mushy.
1. Meanwhile,roast all the ingredients mentioned under for masala paste in oil and grind them into fine paste using a mixer.
1. Combine the grounded masalas with cooked dhal and snake gourd and cook until the raw smell goes off.
1. Heat oil in a small kadai and add some oil,mustard seeds, urad dhal, red chill and curry leaves.
1. Once the mustard seeds splutter, add them into the vegetable dhal mixtureYour Pudalangai (Snake Gourd)  Milagu Kootu Recipe is ready to be served.
1. The best way to serve Pudalangai (Snake Gourd)  Milagu Kootu is with some hot rice and ghee on top.
