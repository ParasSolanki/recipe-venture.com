### Ingredients

- 2 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - roughly chopped or sliced.
- 2 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled and coarsely chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Maharastrian Batatyachi Bhaji Recipe, we prepare all the ingredients and keep them ready.
1. Heat the oil in a heavy bottomed pan; add mustard seeds, cumin seeds and allow it crackle.
1. Add in the ginger, green chillies, onions, curry leaves, and asafoetida.
1. Saute for a few minutes until the onions turn soft.
1. At this stage add in the turmeric powder, salt and potatoes.
1. Stir well until all the ingredients are well combined.
1. Cover the pan, turn the heat to low and simmer the Batatyachi Bhaji few minutes until the aloo is well combined into the masalas.
1. Once the potatoes are done check salt to taste and stir in the coriander leaves.
1. Remove Maharastrian Batatyachi Bhaji into a serving bowl.
1. Serve the Maharastrian Batatyachi Bhaji along with Puri, Sooji Halwa and Aluchi Bhaji for weekend lunch.
1. Traditionally it is served with Puran Poli Recipe With Toor Dal And Chana Dal.
