### Ingredients

- 1 cup Whole Wheat Bread crumbs - for dusting.
- 8 Pav buns.
- 1 teaspoon Butter.
- Herb Butter - for toasting the buns.
- 1 cup Spinach - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 4 Potatoes (Aloo) - boiled and mashed.
- 4 tablespoons Classic Mayonnaise (With Egg).
- 1/4 cup Lahsun Ki Chutney (Red Garlic Chutney).
- 1/4 cup Butter.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.
- 1 Onion - cut into rings.
- 1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder.
- 1/2 cup Sweet corn - steamed.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Vada Pav with Spinach Corn Tikki Recipe, first get all the ingredients together.
1. Prepare the Lahsun Chutney and keep aside.
1. The next step is to make the Spinach Corn Tikki for the Vada Pav.
1. Heat butter in a skillet pan and allow it to melt.
1. Stir in the onions, garlic, ginger, green chillies and sauté for a couple of minutes till onions are soft and light golden in colour.
1. Finally stir in the spinach and cook the spinach and mint leaves for a few seconds until it wilts down and the moisture is almost evaporated.
1. Add the mashed potatoes and steamed sweet corn, chaat masala and sauté for a couple of minutes and it becomes a thick firm tikki mixture.
1. Allow the spinach corn tikki mixture to cool down.
1. Shape the Spinach Corn Tikki mixture into round balls and keep aside.
1. Coat each of the spinach corn tikki with bread crumbs.
1. Preheat a skillet, melt the butter and pan fry the Spinach Corn Tikki's until they are browned and crisp on all sides.
1. Take care not to turn the heat to high as this will not evenly cook the batter and you might just end up with over browned and uncooked portions.
1. Assembly of Vada PavSlice the pav buns into half horizontally.
1. Spread the herbed butter and toast them on the pan on both sides, until the surface is lightly golden and crisp.
1. Once done, Spread some Lahsun Chutney on one half and Veg Mayo on the other.
1. Place a Spinach Corn Vada and a few onions rings between the buns and the Vada Pav is ready to be served.
1. Serve Homemade Vada Pav Recipe With Spinach Corn Tikki with Masala Chai Recipe - Indian Spiced Tea as a tea time snack.
