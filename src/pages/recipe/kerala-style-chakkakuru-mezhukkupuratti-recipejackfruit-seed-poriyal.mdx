### Ingredients

- 8 Pearl onions (Sambar Onions) - sliced.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.
- 1/4 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped finely.
- 1/2 cup Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Kashmiri Red Chilli Powder.
- 1 cup Jackfruit Seeds (Kathal) - cleaned and sliced.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Kerala Style Chakkakuru Mezhukkupuratti  Recipe we will first boil the jackfruit seeds.
1. In a pressure cooker, add the jackfruit seeds, water and pressure cook for about 3 whistles.
1. Turn off the flame, and allow the pressure to release naturally.
1. Drain the water and peel of  the skin of the jack fruit skin carefully.
1. Now slice them into thin slivers.
1. Heat a kadai on medium flame, add mustard seeds and curry leaves, let them splutter.
1. Next, add in the onions and add the red chilli powder and black pepper powder.
1. Finally add in the jackfruit seeds and saute them till it is coated well.
1. At the end add grated coconut and give the Chakkakuru Mezhukkupuratti  a stir.
1. Serve the Kerala Style Chakkakuru Mezhukkupuratti  Recipe along with Kerala Style Kanji Payar Recipe.
