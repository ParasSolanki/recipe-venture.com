### Ingredients

-  1 green chili - chopped.
-  salt - 1 tablespoon lemon juice.
- 150 grams coconut - grated.
-  according to taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  100 grams coriander - fresh.
-  1 inch ginger - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To make coriander and coconut chutney, first put coconut, coriander, green chillies, ginger, cumin and salt in a mixer grinder.
1. Grind well.
1. After grinding, take it out in a bowl.
1. Now add lemon juice to it.
1. Mix and serve.
1. Serve coriander and coconut chutney with dosa or idli for breakfast or for an evening snack with pakoras and tea.
