### Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons Flax seed powder (flax meal).
- 1 cup Badam (Almond).
- 2 cups Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 5 tablespoon Coconut Oil - or melted butter.
- Fresh fruits - bananas.
- 4 tablespoon Peanut Butter.
- 1/2 cup Almond Milk (Badam Milk) - or milk.
- 4 tablespoon Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 1/3 cup Maple syrup - or honey.
- 4 tablespoon Jam.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Fruit Tarts Recipe, get prep with all the ingredients and preheat Oven to 190 degrees Celsius.
1. Also, grease the muffin/mini tart molds.
1. Stir flaxseed powder with almond milk and set aside for 10 minutes to let it thickens.
1. Blend oats and almond together finely in a blender.
1. In a mixing bowl, add the blended oat-almond flour, soaked flax meal, maple syrup,almond milk and bring it together like a dough.
1. It is going to be a sticky mix, grease your hands to handle the dough if necessary.
1. Shape the dough into small balls and press the dough ball into a greased muffin and mold it into the shape of tartlets.
1. Bake for 12-15 minutes and let it cool completely.
1. Fill them in with hung curd and your favorite fruits and serve.
1. Serve Fruit Tarts Recipe at the end of a meal with Cauliflower Au Gratin Recipe and Whole Wheat Rosemary Focaccia Bread Recipe.
