### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Fresh coconut - chunks/ tender coconut chunks.
- Tender coconut water - as required.
- 1/2 cup Crushed ice.
- 1 teaspoon Kewra essence.

### Instructions

1. To make this super easy Coconut Kewra Drink Recipe, get the ingredients handy first.
1. Mix all the ingredients including fresh coconut chunks, kewra essence, tender coconut water except crushed ice and shake well for a minute.
1. Serve this yummy and cool coconut kewra drink with crushed ice topped on it.
