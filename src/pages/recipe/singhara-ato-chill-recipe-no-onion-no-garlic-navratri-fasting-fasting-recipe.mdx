### Ingredients

- Ghee - or oil for cooking.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 3/4 cup Singoda Flour (Water Chestnut).
- 2 teaspoon Black pepper powder - coarsely crushed.
- 1 Green Chilli - chopped.
- Rock Salt - or salt to taste.
- 1/2 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Singhara Atta Cheela Recipe (No Onion No Garlic Navratri Fasting/Vrat Recipe) by taking a mixing bowl and adding all the ingredients.
1. Mix the ingredients with enough water till you get a pouring consistency.
1. Heat a Flat non-stick pan and add one ladle of the batter and spread little using the back of the ladle.
1. Do not spread too much as it will get stuck to the pan.
1. Swirl the pan so the cheela batter spreads on its own.
1. Add a teaspoon of oil or ghee and sprinkle with sesame seeds and cook on both the sides for 2 minutes till it is done.
1. Serve your hot Singhara Atta Cheela with Apple Tamatar Ki Subzi or Coconut Peanut Chutney.
