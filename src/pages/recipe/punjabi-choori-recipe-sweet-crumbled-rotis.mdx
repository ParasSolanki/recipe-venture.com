### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Ghee.
- 2 tablespoons Sugar - or grated jaggery.
- 4 Tawa Paratha - leftover or fresh.
- Mixed nuts - like cashew and almonds.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Punjabi Choori Recipe, first break the paranthas in small pieces, by shredding it with your hands.
1. Then place it in a mortar and pestle and further pound it into a coarse crumbly mixture.
1. This can also be done in a chopper or food processor.
1. In a kadhai, warm some ghee and roast the dry fruits for about 2 minutes till fragrant and nutty.
1. Add the crumbled roti powder to the ghee and nuts.
1. Saute together for 2-3 minutes.
1. Switch off the flame at this point and add the sugar or jaggery powder.
1. Toss it all together for a couple of minutes till incorporated.
1. Turn off the heat, transfer into a bowl and serve the Punjabi Choori recipe warm with a glass of milk.
