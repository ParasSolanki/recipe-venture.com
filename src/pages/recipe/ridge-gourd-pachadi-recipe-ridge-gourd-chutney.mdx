### Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Tamarind Paste.
- 1 Ridge Gourd (Turai/ Peerkangai) - peeled and finely chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Sunflower Oil.
- 2 Green Chillies - sliced.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Ridge Gourd Pachadi Recipe, in a skillet heat a teaspoon of oil.
1. Add the chopped ridge gourd, green chillies, tamarind and saut√© on a low flame for 3-4 minutes or till ridge gourd is cooked and nicely coated in tamarind.
1. Turn off the heat and let the mixture cool down before grinding.
1. Put the cooked ridge gourd in a grinder along with the cumin and garlic.
1. Grind to a smooth consistency using little water to get the desired consistency.
1. But the pachadi should not be watery, instead it should be grainy and coarse in texture.
1. Transfer Ridge Gourd Pachadi to a bowl.
1. Check the salt and spices and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Serve Ridge Gourd Pachadi with rasam, steamed rice and mixed vegetable curry.
