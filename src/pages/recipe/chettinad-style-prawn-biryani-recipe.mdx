### Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons Fresh coconut - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 2 Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- 200 grams Shrimps - or prawns.
- 1/2 cup Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 2 Bay leaves (tej patta).
- 1 teaspoon Green Chilli - paste.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 2-3 Dry Red Chilli.
- 5-6 Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 1 cup Sunflower Oil.
- 1-1/2 cups Basmati rice.
- 4 Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 4-5 Shallots.
- 2 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 inch Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 1 tablespoon Ginger - paste.
- 2 tablespoons Ghee.
- 1 tablespoon Garlic - paste.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Chettinad Style Prawn Biryani Recipe, wash and clean prawns well, collect in a bowl and add ginger garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, few drops of lemon juice and 2-3 pinch salt.
1. Mix well and marinate for 15-20 minutes.
1. Wash and soak rice in water for a minimum 30 minutes before cooking.
1. Heat oil in a large and thick bottomed handi and add the whole spices and wait till they splutter.
1. Now add shallots and saute them till they are pale in color.
1. Add green chili paste, grated coconut and marinated prawns and mix well.
1. Fry for 4-5 minutes and then slowly add curd and carefully mix everything to avoid breaking the prawns.
1. Keep the flames high and let the water from curd dry up completely.
1. Season with coriander powder, garam masala powder and salt.
1. Fry till it is cooked and deeper in color.
1. Now add the soaked rice and again stir well such that everything incorporates well with each other.
1. Add warm water (double the quantity of rice), check salt and add a dollop of ghee and mix well.
1. Allow the rice to come to a boil, close the lid and let the rice cook at low heat.
1. Keep the lid covered till the rice is done.
1. Gently mix the rice and collect in a serving bowl.
1. Garnish with chopped coriander and chopped curry leaves.
1. Serve the Chettinad Style Prawn Biryani hot with raita for a wholesome meal.
