### Ingredients

- 1 Carrot (Gajjar) - diced.
- 2 cups Kaddu (Parangikai/ Pumpkin) - cut into cubes.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 cup Homemade tomato puree.
- 1 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - soaked overnight.
- Salt - to taste.
- 4 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1 teaspoon Cinnamon Powder (Dalchini).
- 2 tablespoons Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 Lemon - juice.
- Extra Virgin Olive Oil - for cooking.
- 1 inch Ginger - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Pumpkin Stew With Chickpeas Recipe, prep all the ingredients and keep them ready.
1. Soak the chickpeas overnight or for at least 8 hours.
1. Pressure cook the chickpeas along with a little salt and cook until soft for about 30 minutes.
1. This will yield about 2-1/2 cups of cooked chickpeas.
1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat;  Add onions, ginger and garlic and saute until the onions become tender and translucent.
1. Once the onions have softened, add in the carrot and pumpkin, sprinkle salt to taste and a little water, cover and cook till the carrots and pumpkins are cooked until firm.
1. Take care to ensure it does not become too mushy.
1. Once the vegetables are cooked; add in cumin powder, coriander powder, cinnamon powder, salt, chilli flakes and stir it into the vegetables.
1. Add the tomato puree and brink the Pumpkin Stew With Chickpeas to a brisk boil.
1. Add a little water if required to adjust the consistency of the stew accordingly.
1. Check the salt and seasonings and adjust according to taste.
1. After the brisk boil for a couple of minutes, stir in the black raisins to the Pumpkin Stew With Chickpeas and turn off the heat.
1. Transfer the Pumpkin Stew With Chickpeas Recipe to a serving bowl and serve hot.
1. Serve Pumpkin Stew With Chickpeas Recipe With Israeli Couscous and a Greek Style Beans Stir Fry for a delicious continental dinner with friends and family.
