### Ingredients

- Ghee - or oil for cooking.
- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/4 teaspoon Ajwain (Carom seeds).
- 2 cups Gram flour (besan).
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - small bunch.
- 1 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Besan & Atta Ki Roti Recipe (Chickpea Flatbread), first get all the ingredients ready and keep aside.
1. Combine all the ingredients into a large mixing bowl (except the oil or ghee).
1. Add a little water at a time and knead to make a smooth dough.
1. Drizzle a teaspoon of oil over the dough and knead for a couple of minutes until smooth and firm.
1. Divide the besan roti dough into 10 portions.
1. Preheat the Roti Tawa on medium heat.
1.  Roll each portion of the dough, but dusting in flour into a thin circle.
1. The thickness of the besan ki roti will be based on how thin you can roll it.
1. Once rolled, place the besan ki roti on the Roti Tawa and allow it to cook for a few seconds.
1. Once you see air bubbles popping out, flip and turn.
1. Smear ghee or oil over the roti and cook on both sides until browned and lightly crisp.
1. Once done, remove the Besan Atta Roti from the Roti Tawa and proceed the same way with the remaining portions of the dough.
1. Serve the Besan & Atta Ki Roti along with a Baingan Bharta, Sweet & Spicy Tomato Chutney and Kachumber Salad for a wholesome weeknight dinner or even for breakfast.
