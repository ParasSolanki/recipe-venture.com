### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Light soy sauce.
- 1/8 teaspoon Chinese 5 Spice Powder.
- 4 tablespoons Water - warm.
- 1 cup Wheat Noodles - boiled.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Mooli/ Mullangi (Radish) - pickled.
- 2 tablespoons Tahini.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- 2 cloves Garlic - finely chopped.
- 2 sprig Spring Onion Greens - chopped.
- 1 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 1/2 tablespoon Dark soy sauce.

### Instructions

1. We begin making the Fujian Style Hot Dry Noodle Recipe by boiling the noodles in a saucepan with water, boil till it cooked, strain the water and show the noodles under tap water.
1. Then drizzle some oil and toss it up till it is evenly coated.
1. In order to pickle the radish, thinly slice the radish using a peeler and then place it into a bowl, add 2 teaspoon of vinegar, 1 teaspoon Sichuan sauce, Â½ teaspoon of sugar.
1. Mix well and rest it for 10 minutes.
1. Next, to make the sauce, add all the ingredients in a big mixing bowl and mix it till it combines, you adjust the taste by your liking.
1. Then add the cooked noodles to the bowl and toss it well till every noodle is coated, add the picked radish, spring onions and garlic chopped.
1. Toss them well.
1. Serve the Fujian Style Hot Dry Noodle along with steamed dumplings to enjoy your Chinese Sunday meal.
