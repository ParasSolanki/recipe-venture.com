### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Instant Oats (Oatmeal).
- 1 tablespoon Cocoa Powder.
- 2 tablespoons Peanut Butter.
- 4 Dates - pitted and chopped.
- 2 Ripe Bananas - roughly chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Chia Seeds - or nuts.
- 2 cups Milk.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Nutty Chocolate Banana Smoothie Recipe, into a blender add the ripe bananas, milk, dates, peanut butter, oats and cocoa powder and make a smooth mixture.
