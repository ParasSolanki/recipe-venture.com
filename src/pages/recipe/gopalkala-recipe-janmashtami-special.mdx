### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped or slit.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - a small bunch chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - grated.
- 1 teaspoon Ghee.
- 3 tablespoons Fresh coconut - freshly grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1/4 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 cup Poha (Flattened rice).
- 1 Cucumber - peeled and chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Gopal Kala Janmashtami Special Recipe, soak beaten rice in water for about 1-2 minutes, the variety of beaten rice used required just about 2 minutes to soften.
1. If the beaten rice is of a thicker variety then it would take about 5 minutes to soften.
1. Once beaten rice/poha softens, drain all the water and keep aside in the strainer for a minute until all water is drained off.
1. We will now prepare the seasoning for the Gopalkala.
1. Heat ghee in a pan on medium heat; add the mustard seeds and allow it to crackle.
1. Next add cumin seeds, green chilies, ginger pieces and fry for a minute and turn off the heat.
1. Add the seasoning to the beaten rice, along with yogurt, finely chopped cucumbers, grated coconut, salt to taste.
1. Stir to combine the Gopalkala very gently.
1. Check the salt and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Finally stir in the chopped coriander leaves and serve.
