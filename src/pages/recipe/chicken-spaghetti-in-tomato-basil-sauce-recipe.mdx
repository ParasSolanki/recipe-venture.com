### Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Sugar.
- 100 grams Chicken breasts - cut into pieces.
- 3 tablespoons Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- 3 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1 teaspoon Tarragon.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 150 grams Spaghetti Pasta.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli flakes.
- 1 teaspoon Rosemary.
- 5 Tomatoes - cut into half.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken Spaghetti in Tomato Basil Sauce Recipe, Wash the tomatoes and cut them into half, making sure the core and the stem are removed.
1. Add the tomatoes in a blender and grind to form a smooth puree and keep it aside.
1. To Cook the pasta keep a saucepan filled with water over a medium heat.
1. Drop in the Spaghetti pasta and a pinch of salt and leave it to boil.
1. Boil the pasta till it gets cooked until you get a biting consistency which is the al dente consistencyStrain the water from the cooked penne pasta and rinse it under cold water.
1. Drain all the water and drizzle olive oil over the pasta so it does not stick to each other.
1. Heat a sauce pan with oil on medium flame, add garlic and onions, saute until they turn into a nice brown colour.
1. Now add tomato puree and allow it to boil.
1. Add sugar, red chilli flakes, dried rosemary, tarragon and basil leaves.
1. When the sauce is reduced and the raw smell goes away, add chopped chicken pieces and saute them until they are done.
1. Add boiled spaghetti and coat the pasta well.
1.
