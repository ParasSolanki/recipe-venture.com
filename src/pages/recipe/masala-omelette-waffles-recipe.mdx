### Ingredients

- Butter - for cooking.
- 1 Onion - small in size and chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/4 cup Broccoli - cut into small heads.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Milk.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 1 teaspoon Coriander Powder (Dhania).
- 1/4 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1/2 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1 Tomato - deseeded and chopped.
- 4 Whole Eggs.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Masala Omelette Waffles Recipe, chop all the ingredients and keep it ready in a bowl.
1. Break all the eggs, add milk as well and all the spice powders mentioned above including red chilli powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, salt to taste and give it a nice whisk.
1. Once you mix it, add all the vegetables including onions, tomatoes, broccoli and green bell pepper.
1. Once done, heat your waffle maker to a medium temperature.
1. Once the waffle maker is hot enough, brush it with enough butter so that the waffle does not get stuck to it.
1. Slowly pour the whisked egg mixture into the waffle maker, just enough so that it fills up.
1. Close the waffle maker and let it cook for at least 15 minutes.
1. Open after 15 minutes and you will see the waffles have risen and has changed to light brown color.
1. Slowly lift the egg waffles and serve it onto your serving plate.
1. Serve the Masala Omelette Waffles Recipe along with Mexican Salsa or a Toast by the side with a nice cup of Vegan Instant Coffee to go with for your breakfast.
