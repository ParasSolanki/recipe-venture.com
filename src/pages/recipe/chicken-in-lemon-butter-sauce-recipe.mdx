### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 1 teaspoon Corn flour.
- 2 tablespoon Butter.
- 3 sprig Fresh oregano.
- 1 teaspoon Honey.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 1/2 Lemon juice.
- 1/4 cup Fresh cream.
- 1 Lemon juice.
- 4 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1 Chicken breasts - cut into two pieces.
- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
- Salt and Pepper - for seasoning.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Chicken in Lemon Butter Sauce Recipe, we will first marinade the chicken breast in a bowl.
1. Add lemon juice, salt and place the pieces of Chicken in it.
1.  Mix everything well and rest the chicken pieces for at least 30 minutes.
1. After marinating, heat a skillet with butter.
1. Place the Chicken breast and slightly press so that the breast piece is charred well.
1. After 10 minutes flip it on the other side and cook again for 10 more minutes.
1. Once done, take the chicken out and in the same pan, add one more tablespoon of butter, add onions and saute till they turn translucent .
1. Once the onions are translucent, add in the chopped garlic and saute till they soften.
1. Squeeze in lemon juice, fresh oregano, parmesan cheese, honey and stir well for some time.
1. Finally, add cream and 1/2 cup water mixed with corn flour and bring it to a boil.
1. Finally add seasonings and adjust according to your palate.
1. Add this sauce on the chicken and serve hot.
1. Serve the Chicken in Lemon Butter Sauce Recipe along with Garlic Bread/Bun and Light And Creamy Broccoli Soup by the side to make it a complete meal.
1. You can also serve it with Steamed Rice.
