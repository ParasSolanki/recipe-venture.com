### Ingredients

-  1 teaspoon black pepper powder.
-  1/4 cup coconut - grated.
-  2 green chillies - finely chopped.
-  1 tablespoon chisemis - chopped.
-  1 tablespoon coriander - finely chopped.
-  3 sprig coriander - finely chopped.
- 10 parwal - 1 bay leaf.
-  1/4 teaspoon cumin seeds.
-  1 cup tomato puree.
-  1 inch cinnamon.
-  1 green chili - finely chopped.
-  2 cardamom.
-  2 teaspoon poppy seeds - 1 cup cheese - grated.
-  4 long.
-  salt - as per taste.
-  1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder.
-  1/4 teaspoon red chilli powder.
-  1 inch ginger - finely chopped.
-  Oil - As per use.
-  12 cashews - soaked and ground.

### Instructions

1. Firstly prepare all the ingredients to make the potala stock.
1. To begin making the Potala Rasa recipe, prep all the ingredients and keep it ready for cooking.
1. Then grind the grinding ingredients in a mixer grinder and make a paste.
1. Clean the dwarf the parwal thoroughly.
1. Remove the skin of Parwal with the help of a knife.
1. Cut it down and take out the seeds inside it.
1. Apply cold salt on it and keep it on the side for 10 to 15 minutes.
1. Now heat oil in a pan, fry the parwal and cook till it becomes light brown.
1. After that keep it separately.
1. Now we will make masala to fill in the parwal.
1. In a bowl, mix cottage cheese, green chillies, kisses, black pepper powder, salt and coriander leaves.
1. Mix and keep it aside.
1. Now fill this spice in the cooked parwal and keep it aside.
1. Heat some more oil in the same pan.
1. When the oil is hot, add bay leaves and cumin seeds.
1. Then add the ground spices and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
1. Now add turmeric powder, salt, red chili powder and cook for another 1 minute.
1. Add tomato puree, cashew paste and let it cook.
1. After 2 minutes add 1 cup of water and let it cook for 5 minutes.
1. After that, bhava parwal dike, cover the pan and let it cook on low heat for 3 to 5 minutes.
1. Add green coriander and serve hot.
1. Serve the Potala Rasa with Phulka, Bundi Raita and Kachumbar Salad hot.
