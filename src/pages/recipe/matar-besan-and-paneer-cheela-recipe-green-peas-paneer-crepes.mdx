### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1/2 cup Paneer (Homemade Cottage Cheese) - grated.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 2 Sunflower Oil - for cooking cheela.
- 1 teaspoon Ginger - roughly chopped.
- 2 Green Chillies - finely chopped.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil - for pan roasting.
- 1 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 1 cup Green peas (Matar) - steamed.
- 1/2 teaspoon Salt.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Matar Besan And Paneer Cheela Recipe, add the Green Peas/Matar, green chillies and ginger into the mixer grinder, add water and make a thick puree.
1. Add the matar paste in a large mixing bowl.
1. Into the bowl, add besan, grated cheese, turmeric powder, cumin powder and salt.
1. Stir in water and whisk well to make a thick cheela batter.
1. Preheat a pan over medium heat.
1. Take a medium scoop of matar and paneer cheela batter and drop in the center of the pan and gently spread to make cheela.
1. You can adjust the thickness of the cheela to suit your taste.
1. Drizzle oil from the sides and cook the matar besan cheela until you see golden spots on all sides.
1. Once the bottom side is roasted well, carefully flip the matar paneer besan cheela with a spatula and roast the other side by adding few drops of oil from sides again.
1. Transfer the Matar Besan And Paneer Cheela to a serving plate and serve hot.
1. Serve Matar Besan And Paneer Cheela with Tomato Onion Chutney, Tomato Garlic Chutney or any other chutney of your choice and Masala Chai for your breakfast or as a tea time snack.
