### Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Extra Virgin Olive Oil.
- Salt and Pepper - to taste.
- 2 Mango (Ripe) - peeled and diced.
- Lemon juice - 1 large lemon.
- 1 Avocado - peeled and diced.
- 1 cup Red Romaine lettuce.
- 1 cup Kabuli Chana (White Chickpeas) - cooked.
- 1 Dry Red Chilli - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Mango, Avocado And Chickpea Salad Recipe, first prep all the ingredients.
1. Having a day old chickpeas, that has been refrigerated helps in making this salad faster.
1. In a small mixing bowl, combine the ingredients for the dressing and whisk it well and keep it aside.
1. In a large mixing bowl, add the mangoes, avocados, cooked chickpeas and lettuce.
1. Mix well to combine them all together.
1. When you are ready to serve the Mango, Avocado and Chickpea Salad, drizzle the dressing over the salad, give it a gentle mix.
1. Serve the Mango, Avocado And Chickpea Salad along with Light And Creamy Broccoli Soup and Herb Mushroom Bruschetta.
