### Ingredients

-  1 inch ginger - tighten it.
-  1 bay leaf - break.
-  2 green chillies - thin And cut it straight.
-  4 sprigs of coriander - finely chopped.
-  1/2 tsp turmeric powder.
-  1 tbsp butter.
-  1/4 tsp cumin seeds.
-  lemon juice - as per use.
-  salt - as per taste.
- 1 cup yellow moong dal.
-  1 tsp ghee.

### Instructions

1. To make Dal Dal Tadka Recipe, first cook the lentils with 2-1 / 2 cups water, turmeric powder, salt until they become soft.
1. Cook till 3 cities come and turn off the gas.
1. Let the pressure drain automatically.
1. Heat ghee in a pan.
1. Add cumin seeds and let it splutter.
1. After the cumin seeds crackle, add ginger, green chillies, bay leaves and cook for 30 seconds.
1. Add this tempering to the dal and mix it.
1. Let the boil come and add butter to it.
1. Turn off the gas after 3 to 4 minutes and add lemon juice and green coriander to it.
1. Serve Dal Tadka with Paneer Butter Masala, Gourd Raita, Cumin Rice and Phulka for dinner.
