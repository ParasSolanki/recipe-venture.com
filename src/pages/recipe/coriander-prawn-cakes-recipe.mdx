### Ingredients

- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoons Dried oregano.
- 1 Onion - finely chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Rosemary.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli flakes.
- 500 grams Prawns - shelled.
- 4 sprig Coriander (Dhania) Leaves.
- 1 Leek - sliced.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1/4 cup Makki Ka Atta (Yellow Corn Meal Flour).
- 1 teaspoon Dried Thyme Leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Coriander Prawn Cakes, we will heat a skillet with oil.
1. Add onions and saute until they turn translucent.
1. Add leeks as well along with prawns.
1. Cook until the prawns curl up.
1. Add all of the seasoning along with maize flour.
1. Keep mixing till it comes together and forms a sticky mixture.
1. Allow it cool down and start shaping them into a flat cake.
1. Heat a flat skillet, drizzle some oil, place the cakes and carefully cook on either side till it golden brown.
1. Serve the Coriander Prawn Cakes along with a Tzatziki dip or dips of your choice to create a wonderful appetizer.
