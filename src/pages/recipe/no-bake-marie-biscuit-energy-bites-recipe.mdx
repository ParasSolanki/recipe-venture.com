### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Peanut Butter - melted.
- 1 cup Marie Biscuit - powdered.
- 1/3 cup Honey.
- 1/4 cup Sesame seeds (Til seeds) - dry roasted.
- 1 teaspoon Coconut Oil.
- 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract - (essence).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the No Bake Marie Biscuit Energy Bites, in a mixing bowl, add marie biscuit powder, peanut butter, honey, coconut oil and vanilla essence.
1. Mix well to combine.
1. Pinch small portions out of the biscuit and peanut mixture and roll to make small balls.
1. Put the sesame seeds onto a plate and roll the biscuit balls in the sesame seeds.
1. Keep the No Bake Marie Biscuit Energy Bites in the freezer for an hour.
1. After an hour take it out and serve the No Bake Marie Biscuit Energy Bites as a healthy snack.
1. Serve these No Bake Marie Biscuit Energy Bites with chai for an evening tea party or serve along with Cocoa Banana Almond Date Smoothie Recipe to your kids as an after school snack.
