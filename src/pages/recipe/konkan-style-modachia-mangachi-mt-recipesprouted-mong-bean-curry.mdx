### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Green Moong Dal (Whole).
- 3 Dry Red Chillies.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoons Sunflower Oil - or ghee.
- 1 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera).
- 1 teaspoon Mustard seeds.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 2 tablespoons Dessicated Coconut.
- 1 Kokum (Malabar Tamarind) - or tomato.
- 1/2 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 3 cloves Garlic.
- 1 Onion.
- 1 sprig Curry leaves.
- 1 pinch Asafoetida (hing).

### Instructions

1. To begin making Modachya Moongachi Amti Recipe, first pressure cook the sprouted moong beans with a little salt and two cups of water for one whistle.
1. Once cooked, keep them aside.
1. In the meantime, prepare a masala paste of onion, coconut, red chilies, garlic, ginger, cumin seeds, red chili powder, turmeric powder and kokum along with some water, and grind to a paste consistency in a mixer or a hand blender.
1. Heat a wok in medium flame, pour oil, add the mustard seeds and asafoetida.
1. Add the curry leaves and let it splutter.
1. Add the cooked sprouted moong beans and stir it.
1. Now add the ground masala paste and enough water to your desired consistency and season with salt.
1. Simmer for ten minutes and switch off the flame.
1. Serve Modachya Moongachi Amti recipe with phulkas or steamed rice, cucumber salad and roasted papad for a filling weeknight dinner.
