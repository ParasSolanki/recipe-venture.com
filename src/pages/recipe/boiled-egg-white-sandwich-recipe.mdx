### Ingredients

- Salt - as required.
- 2 tablespoon Butter.
- 1 Cheese - slice.
- 1/2 teaspoon Black pepper powder.
- 1/2 Onion - julienned.
- 3 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.
- 2 Whole Eggs - boiled.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Boiled Egg White Sandwich, De shell the boiled eggs and separate the egg yolk.
1. Cut the egg whites into small cubes.
1. To a mixing bowl, add the egg white cubes, julienned onions, cheese, black pepper powder and salt.
1. Mix everything well.
1. The next step is to assemble the sandwiches.
1. Apply butter on one side of the bread slices.
1. Take a slice of bread and spread some egg white and onion mixture on it.
1. Top this with another slice of bread.
1. Again spread some more egg white and onion mixture on this.
1. Close this with the third bread slice.
1. Grill the sandwich in a grill pan till the top most bread turns brownish.
1. Cut into triangle and it is ready to be served.
1. Boiled Egg White Sandwich can be served along with Masala Chai or juice, when you have guests in the evening or for kitty parties.
