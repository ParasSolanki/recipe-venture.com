### Ingredients

- Mozzarella cheese - or cheddar cheese.
- 1/4 cup Red Chilli flakes.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin powder (Jeera).
- 1 cup Mint Leaves (Pudina).
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/4 cup Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread - whole wheat.
- 1 cup Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Ginger Garlic Paste.
- Butter - as required to spread.
- 1/4 cup Fresh coconut - chopped.
- 1/2 Lemon juice.
- 2 Green Chillies - chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Chutney Chili Cheese Toast, firstly grind all the ingredients mentioned under ‘Chutney’ in a mixer grinder to form a smooth paste without adding water.
1. Transfer it to a bowl.
1. Preheat oven at 180 degree C for 10 minutes.
1. Take a baking tray and cover with a foil sheet.
1. Keep it aside.
1. To prepare toast, take a bread slice and cut into 2 or 4 halves.
1. Take a half bread slice and spread butter on one side.
1. Take a teaspoon of chutney and spread it over the butter nicely.
1. Top the bread slice with some chopped tomato and green capsicum.
1. Sprinkle some cheddar cheese and chili flakes on it.
1. Arrange bread slices on baking tray and bake it in an oven for about 5-8 minutes.
1. Serve Chutney Chili Cheese Toast along with a cup of hot Masala Chai and Linzer Cookies as a tea time snack.
