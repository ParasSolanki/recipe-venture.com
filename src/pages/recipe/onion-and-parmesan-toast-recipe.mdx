### Ingredients

- 6 tablespoon Parmesan cheese - grated.
- 2 tablespoon Butter.
- 4 Whole Wheat Brown Bread - whole wheat.
- 6 tablespoon Classic Mayonnaise (With Egg).
- 2 teaspoon Italian seasoning.
- 2 Onion - cut lengthwise.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Onion And Parmesan Toast, first spread butter over the bread slices and  grill the bread using a panini grill press or a pan.
1. While the bread is getting grilled, mix mayonnaise and onions and keep aside.
1. After the bread is grilled, remove the bread slices from the pan and spread 2 tablespoon mayonnaise onion mixture on the bread slices.
1. Over this, spread 1 tablespoon grated parmesan cheese.
1. Microwave the onion and parmesan toast for 1 minute or until the cheese starts bubbling.
1. Once done remove Onion And Parmesan Toast and garnish with Italian seasoning.
1. Serve the Onion And Parmesan Toast along with masala chai for an evening snack.
