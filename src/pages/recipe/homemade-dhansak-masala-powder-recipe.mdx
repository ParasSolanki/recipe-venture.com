### Ingredients

- 5 grams Nutmeg powder.
- 25 grams Mustard seeds.
- 25 grams Whole Black Peppercorns.
- 50 grams Cloves (Laung).
- 25 grams Poppy seeds.
- 50 grams Cinnamon Stick (Dalchini).
- 25 grams Black cardamom (Badi Elaichi).
- 250 grams Coriander (Dhania) Seeds.
- 25 grams Bay leaf (tej patta).
- 25 grams Star anise.
- 25 grams Cardamom (Elaichi) Pods/Seeds.
- 50 grams Dry Red Chillies.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Home Made Dhansak Masala Recipe by combining all the ingredients in a blender.
1. Blend all the spice into a smooth powder.
1. Keep the home made spice powder in an air-tight jar for a long lasting life.
1. You can use the Home Made Dhansak Masala recipe for making Ringan Ravaiya Recipe (Parsi Style Stuffed Eggplant Recipe).
