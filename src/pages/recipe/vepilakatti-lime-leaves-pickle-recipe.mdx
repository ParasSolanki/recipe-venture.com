### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing) - powder.
- 3 Shallots.
- Salt - to taste.
- Tamarind - small ball.
- 2 Dry Red Chillies - (adjust).
- 10 Kaffir lime leaves - or lemon leaves.
- 5 Curry leaves.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Veppilakatti - Lime Leaves Pickle Recipe, wash the Kaffir lime leaves & curry leaves, pat them dry.
1. Dry roast the red chilli in a shallow frying pan until they turn crispy.
1.  Keep aside.
1. Tear the stem of the kaffir lemon leaf.
1. Add all the ingredients like the kaffir lime leaves, roasted red chillis, shallots, curry leaves, salt, tamarind and asafoetida in a Blender and blend.
1. Do not add water.
1. I ground coarsely like it is done traditionally.
1. You can also blend into a fine paste.
1. Make them into small balls and store them in an airtight container.
1. Serve Veppilakatti | Lime Leaves Pickle Recipe with Kerala Style Varutharacha Sambar, Steamed Rice Recipe, Homemade Yogurt and Elai Vadam.
