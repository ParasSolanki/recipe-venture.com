### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Horse Gram Dal (Kollu/ Kulith).
- 2 tablespoons Black Urad Dal (Split).
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 tablespoons Yellow Moong Dal (Split).
- 2 tablespoons Chana dal (Bengal Gram Dal).
- 1/2 teaspoon Asafoetida (hing).
- 1/2 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 2 tablespoons Arhar dal (Split Toor Dal).
- 1 tablespoon Sesame (Gingelly) Oil.
- 10 to 12 Dry Red Chillies.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Andhra Style Paruppu Podi, heat oil in a kadhai/wok & roast the red chillies for abaout 3-4 minutes.
1. When the chillies are roasted collect them in a plate.
1. In the same pan, add 1/2 teaspoon of oil & roast the dals -  chana dal, urad dal, toor dal, moong dal and horse gramon low flame until golden brown.
1. When done, collect them in a plate.
1. Allow the ingredients to cool down completely.
1. Grind salt, jaggery, red chillies, hing into fine powder in a grinder.
1. Then add in the dals & grind them into coarse powder.
1. Andhra Style Paruppu Podi is ready to serve.
1. Serve this Andhra Style Paruppu Podi with hot steamed rice with a dash of ghee or gingerly oil and Keerai Sambar for a perfect South Indian meal.
