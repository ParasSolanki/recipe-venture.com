### Ingredients

- 1 Onion - sliced.
- 4 cloves Garlic.
- Salt - to taste.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- 3 teaspoons Maharashtrian Goda Masala.
- 1 inch Ginger.
- 1 cup Sev.
- 1/2 cup Dry coconut (kopra).
- 1 teaspoon Jaggery.
- 1 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Khandeshi Sev Bhaji Recipe, saute the onions on a flat skillet with oil.
1. Saute until they turn light brown.
1. Allow it to cool down, add onions and the rest of the ingredients to grind to form a smooth paste.
1. Heat a kadai with oil, add turmeric powder and red chilli powder and add the ground paste.
1. Add red chilli powder, turmeric powder and goda masala, cook until the onions turn dark brown.
1. Add jaggery and cook for 2 more minutes.
1. Add 1 cup of water and salt according to your taste and allow it to boil.
1. Once the curry begins to boil add sev and simmer for 2 more minutes and serve hot.
1. Serve the Khandeshi Sev Bhaji along with Lasun Batata Poori and Bihari Karela and Aloo Ki Subzi to make it a complete meal.
