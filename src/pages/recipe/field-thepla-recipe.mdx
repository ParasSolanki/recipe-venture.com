### Ingredients

- 1 Green Chilli - finely chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 cup Bottle gourd (lauki) - peeled and grated.
- 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1-1/2 cups Whole Wheat Flour.
- 1/2 cup Gram flour (besan).
- 2 tablespoons Hung Curd (Greek Yogurt).
- 2 tablespoons Ghee.
- 2 teaspoons Red Chilli powder.
- 2 tablespoon Coriander (Dhania) Leaves - finely chopped.
- 1 inch Ginger - peeled and crushed into paste.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Lauki Thepla Recipe, in a mixing bowl, add wheat flour, gram flour, turmeric powder, red chili powder, chopped green chili, ginger paste, garam masala, salt to taste and grated lauki.
1. Mix everything with your fingers.
1. Keep on mixing till a firm dough is formed.
1. Add curd only if needed.
1. Normally bottlegourd releases water.
1. But if the dough becomes too dry, add curd little at a time and make firm dough.
1. Do not let the dough rest as the bottle gourd in it will release water and make the dough sticky.
1. Make balls of equal size.
1. Dust the rolling board with flour.
1. Flatten the dough balls and roll it into thin theplas.
1. Do not make them thick.
1. Heat a tawa, spread 1 teaspoon ghee.
1. Fry the thepla by applying ghee on both sides.
1. When the theplas turn brownish they are done.
1. To keep theplas soft, apply more ghee/oil to the theplas.
1. Serve Lauki Thepla along with Aam Ka Achaar and Filter Coffee for your weekday breakfast.
