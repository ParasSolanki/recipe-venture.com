### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Sweet corn.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Dried oregano.
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1/4 cup Mozzarella cheese - grated.
- Black pepper powder - to taste.
- 4 tablespoons Homemade Pizza And Pasta Sauce.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- Butter - to grease waffle iron.
- 15 Baby spinach.
- 8 Whole Wheat Brown Bread.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Bread Waffle Pizza Recipe, firstly cut the sides of each bread slice.
1. Grease waffle iron with butter and put one bread slice over it.
1. Now spread pizza sauce and arrange chopped vegetables including onion, green bell peppper, sweet corn, tomato and baby spinach over it.
1. Add mozzarella cheese over the bread slice.
1. Now, sprinkle black pepper powder and dried oregano leaves.
1. Cover it with another bread slice.
1. Cover and cook it for 5 minutes or until it turns golden in colour.
1. Repeat the same process to make more waffles.
1. Serve Bread Waffle Pizza Recipe along with Watermelon Panzanella Salad, Penne Pasta Arrabiata and Chocolate Ganache Tart for your weekend brunch.
