### Ingredients

- 2 tablespoon Badam (Almond) - roasted.
- 1 cup Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 cup Tapioca Flour (Sago) - soaked.
- 4 tablespoon Honey.
- 1/2 cup Anardana Powder (Pomegranate Seed Powder).
- 1 Apple - cubed.
- 1 Ripe Bananas - sliced.

### Instructions

1. We begin making Sago Yogurt Parfait Recipe ( Creamy Tapioca Pearls With Assorted Fruits Recipe) by soaking the tapioca pearls in water for about 4 hours.
1. Then later drain the water and boil it in water with 1/2 cup of water for about 5 minutes till it is soft and done.
1. Keep all the fruits ready and cut.
1. Mix the boiled sago with yogurt, fruits, and nuts in a bowl.
1. Drizzle the honey over it.
1. Serve your Sago Yogurt Parfait ( Creamy Tapioca Pearls With Assorted Fruits) for a quick breakfast with Spinach omelette recipe or broccoli pesto open toast for a healthy start.
