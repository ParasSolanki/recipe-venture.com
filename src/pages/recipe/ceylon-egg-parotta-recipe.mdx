### Ingredients

- 1 cup All Purpose Flour (Maida).
- 5 cloves Garlic - chopped.
- 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric powder (Haldi).
- 1 inch Ginger - chopped.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Whole Wheat Flour.
- Salt - a pinch.
- 5 tablespoon Curd (Dahi / Yogurt).
- 1 Onion - chopped.
- 1 Green bell peppers - chopped.
- 1 teaspoon Red Chilli powder.
- 3 Whole Eggs.
- Sunflower Oil.
- 1 Tomato - chopped.
- Sunflower Oil - as required.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Ceylon Egg Parotta Recipe, we will add all the ingredients into a mixing bowl except oil.
1. Add little water slowly and mix to form a soft and sticky dough.
1. Add a tablespoon of oil and knead it a little more and rest for at least 20 minutes outside.
1. To make the stuffing, heat a skillet with oil, add ginger and garlic and saute till it softens.
1. Add onions and saute till they turn translucent and later you can add bell peppers and keep cooking until it softens.
1. Add chopped tomatoes and green chilies, sprinkle with salt and cook till the tomatoes turn mushy.
1. Add in red chili powder, turmeric powder and cook for another 5 minutes and switch off the heat.
1. Whisk the eggs in a bowl, add salt and saut√©ed vegetable mixture and start beating them till it combines.
1. Heat a roti tawa on a medium heat and leave it side.
1. Pinch out a little dough from the kneaded dough.
1. Dip it in the flour and start rolling to form a thin dough.
1. Place the rolled dough on the tawa and cook till you seed small boils and flip it over.
1. Add some egg mixture in the center and fold over on either side to form a small pocket.
1. You can seal by adding little egg after every fold as well.
1. Flip it over and cook on either side, drizzle some oil as well and cook till it light brown.
1. Serve the Ceylon Egg Parotta Recipe as a snack or just as it is for breakfast or lunch with a cup of Masala Chai or a fresh Muskmelon (Kharbuj) Juice by the side.
