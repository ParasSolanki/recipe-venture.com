### Ingredients

- 1/2 tablespoon Salt.
- 1 cup Sugar.
- 1 tablespoon Vanilla Extract.
- 1/4 cup Corn syrup.
- 1 tablespoon Sunflower Oil.
- 5 tablespoons Butter (unsalted).
- 1/4 tablespoon Baking soda.
- 1/3 cup Dry Corn Kernals.

### Instructions

1. To begin making Salted Caramel Popcorn Recipe, heat oil in a large pot, add corn kernels and cover.
1. Shake in between till all kernels popped properly.
1. Keep aside.
1. Melt butter in a large pan,add corn syrup and sugar on medium flame.
1. Bring the mixture to a boil, keep boiling until it turns to amber colour.
1. Remove the mixture from heat, stir in vanilla, baking soda and salt.
1. Take a large bowl and add popcorn, pour sugar mixture over popcorn and coat all popcorn properly.
1. Spread coated popcorn on a baking sheet, let it cool completely.
1. Salted Caramel popcorn is ready.
1. Serve Salted Caramel Popcorn Recipe along with Cold Coffee Smoothie Recipe on a movie night.
