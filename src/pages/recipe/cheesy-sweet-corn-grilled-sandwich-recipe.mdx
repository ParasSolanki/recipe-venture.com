### Ingredients

- 1/2 teaspoon Whole Black Peppercorns - crushed.
- 6 Whole Wheat Brown Bread - whole wheat.
- 1 Tomato - finely chopped.
- 3 tablespoons Butter - melted.
- Salt - to taste.
- 1/2 cup Sweet corn - kernels.
- 2 teaspoons Dried oregano.
- 1/2 cup Mozzarella cheese - grated.
- 1 Green Bell Pepper (Capsicum) - finely chopped.

### Instructions

1. To begin making the Cheesy Sweet Corn Grilled Sandwich Recipe, In a mixing bowl, add mix sweet corn kernels, diced green capsicum, diced tomato, crushed black pepper, oregano and salt.
1. Gently toss the mixture, check the salt and adjust to suit your taste.
1. Brush butter on one side of the two bread slices and on the non-buttered side spread the above made mixture evenly.
1. Top with grated mozzarella and then top it with other two bread slices and brush with butter.
1. Using a sandwich maker or grill pan, grill the sandwiches until crisp and browned on both sides.
1. Proceed the same way with the remaining slices of bread.
1. Serve the Cheesy Sweet Corn Grilled Sandwiches along with a glass of Fresh Ginger Lemonade or a cup of Espresso Coffee for a breakfast, or pack into a lunchbox for kids.
