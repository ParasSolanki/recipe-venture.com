### Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Brown rice flakes.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1 cup Water.
- 2 cups Milk.
- 3/4 cup Condensed Milk.

### Instructions

1. To prepare Brown Rice Flakes Pudding/Desi Poha Kheer Recipe, rinse the poha well and soak in a cup of water for about 15 minutes.
1. Drain all excess water.
1. Transfer poha into a Pressure cooker.
1. Add the milk and cardamom powder.
1. Close the lid and pressure cook at low heat for 15-20 minutes until 1 whistle, or till cooked well.
1. Switch off the flame and allow the pressure to release by itself.
1. Mix in the condensed milk and serve Brown Rice Flakes Pudding/Desi Poha Kheer Recipe after a party dessert.
