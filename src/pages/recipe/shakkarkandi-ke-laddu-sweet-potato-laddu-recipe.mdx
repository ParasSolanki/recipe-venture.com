### Ingredients

- 1/4 cup Ghee.
- 3/4 cup Sugar - adjust as per taste.
- 10 Raisins.
- 1/2 teaspoon Cardamom Powder (Elaichi).
- 1 tablespoon Cashew nuts - crushed coarsely.
- 2 cups Sweet Potatoes - boiled and mashed.
- 1 tablespoon Badam (Almond) - crushed coarsely.
- 3 tablespoons Fresh coconut - (grated) for filling + 1 tablespoon (for garnishing).

### Instructions

1. To start making Shakkarkandi ke Laddu, heat ghee in a non-sticky pan.
1. Add mashed sweet potatoes and saut√© for 12-15 mins.
1. Add grated coconut, green cardamom powder, almonds & cashews, raisins and sugar.
1. Mix well over medium heat.
1. Keep stirring until the mixture comes together as a single mass.
1. Takes about 3-5 minutes.
1. When the mixture is still warm, shape them into small balls.
1. Put grated coconut in a plate and roll the balls over it.
1. Place them in the fridge for 10-15 mins and serve the yummy Shakkarkandi ke Laddu/ Sweet Potato Laddu recipe for teatime.
